# Tube mill.

## Abstract
A gas swept tube mill has a rotary shell 1 supported on a bearing 5 by a slide ring 3 , which is reinforced by a stiffening web plate 4 . Ground material and gas pass out of the mill shell through a sieve plate 10 and openings 8 in a frustoconical connecting section 12 into a stationary casing 9 . The wider end of the section 12 is of similar diameter to the shell 1 , to which it is fastened, and the narrower end of the section 12 is fastened to a drive shaft 7 for rotating the shell 1 .
