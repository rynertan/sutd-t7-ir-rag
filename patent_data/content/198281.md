# Nuclear fuel assembly with a removable top nozzle.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a nuclear fuel assembly the top nozzle 22 of which is slip fit onto guide thimble upper end portions which carry load collars 44 for transferring compres sive loads from the top nozzle to the guide thimbles 14 . The uppermost spacer grid 38 has upwardly extending skirt por tions 40 which are releasably latched to the top nozzle and adapted to transfer tensive loads. The top nozzle is removable simply by moving the skirt portions 40 out of interlocking engagement with the top nozzle and then lifting the latter off the guide thimbles.
