# THREE DIMENSIONAL MULTI COLOR PLASTIC PARTS AND METHOD OF MAKING SAME

## Abstract
Three dimensional multi colored plastic parts and a method for manufacturing such parts for automobile interior trim door panels, consoles and instrument panels and the like includes dry cast thermoplastic powder sized in the range of 60 400 microns and with two or more colors of particles mixed for distribution onto a heated mold section with the mixed powder particles being heated to randomly flow at particle margins to form two or more shade tones in a resultant three dimensional multi colored textured part of fuzed material.
