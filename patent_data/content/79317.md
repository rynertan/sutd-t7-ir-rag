# Pneumatic power tool.

## Abstract
In a pneumatically powered portable tool there is provided a speed governor and an overspeed safety device the purpose of which are to ensure a predetermined maximum speed by controlling the air inlet flow to the motor. A cup shaped shell insert 28 is detachably mounted in the housing 10 to form with the latter an air supply passage 58 and to support the valve means 51 of the overspeed safety device. The top surface of the shell insert 28 is exposed through an aperture 57 in the housing 10 and carries the maximum speed marking 52 of the tool.
