# Exposure device.

## Abstract
Exposure device for image wise exposure dot by dot along a line, of a movable light sensitive layer 21 to a laser 10 , in which a slit 19 is disposed near the light sensitive layer 21 , the slit width 18 being less than the dimension of the laserbeam 14,17 as considered in the direction of slit width at the slit. By using a narrow slit of this kind, it is attained by the use of simple means that the laserbeam always describes at least one and the same line, also when run out occurs.
