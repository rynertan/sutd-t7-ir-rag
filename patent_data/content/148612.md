# A STEREOSPECIFIC MICROBIAL REDUCTION

## Abstract
The dopaminergic antagonist ciladopa, also known as S 2 4 2 hydroxy 2 3,4 dimethoxyphenyl ethyl 1 piperazinyl 2,4,6 cycloheptatrien 1 one, is prepared by stereospecific micro bial reduction of 2 4 2 3,4 dimethoxyphenyl 2 oxoethyl 1 piperazinyl 2,4,6 cycloheptatrien 1 one.
