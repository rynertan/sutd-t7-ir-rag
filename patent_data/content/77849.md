# Resin forming material and restorative material suitable for medical or dental use.

## Abstract
Resin forming material and restorative material suitable for medical or detal use comprising dipentaerythritol penta or hexa acrylate or methacrylate or a blend of dipenta erythritol penta or hexa acrylate or methacrylate and tetramethylolmethane tri or tetra acrylate or tetra methylolmethane tri or tetra methacrylate.
