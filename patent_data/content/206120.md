# Pyrolytic boron nitride crucible and method for producing the same.

## Abstract
A pyrolytic boron nitride crucible is provided. The crucible contains at least five wall layers consisting of first and second wall layers. The thickness of each of the first wall layers is 5 to 100 microns and the thickness of each of the second wall layers is 1 50 to 1 1 of that of the first wall layer. The total thick ness of the entire wall layers is 0.5 to 3 mm. The first wall layer is bonded to and laminated alternately with the second wall layer.
