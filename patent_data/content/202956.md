# STADIUM SAFETY SYSTEM

## Abstract
A stadium safety system comprises a high pressure water supply 5, 6 connected to a water pipe 2 which extends around the boundary of a playing field 3 . Adjustable nozzles 4 are provided at spaced intervals around the pipe 2 and water from the nozzles 4 may be used to extinguish fires, or for crowd control, in the crowd enclosure 13 or to water the playing field 3 .
