# Closure mechanism for an athletic boot.

## Abstract
A closure mechanism 10 for overlapping closure flaps of an athletic boot comprises at least one cable 32, 34 arranged to overlap the flaps and travel interiorly of the boot to a position remote from the overlapping flaps. The cable 32, 34 then exits the interior of the boot and is connected to a latch for pulling the end of the cable 32, 34 to draw it about and close the overlapping flaps. The closure system allows comfort and convenience.
