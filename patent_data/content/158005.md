# Method for adjusting a vacuum brake booster output member relative to a master cylinder piston.

## Abstract
A vacuum brake booster 10 is coupled to a master cyl inder 40 at the same time that an output member 80 for the vacuum brake booster is adjusted relative to a piston 46 for the master cylinder. The adjustment is carried out by a plunger 82 adapted to activate a bonding material 88 for bonding the plunger to the output member.
