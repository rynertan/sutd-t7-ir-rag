# Lecterns.

## Abstract
A lectern for standing on a generally flat supporting surface and holding an article such as a book, binder, clipboard or other article carrying matter for viewing, reading, or lecturing from, comprises a generally planar member 10, 10a, 10b, 10c, 10d which is adapted to rest in a stable manner on a supporting surface with support means 17, 47, 57, 67 formed or provided at or adjacent to one edge of the planar member for supporting the article by engage ment of a marginal portion thereof in a predetermined display position in which ther article extends in a generally upright manner whilst the base member is arranged sub stantially horizontally. When not in use, the base member can be accommodated within the cover plates of an associ ated binder.
