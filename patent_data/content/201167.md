# Asphalt elastomeric blends.

## Abstract
An elastomeric composition which may form a water proof membrane useful as a coating or flashing for roofs or as a liner for containing liquids. The composition contains a neutralized sulfonated EPDM polymer and asphalt, the for mer component comprising at least 30 by weight of the to tal weight of the polymer and asphalt. The composition has outstanding physical properties and an unexpectedly high tensile strength.
