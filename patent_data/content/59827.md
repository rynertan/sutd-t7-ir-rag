# An ink composition for ink jet printers.

## Abstract
This invention relates to an ink composition for ink jet printers. The ink is both waterfast and lightfast and includes an aqueous solution of a copolymer of styrene and acrylic acid, a volatile base capable of solubilizing said copolymer a blue dye which is compatible with said copolymer, a non compatible, lightfast, copper phthalo cyanine water soluble blue dye and a red tinting dye.
