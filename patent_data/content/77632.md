# Compositions comprising a bituminous material and an epoxylated amine asphalt anti stripping agent, aggregate coated therewith and substrates coated with said aggregate.

## Abstract
This invention provides compositions of matter compris ing a bituminous material and, as an anti stripping agent, an epoxylated polyamine wherein at least two of the amino nitrogen atoms are separated by six carbon atoms. These compositions provide improved adhesion between aggre gate materials and the bitumen containing material.
