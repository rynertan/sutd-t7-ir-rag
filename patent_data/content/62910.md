# Combustion wick.

## Abstract
There is provided a fuel suck up portion 102 wherein liquid fuel is sucked up and a fuel gasifying portion A provided above said fuel suck up portion 102 wherein of said fuel suck up 102 and fuel gasifying portions A , at least the fuel gasifying portion is formed from silica alumina type ceramic fibers with an organic binder, with at least a part of said portion being impregnated with a coating material composed principally of an inorganic pigment, silicic anhydride and a surface active agent. By impregnating at least part of the fuel gasifying portion 101 with the coating material, no or little tar like substance is formed or deposited on the fuel gasifying portion 101 .
