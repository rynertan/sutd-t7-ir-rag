# Composition for cleaning and surface treatment of phonograph records and similar objects and its use.

## Abstract
The invention provides a composition for cleaning and surface treatment of objects such as phonograph records, video or sound recording heads, tape paths in tape recor ders and pick up styli, comprising in dissolved or emul sified form, a from 0.001 to 1.2 by weight of natural or synthetic wax or wax like substance, b from 0.001 to 2.5 by weight of fluid silicone oil with lubricating activity, c from 0.001 to 2.5 by weight of surface active poly siloxane copolymer, d from 0.001 to 1.2 by weight of fluorinated organic surface active compound, and e from 0.001 to 2.5 by weight of nonionic surfactant, the percentages being based on the total weight of the composition. Such compositions may be used for the stated purpose with excellent results.
