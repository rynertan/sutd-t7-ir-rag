# Vehicle brake booster.

## Abstract
A vehicle brake booster comprises a brake master cylin der 14 operable by a brake pedal 11 , and a power valve 19 including valve means 20, 22 operable by the brake pedal 11 to admit fluid under pressure from a source 16 to a boost pis ton 31 for applying a boost force to the master cylinder 14 , and to apply a reaction force to the brake pedal 11 , the valve means 20, 22 are movable against a spring 21 by fluid under pressure from the source 16 from an inactive position to an active position so long as fluid under pressure is available.
