# Box filter.

## Abstract
A disposable box filter is provided comprising a box housing 1 in at least two portions 2, 3 and having a fluid chamber 4 with two fluid ports 5, 6 , and a filter element 10 in corrugated sheet form extending across the fluid chamber 4 , held by the housing across the line of flow between the fluid ports 5, 6 , with the corrugations supported in a fixed uniform spacing against differential fluid pressure and fluid flow.
