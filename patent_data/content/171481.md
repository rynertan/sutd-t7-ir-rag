# Improved clamping collar.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to an improved clamping collar of the type comprising a hooped band, one end of which is associated with a support for an endless screw, provided for meshing in teeth stamped at the opposite end of said hooped band. This clamping collar is characterized in that said support is formed by a portion having a U shaped section 6 which has longitudinal entrant portions which are fitted into the neck of the screw 10 for providing the necessary support for same, offering a great rigidity and avoiding its coming out on slackening the clamping collar.
