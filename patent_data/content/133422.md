# AUTOMATIC APPARATUS FOR HANDLING AND BINDING PACKS BUMPS OF TEXTILE FIBRE TAPES

## Abstract
The apparatus comprises containers defined by cylin drical walls 9 , with raisable bottom 20 means for sliding the containers on smooth slide surfaces up to a transfer truck 12 means for bringing said truck under a press 3 which acts onto said bottom 20 to perform the pressing of the material and its subsequent lifting from the cylindrical walls 9 means 41 for binding the material while it is kept press ed, and means for transferring the bound material up to a container 5 having a plurality of seats which is arranged for further handlings.
