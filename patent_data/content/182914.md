# COLOR FILTER.

## Abstract
A color filter comprising a transparent base plate 1 having provided thereon a porous active membrane layer 2 formed by active alumina, active silica. or a mixture thereof, a colored region or pattern 5 formed by a dye 4 deposited into micropores 3 of the active membrane layer, and a coating layer 6 sealing the upper portion of the micropores 3 . This color filter can be prepared relatively easily, possesses excellent resistances to heat, water, chemicals, solvent, etc., and is suitable as a display for various devices, particularly a liquid crystal display.
