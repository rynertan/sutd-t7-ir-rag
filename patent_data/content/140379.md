# Method and apparatus for measuring the degree of reaction between antigens and leukocyte cellular antibodies.

## Abstract
Method and apparatus for the objective determination of the degree of reaction between a suspected allergen and a blood sample, by comparing the number and or size distribution of white blood cells present in the blood sample before and after reaction thereof with the suspected allergen. The apparatus 10 comprises a counter 12, an analyzer 14, a computer interface 16 and a computer 18, each connected in series, and a disc 20, a printer 22 and a printer 24 each connected to the output of computer 18.
