# Fluid cleaner systems.

## Abstract
A fluid cleaner system including a filter element 50 of substantially cylindrical construction corrugated in a cross section including the axis thereof to allow a fluid to flow axially from one end to the other end. The filter element 50 is supported and sealed by support means 1 and sealing means and mounted in a case 7 and a cap 6 , with gaps between the filter element 50 and the case 7 and between the filter element 50 and the cap 6 .
