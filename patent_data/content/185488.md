# Container heated by microwave oven.

## Abstract
This invention provides a container 11 for use in containing a material 12 to be heated in a microwave oven, which container 11 is capable of preventing locally occur ring over heating, and of allowing its contents 12 to be uniformly and effectively heated. The container 11 compris es a container proper, a lid 16 and a microwave shielding layer 15 , the shielding layer 15 being disposed at least at the position 14 , through the intermediary of an air layer, where the upper surface of the material 12 contacts the side surface of the container proper 13 , i.e., is disposed on the portion 17 of the lid which overhangs the side wall of the container proper 13 .
