# Gas flow arrangement.

## Abstract
To achieve uniform gas flow in a chamber to which gas is fed from a cylindrical pipe 1 pipe has a closed end wall 6 at least two spaced rectangular apertures 7,8 on one side near said end wall and, between each adjacent pair of aper tures, a radial partition wall 9 , having a central circular aper ture 12 . The arrangement is applicable to gas flow to a gas burner or a gas filter.
