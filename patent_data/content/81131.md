# Contraceptive vaginal barrier.

## Abstract
Contraceptive vaginal barrier comprising two separate parts to be associated before using in order to form a vaginal diaphragm of oval or circular perimeter, a first part being an auter ring structure, contained in a plane, equal or similar with an outer structure of a known vaginal diaphragm, a second part being a container made of fabric, impregnated with a spermicide and or medicinal substance, having a first aperture for introducing and permanently closing therein an outer structure and a second aperture for removing the outer structure therefrom.
