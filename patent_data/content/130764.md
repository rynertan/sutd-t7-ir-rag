# Highly absorbent substrate article.

## Abstract
A reusable highly absorbent article, for example a cloth or towel for mopping up household spillages, comprises a sub strate carrying a pressure sensitive polymeric material capable of absorbing, retaining, releasing and reabsorbing large vol umes of of water or electrolyte. The polymer is preferably the sulphonation product of a high void volume styrene polymer prepared by polymerisation of a high internal phase emulsion.
