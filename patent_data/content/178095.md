# Process for preparing ink for ink jet printer.

## Abstract
In a process for preparing an ink for a drop on demand type ink jet printer, sonic vibration is applied to the ink 21 which is being prepared or has been prepared so that a gas 25 en trained into the ink by incorporation of a dye therein can be re moved. This ink can be stably injected through a recording head a, b , even if the size of the head is made smaller to in crease the atomizing frequency, or the recording speed.
