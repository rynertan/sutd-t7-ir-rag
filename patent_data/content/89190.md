# The production of descriptive labels for different items.

## Abstract
A label printing system includes an input device 1 , a memory 2 , a CRT display device 3 , a printing mechanism 4 , registration means 6 and label printing means 5 . The input device, comprising manual data input means, enters label creation data and edits the data to provide label creation information. The memory is adapted to store the input data and the label creation information obtained by editing the same, the information being displayed on the screen of the CRT and printed by the printing mechanism. The registration means is adapted to register the label creation data and information in the memory. The label printing means causes the information to be displayed on the screen of the CRT, for checking, and causes the printing mechanism to print out the information on a label.
