# Process for recovering gases from landfills.

## Abstract
A process for treating a landfill 10 for recovering meth ane therefrom wherein a mixture of methane and carbon dioxide is withdrawn from a central portion 16 of the landfill 10 and the carbon dioxide and methane are separated. The methane is fed to a pipeline 24 and a portion of the carbon dioxide is fed to wells 31 spaced around the periphery of the landfill at a pressure of 7 to 170 kPa to prevent air from enter ing the landfill 10 around the periphery thereof.
