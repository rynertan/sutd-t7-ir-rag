# High permeability alloy.

## Abstract
A high permeability alloy having an excellent wear resistance, comprising 10 wt of Al, 4 11 wt of Si, 2.1 20.0 wt of Ru and the balance Fe, and further contain ing or not containing 0.01 1.0 wt of at least one element selected from a group consisting of rare earth elements, Zr and Nb, and or 0.2 0.5 wt of Ti, or 0.5 10.0 wt of Cr.
