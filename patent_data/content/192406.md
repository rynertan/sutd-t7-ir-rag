# DEBRIS REMOVAL SYSTEM FOR A NUCLEAR FUEL ASSEMBLY

## Abstract
A system for removing debris from a nuclear fuel assembly includes a work platform disposable on the fuel assembly racks in a water pool. The system uses a rotatable turret type tool mount carrying four different resiliently mounted cleaning tools, including a pick, a brush, a water lance and water actuated tweezers, and includes drive means for effecting X Y Z movement of the tool mount for working on the fuel assembly.
