# Image forming apparatus.

## Abstract
An image forming apparatus is provided with a photosensi tive drum 7 rotatably disposed in a housing 1 , and a develop ing device 10 for developing the latent image formed on the surface of the photosensitive drum 7 . The developing device 10 includes a first developing unit 32a for developing the latent image by means of a first red developing agent G1 and a second developing unit 32b for developing the latent image by means of a second black developing agent G2 . The first and second developing units are alternatively driven by a switch 156 pro vided on a control panel which is disposed on the housing 1 .
