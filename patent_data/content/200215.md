# Raw material supplying device and process for using this device.

## Abstract
A raw material supplying device having a gas tight tank 1 for receiving a material 2 to be gasified by heating heating means for heating the raw material 2 within the tank 1 and a plurality of pipes 51,52,53 for conveying a plurality of streams of gas in parallel to each other to a plurality of ports of 111,112,113 of a reaction apparatus 12 , and a process for using the raw material supplying device. The raw material supplying device can be used in a system for manufacturing glass fibers in which the ends of the plurality of pipes 51,52,53 are connected to the gas feed ports of a least one multi layer burner 111,112,113 .
