# CROSSLINKABLE FLAME RETARDANT COMPOSITION OF POLYPHENYLENE ETHER AND ELASTOMERS

## Abstract
Flame retardant mixtures of a polyphenylene ether resin, an alkenyl aromatic elastomer, an organic phosphate flame retardant agent and a crosslinking agent are described. They are curable by heating or by exposure to high energy radiation. Articles such as electrical wires and cables com prising insulation made of the composition, and a method of their production, are also disclosed.
