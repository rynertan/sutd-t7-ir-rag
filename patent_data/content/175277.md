# A METHOD FOR THE MANUFACTURING OF SHEETLIKE OR WEBLIKE MATERIAL PROVIDED WITH MARKINGS BECOMING APPARENT IN TRANSMITTED LIGHT

## Abstract
The invention relates to a method for the manufacture of sheetlike or weblike material which is the carrier of markings apparent in trasmitted light, the said markings being produced in that sheets or webs of paper with marking of the desired type printed onto them are laminated to one another with the print carrying surfaces facing towards one another and that the lamination is carried out with a thin plastic layer.
