# Stabilised photographic color developer compositions and processes.

## Abstract
Photographic color developing compositions containing a primary aromatic amino color developing agent and a hydroxylamine compound are stabilised against decomposi tion of hydroxylamine and formation of sludge by incorpor ating stabilised chelating compounds of the formula The composition may also contain another different chelating agent, e.g. of the aminopolycarboxylic or amino polyphosphonic acid type.
