# PRESSURE COMPENSATION VALVE, PARTICULARLY FOR HOT OR COLD ISOTHERMAL ROOMS

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a pressure balancing valve for isothermal rooms, comprising a hollow member designed to place the interior of an isothermal room directly into communication with the outside, this member being provided transversely with a set of flap valve elements which are freely hinged to said hollow member and divide the interior thereof into a group of successive or cascade compartments there being also provided for said hollow member a containing cup which is designed to be mounted orthogonally, either in an encased manner or projecting towards the outside, relative to a vertical wall of the corres ponding isothermal room which receives it.
