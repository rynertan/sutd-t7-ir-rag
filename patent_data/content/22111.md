# An automatic apparatus for feeding and fitting a gore piece to the inside thigh opening of a stocking material.

## Abstract
A material strip 30 intermittently delivered from a supply reel 403 , gore pieces 30 are separated one after another from the material strip after trimming into a diamond shape by means of a pair of cutters 421, 422 , and individual gore pieces are fed and fitted, in a folded state, to the inside thigh opening of a stocking material.
