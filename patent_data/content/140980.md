# THERAPEUTIC APPLIANCE EMPLOYING FLAT BATTERY.

## Abstract
A therapeutic method employing a flat battery comprises applying the negative electode of the flat battery to the affected part of the body, and attaching the battery to the skin by a substance coating the positive electrode of the battery. A therapeutic appliance is also provided in which the positive electrode side of the flat battery is mounted on a coating material of an area larger than that of the battery, and the circumferential edge of the coating material can be attached to the skin with the negative electrode side of the battery applied to the affected part of the body.
