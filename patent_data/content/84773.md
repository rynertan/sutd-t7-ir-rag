# Expanding headpiece for reels in general.

## Abstract
The invention concerns an expanding headpiece 1 for reels in general, which comprises first 2 and second 3 flanges, mutually secured coaxially together in spaced apart re lationship, at the area included between said flanges there be ing provided an annular chamber 10 of an elastically defor mable material which can be expanded pneumatically to make said headpiece rigid with the interior of a reel core 11 or the like. A peculiarity of the invention is that between said first 2 and second 3 flanges, at at least one base of said annular chamber 10 , means 16, 17 are provided for centering said headpiece 1 in said core 11 which can be expanded radially by expanding said annular chamber 10 in an axial direction.
