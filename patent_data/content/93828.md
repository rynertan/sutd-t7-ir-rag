# Sulpho free basic azo compounds in metal free or 1 1 or 1 2 metal complex form.

## Abstract
Sulpho free mono and disazo compounds in their metal free 1 1 metal complex or 1 2 metal complex form having an average of at least 2 water solubilizing basic groups per dyestuff unit, of the general formula These compounds are useful as dyes for leather and paper.
