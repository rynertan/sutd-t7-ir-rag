# AUTO TELEPHONE ANSWERING APPARATUS

## Abstract
In an auto telephone answering apparatus for playing back an outgoing message recorded on a magnetic tape 1 and sending the outgoing message to a calling party and for recording an incoming message from the calling party on the magnetic tape 1 , an auto answer mode is set for automatic ally connecting telephone lines to the auto telephone answering apparatus upon detection of an incoming call from the calling party, playing the outgoing message recorded on the magnetic tape 1 , and setting an incoming call wait state for recording the incoming message from the calling party on the magnetic tape 1 after playback of the outgoing message, an end of playback of the incoming message or messages recorded on the magnetic tape 1 is detected in a playback mode and results in an incoming message end signal, and a control 10 causes establishment of the auto answer setting mode in response to the incoming message end signal.
