# Emulsifier and its use for the manufacture of an emulsion in mineral oil.

## Abstract
Emulsifier containing a mixture of a a nonionic surface active ethylene oxide adduct having the general formula R OC₂H₄ The weight ratio between the nonionic surface active ethylene oxide adduct and the calcium salt of dodecylbenzene sulphonic acid is from 80 20 to 40 60. The emulsifier is used to emulsify 1 10 by weight of water in mineral oil.
