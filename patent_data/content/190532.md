# A transfer apparatus for tire carcasses and tire bands.

## Abstract
A transfer apparatus 10 for an uncured cylindrical tire component and which comprises a hollow cylindrical hous ing 11 having a pair of annular sealing and gripping means 14 , preferably air operable bladders 15 , located one at each end of the housing. The housing 11 has a vacuum port 12 located between the two annullar gripping and sealing means 14 so after said means 14 seal against the outer side of the cylindrical component, air is evacuated through said port 12 to retain the component in the housing 11 . The invention also describes a method of transporting a cylindrical tire component using the above apparatus.
