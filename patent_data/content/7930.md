# Arylpyridazinylhydrazine salt, processes for preparing it and pharmaceutical compositions containing it.

## Abstract
A process for preparing a new compound, racemic 3 2 3 t butylamino 2 hydroxypropoxy phenyl 6 hydra zino pyridazine dihydrochloride monohydrate, which is a useful antihypertensive and which can be recrystallised with consistent water content and which is stable under normal conditions of temperature and humidity.
