# SUBSTITUTED ANILINOPYRIMIDINES, COMPOSITIONS AND USE THEREOF AS HERBICIDES, PROCESSES FOR THEIR PREPARATION AND INTERMEDIATES FOR USE THEREIN

## Abstract
The invention concerns novel compounds of the formula The compounds are herbicides and in further embodi ments the invention provides herbicidal compositions con taining as active ingredient a compound of formula I, a pro cess for severely damaging or killing unwanted plants by applying to the plants or to the growth medium of the plants an effective amount of a compound of formula I, processes for the preparation of compounds of formula I and intermediates useful in the preparation of compounds of formula I.
