# Means for supporting a hinge pin on beams joist and the like.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a hinge pin support for wooden beams. The support comprise two members 16,18 which when mounted on opposite sides of a wooden beam 10,12 can support a hinge pin 14 between them, and which are attached to the beam 10,12 by a plurality of nail like projections which are formed integrally with each member 16,18 . The members may also include interengaging projec tions and grooves 22,24 for locating the members 16,18 relative to each other to ensure that the hinge pin support means of the members can be correctly aligned with each other.
