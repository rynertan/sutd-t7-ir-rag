# Take out mechanism for a glassware forming machine.

## Abstract
The take out mechanism is operable to remove articles from the mould of a glassware forming machine. Two gripper member supports 46, 48 92 are mounted on a horizontal shaft 44 90 for arcuate movement and driving means 18, 34, 36, 40, 58, 60, 62 84, 102, 98, 94 is operable to move the support to move grippers 10, 12, 64 70, 72 mounted on the supports. The supports are moved by moving two racks 58, 60 94 past the shaft on opposite sides of the shaft each rack being meshed with teeth on a surface of the support.
