# FLAME RETARDANT NON DRIPPING POLYCARBONATE COMPOSITIONS

## Abstract
Flame retardant and drip retardant polycarbonate com positions exhibiting good thick section impact comprised of, in physical admixture i at least one halogen free and sulfur containing poly carbonate resin derived from a at least one halogen free, sulfur free and non polycyclic dihydric phenol, b at least one halogen free thiodiphenol, and c a carbonate precur sor, and ii fibrous glass These compositions contain an amount of said fibrous glass and an amount of said thiodiphenol effective to render said compositions flame retardant and drip retardant.
