# Control arrangement for a hydraulic assist turbocharger.

## Abstract
A control arrangement 16 for an hydraulic assist turbocharger 12 of the type including an hydraulic turbine 58 driven by hydraulic fluid under pressure to supplemen tally drive an exhaust gas driven turbocharger 18, 20 of an I.C. engine 14 during engine operating conditions when supplemental air flow is required. The control arrangement includes a hydropneumatic control valve 76 responsive to engine speed and load to regulate the volume of hydraulic pressure fluid supplied to the hydraulic turbine from a pair of parallel output positive displacement pumps 68, 70 driven by the engine.
