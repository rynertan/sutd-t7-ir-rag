# SECTIONALISED BOAT

## Abstract
A boat 10 having a plurality of separable water tight transverse sections 14, 16, 17, 18 combined end to end to form a hull assembly 12 . A pair of tendons 34 extends longitudinally along the hull assembly and each tendon is attached to the bow and stern sections. The tendons follow geodesic lines along opposite sides of the longitudinal centre line B of the hull below the waterline and below the neutral axis A .
