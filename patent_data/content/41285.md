# Safety device for a burner of a coal gasification plant.

## Abstract
A safety device for protecting a burner 83 of a coal gasification plant against solid blockage. Solid blockage may occur in a burner 83 of a coal gasification plant fed with a mixture of coal particles and fluid. In order to protect the burner 83 against solid blockage a safety device 81 is pro posed which is arranged in the supply line 80,82 to the burner 83 . By means of this safety device 81 relatively large pieces of solid material, which might cause blockage of the burner 83 can be eliminated from the mixture of coal parti cles and fluid supplied to the burner 83 .
