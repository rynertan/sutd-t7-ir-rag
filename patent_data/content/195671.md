# AN IMPROVED FOOTREST

## Abstract
A footrest providing for selective height and inclination adjustment and comprising foot platform 5 formed integrally with depending legs 6 and 7 having chamfered outer ends 25 and detachable foot plates 13 to support the platform from a floor with an inclination from a rear edge 11 to a forward edge 12 , elternative foot plates 13A having a chamfered face may be provided to modify the inclination of the platform and are reversible so as to increase or decrease the inclination as desired, spacing pieces 21 attachable between the foot plates and the legs for selection of height of the platform, and means 18 and 19 for storing beneath the platform the foot plates and spacing pieces not selected for use.
