# Incandescent lamp having two lead in conductors sealed within one end thereof.

## Abstract
A tungsten halogen lamp including a tubular quartz enve lope wherein both of the lead in conductors designed for pro viding electrical energy to the lamp s coiled filament are strate gically positioned within a single, press sealed end portion of the lamp s envelope. Electrical connections to the lamp may thus be provided and only one end thereof to thereby facilitate positioning and connection of the lamp within a photocopier or similar machine.
