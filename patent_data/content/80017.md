# Linear positioning apparatus for a read write head.

## Abstract
A linear positioning device having a carriage 11 slid ably mounted on a guide rail 14 incorporates a rolling bias spring 17 located in a movable pocket formed by the car riage and the guide rod. The spring 17 is formed with a flexible band material in a loop which is affixed to the carriage and is arranged to roll along the guide rod 14 while exerting a force separating the carriage 11 and the guide rod.
