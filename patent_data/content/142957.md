# EYEWEAR FOR DUSTY ENVIRONMENTS

## Abstract
Eyewear, such as safety glasses 22 , includes means 20, 50 between the brow guard 30 and the brow of the wearer, the means being adapted to trap dust particles. For ferromagnetic particles the means 20 is a magnetic mate rial, and for non magnetic dust the means 50, Figure 3 has a tacky adhesive layer 54 . Preferably a stack of layers 52 is provided, the layers being discarded when they are dust laden.
