# Method and apparatus for closing the toe end of a hose.

## Abstract
A pair of cam driven finger pieces 31,32 of a holder unit A for a hose such as a seamless hose allow travel of the toe section 3 of the hose across the sewing region on a sewing machine 89 along a prescribed arc locus 5A and timely controlled ejection of air jet 101 102 in the sewing region enables successful and stable sewing action on the toe sec tion even with use of a fixed sewing machine. High production rate is resulted despite simple and compact construction.
