# IMPROVED MECHANICAL HANDLING APPARATUS

## Abstract
Apparatus for transferring a container from a vehicle to a support or another vehicle or vice versa comprising telescopic laterally extendable members 20, 21 having pivotable contai ner bearing beams 18 mounted thereon to support the con tainer during transfer, and fluid powered rams 32 used with ground reaction members for tilting the container parallel to a longitudinal axis of the vehicle whilst the members 20, 21 and beams 18 are being engaged. The apparatus is vehicle mount ed for self loading and unloading.
