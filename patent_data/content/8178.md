# Powder, process for its preparation, container holding it, and syrup comprising the powder reconstituted with water.

## Abstract
A powder which may be reconstituted into an orally administrable syrup by the addition of water, which powder contains a water soluble salt of a penicillin of the formula I CHEM wherein R is hydrogen, chlorine or fluorine and R1 is chlorine or both R and R1 are hydrogen in mixture with a solid acidic material the molar ratio of the penicillin to the acidic material is from 10 1 to 1 1. The powder gives a syrup of improved taste and useful stability.
