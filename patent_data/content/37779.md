# Spray nozzle.

## Abstract
A spray nozzle, which is attached to a conduit or header carrying a fluid under pressure, the purpose of which is to generate a circular umbrella type liquid spray. Spray nozzle includes a cylindrical member 4 and a baffle 20 therein. The spray nozzle receives liquid from the header and conducts it to its concavely curved dispersing member 9 from where it is discharged in a circular spray pattern to a bank of tubes or cooling tower fill below.
