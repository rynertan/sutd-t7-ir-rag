# IMPROVED SEALS COMPRISING A FILM OF SUBSTITUTED ORGANOSILANE FOR ALKALINE ELECTROCHEMICAL CELLS

## Abstract
The present invention deals with improved seals com prising a film of substituted organosilane for alkaline elec trochemical cells. The improved seal for alkaline cells according to the invention comprises a gasket having a coated layer of a polyamide, an epoxy resin, asphalt or a cured epoxy polyamide resin and a film of a substituted organosilane disposed and compressed between the interface of the cell s cover and the coated gasket of the cell. The film in question provides a more effective seal at this interface.
