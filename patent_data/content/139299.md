# Auxiliary power output assembly of an internal combustion engine.

## Abstract
An auxiliary power output device for an internal com bustion engine comprises a ring gear 4 fixed to a crank shaft 2 of an internal combustion engine 1 and interlocked with a pinion 10 of a starter motor 9 , a pulley 13 attached to an auxiliary output shaft 11 and a belt 14 wound between the pulley 13 and the ring gear 4 .
