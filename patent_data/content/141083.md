# Dispenser for flowable material.

## Abstract
A dispenser 10 for flowable material having a squeez able container 14 formed with filling and discharge openings 16,18 at opposite ends, a combination handle and closure member 24 connectable to the filling opening 18 , and a flexible discharge spout 30 and second closure member 34 being connectable selectively to the discharge opening of said container, the connections all having snap acting co engaging members and the discharge spout being elongated and flexible and of uniform very small diameter to control discharge of precise amounts of material directed to desired locations of use by an operator holding the dispenser pen like in one hand.
