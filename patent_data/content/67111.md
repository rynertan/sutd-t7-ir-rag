# TIRE WITH DUAL TREAD COMPOUND

## Abstract
A tire 10 which has an outer 34 and an inner layer 36 of compound in the non skid portion 30 of the tread 24 thereof. The outer layer 34 is composed of a lower rolling resistance com pound than the inner layer 36. The inner layer 36 is composed of a compound which provides better traction than the compound of the outer layer 34.
