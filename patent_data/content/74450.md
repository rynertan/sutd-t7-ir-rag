# Conductive moulding compositions containing copolymers of vinyl chloride and long chain alpha olefins.

## Abstract
Conductive moulding compositions comprise a vinyl chloride α olefin copolymer which contains from 0.1 to 5 by weight of an α olefin having 10 to 22 carbon atoms, conductive carbon black particles, a stabilizer component, a lubricant component, and a plasticizer and processing aid component.
