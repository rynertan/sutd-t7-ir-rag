# Body fluid filtering device.

## Abstract
A body fluid filtering device includes a housing 11 provided with a body fluid inlet 11a , a filtrate outlet 11b and a filtration residue outlet 11c , and a filtering means disposed in the housing 11 and including first and second filtering elements 12₁, 12₂ alternately super posed on each other with a space 21 therebetween. The first and second filtering elements 12₁, 12₂ are each formed of a meshed core material and filtering membranes covering the both sides of the core. The core material of the first filtering element 12₁ has openings of 700 to 1300 microns, and the core material of the second filtering element 12₂ has openings of 300 microns or less.
