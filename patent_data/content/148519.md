# Continuous roll baling machine.

## Abstract
A machine 10 is provided for continuously forming roll bales of crop material while moving across a field without stopping to discharge the bales. The machine includes a lower apron 20 and a pair of upper aprons 26, 52 . One of the upper aprons 26 cooperates with the lower apron 20 to define a front bale forming chamber 50 while that the two upper aprons 26, 52 cooperate with each other to de fine rear bale forming chamber 64 .
