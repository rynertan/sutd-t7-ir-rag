# Control of a vehicle automatic transmission.

## Abstract
A vehicle automatic transmission 1 has a torque converter 10 and a multiple stage gear mechanism 20 . When the vehicle is stopped and the shift lever 53a is in the D range, the gear mechanism is shifted to the higest gear stage if the vehicle brake B is engaged and the engine 2 is idling, so that transmittal of the engine idling vibrations to the vehicle body can be prevented by the transmission.
