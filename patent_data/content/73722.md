# Master cylinder.

## Abstract
A master cylinder with a housing 20 forming a longitudinal bore 24 for receiving a pair of pistons 36, 38 and also forming an outer wall 26 defining a fluid reservoir 28 , the housing further including a pair of compensation ports 100, 132 and at least one pair of outlet passages 66, 76 for connection with respective brake circuits, at least one of said outlet passages including a proportioning valve 64 received in a boss 60 protruding outwardly from the housing According to this invention, the boss 60 has its axis directed tangentially with respect to the master cylinder housing 20 and cooperates with the outer wall 26 to divide the fluid reservoir 28 into a pair of separate cavities 84, 86 For use in automotive braking systems.
