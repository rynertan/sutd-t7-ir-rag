# Metal containing polymeric dispersing agent.

## Abstract
A dispersing agent of the formula Optionally in combination with an aliphatic diamine NHR₂ T NH₂, wherein R₂ is hydrogen or C₁ ₂₀ alkyl, and a salt of said diamine and a residual carboxylic acid deriving from the polyester mixture. The dispersing agents are useful for dispersing pigments and dyes in organic liquids.
