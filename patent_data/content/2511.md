# Electrode for electrolytic processes, electrolysis cell and method for electrolysis.

## Abstract
Novel dimensionally stable, porous electrodes made of vitreous or glassy carbon forming a porous body optionally provided on at least a portion of its outer surface with a coating containing an electrocatalytic agent, electrochemical cells containing the said electrodes and a method of electrolysis, preferably wherein a halogen is evolved at the anode.
