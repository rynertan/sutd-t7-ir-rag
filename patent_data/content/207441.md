# A WALL STICK ON POCKET SIZE GADGET CONTAINING AN EXTRACTABLE ARTICLE

## Abstract
A pocket size container capable of storing one or more articles 20 for everyday use, such as address books, index books, block notes, make up sets, or similar and comprising a permaneng magnet 35 and a first cavity 17 apt to receive said article 20 which is provided with a gripping portion 22 protruding from said container. A second cavity is apt to receive a writing means 31 .
