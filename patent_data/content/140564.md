# ELECTROPLATED PRODUCT AND METHOD

## Abstract
This invention is directed to an electroplated product having improved resistance to corrosive attack, and to the method of making such product. More particularly, the in vention hereof relates to a zinc or zinc alloy electroplated product containing a homogeneous dispersion of corrosion inhibitive pigment produced by subjecting a ferrous sub strate to an electroplating bath containing a corrosion in hibitive pigment, such as barium chromate.
