# Television system for high definition television and a television transmitter and receiver suitable therefor.

## Abstract
A compatible television system for high definition and for standardized low definition television. In a transmitter 1 in the system a high definition video signal VS1 is gener ated which is split into a contour signal HVC2 which on display shows details in the horizontal and vertical direc tions, and a low definition video signal VS6 . The low definition video signal VS6 is converted into a low definition video signal
