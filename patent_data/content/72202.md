# Use of furanones for inhibiting growth of food poisoning organisms.

## Abstract
Furanones having the formula CHEM where R represents hydrogen or a substituted or unsubstituted alkyl groups of up to 10 carbon atoms B represents a double bond and w represents 0 or 1 and R1 represents ethenyl when w represents 0 and ethyl when w represents 1, are useful for inhibiting the growth of micro organisms.
