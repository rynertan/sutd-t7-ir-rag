# A frame for releasably framing articles.

## Abstract
The present invention is directed to a frame 10 for releasably framing articles, such as pictures, photographs, posters and menus. The frame comprises a plurality of elongate members 12, 14, 16, 18 having mitred ends mated together, and a tensible loop 20 wherein the loop is located in a continuous recess 28 formed, on assembly of the frame, by the alignment of sub recesses 27 located along the length of each member, and wherein the loop is in tension around the frame so as to pull the members together.
