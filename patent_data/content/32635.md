# Fishing reel.

## Abstract
A spin casting style fishing reel that has an anti reverse mechanism that prevents the crank assembly 310 from being rotated in the reverse direction when desired. The anti reverse mechanism is silent in operation and can be selectively placed into or taken out of operation. In minia ture sized reels, the switch 150 to engage or disengage the antireverse mechanism can be handably mounted on the side of the fishing reel.
