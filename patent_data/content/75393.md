# Electromagnetic relay and method of making it.

## Abstract
A miniature DIL relay comprising a motor unit and a cas ing 1 housing the motor unit 2 and including opposed side walls 14, 14a of insulating plastics material, each sidewall hav ing embedded therein a conductor frame providing external connection terminals 15, 16, 17 of the relay and supporting the fixed and moving relay contacts 8, 11, 6, 7 in the casing, the motor unit having a bobbin with a pair of external connec tion terminals 18, 21 mounted thereon which project through the casing and which are connected to the motor unit winding.
