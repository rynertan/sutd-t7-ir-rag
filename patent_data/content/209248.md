# Firearm.

## Abstract
A firearm has a number of barrels which are spaced around a central axis. A hammer 49 pivots about an axis 48 coaxial with that central axis and has a firing pin 83 which is spaced from that axis 48 . Pivot means 50, 52, 53, 56, 58 pivots the hammer 49 about its axis 48 between shots to advance the firing pin 83 to the next barrel.
