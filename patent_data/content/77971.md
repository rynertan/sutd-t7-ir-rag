# Immobilization of the sucrose mutase in whole cells of Protaminobacter Rubrum.

## Abstract
The present invention is a method for the immobilization of the sucrose mutase in whole cells of Protaminobacter rubrum having sucrose mutase activity which involves contacting the cells with tannic acid, polyethylenimine, and an adduct of glutaraldhyde and an epihalohydrin polyamine copolymer.
