# Portable grinding machine.

## Abstract
A grinding machine includes a casing 31 having intake apertures 32d for introducing the air and discharge aper tures 34c for discharging the air. A motor 14 is mounted within the casing 31 and has a rotatable shaft 14c . A fan member 40 is fixedly mounted on the shaft 14c for rotation therewith and has a mounting portion 40b and a plurality of blades 40c provided on the mounting portion 40b for flowing the air through the casing 31 from the intake apertures 32d to the discharge apertures 34c for cooling the motor 14 . A grinding wheel 22 is secured to the mounting portion 40b .
