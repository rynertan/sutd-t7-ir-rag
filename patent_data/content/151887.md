# INSTRUMENT HOUSING

## Abstract
An instrument housing of the knockdown type includes a base member, a top member and a plurality of side members each of which is molded from a plastic material in the form of a generally flat panel. The members are detachably secured together to form the housing whereby the size and dimen sions of the housing may be varied by selective replacement of some of the members and whereby the members may be shipped and stored in a flat condition in kit form.
