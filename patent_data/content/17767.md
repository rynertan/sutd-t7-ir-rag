# Trifluoromethyl pyridinyl oxy thio phenols.

## Abstract
Compounds of the formula The compounds are useful as intermediates for the preparation of herbicides, and are prepared by condensing a salt of 4 methoxy or of 4 mercaptophenol with a trifluor methyl 2 chloropyridine. If a methoxy group is present, a dealkylation reaction is then carried out.
