# Water reducible coating composition based on an epoxy resin.

## Abstract
The invention provides a water reducible coating composi tion comprising an epoxy resin, an amino group containing curing agent for the epoxy resin and a watersoluble organic compound which occurs in at least 2 tautomeric forms, in one of which a labile hydrogen atom is linked to a carbon atom and which compound contains at least one carbonyloxy group or sulphonyl group.
