# A banknote access station for an automatic banknote dispenser.

## Abstract
In automatic banknote dispensing apparatus, a banknote access module comprises an enclosure 44 which can be fixed in the outer wall 42 of a bank and within which is a platform 15 mounted to pivot about a fixed axis 9 between a first position as shown , in which it receives banknotes from an inlet aperture 52 in the enclosure and is accessible to a customer when a door 13 of the enclosure is unlocked fol lowing a correct delivery of notes to the platform, and a sec ond position in which it ejects notes from the enclosure through a reject outlet aperture 45 , for example following an incorrect delivery of notes. A shroud 43 formed integrally with the door blocks the banknote delivery inlet aperture when the door is open.
