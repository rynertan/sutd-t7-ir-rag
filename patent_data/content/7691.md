# Compositions for use in decreasing appetite for calories as carbohydrates.

## Abstract
Compositions and substances are provided for use decreasing appetite for calories as carbohydrates. The com positions comprise tryptophan effective to increase brain serotonin levels and an insulin releasing carbohydrate e.g., sucrose the compositions may also comprise a mild stimul ant such as caffeine or tyrosine in an amount effective to maintain or increase brain dopamine and or norepinephrin levels. Instead of being administered as a composition the active substances may be administered separately.
