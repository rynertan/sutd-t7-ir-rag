# PRODUCTS DISPLAYING THE ANTIGENICITY OF HEPATITIS B VIRUS E ANTIGENS AND METHODS OF PRODUCING THOSE ANTIGENS

## Abstract
Polypeptides displaying the antigenicity of hepatitis B vi rus e antigens, DNA sequences coding for those polypeptides, antibodies to those polypeptides and methods of producing and using those polypeptides antibodies and DNA se quences. The polypeptides and antibodies of this invention are characterized by their use in compositions and methods for detecting hepatitis B virus infective carriers and in evaluating the course of HBV related chronic active liver disease.
