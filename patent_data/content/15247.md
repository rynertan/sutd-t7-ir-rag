# An aerosol inhalation device.

## Abstract
An aerosol inhalation device comprising an elongated chamber, an outer chamber part 1 of which has an outlet opening 4 and is telescopically displaceable over an inner chamber part 5 which is connectable to an aerosol dispenser 7 and provided with a flexible tongue 10 which on telescop ing the device together closes the outlet opening 4 .
