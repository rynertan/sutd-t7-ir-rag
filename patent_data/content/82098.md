# Power tong unit, especially for gripping a pipe.

## Abstract
A power tong unit 11 is shown which is operable as a closed head or open head unit. The jaws 147 of the tong unit are rotatable to an extended pipe gripping position from either of opposite directions to make up or break out pipe from a single retracted position. An improved reversal means 195, 197, 199, 203, 205, 207 controls the direction of rotation of the jaws 147 between the make up and break out positions.
