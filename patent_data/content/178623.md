# PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITION FOR THE TOPICAL TREATMENT OF SKIN INFLAMMATORY DISORDERS

## Abstract
Pharmaceutical composition for topically treating skin inflammatory disorders by administering to the inflamed area a therapeutically effective amount of candelilla wax or a saturated hydrocarbon fraction from candelilla wax compris ing a mixture of saturated straight chain C₂₉ C₃₃ hydrocar bons, predominantly C₃₁ hydrocarbon.
