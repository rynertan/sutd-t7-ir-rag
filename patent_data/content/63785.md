# Variable pulley transmission.

## Abstract
A transmission system includes a control system to control a fluid actuated clutch 16 and movable sheaves 68, 152 . The clutch includes a deeply grooved pattern 345 to provide adequate cooling with low pressure oil before its return to sump and comprises a central disc with opposed surfaces 336 , a resilient material layer affixed to each surface 341, 343 and a friction material layer 338, 340 affixed to each resilient layer.
