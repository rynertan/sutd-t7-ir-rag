# Process for the regeneration of a sorbent.

## Abstract
A process is disclosed for the regeneration of a sorbent, particularly an ion exchanger used in the recovery, isolation or production of sugars, wherein a regeneration solution is used which contains at least 20 meq l of an alkali metal hyd roxide or hydroxide of an alkaline earth metal having an atomic number of at least 20, and at least 1 by weight of a monosaccharide or disaccharide.
