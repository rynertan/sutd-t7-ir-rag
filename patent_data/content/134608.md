# PACKAGE FOR LIQUIDS WITH A SPHERICAL CONTAINER BODY

## Abstract
The invention refers to a package for liquids which package has a spherical container body 2 with a tubular opening 3 projecting from the container body 2 and an annual supporting foot 4 located opposite the opening 3 . The length of said tubular opening 3 shall be at least 0,21 times the diameter of the spherical body 2 .
