# Beverage quality security apparatus for post mix beverage dispenser.

## Abstract
A beverage dispenser including a concentrate supply compartment 15 for supporting concentrate containers 12 in dispensing positions, an access door 11 to the compart ment and protrusions 18 on the inside of the access door which fit into recesses in the containers when the door is closed. The door also must actuate a limit switch 24 when the door is closed before the dispenser can be electrically energized to dispense a selected beverage.
