# Disposable applicator.

## Abstract
The invention concerns a disposable single dose syringe type applicator in the form of an elongated body. Thos body includes two integrally joined sections which can be sepa rated by breaking. One section constitutes a medicament re servoir having two openings. The other section is a plunger which is arranged so as to seal one opening of the reservoir which is prefilled with a medicament. Furthermore, the pun ger slidably fits to the reservoir in such a way that essentially the whole medicament content of the reservoir is ejected therefrom when the plunger is inserted into the reservoir. At the other opening end of the medicament reservoir there is arranged an easily removable sealing means.
