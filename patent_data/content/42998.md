# Positive shutoff for individual units of a central air conditioning system.

## Abstract
In a central air conditioning system wherein air flow is regulated by inflation of bellows 14,15 which are controlled by a bleed type thermostat 50 , simultaneous heating and cooling in a zone is prevented by disabling the bleed type thermostat 50 when the heating system 66 is activated by closing a solenoid valve 60 in the line 39 to the thermostat 50 whenever the heating system 66 is activated.
