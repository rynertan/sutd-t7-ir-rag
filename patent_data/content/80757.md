# Improved homogenizer device, in particular for the production of whipped cream.

## Abstract
A homogenizer device, in particular for the production of whipped cream, comprising in combination, the following components an external sleeve 10 and an internal sleeve 11 between which is formed a convoluted labyrinth, 9 the internal sleeve housing a valve element 12 which forms with it a chamber 15 communicating with said labyrinth and with the delivery tube 22 for the base mixture, said valve element consisting of a stem bearing a valve 16 at one of its ends and a valve 17 at its opposite end, the former being a valve for controlling the dispensing of the whipped cream and the latter being a valve for controlling the conduction of the mixture through said labyrinth, said valve element being also translatable against a counter acting spring 20 .
