# DOUBLE WALLED SCREEN FILTER WITH PERFORATED JOINTS

## Abstract
A well screen filter includes a pair of substantially spaced concentric screens defining an annular filtrating space therebetween, connected with perforated joint s closing the lower end of the filtrating space, annular space filled up with filtrating materials gravel or syn thetic balls as a pack and upper joints as cover caps of the annular filtrating space seal of the pack . A lower perforated joint includes holes for passage of the fine particles to the sedimentation tube, preventing clogging of the pack and enables proper functioning of the double walled screen filter.
