# Rake adjusting device, particularly for rear entrance ski boots.

## Abstract
A device for adjusting the rake particularly in ski boots of the rear entrance type comprises, located on the boot shell 3 , a plurality of spaced seats 13 . Releasably engageable in one of the seats 13 is a dog 12 , provided on one lever 11, 20 journalled on the boot front quarter 10 to set the front quarter rake relatively to the boot shell 3 .
