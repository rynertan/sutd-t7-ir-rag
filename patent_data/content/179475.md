# Support for automobile instrument cluster tiltable with a steering column.

## Abstract
A support for an automobile gauge cluster 2 on a steer ing column for permitting a steering wheel to tilt up and down. The gauge cluster 2 is provided for tilting motion in response to the tilting of the steering wheel and there is employed a bracket structure 5 for the support of the steer ing column having a steering shaft 1 rotatably extending therein. The bracket structure 5 is rigidly connectable with a portion 10,12 of an automobile body structure and has a guide 6 provided therein for guiding the tilting motion of the gauge cluster.
