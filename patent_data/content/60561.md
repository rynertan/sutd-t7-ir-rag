# STRUCTURE FOR REINFORCING A PLATE LIKE MEMBER

## Abstract
A structure for reinforcing a plate like member 20 , includes a expandable portion 22 and a reinforcing cover 23 laminated onto a surface of the expandable portion 22 . The expandable portion 22 and the rein forcing cover 23 combine to form a rib like reinforcing part 24 of predetermined total height or thickness. The rib like reinforcing part 24 is integrally bonded to the plate like member 20 to reinforce the plate like member 20 . The side portions 25, 26 of the expand able portion 22 are not covered by the reinforcing cover 23 so that the side portions thereof are exposed.
