# Exhaust control system for two cycle engine.

## Abstract
An exhaust control system for a two cycle engine wherein the cross sectional area of an exhaust is controlled in accordance with the rpm. of the engine by opening and closing at least one auxiliary exhaust passage 16, 17 with respect to a main exhaust passage 15 . The main exhaust passage 15 and the auxiliary exhaust passage 16, 17 connected to the cylinder 10 are located parallel to each other circumferentially of the cylinder 10 , and the rotary valve 18,19 in the form of a shaft disposed parallel to the center line of the cylinder 10 is operative to open and close the auxiliary exhaust passage 16, 17 with respect to the main exhaust passage 15 in accordance with an increase and a decrease in the engine speed.
