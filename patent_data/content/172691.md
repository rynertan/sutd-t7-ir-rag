# Weather resistant polyacetal resin composition.

## Abstract
A polyacetal resin composition comprises a polyacetal resin, A a light stabiliser selected from the group consisting of a benzotriazole compound, a benzophenone compound, an aromatic benzoate compound, a cyanoacrylate com pound, and an anilide oxalate compound and B an aliphatic acid ester, to give improved stability and resistance to weathering.
