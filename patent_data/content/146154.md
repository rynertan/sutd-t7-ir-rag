# Device for feeding electronic components packed in tape to a pick up position.

## Abstract
A device for feeding electronic components packed in tape to a pick up portion, comprising a housing with a hold er for a supply reel, a feeding and locking mechanism, a lift ing mechanism and a wind up mechanism, which mecha nisms in combination with the housing form a cassette unit. By exchange of a number of exchangeable parts, the cas sette unit can be adapted in a comparatively simple manner for processing tapes and components having different di mensions.
