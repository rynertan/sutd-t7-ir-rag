# Saw chain.

## Abstract
Saw chain including trailing extensions 72a, 72b which are mounted to swing through a selected angle upwardly and forwardly out of mating sliding engagement with bearing sur face formations 66a, 66b in accordance with a selected sub stantially uniform change in direction of the saw chain dictat ed by a change from a straight line path of travel to a curvate path of travel whereby the trailing extension projecting por tions A1, A2 are presented ahead of said following associat ed cutter portions at a selected radial extent sufficient to ef fectively limit penetration of the cutter portions 62a, 62b throughout the curvate path of travel and thereby function as a depth gauge.
