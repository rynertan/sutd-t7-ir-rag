# TREATMENT OF A ZEOLITE CONTAINING CATALYST CONTAMINATED WITH VANADIUM

## Abstract
The present invention provides a method for the treat ment of a zeolite containing catalyst contaminated with vanadium characterized in that the vanadium contaminated catalyst is treated with a material comprising tin or a source thereof, the amount of tin utilized being from 1 to 1999 ppm of catalyst. Such treatment can be performed during a process for the catalytic cracking of hydrocarbons.
