# Illuminated switch.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to an illuminated switch and in particular to one having a housing 20, 30 , switch contacts 24, 25, 26, 27 in the housing, an electrical device 50 disposed in the housing and operatively associated with the switch contacts, a light transmissive colored filter 32 mounted over a neon lamp 511 of the electrical device, and a transparent member 35 located over a CDS 512 of the electrical deivce, whereby the neon lamp will give light to provide indication of the switch location in darkness and will be extinguished when the switch is turned on.
