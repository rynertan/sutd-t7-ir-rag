# Thermoplastic leather material and its preparation.

## Abstract
A thermoplastic composition of matter is provided, substantially consisting of leather as herein defined and optionally including additives and or fillers, and obtained by objecting the leather in a closed die to a pressure of about 200 to about 900 bar at a temperature of about 50 DEG to about 250 DEG C. The invention also relates to the process for producing this thermoplastic material.
