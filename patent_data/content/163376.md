# A ball retaining structure in a power transmission apparatus.

## Abstract
A ball retaining structure is suitable in a transmission apparatus for transmitting a power by means of magnetic balls 14 between an input rotating body 10 and an output rotating body 16 . The input rotating body and or output rotating body is are provided with at least one magnet 22 so as to enable rotatable retention of said balls. The input rotating body and or the output rotating body can be of non magnetic material.
