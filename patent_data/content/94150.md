# Stud fastener receptacle.

## Abstract
A receptacle 20 for a stud 30 is mounted in a rect angular aperture 38 in a member 34 . The receptacle has a housing 22 with a base 40 and flat legs 42, 44 and is held on the member 34 by being gripped between flanges 46, 48 on the legs and a non circular element 28 . Each of the legs has a side flange 54 on each side and diametri cally opposite one of the said flanges have a slot 55 . The pair of opposed flanges on each side of the receptacle de fine an elongate slot between them in which the non cir cular element is free to move after rotation from a position in which it is held within the transverse slot 55 .
