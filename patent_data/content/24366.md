# Apparatus for the gasification of fuel.

## Abstract
An apparatus for the preparation of a combustible gas mixture from solid or liquid fuels, consists of a housing 1 , which comprises at least one rotatably disposed, conical basket 2 a fuel inlet 5 , an inlet 9 for a gasification fluid, an ash outlet 6 and an outlet 8 for the produced combustible gas mixture. The fuel to be gasified is introduced into the inlet 5 and the apparatus is so operated that a number of revolu tions of the basket s is maintained such, that the fuel is pressed against the said basket which also functions as a supporting grate.
