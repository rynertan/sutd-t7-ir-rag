# Article coding and separating system.

## Abstract
An article coding and separating system such as for in stance, in which articles or defective eggs which are randomly dispersed in a plurality of similar articles are coded and their location identified. An elongated pivotally mounted pointer means 13 is disposed so as to be pivotable over an inspection area in mutually orthogonal directions, parallel and transverse to the longitudinal axis of a conveyor means, and axially mov able towards and away from the articles being conveyed. The pointer includes means for generating signals for simulta neously indicating the position of articles on the conveyor within the inspection area 32, 33, 37, 38, 40, 41, 42 , and the type of article being identified 43, 44, 45, 46, 47, 48 .
