# Sealant composition comprising a fibrillated fiber and a particulate material for automobile cooling system.

## Abstract
The present invention deals with a sealant composition comprising a fibrillated fiber and a particulate material for automobile cooling system. This composition for stopping leaks comprises the combination of a fibrillated fiber such as fiber of poly imino 1,4 phenylene imino terephtaloyl with a particulate material, such as a seed meal.
