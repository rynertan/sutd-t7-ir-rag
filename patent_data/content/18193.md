# A cable or hose guide.

## Abstract
A cable or hose guide has a series of alternately stag gered intrusions 20 extending into the opening of an ex tended channel 10b so that a cable or hose 15 must be deflected alternately around the intrusions 20 as it is la terally fed into or out of the channel 10b , thereby pre venting the hose 15 from buckling and falling out of the channel 10b while therein.
