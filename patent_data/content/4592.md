# Bisimide ether compounds, compositions containing these compounds, and a method for their production.

## Abstract
A bisimide ether compound having the general formula is obtained by reacting an ethylenically unsaturated dicarboxylic acid anhydride, a diamine compound and a phenolic compound having in the molecule at least two hydroxyl groups, or by reacting a prepolymer of the anhydride and diamine compound with the phenolic compound
