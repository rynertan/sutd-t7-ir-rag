# Digital photodetector.

## Abstract
The digital photodetector, formed as a monolithic semi conductor integrated circuit, comprises a semiconductor photosensor 1 , two depletion mode FET s 3, 9 and an enhancement mode FET 6 . The gates of FET s 3 and 5 are connected to node 4 , and the output node 8 is connected to the gate of FET 9 .
