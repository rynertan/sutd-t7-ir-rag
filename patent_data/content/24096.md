# Complexes of bivalent copper, methods of preparation thereof and compositions containing said complexes.

## Abstract
Neutral copper complexes, particularly copper salicylate complexes having the formula Cu C₆H₄ OH COO 2γ in which γ is dimethyl sulphoxide or dimethylformamide, have been found to have therapeutic activity in the treatment of inflam matory diseases and copper deficiency conditions. The com plexes have been found particularly suitable for the treatment of Menkes syndrome.
