# Sprinkler head housing.

## Abstract
A concealing housing for a ceiling mounted fire sprinkler head 6 in which a bottom cap 17 is automatically released to uncover the sprinkler head in the advent of fire, the housing comprising a body 14 supported by the sprinkler head, and a plurality of connecting studs 18 to hold the cap to the body, each stud being of sheet metal and being releasably connected at a foot portion 21 by low temperature solder to the cap and threaded by a head portion 33 through a hole 20 in the body until an intermediate shoulder 29 on the stud engages the body said stud being bent over at its head portion for retention upon the body.
