# Specimen dish.

## Abstract
A multiple well specimen dish is disclosed primarily for use in cell culture studies. The dish includes a tray 20 and a plural ity of individual cells 22 in the form of vessels which can be removably located in the tray. A lid 44 is also provided for the tray and is physically identical therewith. Each vessel has a cover 24 which is received in an opening in the lid when the tray and lid are assembled, so that pressure sensitive tape 48 can then be used to releasably secure the covers to the lid while allowing one or more of the covers to be released when appro priate by peeling back the tape.
