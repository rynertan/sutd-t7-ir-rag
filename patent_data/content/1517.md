# Flexible band wrench.

## Abstract
Wrench in which a flexible band 11 cleated on one side is wrapped around a workpiece 40 and is torqued by means of a camming surface 25 on a handle member 10 attached to the band. In accordance with the invention the handle includes teeth 24 which can engage in the cleats 36, 37 when the band is wrapped round the workpiece cleated side outermost. When the band is used cleated side innermost the camming surface 25 acts on the smooth back surface 35 of the band. The band can be of a link construction or a belt construction and a pin 17 securing the band in place can be made removable to permit easy exchange of bands. The wrench is useful for use in restricted access areas and for a wide range of different types, shapes and sizes of workpiece.
