# A method for producing a compostable mixture of sludge from sewage purification plants, and apparatus for using the mixture.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a method for producing a product from which compost can be suitably produced and which contains sludge obtained from a sewage purification process, in which method the sludge is mixed in a fluid state with a finely divided carboncarrier selected from the group sawdust, peat and bark, and with polyelectrolyte, and is then mechanically dewatered to a dry substance content of at least about 28 by weight.
