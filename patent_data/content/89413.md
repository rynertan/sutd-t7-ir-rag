# Concentrated emetic herbicidal composition and method for the preparation thereof.

## Abstract
A concentrated emetic herbicidal composition compris ing an aqueous salt of a herbicidal bipyridinium quaternary cation, a nonionic surfactant, a peripherally acting emetic, and a malodorous synergist for said peripherally acting emetic. A method for rendering herbicidal compositions emetic to warm blooded animals is also described.
