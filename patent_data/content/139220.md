# Structure incorporating stabilising means.

## Abstract
Stabilising means is disclosed for a structure comprising a plurality of rectangular panels 8, 8a each comprising two transverse members 12 and two uprights 10 extensions 14 of which, in the form of spigots, project beyond the transverse members at upper and lower ends of the frame and engage in sockets provided by connecting members 34 . The stabilising means comprises a vertical rail 22 carrying a plurality of connector elements 34 providing sockets 40 receiving spigots 14 of adjacent panels.
