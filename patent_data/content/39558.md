# SWEEPER

## Abstract
The invention relates to sweepers with particular referen ce to a ground sweeper having a first sweeper brush of relati vely large diameter, a second sweeper brush of relatively small diameter, drive means adapted to drive said first and said sec ond sweeper brush in an opposite sense whereby the said sec ond sweeper brush serves to contact the surface to be swept and wherein the first sweeper brush serves to compliment the second by directing larger objects onto the second brush for collection and transport to said receptacle. The invention parti cularly describes a manually propelled sweeper in which the first sweeper brush is constructed and arranged whereby beer cans, paper cups and the like are swept into the or onto the second sweeper brush for entrainment and subsequent trans port to a collection receptacle.
