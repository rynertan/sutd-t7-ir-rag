# Carboxy modified vinyl ester urethane resins, their application and use.

## Abstract
Carboxy terminated vinylester urethane polymers are useful as a thickening agent for sheet molding compositions when reacted with metal oxide which comprises a com pound having the general formula B I A M A M The polymer is employed as a thickening agent in sys tems containing vinylmonomer and other ethylenically un saturated resins.
