# Sustained release granular preparations.

## Abstract
A favorable sustained releasing property is given to granular preparations by incorporating therein at least one of C₁₆ C₁₈ saturated fatty acid and or metal salts thereof at a rate of 10 50 of the total weight of the dosage form. The disclosed dosage form releases the active ingredient both in a low pH region of the stomach and in a high pH region of the intestine at an appropriate rate.
