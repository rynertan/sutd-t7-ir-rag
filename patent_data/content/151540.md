# Multi layer adhesive articles.

## Abstract
A multi layer article having a padded appearance is disclosed. The article includes a layer of polished, highly plasticized polyvinyl chloride material suitable to adhere to a smooth high gloss surface and a padded layer. The article is produced by a high frequency welding technique. Also disclosed are a combination of a substrate together with one or more of the multi layer articles, with an article, in the form of a playbrick, having a padded appearance and being capable of adhering to smooth, high gloss surfaces including the surface of other such articles.
