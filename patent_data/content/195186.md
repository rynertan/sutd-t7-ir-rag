# Stabilizing orthesis.

## Abstract
The stabilizing prosthesis device comprises a functional skeletal structure 1 having a rigid flat sole 2 extending parallel to the ground surface, and at least one upright half shell 3 adapted to at least partially envelop the patients limb and provided with means of anchoring the limb. Extending between the sole 2 and the half shell 3 there being a connection part 4 having a bottom portion 5 defining a sloping upper surface 2a whereby the patient s center of gravity can be shifted forward in alignment with the extension of the sole 2 .
