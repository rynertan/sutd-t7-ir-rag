# Protective relay.

## Abstract
In a protective relay employing a digital computer and making decisions on operating conditions of an electric power system for protecting equipment in the system, com putation circuits of the protective relay is adapted such that output of a first comparison and decision element for mak ing a decision in accordance with a first principle expres sion and output of second comparison and decision element for making a decision in accordance with a second principle expression are input to an inhibit element whereby a final output is delivered.
