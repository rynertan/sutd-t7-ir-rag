# HEXAHYDRO 3,5 DIOXO 1H PYRROLIZINE 2 CARBOXYLIC ACID AND DERIVATIVES AS COGNITION ACTIVATORS

## Abstract
Hexahydro 3,5 dioxo 1H pyrrolizine 2 carboxylic acid, its esters, ammonium, metal, and organic salts are disclosed as being useful for the treatment of senility, for enhancing memory, and for reversing electroconvulsive shock induced amnesia in humans. Pharmaceutical compositions including these compounds, and a process for their production are also disclosed.
