# Block and bleed valve system.

## Abstract
A double block and bleed valve system comprising a chamber 2 havng first and second fluid passage openings 6, 7 provided with first and second valve means 10, 11 , respectively, is connectable, in use of the system, to respec tive pipe sections 26, 27 from which said chamber 2 is isolatable in use by closure of said first and second valve means 10, 11 and a drain opening 9 provided with a drain valve 15 . A spray means having an inlet in communication with the exterior of the chamber for connection, in use, with a cleaning fluid supply 24 , and having at least one spray head 21 is formed and disposed in said chamber 2 for spraying, in use, substantially all the interior surfaces of said chamber 2 with cleaning fluid supplied thereto.
