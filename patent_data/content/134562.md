# Foot locking device particularly for front entrance ski boots.

## Abstract
A foot locking device for front entrance ski boots comprising a band element 1 associated at its ends with the two parts 2,3 of a locking lever 4 provided on the flaps of a quarter 10 . The band element 1 crosses over at the rear of the skier s foot and is passed under the foot itself. The parts 2,3 of the lever 4 are slidably connected to the respective quarter 10 flaps.
