# Process for producing a mixed halogen containing polycarbonate resin.

## Abstract
A process for producing a first halogen containing polycarbonate resin characterized by washing, with an aqueous washing liquid, an organic solvent solution of a mixture of a halogen containing polycarbonate resin having a halogen content of not less than 4 wt and a second polycarbonate resin having a halogen content of not larger than 4 wt and being 0.05 4 times in weight of the first polycarbonate H, and collecting a mixed polycarbonate resin having a halogen content of not less than 4 wt from said solution.
