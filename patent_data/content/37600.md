# Sheet receiving and storage apparatus.

## Abstract
A sheet receiving and storage apparatus comprises a housing 12b and an open bottomed magazine 13 releas ably connected therewith in which sheets 14 are collected. The sheets 14 are intercepted by a support 22 located below the entrance opening 21 of the magazine 13 . The support 22 is raised when a predetermined amount of sheets 14 are stacked thereon, so that said stack of sheets may be introduced into the magazine 13 . Subsequently, the sheets 14 are retained in the magazine 13 with the help of a sheet retaining element 24 , the support 22 is again lowered and a light trap 23 light tightly closes the magazine 13 . After these steps are carried out, the magazine 13 may be taken out of the apparatus.
