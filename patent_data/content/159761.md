# FASTENING DEVICE FOR FASCINE MATTRESSES

## Abstract
Fastening device for fascine mattresses, which is fastened to a floating carrier 1, 2 and which comprises two fixed parallel spaced apart walls 11, 12 between which is retained an inflatable and deflatable hose 18 of which the minimum dimen sion in freely inflated condition in transverse direction between the fixed walls 11, 12 is greater than the distance between the walls. An end edge part 6 of the mattress to be fastened is inserted between one wall 12 and the deflated hose 8 , and then the hose 18 is inflated thereby clamping the end edge part 6 between the hose 18 and the wall 12 .
