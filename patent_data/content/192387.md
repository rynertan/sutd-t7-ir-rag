# Castor wheel and method of manufacture.

## Abstract
A moulded plastics artcle 10 having a closed bore 15 to receive a spindle 11 , the 15 bore being formed with at least one radially inwardly projecting annular rib 16, 17 adapted to locate in a circumferential groove 21, 22 in the spindle 11 , characterised in that the or each rib 16, 17 is interrupted to form a gap 18, 19 for the passage of fluid from one side of the rib 16, 17 to the other when the spindle 11 is inserted into the bore 15 .
