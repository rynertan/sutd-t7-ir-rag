# 16 Sulfinylmethyl steroids, their preparation and use.

## Abstract
Novel 16 sulfinylmethyl steroids are prepared by reac tion of the corresponding 16 methylene 17β hydroxy steroid with a sulfenylating agent, and are converted to correspond ing 16 methylene 17α hydroxy progesterones by reaction with a thiophile. 17α Hydroxy 6,16 dimethylene pregn 4 ene 3,20 dione is simultaneously isomerised and acylated to give melengestrol acetate.
