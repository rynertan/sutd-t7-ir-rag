# A handling device for handling meat portions.

## Abstract
A device for handling, like massage or mix, meat por tions, provided with a wheeled 10 container 5 having a closed lid 4 , for receiving a meat portion and stirring means 7 operative in said container 5 and beared in said lid 4 wherein the inclination of the rotary axis of the stirring means is adjustable and the quantity of meat moved by the stirring means 7 per rotation, is determined, and therefore the rate of massage, said stirring means 7 being provided with at least one conduit 20 for a cooling fluid to retain the meat tempera ture at a desired level.
