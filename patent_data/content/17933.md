# Method and system for engine control.

## Abstract
A method and a system for engine control are disclosed in which an engine control signal is generated on the basis of a signal produced at each moment from at least one sensor 24 or 27 for detecting the operating condition of the engine while being driven, said control signal being used for engine control.
