# Adapter seal.

## Abstract
A seal 42 for use with a conventional conical nose 26 conduit fitting interposed between the conduit fitting parts wherein the seal 42 includes a conical metal portion 50 compressed between the coupling parts establishing metal to metal engagement, and the seal 42 also includes an inner end to which an elastomer 58 is bonded, the elastomer 58 being compressed between the fitting sur faces to additionally provide elastomeric sealing.
