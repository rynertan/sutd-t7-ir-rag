# IMPROVEMENTS IN OR RELATING TO TRACK LAYING VEHICLES

## Abstract
A tracklaying vehicle 1 has detachable wheels 14, 15, 32 which support the vehicle 1 with its tracks 2, 3 clear of the ground. The wheels 14, 15, 32 may sink into soft ground to allow the tracks 2, 3 to resume drive. Two wheels 14, 15 are mounted at one end of the vehicle coaxially with the track driving wheels 8, 11 so as to be driven. The other end of the vehicle 1 is supported by a castor wheel assembly 28 .
