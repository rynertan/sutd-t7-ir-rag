# Rotor and method of making same.

## Abstract
A rotor 25 of a fluid energy translating device, wherein the rotor 25 has an annular passage 47 entirely within the rotor 25 , a plurality of radially extending vane slots 35 intersecting the passage and axial openings 49 intersecting the annular passage 47 . The annular passage 47 is made by forming an annular groove 61 in a solid body 60 generally cylindrical shape and closing the open side of t groove 61 by segment means 62 .
