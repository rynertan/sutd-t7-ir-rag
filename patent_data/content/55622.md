# Process for producing acetic anhydride.

## Abstract
A process for producing acetic anhydride which com prises reacting methyl acetate or dimethyl ether with carbon monoxide in the presence of a catalyst comprising a nickel or a nickel compound, b at least one halide selected from the group consisting of bromides, iodides and mixtures thereof and c at least one organic nitrogen group com pound and together with a specific co catalyst is disclosed. According to this invention acetic anhydride is produces by using highly active non expensive catalyst and co catalyst.
