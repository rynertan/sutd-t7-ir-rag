# APPARATUS AND METHOD FOR FILLING A VALVE BAG WITH PARTICULATE MATERIAL

## Abstract
An apparatus and method for filling with particulate mat erial valve bags having an elongated valve lined with a water activated sealing means including water spray supply means having at least one spray nozzle 36, 36 for delivering an air water spray to a location, preferably a cavity 34 in the outside of an elongated filling nozzle 24 , adjacent the valve lining to activate the sealing means. An air supply 32,32 supplies air under pressure to the cavity 34 during filling to maintain the cavity free from contamination by the particulate material. The spray nozzle may be separate from the filling nozzle.
