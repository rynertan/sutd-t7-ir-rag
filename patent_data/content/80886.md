# Masonry cement composition, masonry construction and method.

## Abstract
A masonry cement composition which is a dry blend of portland cement, from .05 to 0.8 diatomaceous earth and 5 to 16 water swellable colloidal clay, such as bentonite percentages by weight of the composition . A mortar is made by mixing this composition with sand and water. The composition will not normally contain any limestone, the diatomaceous earth and clay in an amount of less than 10 , replacing the conventional 50 limestone.
