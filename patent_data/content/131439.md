# Instant hot or cold, reusable cold pack.

## Abstract
A three compartment, instant hot or cold, reusable cold pack 10, 36 for transferring heat to or from an object. A first compartment 18, 38, 51, 62 contains a predetermined amount of a solvent 19 comprised primarily of water. A second compartment 20, 40, 46, 64 contains a predeter mined amount of a solute 21 capable of essentially com pletely dissolving in the solvent. A third compartment 22, 44, 48, 66 contains a gelling agent 23 capable of gelling with the solvent and solute solution and producing a gel that is rela tively soft and moldable when frozen.
