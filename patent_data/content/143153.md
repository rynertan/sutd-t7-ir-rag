# Insecticidal 1,1 Biphenyl 3 ylmethyl esters, their production and use and compositions containing them.

## Abstract
Compounds of the formula CHEM in which Y is hydroxyl or leaving group readily displaced by carboxylate anions, e g. bromine, chlorine, acetate, p toluenesulphonate or methanesulphonate are novel and useful intermediate compounds in the production of valuable pyrethroid insecticides.
