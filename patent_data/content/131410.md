# Device for reading and writing IC external storage card.

## Abstract
Not only IC card A but also RAM pack G made in the card plate form although thicker than that may be applied to the device of invention to be coupled with computer for read ing and writing. It has one board 10 for holding such external storage card and the other board 20 mounted with block member 31 planted with contact pins 32 via a coiled spring 33 so as to yieldingly movable so that when said two boards are in engaged position said contact pins may reliably contact with corresponding contact points of said loaded card. Such compact and easily operational device may be cor porated in portable computer to expand scope of jobs to be made at the site.
