# Automobile roof structure.

## Abstract
A structure for attaching a drip moulding 5 to a roof pan el 1 of an automotive vehicle, includes a clip 13 formed ofa resin. The drip moulding 5 is attached by way of the clip 13 to a flange 1a of a roof panel 1 at a side edge of the roof panel 1 . The flange 1a is bent upwardly to form a drip chan nel 4A . A roof side rail outer panel 2 is joined to the roof panel 1 by spot welding and formed with an attachment aper ture 12 in which the clip 13 is inserted to be fixedly at tached.
