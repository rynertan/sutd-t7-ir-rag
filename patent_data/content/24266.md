# POULTRY LOADING APPARATUS

## Abstract
An apparatus for loading poultry from a rearing house A to a series of hen coops B arranged in vertical rows on a transport vehicle C comprising an elevator conveyor 12 which takes the animals from an assembly enclosure to a vehicle 20 movable transversely of the elevator conveyor 12 and carrying a first horizontal conveyor 26 directed parallel to the longitudinal axis of the vehicle 20 and able to be supplied from the elevator conveyor 12 , an adjustable inclination conveyor 54 alongside the first horizontal con veyor 26 and supplied from it by means of a second horizon tal conveyor 46 disposed transversely, and a pneumatic forced supply unit 72 located transversely at the output end 54d of the adjustable inclination conveyor 54 .
