# Handknife with rotating annular blade.

## Abstract
An improved hand knife 20 of the type used for trimming meat with a rotary driven ring like blade 34 guided by a ring like housing 30. The blade is retained by a partial peripheral flange 90 of the housing and a pivoted retaining shoe 36 secured to the knife hand piece 22. A securing screw 40 and an adjustable abutment 80 retain and position the shoe against the blade. The arrangement allows convenient blade changing.
