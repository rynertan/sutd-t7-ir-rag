# PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF STABLE MIXTURES OF COAL AND OIL

## Abstract
A process is disclosed for the preparation of stable mixtures of coal and oil comprising the following steps a grinding of the coal down to a particle size lower than 200 Âµm b mixing of ground coal with water c mixing of moisturized coal with oil, a mixture being obtained with coal content not greater than 50 by weight, and with a water content not greater than 20 by weight relatively to the mixture itself d homogenizing by fine subdivision of the components of the mixture as under point c .
