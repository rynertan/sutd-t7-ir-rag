# Centrifugal pump with impeller adjusting means.

## Abstract
The impeller 14 is fastened on a shaft 20 which is supported in a shaft support 62 , said shaft support being axially adjustable on a stationary member 34 by means of at least two threated bolts 100 projecting from said stationary member 34 in a direction parallel to the axis of said shaft, and reference nuts 104 for fixing the position of said shaft support on said bolts.
