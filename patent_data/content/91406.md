# MOTOR VEHICLE TRANSMISSION SYSTEM

## Abstract
A transmission system for a motor vehicle which has a transversely mounted front engine 1 , a gear box 2 also mounted transversely, and a differential 3 connecting the secondary shaft of the gear box to the two half axles 5 of the front wheels, includes an epicyclic reduction gear train 14 housed in the differential housing and connecting the secondary shaft of the gear box 2 to the planet carrier 10 of the differential, and a device 28 for selectively rendering the said epicyclic reduction gear train operative and inopera tive.
