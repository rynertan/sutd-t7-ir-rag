# Apparatus for increasing brain activity.

## Abstract
Apparatus for increasing the brain activity of a person lying horizontal includes heating means 324, 340 to heat the person s feet to 25 to 60 C, and cooling means 325, 325A, 370 to cool the persons head to 0 C to 20 C, and to cause a temperature difference of about 5 to 60 C between the feet and head.
