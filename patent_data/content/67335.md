# Automatic rearview mirror for automotive vehicles.

## Abstract
An improved inside rearview mirror 20 particularly adapted for use with automotive vehicles, the mirror incor porating improved means for detecting and comparing the relative brightness of light forwardly and rearwardly of the vehicle and improved means responding automatically to annoying glare from the rear of the vehicle to move a reflective element from a full reflective mode to a partial reflective mode and also automatically return the reflective element to a full reflective mode after the source of the annoying glare is removed.
