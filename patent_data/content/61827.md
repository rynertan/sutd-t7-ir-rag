# Extended travel damper in a lock up clutch for a torque converter.

## Abstract
An extended travel vibration damper 10 for a lock up clutch utilized in a torque converter where both the clutch friction surfaces and the damper structure are located radially outwardly in the torque converter housing 14 , with the damper being formed of a plurality of annular members with damper springs to provide a compact structure where there is limited space in the torque converter.
