# Assembly structure particularly for tables, trolleys, shelves, and other furnishing equipment.

## Abstract
The invention relates to an assembly structure par ticularly for tables, trolleys, shelves, and other furnishing equipment, which is characterized in that it comprises con nection and rest elements 3, 4, 5 each having at least two cylindrical seats with substantially parallel axes, into which at least two vertical uprights 1 can be force fitted. These two vertical uprights 1 are provided at each corner edge of a deck 13 supported on connection and rest elements 3, 4, 5 .
