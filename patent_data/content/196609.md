# Arrangement for loading fuel rods into grids of a fuel assembly.

## Abstract
An arrangement for facilitating the loading of fuel rods 34 into a fuel assembly 20 having a plurality of fuel rods 34 and at least one grid 32 formed of interleaved straps 84 having springs 88 for engaging and supporting said fuel rods 34 wherein first means 108 are insertable into said grid 32 for moving said springs 88 to retracted positions and second means 114 are insertable concurrent ly into said grid 32 , prior to withdrawal of said first means 108 for holding said springs 88 in their retracted positions to facilitate insertion of the fuel rods into the assembly.
