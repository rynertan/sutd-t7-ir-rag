# Bearing material.

## Abstract
An aluminum base alloy for use as a bearing material, and containing Bi 4 to 9 , preferably 4,25 to 7 by weight Si 1 to 4,5 , preferably 2 to 2,5 by weight Cu 0 to 1,7 , preferably 0,7 to 1,3 by weight Pb 0 to 2,5 , preferably 1,25 to 2,3 by weight as optional elements one or more of the following metals Ni,Mn,Cr in an amount up to 1 by weight Sn,Sb,Zn in an amount up to 5 by weight
