# Friction clutch cover assemblies.

## Abstract
A friction clutch cover assembly comprises a cover 11 and a pressure plate 13 connected to the cover 11 by flexi ble straps 16 . An outer flange 22 of the cover 11 is de formed adjacent to each flexible strap 16 to provide a respec tive abutment surface 34 , which in the event of excessive pressure plate 13 movement away from the cover 11 each flexible strap 16 will contact, to limit said movement.
