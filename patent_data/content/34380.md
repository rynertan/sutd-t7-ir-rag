# Improvements relating to drive motor mountings for record players.

## Abstract
A phonographic apparatus has a platter 10,11 and a pickup arm assembly 6 both mounted on a platform 4 which is resiliently mounted in a carcass 1 by three resilient assemblies 13,16 . The platform 4 is aligned along a line between the rotational axis of the platter 10,11 and the mounting point of the pickup arm assembly 6 . Two of the resilient assemblies 13,16 are disposed on opposite sides of said line, and the third on the line.
