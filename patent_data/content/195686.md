# Nuclear reactor.

## Abstract
A nuclear reactor includes a core 43 having a plurality of fuel assemblies 45 and upper internals 81 , wherein neutron absorber control rods 53 are moveably supported. The upper internals 81 include guide means 83 99 for the control rods 53 with a plurality of columnar guides 83 having a plurality of coextensive generally vertical guide sections 87 aligned to receive said certain rods 53 , and a plurality of generally horizontal plates 85 positioned so as to be vertically spaced from one another and secured to adjacent guide sections 87 and having vertically aligned passages serving as guides for the other rods.
