# DIAGNOSTIC KIT FOR DETECTING AMINES AND ITS PRODUCTION, AND A METHOD FOR DETECTING AMINES

## Abstract
Methods and a kit are provided for the measurement of to tal polyamines, or of diamines and polyamines separately, in biological fluid samples. The methods are simple to carry out and extremely sensitive. The methods are applicable to the measurement of polyamines generally, and they may also be useful for the detection of neoplastic diseasse and for the monitoring of the results of therapeutic measures taken against such disease.
