# AN INDUSTRIAL ROBOT HAND HOLDING DEVICE

## Abstract
An industrial robot hand holding device having a hand holding unit attached to the extremity of the robot wrist, a hand attaching unit having mounted thereon one of a plurality of diverse robot hands and being detachably attached to the hand holding unit, and a safeguard unit provided between the extremity of the robot wrist and the hand holding unit so as to safeguard the robot hand against an extraordinary force applied to or acting on the robot hand.
