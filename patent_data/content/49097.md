# Combined sheet inverter and sorter.

## Abstract
A combined sheet inverter and sorter comprises a plurali ty of bins B , a gate D associated with each bin which per mits a sheet 5 to travel downstream past the bin opening but guides a sheet travelling in the opposite direction into the bin 13 , and a reversible conveyor 102 for advancing sheets past the bin openings and reversing the sheets into the bins B . The conveyor 102 is controlled so that each sheet is reversed when its trail edge is located between a pair of adjacent gates whereby the sheets 5 may be guided into selected bins B in accordance with a predetermined sequence.
