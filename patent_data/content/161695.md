# A cross member for a cable type tire chain.

## Abstract
A cross member 18 for a cable type tire chain comprising a cable 26 covered over at least part of its length with a sheath 30, 31, 32 comprising a wire wound helically around the cable 26 , the sheath being free to rotate on the cable, there being traction means 20 also freely rotatably mounted on the cross member 18 .
