# Fixed partial dentures.

## Abstract
Fixed partial dentures are described consisting of abut ment crowns 1, 6, 10, 17, 20, 24 and pontics 4, 9, 13, 18, 21,28 connected through male female attachments 2, 3 7, 8 11, 12 19, 22, 23 25, 27, 29, 30 and being sealed together by means of a joining medium. Preferably, the parts of the dentures are formed from machinable glassceramic materi als and the joining medium is curable through exposure to visible light radiation.
