# IMPROVEMENTS IN OR RELATING TO STRUCTURAL MEMBERS AND METHODS OF USING SUCH MEMBERS

## Abstract
There is described a structural member for facilitating the provision of a part course of inclined bricks 7 in a wall 6 , for example, as decoration below a window opening. The structural member is formed of sheet material and compris es a first portion 1 for location against the inner face of the wall 6 . a second portion 2 joined to the upper edge of said first portion and inclined downwardly with respect thereto, said second portion extending at least to the outer face of said wall 6 , and at least one third portion 3 projecting from said first portion 1 in a plane perpendicular thereto and extending part way between the inner and outer faces of said wall 6 . The method of constructing a wall using the member is also described.
