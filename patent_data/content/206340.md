# Multi bed fluid bed boiler.

## Abstract
Multi bed fluid bed boiler having at least two fluidizable beds 7, 8 , in which boiler ordinary fuel, for example coal, is supplied to the first bed 7 , and in which the combustion of this fuel takes place in the first bed 7 and in the following bed 8 . According to the invention alternative fuel 13 , such as wood chips, waste wood, waste paper, refuse, etc., is adapted to be supplied to the second bed 8 in order to be burnt there. The invention thus provides a simplified fuel supply to the second bed 8 and increased protection of the nozzles 11 for fluid gas from the first bed 7 to the second bed 8 against clogging and the like.
