# Electrophotographic copier.

## Abstract
An electrophotographic copier comprises a photosensi tive belt supported on first and second rollers and driven thereby, a development unit, a transfer corotron, and an exposure unit. The development unit is disposed adjacent to the first roller, and the transfer corotron is positioned on one side of a line connecting the first and second rollers, while the exposure unit is positioned on the other side of the line.
