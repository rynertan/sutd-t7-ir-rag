# Combine harvesters.

## Abstract
A combine harvester having threshing and or separating means comprising a rotor 11 , concave means 13 extend ing at least partially around the rotor at a location above the axis of rotation of the rotor, and means 43 to 48 disposed in the vicinity of the concave means 13 and operable to sweep crop material passing through the concave means away from the latter so as to prevent any excessive build up of crop material on the concave means.
