# Device for replaceably connecting a drag head to a suction pipe.

## Abstract
Suction pipe 1 with drag head for a dragging suction dredger, said pipe being provided at its lower end with a connection piece 3 of the drag head in which the essentially rectangular throughflow opening of the drag head passes into the circular cross section of the pipe. A replaceable end piece or drag head part 13 , which forms the suction aperture or suction slit, is or can be detachably fixed at the side facing the connection piece by means of a quick action coupling to the connection piece in such a way that it rotates about a horizontal cross shaft.
