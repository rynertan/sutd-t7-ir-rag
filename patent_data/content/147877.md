# Device for picking up animals e.g. chickens from a ground surface.

## Abstract
A device for picking up animals such as chickens from a ground surface and for conducting them away, comprising a frame 2 , a suction conveyor 3 arranged on the frame and provided with suction means and delivery means adjoining said suction conveyor. The suction conveyor comprises a suction surface 5, 6 having suction apertures through which the suction means can suck in air and guiding and driving means for moving animals picked up by the suction surface towards the delivery means.
