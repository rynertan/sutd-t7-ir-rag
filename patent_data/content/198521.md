# Preparation of carboxylic di esters or acids.

## Abstract
A process for the carbonylation of a conjugated diene with CO and an alcohol or water in the presence of an aprotic solvent is carried out in the presence of a dissolved catalytic system prepared by combining a Pd II compound, at least 5 mol of a triarylphosphine per gram atom of Pd and at least 1 mol of HCl per gram atom of trivalent P.
