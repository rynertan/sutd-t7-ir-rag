# An essential oil used as oral antibacterian.

## Abstract
The bicyclic sesquiterpene 1 alpha cadinol is present in plant essential oils, e.g. cubeb oil and can be isolated therefrom. The cadinol is used as antibacterian against anaerobic bacteria such as Streptococcus mutans, in an amount of at least 1 50,000 by weight. It may be incorporated into a foodstuff, chewing gum, dentifrice or mouthwash.
