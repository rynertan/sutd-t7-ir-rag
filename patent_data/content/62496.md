# Fiber reinforced metallic composite material.

## Abstract
A fiber reinforced metal composite material comprising a reinforcing material and a matrix, the reinforcing material com prising inorganic fibers containing at least two components se lected from carbon as a simple substance , a metal oxide, a metal carbide, a metal nitride and a metal boride, an the matrix comprising a metal alloy containing zinc and aluminium, or zinc and magnesium as the main component thereof.
