# UROSTOMY APPLIANCE

## Abstract
A urostomy appliance has one part which is attachable to the body of the wearer by a pad of medical grade adhesive. The pad has a central hole, and, on its side remote from the skin of the wearer, a coupling element. The first part above mentioned is constructed to co operate with a second part consisting of a single piece of moulded plastics material. The second part includes a coupling element constructed for a snap fit or a push fit engagement with the first coupling element. Moreover, it has a cover wall in the shape of a dome and an outlet pipe.
