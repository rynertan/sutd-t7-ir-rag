# Method of manufacturing products of polyurethane.

## Abstract
In order to facilitate the release of polyurethane from a steel or aluminium mould, the metal surface is provided with a mono layer of an alkylmercaptan. A silver layer may be provided between the mould me tal and the alkylmercaptan monolayer. The invention can be advantageously used for manufac turing a luminaire for a tubular discharge lamp.
