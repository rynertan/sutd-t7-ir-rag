# Method and apparatus for remote sensor monitoring, metering and control.

## Abstract
A remote unit for a remote meter reading system. The remote unit 110 initiates a telephone call to a central complex 100 at a predetermined callback time. In re sponse to an acknowledgement signal indicative of a com pleted connection, the remote unit transmits indicia of its identity and collected data. The central complex responds with an instruction word, to control the next scheduled callback time and operational mode of the remote unit. Error code, load management and demand metering pro visions are disclosed.
