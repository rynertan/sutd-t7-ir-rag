# LIQUID COOLER

## Abstract
A liquid cooler comprises a tank 1 for receiving liquid to be cooled and an evaporator tube 2 in the form of a coil in the tank 1 . In order to enable the liquid in the tank to be used directly, the evaporator tube 2 comprises an inner tube 4 for refrigerant and an outer tube 3 surrounding the inner tube 4 and of a material having no adverse effect on the liquid.
