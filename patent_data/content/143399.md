# Cysteine derivatives, a process for their preparation, and pharmaceutical compostions which contain them.

## Abstract
New cysteine derivatives of mucolytic, antipyretic, anal gesic and anti inflammatory action are described, they being obtained by condensing S carboxymethyl L cysteine with acetylsalicylic acid. The present invention also relates to salts of the new compounds formed with alkaline and or alkaline earth metals, ammonium salts, salts formed with compatible amines, with amino acids or with physiologically acceptable acids. A process is also described for their preparation.
