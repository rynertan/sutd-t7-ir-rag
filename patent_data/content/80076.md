# A spindle drive type yarn winding apparatus.

## Abstract
A spindle drive type yarn winding apparatus comprises a yarn traverse device and a spindle for inserting a bobbin. The apparatus further comprises mechanism for adjusting the wind ratio connected to the traverse device 3 or the spindle 2, 3 a first synchronous motor 51 connected to the traverse device and driving the traverse device a second synchronous motor 21 connected to the spindle and driving the spindle and a frequency variable power source 23 capable of con necting to both the first and second motors. The apparatus can wind a yarn at a constant wind ratio.
