# A chair.

## Abstract
A chair provided with a seat 1 and possibly a back rest 4 said seat 1 having a longitudinal saddle shaped part, the upper surface cross section of which having approximately the shape of an inverted U, and saddle like members 2, 3 projecting transversely out from a mid portion of each longitudinal side of said seat part to support the rear, upper thigh portion of the chair user, said seat as viewed from above being substantially shaped like a groin vault or a Welsh or underpitch vault.
