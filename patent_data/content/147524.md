# Applicator for the non invasive transcutaneous delivery of medicament.

## Abstract
An applicator 10 for the electrophoretic deposition of a medicament through or on a skin surface 18 . The device 10 consists of layers 24,28 of a reservoir 24 containing the drug, a battery layer 28 superimposed on the reservoir 24 , and a cover 12 of electrically conductive material fully enclosing the layers 24,28 and having a lip 16 along its periphery to engage the skin surface 18 . An electrically conductive adhesive 36 bonds the lip 16 of the cover 12 to the skin 18 .
