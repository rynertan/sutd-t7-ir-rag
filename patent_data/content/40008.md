# Method and apparatus for applying elastic bands transversely to a diaper line.

## Abstract
Method and apparatus for attaching elastic bands 48 to a web comprised of absorbent articles 32 such as diapers which are fabricated to lie transversely of the length of a continuous fabrication line. The bands are stretched in a direc tion transverse to the fabrication line and are attached to the web in their stretched condition and are maintained in stretched condition transversely to the web until the article is fabricated.
