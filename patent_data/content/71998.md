# Corona discharging apparatus for an electrostatic photographic copying machine.

## Abstract
A corona discharging device A used in an electro static photographic copying machine, wherein a shield case 11 is provided with an open portion 12 opposite to the circumferential surface of a sensitive drum 2 and detachably and dislocatably provided with a shield cover 17 for regulating the electrically effective width achieved by a thin corona discharging wire 13 ar ranged in said shield case 11 at the lengthwise end of said open portion 12 .
