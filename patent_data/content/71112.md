# ENERGY ACCUMULATOR AND DEVICE COMPRISING SAID ACCUMULATOR

## Abstract
The invention describes an energy accumulator and a device comprising it, where energy is stored in elastic elements 3 or 12 or 20 or 23 or 26 or 38 compressed by means of a flexible and inextensible element 9 or 18 or 29 passing inside them which winds unwinds on and from a rotating shaft 1 or 35 .
