# FILM LAMINA HANDLING

## Abstract
A film lamina handling and storage device particularly suitable for handling microfiche has a central transfer area 2 formed between upper 58 and lower 56 plates in which a cushion of gas is maintained to support film laminae on the cushion the laminae may be moved around the area by gas flowing to outlets 27 venting outwardly of the perifery of the plates stores for individual 20 and possibly stacks 22 of laminae are conveniently situated around the transfer area, the individual laminae stores 20 being at the same level as the transfer area the transfer area may have copying or other treatment positions 4,6 .
