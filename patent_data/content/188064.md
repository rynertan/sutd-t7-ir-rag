# Metal chain belt.

## Abstract
A chain belt 32 comprising a plurality of interleaved sets 34 of links 36 and load blocks 50 associated therewith. Each link is defined by toes 42A,42B , the toes of each link being defined by parallel inside flanks 46A,46B joined by a crotch 48 . A load block 50 is received between the inside flanks and further a hardened insert 62 is located between the links and the blocks to protect the links and improve the durability of the chain.
