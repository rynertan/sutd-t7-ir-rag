# Assembly machine and mechanical control therefor.

## Abstract
A machine having a multiplicity of work stations WS 1A, 2A, 3A for performing a series of sequential operations on a work piece has similar operations at the work stations grouped together by common linkages 66, 68, 70, etc. , the motions required by each group to perform operations being provided by a single motion generating cam 52, etc. , there being only one cam for each group of operations.
