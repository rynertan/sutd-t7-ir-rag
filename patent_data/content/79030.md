# Contact lens rejuvenating solution.

## Abstract
A solution for removing occluded and or adsorbed che micals, such as cationic preserving agents, anionic preserving agents and mixtures thereof, from contact lenses which solution comprises a nonionic surfactant a cationic ion exchange resin, an anionic ion exchange resin, or mixture thereof water and optionally sodium chloride. Such solutions are useful to reduce and or prevent irritation of the eye of the contact lens wearer.
