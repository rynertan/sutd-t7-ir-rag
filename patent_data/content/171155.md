# Catalyst component for polymerization of olefins.

## Abstract
A catalyst component for polymerization of olefins which is prepared by contacting a product obtained by contacting A a magnesium alkoxide, B a silicon compound having the hydrogen silicon bond, C a titanium compound with one another, with D a a hydrocarbon, b a halogenated hydro carbon, and or c a halide of an element selected from the el ements of Groups IIIa, IVa, and Va of the Periodic Table.
