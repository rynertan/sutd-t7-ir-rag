# Apparatus for the parenteral administration of liquids at a constant and adjustable rate of delivery.

## Abstract
An apparatus is disclosed for parenterally administering liquids with a constant and easily adjustable flow rate both from rigid wall vessels, such as glass bottles, and from flexible wall vessels, such as plastics materials bags and bottles. The apparatus comprises a flow rate adjusting and stabilizing device 2,102 which, from one side whereof, is connected to an end member of the cover of a dripping chamber and, on the other side whereof, is connected to a lower outlet end member of the dripping chamber. This latter, in turn, is connected to the vessel of the liquid to be adminis tered. The flow rate adjusting and stabilizing device is housed in and supported by a sliding member effective to slide on a graduated rod fixed on the same supporting member as the vessel.
