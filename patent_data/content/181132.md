# MACHINES FOR PRESSURE FORMING SURFACE CONFIGURATIONS ON A ROTARY WORKPIECE

## Abstract
The invention relates to apparatus 1 for holding a tool of a machine 2 for pressure forming surface configurations on a rotary workpiece comprising an elongate support 3 of L shape on which a tool 4 is mountable, first automatic 5 means for shifting the support 3 longitudinally for adjusting the position of the tool 4 , and second automatic means 6 for securing the support 3 in a shifted position.
