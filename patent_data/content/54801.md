# Antibiotic AM 5344 A2 and process for the preparation thereof.

## Abstract
An antibiotic AM 5344 A₂ substance, and a process for the preparation of the antibiotic AM 5344 A₂ substance are described. The antibiotic AM 5344 A₂ substance is prepared by cultivating an AM 5344 A₂ substance producing strain belonging to the genus Streptomyces, for example, Streptomyces sp. AM 5344 in a nutrient medium, and recovering the AM 5344 A₂ substance from the culture.
