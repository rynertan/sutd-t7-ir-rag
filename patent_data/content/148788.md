# Smoking compositions containing acylpyrazine ether flavorants.

## Abstract
The present invention provides a smoking composition which contains a novel type of acylpyrazine ether flavorant additive as exemplified by 1 3 furfuryl thio 2 pyrazinyl 2 methyl 1 propanone Further aspects of the invention provide a process for the preparation of acylpyrazine ethers novel haloacyl pyrazine intermediates in the process and a process for the preparation of haloacrylpyrazine intermediates.
