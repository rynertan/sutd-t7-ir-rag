# Pressure sensitive adhesive having a broad spectrum anti microbial therein.

## Abstract
A composition and a process for making the same is provided wherein a chemical and storage stable pressure sensitive adhesive has homogeneously dispersed therein a broad spectrum antimicrobial agent. When the composition is placed in contact with the skin it uniformly and controll ably releases the broad spectrum antimicrobial agent with substantially unaltered broad spectrum antimicrobial activ ity and is useful in or on tapes, drapes and other medical devices.
