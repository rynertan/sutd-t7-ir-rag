# Sole and heel structure having premolded bulges.

## Abstract
In one embodiment, the sole and heel structure includes an outer member 30 having a sole portion 31 and a heel portion 32 . A bulge 36 is molded into the heel portion 32 and a bulge 35 is molded into the sole portion 31 in the metatarsal region thereof. A thin sealing member 22, 75 is attached to the outer member 50 with adhesive 65 to hermitically seal the cavities 40, 50 defined by the two bulges 35, 36 . A passageway 60 between the two cavities 40, 50 enables air to move back and forth during movement of a person wearing shoes incorporating such sole and heel structure.
