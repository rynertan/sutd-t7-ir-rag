# A MECHANISM FOR SECURING A PRINTING PLATE TO A PRINTING CYLINDER

## Abstract
In apparatus for securing a flexible printing plate to a printing cylinder a locking element engages the controlling edge of a flexible printing plate and is pivotal in use between a locking position in which the printing plate is tensioned and a release position in which the printing plate can be fitted or removed. Biasing means is provided with over centre action spring means for urging the body into the locking position and the release position depending upon the position of the locking member.
