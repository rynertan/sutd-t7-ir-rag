# Biotransformation.

## Abstract
A process for the production of an alkylene oxide containing at least 3 carbon atoms which comprises cultivat ing an ethylene utilizing microorganism under aerobic con ditions in a liquid nutrient medium containing the corres ponding alkene together with assimilable sources of nit rogen and essential mineral salts, the cultivation being carried out in the presence of ethylene in a molar proportion to alkene below 1 40.
