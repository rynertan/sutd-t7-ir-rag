# Nuclear reactor loss of coolant protection system.

## Abstract
A pressurized water reactor system having circulation means outside the containment vessel 1 for the circulation of loss of coolant emergency water is provided which prevents exposure of such water to the atmosphere. The system includes an enclosure 9 , separate from the contain ment vessel 1 and having a pump 13 and encased inlet and outlet conduits extending between the interior of the containment vessel 1 and the pump 13 . The pump 13 with drive motor 15 are so arranged that they are easily removable from without the enclosure 9 .
