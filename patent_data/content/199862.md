# Chain replacement apparatus.

## Abstract
Apparatus for use in replacing a mounted endless chain 14 which connected a pair of sprockets 10, 12 , where one of the sprockets 10 has an exposed portion facing away from the other sprocket 12 . The apparatus includes a frame plate 22 and a pair of spaced, confronting chain abutments 36, 38 carried on the frame plate 22 adapted to contact a pair of spaced regions of the chain 14 , where such is trained over the exposed sprocket portion. An opening 32 formed between the two members is adapted to receive the two disconnected end portions of a replacement chain. Chain support means 44 are provided to support a replacement chain 16 .
