# Laminated soft faced spiral woven papermakers fabric.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a papermakers fabric having an upper layer comprised of a batt 24 and an under layer 2 comprised of a plurality of intermeshed monofila ment spiral coils 4 , retained by pintle means 10 the upper and lower layers being unified into a single fabric by application of adhesives to the interface between the batt layer 24 and the under layer 2 .
