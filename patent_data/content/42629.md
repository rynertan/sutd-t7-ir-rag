# Activation of blowing agents.

## Abstract
A composition comprising a gas expandable polymeric material and an activated hydrodicarboxylate blowing agent, wherein the activator is selected from nickelbis 3,5 di t butyl 4 hydroxybenzoate, a mixture of 2 hydroxy 4 n octoxy benzophenone and nickel bis 2 oxy 4 n octoxybenzo phenone , nickel stearate, nickel trifluoroacetyl acetonate, 2,2 thiobis 4 t octylphenolato nickel, nickelbis o ethyl 3, 5 di t butyl 4 hydroxy benzyl phosphonate , nickelammoniumsulfate, ethylene diaminetetra methylenephosphonic acid , and hexa methylenediaminetetra methylene phosphonic acid .
