# Process for the preparation of 1 chloro 1,2,2,2 tetrafluorethane.

## Abstract
A method for the preparation of 1 chloro 1, 2, 2, 2, tetrafluoroethane which comprises treating 1, 1 dichloro 1, 2, 2, 2 tetrafluoroethane, optionally in the form of an isomer mixture, with an alkali metal amalgam in an active hydrogen containing liquid medium which includes a solvent for the dichlorotetrafluoroethane.
