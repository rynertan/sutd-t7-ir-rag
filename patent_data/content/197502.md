# High pressure fluid processing device.

## Abstract
A high pressure fluid processing device 1 is disclosed including a frame 2 positioned across a passage for conveying workpieces, a header 3 having a plurality of nozzles 7 thereon arranged at intervals P , a driving mechanism 9,10 for moving the nozzles 7 on circles with the radius e , and the radius e and the nozzle interval P satisfying the equation e P 2.
