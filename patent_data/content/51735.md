# Demountable sealed joint and method for the fluid tight connection of a sheet member to a support.

## Abstract
The support 3 , is provided with a dovetail groove 5 engaged by a bead 4 of substantially the same shape as said groove and forming a portion of the sheet member 2 and substantially rigid in a transverse direction the groove 5 comprising two longitudinal undercuts 6, 7 engaged by respective longitudinal extensions 14, 15 of said bead 4 and of which one is more pronounced than the other, the sheet member 2 being connected to the bead 4 substantially at the base of the extension 15 engaged in the more pronounced undercut and at a predetermined distance from the other extension 14 .
