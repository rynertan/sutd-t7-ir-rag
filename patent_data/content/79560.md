# Forming, filling and sealing machine.

## Abstract
This disclosure illustrates and describes a liquid packag ing machine 10 including a base frame 12 having a vertical keel 14 mounted along the longitudinal centerline thereof. The units relating to forming blanks 18 into cartons, filling the cartons with consumable liquids, and sealing the filled cartons, along with the required conveying means 34 are all mounted on one side of the keel, while the motor and related drive and indexing mechanisms are mounted on the other side of the keel, operatively connected to the forming, filling, sealing, and conveying means.
