# Method of and apparatus for measuring coordinates.

## Abstract
A coordinates measuring apparatus comprising a detect ing unit constituted by an optical system for obtaining an image of an object and a detecting system having a plurality of arrayed detecting elements disposed in such a manner that respective detecting signals are superposed at the position of said image, a control unit for taking out data from each of said arrayed detecting elements, and a storage unit for storing the data of each array taken out from said control unit.
