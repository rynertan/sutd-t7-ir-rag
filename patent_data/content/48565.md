# An internal shoe drum brake adjusting device.

## Abstract
An internal shoe drum brake automatic adjuster com prising a strut 18 extending between one brake shoe 12 and a wedge 19 mounted between the strut 18 and an abutment 22 on the other brake shoe. The wedge 19 is spring loaded to increase the spacing between the adjacent end of the strut 18 and the abutment 22 . The abutment 22 is pivoted to the shoe 12 and spring loaded against the wedge 19 to maintain a clearance S between the abutment 22 and a stop 25 secured to the shoe 12 .
