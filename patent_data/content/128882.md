# Device for isostatic pressing.

## Abstract
The device includes a closed press frame 2 and within a pressure chamber 1 supported by the upper yoke 22 of the press frame 2 . Displacement means with a portion 72 are arranged for lifting a chamber closure 3 from below. The portion 72 constitutes the lower press table, which is supported during the pressurization in the pressure cham ber 1 by two retractable support legs 4, 5 . The legs 4, 5 are pivotably mounted on the lower yoke 23 . The upper ends of the legs 4, 5 may have a circular shaped end surface and may coact with complementally formed surfaces on the underside of said portion 72 .
