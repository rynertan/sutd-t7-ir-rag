# Improved gas bearing.

## Abstract
A gas bearing is provided which comprises a body 110 having a spherically contoured pocket 14 with a gas supply passage 18 which opens in the center of the pocket. The gas bearing is coupled to the load and is supported by a stem 22 and a ball joint 24 through which the gas is supplied to the bearing. An additional passage 62 allows gas to flow between the pocket 14 and a closed chamber 60 in the body of the bearing.
