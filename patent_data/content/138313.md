# Vehicle anti skid wheelspin device.

## Abstract
A vehicle anti skid wheelspin device, comprising a force application means which may be either a brake 17 or a driven shaft, a hub 3 to which the vehicle wheel may be at tached, a wrap spring clutch 23 to connect the force applica tion means to the hub and an inertia responsive means such as a flywheel 35 to disconnect the force application means from the hub by causing the wrap spring clutch to unwrap when the acceleration or deceleration of the hub relative to the flywheel exceeds a predetermined value.
