# Preparation of polytetramethylene adipamide.

## Abstract
A process for the preparation of white nylon 4,6 polyamide of high molecular weight from a tetramethy lenediamine adipic acid prepolymer, formed under con trolled conditions of temperature and water vapor pres sure and wherein the plurality of cyclical end groups in the prepolymer does not exceed 0.20 mg. equivalent per gram of prepolymer.
