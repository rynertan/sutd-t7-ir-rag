# Automatic pocket label stitcher.

## Abstract
In a sewing machine 10 , a clamp type work feeder in cludes an articulated, rotatable, telescoping tube carriage 64, 72 which can move in either direction parallel to the lower arm 21 and rotate about the axis c of the cylindrical end of the lower arm 21 in accordance with a stored stitch program. A cylinder actuated clamp mechanism 110, 132 pivotally at tached to the carriage 64, 72 allows simple work insertion and full peripheral stitching without unduly obstructing the operator s view or access to the stitching area.
