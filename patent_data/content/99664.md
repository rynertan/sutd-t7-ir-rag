# Improved toboggan.

## Abstract
A hand toboggan to accommodate a seated or knealing rider and having a bottom surface 7 with a longitudinally extending medial portion 16 and two opposite flanks 19,20 upwardly and outwardly inclined from the medial portion, a pair of parallel longitudinally extending ribs 17 bordering the medial portion, and a plurality of parallel inwardly curving ribs 21,22 on each of said flanks to enable the rider to turn the toboggan in whichever direction he should lean.
