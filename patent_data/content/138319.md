# Gas fired water heater.

## Abstract
A compact gas fired water heater in which a reservoir of bulk water 6 is heated, indirectly by a gas burner fired im mersion tube 7 , and directly by mutual contact between the hot flue gases from the immersion tube outlet 8 and feed water droplets as they pass each other in contra flow through apertures 14, 15 in a plurality of plates 4, 5 .
