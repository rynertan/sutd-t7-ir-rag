# Surgical drape with arm board cover.

## Abstract
A surgical drape having arm board covers 18, 19 is disclosed. The arm board covers 18, 19 are formed in an arm board portion 15 of the drape by cutting and folding the drape material so that it may be attached to the body portion 10 of the drape along a single line of attachment. The drape may also provide leg covering elements integral with the body portion 10 of the drape.
