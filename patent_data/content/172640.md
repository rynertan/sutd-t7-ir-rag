# Arrangements and methods for testing various electronic equipments.

## Abstract
An arrangement and a method for testing various kinds of electronic equipment 1 , each having an internal bus 6 , during assembly and during subsequent servicing, wherein a plurality of software packages, each storing instructions and data pertinent to the testing and adjustment of a specific kind of equipment 1 are prepared and a computer 8 is connectable in common to the various kinds of equipment 1 via their internal buses 6 for testing any one of the various equipments 1 according to the contents of the appropriate software package.
