# Mouthpiece for a cigarette and a cigarette with such a mouthpiece.

## Abstract
This invention relates to a filter cigarette 10 hav ing inlet vents in the filter mouthpiece 14 divided into two groups 20 and 22 . The distance between the two groups of inlet vents is selected to give an icreased resistance to draw without appreciably decreasing dilu tion or total particulate matter, and is preferably from 4 to 10 mm.
