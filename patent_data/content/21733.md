# Cycle hubs.

## Abstract
An annular sealing assembly for a bearing means 2,3 between a stationary member 1 and a member 4 rotatably supported thereon comprises at least two first and at least two second annular sealing means 11, 12 , each with an annular projecting seal means 13,14 , which are dimensioned and arranged on the rotary and stationary members 1,4 with their projecting seal means 13,14 interleaved, to define a narrow zig zag passage, to prevent the ingress of foreign material to the bearing means 2 .
