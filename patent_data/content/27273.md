# Fibrillated and orientated reinforcement films made of at least one film forming, orientable synthetic polymer, process for the preparation of such films and hydraulic binder based products containing these films.

## Abstract
Fibrilled polyolefinic films, of a net like structure, contain ing incorporated from 1 to 20 by weight of a cement based material, are used as a reinforcement in the preparation of manufactured products based on hydraulic binders.
