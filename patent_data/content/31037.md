# METALLIZED RECORDING MEDIUM AND METHOD OF RECORDING

## Abstract
The invention relates to a metallized recording medium and method of recording. The metallized recording medium comprises an insulative base material of a first color, if neces sary, a coating of a dark material on its upper surface, and a metal layer of a second color the metal layer being colored by overprinting discrete limited areas of its surface with one or more inks of preselected colors to provide a distinctive half tone tint to the surface without adversely affecting the record ing quality of the medium during subsequent selective removal of the metal coating.
