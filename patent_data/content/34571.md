# LADLE SHAPED BALANCE

## Abstract
A dynamometric balance comprises an elongate body 1 serving as a handle, a dynamometric device 2 contained within the body 1 and in turn comprising an oscillatory arm 3 projecting from one end of the body 1 , elastic means 9 to return the oscillatory arm 3 to a raised position and scale means 14, 16 to indicate the weight corresponding to each position of the oscillatory arm 3 , and a receptacle 5 in the form of a scoop or ladle connected to the oscillatory arm 3 .
