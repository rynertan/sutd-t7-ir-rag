# Road wheels.

## Abstract
A demountable wheel rim assembly for a road vehicle and comprising a rim 16 and an adapter band 24 extending outwardly well beyond the rim so that two assemblies can be placed on a hub 10 for a dual type wheel with the bands abutting to space the rims without a separate spacer band. The band may have notches 49 or openings 127 in its free end portion to receive clamps 46, 130 for use when it is mounted singly on a hub 128 .
