# Display analyzer and method for analyzing an image from a unit under test.

## Abstract
Display analyzer for analyzing an image from a unit 10 under test comprising a carriage 18, 20 having at least two degrees of freedom for adjustably supporting a detection device 16 having a given field of view and being capable to produce a viewing signal responsive to the image size and brightness within the field of view, this viewing signal being produced with a predetermined characteristic in response to the appearance of a given image at the center of said field of view said display analyzer also comprises an analyzing device connected to the detection device 16 for producing a carriage adjustment signal, said carriage 18, 20 being operable to move in response to the adjustment signal, and the analyzing device being operable to produce information in response to the production of the viewing signal.
