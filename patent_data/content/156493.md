# Binder compositions, coating compositions containing said binder compositions, and articles coated with said coating compositions.

## Abstract
This invention concerns compositions suitable as bind ers for coating compositions, comprising one or more ester of acrylic and or acryloxypropionic acid, multifunctional mer captan, and vanadium compound. Coating compositions comprising the binder composition of the invention are cu rable at ambient conditions to give coatings which have high humidity resistance and good weatherability. The invention also concerns articles coated with said coating composition.
