# Surgical element.

## Abstract
A surgical element 1 , such as a suture or a replacement for a ligament, comprises a core 2 consisting of a multiplic ity of flexible filaments, such as fine carbon fibres, and partial sheathing 3 formed of standard implantable grade polym er, such as a polyester, in at least two groups 3X, 3Y totalling less in width than the circumference of the core and interwoven in contra rotation so that areas 4 of the core are exposed at frequent intervals, to encourage penetration and ingrowth of fresh tissue between the core filaments.
