# Folding caddy.

## Abstract
The caddy comprises a foldable bottom consisting of at least two bottom members 7, 8 with wheels 3 attached thereto, a handle 6 for pushing or pulling the device, and a rear wall 2 which is also foldable and consists of at least two rear wall members 10, 11 . The rear wall members are hinged 12, 13 to wheel guards 4, 5 and the bottom is, on opposite sides, also hinged to the wheel guards.
