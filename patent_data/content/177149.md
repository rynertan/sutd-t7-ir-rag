# Hemming apparatus.

## Abstract
A hemming apparatus A for sewing machine compris ing a hem folding attachment provided on a sewing machine and an aiding apparatus B for hemming for prefolding the hem into S shape in hemming is provided. The aiding apparatus B for hemming comprises a free roll 19 , a ring 13 for position setting means, a fitting means 37 , an under guide plate 29 , and a base guide 35 . When the operator simply allows to advance the side edge of the hem bridged over between the hem folding attachment and the free roll 19 along the ring 13 , the lower folded section of the hem which is formed into S shape is folded gradually deep, and hemming is operated while shaping the hem into the prescribed S shape.
