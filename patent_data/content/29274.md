# SCREW CAP

## Abstract
Screw cap of the type that is formed of at least an annular body which is screw threaded and at one end is provided with a sealing wall, characterized thereby that at the inner side of said sealing wall 6 an annular projection 8 is provided, in the vicinity of said sealing wall 6 the inner side of said annular body 2 possesses a cylindrical part, and a small rib 10 is provided between the inner side 7 of said annular body 2 and the outer side 9 of said annular projection 8 .
