# Cutter assembly.

## Abstract
A drill bit with multiple fluid jet cutting nozzles 7 designed so that the drill bit workface 8 includng the cutters 9 is a separate piece from the drill bit body 3 that houses the fluid jet nozzle orifice mounts 7 . The cutter assembly 8 protects the nozzle housing 3 from rapid wear and it can be easily removed from the nozzle housing 3 without disturb ing or removing any of the nozzle orifice mounts 7 .
