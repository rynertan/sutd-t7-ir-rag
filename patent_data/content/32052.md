# Method for producing panels.

## Abstract
Instrument panels or other such panels are made by injection and distribution of foam precursors into a cavity between rigid and flexible sheets tightly clamped at their borders. The Figure shows relative portion of rigid sheet A , flexible sheet B and mold members M1 and M2 . Injection preferably occurs through an injection port at a parting line between the mold members M1 and M2 defined also by sheets A and B .
