# Electrical appliance control.

## Abstract
A transmitter, for electrical appliance control by transmitting digital control signals on a power main, comprises two releasably connected parts 3, 4 , a first part 3 , comprising m actuators 1 to initiate the generation of m control signals, and the second part 4 containing generating means 20 for the control signals, and a selector 2 for selecting m out of n control signals. All n control signals can be produced, in groups of upto m signals for each condition of the selector 2 . Means 39 are provided to monitor the main to achieve transmitter queueing dependent upon the selector 2 settings at the transmitters.
