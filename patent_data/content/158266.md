# FOAMABLE POLYCARBONATE COMPOSITIONS, ARTICLES AND METHODS

## Abstract
A foamable thermoplastic composition comprising in admixture a a polycarbonate resin, alone, or with b a polyester, c a minor amount of a foaming agent and d a minor amount of an inorganic non fibrous particulate nuc leating agent, alone, or with a particulate organic polymeric material, wherein d has an aspect ratio of about 1 and a small particle size.
