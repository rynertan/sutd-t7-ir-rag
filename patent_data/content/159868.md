# TRANSMISSION LINE

## Abstract
An electrical transmission line is provided comprising at least one elongate signal conductor 2 , one or more other con ductors 3 placed away from and substantially parallel to said signal conductor, all conductors encased in an outer insulating resin covering 4 having a rectangular cross section, the signal conductor s being further encased in an inner insulating po rous resin covering 6 , the porous resin covering having an electrical shielding layer 7 thereover.
