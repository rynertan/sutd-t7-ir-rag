# CONTAINERS FOR SOFTWARE

## Abstract
A container for a floppy disc comprising two members 1, 2 hingedly connected together, with one member 1 being provided on its inside face with a compartment 5, 16, 15 for accommodating, and retaining entirely therewithin, at least one floppy disc. The other member 2 may also have a compartment on its inside face for one or more floppy discs, or it may be provided with means 24 for holding one or more cassettes of magnetic tape and or one or more diskettes.
