# CLOSET BOWL PROVIDED WITH MEANS FOR WASHING THE BODY OF THE USER

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a closet bowl, provided with means for washing the body of the user, which compris es a bowl body 2 including, in its inside, a cavity to be coupled to a discharging duct. The main feature of the invention is that it comprises washing nozzles 5 leading to the cavity and which can be controlled to deliver washing jets to the part of the user s body to be washed, with water having a preset temperature.
