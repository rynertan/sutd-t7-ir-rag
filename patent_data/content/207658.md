# POWERED PALLET REPAIR TOOL

## Abstract
A powered pallet repair tool 10 for severing a fastener which fastens a deck board 51 to a stringer 53 of a pallet has a pair of cutting blades 11, 12 which are offset laterally relative to the axis of linearly reciprocating drive members 15, 16 which support the blades spaced apart sufficiently to straddle the stringer to facilitate locating the blades at the cutting line where the boards are fastened together. The cut ting blades are driven into engagement during a cutting oper ation. The blades each have a bevelled surface 11a, 12a leading away from the cutting edge to facilitate prying the deck board away from the stringer during a cutting cycle.
