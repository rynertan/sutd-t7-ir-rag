# Ink jet printer.

## Abstract
The ink jet printer contains an ink jet nozzle 1 connected to a pressure chamber 5 which is recessed in a body 7 and is covered with a diaphragm 13, 15 on which a driving ele ment 17 is fitted. The diaphragm contains an outer plate 13 on which the driving element 17 is fitted and an inner plate 15 provided with an oblong opening 19 which extends between the pressure chamber 5 and the ink feed channel 1 . The opening 19 forms a narrow passage with very accu rately defined dimensions between the ink feed channel 11 .
