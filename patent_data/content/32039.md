# Syringes.

## Abstract
A syringe 12 having a barrel 12.1 and a plunger 14 moveable axially in the barrel is provided with locking means for locking the plunger to the barrel. The locking means 20 includes cam means 22 carried between the barrel and the plunger, and means for moving the cam into a position forc ing the plunger into binding contact with the barrel.
