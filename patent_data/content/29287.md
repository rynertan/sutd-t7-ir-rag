# Die for extruding a honeycomb structural body and method for manufacturing the same.

## Abstract
A die for extruding a honeycomb structural body for a ca talyst support, which catalyst removes harmful exhaust gases, comprises an integral metal block 4 having one surface thereof provided with a pattern of circular holes A of a given depth and a grid of slits 5 conforming to the cross sectional shape of a honeycomb structural body to be extruded and hav ing another surface thereof provided with a pattern of holes B of a given depth, the slits connecting with the holes B , having a smaller width than the diameter of the holes A and passing through the centres of the holes A . When manufacturing such a die the slits are provided after forming the holes A .
