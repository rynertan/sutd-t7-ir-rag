# Open sleeve support for wire marking elements.

## Abstract
This open sleeve support is consisting of a central flat or slightly curved part 1 , preferably of constant thickness, provided with two lateral branches 2 growing more narrow downwards, the latter being connected through a small fillet, to the two flat and parallel flanges 4 bent inwards and almost reaching the center of the sleeve, so that the support can be easily fitted on cables of various size 7 by slipping the sleeve on laterally, without risk of failure, while remain ing firmly in place.
