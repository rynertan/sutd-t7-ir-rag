# Two piece blow molded container with handle and method of molding same.

## Abstract
A preform 10 having a neck portion 12, 14 and a tubular body 16 is inserted into a prefabricated attach ment ring 20 which has a centrally located opening therein smaller than the preform neck and which is dis posed in a mold cavity. The attachment ring has a handle 24 integrally attached thereto. The preform body is blow molded and expands to form a container 30 . During ex pansion, the preform and attachment ring become inter locked to form a two piece container having a handle.
