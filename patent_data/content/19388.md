# Preparation of trifluoromethyl substituted phenols and phenates and the preparation, from these phenols and phenates, of nitro and trifluoromethyl substituted diphenyl ethers.

## Abstract
A novel process is provided for the preparation of a substituted phenate or the corresponding phenol of the formula The phenates or phenols obtainable by the novel process may be used as intermediates in the preparation of trifluoromethylphenyl nitrophenylether herbicides optionally containing one or two additional ring substituents
