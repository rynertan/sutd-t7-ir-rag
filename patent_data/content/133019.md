# TREE OR PLANT PROTECTION

## Abstract
A sapling 11 can be protected when it is first planted by means of a sleeve 12 curved or folded around the trunk or stem from corrugated translucent polypopylene sta pled at the meeting edges at 19 and retained and held to a stake 13 by one or more bent wire clips 14 surrounding the sleeve, crossing over, and having their free ends held together by a tie 15 .
