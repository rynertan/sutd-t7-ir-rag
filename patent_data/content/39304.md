# Apparatus for adjusting the timing of a fuel injection pump.

## Abstract
An apparatus for temporarily advancing the timing of a fuel injection pump 10 of the type having timing means 64 responsive to a source of fuel under pres sure correlated with the speed of an associated engine and also to the pressure of fuel in the pump housing. A valve means 81 is provided for venting the pump housing which means may be manually or automatically actuated such as by the use of a solenoid 94 .
