# IMMUNOGENIC COMPLEX FROM N. GONORRHOEAE

## Abstract
A vesicular, immunogenic, non pyrogenic complex is obtained from isolates of N. gonorrhoeae by cultivating N. gonorrhoeae on a suitable nutrient medium harvesting and centrifuging the cells extracting the cell pellet with TRITON X 100 at alkaline pH suspending the solids remaining after extraction in aqueous medium and precipitating in the cold with ethanol.
