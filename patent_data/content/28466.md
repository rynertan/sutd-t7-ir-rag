# Smokers lighter.

## Abstract
A gas burning lighter with electrical spark ignition has a closing casing 11 . Successive downward operative move ments applied to an actuator slide 15 first releases a pivotal element 23 which moves outward to an open position, creat ing a flame aperture 33 and opening a burner valve, and secondly operates a piezoelectric ignition mechanism to cause discharge of a spark and ignition of the gas to produce a flame 36 .
