# Video printer.

## Abstract
Disclosed is a video printer for printing a desired picture scene of an input video signal onto a recording paper, in which a medium, such as a magnetic sheet disc 8 or magnetic tape for a video cassette recorder, capable of recording reproducing the information of the picture scene is employed. In order to obtain a reproduced video signal from the recording reproducing medium with normal operation, the video printer is provided with a mechanical vibration proof mechanism 12 and an electrical reproduced signal correction circuit 141, 142 . Depending upon the require ments of the situation, a plurality of recording reproducing heads are provided so as to make it possible to record a picture scene to be printed next on the recording reproduc ing medium during the printing operation as to a certain picture scene of an input video signal.
