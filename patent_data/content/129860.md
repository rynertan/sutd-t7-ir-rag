# A KITCHEN POT AND A SET OF A KITCHEN POTS

## Abstract
A kitchen pot or kitchen utensil has a substantially square shape with rounded corners, a flat lid, and handles or attach able means for a detachable handle received in recesses formed in the sidewalls of the pot and in the lid so as to not project over the outer shape of the pot. In this manner the pots can be placed tigthly side by side or stacked on top of each other in a cupboard, frigidaire etc. within a minimum of avail able space.
