# Carton with carrying strap and blank therefor.

## Abstract
A carton 1 for accommodating beverage containers B and the like includes a carrying strap 7 comprising an endless band which includes a part 29 anchored internally of the carton by being looped around one or more of the containers and an exposed handle part 25 located adjacent one end of the carton which part can be grasped to lift and carry the carton.
