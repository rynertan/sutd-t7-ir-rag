# PURIFIED SEMINAL ANTIGENIC FRACTION, PROCESS FOR ITS PREPARATION AND USE OF SAID FRACTION FOR THE DOSAGE OF THE SPERMAGGLUTINATING ANTIBODIES

## Abstract
A purified seminal antigenic fraction, consisting of five glycoproteins being characterized, each of them, by a specific apparent molecular weight and by a specific isoelectric point, is obtained from seminal plasma and or sperm coating pro teins by means of immunoaffinity chromatography.
