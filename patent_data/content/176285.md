# DISPENSER FOR FLUIDS

## Abstract
A dispenser 10 for fluent material is made up of successive plates which comprise a cut off or regulatory plate 14 , a first stationary plate 16 to which is fixed a shaft 18 having a bevel gear 20 at one end, a rotating intermediate member 22 and a second fixed plate 24 . The plates 14, 16 and 24 each have four sector shaped cutouts 26 and the plate 22 has five such cutouts 28 through which material is dispensed on rotation of the member 22 . The dispenser may be fed from a hopper with liquid or granular material.
