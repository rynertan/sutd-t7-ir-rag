# Image display system.

## Abstract
An image display system suitable for use in displaying, at an enlarged scale, any desired portion Si of an image pre sented on a picture surface of a display apparatus, or in dis playing, at a reduced scale, the whole portion DFS of the image presented on the picture surface. The picture surface is divided into sub regions arranged in m lines and n columns. Sub region appointing numeral keys are arranged to corres pond to these sub regions, so that any desired sub region Si is appointed by a depression of the corresponding appointing key or keys, to make the image portion in the appointed sub region Si be enlarged and displayed over the entire area DFS of the picture surface or to make the whole part DFS of the image on the display surface be reduced and displayed in the appointed sub region Si of the picture surface.
