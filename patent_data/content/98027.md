# Advertising display means for parking meter and the like.

## Abstract
An advertising display means 18 is provided for a parking meter 10 of the type having a removable housing component 16 accessible internally of the meter 10 . The display means comprises a unitary member 18 having a first portion 19 which is substantially identical to the removable housing component 16 and is substituted therefor out in the field or during routine maintenance in the workshop. The unitary member 18 has a second planar portion 20 , integral with the first portion 19 and extending vertically above the meter 10 . A pressure sensitive adhe sive decal 28 or label, bearing an advertising message, is secured to the planar portion 20 .
