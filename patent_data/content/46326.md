# Whey protein containing cosmetic formulations, process of preparing the same and a method for setting hair by using a non hydrolyzed whey product.

## Abstract
A cosmetic formulation for treating skin and hair having incorporated tnerein a substantially non hydrolyzed whey protein containing product. A method of preparing the same and a method for setting hair by using said cosmetic formulation. The whey products provide substantive results without the expense of hydrolysis.
