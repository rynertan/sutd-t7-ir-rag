# Emulsion containing prostaglandin E1 and method for production thereof.

## Abstract
A fat emulsion for injection containing prostaglandin E₁ has a vasodilative action as well as a hypotensive action and is effective in treating chronic arteriostenosis diseases. It can be prepared by dissolving the prostaglandin E₁ and a phospholipid in soybean oil, mixing the resulting solution with water to form a coarse emulsion and homogenizing the coarse emulsion.
