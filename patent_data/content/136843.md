# Process for producing photographic paper.

## Abstract
A process for producing a photographic paper compris es coating a composition containing an unsaturated organic compound capable of being polymerized by irradiation with electron beams and an inorganic white pigment on the surface of a web support, and irradiating the coated layer by irradiation with electron beams while pressing the web support on a roll having a radius of curvature of from 25 cm to 250 cm with the coated layer on the outside to harden it in a convexly curled state photosensitive emulsion is then coated on the convexly curled surface of the web support.
