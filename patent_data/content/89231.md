# Silver halide photographic material.

## Abstract
A silver halide photographic material capable of produc ing a photographic image of good quality even when it is subjected to rapid processing at high pHs and temperatures is disclosed. This photographic material comprising a sup port and one or more layers at least one layer containing a compound of formula I and at least one compound selected from the group consisting of a compound having an alkylene oxide chain with a molecular weight of 250 or more which is selected from a polyalkylene glycol and a condensation product of alkylene oxide and an organic compound having active hydrogen Formula I
