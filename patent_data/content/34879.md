# Improved venipuncture device.

## Abstract
An improved venipuncture device 10 comprises a cathe ter having a catheter hub 14 at one end, with a needle having a needle hub 20 at one end and a sharpened tip 24 at the second end 26 extending through the catheter 10 and catheter hub 14 . The needle 18 is adapted for insertion and removal from the catheter 10 . A fin 30 extends substantially vertically from the needle hub 20 and is constructed and arranged for gripping the device 10 in a manner which facili tates venipuncture.
