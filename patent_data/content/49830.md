# Mouthwash compositions comprising hexetidine and zinc salts for the synergistic inhibition of dental plaque.

## Abstract
Oral compositions, such as mouthwashes, sprays, gels, oral foams, toothpastes and the like comprising a combina tion of antibacterial pyrimidine amine base, especially hexetidine plus one or several zinc salts which combination inhibits the formation of dental plaque synergistically without staining of teeth. The names Hibitane, Alexidine, Vantocil, Domiphen bromide, Lavoris, Drossadine, Hexoral, Hextril, Oraldene, Sterisol, Lycasin, Cremophor, Plak Out and Displac are trade marks.
