# Filter means.

## Abstract
A filter means 10 for filtering fluid comprises a first filter section 9a and a second filter section 9b . The filter means 10 is formed by using a folded sheet material having 9 a trapezoid or a sector shaped form. The second filter section 9b of the filter means 10 has a filtering performance different from that of the first filter section 9a of the filter means 10 .
