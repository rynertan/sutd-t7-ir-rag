# Tilting ashtray.

## Abstract
Tilting ashtray comprising a container 1 for the collec tion of stubs and ash and a closure lid 2 for the container 1 , wherein the bearing base 4 of container 1 has a convex shape, while closure lid 2 includes a plate 6 adapted to close the top of the container 1 and provided with an opening 8 whereby under said opening 8 an additional plate 9 is located for temporary collection of stubs and ash before they are caused to fall into container 1 by means of a tilting thereof.
