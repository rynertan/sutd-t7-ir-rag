# Thermal ink jet printer.

## Abstract
In a printer 10 in which a plurality of holes to be filled with ink are formed in an ink film 108 , selectively heating ink generates bubbles from the holes and ejects ink due to the pressure of bubbles, thus printing an image on a sheet. A cartridge for integrally holding the ink film 108 with holes 109 , a pair of rollers 204, 206 for holding the ink film, ink soaking members 164, 166 for supplying ink to the ink film 108 , and a seal member 144 for sealing the ink soaking members 164, 166 from outer atmosphere is detachably mounted.
