# Chloro substituted ketone imidazole derivatives.

## Abstract
This disclosure relates to a class of novel compounds of the formula CHEM wherein R 1 is C1 C4 alkyl, phenyl or C1 C4 alkylphenyl R 2 is C1 C7 alkyl, benzyl, pyridyl, phenyl, halophenyl or dihalophenyl and R 3 is hydrogen, a CHEM group or a CH CH R c group wherein R a is hydrogen, amino or a OR d group wherein R d is benzyl or C1 C4 alkyl R b is OR e or a CHEM group wherein R e is hydrogen or C1 C4 alkyl and R c is hydroxyl or a CHEM group wherein n is an integer of from 1 to 6 and R a and R b are herein defined and pharmaceutically acceptable acid addition salts thereof. This invention further relates to pharmaceutical compositions containing the compounds and to the use of such compounds and compositions as anti anaerobic agents.
