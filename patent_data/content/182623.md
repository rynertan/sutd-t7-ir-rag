# METHOD OF MAKING SHAPED STRATIFIED MEMBERS SUCH AS ROOF TILES AND WALL TILES

## Abstract
A method of making shaped stratified members such as roof tiles and wall tiles, the method including the steps of depositing a first layer of mortar into a mould m1, m2...mn , compacting the mortar in the mould by means of a moulding head c1...cn , repeating these two steps as many times as necessary to achieve stratification of as many layers are desired in the stratified member and then cutting the mortar by means 3 compacting open end surfaces of the mould. In practice, a plurality of moulds is provided, these moulds being moved at constant speed along a conveyor belt.
