# CONVEYOR BELT STRUCTURE FOR SURFACE WORKING TILES OF CERAMICS, MARBLE, GRANITE, AND THE LIKE

## Abstract
The conveyor belt structure comprises a continuously movable web 2 formed of a lower layer 20 , impregnated with polyurethane and or rubber, an intermediate load bearing layer 21 and an upper layer 22 , of vulcanized rubber. Formed on the upper layer 22 are projections 30 , having be velled lead in edges 32 and defining seats 40 for removably accommodating therein tiles 5 .
