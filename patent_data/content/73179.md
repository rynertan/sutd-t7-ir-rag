# Combine harvester.

## Abstract
A combine harvester includes a threshing unit, a pair of transverse contra rotating distributor augers 11 located downstream of the threshing unit with respect to the direction of flow of the product and arranged to receive the product leaving the threshing unit and discharge it down wardly for distribution over the entire width of the harvester, an accelerator roller 14 located below the two distributor augers 11 for accelerating the fall of the product, a substantially vertical fixed wall 17 adjacent the accelerator roller 14 and cooperating therewith to accelerate the product, and a cleaning unit located below the accelerator roller 14 and including at least one fan for directing a current of air at the product accelerated by the accelerator roller 14 . The substantially vertical wall 17 adjacent the accelerator roller 14 has a rough hull surface.
