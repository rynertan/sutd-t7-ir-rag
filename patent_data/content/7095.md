# The use of an ascorbic acid derivative as bio available vitamin C in a food containing an anthocyanic pigment and a color stable food compositions containing the same.

## Abstract
The novel use of an enolic OH substituted derivative of ascorbic acid selected from the group consisting of inorganic esters, aliphatic or alicyclic esters, and O alkyl ethers of ascorbic acid as bio available vitamin C in food products con taining anthocyanin and or anthocyanidin pigments. Food products containing said ascorbic acid derivative are color stable. They can be produced by combining a food base with said ascorbic acid derivative prior to consumption.
