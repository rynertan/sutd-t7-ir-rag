# SEISMIC STREAMER SECTION

## Abstract
A hydrophone section for a seismic streamer towed array in which the hydrophones 2 are arranged in groups of not more than 7, the spacing of the hydrophones within each group being such that each group has an overall length 1 0.8 1.2m. The hydrophone mounts 5 and mechanical spacers 3 are attached at their periphery to aramid fibre strainwires 4. The hydrophone mounts and mechanical spacers are acous tically open structures.
