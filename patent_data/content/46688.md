# Extrusion die.

## Abstract
A die structure for forming a composite rubber cord strip is provided having a die throat 40 whose aperture width 45 at entry is greater than that 47 at exit, while its height at entry 46 is smaller than at exit 48, thus providing a substantially consstant cross sectional area of the throat throughout rubber is supplied to the entry along a path parallel to the cords, and from a cord guide 4 whose hole 7 spacing is greater than the cord spacing in the ply at the die exit.
