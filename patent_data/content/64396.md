# A method of producing shaped resinous articles and casting moulds for use in the method.

## Abstract
Liquid curable resins, such as unsaturated polyester resins, unsaturated epoxy resins, polymethyl methacrylates, are cured in a mould whose casting surface is made of a vinyl alcohol olefin copolymer, to produce a shaped resinous article with an improved and even surface gloss.
