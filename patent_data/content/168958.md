# Method for supplying a water in oil emulsion explosive into a cartridge machine and an apparatus used therefor.

## Abstract
A feeder comprising a hopper 2 and a pusher 4 , the pusher comprising a pusher sleeve 6 , which is provided at its bottom with a perforated plate 3 and a cylinder 5 actuating the pusher sleeve 6 along the wall surface of the hopper, can supply directly a W O emulsion explosive into a cartridge machine without accompanying with air and without breakage of hollow microspheres.
