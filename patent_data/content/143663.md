# Cash processing system.

## Abstract
A cash processing system comprising a transaction ter minal 11 having an opening for taking out and putting in a cash, and means for demanding payment or receipt of the cash a cashier terminal 2 which is provided separately from the transaction terminal to effect the payment and receipt of the cash a passage 3b for connecting the transaction terminal and the cashier terminal a carriage 3a capable of moving along and on the passage for conveying the cash between the terminals a system controller 4 for controlling the receipt and payment of the cash and a conveyance controller 5 for controlling the conveyance of the cash.
