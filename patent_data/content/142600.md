# Device for installing automobile radios on automobile dash panels by frontal application and without the use of screws.

## Abstract
A device for installing automobile radios, comprising a fixed support box inserted into the aperture in the automobile front panel and fixed to it by means of a stop ledge and dogs which press against said panel, and in which, if provided, the elements for locking the radio into said fixed support box and releasing it therefrom are controlled by the movement of the mobile handle fitted to the casing of said radio.
