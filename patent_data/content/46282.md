# 4 PHENOXY 2 BUTENE DERIVATIVES AND THEIR USE AS PLANT GROWTH REGULATORS

## Abstract
4 Phenoxy 2 butene derivatives are disclosed as in hibitors of cytokinin plant growth regulator activity and as possessing seed germination regulatory properties and senescence delaying activity when applied to plants. 4 Phenoxy 2 butene derivatives can also be useful as plant dwarfing agents, agents to retard seedling devel opment or as herbicides.
