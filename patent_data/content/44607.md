# Brake and transmission control system for a vehicle.

## Abstract
A brake and transmission control system 10 for a vehicle having a hydraulically controlled power shift trans mission including a control valve 20 having operating and neutral positions and biased to the neutral position, and a spring applied hydraulically released parking brake. The system comprises a two position valve 26 associated with the operator s seat whereby when the operator leaves the seat, the valve will shift from an operating position to a drain position whereby the transmission is automatically shifted to neutral and the parking brake is automatically applied. A fluid pressure actuated detent 28 is arranged to hold the control valve 20 in operating position when energized. Both the parking brake and the detent 28 are energized when said two position valve 26 is in its operating position.
