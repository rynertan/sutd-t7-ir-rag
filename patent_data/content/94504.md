# Lock mechanism for a carriage assembly particularly for use in a disk storage device.

## Abstract
A mechanical lock which serves to hold a transducer carriage 67 in a fixed position at all times except when the storage disk is rotating at a speed sufficient to ensure that the transducers are flying at a height sufficient to avoid damage to the disk surface. The lock 105 is disengaged by means of a solenoid 131 and armature 132 .
