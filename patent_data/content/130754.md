# Decorative carbon coating and method.

## Abstract
Coatings are provided that are especially useful for deco rative purposes 12, 16 . In one embodiment, black coatings are provided that include a black layer of disordered carbon. The carbon layer may be formed by sputtering from a carbon target. The coatings can be characterized as having a high gloss, a high degree of blackness and being non transparent with good adhesion and physical integrity.
