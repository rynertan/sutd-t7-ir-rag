# PREFABRICATED BUILDING, SUCH AS A HOSPITAL

## Abstract
Prefabricated building, such as a hospital, school or other community building, consisting of a number of standard contain ers 1 which are erected in accordance with a determined pattern and joined to one another, and which are, at least in part, previously provided with substantially all apparatus and connec tions and are filled with other building components, such as wall and floor parts, which are packed therein during the transport of the containers, so that during its transport the entire building consists solely of a number of filled containers 1 .
