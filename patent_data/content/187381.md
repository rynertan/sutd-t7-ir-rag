# METHOD OF PRODUCING AN ELECTRICAL CONNECTOR AND RESULTING CONNECTOR

## Abstract
A method of producing an electrical connector which includes at least one electrical contact 4 retained in a contact retaining plate 7 disposed in a housing 1 , and a contact insert 2 also disposed in the housing. In one embodiment, the contact insert 2 is formed of a plastic material and is ultrasonically welded to the housing 1 . In another embodiment, a recess 8 is provided in the housing and a shoulder 9 is formed on the contact insert 2 . A plastic element 10 is disposed in recess 8 against shoulder 9 and is ultrasonically welded to the contact insert 2 and housing 1 .
