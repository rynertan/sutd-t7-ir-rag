# Automatic express coffee machine and process for brewing coffee according to the machine.

## Abstract
The automatic express coffee machine brews coffee from coffee pods 6 disposed in an extraction head 14, 15 or in a plurality of extraction heads and comprises an electronic control system, B including a microprocessor and memory means, which operates all the operations of the machine and, in particular, carries out an adaptive control of the water temperature in a boiler 10 in order to keep that temperature in a narrow range of a selected temperature, controls that the volume of the hot water delivered to the extraction head or heads is in a narrow range of one or more predetermined volumes, in correspondence with each extraction head and carries out a diagnosis of the possible failures and depleted supplies by showing in a display in code the kind of failure or of needed supply. The process for brewing coffee is per formed by the machine.
