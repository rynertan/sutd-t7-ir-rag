# DROP WIRE BRANCH ARTICLE

## Abstract
A branch off article includes a branch off member having a body portion and at least two tubes extending from the body portion to an interior region of a splice cavity, with individual break out members extending through the indi vidual ones of the tubes. End portions of individual ones of the tubes are recovered and sealed to individual ones of the break out members and the body portion is sealingly secured to a member which surrounds the splice region and the tubes.
