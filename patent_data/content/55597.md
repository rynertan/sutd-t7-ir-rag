# Cycle pedal with main and auxiliary supports.

## Abstract
This invention relates to a cycle pedal comprising a pedal body 10 having a pedal shaft 15 , said body 10 having an upper surface on which are provided first and second foot support surfaces. Known such pedals provide relatively poor support to the bone structure of the foot. According to the present invention the first and second foot support surfaces comprise main and auxiliary foot support surfaces 20,40 formed and arranged for simul taneously supporting forward portions T₁ T₅ of the meta tarsi K₁ K₅ and rearward portions of the metatarsi K₁ K₅ of a cyclist s foot, respectively, during pedalling.
