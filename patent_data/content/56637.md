# 4 3H quinazolinone derivatives, process for production thereof and pharmaceutical compositions comprising said compounds.

## Abstract
4 3H quinazolinones of formula Z₁ and Z₂, independently from each other, represent an oxygen or sulfur atom and an acid addition salts thereof, are useful as hypotensive agents. There is also disclosed a process for the production of these derivatives and phar maceutical compositions comprising said compounds.
