# Reflector lamp module for photoflash array.

## Abstract
A multilamp photoflash unit has a housing formed to provide a multiplicity of cavities, each having a re flective surface and formed to receive a flashlamp. Each of the cavities has a reflective surface width opening to flashlamp diameter ratio of less than about 1.2, and least in the area around the hot wire 13 . The inlet open the cavity configuration includes a planar rear wall sec tion and a pair of bi planar sidewall sections.
