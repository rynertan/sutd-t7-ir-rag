# Electricity metering equipment.

## Abstract
An electricity metering equipment wherein, in order to reduce the possibility of fraudent consumption of electricity, there is provided in addition to the usual meter 3 for measuring the amount of electricity consumed, an indepen dent device 5 which produces a signal corresponding to the supply of electricity. A comparison means 4 then compares the signal with the amount of electricity consumed as measured by the meter, and an output signal is produced if there is a discrepancy which may be used to disconnect the supply and or transmit a signal to the supply authority.
