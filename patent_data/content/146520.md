# Thermoformable laminate structure.

## Abstract
A thermoformable laminate structure of a least one lay er of foamed thermoplastic material having a coating of a fabric impregnated with an acrylic resin having a softening temperature greater than 75 C applied to at least the outer surfaces of said layers of thermoplastic material. A twin sheet thermoformable laminate structure is provided which has two layers of foamed thermoplastic material.
