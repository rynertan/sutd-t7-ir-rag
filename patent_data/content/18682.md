# Oxiranecarboxylic acid ester derivatives, their preparation and their inclusion in lipogenesis inhibiting compositions.

## Abstract
This invention relates to oxiranecarboxylic acid ester derivatives. There are provided 3 substituted 2 aminocarbonyl oxiranecarboxylic acid esters of formula Oxirane carboxylic acid ester derivatives of formula I have exhibited lipogenesis inhibiting activity.
