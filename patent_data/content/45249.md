# A device for removing vehicle windscreens.

## Abstract
A device capable of use either by hand or with a powered reciprocating actuator for removing vehicle glass panels that have been adhesively bonded into a vehicle frame using an elastomeric sealing strip, said device com prising an elongated flexible blade 1 having a gripping element 8, 15 and a sharpened edge 4 at opposed lon gitudinal ends thereof, the sharpened edge being curved and coplanar with one face of the blade.
