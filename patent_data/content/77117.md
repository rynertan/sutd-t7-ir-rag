# Rear projection apparatus.

## Abstract
A rear projection apparatus for displaying a color image comprising red, green and blue color image sour ces 1, 2 located horizontally to each other in this order producing respective color images of different colors projection lenses 3, 4 located respectively in front of said image sources 1, 2 for projecting light flux of the respective image sources 1, 2 along converging optical paths toward front side and a screen 5 located at convergent point of said projected light flux and includ ing a light transmitting lenticular sheet having rear sur face and front surface the rear surface of the lenticular sheet being constituted by horizontally diffusing lenticu lar profile of which lenticulated depth X
