# Fasteners for jewellery and the like.

## Abstract
A fastener for jewellery and the like is of a box shaped structure 3,5,10 made of foil and has, at both ends 10A, 9 , two transverse seats which receive fastening elements of a chain or the like to which the fastener is attached. One end is formed by a half open hook 9 which is closed by a resiliently mounted tongue 10 forming part of the box shaped structure.
