# A trailer.

## Abstract
The trailer comprises a container 2 supported between a pair of scissors lifts having first and second arms 3,4 mov able about a pivot 5 by a ram 19 . The arms 3,4 support the container 2 by a pair of rollers 12 and a mounting means 7 . The mounting means 7 comprises a lug 8 on the con tainer 2 for engagement with a pair of U shaped slots, a tilt slot 10 and a lift slot 11 . The lug 8 engages the lift slot 11 when the trailer is to be lifted. For tilting the lug 8 engages the tilt slot 10 thereby causing the rollers 12 to disengage the container to permit tilting.
