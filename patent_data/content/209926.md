# Device for supplying a lubricant.

## Abstract
The complete replacement and waste which is applicable to the known grease dispensing devices when they become empty has been solved by the invention due to the feature that the improved device comprises of two parts 1, 2 which are detachable connected to each other. One part 2 forming the driving means i.e. galvanic element 8 , its activating means 10, 14 together with the cylindrical disk 5 and its membrean 6 being a separate unit, which is detachable connected with the housing 1 . Said housing 1 is permanent connected to an apparatus and can always be refilled with grease 4 .
