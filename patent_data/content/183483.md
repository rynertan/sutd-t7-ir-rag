# METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR FEEDING SLICERS

## Abstract
A centrifugal slicer is provided with an in feed apparatus 28 for feeding one item of produce at a time to be sliced by a plurality of knives 22 . The in feed apparatus includes a block 32 with a passageway 44 having an inlet 46 at the center of the slicer impeller 14 for gravity feed of items to be sliced to an outlet 48 located at a single area on the periphery of the slicer impeller 14 .
