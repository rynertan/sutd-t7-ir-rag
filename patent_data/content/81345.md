# Hand held setting tool and method for setting deformable head fasteners.

## Abstract
A two handle held tool for setting a variety of types of fasteners 78 by pulling the fasteners stem 73 to deform the head 71 having a number of reciprocable pullers each puller adapted to pull a certain type of fasteners. The pullers are mounted in a mandrel holder 29 which holder is adjustable to permit the handles to be spaced for ease of operation.
