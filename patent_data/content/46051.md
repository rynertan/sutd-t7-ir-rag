# Filament type vegetation trimmer.

## Abstract
A filament type vegetation trimmer having a slinger head 32 for rotating a portion of the filament 30 in a radial plane, is provided with a filament support tube 58 for supporting the filament 30 in the radial plane, the filament support tube 58 being provided with means 120 which coact with means 54, 56, 57 provided in the slinger head 32 for cooling the filament 30 supported therewithin.
