# Rotary weigh sorter.

## Abstract
Rotary weighsorter comprising a frame, a driving turret supported on a vertical shaft, a plurality of con tainers, each connected by a radial arm to said turret, a pivot connection between the arm and both the turret and the container having horizontal pivot axes at least two weighing devices are supported in the frame at dif ferent angular positions for independently weighing the containers on passing across, which weighing devices are connected to a computing means which is also connected to means for removing articles from con tainers.
