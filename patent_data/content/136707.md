# PLATE CYLINDER FOR PRINTER

## Abstract
A plate cylinder A for a printer has a printing plate 2 made of a resin, and a cushioning medium 8 interposed between the printing plate 2 and the cylinder A . In the vicinity of a slot 4 where the ends 2a,2b of the resin printing plate 2 are inserted and fixed the end portions of said printing plate 2 may be additionally supported by backing plates 9, 10 in order to prevent these portions of the printing plate to sink into the cushioning medium 8 .
