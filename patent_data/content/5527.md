# Process for the preparation of substituted norbornene derivatives and norbornene derivatives.

## Abstract
Preparation of substituted norbornene derivatives by the reaction in the liquid phase of cyclopentadiene or dicyclopentadiene with an olefin or cycloolefin temperature 100 300 DEG C pressure 3,4 170 atm. solvent chlorinated or brominated hydrocarbon. Tricyclic norbornene derivatives e.g. tricyclo 12.2.1.0 2. 13 heptadeca 5,9,15 triene tetracyclo 8.2.1.0 9,2 0 7,3 tridecene 11.
