# Gastrointestinal compositions.

## Abstract
Pharmaceutical compositions containing from 99 to 70 percent by weight of plantago and from 1 to 30 percent by weight of pectin, useful for inhibiting the occurrence of gastroduodenal ulcers and for preventing their recurrence, for reducing the incidence and severity of undesirable side effects caused by anti ulcer drugs, and for alleviating discomfort occasioned by gastroesophageal reflux. Methods for their preparation are also disclosed.
