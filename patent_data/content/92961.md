# A fungicidal composition.

## Abstract
A fungicidal composition comprises an inert carrier and as an active ingredient E 1 2,4 dichlorophenyl 4,4 dimethyl 2 1,2,4 triazol 1 yl 1 penten 3 ol and a benzimida zole thiophanate type fungicide in the ratio of 1 0.1 to 1 10 by weight and in a total amount of 0.1 to 99.9 by weight of the composition.
