# Method for manufacturing a drum brake shoe.

## Abstract
A method for manufacturing a drum brake shoe 30 comprises the steps of providing a strip of material 50 , forming a plurality of connected blanks 58 with the strip, bending each blank to form an arcuate surface 72 with adjoining sides, cutting one of the blanks 58 by serving a connecting link 62 and trimming the one blank to form a contour for the adjoining sides. Thereafter, a friction lining 40 is attached to the arcuate surface 72 .
