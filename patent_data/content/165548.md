# Small electronic apparatus with optical input device.

## Abstract
A small electronic apparatus with an optical input device has an apparatus case 1 for housing a main body therein, the apparatus case 1 being provided with an optical input section 3 , at least part of which is constituted by a light transmitting member exposed to the atmosphere, a photosensor ln arranged in the apparatus case 1 at a position opposite to the optical input section 3 , and a logic circuit arranged in LSI 9 for receiving and processing, as an input signal associated with the main body, an output from the photosensor ln .
