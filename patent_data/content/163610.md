# Apparatus for cleaning teeth.

## Abstract
The apparatus operates in accordance with the mecha nical sandblasting principle and is equipped with a barrel provided with a multiple nozzle 9 , and with means of connection 11 identical to those of a dentistry turbodrill. In order to obtain a gas power mixture that is permanently constant or, through control means, is rendered variable, the handgrip 2 of the barrel 1 is fashioned hollow and divided into a receiving chamber 39 , a swirl precombustion cham ber 40 and a swirl chamber 47 communicating via elements 41 48 having a calibrated passage hole. The powder and a small quantity of gas are inserted into the receiving chamber 39 , while the major quantity of gas is inserted into the swirl precombustion chamber 40 through a powder percentage regulator 46 .
