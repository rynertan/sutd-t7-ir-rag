# Cathode ray tube for projector.

## Abstract
A cathode ray tube for a projector, wherein a heat conduction member 22 of a mesh or reed screen shape is disposed on an outer surface of a faceplate 11 correspond ing to an effective area of a phosphor screen 14 without degrading the quality of an image formed on an inner surface of said faceplate 11 , and said heat conduction member 22 of the mesh or reed screen shape is conductive ly in contact with a heat dissipator or a heat dissipating body 21 which is formed on the outer side of the said faceplate.
