# Improvements relating to chain components.

## Abstract
An elongate main link 2 for a chain or cable sling shaped as a pair of generally semi circular crown portions 4 connected by parallel extending side portions 6 has the crown portions 4 formed with inner and outer circumferen tially extending flanges 8 and 10 connected by webs 12 and the side portions 6 formed with inner flanges 14 joined to outwardly extending webs 16 .
