# Quick release and attachment assembly.

## Abstract
A quick release and attachment assembly for the operating tools 10 for earthmoving or excavating machines has an attachment head 23 pivotally mounted on the boom 24 of the machine. A locating head is fixed to, or formed integrally with, an operating tool 10 and has a pair of spaced side plates 12 , a transverse bar or beam 14 and a hook or jaw 19 . The attachment head 23 is located between the side plates 12 with V shaped slots 33 at one end of the head 23 engaged with the beam 14 . A movable block 39 in the head 23 is moved by a hydraulic ram 43 into engagement with the hook or jaw 19 to lock the attachment head 23 to the operating tool 10 .
