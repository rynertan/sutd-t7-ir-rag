# Bottle stoppers or closures.

## Abstract
A bottle stopper has a head 10 and stem 11 having therethrough a continuous passage 14, 16 wherein is located a valve comprising a restriction 15 in the head of passage 14 and affording at the stem remote side thereof a seat for a resiliently loaded stop 17 . The valve is resiliently urged into a closed position but yieldable to open when pressure in the bottle to which it is fitted exceeds the resilient pressure. The head 10 and stem 11 are engageable, respectively, over the mouth and against the internal surface of the neck of a bottle when fitted to a bottle.
