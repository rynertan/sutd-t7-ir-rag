# 6 Substituted 5 phenyltetrazolo 1,5 a 1,2,4 triazolo 1,5 c pyrimidines.

## Abstract
This disclosure relates to a class of novel 6 substituted 5 phenyltetrazolo 1,5 a 1,2,4 triazolo 1,5 c pyrimidines and to pharmaceutical compositions containing such compounds. The compounds and compositions disclosed are useful as renal vasodilators, diuretics and or anti hypertensive agents for the treatment of hypertensive disorders in humans.
