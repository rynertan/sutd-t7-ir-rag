# Method of and apparatus for data recording.

## Abstract
This disclosure is concerned with a novel digital multipoint recorder that is microprocessor controlled and enables impact printing from in back of the recorder paper to provide a front visible flat print out of pages of on line running summary data suited for operating viewing in industrial and similar locations, and providing a record for direct use in reports and logs. The apparatus includes a technique for automatically ensuring a proper zero level of the analog to digital conversion circuit contained therein.
