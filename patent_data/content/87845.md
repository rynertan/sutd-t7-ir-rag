# Fluid distribution system.

## Abstract
A fluid distribution system comprises a fluid distribution conduit 1 a fluid inlet 9 and a plurality of closeable fluid outlet branches 2 . The distribution conduit further compris es a plug 4 which can be propelled in response to the fluid pressure, whereas valves 3 are provided with retaining means 39 51, 54a, 54b for retaining said plug contrary to the fluid pressure for opening said valves. The said retaining means become inoperative when the pressure of the fluid is being altered.
