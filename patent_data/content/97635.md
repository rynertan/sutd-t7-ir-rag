# DEVICE FOR PRODUCING HEAT ENERGY FROM A WINDMILL OR WIND TURBINE

## Abstract
There is described a device for producing heat energy and simultaneously auto regulating the speed of a windmill or wind turbine, which comprises a water brake or hydraulic brake mounted on the rotating shaft of a windmill or wind turbine, which brake is comprised of a cylinder shaped body 1 , at least one rotor 16 mounted on said rotating shaft 2 , and a stator 15 mounted about said rotating shaft 2 , adjacent to said rotor 16 .
