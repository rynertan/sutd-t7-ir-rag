# SCANNING MICROSCOPE

## Abstract
A vibratory specimen stage for a scanning microscope, said stage 7 being mounted within a support frame 8 in such a way as to enable scanning movement to be effected in the plane of the drawing the X Y plane . The stage is attached to the frame by means of four leaf springs 9, 10, 11, 12 made of sheet material and scanning movement is effected in the X direction by a pair of linear motors 15, 16 and in the Y direction by a further pair of linear motors 17, 18 .
