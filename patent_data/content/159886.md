# A polyvinyl alcohol and lignin sulphonate based adhesive composition.

## Abstract
An aqueous adhesive composition possessing good adhe sion, tack, open time, thermal stability, biological stability, di mensional stability, flexibility and adhesion contains a poly vinyl alcohol and lignin sulphonate in an aqueous base, there being from 1 to 8 parts by weight of the lignin sulphonate per part by weight of the polyvinyl alcohol.
