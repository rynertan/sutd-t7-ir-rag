# DIAPHANOSCOPY APPARATUS

## Abstract
In a diaphanoscopy apparatus a light source 10 is provided for illuminating the object with light having at least two different wavelengths, detector menas 15 are provided for sensing transilluminated light in relation to each of the different wavelengths recording means 38,40 are provided for separately recording the output of the detector means 15 relating to the different wavelengths and display means 66 are provided for producing a display which represents the absorption characteristics of the object at the different wavelengths.
