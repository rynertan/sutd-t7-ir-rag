# Automatic focus for overhead projector.

## Abstract
An overhead projector 10 includes a projection head 16, 46 which contains a pivoting mirror 22, 48 and a pivoting projection lens 20, 52 interconnected by a linkage 40, 62 . Rotation of the mirror 22, 48 causes rotation and translation of the projection lens 20, 52 and automatically corrects for Scheimpflug distortion and loss of overall focus of the projected image.
