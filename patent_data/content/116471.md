# Flexible magnetic discs.

## Abstract
A centre core secured at the centre of a flexible magnetic disc has a spindle receiving aperture 30 therein and, projecting into the spindle receiving aperture 30 , a pair of spindle positioning projections 20a, 20b made of metal and a resilient member 35 . The resilient member 35 serves to push a drive spindle 40 of recording and or reproducing apparatus against the pair of spindle positioning projections 20a, 20b .
