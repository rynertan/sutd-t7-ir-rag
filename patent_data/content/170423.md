# Fail safe digital phone.

## Abstract
A fail safe digital telephone set 30 is connected to a digital PBX 10 which has several normal digital telephone sets 20 attached thereto and which further interfaces with telephone lines 6 to a central office. The fail safe digital phone includes logic for providing digital communication of voice information with the PBX, logic for providing analog communication of voice information with the telephone lines, and a switching element 38 which activates the digital logic and connects the digital logic to the PBX. In the event of failure of the PBX, the switching element 38 activates the analog logic and an associated switching element 12 connects the analog logic to the telephone lines.
