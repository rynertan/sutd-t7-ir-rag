# Device for limiting output force applied by a rotary transmission.

## Abstract
The device includes input and output members 12, 14 , a clutch 21, 27, 28 responsive to torque transmitted between the input and output members 12, 14 and also the operating position of the output member, and a brake 24, 25, 26 re sponsive to relative angular movement between the clutch parts 20, 23 to arrest movement of at least the output mem ber 14 .
