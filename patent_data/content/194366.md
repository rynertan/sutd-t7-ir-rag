# Method of cleaning exhaust gases.

## Abstract
A method of cleaning an exhaust gas containing as a toxic component at least one of arsine, phosphine, diborane and hydrogen selenide, comprises contacting the gas with a molded, particulate cleaning agent comprising 1 cupric oxide and 2 at least one of silicone oxide, aluminum oxide and zinc oxide. The agent has a density of from 1.5 to 3.5 g ml and a metal atomic ratio M M Cu of from 0.02 to 0.7, wherein Cu represents the number of gram atoms of copper and M represents the total number of gram atoms of silicon, aluminum and or sinc.
