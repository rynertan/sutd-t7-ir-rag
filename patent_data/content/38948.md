# CROP DESICCANT AND DEFOLIANT FORMULATION AND A PROCESS FOR THE DESICCATION AND DEFOLIATION OF CROPS

## Abstract
An improved crop desiccant and defoliant formulation comprises a synergistic mixture of paraquat and an ammonium, quaternary ammonium, alkali metal or alkaline earth metal thiocyanate and process for the desiccation and defoliation of crops in need thereof.
