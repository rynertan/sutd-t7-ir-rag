# Support member for a rack and pinion assembly.

## Abstract
A support member for a rack and pinion assembly comprising a housing 10 with a first bore 12 and a second bore 14 intersecting the first bore, a rack 26 movably disposed within the second bore 14 , a pinion 22 having teeth meshing with teeth formed on the rack and being rotatably disposed within the first bore 12 , the support member 50 cooperating with said rack 26 to bias the latter into meshing engagement with the pinion 22 said support member including a first arm 58 and a second arm 60 which extend into said first bore 12 , the first and second arms being engageable with the wall of said first bore 12 to maintain said support member 50 in engagement with the rack.
