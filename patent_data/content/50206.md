# Pipe type oil filled cable line.

## Abstract
A pipe type oil filled cable line which comprises a steel pipe 21 and a cable core 22 drawn into the steel pipe 21 , and which is characterized in that at least one curved offset section 24 is arranged at a part of the steel pipe spaced from a cable joint 23 at a predetermined distance with a straight portion 27 of the steel pipe 21 interposed therebetween.
