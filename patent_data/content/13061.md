# Spinal restraint device.

## Abstract
A spinal restraint device in the form of a body member 10 having a head support portion 12 , a neck support por tion 14 , and a back support portion 16 . The back support portion 16 has a body wraparound arm portion 30 extend ing laterally from each of its sides. The body member 10 has a front sheet like layer of flexible material, a middle sheet like layer of flexible material, and a rear sheet like layer of flexible material. Laterally spaced longitudinal rows of stitching sec ure the front, middle, and rear sheet like layers together to form a plurality of longitudinal sleeves. The longitudinal sleeves extend substantially across the entire width of the body member. Stiffener members are located within the lon gitudinal sleeves. The spinal restraint device has a pair of leg loop straps 42 attached to the rear surface of the back sup port portion 16 and a hoisting loop 50 attached to the rear surface of the head support portion 12 . The pair of leg loop straps 42 and the hoisting loop 50 are formed from one continuous length of strap material.
