# An applicator tool.

## Abstract
An animal ear tag applicator tool comprising a first lever 10 and a second lever 14 forming handles and coupled to each other by pivot means to be pivotable about a pivot axis. First and second jaws 12 and 16 are actuatable by the levers 10 and 14 and each jaw is provided with retention means 38 and 39 for retaining a part of an animal ear tag. The tool is characterized by the pivot means being an open central pivot 24 with the centre of the pivot being in open communication with the space between the jaws 12 and 16 at the open and closed positions of the jaws as well as during movement of the jaws between the open and closed positions.
