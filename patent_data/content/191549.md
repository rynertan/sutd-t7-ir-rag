# Wire dot printing head.

## Abstract
A wire dot printing head has an armature 15 , a printing wire 14 fixedly mounted on a tip end of the armature 15 , a leaf spring 16 fixedly mounted in one end thereof on a rear end of the armature 15 and fixed in the other end, a spring branch part 16b provided on the leaf spring 16 in the vicinity of the fixed end of the leaf spring 16 , and an adjusting screw 23 being adjustable from the outside to be brought into close contact with the spring branch part 16b for deflecting the leaf spring 16 for thereby adjusting a deflection of the leaf spring 16 .
