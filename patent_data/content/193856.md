# IC card.

## Abstract
In an IC card, contact pads 7A and wiring patterns 7B are formed on a sheet like structure 4, 12, 13 and contact seg ments 7 1 to 7 8 are formed on the contact pads 7A. Main chip 5 and sub chip 6 are mounted on the sheet like structure 4, 12, 13 . A sheet film 3 is laminated on the sheet like structure 4, 12, 13 and the contact segments 7 1 to 7 8 are fitted in openings 11A of the sheet film 3 . The sheet film 3 has a thickness substantially equal to that of the contact segments 7 1 to 7 8 .
