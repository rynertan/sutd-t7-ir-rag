# Fibre reinforced synthetic material wheel.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a compression mouldable preform of a synthetic material wheel, which pre form has a substantially planar spider portion 10 comprising random, chopped fibre reinforced resin a first tubular section 12 comprising fibre reinforced resin sheet surrounding said spider portion 10 about an axis substantially normal to the plane of said spider portion 16 and a second tubular section 13 comprising fibre reinforced resin sheet surrounding and substantially coaxial with said first tubular section, said fibre reinforced resin sheet of said first tubular section and said second tubular section each comprising random chopped fibre and continuous fibre.
