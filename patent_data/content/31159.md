# Electrical contact.

## Abstract
Disclosed is an electrical contact of composite layer type, characterized in that an interrupting layer 2 consisting of Cu W alloy, Cu WC alloy or Cu W WC alloy containing 20 to 60 wt of Cu is bonded to the top surface of a base plate 1 of electroconductive material, and that a contacting layer 3 consisting of Ag WC alloy or Ag W alloy or Ag W WC alloy containing 20 to 60 wt of Ag is bonded to the top surface of said interrupting layer.
