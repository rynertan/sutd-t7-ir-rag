# Apparatus for unloading pallets of building components from racks.

## Abstract
In a plant for making concrete roof tiles, the tiles are loaded on to racks 30 and cured in a curing chamber 6 . Apparatus for unloading the tiles on their pallets P from shelves in the racks on to an outfeed conveyor 40 comprises a descending conveyor means 574, 576 having pairs of supports 598 , the spacing of the pairs of supports being equal to the spacing of the shelves. A pusher 604 moves columns of tiles from the shelves on to the pairs of supports which engage the edges of the tiles. A cam 706 moves the descending conveyor away from the rack and the conveyor then is activated to deposit the tiles on to an outfeed conveyor in spaced relationship.
