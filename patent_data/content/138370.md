# APPARATUS FOR USE IN LEAK DETECTION

## Abstract
Apparatus for use in the detection of leaks comprises an annular flexible, gas permeable skirt 18, 47 carried by and projecting from a carrier member 10, 36 which closes one axial end of the skirt 18, 47 , the opposite axial end of the skirt being closed in use by engagement with the surface being searched, such that said surface and said carrier member contain between them a sample volume 21 bounded by said skirt, and, a sensing probe 15, 35 commu nicating with said sample volume for detecting the presence of a signal gas in said sample volume.
