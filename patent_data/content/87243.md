# ADJUSTABLE SOCKET

## Abstract
A socket shaped to engage a nut or the like and having at least one insert which is geometrically similar to the interior work part of the socket, the insert s being a sliding close fit within the socket so as to be movable to and from a position of use and so that either the socket or the or an insert can be used to engage a nut, and including means to urge the insert s towards the position of use, characterised by means 27, 43, 50, 68, 179, 95, 210 for selectively restraining the or a selected number of inserts in the position of use.
