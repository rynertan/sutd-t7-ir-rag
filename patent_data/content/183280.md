# Siloxa cyclic compounds.

## Abstract
Siloxa cyclic compounds of the general formula CHEM wherein R 2 is a methoxy or ethoxy radical, R 1 is methyl, X is a divalent alkylene radical selected from CH2CH2 , CH2CH2CH2 and CH2CHCH3, and b is 0 or 1. A process for producing same by hydroformylating an hydrolyzable silane as well as the use of said compounds as coupling agents.
