# Electrical contact lubricant composition and method of lubrication.

## Abstract
An electric contact lubricant is disclosed which compris es a predominant amount of an evaporable solvent and a lubricant which, in turn, comprises a predominant amount of a partially crosslinked polyol ester formed by esterification of an aliphatic mono carboxylic acid with an aliphatic polyol in the presence of a dibasic acid crosslinker, a lesser amount of a phosphate ester fluid, and one or more corrosion and oxidation inhibitor compounds.
