# Electrical connector for a slide in component.

## Abstract
An electrical connector 10 for connecting a slide in radio 34 to a plurality of related, remotely located components by a plurality of wiring harnesses 22, 24, 26, 28 comprises a plurality of connector blocks 12, 14, 16, 18 attached to respective wiring harnesses 22, 24, 26, 28. The connector blocks 12, 14, 16, 18 are arranged in two longitudinally spaced laterally offset rows by a holder 20 which is attached to a receptacle 32 for the radio 34. The radio 34 has a side mounted terminal block 36 which has a plurality of terminal pads 92, 94, 96, 98 arranged to complement the connector blocks 12, 14, 16, 18. The terminal pads 92, 94, 96, 98 and connector blocks 12, 14, 16, 18 each have a plurality of terminals 106 which engage when the radio 34 is mounted in the receptacle 32.
