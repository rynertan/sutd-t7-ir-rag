# COUPLING MEMBER FOR A WHEEL SUPPORT OF A VEHICLE AND DRIVING DEVICE FOR SUCH A WHEEL SUPPORT

## Abstract
A coupling member 7, 10, 11, 12 is shown for fastening to a wheel support 2 6 of a car, which member has an oblong hole 8 located in the line symmetry of a V shaped recess and a connecting rod 12 parallel to said line, so that by tightening only one bolt or mutter a perfect mounting and positioning of the member is possible with respect to the rotational axis 1 of the wheel support, with the connecting rod always being located exactly above said rotational axis.
