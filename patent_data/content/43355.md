# Combine harvester having an axial flow threshing and separating unit.

## Abstract
A combine harvester 12 comprises an axial flow threshing and separating unit 9 which includes a threshing and separating cylinder 10 disposed with its axis lying in a longitudinal vertical plane. The combine harvester 1 is provided with means 14 for feeding the harvested crop tangentially to the said axial flow threshing and separating unit 9 .
