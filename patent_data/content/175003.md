# ADJUSTABLE INTERIOR PIPE CUTTER

## Abstract
A pipe cutter is provided whereby a pipe or workpiece is cut from the inside out said cut accomplished by rotating cutters that are mounted on the ends of pivoted sliding guides wherein the guides are pivoted at the end opposite to that of the cutters and the pivoting action expands the end of the guides wherein the cutters are attached and by driv ing the pivoted sliding guides by a rotating motion the inside wall of the workpiece will be cut.
