# CABINET

## Abstract
An improved cabinet for television receivers and the like comprises a main body 1 and an escutcheon panel 3 . The panel 3 is provided with a plurality of flanges 4 around its periphery and which extend rearwardly therefrom. A projec tion 5 is provided at the forward edge of each flange 4 , while a recess 2 is formed in an inner wall of the cabinet main body 1 and arranged to receive the projections 5 so that the escutcheon panel 3 is readily fixed to the cabinet main body 1 through a simple operation.
