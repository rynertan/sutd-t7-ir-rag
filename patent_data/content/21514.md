# Original feeding procedure for a copying machine and device for carrying out said procedure.

## Abstract
Original feed device comprising a holder 1 for stacking originals, means 2, 2a, 2b for removing the bottom original from the holder, a first transport path 3 for transporting the original from the holder to the inlet side of an illumination station 4 with the side which faces downward in the holder directed to the illumination station, means 9, 10, 14 up to 21 for transporting the original from the exit side of the illumination station to a reversing device 22 and from the reversing device again to the inlet side of the illumination station, and a second transport path 33 for returning the original to the top of the holder. The original feed device enables automatic copying of originals which contain, on both sides, text to be copied.
