# Tobacco composition containing apocynum venetum or an extract thereof, and process to prepare it.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a tobacco composition comprising tobacco and bluish dogbane in the form of bluish dogbane leaves or bluish dogbane extract, a process for mak ing the tobacco composition and a process for producing bluish dogbane extract. The tobacco composition of the pre sent invention is useful to alleviate and cure chronic bronchi tis caused by smoking.
