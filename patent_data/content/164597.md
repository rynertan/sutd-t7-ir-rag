# Disc case.

## Abstract
A disc case in constructed for receiving a disc on which information data are recorded and for supporting the disc rotatably in the disc case 35 . The disc case 35 can be set into a reproducing apparatus with the disc 31 received in the disc case. The disc case 35 receives the disc releasably. When set into the reproducing apparatus, while the disc case 35 is fixed in position within the reproducing apparatus, the disc 31 is driven in rotation and thus the information data are reproduced by the reproducing apparatus. The disc case 35 comprises a disc case carrying mechanism for carrying the disc case in cooperation with the reproducing apparatus so as to be loaded or unloaded to the reproducing apparatus.
