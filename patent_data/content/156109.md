# Thermoplastic polymer blend composition and membrane formed therefrom.

## Abstract
A blend of chlorinated polyethylene with two or more different ethylene vinyl acetate copolymers is provided suit able for use in single ply roofing membranes. One ethylene vinyl acetate copolymer has a polymerized vinyl acetate con tent of 38 to 60 percent by weight of copolymer. The second has a polymerized vinyl acetate content of 13 to 27 percent of weight of copolymer.
