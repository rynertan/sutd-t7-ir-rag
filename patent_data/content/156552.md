# Interlocking universal joint seal assembly.

## Abstract
An interlocking seal assembly 25 for a universal joint which acts both to seal the bearing assembly as well as a retention means to prevent a bearing block from falling off the spider in the joint. This assembly includes a resilient sealing member 27 engaging a trunnion 17 and having a backing ring 32 housing the seal and adapted to be press fitted into the bearing block 21, 22 on the trunnion and a loose fitting generally cylindrical shield 36 adapted to be press fitted onto a diameter of the spider 15 . The backing ring 32 and shield 36 have overlapping lips 34, 37 providing the interlock to retain the bearing block on the spider.
