# Releasable fastening device.

## Abstract
A fastener for a brief case comprises a hasp 18 secured inside a lid 11 of the case and a movable hook 21 for hold ing the case closed. Movement of the hook to permit open ing of the case is controlled by a bolt 23 which, when the case is inverted, is held in a fastening position by a ball 31. When the lid is uppermost and when the side walls bearing the fastening device are uppermost, the ball is urged into a position where it is clear of the bolt.
