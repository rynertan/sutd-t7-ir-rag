# Magnetic recording disc cartridge.

## Abstract
A magnetic recording disc cartridge so called a floppy disc comprising a cover jacket member 1 enclosing a magnetic recording disc with a cleaning sheet 6 disposed between the inside face of the cover jacket member 1 and the magnetic recording disc, an improvement comprises ther mally bonding portions for bonding the sheet member 4 and the cleaning sheet 6 , in which the bonding portions are a plurality of dots 13 formed around the peripheral portion of a central window 8 along a plurality of lines radially extending from the peripheral edge of the central window 8 with a predetermined space.
