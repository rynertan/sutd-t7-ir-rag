# POLYACRYLATE THICKENING AGENTS

## Abstract
A novel polyacrylate thickening agent is composed of monomers comprising 1 meth acrylic acid, 2 glyceryl allyl ether derivative and optionally 3 one or more other polymerisable monomers. Liquids are thickened therewith. A print paste contains the novel polyacrylate thickening agent.
