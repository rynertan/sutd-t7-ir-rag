# Device for releasing conical connectors.

## Abstract
A device for releasing conical coupling connections includ ing handle means 10 including opposing jaws 16 which define wedge shaped cross sections. Preferably, the jaws 16 define a central semi circular portion 20 adapted to engage the circumference of the conical connector 22 and defining a cross section of a truncated cone. The handle means 10 may include a scissor like body or a unitary U shaped body.
