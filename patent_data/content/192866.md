# Apparatus for a continuous casting facility.

## Abstract
Continuous casting facilities generally including a pair of moulds with a rail on each side thereof. Self propelled tundish cars each carrying a tundish 2 with a detachable immersion nozzle 11 ride on the rails 4 . The apparatus includes a truck 5 running on the rail opposite the operator s side of the moulds connectable with one of the tundish cars and carrying an immersion nozzle exchanger 8 . A connecting piece feeder, a powder feeder 7 , and an immersion nozzle preheater are also mountable on the truck. Equipment interferences are eliminated by the specific structures and the operator s view of the moulds is not obstructed.
