# Tablet hardness tester.

## Abstract
Apparatus 10 for measuring tablet hardness, which com prises ram and anvil means 12 means 13 for supporting a tablet in a position such that it can be fractured between the ram and anvil means and means for measuring the force sufficient to fracture the tablet characterised in that the supporting means comprises a plurality of spaced apart, elongate support members past which when horizontal fractured pieces of the tablet can fall past the support members.
