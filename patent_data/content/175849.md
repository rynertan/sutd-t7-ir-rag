# Aerosol paint.

## Abstract
An aqueous aerosol coating composition consists of a an ammonium or amine salt of a drying oil modified copolymer of butyl acrylate and isobutyl methacrylate having an average molecular weight of not more than about 9,000 b a water soluble organic solvent c water and d dimethyl ether. The coating composition is non flammable and has good storage stability.
