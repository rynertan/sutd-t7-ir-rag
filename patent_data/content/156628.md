# Smoking article with a wrapper including an expanded web.

## Abstract
A cigarette has a tobacco rod 12 surrounded by a wrap per which comprises an inner layer 15, an outer layer 19, and a honeycomb structure 11 between the inner and outer lay ers. The honeycomb 11 gives stiffness to the wrapper and is permeable only in the radial direction. Filter 16 is attached by tipping paper 18.
