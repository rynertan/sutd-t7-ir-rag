# Chromatographic device having modified edges and diagnostic kit incorporating it.

## Abstract
A chromatographic material for use in a chromatographic system wherein a component of a mixture is partitioned be tween a liquid phase and an immobile phase, the component traversing at least a portion of the chromatographic material. The chromatographic device generally has at least one lon gitudinal edge substantially corresponding to the direction of traverse of the component, and the longitudinal edge includes means, such as indentations or a chemical substance, for controlling the shape of the front of the traversing component.
