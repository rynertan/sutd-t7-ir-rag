# Ultrasonic diagnostic apparatus.

## Abstract
An ultrasonic diagnostic apparatus including a com pound scanner 28 , a transmitting and receiving control circuit 12 , a digital scanning converter 24 , a display section 26 , a mode change over switch 44 , a position signal generator 46 and a heart beat synchronizing signal generator 48 , whereby operation of the change over switch makes it possible to display dynamic picture images or still picture images at optional time phases for both real time sector scanning and compound sector scanning.
