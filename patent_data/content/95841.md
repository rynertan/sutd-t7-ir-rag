# Clutch actuation arrangement for a motor vehicle transmission.

## Abstract
A clutch actuation arrangement for a motor vehicle transmission incorporates an annular hydraulic slave cy linder 16 surrounding a gearbox input shaft 13. The slave cylinder has an integral outward projection 17 extending through a passage between the front face of a gearbox cas ing 15 and the rear face of a bell housing. The main hydrau lic supply line 27 connects to the outer end of the projection outside the gearbox and bell housing assembly, facilitating the establishment of hydraulic connections. A bleed screw not shown is also connected to the outer end of projection 17 to facilitate bleeding.
