# SPACE HEATING APPARATUS AND VALVE ASSEMBLIES THEREFOR

## Abstract
A double line central heating system is provided with separate flow and return valve assemblies 16, 18 for each radiator. Each valve assembly comprises a bypass 91, 51 in a horizontally elongated housing portion for connection to the flow and return lines 12, 14 respectively. Modern flexible tubing of soft thin walled copper or steel or of plastics may be used for the flow and return lines and all joints are readily accessible joints underfloor or otherwise inaccessible are avoided.
