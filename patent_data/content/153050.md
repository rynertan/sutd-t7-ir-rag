# System for controlling an electromagnetic clutch.

## Abstract
A system for controlling an electromagnetic clutch has a computer 54 which produces a clutch engaging and clutch disengaging engine reference speed signals in accordance with vehicle speed in a low vehicle speed range. Engine speed from sensor 51 is compared with the engine speed reference signals by a comparator 55. The clutch is engaged at a higher engine speed than a clutch engaging speed and disengaged at a lower engine speed than a clutch disengaging speed in accordance with the result of the comparison.
