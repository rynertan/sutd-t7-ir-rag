# Scanning optics copier with adjustable copy pitch.

## Abstract
A reproduction machine having a scanning device for projecting images of documents onto a moving photosensitive surface wherein the control of the reproduction machine is a function of the size of the copy sheets. The copy sheets from a supply actuate a timing switch to determine the feed length of the copy sheet. A timer responsive to the switch converts the lengths of the copy sheet into a scan drive signal used to terminate the scan drive in accordance with the length of the copy sheet.
