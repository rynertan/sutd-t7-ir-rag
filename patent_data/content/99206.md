# Method of cooling a mould.

## Abstract
A method of cooling a blank mould which is used for making a parison in a glass container forming machine. The mould is provided with air passages 22 which communi cate with air passages 40 in a baffle 36 of the mould when the baffle 36 is in position on the mould and air is blown through the passages 40 in the baffle 36 into the passages 22 in the mould to cool the mould. The method may be used whether the parison is formed by blowing or by pressing. A baffle for use in the method is also claimed.
