# Xerographic copying apparatus.

## Abstract
A xerographic copying apparatus including a device for monitoring the concentration of toner in a xerographic devel oper composed of a mixture of ferromagnetic particles and non ferromagnetic toner, said device comprising an measur ing induction coil 27 that is mounted close to the developer supply 21 so that its magnetic field traverses a portion of the mass of the developer, and a reference induction coil 28 that is mounted close to the measuring coil, but remote of the de veloper supply, and suitably closely against the hopper 24 for the toner supply.
