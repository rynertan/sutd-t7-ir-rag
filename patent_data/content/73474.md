# Ink ribbon cartridge.

## Abstract
An ink ribbon cartridge comprises a cartridge housing 1 , an endless length of ink ribbon 7 received in the housing 1 , and a take up roller 8 and a pinch roller 9 for transport ing the ribbon 7 and guiding it in a repetitively folded manner. At least one of said take up and pinch roliers 8, 9 consists of a compressed body of flexible or semi rigid polyurethane foam. The use of compressed flexible or semi rigid polyure thane foam results in improved rollers which have the good rib bon driving performance and driving stability.
