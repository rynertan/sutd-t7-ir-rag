# A COMPOSITION AND PROCESS FOR TREATING HAIR

## Abstract
The composition comprises a water soluble bisulfite salt, a cationic surfactant penetrating agent, unsubstituted or substituted imidazolidinedione, and a water soluble organic acid salt buffered to a pH between 5.0 6.5. The process for treating hair comprises applying said composition to the hair, shaping the hair and, after an appropriate time, rinsing the hair with water.
