# Method for unloading bulk freight from a cargo body or container as well as cargo body or container for carrying out the method.

## Abstract
A method for unloading bulk freight from a cargo body or container 3 or other storage space provided internally with a foil 4. The bulk freight is unloaded through an under edge of the bottom 10 of the cargo body or container 3. Before filling a continuous foil web 4 is provided at least over the bottom 10 and a portion of the front wall opposite of the unloading edge or side wall. The foil web 4 is laid down over the bottom in a sequence of folds 5. The foil 4 is pulled outwards over the unloading edge for unloading the cargo.
