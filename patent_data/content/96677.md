# An automatic scanning device and its control for opto mechanical processing applications.

## Abstract
An automatic scanning device suited for printing, cartog raphy and medical imaging, which comprises a light source 2 optical components 3, 5 a control computer 28 a linear motor 16 , and a solid state commutator 26 . The scanning carriage is driven by the linear motor 16 which is fed with a current controlled on a preprogrammed way by the computer 28 .
