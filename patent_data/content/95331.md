# Plunger type valve.

## Abstract
A three way valve assembly has a reciprocable valve plunger 42 movably controlled by a solenoid 24 . The valve housing 14 has an elastomeric orifice member 51 seated therein, which orifice member controls flow of pressure fluid from a source. The valve plunger 42 has an enlarged nondeformable valve surface 46 thereon which engages a projecting end portion of the orifice member for sealingly closing off the orifice.
