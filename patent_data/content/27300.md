# High voltage cable having a polyethylene containing insulation sheath which is provided with means to avoid or impede the formation or the growth of watertrees.

## Abstract
A high voltage cable comprising a conductor and a polythene containing insulation sheath 2 is provided with a metal complex of a diketone, salicylic acid optionally substi tuted with 1 to 2 lower alkyl groups, or a Schiff s base formed from an amine and salicylaldehyde optionally substituted with 1 to 2 lower alkyl groups, in order to prevent or impede the growth of water trees during use.
