# A WIRE HOLDING JIG FOR APPARATUS WHICH INSTALLS ELECTRICAL WIRES INTO A CONNECTOR ASSEMBLY AND A METHOD OF MANUFACTURING THE JIG

## Abstract
A wire holding jig is disclosed, for apparatus which installs electrical wires into a connector, and a method of manufacturing the jig is disclosed, in which a metal blank 1 , has a a row of apertures 4 formed by etching. A ridge 12 at a bend in the blank 1 is intercepted by the apertures 4 thereby providing wire holding passage ways. The peripheries 6 of the apertures are whet to keen edges that trim to length, any wires 54 laid into the passageways.
