# Toothbrushes.

## Abstract
This invention relates to toothbrushes of the kind in which a head 2 having bristles B projecting from at least one side thereof is articulated to an adjacent end of a handle 1 for adjustment of the head with respect to the handle. The articulating means of the invention include a pair of projections 7,8 integral with either the head 2 or the handle 1 and journalled in cooperating sockets 9,10 in the other part, and means 11, 12, 14, 15, 16, are provided for resisting rela tive turning movement between the head and handle, whereby the head and handle are adapted to be set in a desired position relatively to one another without being locked in said position.
