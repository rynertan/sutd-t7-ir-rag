# SOLID FUEL OIL MIXTURES

## Abstract
Fuel composition comprises a 15 to 60 by weight, preferably 40 to 55 , of a friable solid fuel, e.g. coal, b a stabilising additive composition and c a fuel oil. Tile additive comprises the combination of a polymer containing functional groups, e.g., maleinised polybuta diene, and a surfactant. The composition is suitable for use as a liquid fuel for industrial burners.
