# APPARATUS FOR STORING, CARRYING AND SHAKING DOWN A CLINICAL THERMOMETER

## Abstract
An apparatus is provided for storing, protecting, car rying and shaking down a thermometer. The apparatus in cludes hollow generally cylindrical base and cover mem bers which are engageable with one another and are adapt ed to enclose a thermometer. An elastic member extends through and is secured to the cover. An elastic retention member and cap grasp the elastic member adjacent the top of the apparatus. The cap can be grasped by thumb and forefinger to enable the remainder of the apparatus to be ro tated around the elastic member.
