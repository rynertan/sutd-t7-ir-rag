# Improved drum brake.

## Abstract
An improved expanding shoe drum brake 10 is pro vided. The brake includes a shaft like mounting structure 42 for retaining the hook ends 40 of a heavy duty brake return spring 28 . The retaining assembly includes a fixed portion 44 and an axially movable portion 46 . The axially movable portion is normally abutting with the fixed portion but may be selectively axially moved away therefrom for assembly and disassembly of return spring 28 from the brake assembly 10 .
