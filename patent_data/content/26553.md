# Self aligning optical fiber connector.

## Abstract
An optical fiber connector having two half round holding members 40 mounted in a central coupler 10 . The half round members 40 have flat mating surfaces 42 with an opposed longitudinal V groove 44 for receiving fiber ends 56 of either similar or dis simiiar diameters. The opposed fibers 56 spread the holding members away from each other an elastic member 52 retains the holding members in opposed relationship.
