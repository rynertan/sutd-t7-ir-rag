# Air conditioning apparatus.

## Abstract
An air conditioning machine having, in blocks within the same box body, an indoor air circulation wind passage, an outdoor air circulation wind passage, a ventilation wind passage. An opposite flow heat exchanger, designed to recover the heat in the ventilation wind passage, is provided. One of the opposite two faces having two wind passage openings in one face of the opposite flow heat exchanger faces the outdoor air circulation wind passage. The other thereof faces the indoor air circulation wind passage. And shutters or dampers are provided on both of the faces. Functions of cooling heating, heating exchange ventilation, simultaneous feed exhaust non heat exchange ventilation can be exhibited.
