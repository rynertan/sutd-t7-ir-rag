# Discharge containment device for incontinent males and method for forming same.

## Abstract
An absorbent pouch 40 for the male genitalia has a generally triangular opening wherein one side 42 of the triangle is adapted to be worn flat against the upper genital region and the other two portions of the triangular opening extend into the crotch and meet behind the scrotum 50 . The pouch 40 extending downward from the triangular opening has sufficient capacity to comfortably contain the male genitalia 48, 50 and the opening at the top is sufficient to allow air circulation. The allowance for air circulation decreases skin problems.
