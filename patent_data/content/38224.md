# Composite material for ozone removal.

## Abstract
A composite material 10 is provided for the removal of ozone from a gaseous stream 15 which material is made of a support matrix 11 having coated on its surface an adher ing layer of fine particles 12 of hopcalite. The support matrix is preferably made of a material that has a relatively broad transition region. A particularly preferred material for the matrix is a polymeric ethyl vinylacetate.
