# Silver halide photographic light sensitive material.

## Abstract
A silver halide photographic light sensitive material characterized in including at least one of the layers containing a hydroxytetrazaindene compound represented by the following Formula I or II on a paper support covered with a resin hardened by irradiating an electron beam. Formula I CHEM wherein R1 and R2 represent hydrogen, an aliphatic group or an aromatic group, respectively and n is an integer of 1 or 2.
