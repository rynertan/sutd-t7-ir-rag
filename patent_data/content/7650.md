# Handling apparatus for dual in line packed DIP integrated circuits.

## Abstract
A DIP handling apparatus comprising a support chassis 12 , an input tray 14 attached to the support chassis, a metering and reorienting device 16 provided for receiving a DIP from said input tray and discharging the DIP to a test head station 18 , an output tray 20 attached to the support chas sis, and a shuttle device 22 for transporting the DIP from the test station to a particular track of the output tray as deter mined by the result of the tests performed at the test station.
