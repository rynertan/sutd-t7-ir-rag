# ROLLER GUIDE FOR SUSPENDED VERTICAL PANELS

## Abstract
The roller guide for suspended vertical panels 1, 1 comprises a frame 6 , provided with sliding wheels 7 along a horizontal rail 8 placed on the upper of a wardrobe or the ceil ing of a space, and a jointing bar 10 placed between the frame and the panel 1 . In the frame there are two stops 12, 12 against which the bar 10 , moved by a spring 11 placed between the bar and the frame themselves, elastically leans near two symmetric extreme positions. In these posi tions the jointing axis bar 10 panel 1 is alternatively ad vanced or backward with respect to the jointing axis bar 10 frame 6 .
