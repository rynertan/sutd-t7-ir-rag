# Weaving machine.

## Abstract
A shedding device 9 for a loom L , having a plurality of thread separator discs 10 , inner abutments 30 on one side of each disc 10 , outer abutments 32 on a face of the disc 10 , subtending a predetermined arc, on which a thread T1, T2 may be raised, a central opening 22 through the disc 10 , a spring 39 extending into the opening 22 , grooves 54, 56 on the disc 10 for receiving the inner 30 and outer abutments 32 of the next disc, a plurality of openings 25 formed through the disc and spokes 24 defined by the openings 25 at spaced apart locations.
