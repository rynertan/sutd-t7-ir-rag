# Pilferproof closure.

## Abstract
A screw on plastics closure for a container 8 has a cap 1 and a locking ring 4 for securing the closure to the container 8 in use to prevent unscrewing of the cap 1 from the container 8 . The cap 1 is integrally connec ted to the locking ring 4 by means of a plurality of brid ges 11 which extend from the adjacent part of the cap 1 to the ring 4 at an angle inclined in the direction of unscrewing of the cap 1 .
