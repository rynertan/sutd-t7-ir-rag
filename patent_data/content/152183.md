# Printed circuit board load unload system and method.

## Abstract
A printed circuit board load unload system for transfer ring printed circuit boards to and from a printed circuit board processing portion 1 comprises a magazine stock portion 3 which conveys a plurality of magazines M each of which houses a plurality of printed circuit boards P along on a conveyor route and a load unload portion 4 where a printed circuit board is transferred between the magazine in the magazine stock portion 3 and the printed circuit board processing portion 1 . The conveyor route of the stock portion includes a bypass route 8 which bypasses the load unload portion.
