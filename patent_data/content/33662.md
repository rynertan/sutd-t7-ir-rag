# PAPERMAKERS FELT AND METHOD OF MANUFACTURE

## Abstract
A composite papermakers felt made up of a textile base layer 12 and an upper layer 14 of a polymeric resin foam is provided. The foam consists of strips laid side by side with the lengthwise direction of the strips fixed at an angle to the direction of both the machine direction and the cross machine direction yarns in the base layer. The felt is useful in the wet press section of a papermaking machine. A method of man ufacturing the felt of the invention is also described.
