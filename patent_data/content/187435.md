# Lawnmower.

## Abstract
For use with a powered weedcutter a having an operating shaft 1 with a motor 2 at one end and a rotary cutting blade 3 at the other end arranged to rotate in a horizontal plane in use, a device b for converting the weedcutter to a lawnmower, such device comprising a housing 11 mountable to the said other end of the weedcutter to enclose the said cutting blade, a grass discharge port 15 in said housing, and a pair of front wheels 14 mounted on said housing so as to be vertically adjustable relative thereto.
