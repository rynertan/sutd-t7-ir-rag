# A method for producing cyclopropanecarboxylic acid derivatives.

## Abstract
A method for producing 2,2,3,3 tetramethyl cyclopropane 1 carboxylic acid and intermediate com pounds thereof. The 2,2,3,3 tetramethylcyclopropane 1 carboxylic acid is a very useful intermediate for synthesizing insecticidal and acaricidal compounds of pyrethroid type.
