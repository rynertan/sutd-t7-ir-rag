# Rotary latch.

## Abstract
A rotary latch including a hook arm 11 joined to a drive sleeve 13 having an end flush with a surrounding surface, the sleeve 13 having a tool receiving socket 14 also receiv ing a reciprocal signal shaft 19 occupying a flush position when the latch is secured but automatically movable to an extended position or retracted position to signal that the latch is not secured.
