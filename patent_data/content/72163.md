# Apparatus for continuously discharging dough.

## Abstract
An apparatus for continuously discharging dough through a gap 9 to produce a dough strip 15 . Dough 16 supplied in a hopper 1 is advanced by a conveyor 2 serving as the moving floor of the hopper toward said gap which is de fined between a lower portion of a side wall of the hopper and a part of said conveyor, a cutter 10 being provided along the top of the gap and directed inwardly of the hopper. Thus dough can be smoothly discharged through the said gap without jam ming it.
