# Vacuum transfer device.

## Abstract
The vacuum transfer device comprises a central proces sing chamber 2 and a plurality of additional chambers 11 in vacuum tight connection therewith. Inside the central chamber 2 is mounted a rotable coulisse arrangement 21 driven by motors 22,33 located outside the chamber 2 and extendable so as to reach into the additional chambers 11 when correctly aligned. The device serves to transfer objects between manufacturing or measuring work stations without intermediate venting and re evacuation of the system of chambers.
