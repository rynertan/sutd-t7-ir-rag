# Magnetic rolls and a method of making the same.

## Abstract
A magnetic roll 7 having a plurality of magnets 2, 3a 3e integrally set fast with a retaining member 4 at stated portions of the periphery of a roll shaft 1 thereby forming a magnetic force generating part, in which the retaining member 4 is made from a rigid synthetic resin or resin foam, and optionally a strain absorbing groove 5 is provided at a portion outside of said magnetic force generating part.
