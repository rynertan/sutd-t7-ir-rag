# Disposable syringe.

## Abstract
A disposable syringe has a barrel 2 , hub 3 and needle 4 with a plunger rod protector 12 attached to the outer suface 6 of the barrel near the hub by flexible protrusions 16 from the inner surface of a broader end section 14 of the plunger rod protector. The flexible protrusions frictionally engage the outer surface 6 . Also the plunger rod protector itself, with the flexible protrusions from the inner surface of a broader end section thereof for gripping the outer surface of the barrel when present .
