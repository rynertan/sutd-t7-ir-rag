# GENE CODING FOR THERMOSTABLE BETA GALACTOSIDASE, BACILLUS SUBTILIS HAVING THE GENE, ENZYME CODED BY THE GENE AND A PROCESS FOR THE PRODUCTION THEREOF

## Abstract
A novel gene coding for thermostable β galactosidase, novel recombinant DNA in which DNA fragment containing the above gene is inserted, novel Bacillus subtilis in which the above recombinant DNA is introduced, and novel thermostable β galactosidase obtained by cultivating the above Bacillus subtilis.
