# An irrigation sleeve.

## Abstract
An irrigation sleeve 16 for colostomy patients 14 who have a stoma, comprising a pair of ends 26, 28 , at least one 26 of which is closed, and a pair of openings 30, 32 located between said ends 26, 28 , one of said openings 30 being sized and shaped so as to receive the stoma and the other opening 32 being movable between a first posi tion in which it is aligned with said one opening 30 and a second position in which it is not aligned with said one opening 30 .
