# Stabilisable optical sights and tank gun control system incorporating such an optical sight.

## Abstract
Stabilisable optical gun sight including an optical system having an eye piece 1 and an objective lens 2 and between the eyepiece and objective lens, two optical wedges 8,9 which are arranged to be driven differentially and together in order to deflect the line of sight through the optical system in response to servo control signals 6 applied to the driving means.
