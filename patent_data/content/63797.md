# Filter apparatus.

## Abstract
A filter apparatus for filtering a liquid containing solid materials comprises a filter member 46 made of a flexible sheet having a number of through holes 48 which prevent passage of solid materials while allow passage of the liquid thereby filtering the liquid, first wipers 36 which are slidably arranged over the surface of the filter member at the liquid inlet side and which scrape off the solid materials deposited on the surface of the filter member upon sliding movement thereof second wipers 60 which are slidably arranged over the surface of the filter member at the liquid outlet side and driving mechanism 39 for sliding the first and second wipers on the surfaces of the filter member at the same speed in the same direction so that the first and second wipers oppose each other with the filter member disposed therebetween.
