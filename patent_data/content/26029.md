# Fuel assemblies for nuclear reactors.

## Abstract
A nuclear fuel assembly 10 includes a sleeve 100 that surrounds but is not attached to a guide tube 14 . The sleeve 100 is attached to a wall of a cellular void 24 of a spacer grid 15 , through which void the guide tube 14 extends. The sleeve 100 has tabs 101 projecting outwardly therefrom to modify the flow of a coolant.
