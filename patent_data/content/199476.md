# ALKALINE ELECTRIC STORAGE CELLS

## Abstract
A method of assemblying an alkaline electric storage cell wherein, subsequent to the step of arranging positive and negative plates and separators to form a pack 11 , a plurality of plate lugs 12 13 of the same polarity are introduced between the open limbs of a U shaped clip 14 , the two limbs of the clip 14 are pressed towards one another to trap between them the selected lugs, and an aperture 15 is formed through the trapped lugs.
