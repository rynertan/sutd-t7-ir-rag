# Solid state lithium cell.

## Abstract
Disclosed is a solid state lithium cell comprising layers of a cathode active material 1 , a solid electrolyte 2 , and an anode active material 3 consisting of lithium metal, which are arranged in this order, which is characterized in that said cathode active material 1 is a solid solution composed of lead iodide and at least one element selected from the group consisting of bismuth, antimony, thallium, indium and gallium and iodides thereof.
