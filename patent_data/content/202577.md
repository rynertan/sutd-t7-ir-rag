# N Acyldipeptidyl aminoglycols.

## Abstract
The invention describes novel N acyldipeptidyl aminoglycols of the formula CHEM wherein R1 is alkoxy containing one to six carbon atoms or lower alkyl containing one to six carbon atoms R2 is benzyl or napthylmethyl, R3 is lower alkyl containing one to six carbon atoms or imidazolemethyl R4 is benzyl, R5 is hydrogen or lower alkyl and n is 0 or 1. These compounds are useful as renin inhibitors.
