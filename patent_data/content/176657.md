# Muffler for exhaust gas from internal combustion engine.

## Abstract
A muffler for exhaust gases of an internal combustion engine. The muffler is a hybrid consisting of gas expansion chambers 16, 17, 18 defined by partitions 11, 12 in a muffler casing 2 , and a sound absorbing body 15 . The sound absorbing body 15 includes a perforated pipe 3 surrounded by a porous sound absorbing material 7 with a thin metal film 9 sandwiched therebetween.
