# ENDOSCOPE PARTICULARLY USEFUL AS AN ANOSCOPE

## Abstract
An endoscope device particularly useful as an anoscope, includes three sections 11, 12, 13 each of the configuration of a segment of a cylinder. Two 11, 12 of the sections are pivotable to each other to a tubular position wherein the three sections 11, 12, 13 define a tubular endoscope insertable into a body cavity for examination, or to an open position wherein the two 11, 12 pivotable sections define a retractor for re tracting tissue in the body cavity to facilitate performing a clinical treatment therein. The device further includes a man drel 4 receivable within the sections 11, 12, 13 of the endo scope when in their tubular positions to facilitate the insertion of the endoscope into the body cavity.
