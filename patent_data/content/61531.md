# A pipe coupling.

## Abstract
A pipe coupling for connecting pipes as fluid conduits comprises a split clamping ring 17 which is of arcuated configuration in cross section and has a pair of spaced circular sharp edges 19a, 19b directed toward the pipe for firm engagement therewith and which is disposed in an annular groove 12 formed internally of a body 10 so as to be slightly displaceable relatively to the groove 12 in an axial direction of the body 10 when a certain axial force is applied to the pipe 21a, 21b a deformable sealing ring 30 disposed in another annular groove 13 formed internally of the body 10 and a plurality of depressing members 15 provided for adjustably compressing the clamping ring 17 diametrically.
