# Method and apparatus for the manufacture of a screen roller.

## Abstract
A method for manufacturing a loose screen stencil roller showing at its cylindrical outer surface a pattern of indenta tions, comprising the steps of starting with a smooth walled support cylinder 1 being either a hollow thick walled roller or a massive roller, upon which a thin walled sieve 2 is pushed, after which the sieve 2 is clampingly secured upon said cylinder 1 the invention also deals with an apparatus for applying this method comprising a support cylinder 1 with fluid passages 10, 11, 14 enabling the mounting and removal of the sieve 2 around its outer periphery.
