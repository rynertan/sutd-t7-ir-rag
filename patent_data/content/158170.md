# Erythromycin A salt with mucosecretolytic and fluidizing activity, a process for its preparation and pharmaceutical compositions thereof.

## Abstract
The erythromycin salt of 5 3 carboxy 1 oxopropoxy α,α, 4 trimethyl 3 cyclohesene 1 methanol acid is described. This salt shows a mucosecretolytic and fluidizing activity. There is further described a process for the preparation of said salt, as well as pharmaceutical compositions with mucose cretolytic and fluidizing action which contain the novel salt.
