# Doubled ended throwing knife.

## Abstract
An elongate blade 11 formed on its oppooosite ends with points 13, 15 and including first, second and third pairs of bores 19,18, 21 disposed respectively at one end of such blade, centrally on such blade and at the opposite end of such blade. A handle 17 is received telescopically on the blade and includes a latch for selectively engaging the respective bores to selectively latch the handle centrally with both points exposed for convenient throwing or for latching at one or other of the opposite ends of the blade.
