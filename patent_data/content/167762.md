# Silver halide color photographic material.

## Abstract
A silver halide color photographic material is disclosed which has on a support at least one silver halide emulsion layer containing a non diffusible coupler, wherein a com pound of formula I is incorporated in either said silver halide emulsion layer or a layer adjacent thereto or both R₁ O R₂ I wherein R₁ and R₂ each represents an alkyl group, a cycloalkyl group or an alkenyl group.
