# Building system.

## Abstract
Building system in which the supporting elements are formed by partitions or plates 1 16 one group of plates 1, 2, 3, 4 being placed in spaced apart parallel vertical planes with all plates 1, 2, 3, 4 of said group having the same width in horizontal direction which width cor responds to the width of a cupboard the plates 1, 2, 3, 4 of said group being placed between at least two vertical plates 9, 10 which extend perpendicular to the plates of the first group. Preferably a third plate 18 or group of plates is placed in a horizontal plane be tween the vertical plates.
