# Static mixer and method of mixing fluids.

## Abstract
A static mixer comprises a passage for the flow there through of a liquid or a suspension of particulate solid in a liquid, said passage having in the direction of flow a lengthwise divergent portion 2 followed by a lengthwise convergent portion 3 . An inlet 4 for fluent material to be mixed with the supension may converge with and project into the divergent portion. Cement and coal slurry may be mixed in this apparatus.
