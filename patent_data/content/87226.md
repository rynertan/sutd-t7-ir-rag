# High temperature seat and valve therefor.

## Abstract
A valve seat for use in a fluid control valve employing a pivotable valve closure element 22 to open and close the valve, the seat comprising an annular polymeric member provided with a seating surface 61 which sealingly engages the valve element when the valve is in the closed position, the seat further including a multiplicity of circumferential windings of strands of material 102 wrapped around said annular member.
