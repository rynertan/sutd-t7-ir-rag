# CONTAINER HAVING AN EASY OPENING END WITH A TAB AFFIXED BY ULTRASONIC WELDING

## Abstract
A container having an easy opening end with a tab 1 affixed by welding and a method of its production is provided. In the easy opening end the top wall 5 is made of surface treated steel sheet and the tab 1 is made of stainless steel sheet or tin free steel sheet and the ultrasonic welding is used to fix the tab 1 to the top wall 5 . Through the invention monometalized containers can be supplied easily and cheaply.
