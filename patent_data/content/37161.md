# Creamy cleansing compositions.

## Abstract
A creamy cleansing composition comprises the follow ing four components A one or more phosphoric ester surfactants, B an organic or inorganic salt, C polyethylene glycol and D an ethylene oxide addition type non ionic surface active agent or a cationic surface active agent. The cleansing composition is gentle to the skin or hair, and stable without separation for a long period of time,
