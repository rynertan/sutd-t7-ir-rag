# Workpiece loading apparatus.

## Abstract
A workpiece loading apparatus in a rig for testing aircraft wings, for example, comprises a double ended servo jack 15 which actuates an upper drive lever 18 coupled by a tension strap 26 to a lower drive lever 19 . The drive ends 22 23 of the levers are coupled to contour boards 32 mounted on the wing surfaces by whiffle trees 30, 31 so that the single jack applied both upward and downward forces to the wings.
