# OPTICAL COUPLING DEVICE ADAPTED TO A FIBRE OPTIC EXAMINING DEVICE

## Abstract
An examining or viewing device such as a laryngoscope having improved optical coupling between a light source and a light receiving face of a light conductor. The light source is provided with a reflector preferably ellipsoidal in shape which concentrates the light reflected into a converging conically shaped light beam which fall essentially in its entirety onto the light receiving surface of the light con ductor.
