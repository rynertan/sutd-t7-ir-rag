# PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS

## Abstract
Pharmaceutical compositions comprising mixtures of proglumide and polyacrylic acid or their sodium salts in a specified range of ratios have been found to exhibit synergis tic effects in an in vivo test model for anti ulcer or mucosal protecting agents. Pharmaceutical compositions comprising mixtures of proglumide and polyacrylic acid or their sodium salts in the range of ratios are described for use in the treatment of gastritis or of gastro duodenal ulcers.
