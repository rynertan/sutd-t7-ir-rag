# Stabilizer for an automotive vehicle.

## Abstract
Extensible hydraulic cylinders 34, 36 are used for at taching a stabilizer bar 26 to a vehicle body so that attach ing portions 28a, 28b of the stabilizer bar are movable to ward and away from the vehicle body as the hydraulic cylin ders extend and contract, to change the effectiveness in ac cordance with variations of the running condition of the vehicle.
