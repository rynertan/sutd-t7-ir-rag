# Coupling for an ostomy bag.

## Abstract
A coupling for an ostomy bag is described. The coupling includes a pair of cylindrical elements 14, 30 which each have a headed pin attachment means 20, 22 38, 40 mounted on a respective flange 16, 34 . One of the coupling elements 10 is adhesively attached to a wearer, and the other coupling 12 element has an ostomy bag attached to it. Thus, an ostomy bag may be readily and securely attached to a user through the use of the present invention.
