# Apparatus for receiving solar energy.

## Abstract
Apparatus for collecting solar energy comprises a collec tor 11 having a solar energy collector surface 40 . The col lector 11 is mounted on a support 20 and is pivotable between an exposed position in which the collector surface faces the sun and a substantially horizontal sheltered position in which the collector surface faces the earth, thereby protect ing the collector surface from the elements and facilitating acess thereto for maintenance.
