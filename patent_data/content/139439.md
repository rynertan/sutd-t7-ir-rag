# A new compound, FR 900447, production and use thereof.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a new compound, designated FR 900447, and pharmaceutically acceptable salts thereof. The compound has antimicrobial and antitumor activities and is effective in the treatment of various infectious diseases and tumors in both human beings and animals. There is also provided a process for preparing FR 900447 compound which comprises culturing a FR 90047 producing strain belonging to the genus Streptomyces in a nutrient medium. Pharmaceutical compositions comprising FR 900447 as an active ingredient are also described.
