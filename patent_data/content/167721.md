# Light diffuser.

## Abstract
An edge lighting type light diffuser is disclosed which has a plane of light incidence 1 , a light transmitting means 3 and a plane of light emission 2 . The light transmitting means is composed of a laminate of a plurality of light transmissible plates 5 . This structure provides a light diffuser capable of uniform plane illumination usable for sign boards, large sized display devices, display devices for various types of machinery, and liquid crystal devices.
