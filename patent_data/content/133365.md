# Ball dispenser.

## Abstract
Apparatus for dispensing balls, for example, golf or tennis balls, comprising a magazine 10 , which can be mounted to extend downwardly to receive a plurality of balls 14, 14 to be dispensed from the lower end thereof, and ball dispensing means 16 having a first condition in which the lowermost ball 14 is restrained in the magazine 10 and a second condition in which the lowermost ball 14 is released and another ball 14 is restrained in the magazine 10 , the ball dispensing means 16 thereby being operable by change between its two conditions to dispense at least the lowermost ball 14 whilst restraining at least one other ball 14 in the magazine 10 .
