# Door latch handle fittings and retainers therefor.

## Abstract
A door latch lever handle fitting is provided with a retaining member 26 which is pushed onto a shank 14 of a lever handle 12 . The member 26 has tongues 38 which engage flats 16 and tongues 32 which engage in groove portions 40 to grip the shank 14 . In other embodiments no groove portions are provided and different retaining mem bers are provided with modified gripping tongue arrange ments. The member 26 is stressed to give axial thrust to take up any residual play in the assembly. Ears 50 are engageable with abutment lug 52 so that the door handle 12 may be turned through a predetermined angle.
