# Device to apply a paper sheet to a closet seat.

## Abstract
A device for applying a paper sheet to a toilet seat 11 which includes a paper sheet container 13,53 adapted to move from a substantially vertical position to a position over the toilet seat 11 , and then back to a substantially vertical position by manually operating a tilting mechanism 51,29, 31,43,45 . A rotatable roller 57 is positioned in the container and is driven to feed a paper sheet 55 into position on the toilet seat 11 as the container 13 undergoes return movement from its position over the toilet seat 11 back to its substantially vertical position.
