# Tire with tread rubber containing medium vinyl polybutadiene with clay and carbon black.

## Abstract
A pneumatic tire with sulfur cured rubber tread composi tion containing a medium vinyl polybutadiene rubber and at least one rubber selected from cis 1,4 polyisoprene rubber, styrene butadiene copolymer rubber and cis 1,3 polybutadiene rubber. The composition also contains both carbon black and clay reinforcement in conjunction with a 3,3 bis trimethoxysilylpropyl polysulfide.
