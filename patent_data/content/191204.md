# A removable slider shoe for a translating sleeve used on a jet engine cowl and duct.

## Abstract
A removable slider shoe for mounting on a translating sleeve 12 of a jet engine cowl 10 and duct 14 . The sleeve 12 includes a slider 20 which moves in a track 22 on the cowl 10 when the engine is in a reverse thrust mode in order to expose airflow turning vanes. The removable shoe is mounted on the slider 20 .
