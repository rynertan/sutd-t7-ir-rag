# Rotor for rotary machine.

## Abstract
A rotor for rotary machines of a type having a fan 5a on a side surface of a magnetic field core 2a . The fan 5a has recessed portions 51 for holding lead wires 31 at portions of the fan 5a so that the fan 5a can be fixed to the rotary shaft 1 of the machine after making the connection between the lead wire 31 of the magnetic field core 3 and a slip ring 4 .
