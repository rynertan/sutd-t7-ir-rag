# Sound absorption for a lawnmower.

## Abstract
This disclosure relates to a sound absorbing arrange ment for a power lawnmower in which sound absorbing material 40 is provided within the body of the lawnmower to reduce noise by attenuating resonance caused by the lawnmower motor 12 . In addition to reducing noise, the sound absorbing material 40 imparts structural rigidity to the lawnmower body or housing 14 , and also may be used to define air passages 50 within the lawnmower body for use in cooling the motor 12 .
