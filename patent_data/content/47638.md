# Improvements in or relating to aircraft handling systems.

## Abstract
An aircraft handling system, particularly applicable to heli copters on board ship, said system incorporating a plurality of hydraulic winches 33, 34, 35, 63, 64, 65, 83, 84, 85 for effect ing movement of an aircraft from a first position to a second position and secondary restraining means preferably includ ing hydraulically actuated constant tension reels 41, 42, 43, 44, 72, 73, 93, 94 for controlling movement of an aircraft from said first position to said second position in order to prevent lateral movement or toppling of the aircraft.
