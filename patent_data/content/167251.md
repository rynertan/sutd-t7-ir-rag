# Process for drying aminoguanidine bicarbonate.

## Abstract
A process for drying a wet cake of aminoguanidine bicarbonate AGB , which comprises subjecting the wet cake of aminoguanidine bicarbonate to drying temperatures from about 35 C to about 95 C under a carbon dioxide containing atmosphere for a sufficient amount of time to form a dried aminoguanidine bicarbonate product which is substantially free of moisture.
