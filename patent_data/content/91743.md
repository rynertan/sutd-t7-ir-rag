# Apparatus for a camera mount.

## Abstract
In a camera mount in which a front plate 19 is attached to the front of the camera and a base plate 21 extends from the front plate beneath the camera to secure the camera bottom and to exert a predetermined supporting force upwardly on the camera to counter the moment of the camera on the front plate, an interlock 126, 127 is provided to control the operation of the securing means 86 to sequentially follow the operation of the supporting means in both securing and releasing modes.
