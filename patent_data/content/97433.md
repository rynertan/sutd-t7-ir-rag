# Apparatus for protecting electrical components in high temperature boreholes.

## Abstract
Apparatus for use in high temperature well boreholes for protecting electrical components against the high ambient temperatures therein, the apparatus comprising a fluid tight hollow body in which is a hollow support 22,32 extending longitudinally of the body and containing a heat absorbant material 23,33 distributed along its length, the member being adapted to have one or more electrical components mounted thereon. The body may include a vaccum flask 21,31 within which the support is housed.
