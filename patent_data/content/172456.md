# Irrigator device particularly for low viscosity fluids.

## Abstract
This irrigator device comprises a hollow body 2 formed with one or more inner chambers 3 communicating at the bottom with a hollow slanted spout 11 . The device body 2 is provided with two upper openings 9, 10 for admitting a fluid. To one 10 of said openings there is associated a detachable member 16 for irrigating a fluid. Furthermore between the spout 11 and the device body 3 there is provided a holder 13 which can be used as handle 14 . In this way it is possible to irrigate one or more fluids in different modes.
