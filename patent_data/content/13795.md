# Watertight seal for toggle switches.

## Abstract
A toggle switch 24 of the type used on the casing of a camera or intercom device 20 solves the problem of moisture entering the casing 21 through the switch 24 to thereby damage the elctronic circuitry, causes rust, mil dew and other damage. The exterior of the switch housing 26 is water tight by reason of a turn nut 33 compressing a O ring 28 . The interior of the toggle switch handle housing 26 is made water tight, whilst still permitting toggle motion, by an elongate seal 47 of solid cross sec tion engaging the surface of the spherical portion 37 of the handle 35 and a second elongate seal 53 of solid cross section also engaging the surface of the spherical portion 37 .
