# Propranolol immunogens, antibodies thereto, test device and compounds for propranolol immuno assay.

## Abstract
Propanol immunogens which are 3 1 naphthoxy 2 hydroxypropylamine and 3 N 2 hydroxy 3 1 naphthoxy 1 propyl aminobutyric acid coupled to an immunogenic carrier antibodies prepared therefrom, a labelled conjugate of 3 N 2 hydroxy 3 1 naphthoxy 1 propyl aminobutyric acid intermediates an immunoassay method a test kit and test device.
