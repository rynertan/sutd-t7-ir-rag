# Gas burner.

## Abstract
Gas burner for domestic cooking apparatus comprising a gas feed tube 1 , a mixing tube 9 and a burner cover 15 near the outlet of the mixing tube 9 . The outlet member is formed as an appertured flow plate 3 which is connected to the gas feed tube 1 in sealing relationship with the intermediary of a flexible ring 4 . By exchanging the flow plate 3 the burning capacity can be changed at the site of use.
