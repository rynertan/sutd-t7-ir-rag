# Tube storage and delivery device.

## Abstract
This invention provides an article for storing and dispensing tubes 14 open at two ends and containing materials 15 which are pure or must be protected from the environment or which are present in premeasured amounts. The article of this invention comprises flexible means 12 for sealing the ends of the tubes 14 , which flexible means 12 is movable within a case 5 to hold the tubes 14 sealed in the case 5 and discharge the tubes from the case. One embodiment is a cassette for tubes containing adhesive 15 for splicing optical fibers.
