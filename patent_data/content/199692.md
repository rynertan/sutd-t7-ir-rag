# Door hinge for motor vehicles or the like.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a door hinge for motor vehicles or the like, comprising two hinge halves which are pivotally joined to each other by means of a substantially cylindrical pivot pin. The first hinge half consists of a C bracket holding between its legs the pivot pin. The second hinge half 7,8 is provided with a channel 12,14 for receiving the pivot pin. According to the invention, the second hinge half 7,8 consists of a bracket portion 7 and a clamping member 8 , which is releasably joined to the bracket portion 7 by means of at least one fastener 17 . The facing surfaces of the bracket portion 7 and the clamping member 8 define together the channel 12,14 for the pivot pin when these parts are screwed to each other.
