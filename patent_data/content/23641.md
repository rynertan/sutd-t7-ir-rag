# Rolling bearing.

## Abstract
A rolling bearing comprises an inner ring 1 and an outer ring 2 each of which is provided with a tapered rolling race and one or more rows of rolling bodies 3 disposed between the said rings and apt to roll on corresponding races. The rol ling bodies 3 are defined by an outer conical surface, and the respective axis is laying on a conicai surface. The ratio be tween the axial length L and the maximum diameter 2R of each of the rolling bodies 3 is more than 3.
