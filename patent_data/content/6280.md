# Dynamometer test rig.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a dynamometer test rig for coupling to a drive sprocket of a vehicie to be tested, the dynamometer including a rotary input member 28 adapted to be rotated about an axis and having a coupling 30 in cluding at least two slides 58 movably received in guides 46 , a screw threaded shaft 62 connected to the slides 58 , the shaft being fixed against longitudinal movement to effecting simultaneous movement of the slides 58 towards or away from the axis on rotation. A detent engages the shaft 58 to holding it against rotation. A plurality of teeth 32 are mounted one on each slide 58 , each and extending therefrom in a direction generally parallel to the axis for receipt between the teeth of a sprocket.
