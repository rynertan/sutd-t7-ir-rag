# A single seat bicycle easily convertible into a two seat bicycle.

## Abstract
A one seat bicycle convertible into a two seat wherein rearwardly of the stiffening cross pieces for the rear wheel, the frame has an additional fork 16 parallel to the column supporting the normal first saddle S1 of the bicycle, said fork 16 terminating with a receiving means 18 capable of supporting an additional saddle S2 or a basket C holding different articles.
