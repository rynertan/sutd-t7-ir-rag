# 2 Aminocycloalkenyl carboxamides and their tritiated 2 aminocycloalkyl carboxamide derivatives.

## Abstract
2 Aminocycloalkenyl benzamides and phenylacet amides, e.g. 3,4 dichloro N methyl N 6 1 pyrrolidinyl 3 cyclohexenyl benzeneacetamide, and their salts, are of poten tial value as analgesics. The corresponding tritiated, saturated compounds are also new, and of utility as radio labelled com pounds for pharmacological and metabolic studies in ani mals.
