# Day and night observation apparatus.

## Abstract
Apparatus for viewing a multiplicity of images compris es first and second image generating means 11, 12 , first and second means 14, 17 for receiving optical images, and beam splitting means 15 positioned in the optical paths of images generated by the first and second image generators 11, 12 for directing the images to the first and second optical image receiving means 14, 17 , the beam splitting means 15 being operable controllably to reflect and transmit portions of a light beam incident thereon so as to vary the percentages of the incident light beam reflected and transmitted, the range of variation including substantially 0 reflection and substantially 100 transmission.
