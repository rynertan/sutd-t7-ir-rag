# Reaction products of alkenylsuccinic compounds with aromatic amines and lubricant compositions thereof.

## Abstract
Products made by reacting i an alkenylsuccinic com pound with ii an arylamine and iii an alkanolamine, an aminomethane or a hindered alcohol and borated reaction products thereof which provide superior dispersant and antioxidant activity to lubricant compositions when incorpo rated therein.
