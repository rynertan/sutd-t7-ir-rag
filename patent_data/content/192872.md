# Saw blade and tip therefor.

## Abstract
A circular saw blade 20 includes a circular body 22 with a plurality of equally spaced teeth 32 and a corres ponding plurality of gullets 34 formed integrally with and about the periphery of the body. Each alternate tooth 32 of a first set of alternating teeth has mounted thereon a carbide tip 36 with a cutting point 60 on the right side of the blade 20 . Each of the remaining teeth 32 form a second set of alternating teeth and has mounted thereon a carbide tip 38 with a cutting point 72 on the left side of the blade 20 . Each of the carbide tips 36 and 38 is formed with a concave front face 42 , top bevel angle, side clearance angles and a front bevel angle.
