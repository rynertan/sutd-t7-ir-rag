# TIRE HANDLING DEVICE

## Abstract
A tire handling device for mounting or removing a tire 13 on a wheel rim 12 while the wheel is attached to the vehicle in which there is a circular track 10 detachably secured to the rim 12 of the wheel and in which there is a motor 11 which is in driving engagement with the track 10 and upon operating of the motor 11 moves around the track. Means are provided for securing a tire tool 18 to the motor so that as the motor 11 moves around the track, the tire tool 18 is carried with it.
