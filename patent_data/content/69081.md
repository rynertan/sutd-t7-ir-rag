# Chick drinker apparatus with automatic water level control.

## Abstract
A chick drinker apparatus comprises a main supply recepta cle 10 connected to a water supply 24 , and a number of drinkers 26 with automatic water level control which are of a size suitable for chicks and are placed at a lower level than the supply receptacle. Each drinker is connected to the supply receptacle by a respective flexible siphon tube 34 which has a resilient gripping member 36 attachable to, and quickly removable from, the rim 4 of the supply receptacle, and also independent priming means 44 for transferring liquid from the supply receptacle to the respective drinker.
