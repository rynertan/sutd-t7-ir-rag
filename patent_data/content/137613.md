# Improvements in or relating to security glazing.

## Abstract
A security glass which possesse substantial resistance to penetration by impact by a heavy implement and which exhibits only a small amount of spall formation is a glass flexible bonding material, e.g. polyvinylbutyral 4 , laminate in which the layer of flexible bonding material positioned other than at the forwardmost flexible bonding material layer position possesses increased thickness. The rearmost glass layer 7 is generally relatively thin and is preferably chemically toughened.
