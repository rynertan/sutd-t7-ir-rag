# Suspension strut assemblies.

## Abstract
A serviceable welded MacPherson type strut 10 is con structed to allow the upper end of the support tube 12 to be circumferentially cut to facilitate removal of the shock absorb er components mounted therein so that an empty support tube is provided. Subsequent to such removal, a shock ab sorber service cartridge 54 is inserted into the support tube and is retained therein by a service nut 74 which is threadedly connected to the internal wall of the support tube.
