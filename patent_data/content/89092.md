# Stationary beer container.

## Abstract
Stationary beer container 1 with connections at the lower and upper side provided with a connection means A, B that is suited to cooperate with a head C known for drawing beer from a barrel or keg, which connection means A, B is connected with a line 4 to the lower side of the beer container 1 and with a vertically extending line 10 to the upper side of the container 1 . The same type of head C can be used for tapping beer from the container 1 , filling the container 1 and cleaning it. The inn keeper is accustomed to such a head C , since it is also used for tapping beer from barrels.
