# Method and apparatus for applying emulsion onto screen printing plate.

## Abstract
A method and an apparatus for applying an emulsion onto a screen printing plate S are disclosed, in which two squeegees 35A, 35B are arranged on either side of the screen S , one 35A of which is directed to the applying operation and the other squeegee 35B is directed to the scraping and recovering operation. In accordance with the invention, a high quality of the screen printing S may be obtained and very convenient operation and handling may be achieved.
