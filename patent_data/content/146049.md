# STAND OFF DEVICE

## Abstract
A stand off device for use with an acoustic transducer 40 in the form of a volume having a flexible cup section 22 and a stiff cup section 2 with the lips of the cups joined to gether and almost filled with liquid 23 . The bottom of the stiff cup section has an opening 4 across which a membrane 6 is mounted and a clip for holding the active section of a trans ducer in contact with the membrane 6 .
