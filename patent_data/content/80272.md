# SHRINK RESIST TREATMENT OF WOOL

## Abstract
A method for the shrinkresist treatment of wool includ ing the steps of chlorinating the wool with an acidified hypochlorite dechlorinating the product acidifying the product and applying an acid colloid solution of suitable methylolmelamine resin to the product. The chlorination is preferably carried out in a suction drum in such a manner that the level of active chlorine on the wool is in excess of 2 m m .
