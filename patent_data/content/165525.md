# Ski boot, particularly of the rear entrance type, incorporating a closure and foot securing device.

## Abstract
The ski boot 1 comprises a shell 2 , having front and rear quarters 3, 4 hingedly connected thereto, and housing a foot instep presser 5 . A cable 10 is adapted to be actuated by a cable pulling element 11 of the type disclosed in an earlier European Patent Application No. 82100223.5, of this same applicant , actuable from the boots exterior. The cable 10 is arranged to tighten the front and rear quarters 3,4 together, extend over the instep presser 5 , rearwardly encompass an area occupied by the heel of the wearer s foot, and being connected at its other end to a fixed point 32 on the ski boot 1 .
