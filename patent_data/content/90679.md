# Process for preparation of 2,3 dihydro 2,2 dimethyl 7 hydroxybenzofuran.

## Abstract
A process for preparing 2,3 dihydro 2,2 dimethyl 7 hy droxybenzofuran in which 2 methallyloxyphenol is heated to a temperature in the range of 150 C to 250 C in the presence of a solvent and a Lewis acid catalyst under an autogenous pressure of 20 to 60 psig is disclosed and exemplified.
