# AUTOMATIC LOUDNESS CONTROLLER

## Abstract
Broadcast apparatus for controlling the loudness level of program signals includes a variable gain device 14 for increasing or decreasing the level of incoming program material as a function of program loudness using control 22 while simultaneously holding by level control 24 the output level below a preset level to assure proper modulation levels and or to prevent excessive reaction by peak limiters not shown which usually follow the loudness controller in the broadcast chain.
