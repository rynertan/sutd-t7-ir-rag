# Milling cutter with adjustable finishing insert.

## Abstract
A milling cutter 10 having an adjustable finishing insert assembly 30 provided with a finishing insert pocket 32 having a planar bottom wall 40 disposed at an inclined angle relative to the planar surface 16 of the cutter. A locating seat 34 , having a finishing insert 36 fixedly mounted therein, is adjustably mounted within the insert pocket 32 . By adjusting the position of the locating seat 34 relative to the insert pocket 32 , the angle of the cutting edge of the finishing insert 36 , relative to the milling cutter 10 is varied.
