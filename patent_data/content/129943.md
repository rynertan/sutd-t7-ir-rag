# Cathode ray tube having an Fe Co Cr shadow mask and method of manufacturing such a shadow mask.

## Abstract
A cathode ray tube of the post focusing type comprising colour selection means in the form of a ferro magnetic plate having a large number of apertures. The plate is magnetized so that a magnetic quadrupole lens is formed in each aper ture. The ferromagnetic plate consists of an Fe Co Cr alloy having 25 27 by weight of Co and the remainder essentially Fe.
