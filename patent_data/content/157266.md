# A container for fixing an animal to be tested in a biochemical test.

## Abstract
A container 3 for restraining an animal 2 to be sub jected to a biochemical test comprising a tubular portion 302 for covering the head 21 of the animal, and a body portion 301 for covering the body of the animal, whereby a micro wave is applied to the tubular portion projecting into an appli cator 1 . At least a part of the tubular portion is made of a material having a high dielectric constant and a high dielectric loss, such that the interior of the head of the animal may be uni formly heated.
