# TEMPERATURE INDICATOR

## Abstract
A self adhesive temperature indicator comprises a piece of transparent or translucent foil 10, one side being provided, over at least a region thereof, with a layer 12 of material which changes colour at a predetermined temperature, over at least another region thereof with a printed layer 14, for example of a material having a visual characteristic e.g. its own colour denoting the predetermined temperature, and over at least another region preferably a peripheral margin with a coating 18 of adhesive.
