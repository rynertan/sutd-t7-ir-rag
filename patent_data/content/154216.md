# Method of manufacturing a means of protection for a cardanic propeller shaft and means of protection obtained by the method.

## Abstract
Method of manufacturing a means 9 of protection for a cardanic propeller shaft 1 and means of protection obtained by the method whereby a portion of plastic tube 11 is cut, a cone 7 is formed for association to the tube 11 by injection of plastic material, a band 10 is formed by injection of plastic material on the larger edge of the cone 7 already formed, the material of the cone 7 and the material of the tube 11 in contact one with the other are welded together and a bearing ring 5 is formed that serves to engage on a Cardan joint 3 of the shaft the means 9 of protection obtained in the form of a single piece of plastic material.
