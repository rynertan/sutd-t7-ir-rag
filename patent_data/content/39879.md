# A soft capsule and process for the preparation of the same.

## Abstract
A soft capsule comprising a pharmaceutically active con tents and a coated shell which further comprises a coating film layer of carnauba wax provided onto the outer surface of the coated shell. Preferably, the amount of the carnauba wax is in the amount of at least 0.002 part by weight based on 100 parts by weight of the amount of the coated shell.
