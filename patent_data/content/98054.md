# Wet clutch assembly.

## Abstract
A wet clutch assembly 10 for use as a starting clutch for a continuously variable transmission comprising a piston 12 fixed to a shaft 11 and guided 27 to rotate with a cylinder 14 encircling the piston and a friction plate 38 engageable by the piston and a pressure plate 32 fixed to the cylinder when fluid pressure increases from idle rpm in a cavity 24 between the piston 12 and the cylinder 14 . Fluid pressure may be supplied either directly or through a pressure responsive valve 46 .
