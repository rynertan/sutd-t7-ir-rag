# Casing sizing means, method and apparatus.

## Abstract
An adjustable sizing means for food casing includes a tubular member 10 having latch means at each end for mounting the tubular member to a stuffing apparatus. A for ward portion 16 of the tube 10 contains a plurality of slits 18 which extend parallel to the longitudinal axis of the tubu lar member.These slits define fingers 24 which are hinged at their ends and mid point so that axiallv compressing the tube causes the fingers to buckle outwardly to a casing expanding size.
