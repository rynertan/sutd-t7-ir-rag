# Visual display device and method of erecting the same.

## Abstract
A large scale display device is made up of a plu rality of stackable display blocks 1, 4 which are as sembled on location. The lowermost block 1 is secured to a trailer 2 having supporting outriggers 2a . Each block carries a portion of a complete display screen. Electrical interconnection of the blocks allows their common operation as a single display screen.
