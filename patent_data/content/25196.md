# Trailer with foldable load platform and drawbar.

## Abstract
A folding taller 20 is provided having a chassis 21 support by at least one pair of road wheels 24 and a load bearing platform 22,23 supported by the chassis 21 , the load bearing platform 22,23 being formed in two sections 22,23 which are foldable between a first position in which the sections 22,23 lie in a common plane and a second position in which the sections 22,23 lie juxtaposed in parallel planes. When in the second position the trailer 20 can be moved into a substantially vertical plane for stowage.
