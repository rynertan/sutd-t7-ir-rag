# Method of and device for detecting the presence or absence of an article on or from a vacuum pick up means.

## Abstract
A method of and a device for detecting the presence or absence of an electrical or electronic component 103 on or from a vacuum pick up means 105 in which, in the absence of a component 103 , the air flow produced by the vacuum is converted into an acoustic signal 125 127 which indicates the absence of the component 103 .
