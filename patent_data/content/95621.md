# Comminution apparatus.

## Abstract
An apparatus for comminuting cut crop comprises re ciprocating crop comminuting knives housed in the sub frame 47 of a main frame 40 . The crop is fed towards the knives by a reciprocating piston, the frame 40 moving in the direction of crop advancement when the crop pressure on the knives exceeds the resistance of the compression springs 46 . The crop comminuting knives remain in con stant contact with the crop for efficient comminution.
