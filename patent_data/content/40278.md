# A method of sealing a rotary shaft, a magnetic seal apparatus and a container for ferrofluid for use therein.

## Abstract
A self activating ferrofluid seal apparatus for a memory disc drive shaft comprises a ring magnet 12 and a pair of pole pieces 18 which form a cavity therebetween, a closed loop plastics tubular container 22 with ferrofluid 20 therein positioned within the cavity, and a penetration clamp 24 having prongs 26 to penetrate the plastics container prior to installation of the shaft element therein. The tubular container 22 comprises a tube having a sealed closed end received in the opposite open end to close and seal that open end.
