# Electrical connector for printed circuits.

## Abstract
An electrical connector for printed circuits comprises a dielectric connector body 30 and a plurality of sinuous contact strips 32 which have clip portions 72 that are disposed in a socket portion 34 of the connector body for detachable connection to an end tab 26 of a printed circuit board 14 inserted into the socket portion, and elongate portions 74 which are associated with a trailing attachment portion 36 of the connector body for permanent connection to a flexible printed circuit 12 by crimped splice ferrules 86 .
