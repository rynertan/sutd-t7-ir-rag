# Self loading magnetic head air bearing slider.

## Abstract
The air bearing surface of a self loading magnetic head slider 10 has a front section 16 , two side rails 12, 14 and a recessed section 18 there between. The air bearing surface has a convex curved shape in longitudinal section to provide very small spacing between the trailing end of the slider, which supports magnetic transducers 24,26 , and a moving magnetic medium thereunder.
