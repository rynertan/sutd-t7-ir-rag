# THIENOPYRIDONES, PROCESSES AND INTERMEDIATES FOR THEIR PREPARATION AND PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS CONTAINING THEM

## Abstract
Novel thienopyridones with antihypertensive activity have the formula I, The fused thieno ring may carry one or two substituents. Formula I encompasses thieno 3,2 b pyridones, thieno 3,4 b pyridones and thieno 2,3 b pyridones. Processes for preparing the novel thienopyridones and pharmaceutical compositions containing them are described. Pharmaceutically acceptable acid addition halts of the com pounds of formula I are also described. The thienopyridones of formula I are antihypertensive agents and are also indicated for use in treating heart failure and ischaemic heart disease.
