# Modular display support.

## Abstract
A modular merchandising display system 10, 410 in cluding a plurality of upright members 30, 430 , a plurality of lateral members 70, 470 interconnecting the upright members, and a plurality of decor strips 50, 150, 250, 350, 450 releasably mounted on and encapsulating each upright member. Each decor strip includes two flexible sides 59, 159, 259, 359, 459 , each of which is adjacent an edge of another decor strip on the upright member so that the upright members are hidden. The lateral members extend between adjacent sides, which flex to accommodate the lateral member.
