# Method and apparatus for feeding bar like materials.

## Abstract
A method for feeding cigarettes or filter plugs or the like to a working machine, such as a transporting machine, using a flat feeding passage 10 with a bottom which can store and convey the cigarettes etc. in the horizontal direction, comprising introducing receiving boxes a containing the cigarettes etc. into the feeding passage 10 and moving them to a feeding station A , and raising the receiving boxes a at the feeding station A so that the cigarettes etc. in the receiving boxes a are discharged at one time. The invention discloses also an apparatus for carrying out the method.
