# Apparatus for packaging given quantities of snuff.

## Abstract
An apparatus for packaging snuff ready for use in the form of portions enclosed, for example, in a paper sachet 3, 9 from a snuff store. The apparatus is characterizing in that the portioning means 3,9 is arranged to compress the snuff portion in a portion pocket 9 , wherewith each portion pocket comprises a through passing opening in a portion conveyor 4 . The compressed snuff portions are blown by pneumatic means 11, 14 to an intended position in a packaging band 16 .
