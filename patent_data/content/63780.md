# Preparation of 4 amino 3,5,6 trichloropicolinic acid.

## Abstract
Process for producing 4 amino 3,5,6 trichloropicolinic acid by reacting 3,4,5,6 tetrachloropicolinonitrile with ammonium hydroxide, preferably at 130 to 160 C. The free acid is precipitated upon acidification of the reaction mixture and may be recovered therefrom.
