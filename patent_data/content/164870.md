# CALCULATOR

## Abstract
An electronic calculator comprising facilities for performing conventional arithmetical opera tions, date input means, manual input means for receiving numerical data for said facilities and for receiving as a manual input data corresponding to the position on the earth of a location, preprogrammed means for calculating the times of sunrise, sunset, noon and the times knows as Fajer , Asser , and Issha in the Moslem day for that location, and display means for displaying any of said calculated times and for displaying the results of any said conventional arithmetical operations.
