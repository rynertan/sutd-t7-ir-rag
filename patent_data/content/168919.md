# Stainless steel.

## Abstract
A new stainless steel comprising iron and the following additional components in weight percent Mo 3 6 Cu 0.25 0.35 Si, max 1.5 Mn, max 1 C 0.12 0.30 with the proviso that the relative proportion between Mo and C is governed by the formula wgt Mo wgt C 16 from 1 to 2.5 . Optionally, the new stainless steel may also include Ni, Cr, Nb and Ta.
