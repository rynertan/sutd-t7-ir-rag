# Collecting box for a garden tool.

## Abstract
A collecting box 29 for collecting debris from a garden tool, for example a lawn mower or a lawn raker, has a light weight, self supporting collecting box body 1 and, on each side of the body, a tubular fastening member 18 for fasten ing the collecting box to the garden tool. The fastening members extend at least one third of the way along the sides 3 of the body with a view to preventing the body being subjected to high stresses, and the ends 19 of the fasten ing members project from the body and can be inserted into sockets 41 in the tool. An intermediate length 42 of each fastening member is exposed to provide a handle for car rying the box.
