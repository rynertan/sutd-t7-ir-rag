# Aminoalkyl pyridine derivatives.

## Abstract
There are disclosed novel compounds described as aminoalkyl pyridine derivatives in which the aminoalkyl pyri dine is connected to a guianidine moiety or a functional equivalent thereof, either directly or through a linear con necting group. Processes for the preparation of such com pounds are also disclosed. The compounds are useful for the suppression of fastric acid secretions in mammals and compositions for such uses are also disclosed.
