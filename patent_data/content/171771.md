# A method for purification of rabies virus.

## Abstract
Disclosed is an improved method for the purification of rabies virus, which comprises subjecting a solution contain ing the rabies virus to column chromatography using, as a gel for chromatography, a sulfuric acid ester of cellulose or a crosslinked polysaccharide. The method can provide highly purified rabies virus which is useful for obtaining an effective vaccine against rabies.
