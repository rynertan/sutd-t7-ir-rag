# Loudspeaker with motional feedback.

## Abstract
A loudspeaker having a main magnet assembly 10, 14, 16 and voice coil 18 driving a diaphragm 24 via a coupling 22 is provided with a motional feedback transducer comprising twin axially disposed coils 38, 40 mounted on the coupling 22 for movement in a secondary, radial magnetic field produced by magnets 28 and 30.
