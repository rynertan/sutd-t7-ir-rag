# Rotary drilling bits.

## Abstract
A rotary drilling drag bit for drilling from the surface deep holes in sub surface formations e.g. for oil has a body 4 with a diameter of at least 100 mm and having a bore for passage of drilling fluid to its face 3 and rotatable cutting elements 6 having a cutting face comprising an agglomerate of diamond particles so mounted on the body 4 that they are free to rotate in use. The cutting elements are of long life and highly effective in drilling.
