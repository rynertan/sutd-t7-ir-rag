# Novel dibenz b,f oxepin and dibenzo b,f thiepin compounds and pharmaceutically acceptable salts thereof, processes for their preparation and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.

## Abstract
The invention provides dibenz b, f oxepin and dibenzo b, f thiepin derivatives of the formula and pharmaceutically acceptable salts of said compounds in which R3 is a hydrogen atom. Compositions of said compounds or salts and processes for their preparation are also provided. These compounds and salts thereof have excellent anti inflammatory, analgesic and antipyretic activities.
