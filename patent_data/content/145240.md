# Method for producing Microcapsule.

## Abstract
A microcapsule produced by preparing a water in oil emulsion comprising an inner aqueous layer containing said water soluble drug and a drug retaining substance therefor and an oil layer containing a polymer substance, then thickening or solidifying said inner aqueous layer to a viscosity of not lower than about 5000 centiposes and finally subjecting the resulting emulsion to in water drying gives prolonged release of said water soluble drug.
