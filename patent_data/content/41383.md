# A WIRE CUT ELECTRIC DISCHARGE MACHINE

## Abstract
A wire cut, electric discharge machine comprises two pairs of rollers 21,22 for gripping therebetween a wire with constant pressure provided in the vicinity of each of upper and lower guides 5, 7 of a wire travelling system. When the wire 1 is broken, the pairs of rollers 21,22 hold the wire 1 to prevent it from coming off the travelling system.
