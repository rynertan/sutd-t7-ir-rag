# Magneto arrangement.

## Abstract
A magneto rotor assembly is disclosed in which a mag net 12 is clamped in position between a pair of pole pieces 13, 14 within a cavity in a rotor 11 . The pole pieces 13, 14 are provided with at least one tapered surface 20, 21 which engages a or the corresponding tapered sur face s of the rotor cavity to prevent movement of the pole pieces 13, 14 and magnet 12 . A fastener 16 is also pro vided to hold each pole piece 13, 14, in engagement with the rotor cavity.
