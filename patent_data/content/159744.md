# Device for cutting off the tail of slaughtered poultry.

## Abstract
Device for cutting of the tail 14 of slaughtered poultry 13 which is moved forward, hanging by the legs 15 on a conveyor track 16 , comprising a cutting knife 1 fixedly mounted under the conveyor track 16 , having a V shaped cutting edge 2 and being supported by a guide 11a, 11b for the bird s body.
