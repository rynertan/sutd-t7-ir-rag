# Dispenser for thin sheet or film.

## Abstract
A cutting device using as its cutting edge a coarse file like face, a method for cutting a thin sheet or film 10 by causing at least the cut starting portion of the thin sheet or film to be pressed against the coarse file like face 9 of the cutting device, and a dispenser 1 for a thin sheet or film provided with the aforementioned cutting device using said coarse file like face 9 as a cutting edge thereof are disclosed. A thin sheet or film 10 is readily torn when a notch or cut is formed at the cut starting portion of the sheet or film. The present invention makes use of this property of the thin sheet or film.
