# Display rack for documents.

## Abstract
Document holder for holding magazines, brochures and other looseleaf papers in an upright position, with a relatively large portion of each document being exposed to view. The device is constructed in modular form being composed of detachably connected modulation units 11 and is readily in creased and decreased in size by adding a substracting unit to hold different numbers of papers. The disclosed embodiment is constructed as a wall mounted rack.
