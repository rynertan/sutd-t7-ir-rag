# PYRAZOLINE DERIVATIVES, PROCESSES FOR THEIR PREPARATION AND PHARMACEUTICAL FORMULATIONS CONTAINING THEM

## Abstract
Compounds of formula I The compounds may be administered as the raw chemical or in association with a carrier as a pharmaceutical formulation. The compounds may be prepared by methods analo gous to those known in the art, e.g., by the method of Duffin and Kendall in J. Chem. Soc. 1954 , 408 415, or by other methods.
