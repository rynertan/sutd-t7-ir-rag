# Disc cartridge.

## Abstract
In a disc cartridge comprising a cartridge case, a magnetic recording disc rotatably mounted in the cartridge case, and head insertion openings formed extending to the side portions of said cartridge case, said openings are opened even at one edge of the cartridge case for facilitating insertion of the head mounted in a recording and playing back device, said cartridge case is reinforced by a reinforce ment member having a smaller diameter or a smaller thickness than the thickness of said cartridge case.
