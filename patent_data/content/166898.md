# Throw away insert.

## Abstract
A throw away insert having a cutting edge 12 formed along the outer periphery and between adjacent noses 11 of the insert. A concave rake surface 8 is formed which extends form the cutting edges 12 toward the center of the insert. Formed on the rake surface 8 are a plurality of bosses 10 which project, and a plurality of projections 9 spaced a predetermined distance from the cutting edges 12 . A plurality of lands 5 which are spaced a predeter mined distance from each other are formed along the cutting edge 12 and on the rake surface 8 .
