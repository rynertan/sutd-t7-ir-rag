# Semi automatic revolver.

## Abstract
A revolver comprising a mount 2 containing the firing mechanism and a slide 3 carrying the barrel 4 and cylinder 5 . The slide 3 is guided movably on the mount 2 between mechanical stops 10 12 . Each recoil stroke of the slide 3 automatically energizes a spring motor 54, R carried in the slide 3 itself which is effective to cause the cylinder 5 to revolve in timed relationship with the cocking. The mount 2 supports a pusher 111 which can energize said spring motor 54, R on request and is operated by the firing mechanism.
