# Writable adhesive tape.

## Abstract
A writable adhesive tape is disclosed, which comprises a polyolefine substrate, having on one side an adhesive layer and on the opposite side a writable anti adhesive layer. The writable anti adhesive layer comprises 25 to 55 by weight of a chlorinated polyolefine, 20 to 50 by weight of a polyaldehyde resin together with 5 to 20 by weight of an anti adhesive agent, and 5 to 25 by weight of a matting charge.
