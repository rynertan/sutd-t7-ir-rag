# Cabinet assembly.

## Abstract
A cabinet framework comprising a vertical post having at least one slot therein a first crossbar at a right angle to the post and having an end terminating in at least one prong receivable in the at least one slot of the vertical post, the prong including means for locking the post and crossbar together in response to vertical movement of the crossbar a second crossbar disposed at right angles to the post and the first crossbar, and inciuding engagement means engageable with the post for preventing rotational and vertical movement of the second crossbar with respect to the post and means for fastening the end of the second crossbar to the vertical post, and for simultaneously preventing vertical movement of the first crossbar to maintain the first crossbar locked to the post.
