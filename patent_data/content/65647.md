# Fuel tank mounting assembly for motor vehicle.

## Abstract
A fuel tank mounting assembly for a motor vehicle, wherein the fuel tank 1 is fixedly mounted, in a suspended manner, underneath a floor panel 3 of the vehicle body. Support means is provided for temporarily supporting the fuel tank until the latter is securely mounted in position. Such support means may comprise lugs 11 provided at the fuel tank side and slots 15 provided at the vehicle body side, the lugs 11 and slots 15 being adapted for engagement with each other to provide the temporary support for the fuel tank 1 .
