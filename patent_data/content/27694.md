# Composition and method for stimulation of aerobic bacteria.

## Abstract
Composition for stimulation of aerobic bacterial from combination of triacontanol, B vitamins or mixtures of these, with an agent derived from mulch cow excrement and method of treating growth media such as sewage or soil to stimulate the growth of aerobic bacterial to improve the con dition of sewage for disposal or availability of soil nutrients for plant growth by addition of the composition to the growth.
