# High pressure sodium vapour discharge lamp.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a high pressure sodium vapour discharge lamp comprising a discharge vessel 1 and a glow starter 17 . The glow starter 17 in the inoperative condition of the lamp is electrically connected parallel to the discharge vessel 1 . In the operative condition the glow starter 17 is switched off electrically by means of a bimetal switch 13 . According to the invention the contact point 15 of the bimetal switch 13 which is connected to the grow starter 17 is situated on the wall of the discharge vessel 1 . Herewith a reliable and simply relizable positioning of the contact point 15 is obtained.
