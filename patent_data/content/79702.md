# Method of sealing a joint between two insulated pipes.

## Abstract
A recoverable polymeric material 1 is coated with discrete regions of a sealant 3 , such as a mastic, and of a heat activatable adhesive 2 , such as a hot melt adhesive. The coated material forms a sleeve which may be of tubular or wrap around configuration. The sleeve may be used to cover weld joints 6 in polyethylene 5 coated steel pipes 4 .
