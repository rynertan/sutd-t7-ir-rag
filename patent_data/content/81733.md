# A hub assembly in particular for a collapsible tent frame.

## Abstract
A hub assembly in particular for a collapsible tent frame comprises a closed loop of at least three double ended hinge members 14 interconnected by hinge pins 18 , each hinge member having a channel therein within which a strut receiving socket 16 is pivotally supported to swing in a plane perpendicular to the plane of the loop, the socket being secured within the channel by the hinge pins.
