# Roller skating shoe and method for its manufacture.

## Abstract
Roller skating shoe with injection molded sole 12 in which receptacles 26, 26a, 62 are provided that support the wheel hangers and wheels 40 . The sole 12 shown has a structural beam T profile, the flange of which supports be foot and the lower rib of which has an irregular lower edge forming a series of apertured angled bosses 22,24 to receive the wheel hanger structure 42 . Receptacles in the form of embedded threaded connectors 26 enable bolting of the wheel hangers directly to the injected sole 12 . The method for forming this shoe includes placing the threaded connectors 26 into the mold and then injection molding the sole of semi rigid plastic directly against a string lasted upper held in place by a last.
