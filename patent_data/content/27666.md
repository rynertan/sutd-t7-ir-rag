# Unsaturated polyester compositions and their use.

## Abstract
The invention provides novel curable unsaturated polyester compositions comprising an unsaturated polyester, a reactive copolymerizable monomer, a paraffin wax as an evaporation reducing agent and as an adhesion promotor a compound, containing 10 30 carbon atoms, of the class con sisting of alkylaromatic, alkenylaromatic, alkylolaromatic compounds or an alochol.
