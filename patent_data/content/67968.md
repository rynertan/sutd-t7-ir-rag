# Safety belt system.

## Abstract
A retractor 13 of a safety belt system mounted on a seat 1 directly involving lap belt section 8 . An attachment buckle 7 is connected to its anchor point 10 by an expandable link device 11 , which normally permits a degree of movement of buckle 7 away from anchor point 10 or towards the latter for example by means of a return spring , but is provided with means to oppose the extraction of the buckle 7 under predetermined conditions of accelera tion of the vehicle and or of the belt.
