# Tube of plastic for yarn bobbins.

## Abstract
A tube of plastic for yarn bobbins of the conical type which has one or more V shaped slits 8 substantially along generatrices of the cone surface and extending at least over the major part of the tube length, the pointed end of the slit 8 being in the broad end of the tube and the open end of the slit 8 being in the narrow end of the tube.
