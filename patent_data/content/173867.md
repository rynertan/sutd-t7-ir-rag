# Three way check valve.

## Abstract
A three way check valve for a vehicle fluid pressure breaking system 10 includes a housing 34 having first, second and third inlet ports 38,40,42 and an outlet port 60 . A pair of shuttles 44,46 are slidably mounted in the housing 34 , and are responsive to variations in the fluid pressure at the first, second and third inlet ports 38,40,42 to communi cate the inlet port 38,40,42 having the highest pressure level to the outlet port 60 , and to terminate communication through the other inlet ports 38,40,42 to prevent back flowing from one inlet port to the other inlet ports.
