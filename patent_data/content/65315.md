# INTAKE SYSTEM FOR TWO CYCLE INTERNAL COMBUSTION ENGINES

## Abstract
A two cycle internal combustion engine having its intake passage 11 equipped with a check valve 12 and an intake plenum chamber 17 , the latter being formed in the crankcase wall of the engine just below the intake passage. The crankcase wall below said intake passage is formed with an opening 1b and a cover 15 is provided to surround said opening. A diaphragm 16 is sandwiched between said crankcase and said cover, so that the cover and the diaphragm define to gether an intake plenum chamber 17 .
