# Method for manufacturing mask for color CRT.

## Abstract
A method for manufacturing a mask for a color CRT. The mask comprises a plurality of mask members 1, 3 having a number of apertures for passing electron beems 33 , and being spaced apart. The mask is produced by the steps of bringing the mask members 1, 3 into tight contact with an insulator 13 interposed between their peripheries fixing them by an adhesive press forming the entire com posite melting the adhesive and shifting apart and fixing the mask members 1, 3 at a predetermined distance.
