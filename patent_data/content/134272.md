# Apparatus for measuring human respiration.

## Abstract
A device for measuring the volume of air entering the human lungs is provided with a volumetric spirometer which comprises a first hollow cylindrical chamber 91 for slidably receiving a piston 44 , at least a second hollow chamber 93 sealed 81 from fluid communication with the first chamber, a breathing tube assembly 78,80 adapted to be in com munication with said first and second hollow chambers and connecting means 72,74 connecting said breathing tube assembly with said first chamber and said second chamber.
