# CATALYST COMPOSITION FOR CATALYTIC CRACKING OF HYDROCARBONS

## Abstract
This invention related to a catalyst composition for catalytic cracking of hydrocarbons, which comprises porous matrix material containing crystalline aluminosilicate and niobium component, characterized in that said niobium component is present in an amount of 0.1 to 7.0 by weight on the basis of Nb element in the final catalyst product.
