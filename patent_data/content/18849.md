# Improvements in or relating to electric fence energisers.

## Abstract
An electric fence energiser 1 including controlling means 3 whereby the characteristics of the output pulse sequence 4, 8 is unpredictable to an animal. One or more of the gaps 5, 6 between pulses, or pulse bursts 4, 8 , the number of pulses in a burst, the voltage of each pulse and the length of each pulse may be varied.
