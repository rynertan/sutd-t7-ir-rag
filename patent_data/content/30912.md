# Pintle chain having extended wear barrel section and sprocket therefor.

## Abstract
The barrels 13 of the links 11 of pintle chain for opera tion open end forward over a drive sprocket 21 are extended inwardly respecting the tooth pockets of the sproc ket 21 and two, flat separately distinct areas of interen gagement are provided. The two areas of interengagement include the bottom face 19 of the barrel 13 and the bottom face 21a of the tooth pocket, and the forward tooth face 23a and rear barrel face 20 . The intermediate corners of the tooth pockets and of the barrel 13 are rounded only for practical purposes.
