# Articulated belt conveyor.

## Abstract
A conveying apparatus which comprises a pair of sprockets 6 provided at front and rear ends thereof, a pair of endless chains 8 passed around the sprockets 6 at both sides thereof, a plurality of support members 10 arranged at regular intervals, each having a box like cross section and ex tending in a transverse direction for supporting objects to be carried on its upper surface 13 , a plurality of fastening members 12 for fastening the support members 10 with the chains 8 , a connecting members 21, 31 22, 32 for connec ting the support members 10 to the fastening members 12 and a plurality of covering members 11, 11a, 11b each securely connected with adjacent support members 10 at its front and rear ends for covering each gap defined there between.
