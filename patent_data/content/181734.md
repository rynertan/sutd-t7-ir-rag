# Improvements in and relating to clamps.

## Abstract
A clamp 1,13 for joining together overlapping sections of elongate members 3 in, for example, telescopic steel arches used in the mining industry comprises a frontal plate 1 and a U bolt 13 , the arms 9 of the U bolt being inclined to a line drawn normal to a load bearing surface of the frontal plate 1 at a preferred angle of between 5 and 40 .
