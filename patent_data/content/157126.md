# A salt of DC 52 and a pharmaceutical composition containing the same.

## Abstract
A pharmalogically acceptable acid addition salt of DC 52 formula I with 0.5 2.0 equivalent weight of inorganic acid, sulfonic acid, acidic amino acid, citric acid, trans aconitic acid, Î± ketoglutaric acid, itaconic acid, malonic acid or ascorbic acid on the basis of DC 52, having the same degree of antitumor activity as DC 52, is superior to DC 52 in stability at powdering or at preservation.
