# A paper coating composition and its production.

## Abstract
A coating composition for paper comprising a mineral pigment and a polymer latex prepared by emulsion polymer ization of an aliphatic conjugated diene monomer, an ethylen ically unsaturated carboxylic acid monomer and a mono olefinic monomer in a weight proportion of 24.5 5.0 0.5 5 45 75 in the presence of a chain transfer agent, characterized in that the chain transfer agent is carbon tetrachloride and an alkylmercaptan in combination.
