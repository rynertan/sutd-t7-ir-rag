# A casing structure for electrical and mechanical units.

## Abstract
A casing structure comprises a first casing unit A including a first compartment A2 and a second compartment A3, and a second casing unit B including a third compart ment B1. The second unit B is pivotably connected to the first unit A to open and close with respect to it. The three compartments A2, A3, B1 have respective inlets and outlets connected in series through connecting passages to define an air flow passage. A source of heat and noise, such as a printer PU, is arranged in the intermediate second compart ment A3 and an air cooling fan 19 is provided in the air flow passage in the vicinity of the source PU.
