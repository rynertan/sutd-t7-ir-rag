# Use of fenpyramin as platelet antiaggregating, vasodilating, antithrombotic and antianginous medicine.

## Abstract
A new use of N 4 pyridyl 3,3 diphenylpropyl amine, known as fenpyramin as platelet aggregation inhibiting medicine and antithrombotic agent as well as vasodilating and antianginous medicine is described.
