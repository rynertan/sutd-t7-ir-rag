# Immune adsorbent and adsorbing device.

## Abstract
An immune adsorbent 8 for autoantibodies and or immune complexes is provided which is comprised of a water insoluble carrier and at least one low molecular weight substance containing a purine base or pyrimidine base as a constituent element, which is fixed to said carrier. The immune adsorbent 8 is usually used in an adsorption apparatus 1 comprising a vessel having fluid inlet and outlet openings 5,5 and having packed therein said immune adsorbent 8 .
