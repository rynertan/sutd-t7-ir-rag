# CORROSION PROTECTIVE PAINTS

## Abstract
The corrosion protective paint includes a resin for paints as a main ingredient and mountain leather which is a clay mineral in a powder form mainly composed of hydrous magnesium silicate or hydrous magnesium aluminum silicate, such as sepiolite and attapulgite. The corrosion protective paint may also include hydrotalcite or metal hydroxide as a corrosion inhibitor. This paint prevents corrosion of an article even when exposed to saline such as sea water.
