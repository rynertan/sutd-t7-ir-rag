# Magnetic water conditioning device.

## Abstract
Water flows from inlet 3 to outlet 4 through a gap be tween valve seat 18 and plug 9 . A magnetic field from perm anent magnet 7 acts across the gap. Magnet 7 is sealed against water by sleeve 8 , plug 9 , and adjusting cap 11 held together by screw 10 and provided with sealing rings 16 . Flow rate and magnetic field are simultaneously adjusted by rotation of external knob 12 to screw cap 11 into housing 5 . Adjustment is limited by stop 15 cooperating with radial indicator pin 14 .
