# SEAMING HEAD

## Abstract
Seaming head which essentially comprises a tubular head means 2 subjected to a downward axial motion promoted by hydraulic, pneumatic and the like means, an expansible pinching means comprised of arms 3 , and a plunger means 7 which will be caused to seam a cap 9 when radial protusions 8 of this plunger means 7 are driven with the head 2 during the downward motion of the latter.
