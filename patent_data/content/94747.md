# CONTAINER CLOSURE SYSTEM WITH VENT OPENING THROUGH THE CLOSURE TAPE

## Abstract
An easy openable tape closure for a container for gas containing beverages and comprising an interior tape spanning a pour opening, an exterior tape sealed to the interior tape in the area of the pour opening and having a vent opening in the area of the pour opening. A pull tab is positioned over the exterior tape and sealed therethrough to the interior tape to provide means for rupturing the interior tape at the vent opening to vent the container.
