# Residual fuel oil conditioners containing metal salts in aqueous solution and method of improving combustion therewith.

## Abstract
Residual fuel oil conditioners comprising an aqueous solution of a from 2.0 to 20.0 by weight of at least one water soluble metal salt selected from the halides, sulfates, and nitrates of magnesium, manganese, zinc, copper, lead, iron, nickel, aluminium, calcium and barium and b from 0.1 to 25.0 by weight of a surfactant, preferably a non ionic surfactant having an HLB of from 12 to 17. Method of improv ing combustion of an residual fuel with this conditioners.
