# Brushcutter kick prevention device and method.

## Abstract
A device on a brushcutter to avoid a sudden, self propelled side motion or kick of the brushcutter working head is disclosed, wherein a kick prevention shield 12 is provided. The kick prevention shield 14 extends over the front portion of the rotating cutting blade, so as to prevent a portion of the front of the rotating blade 10 from contacting an object in front of the brushcutter.
