# A SNOWPLOWABLE ROAD MARKER APPARATUS

## Abstract
The present invention is a reflective snowplowable road marker 1 of unitary construction for fixing in a road surface without adhesive and having an outwardly dished top sec tion 4 with a relieved channel 8 extending across the top surface 5 of the top section 4 with the channel 8 being adapted to receive therein a reflector 6 , and a bottom sec tion 10 extending from the bottom surface 22 of the top section 4 which has a cylindrical shape and a plurality of spaced apart gripping means 14 disposed on the outside surface of the bottom section 10 .
