# Semiconductor device comprising memory circuits.

## Abstract
A semiconductor device comprising memory circuits 2 coated with a resinous film shown by hatching formed for shielding radioactive rays, and the resinous film divided into a plurality of portions which are separated from one another and coat at least memory cells 2 and sense amplifiers 3, or a part of the resinous film is removed at portion other than said memory cells 2 and sense amplifiers.
