# Mechanical pencil and method of assembling the same.

## Abstract
A mechanical pencil in which a lead delivery mechanism composed of a lead containing part 3 , a lead chuck 5 , a chuck ring 6 , a sleeve 7, 8 having a stopper 8b and the like is inserted into an outer cyliner 1 having a stopper engaging portion 1b . A stopper fitting stepped portion 3a is positioned on the lead containing part, spaced apart from the stopper 8b by a distance equal to or less than the knocking stroke of the pencil, that when the lead containing part is knocked the stopper 8b is fitted into the stopper engaging portion 1b of the outer cylinder. A method for assembling such mechanical pencil is also described.
