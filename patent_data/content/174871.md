# Silver halide color photographic material.

## Abstract
A silver halide color photographic material having blue , green and red sensitive silver halide emulsion layers coated on a support is provided, each of said layers being composed of a plurality of sub layers having different sensitivities. In said material, at least a red sensitive silver halide emulsion sub layer having high sensitivity is provided between a first green sensitive silver halide emulsion sub layer of high sensitivity and a second green sensitive silver halide emul sion sub layer having a lower sensitivity than said first green sensitive sub layer, said first green sensitive sub layer and or said red sensitive sub layer with high sensitivity giving a maximum color density of formed dye in said material higher than 0.6 but not exceeding 1.3.
