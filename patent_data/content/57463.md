# Apparatus for reception and transfer of sheet material.

## Abstract
The apparatus guides a continuous trip 18 provided with predetermined separation lines along a predeter mined path through guide means 37, 38, 39, 40, 43 and a severing means 41 to a supporting and stacking means having a stacking support 25 movable between a raised position and a lowered position. Folding and stacking of the strip 18 takes place on the support 25 to form a package 11 after which the strip is severed by the severing means 41 and the package is moved by transfer means 31 to an unloading position from which the form package 11 can be extracted from the apparatus. In one embodiment, two separate stacking stations are provided to receive strip 18 alternately, under con trol of a member 42 .
