# Blends of poly p methylstyrene and a block copolymer of styrene and a conjugated diolefin.

## Abstract
A blend of poly p methylstyrene and a block copolymer of styrene and a conjugated diolefin containing from 60 to 80 weight percent styrene. The weight ratio of poly p methylstyrene to the styrene conjugated diolefin block copolymer is from 70 30 to 40 60.
