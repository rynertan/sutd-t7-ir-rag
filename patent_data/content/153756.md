# Construction of door glass guide in motor vehicle.

## Abstract
A construction of a door glass guide in a motor vehicle, comprising a door glass run 15, 40, 60, 90 including a main body 15A, 36, 56, 86 of the door glass run 15, 40, 60, 90 secured to a guide portion 22, 32, 52, 82 of a door frame 16, 30, 50, 80 , being opposed to an end edge 13, 14, 34, 54A, 84A of a door glass 10, 34, 54, 84 and a lip 15B, 15C, 38A, 38B, 58A, 58B, 88A, 88B , which is extending from end portions directed to the center of the door glass 10, 34, 54, 84 toward the center of the main body 15A, 36, 56, 86 of the door glass run 15, 40, 60, 90 in the widthwise direction thereof and toward the outer periphery of the door frame 16, 30, 50, 80 , being in sliding contact with the glass surfaces at the end edge 13, 14, 34, 54A, 84A of the door glass 10, 34, 54, 84 , and guiding the door glass 10, 34, 54, 84 in the opening or closing directions, wherein there is provided a spacer 20, 21, 42, 62, 72, 74, 92 being opposed to at least a portion of the door glass run 15, 40, 60, 90 in the longitudinal direction, disposed between one of the lips 15B, 15C, 38A, 38B, 58A, 58B, 88A, 88B and the main body 15A, 36, 56, 86 of the door glass run 15, 40, 60, 90 in the rear of the lip 15B, 15C, 38A, 38B, 58A, 58B, 88A, 88B in the direction of the thickness of said door glass 10, 34, 54, 84 and regulating the deformation of the lip 15B, 15C, 38A, 38B, 58A, 58B, 88A, 88B in the widthwise direction of the door glass run 15, 40, 60, 90 .
