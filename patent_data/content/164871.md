# A friction clutch release mechanism.

## Abstract
A release mechanism for use with a clutch release bearing of a pull type clutch comprises an element 9 on which there is a transverse circular groove 10 and locking means comprising a flexible ring 8 coaxial with a di aphragm 1 and bearing on the circumference of a central aperture therein, and a washer 2 with flexible lugs 3 applied against a face of the diaphragm 1 and coaxially with this latter so that the flexible lugs 3 engage around the ring 8 to render it rigid with the diaphragm 1 . The ring 8 is adapted to snap into the groove 10 in the actuating elment 9 at the moment of engagement.
