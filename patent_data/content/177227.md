# Improvements relating to smoking articles.

## Abstract
A smoking article mouthpiece 1 comprises a smoke passage 2, 21 and a ventilation air duct 4 11,12 18,19 22 , both of which extend from end to end of the mouthpiece. The duct is of a depth of 25 or more of the diameter of the mouthpiece. When the mouthpiece is incorporated in a smoking article such that air may enter the duct 4 11,12 18,19 22 , the ratio of the velocities of air and smoke is in excess of ten. The mouthpiece may also comprise a ventila tion air collection groove which extends about the element and opens into the ventilation air duct.
