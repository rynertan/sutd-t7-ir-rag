# Track belt assembly.

## Abstract
The assembly comprises a reinforcing belt structure 18 and a plurality of ground engaging shoes 22 . In order to secure said shoes to said belt structure, the assembly comprises anchor plates 30, 32 extending in parallel superposition with said shoes, and fastening means 44 , which are arranged between the lateral edges 26 of the reinforcing belt structure. The assembly may be combined with an inflatable load supporting structure.
