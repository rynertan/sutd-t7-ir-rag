# LEATHER LIKE TEXTILE MATERIAL

## Abstract
A leather like textile material comprising a multi layered substrate having at least first and second knitted or woven fa bric layers, and a leather like covering provided on one surface of the second fabric layer opposite to the first fabric layer. The first fabric layer is made of a material having a high water per meability, but a low water retentivity, and the second fabric lay er is made of a material having a high water retentivity.
