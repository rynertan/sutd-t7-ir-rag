# HARMLESS COSMETIC AND TOILETRY PRODUCTS AND METHOD OF MAKING SAME

## Abstract
To avoid a problem of the development of carcinogenic nitrosamines in cosmetic and toiletry products which either contain triethanolamine or contain compounds which result when triethanolamine is used to neutralize an acidic sub stance, formulations are made in which there is used in place of the triethanolamine a suitable totally alkoxylated alkylene diamine, such as N,N,N ,N tetrakis 2 hydroxypropyl ethylene diamine.
