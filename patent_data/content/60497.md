# Magnetic recording tape cartridge.

## Abstract
A magnetic recording tape cartridge comprising a top section 1A and a bottom section 1B assembled together to define a chamber therebetween for containing a pair of tape reels 3 each of which is resiliently supported by a spring plate member 6 , base portion of which fixed onto the top section, wherein the improvement comprising a partition wall means 11A,11B,11C,14 for separating at least the base portion of the spring plate member from said chamber.
