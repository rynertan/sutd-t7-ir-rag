# A device for transforming cycles or similar into multiple capacity vehicles for more occupants.

## Abstract
A device for transforming cycles or similar from vehicles for only one occupant into multiple vehicles for two or more occupants, who pedal in synchrony, consisting in a frame A B of telescopic variable dimension, to which a pedal pedal crank P 18 group is applied, said group being added to the main group and connected to the same by means of bars or connecting rods 25 and a sliding structure 9, with functions of saddle support adjustable from 0 DEG to 180 DEG so as to place saddle S at the desired inclination to the horizontal plane.
