# Improvements in or relating to joints.

## Abstract
A jointing member 1 for supporting one or more sheets of material 7,9 comprises a hollow cylindrical member 2 divided longitudinally into several separate compartments 4,5 each having an external wall formed with a slot 6 . The walls are flexible and the edges of the slot grip and support the edge of the sheet of material 7,9 once inserted into the slot 6 .
