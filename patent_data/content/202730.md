# ANGLER S KEEP NET

## Abstract
An angler s keep net of elongate generally cylindrical form with a series of hoops 10 to which the netting 12 is secured at spaced locations along its length. So that the lowermost end of the net can very convenient ly be opened or closed, the tube of netting is open ended and the two lowermost hoops can be connected together one closely overlying the other when the length of netting be tween them has been twisted.
