# Information processing apparatus with data transfer between external memories.

## Abstract
An information processing apparatus for executing information transfer between at least two units com prises a storing means, a first transfer control means for reading out information from one of the units into the storing means und a second transfer control means for writing stored information from the storing means into the other unit. By independently and simultaneously reading information into the storing means and writing previously stored information from the storing means the apparatus is capable of transferring information at a very high speed and can be adapted to transferring infor mation which has different addresses in the one and other unit.
