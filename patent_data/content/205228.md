# Endoscopic instrument for removing stomach insert.

## Abstract
An endoscopic instrument for removing an inflated insert from the stomach cavity of a person being treated for obesity comprises an elongated flexible tube 14 having passage ways 16, 18, 20 therein. A holding device 22 at the distal end of the flexible tube is constructed and arranged to grasp and stabilize the inflated stomach insert, and an operator 26 extending through the passageways is provided for manipu lating the holding device. A deflating device 34 is also lo cated at the distal end of the flexible tube for puncturing or otherwise causing deflation of the insert after stabilization thereof by the holding device. An operator 36 extending through the passageways is connected to manipulate the deflating device.
