# AN ELECTRIC HEATER

## Abstract
An electric heater includes a fan 52 which blow air through apertures 43 in a plurality of semi conductor heat ing disks 28, 30, 32, 34 which are held in coplanar, spaced apart relationship in pockets formed between ceramic holder members 72, 74 which are fastened together. Electrical con nectors 108, 110 make electrical contact with conductive coatings 48, 50 on the semi conductor disks 28, 30, 32, 34 .
