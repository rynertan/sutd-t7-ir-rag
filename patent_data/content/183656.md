# A reinforced conventional concrete pipe having an evenly distributed steel wire reinforcement and method for its manufacture.

## Abstract
A plain concrete pipe having an evenly distributed steel wire reinforcement is disclosed, said pipe being suitable for water piping and the like this pipe being reinforced with a reinforcement constituted by thin wires evenly distributed and arranged in a circumferential pattern and a longitudinal pattern a method is also disclosed for the manufacture of the pipe on an industrial scale in an efficient and economical manner.
