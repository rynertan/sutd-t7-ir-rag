# Actuator for remote devices or the like.

## Abstract
In order to render an actuator which is capable of selectively operating two remotely arranged latches or the like devices 102, 104 highly compact, the device is provided with a reversible motor, a reduction gear and an clutch arrangement which includes a solenoid coil. In some embo diments the clutch includes a two stage arrangement which perits a small power economical solenoid arrangement to be used. The invention further features the use of a compact reduction gear which includes two ring gears 126, 128 and stepped pinion gears 124 .
