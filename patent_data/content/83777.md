# Physiologically active peptide with between 147 and 162 amino acid residues.

## Abstract
A novel physiologically active peptide represented by the following formula BCysX₁CysX₂CysX₃ wherein B stands for a hydrogen atom or a methionyl group, Cys a cysteine residue, X₁ 5 to 9 amino acid residues, X₂ one amino acid residue, and X₃ 138 to 148 amino acid residues , which is excellent in physiological activities such as antiviral action, cell proliferation inhibitive action and NK natural killer cell activity enhancing action.
