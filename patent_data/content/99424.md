# A vehicle towing trailer.

## Abstract
A vehicle towing trailer comprises a substantially T shaped body 1 including an axially extending elongated frame 2 and a cross frame 3 connected to the rear end of the frame 2 , a swivel platform 11 supported on the cross frame 3 , a lift arm 7 mounted on the trailer body for lifting and lowereing the rear end of the trailer body, a pair of wheel assemblies each including one or more ground engaging wheels 14 supported by a bracket 15 which is detachable to the trailer body by insertion of one or more axially extending male members 16 into one or more cooperating female members 18 from a front side toward a rear side of the cross frame 3 in a direction opposite to a running direction of the trailer.
