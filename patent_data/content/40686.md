# Magnetic tape cartridge with file protect feature.

## Abstract
A magnetic tape cartridge 11 comprises a device 16 settable to prevent inadvertent recording and thus erasure on the tape. The device 16 comprises a shaft 21 carrying a control disc 18 intermediate its length. The disc 18 either masks a sensor 23 from a light source 22 or allows illumination of the sensor according to its angular setting. The unit 16 is rotatably mounted in a sleeve 12 formed integrally with a part 11 of the car tridge. The unit 16 has a screwdriver slot 17c and an indicator 17b . The sensor 23 is connected in a read write control circuit.
