# A reversible child restraint for automobiles.

## Abstract
To use the child restraint both in a rearward facing direc tion and a forward facing direction, the undersurface of the restraint seat includes a heel 13 at the back support 11 side of the seat undersurface, a toe 15 at the free end of the seat undersurface, and a concave curved slope 14 joining the heel 13 and the toe 15 .
