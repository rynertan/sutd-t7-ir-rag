# Process for producing 3 deoxyguanosine.

## Abstract
Glycosylation or transglycosylation of a specified guanine derivative, namely 9 substituted or non substituted guanine of formula I with a 3 deoxyribose donor such as 3 deoxyaden osine in the presence of a nucleoside phosphorylase source such as of microorganism origin is disclosed.
