# Wind turbine.

## Abstract
A wind turbine for a wind turbine electrical generator set in which movable nose portions 16 are located at or adjacent the leading edge of the blade and at or adjacent the tip 20 of the blade but is dispaceable purely longitudi nally of the blade, i.e. radially outwardly of the blade, from the normal retracted position, in which the portion contri butes to the lift of the aerofoil section, to an advanced position in which drag is produced, to prevent unwanted increase in the speed of the rotation of the rotor.
