# Method and apparatus for purifying liquid.

## Abstract
In a method and an apparatus of purifying a liquid to be dated, which contains magnetic solid particles therein, by feeding said liquid to a container 8 of an electromagnetic filter including a matrix 9 disposed inside said container and a coil 13 disposed so as to encompass the outer circumference of said container and generating a line of magnetic force to magnetize said matrix 9 when a current is applied thereto, the improvement wherein said coil 13 is cooled by said liquid to be treated.
