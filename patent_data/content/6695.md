# Mixture to protect poultry against salmonella and process for producing this mixture.

## Abstract
The invention resides in a mixture of avian intestinal microflora for administration to poultry to prevent or inhibit paratyphoid salmonella infection including infection by the arizona groups in the poultry or in the presence of infec tion to reduce the incidence and excretion rate of paraty phoid salmonella. The mixture is obtained by transferring, to newly hatched pathogen free poultry, starting avian mi croflora known to be capable of blocking subsequent colo nization, recovering said microflora from the faecal dropp ings of said pathogen free poultry in a manner precluding subsequent contamination by pathogens, and recovering the pathogen free microflora from the faecal droppings of said poultry.
