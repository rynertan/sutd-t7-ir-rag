# ELECTRONIC COIN BOX, PARTICULARLY FOR PUBLIC TELEPHONE UTILITIES

## Abstract
A coin box is disclosed which comprises a single slot 10 for the introduction of coins of different face values and telephone tokens, and a single gathering raceway 11 , electronic means 14 of storing the face value of each coin and related spatial address within the gathering raceway, and electronic means 23 of selectively accepting or return ing the coins contained in the raceway and as sorted by an acceptation optimizing program.
