# BATT ON MESH PRESS FELT HAVING INCREASED ABRASION RESISTANCE, BATT RETENTION AND DIMENSIONAL STABILITY

## Abstract
A batt on mesh press felt having improved abrasion re sistance, batt retention, and dimensional stability comprising a fibrous batt in combination with a woven mesh base. The woven mesh base comprises an ionomer resin encapsulated nylon yarn in the cross machine direction and nylon yarn in the machine direction.
