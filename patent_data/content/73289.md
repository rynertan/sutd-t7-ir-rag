# Remotely controlled mirror apparatus for motor vehicles.

## Abstract
A remotely controlled mirror apparatus having a shock absorbing mechanism and a control mechanism 55, 61 , wherein even when a mirror housing 13 is applied with strong external force thereby to be inclined or fallen, a mirror 11 follows to the inclination of the mirror housing 13 for preventing the mirror 11 from damage thereof and further the mirror housing 13 can be surely returned to an initial or normal position thereof.
