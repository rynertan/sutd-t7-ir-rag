# A device for attaching a second component to a first component in a secure but detachable manner.

## Abstract
A device for attaching a second component handrail to a first component upright in a secure but detachable manner. The second component 3 has a clip 6 in the form of an angle. The first component 1 has a recess 3 large enough to accept the clip 6 . The first component 1 works in conjunc tion with a locking device 8 so designed that in one position it will accept the clip 6b but that in a different position it will permit the locking of the clip 6 .
