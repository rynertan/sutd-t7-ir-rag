# Process for drying alcohols.

## Abstract
A process for the recovery of a water soluble alcohol from an aqueous mixture of the alcohol comprises incorpor ating in the mixture at least 0.5 of a base selected from ammonium or alkali or alkaline metal hydroxide, ammonium or an alkali or alkaline metal carbonate or mixtures thereof and at least 0.2 of an electrolyte. The aqueous alcohol mixture separates into two immiscible layers, and the layers are separated.
