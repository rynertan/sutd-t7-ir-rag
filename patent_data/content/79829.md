# Hydraulically operated fan assembly for a heat exchanger.

## Abstract
A fan assembly for a heat exchanger assembly associ ated with a vehicle having an internal combustion engine, which comprises a hydraulic motor 20 including a drive shaft 46 mounted proximate said heat exchanger assembly 10 , a fan 56 mounted on said drive shaft 46 a hydraulic pump 18 including a shaft 26 and an electrically operated magnetic clutch assembly 36 disposed on said shaft for driving said hydraulic pump by said internal combustion engine and a switch means 64 responsive to a preselect condition to assume an operative mode to activate said electrically operated magnetic clutch assembly 36 .
