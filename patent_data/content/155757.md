# Method and apparatus for de coking.

## Abstract
Method and Apparatus for automated hydro blast de coking of delayed coke drums wherein programmed central control 12 receives input of drill stem tension, drill bit elevation and drill stem rotation along with indications as to hydraulic hoist power and air pressure input to the drill stem rotary motor, and functions to provide control outputs to automatically control the pilot and main bed cutting procedures attendant an entire drum de coking operation.
