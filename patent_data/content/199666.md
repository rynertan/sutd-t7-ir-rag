# Controlling the rate of diamine curing agents in polyurethane cements.

## Abstract
A method for preparing a polyurethane which has good long term flexibility, solvent resistance and resistance to hydrolysis, said method comprising mixing three compo nents just prior to curing said components comprising a prepolymer solution, a cement solution and a crosslinking solution, said crosslinking solution comprising a solvent, an aromatic diamine and the ketimine of an aliphatic diamine.
