# Device for cleaning the rolls of draft rolling mills of textile machines.

## Abstract
A device for cleaning the rolls of draft rolling mills of textile machines, includes a support bar 16 arranged parallel to the axis of the roll 12 , and a cleaning fillet 18 of resiliently deformable material carried by the support bar 16 and having a longitudinal lip 18b in pressure contact with the circumferential surface of the roll 12 . The lip 18b of the cleaning fillet 18 has a longitudinal stiffening reinforcement 18c in the zone adjacent the contact of said lip 18b with the roll 12 .
