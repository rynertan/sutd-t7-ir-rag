# Treatment of heterogeneous liquid materials.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a continuous process for treat ment of feed in the form of a heavy hydrocarbon water dis persion or dispersions. The feed 7 is diluted with a hydrocar bon solvent 8 and contacted in a contactor 9 with a water stream 11 . A product stream 10 containing bitumen oils and a discard stream 12 comprising water and solids are separately removed from the contactor 9 .
