# A METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR STRAIGHTENING DEFORMED VEHICLE BODIES

## Abstract
A method and apparatus for straightening a deformed vehicle body 1 , in which a straightening force is applied to a deformed part 1a of the body. In accordance with the invention the straightening force is given pulsations by means of a pulsatile force generator, e.g. a vibrator 12 18 which is in force transfer connection with a straightening force transfer mechanism 10, 7, 9 .
