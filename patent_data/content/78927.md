# Method of mounting a connector terminal on an optical fibre.

## Abstract
A fiber optic connector includes a ferrule 12 which receives the enct ofa fiber optic cable 14 . The optic fiber 22 of the cable is located in an axial bore 20 in the terminal face of the ferrule, and an annular central portion 32 defined by a groove 30 which surrounds the axial bore is radially inwaraly pinched to position the optic fiber in the center of the terminal face.
