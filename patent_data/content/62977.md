# IMPROVED OIL RECLAIMING APPARATUS

## Abstract
An apparatus for reclaiming lubricating oil which in cludes a housing 11 having an evaporation plate 15 dividing the housing into an evaporation chamber and a filter chamber. There are passages 22 through the evaporation plate so filtered oil can flow into the evaporation chamber, a heater 31 for heating the evaporation plate to cause volatile contaminants to leave the oil and an ultraviolet radiation source 37 which directs sufficient radiation upon the oil to sterilize it.
