# AEROSOL GEL

## Abstract
A pressurized composition in an aerosol container and adapted to form a spray upon release of pressure therefrom which composition is a liquid inside the container and forms a gel on contact with living tissue comprising water, propellant, volatile solvent and a polyoxyethylene polyoxypropylene copolymer, The preferred composition may also advantageously include a skin treating agent, and conventional skin treatment additives.
