# Forced convection oven.

## Abstract
An oven 1 has a housing 2 and a stationary support 109 inside the housing with a position for supporting food. A fan 40 and an electric heating element 96 supply a stream of hot air to the inside of the housing. A rotating drum 50 has a supply opening 80 for directing the stream of hot air towards the position for the food and for moving the stream of hot air about the position for the food to heat or cook the food.
