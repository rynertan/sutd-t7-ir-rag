# External rear view mirror for motor vehicles.

## Abstract
An external rear view mirror for motor vehicles, which can be adjusted from inside, has a body 12 connected through a first articulated joint 28 to a support member 16 by an arm 18 that can be oriented relative to this support member by means of a second articulated joint 26 . The two articulated joints comprise two hemispherical elements 30, 38 formed on the orientable arm 18 and cooperating with corresponding hemispherical elements 22, 44 carried by the body 12 and the support member 16 respectively.
