# Aluminium electroplating solution.

## Abstract
Electrolyte liquid for the electrodeposition of aluminium, containing lithium hydride and or lithium aluminium hydride and at least one aluminium halide dissolved in tetrahydrofur an or halogen and or methyl derivatives, the molar ratio between the Al halide and the lithium aluminium hydride being in excess of 3. In addition, this bath contains an alkali metal aluminum chloride. This liquid is conspicuous for its high conductivity and its great stability.
