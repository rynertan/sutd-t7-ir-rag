# Socket assembly connector for an electrical component.

## Abstract
An electronic package is disclosed including a socket assembly 40 for connecting an electronic component 10 to a circuit board 12 . A header 20 is secured to the circuit board 12 , and receives the socket assembly 40 which is connected to the header 20 along a direction parallel to the plane of the circuit board 12 . The socket assembly 40 includes a housing 42 and tray 44 . The tray 44 which accommodates the electronic component 10 , is slidably inserted into the housing 42 .
