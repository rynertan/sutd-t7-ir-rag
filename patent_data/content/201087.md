# Meltblown and coform materials having application as seedbeds, process and apparatus of making the same.

## Abstract
A seedbed of meltblown material is provided having incorporated therein seeds. It is preferred that in addition to the incorporation of seeds that there is also incorporated absorbent materials such as cellulose fibers and superabsorbent materials such as hydrogels. A process and an apparatus for making such seedbeds are also disclosed.
