# PNEUMATIC VANE PUMP WITH BODY OF STAMPED SHEET METAL

## Abstract
In a pneumatic vane pump of known type, the outer casing is formed from a cast support 1 and a cylindrical body 2 of stamped sheet metal which constitutes the cylin drical seat for the rotor 6 , with a consequent notable reduc tion in cost compared to the conventional solution with a cast cylindrical body.
