# Shaving apparatus.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a shaving apparatus comprising a shear plate 3 with hair entry apertures 4 and a cutting 5 unit which can be reciprocated relative to the shear plate 3 . The cutting unit 5 comprises a frame 8 carrying cutters, each cutter being provided with at least one hair pulling blade 9 which is movable relative to the cutter 6 . A construction which is easy to manufacture and which is also easy to mount and reproduce is obtained by connecting the hair pulling blades 9 to the frame 8 by resilient elements 11 , independently of the cutters 6 .
