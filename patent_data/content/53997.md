# A pneumatic tire and method for making same.

## Abstract
An improved pneumatic tire and improved method for building same on a high crown building drum. The lower bead portion of the tire is provided with an additional reinforcing ply structure 22 which is wrapped about a substantially inextensible bead wire 25. An improved method of building a tire on a high crown building drum wherein the additional reinforcing ply struc ture in the bead area is placed in tension during vulcaniza tion.
