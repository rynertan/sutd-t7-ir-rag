# RECTILINEAR GUIDE COMPRISING FIRST AND SECOND MEMBERS WHICH ARE MOVABLE ONE WITHIN THE OTHER

## Abstract
A rectilinear guide comprises a first member and a second member which is movable in the first member coaxially therewith one of the members comprising bearing elements and the other member comprising axial guide surfaces on which the bearing elements are movable and which are disposed in planes which contain the coinciding central axes of the members.
