# Colour display tube.

## Abstract
By manufacturing the reference studs 8, 9, 10 on the outside of a cone 1 of a colour display tube against which the system of deflection coils is placed from non ferromagnetic material and at least the side of the studs 8, 9, 10 which is secured against the cone 1 from stainless steel, titanium or a titanium alloy, a rigid, lasting glued joint between the studs 8, 9, 10 and the cone 1 becomes possible and the deflection fields are not disturbed.
