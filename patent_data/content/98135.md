# Solubilized platinum II complexes.

## Abstract
Platinum amines with anti tumour activity have the formula cis Pt A ₂ X ₂ , wherein A represents a polyhy droxylated amine or A₂ represents a polyhydroxylated diamine, and X represents a halide or carboxylate radical or X₂ represents a dicarboxylate, sulfate, orthophosphate, or pyrophosphate radical, or a combination of aquo and sulfate, orthophosphate or pyrophosphate.
