# VARIABLE KEYSTROKE PRESSURE APPARATUS

## Abstract
A keyboard which is capable of varying the pressure re quired for the keystroke is provided and comprises an inflatable means 10 being a variable pressure bladder 11 which fits over the keyboard between the base plate 12 and the keys 13 . The operator may vary the pressure delivered to the sys tem as desired and may also preset a cycle of time intervals with pressure on and pressure off . Alternatively, the pressure supply may be linked to a num ber of keyboards and a programmed variable pressure cycle will be delivered to all the keyboards.
