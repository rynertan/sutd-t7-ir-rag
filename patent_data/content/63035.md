# Apparatus and method for baiting hooks of longline fishing gear.

## Abstract
An apparatus and method for semi automatically baiting the hooks of long line used in commercial deep sea fishing includes a bait holding receptacle 9 , and a slotted separator 12 positioned in or in association with the receptacle and extending substantially from end to end thereof, whereby ground line 15 with leaders 16 periodically attached to the ground line is passed behind the separator 12 such that a gangion portion of the leader extends through the slot in the separator 12 and the hook of the leader secures itself to a piece of bait held in the bait holding receptacle 9 .
