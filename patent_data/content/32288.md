# Improved gamma sorbitol polymorph and uses thereof.

## Abstract
A modified gamma sorbitol polymorph having improved compressibility and hardness characteristics when formulated into a tablet while also exhibiting an absence or low degree of grit. Under a scanning electron microscope, the sorbitol polymorph of the invention is found to have a disrupted crystalline structure, substantial amounts of rela tively large crystals not being coplanar with each other.
