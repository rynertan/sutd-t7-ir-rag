# Insecticidal synergistic composition containing O,O diethyl O 3,5,6 trichloro 2 pyridinyl phosphorothioate and O ethyl O 2 chloro 4 bromophenyl S n propyl phosphorothioate and method of controlling insects therewith.

## Abstract
Insecticidal compositions containing a mixture of O,O diethyl O 3,5,6 trichloro 2 pyridinyl phosphorothioate and O ethyl O 2 chloro 4 bromophenyl S n propyl phosphoro thioate. Such compositions are useful in the kill and control of insects.
