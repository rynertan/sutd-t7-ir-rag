# Reforming of sulfur containing charge stock.

## Abstract
Process for reforming a hydrocarbon charge under reforming conditions in a reforming zone wherein added sul fur is introduced into the reforming zone to counteract exces sive hydrocracking of the charge utilizing a catalyst compris ing a minor proportion of platinum on a support and a minor proportion of iridium on a separate support, optionally with an additional metal component such as rhenium being included in the platinum containing component.
