# SOLID FUEL HEATING APPLIANCE

## Abstract
A heating appliance which burns solid fuel has a bottom feed to a furnace vessel 24 conveniently by means of an auger 28 . The furnace vessel 24 is arranged to be driven in rotation through gearing 56,57 which assists keeping a uniformly distributed fire in the furnace vessel, and makes possible the discharge of ash overflowing from the top of the vessel through an airlock system 64 to 71 . A water jacket 56 surrounds the furnace vessel and a poker 102 is moved in the furnace vessel as it rotates. Combustion air can be blown or sucked in by a fan 62 . The heating appliance can take the form of a domestic cooker with ovens 14,16 and also heating water 122 for central heating.
