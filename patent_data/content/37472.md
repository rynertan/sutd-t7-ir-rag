# System and process for determination of liquid solid interface and head in electromagnetic casting.

## Abstract
An apparatus and process for casting metals wherein the molten metal is contained and formed into a desired shape by the application of an electromagnetic field established by an inductor. The casting has a liquid solid interface defining a molten material head and solid material portions of the cast ing. The height of the liquid metal head, the location of the liquid solid interface at the periphery of the forming ingot, and or variations in the values of these parameters is dis played during the casting run by a system which monitors the in phase component of the voltage across the inductor 11 to determine variations in the total equivalent series resistance of the electromagnetic casting system as seen by the inductor 11 .
