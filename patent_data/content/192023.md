# LIQUID CRYSTAL PROJECTOR

## Abstract
A liquid crystal projector is disclosed, which comprises a light source 18 , a TV type liquid crystal display panel 14 and a projecting lens system 26 . The liquid crystal display panel faces the screen and is inclined with respect to a line perpendicular to an optical axis connecting the center of the liquid crystal display panel and the center of a screen 37 by a suitable visual field angle of the liquid crystal display panel.
