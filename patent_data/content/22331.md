# Workpiece manipulator.

## Abstract
A workpiece manipulator 9 has first and second fingers 10 and 11 which can be moved together and moved apart to grip and release a workpiece. The manipulator 9 as two spaced apart pulleys 16 and 17 with an endless belt 18 stretched between and around the pulleys 16 and 17 , to provide two linear belt segments 18a and 18b between the pulleys. The first finger 10 is coupled to one linear belt segment 18a and the second finger 11 is coupled to the other linear belt segment 18b . When the belt 18 is driven the two linear segments 18a, 18b move in opposite directions to bring the fingers 10 and 11 together or to move them apart.
