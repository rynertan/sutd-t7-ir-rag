# Lead frames for electronic and electric devices.

## Abstract
An electronic part composed of an alloy comprising, as a basic metal or metals, Cr and Zr with the total amount thereof being between 0.3 and 2.0 by weight provided that the amount of Cr is 1.5 by weight or less and that of Zr is 1.0 by weight or less and Cu as a balance, the surface of said part having been coated with Sn. The electronic part according to the present invention is excellent in the wetting property of a solder, weather distance of a solder and bonding property to Au and A .
