# Vertical roller mill.

## Abstract
In a vertical roller mill with a grinding table 1 rotatable about a vertical axis and grinding rollers 3 forced against the grinding table, the grinding rollers are rotatable on stationary shafts 4 which at their inner ends are attached to a common central frame 5 . The rollers 3 are forced against the grind ing table 1 by means of hydraulically operated or spring activated draw bars 9 connected to the outer ends of the roller shafts 4 .
