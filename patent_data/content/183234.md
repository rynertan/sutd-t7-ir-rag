# SLIP ISSUING APPARATUS

## Abstract
The slip issuing apparatus comprises a support or a container 1, 3 for supporting or containing plural kinds of slips having different slip widths, reparately a printing device 7 having a printing head 71 for determining each printing range of the printing head according to each specified kind of slip and for printing predetermined data on the specified slip within each determined printing range a conveying device 2, 4 for supplying a specified slip out of the slips to the printing device and another conveying device 5 for conveying the slip printed by the printing device to an outlet 8 . Since the printing range can be changed according to slip widths, it is possible to print slips of plural kinds with a single printing device and to discharge the printed slips to a single outlet.
