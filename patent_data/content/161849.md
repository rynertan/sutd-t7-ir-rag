# CL 1724 ANTIBIOTIC ANTITUMOUR COMPOUNDS, PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS COMPRISING THE COMPOUNDS AND A NOVEL MICROORGANISM FOR PRODUCING THE COMPOUNDS

## Abstract
A strain of actinomycete, NRRL 15758, is capable of pro ducing the CL 1724 complex of compounds under conditions of aerobic fermentation in the presence of assimilable sources of carbon and nitrogen. The compound CL 1724B 2 exhibits antimicrobial and antitumor activity.
