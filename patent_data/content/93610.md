# SUBSTITUTED UREA HERBICIDES

## Abstract
A compound having the formula R²SCH R N R¹ C X NHR³, useful in the control of weeds is disclosed, the groups R¹, R², R³, and X being as indicated in Tables A,B,G,D,E,F and G of the specification. Intermediates, methods and reaction mixtures for mak ing the compounds are disclosed. Also disclosed are herbicidal compositions containing the compounds and methods of controlling weeds using the compounds.
