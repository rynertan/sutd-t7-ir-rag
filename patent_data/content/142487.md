# Novel atrial peptides.

Novel atrial peptides having useful natriuretic activity are disclosed with the following amino acid sequence R₁ cys phe gly gly arg ile asp arg ile gly ala gln ser gly leu gly cys asn R₂ wherein R₁ H, ser, ser ser, and R₂ OH, ser, ser phe arg, ser phe arg tyr, or the physiologically acceptable salts, esters or amides thereof.
