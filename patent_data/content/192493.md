# Process for protecting materials from atmospheric agents and pollutants.

## Abstract
A process for protecting materials such as marble, stone, tiles, cement and the like from the action of atmospheric agents and pollutants, by applying a perfluoropolyether onto the surface of the material, wherein there is utilized a perfluoropolyether selected from certain defined classes and having at one or both its ends functional groups selected from carboxylic, ester and amidic groups.
