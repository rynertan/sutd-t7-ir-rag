# Absorption article such as a diaper or a sanitary napkin, and a method for the manufacture thereof.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to absorption articles such as diapers or sanitary napkins comprising partly a liquid permeable first outer layer 1 and a preferably liquid impermeable second outer layer 2 applied to either side of an absorption body 3 encased therein partly elastic members applied under tension and intended for bringing the edge portions of said article into tightly sealing contact with the body of the wearer. The primary distinguishing feature of the inventive article is that the elastic members 24, 25 are comprised in a network.
