# Insecticidal synergistic composition containing O,O diethyl O 3,5,6 trichloro 2 pyridinyl phosphorothioate and O ethyl O 4 methylthio phenyl S n propyl phosphorothioate and method of controlling insects therewith.

## Abstract
Insecticidal compositions containing a mixture of 0,0 diethyl 0 3, 5, 6 trichloro 2 pyridinyl phosphorothioate and 0 ethyl 0 4 methylthio phenyl S n propyl phosphorothioate. Such compositions are useful in the kill and control of insects, particularly insects of the Lepidoptera order and especially of the genus Heliothis.
