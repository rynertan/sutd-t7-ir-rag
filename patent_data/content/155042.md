# An apparatus for discharging dirty liquid from a vessel under pressure.

## Abstract
An apparatus 1 for discharging dirty liquid from a vessel 2 under pressure, comprising a valve body 3 having a supply and discharge opening, said body accommodating a valve 11 having a stem extending beyond said valve body and adapted for coaction with a central seat 15 having a discharge opening. Said stem 10 is further connected to, or integral with, the piston rod 8 of a piston and cylinder 7 assembly arranged in alignment with said stem. Said valve body, stem and seat are designed and arranged within the valve body in such a manner that the fluid to be discharged can flow from all sides radially inwardly towards the discharge opening.
