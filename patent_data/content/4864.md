# Mandrel for hydraulically expanding a tube into engagement with a tubesheet.

## Abstract
A mandrel 1 for hydraulically expanding tubes 3 into engagement with a tubesheet 5 , wherein the body 7 of the mandrel is flexible and preferably has an electrically isolating impermeable layer 12 and an eddy current coil 33 adjacent the leading end thereof, the tube 3 into which the mandrel 1 is placed and the body 7 of the mandrel 1 serving as leads for operating the eddy current coil 33 in order to determine when the leading end of the mandrel 1 is adjacent the inner edge of the tubesheet 5 . With such a mandrel, also remote and obstructed tube can be reached and the mandrel can be properly positioned therein.
