# Device for moulding bricks.

## Abstract
A device for moulding bricks comprising a press 5 to form blanks, moulding trays 2 and projecting means 8 formed by a carrier surface 15 for inverting a blank by mov ing it along a circular path and for projecting it into a moulding tray 2 wherein the carrier surface 15 is guided in a manner such that it performs a pivotal movement during the move ment along the last part of the circular path.
