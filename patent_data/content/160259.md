# BEZEL CLOSURE DEVICE PARTICULARLY FOR EYEGLASSES FRAMES

## Abstract
The bezel closure device 1 comprises bezel tube halves 2, 3 rigid with the ends of a metal bezel 6 . The tube halves 2, 3 define contiguous bases 7, 8 adapted to contact each other to close the bezel 6 . Each tube half 2, 3 includes a throughgoing hole 9 , which is threaded internally to receive a screw 10 , including a shank 11 , formed with threads 16 at a lower portion thereof. A spring washer 15 prevents the screw 10 from becoming loose in the throughgoing hole 9 .
