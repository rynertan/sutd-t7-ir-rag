# AN ELECTRIC LAMP ARRANGEMENT

## Abstract
For the purpose of providing an imitation candle in accordance with the invention, an electric light bulb 15 is fitted with a light permeable and light scattering lamp casing 1 . Arranged on the inner surface 5 of the lamp casing are spacing elements 6,7,8 which hold the outer surface 14 of the light bulb 15 spaced from the inner surface 5 of the lamp casing 1 . The lamp casing 1 is open at both ends, and air is conducted along the inner surface of the lamp casing, to cool the same.
