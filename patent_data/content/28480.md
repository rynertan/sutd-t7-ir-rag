# Photographic camera with provision for recording supplemental data.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to photography, and, more particularly, to a camera having provisions for recordirig data adjacent to or superimposed on the frames exposed on the film. The camera is provided with an illuminated display 42 positioned behind the frame to be exposed, the display 42 being contained within a space defined by the back of the film 26A . One of the guide rollers 22 that positions the film is divided to leave a gap at its center permitting a data image from the display to be recorded on a portion of the film near the edge of a frame that would otherwise be obsecured. Inter changeable components may be used to permit the data to be superimposed on the primary image or recorded in a space between frames.
