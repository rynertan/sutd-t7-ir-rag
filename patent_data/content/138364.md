# Multifrequency tone distribution using a conference arrangement.

## Abstract
There is disclosed a multifrequency tone distribution ar rangement for a communication system having a common time division multiplexed bus A, B in which digital sam ples of tone frequencies are supplied from a central signal generating circuit 100 to system port circuits 200 via se lected time slots under the control of a central call proces sor 300 . Each port circuit automatically accepts selected signals from the common bus for conferencing to produce multifrequency digit tones and service tones.
