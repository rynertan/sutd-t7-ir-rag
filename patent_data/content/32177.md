# Invalid chair.

## Abstract
An invalid chair comprises a flexible padded seat 18 formed with an opening 24 , reinforcing means 62, 62A located within the seat and at the underside of the seat respectively on either side of the opening, flanges 48 on the underside of the seat, and a removable waste receiving receptacle 44 sliding in the flanges and extending over the full length of the opening. A detachable flexible padded flap is provided for closing the opening after the receptacle has been removed. The removal and closing of the flap, and the insertion and removal of the receptacle can all be accomp lished by the chair occupant without leaving the chair.
