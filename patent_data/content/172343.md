# DIGITAL CONTRAST CONTROL CIRCUIT FOR DISPLAY UNIT

## Abstract
A digital contrast control circuit for a display unit according to the present invention has an updown counter 45 for performing up or down count operations in re sponse to a contrast control key signal supplied from a contrast control key arranged on a keyboard 43 . A digital contrast output from the up down counter is supplied to a D A converter 49 arranged within a liquid crystal display unit 47 . The D A converter converts the digital contrast output to an analog contrast signal.
