# A fence assembly.

## Abstract
The fence assembly 1 comprises a plurality of panels 2 of expanded metal mesh having a plurality of closed meshes 3 . The panels 2 are arranged in zig zag relation ship, and each panel 2 is joined to another panels 2 by means of portion 6 of some of the meshes 3 of the panel 2 adjacent an edge 5 thereof, projecting through meshes 3 of the other panel 2 . The interlocking meshes are intertwined 7 to retain the panels interlocked. The fence assembly 1 may be secured to, or buried in, a trench in the ground.
