# An arrangement for the movement of disabled patients from a bed onto a wheel chair and vice versa.

## Abstract
A device equipped in such a way so to allow to a person to be lying down in a bed 1 or sitting on a wheel chair 4 to pass from the lying to the sitting position on the bed 1 with all the intermediate positions, by means of a liftable section 8 , to lift the lower limbs with a flexion up to 90 of the hips and knees by means of joint rods 9 , 10 , with all the intermediate positions, to pass from the lying position in bed 1 to the one sitting on a wheel chair 4 and viceversa, particularly suited for persons and patients suffering from paraplegia and tetraplegia.
