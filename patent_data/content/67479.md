# N TRICHLOROMETHYL THIO 4 CYCLOHEXENE 1,2 DICARBOXIMIDE CONTAINING COMPOSITION, A PROCESS FOR TREATING SEEDS WITH SAID COMPOSITION, AS WELL AS THE TREATED SEEDS

## Abstract
A seed dressing flowable composition comprising N trichloromethyl thio 4 cyclohexene 1,2 dicarboximide, polyvinyl alcohol, Rhodamine dye and water, and optionally other ingredients, as well as seed treated with said composi tion.
