# Side mirror driving apparatus.

## Abstract
A side mirror driving apparatus having a side mirror 111 swingably attached to a housing 1 and a driving apparatus supported by the housing 1 . The driving appar atus includes a gear 104 driven by an electric motor 108 and the mirror 111 is operatively linked with the gear 104 by means of a lift lever 116, 117 so that the mirror 111 is inclined in an up and downward direction and or left and rightward direction. At a side of a rear surface of the gear 104 , there is provided a set position stopping device 122, 123, 124 , so that the mirror 111 can be automatically returned to its initial position.
