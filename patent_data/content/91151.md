# A rail vehicle disc brake caliper.

## Abstract
A rail vehicle disc brake caliper has a base frame 1 with a pivotally attached brake lever 3 . Brake pads are provided on these parts in the vicinity of a disc to be braked. A brake cylinder 16 for acting on the brake lever is attached to the base frame. In order to obtain a space saving, simple and sturdy design with a required internal leverage and with a slack adjusting function a slack adjuster 15 of the rotary, lever type is arranged with its rotary part on a shaft 14 pivotally arranged in the base frame and its free arm connected to the piston rod 16 of the brake cylinder 16 . This shaft is provided with a cam 7 , preferably connected to the brake lever 3 by means of connecting links 18 .
