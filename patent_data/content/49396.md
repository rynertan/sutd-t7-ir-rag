# Poppet valve shield.

## Abstract
A thermal barrier 10 fixed to a valve head 12 of a diesel engine poppet valve 14 is disclosed. The barrier includes a thin sheet metal cap 18 having a concave shield portion 18a, an annular fold or corrugation 18b circumscrib ing the shield portion, and a cylindrical skirt portion welded to the valve head. The cap and the face portion 12a of the valve head define an evacuated chamber 20 which is preferably filled with an insulating material 24 which also provides structural support for the shield portion.
