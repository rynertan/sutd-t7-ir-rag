# Memory cassette holding apparatus.

## Abstract
An apparatus for holding a memory cassette 11 in a cas sette holder 12 wherein the memory cassette 11 cannot be ejected from the holder 12 , even by pressing an eject button 15 , while data is being written into or read from the memory cassette 11 . Provided are means 16 for generating a signal WR, RD indicating that a read or write operation is in progress, a locking mechanism 19 for locking a cassette ejecting mechanism 13 to render the latter inoperable, and a drive cir cuit 18 responsive to the generated signal for actuating the locking mechanism 19 to prevent ejections of the memory cassette 11 until the read or write operation is completed.
