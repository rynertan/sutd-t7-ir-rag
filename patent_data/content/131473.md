# Composite filter and the use of such a filter.

## Abstract
A composite filter for intercepting light of a radiation spectrum in the region of 600 900 nm comprising at least one organic film filter and at least one glass filter, in which said organic film filter absorbs light of a radiation spectrum in the region of 600 700 nm and said glass filter absorbs light of a radiation spectrum in the region of 700 900 nm. An observa tion system for dark field utilizing a multi channeled photo multiplier tube, which is provided with said composite filter is disclosed. Further disclosed is a filamentary display device containing plural bar type coiling filaments encased in a cas ing having a transparent window portion in which said trans parent window portion is provided with said composite filter.
