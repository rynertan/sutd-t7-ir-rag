# Heat shrinkable laminate film.

## Abstract
Disclosed herein is a heat shrinkable laminate film comprising outer layers of a polyolefine, a gas barrier layer of a copolymer of vinylidene chloride, at least one interme diate layer of an ionomer and adhesive layers disposed be tween any of the above layers, the total thickness of the in termediate layer s of an ionomer being 5 to 20 of the total thickness of the heat shrinkable laminate film. The heat shrinkable laminate film is particularly useful in packaging foodstuffs.
