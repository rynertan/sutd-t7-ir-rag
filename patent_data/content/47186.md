# Jewellery device.

## Abstract
An adjustable necklace 10 or bracelet which comprises a length of chain having a one end and another end first 12 and second 14 engaging elements having passageways therethrough means 18 to secure the one end of the chain to the second engaging element, after the chain passes through the passageway of the first element means 16 to secure the other end of the chain element to the first engaging element, the chain element passing through the passageway of the second engaging element and means 22 to engage frictionally the exterior of the chain element in the passageways of the first and second elements, to permit the chain element to be engaged in a slidable manner in each passageway and to secure the chain element in position after movement.
