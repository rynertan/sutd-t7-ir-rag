# HERBICIDAL PHENOXYALKANOIC ACID DERIVATIVES, THEIR PREPARATION, COMPOSITIONS CONTAINING THEM, AND THEIR USE

## Abstract
The invention provides low volatility derivatives of the herbicide 2 4 chloro 2 methylphenoxy propionic acid which are esters with alkanols of 10 to 20 carbon atoms or polyesters with polyhydric alcohols. A process for their preparation and herbicidal compositions containing them are also described.
