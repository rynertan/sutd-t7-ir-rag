# Process for the dehalogenation of 6,6 dibromopenicillanic acid 1,1 dioxide.

## Abstract
A new process is described for the preparation of penicillanic acid, 1,1 dioxide by dehalogenation of 6,6 dibromopenicillanic acid 1,1 dioxide and or 6 alpha halopencillanic acid 1,1 dioxide with magnesium in associa tion with an acid.
