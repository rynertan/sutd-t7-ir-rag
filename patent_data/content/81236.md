# A compound seasoning composition, a process for its production and its use as a food component.

## Abstract
Compound Seasoning Compositions on the basis of glutamic acid salts are provided, which have a gram equivalent ratio of sodium potassium in the range of 0 3.5 1, a gram equivalent ratio of calcium potassium in the range of 0 1.5 1 and a ratio of mole number of glutamic acid total gram equivalent number of potassium, sodium and calcium in the range of 0.1 1.2 1. These seasoning compositions show superior taste intensifying properties and impart rich full bodied flavor to different kinds of food.
