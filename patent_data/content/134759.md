# A BELLOWS TYPE SHUTTING MEANS THAT CAN BE OPENED FROM EITHER SIDES

## Abstract
The invention relates to a bellows type shutting means having sliding wings, for closing the entrance to a room in dwelling houses and the like, in which the wings 1 , mounted on relevant upper and lower guides by the aid of hinges that are arranged to slide on said guides, can so translate all the way on said guides that the passage to the room will be cleared in a thorough manner.
