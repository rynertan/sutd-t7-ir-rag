# SELF SUPPORTING DECORATIVE STRUCTURE

## Abstract
A self supporting decorative structure has a frame with legs 1 interconnected by struts 2. Channels 4 carried by opposed faces of the legs 1 receive tiles 5 which are prefer ably glazed ceramic tiles. A plant pot may be stood on the resulting structure. Alternative structures may be in the form of a column a window box or a screen and structures may be placed one on top of another and or linked in side by side relationship.
