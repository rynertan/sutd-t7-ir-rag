# Compositions including mercaptoorganopolysiloxanes, aliphatically unsaturated polydiorganosiloxanes and carboxylic acid salts of metals.

## Abstract
Oxygen curable compositions are provided by mixing mercaptoorganopolysiloxane, aliphatically unsaturated polydiorganosiloxane and a metal salt of a carboxylic acid, where the metal is iron, manganese, or nickel.
