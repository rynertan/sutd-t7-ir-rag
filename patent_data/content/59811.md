# Bifunctional air electrodes containing elemental iron powder charging additive.

## Abstract
A bifunctional air electrode 12 for use in electrochemic al energy cells, comprising a hydrophilic layer 17 and a hydrophobic layer 14 , whereby the hydrophilic layer essen tially comprises a hydrophilic composite which includes 1. carbon 2. elemental iron particles having a particle 3. size of between about 25 microns and about 700 microns diameter 4. an oxygen evolution material 5. a non wetting agent, and a catalyst whereby at least one current collector 19 is formed into said composite.
