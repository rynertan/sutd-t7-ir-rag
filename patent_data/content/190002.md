# POLYMERIC COMPOSITIONS, THEIR PREPARATION AND THEIR USES IN PRINTING PASTES

## Abstract
A composition comprising a water soluble, low molecu lar weight, anionic polymer and a diester of a polyethylene glycol with a fatty acid can be included, generally in combination with a particulate, cross linked, water swellable polymeric thickener, in a textile print paste in order to reduce flushing during printing.
