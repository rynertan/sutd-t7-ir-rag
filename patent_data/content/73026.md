# 17,17 Bis substituted thio 3 ketoandrostenes and pharmaceutical compositions.

## Abstract
Described are 3 Ketoandrostenes having in the 17 position the substituents R₁ S and R₂ S wherein R₁ and R₂ are the same or different and each is alkyl, cycloalkyl or aryl, which have antiinflammatory activity.
