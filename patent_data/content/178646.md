# Small size fluorescent lamp.

## Abstract
A small size discharge lamp includes a glass tube 1 having first and second ends, with an outer diameter thereof being smaller than 5 millimeters and a length thereof being shorter than 300 millimeters. At opposite ends of the glass tube, an elongated filament 3 and an elongated getter 4 are provided adjacent each other and parallel to the the axial direction of the glass tube.
