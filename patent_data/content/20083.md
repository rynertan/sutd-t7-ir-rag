# Method and apparatus for embossing.

## Abstract
In the process of embossing film by continuously passing the film 12 through a nip 20 formed by cooperating pattern roll 14 and backup roll 16 , the improvement of passing the embossed pattern while in registration with the pattern roll through a second nip 22 to improve the pattern detail. The apparatus includes a second backup roll 18 in pressurized contact with the pattern roll 14 forming the second nip 22 . The surface configuration of the rolls may be such as to locally perforate the film within the pattern.
