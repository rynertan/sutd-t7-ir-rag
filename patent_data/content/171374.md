# Hydraulic down the hole rock drill.

## Abstract
Pressurized water is used to operate a percussive down the hole drill and the spent motive fluid is used as a flushing fluid. A rear cylinder chamber 24 is constantly pressurized and a valve 28 controls the inlet from the cham ber 24 to a bore 23 through the piston hammer 21 . A valve 42 controls the outlet from the front cylinder cham ber 26 to a flushing channel 38 in the drill bit 21 . A check valve 45 controls the outlet from the bore 23 to the front cylinder chamber 26 .
