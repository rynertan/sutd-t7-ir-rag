# Force motor.

## Abstract
A direct drive valve wherein a force motor 10 controls the position of a valve slide 20 through a linkage 14 . A magnetic assembly 32 includes a permanent magnet 34 and electrical having cantilevered springs 68, 70 that are formed with coils 36, 38 . The force motor includes a spring assembly 62 petals. An inner edge of each spring abuts a respective spacer 80, 82 secured on a rod 50 which is secured to the armature 48 . The armature 48 is guided by a plurality of balls 84 that contact both an internal tube assembly 47 and the armature 48 .
