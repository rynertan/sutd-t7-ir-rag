# A BAG

## Abstract
A bag 40 has two opposite facing surfaces 45,48 defin ing a pre formed opening 47 at least one of the surfaces 45, 48 having a re usable pressure sensitive adhesive 49 thereon so that closure of the opening 47 is effected by pressing the said surfaces 45, 48 together in the area of the adhesive 49 thereby to provide a re openable closure.
