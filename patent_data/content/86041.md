# An inertia flywheel drive mechanism for a toy vehicle.

## Abstract
A toy vehicle having a drive wheel and winch driven by an inertia flywheel. The winch has a winch drum 70 that winds a core attached thereto in only one direction of rotation of the drum. A double detent mechanism 160, 162 precisely positions the winch transmission in its neutral position. A fixed gear tooth 144 brakes or locks the winch drum 70 when the winch transmission is in its neutral position.
