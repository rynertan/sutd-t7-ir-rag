# Disk cleaning mat.

## Abstract
A disk cleaning mat wherein in the surface of a mat body 1 first, second and third annular recesses 4, 7, 14 for holding an LP disk, EP disk and a compact disk, respectively, are provided, and grounding portions 18 electrically connected to the reverse side of the mat body 1 are mounted on the respective recesses 4, 7, 14 . The first and second recesses 4, 7 are concentric with each other, while the third recess 14 is centered at a point offset from the common center of the first and second recesses 4, 7 .
