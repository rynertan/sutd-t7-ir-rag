# Seal for quick disconnect coupling.

## Abstract
A seal 10 , for a quick disconnect coupling. The seal is made up of several washers 10a, 10b, 10c, 10d, 10e , each washer being made of a plastic such as polytetra fluoroethylene PTFE . The several layers allows the male member 14 to be easily inserted while providing a good seal, and the PTFE breaks in quickly for easy operation.
