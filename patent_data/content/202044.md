# Aluminium alloy.

## Abstract
An aluminum alloy having properties including extrusi bility, forgeability and malleability, the alloy containing 4.0 to 12 of zinc, 0.3 to 5.0 of magnesium and one or more elements selected from the rare earth elements, wherein the content of the selected element is in the range of 0.5 to 10.0 and the balance being substantially aluminum and unavoid able impurities.
