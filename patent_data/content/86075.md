# Vehicle mounted spraying system.

## Abstract
A vehicle mounted spraying system includes a spray boom assembly 205 which is mounted onto a vehicle, preferably a tractor. The spraying process is automatically controlled to give correct dosages and the control system includes two microprocessors 206, 207 , a first 206 associ ated with the spray boom assembly 205 which monitors the spray process and a second 207 associated with a display 225 and control 224 unit in the vehicle which monitors the vehicle operating conditions and drives the display to provide information to the operator.
