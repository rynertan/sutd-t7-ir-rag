# ELECTRIC CONNECTOR

## Abstract
An electric connector comprises elongated contacts and an insulating housing. Each the elongated contact comprises at one end a curved contact element having at a front end a support piece extending in a longitudinal direction of the contact, at the other end a terminal element, and an elastic fixing lug integral with the contact and supported at one end on a side of the curved contact element by the contact at an intermediate portion thereof between the curved contact and terminal elements.
