# Control unit for television receivers.

## Abstract
Major adjustment of an equipment such as a television receiver are attained by remote control signals from a remote control signal transmitter 3 provided for the equipment. A memory 5 for storing control data for a signal processing circuit 9 , a control circuit 1 for controlling the signal processing circuit and the memory, and a switching circuit 22 for setting the control circuit in a first mode or a second mode are provided. In the first mode, the signal processing circuit is controlled by the remote control signal, and in the second mode, the signal processing circuit is controlled by the same remote control signal and the resulting control data is written into the memory.
