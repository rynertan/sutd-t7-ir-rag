# A method of producing a suspension of fly ashes in water and an installation for realisation of this method.

## Abstract
The subject of the invention is a method of continous production of fly ashes in water of a high concentration of ash with eventual adding of slag or granular ash and an installation for realisation of this method. The production process is devided into subsequent stages and is realised in separate zones of a flow mixer. Ash, slag, water and granular ash are fed in a continuous manner.
