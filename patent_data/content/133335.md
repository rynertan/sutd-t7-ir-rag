# Stabilized bleach sensitive dyes in automatic dishwasher detergent compositions.

## Abstract
A method for stabilizing automatic dishwasher detergents containing bleach sensitive dyes against decolourization by the bleaching agents of the compositions. The detergent powders comprise a bleaching agent, a bleach sensitive dye and conven tional dishwasher detergent ingredients. Prior to the addition of bleaching agent, the composition ist pre conditioned by contact with a flow of air. The resultant powder has a colour that remains essentially unchanged even after storage for two months at elevated temperatures.
