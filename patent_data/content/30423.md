# A process for the production of 1,4 dihydro 4 oxo quinoline 2 carboxylic acids, or salts, esters or amides thereof and intermediates therefor.

## Abstract
There is described a process for the production of a 1,4 dihydro 4 oxo quinoline 2 carboxylic acid, or a salt, ester or amide thereof, which comprises cyclisation of a corresponding 2 aminobenzoyl pyruvic acid or an ester thereof, and if desired or necessary converting the resulting product to an appropri ate salt, ester or amide thereof, or vice versa.
