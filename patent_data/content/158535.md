# LAMINATED MATERIAL

## Abstract
A laminated material comprises a layer 2 of a microcel lular material capable of changing colour in response to the application of heat and pressure a layer of adhesive 3 bonded to the layer of microcellular material. The laminated material can be used in a method of applying a decorative layer or motif 13 to a substrate 11 using a die 5 . The die includes shaped projections 10 for heating and compress ing discrete areas of the microcellular material in order to change its colour, and includes severing edges 9 for cutting the decorative layer or motif 13 from the laminated material.
