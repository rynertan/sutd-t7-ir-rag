# Detachable connector for optical fibres.

## Abstract
A detachable connector for coupling the ends of optical fibres comprising two connector portions, each connector portion 20a, 20b having fixing means for detachably connecting the connector portions to each other, at least one rotatable symmetrical housing having a hole 37a, 37b which is coaxial to a central axis and a reference face on one end and adjusting means 49a, 49b secured therein which enables an optical axis of an end of an optical fibre to be accomodated in the bush 23a, 23b and adjusted parallel to the central axis of the hous ing when the two inter connected connector portions con tact each other by way of the reference faces.
