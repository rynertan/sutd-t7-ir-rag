# Magnetic recording disc cartridge.

## Abstract
In a magnetic recording disc cartridge, a hub assembly composed of an upper hub member 13 and a lower hub member 14 inseparably connected with each other clamps a peripheral portion of a central hole 15 defined in a magnetic recording sheet 10 between inner portions 13b, 14b of surfaces of the hub members with a portion of the magnetic recording sheet 10 opposed to the outer portions of the hub members unclamped.
