# CHLORELLA NURTURING DEVICE

## Abstract
A chlorella nurturing device comprises a chlorella nurtur ing tank constructed with a transparent material on the upper surface thereof and a reflection mirror assembly for reflecting downward vertically solar rays. The solar rays are reflected on the reflection mirror and guided into the chlorella nurturing tank through the transparent material at the upper surface thereof. The reflection mirror is constructed with a large number of dual surface reflection mirror plates unitarily arranged in parallel in a south north direction.
