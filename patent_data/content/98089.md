# Electrophotographic photosensitive material.

## Abstract
An electrophotographic photosensitive material less susceptible to ozone attack comprises an electroconductive substrate bearing a layer of a photoconductive composition comprising a dispersion of a charge generating pigment in a charge transporting medium, and containing an N,N di substituted carbamic acid salt in an amount of 20 to 200 by weight based on the charge generating pigment.
