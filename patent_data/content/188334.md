# Methods and apparatus for transferring data for digitally controlling video equipment.

## Abstract
A method and apparatus for transferring data in a digitally controllable video equipment TV having an inter nal bus 3 in which data are transmitted via the internal bus 3 using a clock pulse signal CK2 having a high repetition rate during a vertical blanking interval of a video signal and using a clock pulse signal CK1 corresponding to or synchronized with a horizontal synchronizing signal HP during intervals other than the vertical banking intervals.
