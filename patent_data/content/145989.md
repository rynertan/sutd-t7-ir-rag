# System and method for automatically controlling vehicle speed.

## Abstract
In a system and method for automatically controlling a vehicle s speed, the rate of change of the inter vehicle dis tance per predetermined unit of time is calculated from the detected inter vehicle distance between the controlled vehi cle and a preceding vehicle, a basic safe inter vehicle dis tance is corrected according to the calculated rate of change of the inter vehicle distance per predetermined time, and the vehicle speed is controlled according to the difference between the corrected safe inter vehicle distance and the detected inter vehicle distance.
