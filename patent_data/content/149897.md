# DOCUMENT COPIER FOR BOOKS

## Abstract
A document carrier, typically for supporting a book to be copied in a document copier by location of an opened page against an inclined window in a scanner housing, which carrier comprises a pair of separate plates 26, 28 supported in a trough like configuration by arms 27, 29 which connect the backs of the plates to a movable platform 30 , springs 31, 33 being provided, preferably to act between the platform and the supporting arms, to urge the plates into their said trough like configuration.
