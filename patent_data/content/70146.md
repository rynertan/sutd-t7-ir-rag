# Pressure sensitive copying paper.

## Abstract
A condensate free from stickiness and exhibiting an excellent initial color formability when used as color de veloper of pressure sensitive copying paper can be obtained by reacting p hydroxybenzoic acid and one p alkyl substituted phenol or a mixture of two or more p alkyl substituted phenols with formaldehyde in the presence of an acid catalyst and then directly taking out the condensate into cold water without any procedure such as dehydration, concentration, etc.
