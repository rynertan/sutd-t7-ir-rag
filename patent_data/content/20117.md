# CORTICOSTEROID FORMULATIONS CONTAINING SEBACATE CARRIER

## Abstract
Corticosteroid formulations in the form of creams, lotions or ointments are provided which are used as anti inflammatory agents, wherein the corticosteroid, such as halcinonide 21 chloro 9 fluoro 11β hydroxy 16α,17 1 methylethylidene bis oxy pregn 4 ene 3,20 dione is dissolved in a sebacate carrier, such as dibutyl sebacate.
