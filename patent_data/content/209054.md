# Molded case circuit breaker.

## Abstract
A molded case circuit breaker includes an operating mechanism having an overcenter toggle mechanism for ope rating a pair of electrical contacts. A pivotable, one piece cra dle includes a cradle latch surface for latching with a trip arm of a trip mechanism, a contact stop surface to limit movement of one of said electrical contacts and an arcuate cam surface to engage a cradle cam pin during a reset operation. The arcuate cam surface of the cradle is contoured to enable the resetting of the operating mechanism after a trip operation.
