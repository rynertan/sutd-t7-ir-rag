# Metallurgical vessel assembly.

## Abstract
A molten metal processing vessel is supported on a trunnion ring 16 by a plurality of triangular members 32 and adjustable links 75 . Each triangular member 32 is pivotally connected to the vessel and to a clamp ring 30 which engages the upper trunnion ring flange 20 . The con nection at the apex of the triangular member 32 includes a ball and socket 52 for universal pivotal movement. The links 75 are pivotally connected to the vessel and to a second clamp ring 74 which engages the lower trunnion ring flange 21 . A plurality of disc springs 68 are disposed between the second clamp ring 74 and the trunnion ring 16 .
