# Slat feeding apparatus for assembling machine.

## Abstract
Precut and punched fully formed slats for a venetian blind are mounted upside down in a stock 21 on a slat magazine cradle 19 disposed below the assembly station 11 of a venetian blind assembly apparatus, and are picked up, one at a time, by a suction cup 39 and fed in between two endless feed belts 48, 49 which pass around a roller 50, 51 so that the slats are fed the correct way up into the assembly station.
