# A process for preparing whipping cream and whipping cream products.

## Abstract
A process for preparing instantly whippable whipping cream and whipping cream products with an increased content of surfactants, whereby butter fat and lactic acid are separated from milk and or milk products and a surfactant, consisting of lactic acid esters of monoglycerides or of mixtures of lactic acid esters of monglycerides with monog lycerides, is prepared therefrom, and whereby said surfac tants are mixed with butter fat and the fatty mixture is emulsified in a solution containing milk protein and milk sugar.
