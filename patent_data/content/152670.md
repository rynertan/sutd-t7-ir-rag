# FILTER

## Abstract
In a filter of the type in which at least part of a casing is buried with its suction ports in pebbles spread inside a water tank in order to let the pebbles exhibit a filter opera tion and suction means is connected to the casing, the improvement wherein the filter casing consists of a suction portion buried in the pebbles together with its suction ports, and a purification portion connected to the suction portion and having the upper part thereof closed by a detachable lid, a filter material dividing the purification portion into a purification chamber and a non purification chamber and held by a frame member is removably fitted into the purifica tion portion, and the suction means is supported by the lid in such a manner as to face the interior of the purification chamber.
