# ORIENTEERING COMPASS

## Abstract
The invention relates to an orienteering compass com prising a compass housing 14 which is rotatably mounted on or in a transparent base plate 10 and which encloses a rotatably journalled compass needle 16 . The compass according to the invention is mainly characterised in that the base plate 10 has parallel north south line markings 11,12 and in that the compass housing 14 is provided with a course arrow marking 24 and that the base plate 10 is provided with a north and or south marking 30,32 adjacent the compass housing 14 .
