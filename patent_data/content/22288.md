# METHOD OF STERILIZING MALE ANTHERS IN PLANTS, COMPOSITIONS AND COMPOUNDS SUITABLE FOR USE IN SUCH A METHOD, AND METHOD OF PRODUCING F1 HYBRID SEED

## Abstract
A method of sterilizing the male anthers of a plant, which comprises applying to the plant a pyrrolidine derivative which is a compound of the general formula I or a salt and or an ester, N alkylamide, N,N dialkylamide, the amide or hydrazide thereof
