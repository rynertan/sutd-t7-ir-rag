# Hexitol derivatives, their preparation and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.

## Abstract
Hexitols of the general formula The hexitols of the present invention are prepared by hydrogenation of a corresponding benzyloxycarbonyl con taining 1,2 5,6 dianhydrohexitol, reaction of 1,2 5,6 dianhydrohexitol with a dicarboxylic acid anhydride and halogenation of a corresponding 1,2 5,6 dianhydrohexitol.
