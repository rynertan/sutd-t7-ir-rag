# Apparatus for the prevention of pressure sores.

## Abstract
A method and apparatus for the prevention of pressure sores at an unfeeling part of a person s body involves apply ing to the unfeeling part of the person s body a pressure sensor cell which senses the pressure thereat, applying to a feeling part of the person s body a discomfort generator cell, and connecting the pressure sensor cell to the discomfort generator cell such that the pressure sensation sensed by the former at the unfeeling part of the person s body is trans mitted by the latter to the feeling part of the person s body.
