# A machine for hank drawing and doffing.

## Abstract
The invention is concerned with a machine for draw ing yarn hanks from advancing hank carrying supports and doffing the drawn hanks on a carriage or container or conveyor belt. The machine such as 100 comprises a pliers holder head 101 wherein the pliers 125, 125 , each formed of two or more rods 126, 126 , etc. movable near one another, can be adjusted in spacing for adaptation to various dimensions of hanks M, M . The head is rotable about a horizontal axis a to arrange the hanks in hori zontal direction and is tiltable or upsettable about a further horizontal axis. Furthermore, the head is mounted to be movable parallel to the extension of the hank carrying sup ports, and preferably also parallel to the advancement or feeding of the hank carrying supports.
