# A drawer for electrical cabinets.

## Abstract
A drawer comprises a frame 1 5 and a front wall 6 19 52 64 coupled to the frame 1 5 and including at least one removable panel 12 17, 52 63 . In order to provide a modular, cheap and easily assembl able structure, the front wall 6 supports elongated resilient coupling elements 18,19,64 and the panel or panels 12 17 52 63 have stop surfaces 26 that can be coupled to the resilient coupling elements 18,19,64 through a snap action by applying a front thrust to the panel 12 17 52 63 , from the outside of the drawer.
