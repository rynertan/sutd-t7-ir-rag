# LIFTING ARRANGEMENT

## Abstract
A longitudinally extending arm 62 is attached to a vertical column 6 such that it is rotatable about the column and vertically movable therealong. The free end of the arm 62 has spool securing means 66 which in one embodi ment comprises three fingers 67 pivotable to engage a spool 20 . In an alternative embodiment the spool securing means 66 comprises longitudinally extending legs 102 each having an inwardly directed ledge 103 for engagement under a flange 104 of the spool. A vacuum lift 109 is also described as a means for securing a spool.
