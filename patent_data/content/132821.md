# Aqueous solution containing lipid soluble pharmaceutical substance and a process for preparing the same.

## Abstract
Disclosed herein is an aqueous solution containing a lipid soluble active vitamin substance and or ubiquinone and a process for preparing the same. The solution has a pH of 5.5 8 and additionally contains a hydrogenated lecithin and neutral amino acid. It is stable and free from side effects, and is rather easy to prepare.
