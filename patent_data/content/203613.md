# Light sensitive material containing microcapsules and image recording method using the same.

## Abstract
A light sensitive material and an image recording method using the same are disclosed. The material comprises a support having provided thereon a light sensitive layer containing at least a light sensitive silver halide, a reducing agent, a polymerizable compound, and a color image forming substance, wherein at least the polymerizable compound and the color image forming substance are encapsulated in the same microcapsules. The material exhibits high sensitivity and excellent stability with time.
