# FOAMABLE, ACIDIC CLEANING COMPOSITIONS

## Abstract
Soil comprising soap scum and hardness components can be efficiently removed by an acidic cleaning composition which comprises a major proportion of water, and acidic component comprising a mixture of a weak organic acid and a weak inor ganic acid, a surfactant system comprising a major proportion of an amino oxide surfactant and a cosolvent, which is prefer ably applied to the soil as a foam.
