# Sealed closure container package.

## Abstract
A sealed package comprising a container 1 , intended for use or reuse as a drinking vessel, and a cover 7 . The container 1 has a cylindrical surface 2 adjacent its mouth to receive a length of tape 3 which, on one side, carries a layer 5 of pressure sensitive adhesive. which can be peeled from the container 1 without leaving objectionable re sidues. The tape 3 carries a layer 6 of heat sealable material on its opposite face and this is heat sealed to an aluminium foil lid 7 , also bearing a coating of a heat sealing material 8 .
