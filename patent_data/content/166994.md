# Gas separation module.

## Abstract
A gas separating module comprises a container 1 having a gas inlet and outlet 5 and a bundle 10 of porous material tubes 3 that are incorporated in said container 1 and are open at both ends 8 . Each of the porous material tubes 3 has a smaller outside diameter at both ends than in the other portions, said porous material tubes 3 being fixed to said container 1 at said tapered ends. The module is durable and hermetic and can be manufactured at low costs.
