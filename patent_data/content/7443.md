# Bag folding machine.

## Abstract
In a bag folding machine of the type which includes a rolling section 18 for winding the bag 12 into a roll, with the rolling section 18 divided into two sub sections 28, 30 spaced a predetermined distance apart to form an open area 32 therebetween, including means 60 for withdrawing the bag from the rolling section 18 the improvement comprising said bag withdrawal means 60 being arranged to withdraw the bag through the open area 32 in a predetermined dis charge direction with each sub section 28, 30 having an entrance opening 25 with a substantially flat surface 50 on opposite sides of the open area 32 in a common plane trans verse to the discharge direction.
