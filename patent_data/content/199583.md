# Display assembly.

## Abstract
A display assembly has a flat panel 14 with data indicating areas 26 forming a seven segment display. Behind each data indicating area 26 are arranged three bulbs 20 disposed in a trough 30 , and all the seven troughs 30 are integrally formed in a single back panel 16 which is attached to the back of the front panel 14 . The bulbs 20 are mounted on a printed circuit board 18 and project through holes 32 on the bases of the troughs 30 . The bulbs 20 behind appropriate data indicating areas 26 are energised corresponding to the digit to be displayed.
