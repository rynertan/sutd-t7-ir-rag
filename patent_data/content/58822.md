# Method to alter or control the development and or life cycle of various plant species.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a method to influence, alter or control the growth, development and or life cycle of desirable and undesirable plants, comprising applying to same a biologically effective amount of certain substituted quinazolinones. The invention also relates to certain novel quina zolinones, useful to influence, alter or control the growth, development and or life cycle of desirable and undesirable plants.
