# Edible food containers and the method and apparatus for coating said containers.

## Abstract
Edible food containers are processed for use with a food product by applying a barrier coating composition to at least the interior surface thereof. The barrier coating composition comprises a sugar solution having a sugar content of at least 50 by weight and optionally containing other ingredients such as a flavor producing material or a vegetable gum. In another embodiment a moisture resistant barrier coating composition comprising a food grade fat, food grade modified fats or mixtures thereof is used. Disclosed are also methods and continuous methods for applying the coating material to the edible food container as well as apparatus suited for carrying out said methods and continuous methods.
