# Aluminium based bearing alloys.

## Abstract
An alloy for use as a bearing lining on a metal backing comprising, by weight, 1 to 11 silicon, 8 to 35 tin, and 0.2 to 3 copper, the balance being aluminium. The lining is first cast as a billet annealed, cold rolled, annealed and cold rolled a second time and then bonded to the metal backing. The lining optionally carries a soft overlay.
