# Substituted 1 aminoalkylamino 4 aryloxypiperidines, a process for their preparation, intermediates thereof, and their use as medicaments.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to substituted 1 aminoalkylamino 4 aryloxypiperidines of the formula CHEM where X is hydrogen, loweralkyl, CF3, acetyl or halogen n is 1 or 2 R1 is H2 or oxygen R2 is H, loweralkyl or benzyl and R3 is oxygen or O CH2CH2 O , which are useful as analgesic agents, a process for their preparation and intermediate compounds therefor.
