# Sense switch.

## Abstract
A switch comprising a cylindrical housing, actuator means 51 55 disposed at one end of the housing for contacting an object to be sensed by the switch, plunger means 31 35 slidably housed within the housing for receiving a displacement by the actuator means, and switch ing means 21 25 associated with the plunger means for switching a circuit in accordance with the actuation by the plunger means, said means actuator means including a conical supporting member 52 tiltably engaged with a flange member 14 formed in said one end of the housing.
