# A double acting hot gas engine.

## Abstract
A closed cycle in line double acting hot gas engine, such that an ordinary crankshaft can be used in which the regenerator coolers 17 20 are each arranged around the respective cylinders 1 4 concentrically and cylindrically with respect to the cylinder or, as an alternative, arranged symmetrically beside the cylinders line. Four pairs of annular sector manifolds, 1, each pair interconnected by heating tubes, are arranged over the engine so as to form a heat exchanger 21Mi 24Mi,21Mo 24Mo with cylindrical shape.
