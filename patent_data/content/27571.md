# Water sprinkling toy pistol.

## Abstract
Water sprinkling toy pistol 1a, 1b of the type which comprises a water magazine 4 for insertion into the pistol grip 3 . The water magazine 4 is in communication with means for ejecting a sprinkle of water through the pistol barrel 2 . Overlying the magazine 4 is a reservoir 13 for contain ing a liquid producing bubbles of the type of soap bubbles, wherein a bubble blowing ring 14 is immersed. The bubble blowing ring 14 can be raised from the reservoir 13 , through supporting means guided inside the pistol grip 3 , to deliver bubbles in the direction of the pistol barrel 2 .
