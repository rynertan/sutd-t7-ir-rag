# A plastics pipe part with socket provided with longitudinally extending passages.

## Abstract
Pipe part 1 with socket 2 being provided with longitud inally extending channels. The channels 3 also extend in the wall of the socket, the socket is provided with an annular groove 5 formed by sealingly pressing the walls of the chan nels onto each other. In a very preferred embodiment the socket engages a J shaped retaining ring 15 forming with the socket a recess for retaining the flange 22 of a sealing means, comprising a sealing sleeve.
