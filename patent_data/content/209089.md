# Use of quinazolines in the preparation of a medicament for treating and preventing ischemic cardiac disease or arrhythmia.

## Abstract
The use of a quinazoline compound of formula I CHEM in which R is a lower alkyl phenyl a phenyl substituted with a halogen, methoxy s , or methanesulfonyl furyl styryl or a styryl substituted with a halogen, methoxy s or methylenedioxy or a pharmaceutically acceptable salt thereof in the preparation of a medicament for treating and preventing ischemic cardiac disease or arrhythmia is described.
