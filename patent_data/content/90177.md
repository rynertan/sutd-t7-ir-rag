# Process for oxidizing halopyridines to halopyridine N oxides.

## Abstract
Described is a process for oxidizing 2 chloropyridine or 2 bromopyridine to the corresponding N oxide with perace tic acid generated in situ from H₂O₂ and acetic acid in the presence of a catalyst selected from the group consisting of sulfuric acid, alkali metal bisulfates, ammonium bisulfate, and mixtures thereof.
