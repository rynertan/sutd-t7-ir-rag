# Process for protecting materials from pollution.

## Abstract
A process far protecting marble, stones, tiles and arti cles formed from such materials from the degrading action caused by atmospheric and polluting agents, by applying onto the surfaces thereof a protective agent which is a particularly defined perfluoropolyether, the perfluoropoly ether being applied as such or diluted with a solvent or a mixture of solvents having a boiling temperature below 80 C, preferably a chlorofluorohydrocarbon.
