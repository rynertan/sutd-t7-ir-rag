# A new compound with analgesic, antiinflammatory and antipyretic activity, process for the preparation thereof and pharmaceutical compositions therefrom.

## Abstract
The compound 2 4 isobutylphenyl 1 propanol 3,4,5 trimethoxybenzoate is described. The substance is prepared by reacting 3,4,5 trimethoxybenzoic acid chloride with 2 4 isobylphenyl 1 propanol preferably in the presence of an acid acceptor. The compound exhibits valu able analgesic, antiinflammatory and antipyretic properties.
