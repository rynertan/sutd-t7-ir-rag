# Inflatable ball.

## Abstract
There is provided a ball for sports in which an air injection valve 2 attached to a rubber inner tube 1 is covered by outer layers 7,8,4 so as not to be exposed. An aperture 6 for insertion of an air injection needle is provided in the outer layers 7,8,4 so that it is in alignment with the hole 5 for insertion of an air injection needle of the air injection valve 2 .
