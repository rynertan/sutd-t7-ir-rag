# Flat electrical cable with connecting pins.

## Abstract
A flat electrical cable 1 is provided with connecting pins 5 by forming a compacted upstanding fold along its length, and by securing this fold by means of a reinforcing strip 6 bonded to and bridging the conductor s insulation. The rein forcing strip may have pegs 7 which pass between the pins 5 . The connecting pins can be connected to leads 16 by solder containing heat shrunk connecting sleeves 15 .
