# Novel 4 8X 6,11 dihydrodibenzo b,e thiepin 11 one 3 yl 4 oxobutyric acids, methods of preparation, compositions and uses thereof.

## Abstract
Novel 4 8X 6, 11 dihydrodibenzo b.e. thiepin 11 one 3 yl 4 oxobutyric acids of the formula Also described are methods for the preparation of the compounds and pharmaceutical compositions containing them, and their use for treating pain, pyrexia and inflammation, and in delaying parturition in mammals.
