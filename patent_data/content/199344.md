# Longitudinal snap action fastening device for control and or signalling units.

## Abstract
A snap action bayonet type fastening device for control and or signalling units comprising an anular element 3 placed between the operative unit 7 and said control and or signalling unit 6,60 having the form of a coupling ferrule comprising a frame 20 with protuberances 21, 22, 23 designed to engage with appropriate fixing means 14,14a present on said operative unit and two or more flexible appendices 3a, 3b with slots and or teeth 4a, 4b designed to engage with corresponding protuberances 5a, 5b or identations 44, 46 made in said control and or signalling unit 6, 60 .
