# Container grab.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a container grab 1 comprising co operating jaws 18, 23 attached to a frame 6 , between which jaws 18, 23 a container rim 31 is to be accommo dated, characterized in that the under jaw 18 is attached to a carriage 8 slidable along a frame column 7 comprised in said frame 6 and the upper 23 jaw is attached to one end 24 of a lever 25 pivotally attached to said Carriage 8 , the other end 26 of said lever 25 being coupled via a link 27 with said frame column 7 .
