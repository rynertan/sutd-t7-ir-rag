# Adhesively mountable die attach film.

## Abstract
A die attach film 11 adapted for adhesive mounting to a dicing frame characterised in that it comprises a a support film 12 b a curable die bonding adhesive 13 releasably mounted on the support film and c adhesive means 14 to bond the support film to a dic ing frame is disclosed. Also disclosed is a combination of such a film and a dicing frame. The present invention provides advantages over the prior art.
