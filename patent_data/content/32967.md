# Cutting blade for a rotary lawnmower.

## Abstract
A cutting blade 10 for a rotary mower has a generally horizontally disposed, elongated blade body 18 adapted for rotational movement about the vertical axis 54 thereof when operatively connected to a rotary mower, and a plural ity of fins 30, 32 attached to the blade body, each fin extend ing generally vertically from the blade body 18 and away from the vertical axis 54 thereof and disposed at an angle to the longitudinal axis of the blade body 18 such that the outwardly facing surface of each fin 30, 32 is presented to the air upon rotation of the blade 10 . In operation, the fins force air and grass clippings upwardly and radially outwardly from the rotating cutting blade 10 .
