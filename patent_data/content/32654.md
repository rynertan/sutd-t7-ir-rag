# Machine to wash surfaces.

## Abstract
This invention is embodied in a machine to wash surfaces which comprises suction means and brush means and is characterized by including in mutual combination and coordination suction means 14 positioned at least at its front, front humidifier means 15 which can possibly be shut off and which cooperate with absorbing means 12 , sprayer means 13 which can possibly be shut off, and brush means 11 , whereby there are advantageously comprised rear absorbing means 112 and possible intermediate drying means 39 .
