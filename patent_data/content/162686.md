# Tyre transport jig.

## Abstract
A jig for transporting a green tyre carcass in a horizontal position without causing any distortion comprising a lower bead support 3 , a concentric tyre shoulder support 4 and a set of fingers 7 for supporting the upper bead of the car cass. The fingers 7 are spring biased towards a position in engagement with the upper bead, but may be moved radially inwards while a tyre carcass is either placed on or removed from the jig.
