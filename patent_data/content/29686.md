# Continuous self locking spiral wound seal.

## Abstract
A spiral wound seal for effecting a seal between two surfaces 13,16 . The seal consists of a strip 10, 10 of gasket material wound into a groove 32 machined into one of the surfaces. The gasket strip is wider than the groove is deep such that a portion of the gasket material protrudes from the groove. The seal is effected by clamping the second surface onto the first surface and thereby compressing the protrud ing gasket material.
