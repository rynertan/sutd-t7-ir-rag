# Low load burning burner.

## Abstract
A low load burning burner comprising innr air flow nozzles 8 , outer air flow nozzles 11 and a fuel gas nozzle 14 , and further having a motive air supply means, which is operated at the low load burning of a heating furnace, can maintain the furnace temperature uniform, and can heat materials arranged in the width direction of the furnace to a uniform temperature along the width direction of the furnace.
