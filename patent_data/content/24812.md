# Seat belt locking device for an automobile vehicle.

## Abstract
The device of the present invention comprises a slidable but unrotatable wedge shaped seat belt locking member 18 having a pair of roller supporting portions 22a, 22b between which a roller 23 is mounted, and a pair of fixed roller shafts 19a, 19b the cross section of which is the segment of a circle. The seat belt 6 is so arranged as to pass around the roller 23 of the locking member 18 and the two roller shafts 19a, 19b , so that the belt can be firmly caught between the two flat portions 21a, 21b of the wedge shaped seat belt locking member 18 and the respective flat portions 20a, 20b of the roller shafts, when the vehicle is suddenly decelerated.
