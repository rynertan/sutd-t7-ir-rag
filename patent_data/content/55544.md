# Apparatus for dispensing containers.

## Abstract
An apparatus 1 or 2 for dispensing containers, in particular disposable cups, from a stack 22 . The apparatus has two jaw assemblies, each having a catch member 8 and a lever member 7 pivotable about horizontal axes 9, 10 between a first rest position in which the stack of containers is supported by the lever member and a second dispensing position in which the lowermost container is pulled from the stack by the lever member while it is supported by the catch member, and the inner edges of the catch member and lever member are formed as concave segments of a circle to provide good support and even loading on the containers during operation.
