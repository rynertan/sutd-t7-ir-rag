# MOUSE HUMAN HYBRIDOMA PRODUCING ANTIVIRUS HUMAN ANTIBODY, PROCESS FOR ITS PREPARATION, AND ANTIVIRUS HUMAN MONOCLONAL ANTIBODY.

## Abstract
Mouse human hybridoma which produces monoclonal antivirus human antibody against herpes virus. Briareus varicallae, cytomegalovirus, etc., and which is obtained by cell fusion of antibody producing human cells sensitized with virus or protein or glycoprotein of virus origin and mouse myeloma cell in vitro in the presence of mitogen.
