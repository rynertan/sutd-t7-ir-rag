# Method of spraying closed end cans.

## Abstract
Method and apparatus for coating the interior and bottom surfaces of food and beverage cans which require protection against contact with the food or beverage content of the cans. The interior surface is sprayed with a high solids content liquid spray emitted from a pair of nozzles 20,21 one of which 20 sprays the lower portion 22 of the cylindrical side wall 12 as well as the outer portion 15 of the bottom wall 11 and the other of which 21 sprays the top portion 24 of the cylindrical wall and the innermost centre section 14 of the bottom wall.
