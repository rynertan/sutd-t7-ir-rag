# Liner for the outlet port of a rod mill.

## Abstract
A liner 13 for the outlet port 12 of a rod mill including a cylindrical body 15 which projects outwardly through an outlet port 12 and which is provided on the peripheral edge of the opening 14 of the liner 13 . The projecting length of the cylindrical body 15 on the side close to the end portion of the barrel 11 of the rod mill is preferably longer than that on the opposite side.
