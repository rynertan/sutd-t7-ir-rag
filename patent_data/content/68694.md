# Sewing machine.

## Abstract
A sewing machine having a hook 15 rotatable in a horizontal plane includes a needle bar 17 tilted at an angle of Î± with respect to a vertical line to displace a sewing needle rearward to a remote position. The remote needle provides on a bed of the sewing machine a wider space in which sewing operation can be effected. The hook 15 is also displaced rearward below the bed for coaction with the needle.
