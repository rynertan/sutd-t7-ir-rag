# Sliced bale conditioning.

## Abstract
Apparatus for slicing tobacco bales for conditioning in which the bales are moved from a conveyor 2 onto an adja cent lift device 16 by a first pusher 6 , and are then sliced by a slicing blade 20 which is moved horizontally into engage ment with the top of the bale and slices the bale whilst the lift device is dropped slightly to assist in cleaving the bale. The lift is then actuated to raise the bale ready for the next slicing op eration, after the previous slice has been ejected by a second pusher 22 .
