# Collection appliance for body eliminations.

## Abstract
A collection appliance 1 for body eliminations or secre tions having a novel closure means is disclosed. Said closure means comprises a semi rigid strip 23 securely affixed adja cent to one of the walls 3 of the collection appliance 1 . The opening 21 is closed by lap folding the extremity 19 of the collection appliance 1 upon itself and then tucking the folded extremity into a gap between said strip 23 and said wall 3 . The novel closure means is particularly well suited for releas able closure of the drainage opening of an ostomy collection appliance.
