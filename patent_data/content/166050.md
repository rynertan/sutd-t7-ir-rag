# Floor treating apparatus.

## Abstract
Floor treating apparatus 10 , principally for waxing floors includes a drip pan 48 to catch wax from the applica tor pad 77 when the applicator head 70 is in a retracted position. A piston pump 92 has an adjustable variable stroke to provide selection of the quantity of wax to be deposited. Slotted connections between the applicator head and the supporting arms 76, 78 provide independent verti cal movement of each end of the applicator pad to enable the pad to conform to uneven floors.
