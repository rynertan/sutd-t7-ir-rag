# A device for collating sheets.

## Abstract
Device for collating sheets of paper comprising at least one receiving surface 1 made from electrically insulating material, means 2,3,4 to feed a sheet 5 from one side and deposit it on the surface 1 , and means for removing electrical charges from a deposited sheet 5 which comprise at least one conductor 6 connected to earth potential and being disposed on the side remote from the entry side of the receiving surface 1 and forming an abutment with which the leading edge of the sheets 5 comes into contact when it is being disposed.
