# ACCESS DEVICE

## Abstract
The invention relates to a device for enabling access to a relatively moving structure such as a ship. The device has a base 1 , a linkage 5, 8, 9 supported on the base and a work station 10 supported by the linkage. The linkage is adjust able controllably to vary the position of the work station 10 relative to the base 1 and means 2 are provided for attaching the work station 10 to the ship. The linkage 5, 8, 9 is automatically freed from control when the work station 1 is in position on the ship.
