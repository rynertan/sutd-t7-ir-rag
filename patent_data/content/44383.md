# DEVICE FOR FOLDING LINEN

## Abstract
The folding device with vanes of an apparatus for folding cloth articles and especially linen, of the type comprising a folder table having a fixed section and a plurality of vanes hinged externally to said section and tilting towards the same to perform the folding, has both the fixed section and the tilting vanes conceived in the form of belt conveyors. These conveyors are moved at the same speed, the fixed section conveyor under direct control, and the vane con veyors by indirect control through contact with revolving rollers lying under the vanes.
