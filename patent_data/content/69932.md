# APPARATUS FOR USE AS PHYSICAL EXERCISER AND MEANS OF LOCOMOTION

## Abstract
An apparatus which serves as a physical exerciser and as a means of locomotion is built up of a chassis and at least two, but preferably three wheels, one of which is positively driven. The rider occupies a seat and actuates a linearly reciprocable drive member, the motion of which is transmit ted to the driven wheel via a link, the linear movement being transformed into rotatory movement.
