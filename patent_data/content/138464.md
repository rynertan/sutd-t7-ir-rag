# 2 Amino 5 hydroxy 4 methylpyrimidine derivatives.

## Abstract
Novel 2 amino 5 hydroxy 4 methylpyrimidines and substi tuted amino derivatives thereof useful as inhibitors of leukotriene synthesis for the treatment of pulmonary, inflammatory and cardiovascular diseases, cancer and psoriasis. In addition these compounds are cytoprotective and therefore useful in the treat ment of peptic ulcers. A process for the preparation of such active compounds by ring rearrangement of 2 amino 5 acetyloxazole, pharmaceutical compositions containing the novel active com pounds and compositions containing these compounds in combi nation with other anti inflammatory agents or gastric anti secret ory agents.
