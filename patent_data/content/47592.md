# A FUSE LINK AND THE COMBINATION THEREOF IN AN EXPULSION FUSE

## Abstract
A fuse link including a fuse element of known cross sec tional area having a number of holes centrally located therein to define reduced cross sectional areas and a filler material provided in said holes, said embodiment having a short time current melt characteristic at high overload substantially the same as the homogeneous fuse element and a long time current melt characteristic different from the long time current melt characteristic of the fuse element.
