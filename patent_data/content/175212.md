# Control of a vehicle automatic transmission.

## Abstract
A vehicle automatic transmission 1 having a torque con verter 10 and a multiple stage gear mechanism 20 . When the vehicle is stopped and the shift lever is moved from the N range to the D range, the gear mechanism 20 is at first shifted up to the highest gear stage and, when the converter turbine 14 speed is decrease to a very low value, for exam ple 200 rpm, the gear mechanism 20 is shifted down to the first stage. If the vehicle brake 45 is engaged and the engine is idling, the highest gear stage is maintained even after the converter turbine 14 speed is decreased below the afore mentioned value.
