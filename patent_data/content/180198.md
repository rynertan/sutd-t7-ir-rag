# Low wattage metal halide lamp with inverted domed sleeve.

## Abstract
A low wattage metal halide discharge lamp 5 includes an evacuated envelope 7 wherein is disposed a heat reducing member 25 having an arc tube 33 therein. The heat redu cing member and the arc tube have a metal band 31 an outer strap member 39 adjacent one another and adjacent an elec trode 35 with the metal band, strap member and electrode all electrically connected to an electrical lead of one polarity whereby sodium losses from the arc tube are reduced. Impor tantly, the evacuated envelope includes a getter 21, 23 and the heat reducing member is in the form of a domed sleeve having an open end 29 directed away from the getters.
