# PROCESS FOR THE UTILISATION OF SOLID PAPER MILL WASTE

## Abstract
A process for the utilisation of solid paper mill waste comprises introducing earthworms and or earthworm co coons into a mass of the waste and allowing the worms to mature and breed in the waste until it is converted into a friable, soil like material. The earthworms and the friable, soil like material may be subsequently separated the mate rial is suitable for use as a horticultural compost.
