# A pipe connection method and connecting structure.

## Abstract
A connection method comprising the steps of providing a cylindrical joint member 1 for connecting a first pipe 3 and a second pipe 7 inserted into opposite ends of said cylindrical joint member 1 almost to the center of said cylindrical joint member 1 and holding said pipes 3,7 in such a position that their ends are separated only by a narrow gap, and compressing both ends and the center of said cylindrical joint member 1 to form airtight seals between said first 3 and second pipes 7 and the ends of said cylindrical joint member 1 and also between the ends of said first 3 and second pipes 7 and the middle part of said cylindrical joint member 3 .
