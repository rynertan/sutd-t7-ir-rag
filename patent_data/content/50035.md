# Oxygen electrodes and medical devices for determining the concentration of a gas in blood both of which employ a membrane of a hydrophilic linear polyether polyurethane and hydrophilic linear polyether polyurethanes for use in such electrodes and devices.

## Abstract
A device for use in determining blood gas which employs a membrane of material permeable to blood gas to be determined characterised in that the membrane is hydrophilic polyurethane is described.
