# ANTIATHEROSCLEROTIC 1 PIPERAZINE DERIVATIVES

## Abstract
This disclosure describes novel 1 piperazinecarbonyl carboxamides and 1 piperazinethiocarboxamides, such as 4 chloro 4 2 pyrazinyl 1 piperazinecarboxanilide and 4 cyano 4 2 pyrazinyl 1 piperazinethiocarboxanilide, pharmaeutical compositions containing same, a process for their preparation, and a method of treating athero sclerosis therewith.
