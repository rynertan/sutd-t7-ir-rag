# Body support.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a body support comprising one or more flexible containers 5, 6, 12, 17, 39 and a plurality of load transmitting elements 10, 16, 19, 20, 37 which have contact surfaces the area of which at a number of elements differs from the area of other elements to obtain an adaptation to the differing ratio s of volume to weight of the pans of the human body. The invention further relates to a container 39 which can be used in the body support which container 39 has two outer layers 42, 43 of non permeable material and two inner layers 44, 45 all layers 42 45 being interconnected by cross seams 46, 47, 48, 50, 51, 52, 53, 54 to form chambers interconnected at the edges.
