# Multistrand suture package.

## Abstract
An improved package for a plurality of needled sutures comprising a folded suture retainer 10 wherein a bundle of substantially aligned suture strands are coiled between two folded panels while the needles 31 are retained between two adjacent folded panels. The needles 31 are centered on one panel by an opening communicating with the interior of the package. A removable portion 20 in the outer needle retain ing panel provides access to the needles 31 while the bulk of the suture remains coiled within the package.
