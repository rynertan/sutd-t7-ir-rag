# ALIMENTARY COMPOSITION HAVING THE AROMA OF EATABLE MUSHROOMS

## Abstract
Alimentary composition having the aroma of eatable mushroom, obtained by dissolving 1 octen 3 ol, possibly as a mixture with one or more compounds selected from the group consisting of 2 octen 1 ol, 1 octen 3 one, 3 octanone, 3 octa nol, in alimentary fats.
