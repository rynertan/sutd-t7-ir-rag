# Ribbon cartridge with integral pivoted ribbon guide.

## Abstract
A ribbon cartridge having a supply spool, a take up spool hub 27 to receive ribbon 1 , and ribbon guide arms 9 pivoted on pins 62 on the side wall 17 of the cartidge. Downward bias of ribbon guide arms 9 is provided by a coil spring 11 in a housing 13 . A central section 60 is biased by the spring 11 . The central section 60 may be grasped to move ribbon guide arms 9 upward during handling of the cartridge.
