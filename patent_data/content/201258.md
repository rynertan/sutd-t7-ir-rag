# Reusable record tab for videotape cassette.

## Abstract
A reusable record tab 20, 50 for the record lockout opening 14, 44 of a recording tape cassette, which tab 20, 50 comprises a body 22, 52 that blocks the opening and at least one resilient leg 24, 54 projecting from the body 22, 52 . The leg 22, 52 is bowed to press against an inner ramp 15, 58 of the cassette housing to hold the record tab 20, 50 snugly in the opening and the body 22, 52 has a small aperture 32, 66 for receiving the point of a writing instrument to remove the record tab 20, 50 .
