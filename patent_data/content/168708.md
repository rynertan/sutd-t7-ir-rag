# Berth or bunk with deformable structure for cabs of industrial vehicles and the like, particularly suitable to reduce the damages to the driver in case of road accidents.

## Abstract
Berth or bunk 5 5a for cabs of industrial vehicles and the like, positioned rearwards relatively to the driver s and sec ond driver s seats 1 , which are positioned side by side of each other, so structured as to result elastically deformable, in case of a road accident, by the thrust created by the motion backwards of the seats, when to them a device is applied for the automatic or manually controlled back moving, to contri bute to the safeguard of the driver s and of the second driver s safety.
