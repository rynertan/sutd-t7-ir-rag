# Electrophotographic machine.

## Abstract
A electrophotographic machine of the transfer type having a main slide out drawer 17 upon which is mounted most of the paper path 18, 19, 21 , including the fuser 24 and the transfer corona 22 . The main drawer carries paper storage bins 18, 19 paper feed mechanisms 20 , and transport guides 21 . The paper storage bins comprise individual mini drawers slidably mounted in the main drawer.
