# Terrying mechanism for double cylinder knitting machine.

## Abstract
An improved terrying mechanism is adapted for mount ing on that one of the two cylinders 2 of a double cylinder knitting machine, through which fabric is not removed from take down. The mechanism includes a pillar 22 adapted for journalling at one end on a part 14 at the base of the cylinder 2 rotating jointly therewith and carrying at the other end an annular cam system 20 with a retractable cam means 55 for coacting with terrying instruments 90, 91, 94 carried in generally radiaily extending tricks 60 in a dial 62 having a journalling means 68 in axial alignment of the pillar 22 .
