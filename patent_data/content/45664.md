# Control system for hydrostatic power transmission.

## Abstract
A control system for a hydrostatic power transmission having a variable displacement hydraulic pump 2 and a hydraulic actuator 3 connected in a closed or semi closed circuit, and means 2a, 10 for adjusting the displacement of the pump or the delivery thereby in accordance with a manipulated variable of an operating lever 13 .
