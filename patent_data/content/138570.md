# AN APPLICATOR TOOL

## Abstract
An applicator tool for applying an ear tag to an animal, the applicator having retention means 33, 36 which is adapted to, in use, retain a part of an ear tag and pass it through an ani mal s ear. The applicator includes means 44 for engagement of an animal s ear on a side opposite that to which the retention means first penetrates the ear and means 12, 26, 24, 21 to cause relative movement of the retention means 33,36 and engagement means 44 toward one another. The engagement means 44 are adapted to cause at least a portion of the ear tag part retained by the retention means 33, 36 to be compacted during passage through the ear of an animal.
