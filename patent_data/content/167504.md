# Milling cutter.

## Abstract
A milling cutter comprising a shoe 12 , which is secured to the cutter body 10 and carries a cutting insert 11 . The shoe 12 is adjustable by means of an adjustment means 13 and clamped by means of clamping means 14 15, 16 . The adjustment means 13 is adapted to bridge an inner corner 26 on the shoe 12 and the clamping means 14 15, 16 comprises a pin 15 which is displaceably arranged in the shoe 12 at the outer end thereof, said pin 15 being adapted to be forced against a supporting surface 28 on the cutter body 10 opposed to the supporting surface 22 on the shoe 12 by means of a clamping screw 16 .
