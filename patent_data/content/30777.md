# Plant for and method of manufacturing cement clinker.

## Abstract
In a plant for manufacturing cement clinker in which cement raw material is burnt in a kiln 8 after heating in a multi stage preheater 1,2,3,4,5 by hot gases, at least the first stage of the preheater comprises a heat exchanger 1 having a raw material section 13 , a separate hot gas section 14 , and means 12,15 for enabling indirect heat transfer between the hot gas section 14 and the raw material section 13 .
