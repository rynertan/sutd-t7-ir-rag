# Bracket for motorcycle lock.

## Abstract
A bracket 10 is provided for carrying and or storing a large U shaped lock 12 used to secure a motorcycle or the like by passing the U shaped shackle 16 through the wheel of the bike and closing the ends of the shackle by means of a cross bar 18 locked thereto. The bracket is comprised of a flat body portion 22 along at least one edge of which is formed a tubular or semi tubular receptacle 26, 28 adapted to receive a leg of the U shaped lock shackle 16 . Mounted to the body portion is a fastening device 48 for engaging the cross bar of the lock 18 to prevent longitudinal movement thereof with respect to the bracket 10 . The bracket 10 when used on a motorcycle preferably is mounted against the shock absorber 20 for the rear wheel thereof.
