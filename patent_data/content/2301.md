# Cosmetic package.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a cosmetic package comprising a container 2 for a cosmetic composition 5 , a closure 3 for the container, a cosmetic applicator 6 which is attached to the closure 3 by a shaft 7 and a wiper 10 to wipe excess cosmetic from the applicator 6 . In order to enable the user of the cosmetic package to retain on the cosmetic applicator 6 the quantity of cosmetic composition which is desired for a given purpose, the cosmetic package further comprises an adjusting means 13 , 14 , 15 to vary the cross sectional area of the orifice 9 of the wiper 10 .
