# 2 Azaergolines and 2 aza 8 or 9 ergolenes, process for their preparation and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.

## Abstract
Novel 2 azaergolines, 2 aza 8 or 9 ergolenes, which are neuroleptic agents, are described herein. These compounds are prepared by reacting a 7 amino 6 ketobenzo f quinoline with a diazotization agent. e.g. sodium nitrite and a strong mineral acid, to yield the 6 keto 7 diazonium salt intermedi ate, followed by reduction to yield the final product.
