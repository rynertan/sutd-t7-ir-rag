# A plastics bag comprising venting means.

## Abstract
A plastics bag is provided with a filling valve 6 and a gas permeable dust filtering pocket 22 opposite said valve, allowing gas to escape from the interior of the bag. A disconnectable spot adhesive connection 31, 32 exists between a layer 22b of said pocket 22 and the adjacent bag wall parts 33, 34 opposite corner flap 12 . An additional adhesive or welding connection 40 may be present near the free end 15 of the pocket 22 for connecting the layer 22a with the bottom flaps.
