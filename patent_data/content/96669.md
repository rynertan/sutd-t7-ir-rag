# Milling cutter.

## Abstract
A milling cutter for metal cutting having a cutter body 1 in which inserts 5 are retained in recesses 3 solely by spring means 7 11 . An edge 27 of the insert 5 is supported in the recess 3 on an axially displaceable adjusting device 6 which has a somewhat inclined contact surface 13 . The insert 5 is adjustable without the need of loosening any clamping device.
