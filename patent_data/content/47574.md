# Wheelchair lift.

## Abstract
A lift for moving a wheelchair over a stairway 18 includes a ramp 12 , a wheelchair supporting platform 80 and a me chanism 152, 60, 40 for operating the platform 80 over the ramp. When the lift is inoperative, the wheelchair supporting platform 80 can be removed from the ramp 12 and the ramp 12 can be stored such that it does not intrude into the stair way 18 and the operating mechanism is inaccessible.
