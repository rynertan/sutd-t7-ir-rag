# Ornamental lighting device.

## Abstract
An ornamental lighting device 10 includes a plurality of optical fibers 30 to which condensed light from a light source is distributed through a fiber optic cable 26 . The optical fibers are arranged parallel to each other and held between a pair of light transmitting panels 16,14 , which are rigidly mounted on a pedestal 12 . Light propagating through the optical fibers is radiated through the light transmitting panels to the ambience. A plurality of such lighting devices may be arranged side by side and supplied by a common light source.
