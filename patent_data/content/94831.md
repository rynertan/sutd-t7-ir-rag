# IMPROVEMENTS RELATING TO ELECTROPHOTOGRAPHY

## Abstract
A method of preprinting information, e.g. coordinate information, onto a sheet of unexposed electrophotographic material is disclosed. The method comprises either a depositing an image forming layer on the electrophoto graphic material and generating the required preprinted information as an image in said layer, or b securing an image bearing layer to the electrophotographic material. There is also disclosed a composite material which comprises an electrophotographic film which comprises a substrate 1 4 7 11 and an image layer 2 6 8 12 having superposed on one surface thereof a secondary, image bearing or image receiving layer 3 5 9 13 .
