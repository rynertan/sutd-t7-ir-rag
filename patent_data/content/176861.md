# Electrical connector.

## Abstract
An electrical connector of the split cylinder type for terminating or connecting an electrical wire 11 comprising an elongated tubular sleeve section 14 , an elongated wire receiving slot 18 , a flared entryway leading into the wire receiving slot 18 and a wire retaining means comprising a wire retention groove 16 with a neck portion of reduced width for temporarily retaining the electrical wire 11 within the flared entryway in position for termination or connection within the wire receiving slot 18 .
