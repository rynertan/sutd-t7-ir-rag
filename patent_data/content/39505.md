# SYNTHETIC COAL LOG

## Abstract
A method of making synthetic fireplace logs or the like and the logs so produced. Approximately 80 95 by weight of pulverized coal is mixed with 5 20 by weight of dry paraffin to form a heterogeneous mixture. The mixture is fed into a cylindrical log mold and sub jected to pressures of approximately 5,000 to 12,000 psi. The logs are then coated with a liquid paraffin.
