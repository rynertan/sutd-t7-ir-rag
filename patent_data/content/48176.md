# Electrically shielded conduit and method of making same.

## Abstract
An electrically shielding conduit comprises a flexible conduit tube 12 having a flexible electrical shield 16 disposed within it and hollow flexible generally tubular retaining means 18 which urges the shield against the conduit tube. The conduit according to the invention is lightweight and flexible, and will remain open even after severe defor mation of the conduit for example bending or crushing thereof.
