# Repairing sewer pipes.

## Abstract
To repair a fractured sewer line 10 using a line sleeve 29 and an elastomer sleeve 34 around the liner sleeve 29 , the liner sleeve 29 and the elastomer sleeve 34 are in stalled endwise in the sewer line 10 to locate the two sleeves 29, 34 in bridging relation with the fracture 13 , and filler slurry 37 is introduced into a space 38 formed between the two sleeves 29,34 to cause the elastomeric sleeve 34 to expand and seal against the sewer line 10 in bridging relation with the fracture 13 .
