# Brake booster.

## Abstract
A brake booster can switch operating mode from a positive pressure mode using a signal controlled fluid pres sure supplier 41 to a vacuum pressure mode and vice versa. The booster includes a booster converter 44 holding both an air valve 36 and a vacuum valve 37 of the booster closed in the positive pressure mode or holding the booster air valve in its closed and open positions while holding the booster vacuum valve in its open and closed positions respectively when the brake pedal is released and depressed in the vacuum mode. The fluid pressure supplier is enabled while both booster valves are in the closed position in the positive pressure mode.
