# Twisted singles carpet yarn, carpet or rug having tufts thereof, and process for heatsetting a twisted singles yarn.

## Abstract
A bulky, heatset, tangle, twisted singles yarn is provided having exceptional column strength and resistance to bend ing and untwisting. Cut pile produced therefrom has excellent tuft rigidity and endpoint definition. The yarn is produced by passing a bulked, twisted singles yarn through a chamber 4 wherein the yarn is tangled and heatset with a heated fluid such as steam.
