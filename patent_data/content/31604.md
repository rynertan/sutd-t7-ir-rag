# Rotary stirrer with internal heating.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a stirring gear with internal heating comprising a central tubular shaft for the supply of the heat exchange medium and stirring paddles mounted perpendicularly to this shaft and in communication with the hollow interior of the shaft. The paddles are sector shaped and consist of a solid plate comprising a system of small section ducts 15, 16, 17 for circulation of the heat ex change medium therein. Various stirring vessels for batch or continuous operation and comprising said stirring gears are also described.
