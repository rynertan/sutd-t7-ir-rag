# Arc restricting device for circuit breaker.

## Abstract
The present invention provides a circuit breaker of improved performance by providing arc shields 6, 7 and arc runways 601, etc. together with the contacts 202, 302 on the rigid conductors 201, 301 of the contactors 2, 3 of a circuit breaker, and disposing said arc runways 601, etc. on the stationary contactor side with a predetermined rela tionship to arc extinguishing plates 501 such that a portion of a cut out 503 in at least one of said arc extinguishing plates 501 substantially overlaps said arc runway 601, etc. .
