# An electrical switch mounted in a hydraulic cylinder.

## Abstract
An electrical switch 32 mounted in a hydraulic cylinder having a linearly displaceable member 30 , the switch being operable by said member during displacement of said member in the cylinder. The switch 32 has a stationary element 62 and a movable element 60 displaceable by said member 30 between a home position in which said elements 60, 62 are in non co operative relation and an operative position in which said movable elements 60, 62 co operate. Adjustment means 46, 56 74, 86 is provided for adjusting the position of said stationary element by said member 30 upon movement of said member to at least one of said home and operative positions.
