# Formazan reactive blue dyes, a process for their preparation and a process for dyeing cellulose fibers therewith.

## Abstract
A formazan reactive blue dye represented by a free acid of the formula I A Proces for their preparation. This dye is usable for dyeing cellulose fibers in a brilliant blue color with good fastnesses, particularly, chlorine, light and perspiration light fastnesses, as well as good white dischargeability. The cellulose fiber dyed by this process.
