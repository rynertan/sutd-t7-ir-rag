# Optical record disc and method of making it.

## Abstract
A direct read after write optical recording disc having a pair of transparent, disc shaped members 11,11 each being formed with integral inner 15 and outer 17 ring shaped bosses on one of its sides. A thin recording layer 13 of a light sensitive material is coated on each disc shaped member, in a shallow annular recess 19 formed between its two ring shaped bosses, and the two members are disposed in a coaxial relationship, with their respective bosses in abut ment with each other, whereby the two recording layers are located within a sealed, annular chamber.
