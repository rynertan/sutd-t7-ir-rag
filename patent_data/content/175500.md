# Improvements in roof tiles.

## Abstract
The invention concerns an extruded concrete roof tile of the kind which is designed to be laid in broken bond, is generally rectangular in plan view and has underlying and overlying side lock portions 12, 18 at opposed edges respectively. The up per surface 41 of the tile at the upper end thereof, when on the roof, is recessed 40 to receive the side locks of two similar upper tiles laid in broken bond, and the underlying side lock por tion 12 is cut away at the lower end of the tile so that the low er end portion of the tile may lie over the upper surface of the tiles down the roof in use to extend beyond the said recessed portions of the lower tiles. The recessed portion 40 is ar ranged to receive the tile hanging nibs 36,38 of an identical tile when the two tiles are stacked one upon the other.
