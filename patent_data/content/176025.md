# MOLDED CASE CIRCUIT BREAKER WITH A TRIP MECHANISM HAVING AN INTERMEDIATE LATCH LEVER

## Abstract
The invention relates to an electric circuit breaker having improved latching means operatively interposed between the trip bar and the cradle of the circuit breaker mechanism. The latching means comprises a primary latch 148 which cooperates with the cradle 96 , and a secondary latch lever 418 which is operatively interposed between the primary latch and the trip bar 412 . The secondary latch lever has two lever arms 429, 431 of different lengths, the shorter lever arm 431 cooperating with the primary latch, and the latch lever preferably is bent so that its two lever arms define an obtuse angle therebetween.
