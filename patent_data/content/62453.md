# Flowable compositions comprising 2 chloro N isopropylacetanilide herbicide.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a water based, flowable herbicide composition which comprises by weight a 38.0 to 45.0 2 chloro N isopropylacetanilide herb icide b 2.0 to 6.0 of a hydrated amorphous silicon diox ide c 2.0 to 6.0 of a polyoxypropylene polyoxyethylene block copolymer d 0.5 to 2.0 of a taurate surfactant e 0.5 to 2.0 of a hydrated aluminum silicate f 0.5 to 1.0 of a flocculent g 5.0 to 10.0 of an anti freeze agent h 0.5 to 2.0 of a silicon antifoaming agent and i balance being water the solid components of said composition having a particle size of no greater than 25 microns.
