# A cryopanel and a cryopump using such cryopanels.

## Abstract
A cryopump of vertically tiered individual generally conical shaped sections 28,34,44,54 with linearly increasing base diameters arranged smaller to larger base diameter as the distance increases from the coldest end 16 of elongated refrigeration source 10 . Individual cryopanels 20,30,40,50 are a surface of revolution with tapered bayonet joint interlocking portions 22,32,42,52 to permit ease of assem bly while maintaining good thermal contact between sec tions.
