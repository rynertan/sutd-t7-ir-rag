# Process for producing substituted pyrroles.

## Abstract
A process for the preparation of alkyl 1,4 dimethyl 3 ethoxycarbonylpyrrole 2 acetate compounds by reacting in a solvent a diloweralkyl acetone dicarboxylate, a chloromethyl lower alkyl ketone and an aqueous loweralkylamine in the presence of solids formation inhibiting amount of a lower alkanol having from 1 to about 6 carbon atoms.
