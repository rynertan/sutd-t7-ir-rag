# Butterfly valve assembly and method of making same.

## Abstract
A butterfly valve assembly and method of making the same are provided wherein such valve assembly has a valve body structure 31 and a closure structure 35 and is pro vided with a device 135 for locking, in its closed position, a shaft assembly 38 which is used to operate the closure structure 35 . Heat exchange means may be disposed in the outer periphery of the closure structure 35 for controlling the temperature thereof.
