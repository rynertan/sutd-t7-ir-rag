# Use of 2 Pentanoylaminoacetic acid and salts thereof.

## Abstract
2 pentanoylaminoacetic acid and pharmaceutically suitable salts thereof are claimed for use as pharmaceu ticals. The compounds are useful in producing an effect on the central nervous system, especially in the treatment of var ious forms of epilepsy, of disninesiae, of parkinsonism, of memory troubles, of psychic troubles and of cerebral anoxia.
