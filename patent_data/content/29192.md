# DETACHABLE LOCKING DEVICE FOR FASTENING A CYCLIST S SHOE TO THE PEDAL OF RACING BICYCLES AND THE LIKE

## Abstract
A detachable locking device for fastening a cyclist s shoe to the pedal of racing bicycles and the like comprises guiding elements 11, 12, 32 defined on the pedal body 2 and a coupling block 13, 30 associable with the cy clist s shoe and provided with a bayonet coupling 16, 17, 31 detachably insertable in the guiding elements 11, 12, 32 and disengageable therefrom by sliding along a direc tion substantially parallel to the pedal rotation axis.
