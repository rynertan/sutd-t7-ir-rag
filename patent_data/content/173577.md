# Apparatus for stretching plastic dough.

## Abstract
An apparatus for stretching plastic dough comprises a plurality of conveyors 1, 2 arranged in series and a plurality of rollers 3 disposed above the conveyors and cooperative ly acting to stretch dough fed therebetween. The rollers are freely rotatable and their shafts 4 are fixed to connecting arms 5 which reciprocate them above the conveyors.
