# Transmission, in particular for a motor vehicle.

## Abstract
A transmission, provided with an input shaft 1 which, through a drive away clutch 2 , drives the primary shaft 13 of an infinitely variable V belt transmission 4 . The secondary shaft 20 of said V belt transmission is connected with the output shaft 26, 27 of the transmission, whereby the input shaft 1 , and the primary shaft 13 are disposed parellel, but not coaxial to each other and that the primary shaft 13 is driven by the input shaft 1 , through a reduction gear 3 .
