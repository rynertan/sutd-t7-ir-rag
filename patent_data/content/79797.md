# PROCESS FOR INCREASING THE KEEPING QUALITIES AND WATER RETENTION OF MEAT FROM MARINE ANIMALS AND IMPROVING ITS CONSISTENCY

## Abstract
The keeping qualities of fish, molluscs and crustaceans are increased by treatment with an aqueous solution of one or more of the compounds NaOH, KOH, Ca OH ₂, Na₂CO₃ and K₂CO₃. At the same time one achieves a water retention increase and an improvement in consistency.
