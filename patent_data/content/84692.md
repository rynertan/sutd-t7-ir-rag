# Method of dispersing photographic adjuvants in a hydrophilic colloid composition.

## Abstract
Method of dispersing photographic adjuvants in a hy drophilic colloid composition for forming a water permeable photosensitive or non photosensitive colloid layer of a photographic silver halide element with the aid of at least one high boiling substantially water insoluble oil former of the class of 1,3 dialkyloxy 2 propanols and the carboxylic, phosphoric, or phosphonic esters thereof.
