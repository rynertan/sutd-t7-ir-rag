# Improvements in and relating to wire binding machines.

## Abstract
A wire binding machine for accepting and closing together wire binding elements 10 of the Wire O type to bind a stack 200 of sheets, the machine being controlled to present two or more elements 160 to the closing or binding station with the option of a gap SD between the elements so that if required a calendar hanger 404 can be fed between the elements 100 prior to the closing operation, the elements being spaceable by means of a first or feed conveyor 84 feeding up to a parallel section of a second or spacing conveyor 140 and pushing the elements from the first to the second conveyor with an interval representing the desired gap SD .
