# Fire resistant laminates.

## Abstract
A fire resistant laminate is made, comprising a plurality of heat and pressure consolidated thermoset resin impregnated filler sheets 11 , where the filler sheets before resin impregna tion comprised from 60 wt. to 90 wt. inorganic filler, from 0 to 25 wt. cellulose fibers and from 0.5 wt. to 20 wt. resi nous binder.
