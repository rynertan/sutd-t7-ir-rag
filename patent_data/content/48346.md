# Automatic measurement of areas.

## Abstract
The invention provides an apparatus for determining the size of a test piece 10 comprising means to support the test piece 10 in a predetermined position, means S to direct a medium onto the test piece 10 , means 11 responsive to said medium to provide a signal 30 wnich varies in accordance with variations in intensity of said medium reflected from or transmitted from or past the test piece 10 , electrical processing means 12 to process the signal 30 to provide an output O dependent upon the size of the test piece I which can be interpreted to determine the size of the test piece I .
