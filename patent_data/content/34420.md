# FIRE EXTINGUISHING APPARATUS FOR DUCTING SYSTEMS

## Abstract
Fire extinguishing apparatus for ducting incorporating an extractor fan, comprising a fire extinguisher arranged to dis charge extinguishing medium into the duct upstream of the extractor fan at a predetermined temperature and a tempera ture sensing unit operatively connected to a circuit selector control for cutting out the fan when the unit detects a preset temperature less than the operating temperature of the extin guisher and including a by pass circuit whereby the fan can be restarted following discharge of the extinguisher. A visual and or audible indication of the state of the selector control circuit is provided to assist manual setting of the by pass circuit when required.
