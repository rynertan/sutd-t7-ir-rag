# Chain and sprocket transmissions.

## Abstract
Disclosed is a chain 20 and sprocket 26,27 transmis sion in which an endless chain 20 composed of alternate inner links C and outer links D runs around a drive sprocket 26 and a driven sprocket 27 . To uniformize and reduce the amount of wear and heat occurring, during running, at each link to link connection, each inner link is made longer PI than, and each outer link is made shorter Ps than, the tooth pitch Po of the sprocket teeth.
