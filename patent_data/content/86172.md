# Pneumatic tractor tire.

## Abstract
A pneumatic tire 10 for tractors and like vehicles has a tread with alternating long lugs 20, 21 and short lugs 22, 23 extending axially inward from each lateral edge of the tread. Each long lug has three portions and each short lug has two portions. The distances between the lugs provides an open design in the general area of the mid circumferential centerplane of the tire.
