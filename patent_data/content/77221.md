# Water borne hermetic varnish.

## Abstract
A hydrolytically stable heat curable composition suit able for use as a hermetic varnish for wires or other electri cal conductors comprises an aqueous solution of a water reducible epoxy adduct of an epoxy compound, an amino substituted aromatic carboxylic acid and a water soluble amine, a water soluble organic solvent boiling above 150 C, and a water soluble or reducible phenolic crosslinking agent.
