# Filament draw nozzle.

## Abstract
A filament draw nozzle 10 includes a throughbore 14 defining body 12 having a shoulder 16 spaced from the throughbore 14 , a housing 30 positioned on the body 12 abutting the shoulder 16 whereby an aperture 32 in the housing 30 is aligned relative to the throughbore 14 , and a fiber inlet 40 including a fiber inlet feed tube 42 slidably positioned in the housing aperture 32 and concentrically disposed to project within said throughbore 14 .
