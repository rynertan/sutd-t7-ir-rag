# Cycloalkenylcarboxamido derivatives of tetramisole, their preparation and use in medicine.

## Abstract
Compounds of formula I Wherein R¹ is C₅ ₇ cycloalkenyl optionally substituted with C₁ ₄ alkyl groups and or a C₅ or ₆ spirocycloalkyl or alkenyl group, are novel and active against helminths. They are pro duced by conventional methods and formulated conventionally for use in treating humans and animals.
