# Miscible polyblend and molded article produced therefrom.

## Abstract
A miscible polyblend comprising an α,β ethylencially unsaturated dicarboxylic acid or its anhydride copolymerized with at least one monovinyl substituted aryl hydrocarbon monomer and an imide derivative of maleic anhydride copolymerized with at least one monovinyl substituted aryl hydrocarbon monomer is disclosed.
