# Adjusting apparatus for a doctor blade structure for copy machines.

## Abstract
Apparatus 8 for adjusting the position of a movable doctor blade structure 10 . The apparatus 8 includes a block member 30 that presents an inclined surface to a contact made with the doctor blade structure 10 . The block member 30 is moved relative to the contact made with the doctor blade structure 10 to alter the position of the doctor blade structure 10 by rotation of a screw 32 that is captively held in a support means 22 and is threaded to the block member 30 .
