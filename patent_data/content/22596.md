# A method and arrangement for keeping livestock.

## Abstract
A method for keeping livestock whereby the liquid constituents and the solid constituents of the dung from the livestock are separated from each other as soon as possible, and the liquid constituents together with any other liquid are discharged elsewhere as soon as possible. An arrangement comprising a dung interceptor device 7, 8, 9, 10 at least a part of which slopes locally, the lowest portion being connected up with a liquid discharge device 2 .
