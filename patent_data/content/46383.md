# Power take off means in refrigerated vehicles.

## Abstract
A wheeled land vehicle in which at least one free running ground engaging wheel 4, 5 has power take off means con nected thereto for providing power in or on the said vehicle, comprising a drive shaft 6 coaxial with and drivingly connect ed to said ground engaging wheel and extending inwardly therefrom through a non rotary hollow axle shaft 1 which mounts the wheel at its outer end.
