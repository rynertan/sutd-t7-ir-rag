# Disc brake assembly.

## Abstract
A disc brake assembly includes a caliper assembly 20 which is movably mounted on a torque plate 14 to engage a pair of friction elements 26,28 with a rotor 12 . The caliper assembly 20 defines a central opening 30 with abutment surfaces 32, 34 and one arm 66 of the torque plate extends into the central opening 30 to engage one 34 of the abut ment surfaces 32, 34 .
