# HIGH PURITY PALLADIUM NICKEL ALLOY PLATING BATH, PROCESS THEREFOR AND ALLOY COVERED ARTICLES AND GOLD OR GOLD ALLOY COVERED ARTICLES OF ALLOY COVERED ARTICLES.

## Abstract
A high purity palladium nickel alloy plating bath contain ing 1 to 15 nickel, particularly suited for plating electronic parts, process therefor, and articles covered by the alloy and the alloy covered articles further covered by gold or gold alloy.
