# AN INDUCTION REGULATOR FOR AN INTERNALCOMBUSTION ENGINE

## Abstract
An induction regulator for an internal combustion engine for improving the flow of fuel to match engine requirements. In one embodiment the regulator comprises a perfo rated element in the form of a metallic gauze 11 which is supported and surrounded by a frame 9 . The frame 9 has two straps 13 which serve as attachment means for locat ing the regulator in the manifold. A reservoir 19 having an open top and secured to the frame 9 is disposed adjacent the gauze 11 on the downstream side thereof and serves to retain excess unvapourised fuel.
