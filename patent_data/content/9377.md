# Thermoformed shaped articles of poly p methylstyrene and process for their production, adaptable for use as food containers.

## Abstract
There are provided shaped articles, adaptable for use as, for instance, trays and food containers, thermo formed from oriented poly p methylstyrene sheet, oriented foam ed poly p methylstyrene film with oriented poly p meth ylstyrene foam or polystyrene foam, that have been cross linked by exposure to radiation. Such articles are resistent to fats and fatty foods contained therein and can be placed in microwave ovens without destruction by hot, melted fats. The articles can also withstand conventional oven temperatures. There is also provided a process for produc ing such articles.
