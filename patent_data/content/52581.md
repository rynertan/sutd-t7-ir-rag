# A mechanical expansion plug for the internal shaping of plastic material tubes in general, particularly for fashioning the seats of gaskets.

## Abstract
In a kneading machine including a fixed support struc ture 2 , a kneading trough 7 supported centrally for rotation about a vertical axis by the fixed support structure 2 , and a rotary kneading tool 10 located within the kneading trough 7 , the kneading tool 10 is rotatable about a substantially horizontal axis X X . The machine is also provided with fixed counter members 15 which cooperate with the kneading tool 10 .
