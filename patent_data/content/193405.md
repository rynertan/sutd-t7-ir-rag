# Racemization process.

## Abstract
The invention provides a process for the epimerization of N methyl 3 2 methylphenoxy 3 phenylpropylamine to its racemic form with an anion forming compound which is a C₁ C₆ alkyl alkali metal or an alkali metal C₁ C₆ alkylamide using as solvent 1,2 dimethoxyethane or tetrahydrofuran under inert conditions.
