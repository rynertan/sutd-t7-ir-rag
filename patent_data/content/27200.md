# Apparatus for encasing excavations.

## Abstract
An apparatus for encasing, propping or erecting falseworks in soil excavations comprises a crane and extend ible struts 21 intended for installation in the excavation and for actuation by the crane boom. Each strut 21 comprises a main inverted T shaped tubular body and a pair of elements 40 which are mounted, for sliding movement to and fro each other, on the bar of said T and are caused to slide by means ofa nut and screw 41,42 coupling and terminated externally with respective plate like frames 48 intended for propping the excavation walls.
