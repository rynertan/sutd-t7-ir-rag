# A continuous rotary drum filter means.

## Abstract
An improved rotary filter comprises baffle means 80 disposed between the filter drum 20 and the filter cloth 60 in substantial alignment with at least some of the circum ferential openings 34 in the filter drum 20 to decrease the erosive effects on the filter cloth 60 of fluid discharged outwardly from the circumferential openings 34 in the filter drum 20 .
