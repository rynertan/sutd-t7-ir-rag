# Device for use in the treatment of damaged articular surfaces of human joints.

## Abstract
The invention is in respect of a device for treating dam aged articular surfaces of human joints with particular refer ence to arthritic joints. The device comprises a cap 3 of concavo convex len ticular form, i.e. it is formed with a convex surface 3A and a concave surface 3B , and constructed of a bio compatible flexible material. In use the cap 3 is fitted over the damaged joint surfaces 1B and 2B which have been previously covered by living cartilage removed from another part of the same human body, the cap moulding the cartilage as it grows to form a joint surface substantially the same as the original joint surface before it was damaged.
