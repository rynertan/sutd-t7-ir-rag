# Production of prints.

## Abstract
A print assembly for viewing both by reflected and trans mitted light consisting of a first image being a photographic or printed colour reproduction having the appearance of an image of normal density and colour for viewing by reflected light and an identical second image arranged behind and in exact register with the first but being underexposed or of lesser density in re lation to the first. Where a photographic process is used the im ages may be formed on the front and rear of a single transpar ent substrate.
