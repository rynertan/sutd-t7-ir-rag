# SLOT MACHINE

## Abstract
A slot machine adapted to award prizes when prize winning combinations occur on prize winning lines, in which five series 66 70 of symbols displayable in sequence in which at least 52 different cards of a deck of playing cards are included, each of said five series of symbols including at least 12 different cards which consist of at least three cards to every suit, which includes at least any four out of 20 different cards consisting of aces, ten numbered pip cards, and court cards of said deck of playing cards.
