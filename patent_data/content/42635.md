# A rail vehicle brake rigging slack adjuster.

## Abstract
A rail vehicle slack adjuster of the axial type having a barrel 4 non rotatable under normal working conditions. Means between the barrel 4 and a non rotatable pull rod means 6, 48 for making possible a manual rotation of the barrel and thus manual adjustment of the adjuster comprises at least one clip shaped circumferential manual adjustment spring 52 between the barrel and a notch in the pull rod means.
