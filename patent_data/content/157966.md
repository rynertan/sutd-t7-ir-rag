# Hand operated fitting swager.

## Abstract
The invention pertains to a hand operated swager 10 for attaching swaged end fittings to flexible hose. The ap paratus includes an elongated frame 16 having a screw 42 rotatably supported at one end thereof and restrained against axial movement. An adapter 54 cooperates with the screw through a ball nut 58 mounted upon the adapter wherein the adapter is linearly displaceable upon the frame toward and away from a frame mounted swaging die 50 . The linear axial displacement between the adapter and screw is accom modated within the adapter permitting power means to be applied to the screw, if desired.
