# A substantially pure rheumatoid arthritis specific protein and an antibody against the same.

## Abstract
A substantially pure rheumatoid arthritis specific pro tein RASP and an antibody against the rheumatoid arthri tis specific protein anti RASP antibody are disclosed. The RASP is found specifically in the serum or plasma of a pa tient suffering from rheumatoid arthritis, and may be detect ed using an anti RASP antibody easily and effectively. Therefore, the anti RASP antibody of the present invention is useful for the diagnosis of rheumatoid arthritis by the cri terion of the presence of RASP.
