# Airtight rotating joint.

## Abstract
Airtight rotating joint. composed of a fixed part and of a rotating one both equipped with an axial conduit 3,8,23, 17 ending in a chamber 5, 19 , rendered airtight and sepa rated from the outside by means of a second pressurizable chamber 9, 26 . The joint is particularly suited to equipments where high purity materials have to be handled.
