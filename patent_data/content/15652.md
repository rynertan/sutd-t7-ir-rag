# 6 Chloro 6 deoxy D hexitols, their esters, their preparation, fertility control compositions containing them and their use in a contraceptive method.

## Abstract
6 Chloro 6 deoxy D hexitols, a novel class of com pounds, and their esters, possess male antifertility activity and are thus useful in antifertility compositions. They are prepared by reduction of 6 chloro 6 deoxy D hexoses.
