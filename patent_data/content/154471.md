# Food slicer.

## Abstract
In a food slicer, a frame 5 is mounted on posts 3 standing upright on a base 2, a cutting plate 25 is removably secured to an upper portion of the frame, and a transfer rotor 36 is removably secured to an upper portion of a rotary shaft 11 which extends upwardly through the frame and the cutting plate. The rotary shaft is rotatably supported by the frame and driven by a drive member 12 disposed beneath the frame. A hood 65,66 is mounted on the base, surrounding a region in which the drive member beneath the frame is arranged. The hood carries a discharge chute 70 which passes through an opening 22 in the frame to reach an opening 29 in the cutting plate for insertion of a cutting blade 30. The frame is connected to a drain pipe 21a.
