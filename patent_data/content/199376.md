# A sheet feeding device for use in an electrophotographic information printer.

## Abstract
A sheet feeding device in an electrophotogaphic in formation printer comprises a collecting roller 18 and a driven sheet advancing roller 19 and as a drive means coupled mechanically to said collecting roller 18 and or said driven roller 19 an electronic stepping motor 38 which is supplied with drive pulses of a predetermined pulse number and frequency from a motor drive unit 39 which is connected with a progam controlled control unit 35 for operating the stepping motor 38 in an operational se quence for the information printer.
