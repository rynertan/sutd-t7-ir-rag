# Process and device for rendering harmless sticky material adhering to cotton fibers.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a process for rendering harmless sticky material adhering to cotton fibers, termed honey dew . According to the process the cotton 11 is heated for a brief period of time to a temperature adequate to render said honeydew hard and brittle, and this without adversely affecting the cotton fibers. There are also provided means 15, 11 for effecting such treatment of cottong fibers in a continuous manner.
