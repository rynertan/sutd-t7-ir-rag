# DUAL CONDUIT DUAL CONDUIT

## Abstract
A dual wall safety tube assembly 10 comprises an inner, seamless, stainless steel tube 11 of considerable length and an outer, welded stainless steel tube 12 of the same length, in which the inner tube 11 is connected as a process line without any junctions except for end couplings and the outer tube is similarly connected as a monitoring or inerting conduit. The assembly may include further options of electric heating cable, plastics jacketing 70 and thermal insulation 72 of fibreglass or similar material.
