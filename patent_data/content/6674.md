# Bituminous carpet backing.

## Abstract
A fibrous floor covering characterized in that it has an integral backing layer comprising a mixture of a from 50 to 95 w of a bituminous component comprising a bitumen, and optionally a wax, wherein said bituminous component has a penetration of below 30 0.1 mm at 25 C, and b from 5 to 50 w of a block copolymer having the general configuration A B B A
