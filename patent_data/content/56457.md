# Pneumatic tyre for motor vehicles with tread pattern.

## Abstract
The invention concerns a pneumatic tire for motor vehicles comprising a tread pattern showing at least two longitudinal grooves which define a plurality of circumferen tial portions, and a series of axial grooves which divide the two outermost circumferential portions into blocks. The edges of the inner circumferential portions are provided with blind oblique slits which are all inclined in the same sense with respect to the circumferential direction, said slits being substantially parallel to one another.
