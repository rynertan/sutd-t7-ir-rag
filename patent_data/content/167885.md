# ION EXCHANGE RESIN MEMBRANE

## Abstract
A perfluorocarbon type ion exchange resin membrane reinforced with a reinforcing cloth for the electrolysis of an alkali metal halide, characterized in that said reinforcing cloth is a leno cloth with the warp and weft yarns made of perfluoropolymer fiber the warp yarns have from 20 to 100 deniers the weft yarns have from 40 to 200 deniers the warp density is from 26 to 80 yeans inch the weft density is from 13 to 50 yarns inch and the cross sectional flat ratio of each yarn is from 0.9 to 0.1.
