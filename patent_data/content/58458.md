# Cross country ski rear fastening.

## Abstract
Cross country ski rear fastening comprising a body 2 to be fastened to the ski, a fork shaped member 5 for locking the footwear heel 19 , mounted on a stem 6 movable between a front position of engagement of the heel 19 and a rear position of disengagement, a spring means 9 elastically keeping the fork shaped member 5 in disengage ment condition, and a lever system 11, 12 actionable by the skier and acting on the fork shaped member 5 causing the engagement of the heel 19 on overcoming a dead position and its disengagement by elastic return.
