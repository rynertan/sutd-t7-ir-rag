# A process for the preparation of penicillanic acid 1,1 dioxide and derivatives thereof.

## Abstract
Preparation of penicillanic acid 1,1 dioxide and deriva tives thereof by debromination of 6 alpha bromo and or 6,6 dibromopenicillanic acid 1,1 dioxides. The debromina tion is effected in a water containing medium by reduction with zinc in association with an acid having a pKa value measured in water less than 3.5.
