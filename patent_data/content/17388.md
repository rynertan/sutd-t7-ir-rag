# Crash pad for vehicle seats.

## Abstract
A vehicle seat 10 having a rigid rail frame 22 and a crash pad which is vandal proof and inexpensive. A seat back 12 formed of a sheet of flexible material having a high surface hardness is mounted on the rigid rail frame. The top portion of the seat back includes an integrally formed flexible energy absorbing bubble 15 . The energy absorbing bubble extends substantially the length of the seat back and is disposed at the top of the seat back for cushioning the impact of a passenger s head during an impact or rapid manoeuvre. The energy absorbing bubble can encompass a flexible energy absorbing brace 29 , having an L shaped cross section, that is cantilevered from the rigid rail frame.
