# Colour picture tube.

## Abstract
Colour picture tube in which a tube element, e.g. the shadow mask, is constituted by an Fe alloy to which 25 45 wt of Ni and 0.3 10 wt of at least Cr are added. A Cr rich layer is formed on the tube element surface and a black oxide film with a spinel structure containing Cr is formed on the surface of this film.
