# Vehicle steering system.

## Abstract
A four wheel steering for a four wheel drive vehicle has a front wheel steering mechanism 1 , a steering wheel 3 for ef fecting a movement of the front wheel steering mechanism to a desired angle, a rear wheel steering mechanism 7 , a control circuit 25 for effecting a movement of the rear wheel steering mechanism in relation to the steering movement of the front wheels, and a control assembly for compensating the steering angle of the rear wheels in accordance with the drive torque distribution 28 . The ratio of the steering angle of the rear wheels to the steering angle of the front wheels as considered in the same direction of steering is increased in accordance with an increase in the ratio of the torque distribution.
