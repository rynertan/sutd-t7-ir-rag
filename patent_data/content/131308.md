# Fluorinated copolymers with improved cure site components.

## Abstract
Copolymers of tetrafluouoethylene and perfluoromethyl perfluorovinyl ether with a cure site monomer of the formula R₁CH CR₂R₃ wherein R₁ and R₂ are independently selected from hydrogen and fluorine and R₃ is independently selected from hydrogen, fluorine and alkyl or perfluoroalkyl.
