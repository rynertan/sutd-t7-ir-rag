# CO EXTRUDED MULTILAYER PRODUCT HAVING IMPROVED BOND STRENGTH

## Abstract
A co extruded multilayer product 10 has at least two adjacent layers 12, 14 of material and a bond surface 16 formed between the adjacent layers of material. This bond surface appears as an extended bond line when the product is viewed in cross section. There is also disclosed an extrusion adapter and process for producing the co extruded multilayer product.
