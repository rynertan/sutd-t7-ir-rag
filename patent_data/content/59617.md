# Yogurt milk shake.

## Abstract
A yogurt milk shake composition comprises a up to 99.45 of yogurt and milk in the range yogurt milk 80 20 to 53 47 b 0.5 to 2.5 wt. of a soya protein whipping agent and c 0.05 to 0.5 wt. of a stabilizer comprising CMC and xanthan gum in the ratio CMC xanthan gum 0 100 to 100 0. Upon manual shaking, the milk shake produces a drink containing entrapped air. It can retain its milk shake appear ance for about 30 minutes after shaking.
