# Dental prosthesis.

## Abstract
A dental prosthesis which has the whole or part of its surface which is in contact with the mucous membrane of the mouth coated with a soft hydrophilic polyurethane which has a thickness of from 0.1 to 3.0mm when hydrated is described. The polyurethane is applied to the prosthesis in an anhydrous state and is subsequently hydrated using water. The hydrophilic polyurethane adheres well to the material of the prosthesis and provides a soft and comfort able liner for the tissue bearing surfaces of the prosthesis.
