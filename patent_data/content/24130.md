# Electric kettle.

## Abstract
An electric kettle having a metal sheathed element 20 permanently mounted on a metal head 22 so as to produce a hot spot 29 and the switching means comprising a dry over load responsive actuating mechanism 14 responsive to over heating of the hot spot to open switch contacts 71,72 mounted on an electrical insulating mounting 61 which sup ports a movable thrust member 74 which extends from the contacts through a way within the mounting to a steam responsive actuating mechanism 18 for actuation by the latter, and the mechanisms 14 and 18 are preferably assembled on the mounting 61 to form an assembly in which the contacts are disposed in a chamber 75 within the assem bly.
