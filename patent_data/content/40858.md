# Bottom stop applying and gapping apparatus for a slide fastener chain.

## Abstract
An apparatus 10 is disclosed for applying bottom end stops 17 to a slide fastener chain 19 and simul taneously gapping the chain at predetermined intervals. The apparatus includes a means 20 for positioning and holding a group of fastener elements 19 on the chain, said means being pivotally movable in contact with the applied bottom stop 17 in a plane either perpendicular to or parallel with the plane of the chain 19 .
