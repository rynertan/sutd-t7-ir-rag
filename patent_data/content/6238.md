# Magnetic recording tape cartridge.

## Abstract
The present invention is directed to a magnetic re cording tape cartridge 1 having a tape chamber for con taining a recording tape 7 , the intermediate portion of the recording tape being adapted to advance along a predetermined path defined along a plurality of openings defined in the front wall 2 of the chamber, a magnetic shielding plate 17 , a pad device 10 disposed to face one of the openings to which a recording head of a player is inserted and a pair of ribs 13a, 13b extending in the backward direction from positions near the front wall 2 with such a height that the top surface of the ribs is clo sely opposed to the bottom edge of the plate of the pad device 10 so that the pad device 10 is held in the re quired position.
