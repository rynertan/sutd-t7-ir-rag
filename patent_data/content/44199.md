# A cushioning structure.

## Abstract
In order to provide a cushioning structure which is aesthetically appealing and which is comparable in comfort to a feather filled cushion the invention provides a flexible foam casing 1 within which is accommodated a stockinette bag 15 containing shredded or crumbed flexible foam pieces 17 . Preferably the inner faces 11,13 of the foam casing 1 are profiled in the form of fingers.
