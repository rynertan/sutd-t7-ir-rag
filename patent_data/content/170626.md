# A MOUNTING FOR A ROLLER BLIND

## Abstract
A roller blind is carried on a tubular element 12 fitted at each end into a socket defined by a cup shape body 1 having a foot 2 for securing it to a fixed support. The cup shape body 1 is provided with an aperture 5 partly spanned by a projecting tonge 7 with an enlarged head end 7 which engages into an aperture 13 in the end of the tubular body 12 when this is fitted into the cup shape body 1 and turned in to register with the projection 7 .
