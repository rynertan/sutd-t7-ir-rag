# Vibration responsive mounting arrangement for automotive engine or the like.

## Abstract
The present invention features an arrangement wherein a valve or valves 74 which are either manually or automa tically controlled, control the amount of hydraulic fluid discharged from a belt driven pulsation pump 16 and forced into and out of a variable volume chamber arrange ment 64 which is connected in series with an elastomeric insulating member, whereby the desired vibration offsetting pressure pulsations may be produced in said chamber.
