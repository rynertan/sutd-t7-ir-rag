# Automobile antenna system.

## Abstract
The present invention provides a diversity reception type automotive antenna system comprising a pole type antenna as a main antenna and a high frequency pickup as a sub antenna, the high frequency pickup being mounted on a connection between each adjacent vehicle portions and functioning to detect surface high frequency currents in duced on the vehicle body by radio or other waves and flowing on the connection. The high frequency pickup can readily be installed on the vehicle body without any external ly projecting portion which would otherwise damage the design of the vehicle body.
