# Electrical switchgear apparatus.

## Abstract
Electrical switchgear apparatus includes a circuit breaker unit enclosed in a first insulating gas filled grounded tank a disconnecting switch enclosed in a second insulating gas filled grounded tank a lightning arrester and a grounding switch, characterised in that the first tank 20 and the second tank 24 are connected together in end to end relation with a gas seal between them, a third insulating gas filled grounded tank 28 is connected to the second tank on the side thereof opposite to the first tank and with a gas seal between them, the grounding switch includes a fourth tank 34 connected to the third tank on the side thereof opposite to the second tank and the lightning arrester includes a fifth tank 40 connected to the third tank.
