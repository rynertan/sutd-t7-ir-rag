# Device for adjusting the flex in rear entry ski boots.

## Abstract
The device comprises a settable elastic U like link ele ment 3 housed in a receiving seat 1 defined in the rear cuff 2 of the ski boot and coupled to an engagement ele ment 3b protruding from the seat 1 and adapted to engage, with the rear cuff 2 in the closed position, with a retaining element 20 provided rearwardly on the shell 21 of the ski boot.
