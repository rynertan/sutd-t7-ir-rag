# Cassette changing apparatus.

## Abstract
A cassette changing mechanism including a plurality of followers 38, 48 with friction clutches 54 thereon for mov ing cassettes. Limit switches 63, 66, 68, 70 serve a dual function as mechanical stops for the followers and for signal ling a controller. Cassettes are moved to a record playback position and to output stores 16, 17 simultaneously with positive drive to the output stores and spring linked drive the record playback position to eliminate hard jams. The control ler detects the failure of the changer to complete a subcycle of a complete change cycle and repeats an appropriate subcycle to attempt to correct a jammed condition.
