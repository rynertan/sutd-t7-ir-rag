# A synergistic admixture containing bromo 2 bromomethylglutaronitrile and methylene bis thiocyanate and process for inhibiting microbial growth using said components.

## Abstract
Admixtures of 2 bromo 2 bromomethyleneglutaro nitrile and methylene bis thiocyanate have a synergistic antimicrobial activity. The present invention concerns processes for inhibiting microbial growth using both of said active ingredients and furthermore a synergistic antimicro bial composition containing both of said active ingredient.
