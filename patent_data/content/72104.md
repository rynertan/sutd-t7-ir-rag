# Sealed electrical connector.

## Abstract
An electrical connector housing 3, 4 is sealed at its con ductor entry face by means of a sealing member 14 which is compressed against the face and into engagement with a shroud 12 surrounding the face and with conductors 8 passing through holes 15 in the sealing member 14 , by means of a lid 17 which is latched to the shroud 12 . The sealing member 14 when compressed is also engaged by terminals 6, 7 terminating the conductors 8 and prevents back out of the terminals 6, 7 from their passageways 5 in the housing 3, 4 .
