# A LENS HOLDER ASSEMBLY FOR A FIBER OPTIC CONNECTOR

## Abstract
This invention is a lens type fiber optic connector that has a lens holder assembly 70 releasably mounted 54, 78 within the bore of an insert 55 . This arrangement provides for quick and easy installation, repair or replacement of a lens 10 or defective optical fiber 81 without the need to replace or disassemble the entire connector.
