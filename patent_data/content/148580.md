# Cleaning apparatus for a disc drive.

## Abstract
Apparatus for cleaning the read write head of a drive unit for a jacketted flexible magnetic disc comprises a thin substantially square jacket 10 , having, in register on each side, a central aperture 11 and a slot 12 which extends radially from adjacent the central aperture in a direction towards and normal to the rear edge 13 of the jacket. The jacket contains a double sided cleaning element 14 having a pair of opposite cleaning surfaces of which a portion of each is exposed by a respective slot. The cleaning surfaces comprise respective layers 15 of cleaning material secured together via an intermediate layer 16 of a material which is impermeable to magnetic head cleaning fluid.
