# Pick up arm for an optical disk player.

## Abstract
A disk drive apparatus for optical disks includes a rotary pick up arm 33 used for seeking and track following. A light beam L is injected along the axis of rotation M of the rotary pick up arm 33 , guided through a stick shaped prism 35 in cantilever form, and focused upon the record ing surface of the disk 31 theough an objective lens 38 which can be moved over the data tracks by means of the arm 33 . A reflected light beam from the recording surface of the disk 31 is guided back and taken out at the axis of rotation M .
