# Adjuvant for promoting absorption of therapeutically active substances through the digestive tract, pharmaceutical composition comprising said adjuvant and process for preparing said pharmaceutical composition enamine derivatives.

## Abstract
An adjuvant for promoting absorption of therapeutically active substances through the digestive tract having the formula CHEM and pharmaceutical compositions comprising said adjuvant in combination with a therapeutically active substane together with a process for preparing said pharmaceutical composition. The adjuvant is able to effectively elevate the concentration of such active substances in the blood stream even when said active substances are usually absorbable through the digestive tract only with considerable difficulty.
