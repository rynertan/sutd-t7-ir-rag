# Articles made from flexible sheet material.

## Abstract
Flexible sheet material suitable for use in the manufacture of wear resistant laminated articles such as bearings and rotor blades comprises particles of graphite and heat resistant web forming fibres, preferably of poly aromatic amide , bound to gether with an organic binder, the proportions of the ingre dients being To make wear resistant articles, sheets of the material are impregnated with resin, superimposed one upon another, and subjected to high pressure and elevated temperature.
