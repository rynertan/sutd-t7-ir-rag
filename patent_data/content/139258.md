# Electrodeposited grinding tool.

## Abstract
An electrodeposited grinding tool having an electrode posited abrasive layer 6 formed by electrodepositing abrasive grains to an electrodeposition thickness t at least three times as large as the diameter of the abrasive grains. Pores are dispersed in the electrodeposited abrasive layer 6 in a volume ratio of 10 to 70 .
