# METHOD FOR MAKING COLORED PHOTOCHROMIC PRESCRIPTION OPHTHALMIC LENSES

## Abstract
Colored photochromic ophthalmic lenses are produced by combining a photochromic cap element 10 , having an inte grally colored back surface 11, 12 with an ophthalmic crown base element 14 having a prescription ground back surface 15 , the cap back and base element front surfaces 11, 15 having been finished to a matching standard base curvature, thus providing a cemented assembly exhibiting a selected combination of prescribed vision correcting and light filtering properties.
