# Novel heptadecapeptide, a method for its preparation and a pharmaceutical composition containing the same.

## Abstract
A novel heptadecapeptide is described which is represented by the primary structure Tyr Gly Gly Phe Leu Arg Arg Ile Arg Pro Lys Trp Asp Asn Gln. There is also disclosed a process for the preparation of the novel heptadecapeptide as well as a pharmaceuti cal composition containing the same. The heptadecapeptide according to the invention possesses an analgesic activity, especially an opioid activity and is preferably obtained by extraction and purification from porcine duodenum.
