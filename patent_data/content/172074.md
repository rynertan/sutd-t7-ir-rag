# Fruit filler for pastry products and process for its preparation.

## Abstract
A fruit filling, for use in pastry products, prepared by using critical proportions of apple powder, pregelatinized tapioca starch, corn syrup containing at least 70 percent of high fructose solids, and concentrated fruit juice or concen trated fruit puree. The fruit filling is prepared in the absence of heat. Little or no granular sugars are added to the filler.
