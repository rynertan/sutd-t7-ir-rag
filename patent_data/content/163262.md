# Retractable pin sprocket wheel assembly.

## Abstract
A platen assembly 10 including a cylindrical platen 11 for advancing a web of record material through a printing device, the record material having perforations along its borders for receiving pins 13 disposed in pin sprocket wheel assemblies 12 mounted on opposite ends of the platen 11 . As the platen assembly rotates, the pins 13 are drawn into the periphery of the platen 11 where the printhead is operating.
