# Vibration absorbing system for an automotive vehicle.

## Abstract
A vibration absorbing system for an automotive vehicle with a vehicle body, includes an automotive vehicle acces sory 20 and a mounting device 90 . The mounting device 90 has a resiliency. The mounting device attaches the accessory to the vehicle body so that the accessory will be resiliently supported on the vehicle body. The accessory 20 with the mounting device is able to be vibrated and has an eigenfrequency in relation to the vibration thereof. The eigenfrequency is set to a relatively low value.
