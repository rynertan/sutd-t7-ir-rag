# PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION OF ACYLATED INDANES AND TETRAHYDRONAPHTHALENES

## Abstract
Process for producing compounds useful in perfumery including acylated indanes and tetrahydronaphthalenes using a lower acid derivative in the presence of a hydrocarbon or hydrocarbon mixture containing C₅ to C₁₀ alkanes at a temperature of at least 20 C preferably up to 5 C.
