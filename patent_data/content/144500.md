# PET WASHER

## Abstract
The pet washer comprises a transparent box 1 which is divided into upper and lower parts 2 , 3 , with semicircular openings 5 in the center of the front ends of both upper and lower parts so that when the box is closed the pet s head remains outside the box 1 , a spray nozzle 6 or tubular spray 6A which is attached to the upper inner surface of the upper part of the box 2 , a water draining latticed board 9 extending over the bottom surface of the lower part of the box 3 from the front to the rear, and a drain port 10 which is located at the rear end of the lower part of the box 3 .
