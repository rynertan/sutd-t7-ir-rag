# Device for cutting off the wings of slaughtered poultry.

## Abstract
Device for separating the wings 22 from the trunk 21 of slaughtered poultry 20 hanging by the legs on a conveyor track, comprising two cutting knives 6,7 attached to a carrier 1 which is rotatably driven in the conveying device, positioning means for the poultry in between and a wing support for each knife which cooperates with the latter and crosses the track of the knife.
