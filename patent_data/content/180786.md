# Pharmaceutical or dietetic composition having a high antithrombotic and antiarteriosclerotic activity.

## Abstract
A pharmaceutical or dietetic composition for the preven tion and treatment of vascular, arteriosclerotic and thrombo tic pathologies, comprised of lecithin and oils having a high content of eicosapentaenoic C 20 5, n 3 and or docosahex aenoic C 22 6, n 3 acids, and or esters thereof. Said composition affords a considerable enhancement of vascular pathologies therapy by the use of natural ingredients commonly employed in human nourishment without any trouble.
