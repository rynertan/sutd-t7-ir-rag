# Folding aid for the disabled.

## Abstract
A folding backrest of a wheelchair has two attendant handles 5 fixed to upper tubes 4 hinged to vertical lower tubes 2 so as to be turnable into lowered positions. Supported on the upper tubes 4 are respective cams 9 turnable rearwardly by an attendant about a horizontal axis and in opposition to springs 11 , from positions in which they bear against horizontal pins 13 fixed at the rears of the lower tubes 2 and maintain the upper tubes 4 in their raised positions, into positions which allow them to be lowered past the pins 13 to permit the backrest 2 5 to be lowered.
