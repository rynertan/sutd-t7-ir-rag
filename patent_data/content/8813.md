# Processes for the preparation of 3 azabicyclo 3.1.0 hexane derivatives.

## Abstract
2 Cyano 3 azabicyclo 3.1.0 hexane may be prepared by reacting 3 azabicyclo 3.1.0 hex 2 ene or the trimer thereof with an alkali metal bisulphite to form its bisulphite adduct, and treating said adduct with an alkali metal cyanide.
