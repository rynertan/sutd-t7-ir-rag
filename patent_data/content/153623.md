# Plated resin article.

## Abstract
A molded article of a transparent ABS resin comprising 10 to 24 by weight of a diene type rubber having a weight average particle size of 150 mÂµ or less and not containing methyl methacrylate in the continuous resin phase is subjected to wet plating. The resulting plated resin article has an excellent adhesion of the plating layer and a good transparency.
