# Method and system for removing immunosuppressive components from the blood of mammals.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a method and system for extracorporeally treating the blood of a patient having diseased tissue or a condition, such as cancer, which results from or depends upon a dysfunction of the patient s immune system. In accordance with the invention, blood is with drawn from the patient, a low molecular weight fraction of the blood containing immunosuppressive components which retard or prevent immune system responses is selectively separated from the withdrawn blood, preferably by means of an ultra filter, and treated blood is returned to the patient to initiate an immune response.
