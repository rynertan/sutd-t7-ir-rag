# Salts of cholic acids, process for their preparation and therapeutic compositions containing them as active principles.

## Abstract
Salification products between cholic acids chenodeox icholic and ursodeoxycholic acid and trimebutyne trimethoxy 3,4,5 benzoic acid ester of 2 phenyl 2 dimethylamino butanol in the molar ratio 1 1 or 2 1 have been prepared. These new salts show a pharmacotherapeutic action, which, as a whole, is not found in other known drugs, and which makes them useful for the treatment of diskinesiae, biliary dyspepsiae, cholecystopathies, and for the normaliza tion of the kinesis of the intestinal gastro biliary tract.
