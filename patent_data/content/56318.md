# Female incontinence device.

## Abstract
An incontinence device for females is intended for long term wear. It includes a tubular sheath 10 integral with a funnel 12 and a urine conducting pipe 14. The sheath is secured to a pad 18 of adhesive material having a central hole leading into the interior of the sheath 10. The pad is dimensioned so that it can be stuck to the skin of the wearer in the region immediately surrounding the urethral orifice meatus .
