# Antibiotic PA 31088 IV, its salts with bases, a process for its manufacture, pharmaceutical compositions containing this antibiotic, streptomyces tokunonensis PA 31088 and its mutants.

## Abstract
A new antibiotic having Î² lactamase inhibitory activity is described, PA 31088 IV, which is useful as a medicament and a veterinary drug for inhibiting the growth of gram positive and gram negative pathogenic microorganisms, together with a process for its preparation, characterized by the cultivation of a strain of Streptomyces tokunensis sp. nov. in a suitable medium and the recovery of PA 31088 IV from the cultured broth.
