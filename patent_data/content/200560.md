# Roller paint guard assembly.

## Abstract
A paint roller apparatus 10 includes a paint housing 11 adapted to support a paint roller 14 and a separate handle 12 . A closable slit 35 is also provided in the housing 11 and connection means 29 are provided to permit a water hose to be connected to the housing 11 to supply water under pressure which is discharged through the slit 35 to impact the paint roller 14 causing it to rotate and simultaneously wash residual paint from the pain roller. Preferably the handle 12 is removable and has a solid threaded mounting end 30 which, when threadedly con nected to the connection means 29 closes the slit 35 , and which, when disconnected from the connection means 29 , permits connection of the hose to the housing.
