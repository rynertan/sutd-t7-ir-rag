# SHUTTER OPENING AND CLOSING DEVICE FOR A DISK CASSETTE

## Abstract
A shutter opening and closing device for a disk cassette according to the invention comprises a cassette support having a pawl formed in an integral relationship thereon for pressing an engaging body of a disk cassette to open and then slide a shutter as the disk cassette is inserted into the cassette support. A shutter closing member made of an elastic body is disposed with one side of the cassette support for engagement with an engaging hole of the shutter at an instant when the shutter is slid.
