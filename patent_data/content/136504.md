# Tape cartridge.

## Abstract
In a tape reel 3 for use in a recording tape cartridge 1 for winding a recording tape 2 , there are formed a plurality of radial grooves 11 on the upper face of a lower flange 7 of the tape reel 3 and a projected ribs 12 on the lower face corresponding to the grooves 11 so that the wall thickness of the lower flange 7 can be generally equal over all area to improve the flow of the plastic resin material in the molding process.
