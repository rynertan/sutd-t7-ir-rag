# New M 4 and iso M 4 derivatives, their preparation and compositions containing them.

## Abstract
Dihydro M 4 and dihydro IsoM 4 are new compounds which may be prepared by the catalytic hydrogenation of M 4 or IsoM 4 respectively. They, and their salts and esters which may be prepared by conventional salification or esterification reactions with the parent dihydro M 4 or dihydro IsoM 4 , are capable of inhibiting cholesterol biosynthesis in the liver and may be formulated, for ther apeutic use, with conventional pharmaceutical carriers or diluents.
