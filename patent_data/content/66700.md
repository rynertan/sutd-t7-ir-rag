# Recovery of xanthine stimulants from aqueous media.

## Abstract
A process for the recovery of xanthine stimulants from an aqueous solution containing xanthine stimulants extracted from cocoa material, which comprises contacting the solution with a substantally neutral adsorbent and separating the adsorbent, with the xanthine stimulants adsorbed thereon, from the aqueous solution having a reduced content of xanthine stimulants.
