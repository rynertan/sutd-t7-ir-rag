# Electric jig saw with accessory storage device.

## Abstract
An electric jigsaw 10 includes a motor housing 12 , a shoe 15 having a pivot arch 16 and a shoe plate 18 , the arch 16 securing the shoe plate 18 to the jigsaw housing 12 for relative pivotal movement of the housing and shoe plate about the longitudinal axis of the arch 16 . An axial cavity 30 is defined by the arch 16 and the shoe plate 18 , and a plug 40 removably disposed in one end 42 of the cavity, the plug 40 including a plurality of axially extending slots 60, 44 open at one end 46 thereof for removably receiving attachments 48, 54, 56 for the saw 10 .
