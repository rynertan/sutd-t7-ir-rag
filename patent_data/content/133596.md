# Brake actuator linkages.

## Abstract
A brake actuator linkage has a swivel which comprises an axially rotatable member 4 extending through an aperture 3 in a clevis and constrained by a head 5 against movement in one direction has a spring clip having a first part 10 gripping the rotatable member and a second part 11, 12 extending partly around the clevis to resiliently engage the head and continuously urge it against the clevis.
