# Tetrahydro 1,2,4 oxadiazine 5 thione derivatives.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to optically active or racemic tetrahydro 1,2,4 oxadiazine 5 thione derivatives, to methods for their preparation, and pharmaceutical compositions con taining the said compounds. Methods for the anticonvulsive treatment of mammals, especially human beings are de scribed. The compounds of the formula I have similar activity to diphenyl hydantoin but their toxicity is much lower.
