# Games racket frame.

## Abstract
A hollow injection moulded thermoplastics games racket frame 30 comprising a head 21 and a shaft 20 , the head 21 having integrally formed internal supports 25 contain ing the stringing holes 23 and the moulding being polyurethane foam filled. The foam filling is provided in two parts, a lighter filling 32 for the head 21 and a denser filling 33 for the shaft 20 . The shaft filling 33 is integrally formed with a foam handle portion 34 . The handle end 34 of the shaft 20 is preferably provided with moulded in holes 31 through which filling 33 can flow during its foaming.
