# Apparatus and process for removal of toner particles.

## Abstract
Apparatus and method for simultaneously removing and transporting undersirable residual insulating toner parti cles 25 from a flexible imaging member 10 comprising in operative relationship, a cleaning roll means 12 containing on its surface insulating carrier particles 26 , said cleaning roll being charged to a predetermined potential, a deflected flexible imaging member 10 means containing residual insulating toner particles 25 thereon, a cleaning zone 30 encompassed by and situated between said cleaning roll means 12 and said deflected flexible imaging member means 10 , magnet means 14 contained in the cleaning roll means, and magnetic strips 15 contained in the outer periphery of said cleaning roll means.
