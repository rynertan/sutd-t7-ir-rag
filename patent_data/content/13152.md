# Marker sleeves.

## Abstract
A sleeve 1, 1A, 20, 30, 40, 50 or 60 having an outer wall 2 defining a tubular body and a plurality of inner webs 3, 4, 21 23, 31 34, 42, 52 or 62 extending longitudinally of the tubular body. Each inner web is spaced from its overlying outer wall portion along an adjacent longitudinally extending aperture 5, 24 26, 35, 41, 51 or 61 . The marker sleeve is to be positioned about an article which extends through its tubular body.
