# Ornamental attachment for slide fastener sliders.

## Abstract
An ornamental attachment 10 35 for a slide fastener slider 14 , with a pull tab 11 36 comprising a body 17 39 having a channel 18 42 for receiving therein the pull tab, a projection 29 45 extending from one plate 19 40 of the body into the channel for locking the pull tab in the channel, and a pair of spaced slits 27 44 formed in the body and communicating with and extending longitudinally of the channel to define therebetween a resilient web portion 28 43 which is displaceable against the resiliency thereof away from another plate in response to engagement be tween the pull tab and the locking projection when the pull tab is inserted into the channel, until the pull tab becomes locked by the locking projection.
