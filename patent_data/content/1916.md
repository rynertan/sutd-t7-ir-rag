# A reusable badge and a press for making the same.

## Abstract
A reusable badge and a press for making the same, in which the badge comprises a transparent lens 2 with a peripheral flange 4 defining an internal groove 6 that receives a backing plate 12 with a releasable spring fit, a display sheet 10 being located between the lens and the backing plate. The press for assembling the badge comprises a cup shaped anvil 20 for receiving the backing plate 12 and a hammer body 30 for pressing the lens over the backing plate, the anvil and hammer being mounted on respective pivotally interconnected arms.
