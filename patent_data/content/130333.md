# Process for oxidizing halopyridines to halopyridine N oxides.

## Abstract
Described is a process for oxidizing 2 chloropyridine or 2 bromopyridine to the corresponding N oxide with peracet ic acid generated in situ from H₂O₂ and acetic acid in the presence of a catalyst selected from the group consisting of maleic acid, maleic anhydride, phthalic anhydride, and mix tures thereof.
