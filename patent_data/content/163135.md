# PROCESS FOR THE PRODUCTION OF LOW CALORIE ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES

## Abstract
A method for producing a low calorie alcholic beverage whereby a starch hydrolyzate is combined with the fermenta tion mixture used to produce the beverage. The starch hydrolyzate is one obtained by the enzymatic hydrolysis of starch. The fermentation mixture is contacted with a saccharifying enzyme to provide concurrent saccharification and fermentation of the starch hydrolyzate.
