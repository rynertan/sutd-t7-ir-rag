# A device for recovering oil floating on water.

## Abstract
A device for recovering oil floating on the surface of water comprising a framework 50 and a number of discs 51 rotat ably mounted thereon. The framework is supported on floats 58, 59 with the discs partly immersed in the water, and each disc is associated with a scraper 57 and chute 54 so that oil removed from the disc is deposited into a collection tray 52 from which it can be removed via line 53 . Each disc 51 car ries at least one axially directed concentric ring 4, 5 which presents an inwardly directed surface 7, 8 . Each disc may car ry a plurality of rings of the same or different axial depths.
