# Carene modified petroleum resins and their production.

## Abstract
Producing modified petroleum resins by the Friedel Crafts copolymerisation of a C₅ olefines and diolefines, C₆ olefines or diolefines or a mixture of C₅ and C₆ olefines and diolefines feed and carene optionally together with a branched chain olefine.
