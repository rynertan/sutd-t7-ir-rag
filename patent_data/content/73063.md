# Crankshaft pulley of engine.

## Abstract
A crankshaft pulley 10 of an internal combustion engine, comprises a first pulley section 14 for use with a V belt to drive an engine accessory, the first pulley section 14 being formed of sheet metal, and a second pulley section 16 for use with a cogged belt to drive a camshaft of the engine, the second pulley section 16 being formed of sheet metal and integral at at least a part thereof with the first pulley section 14 .
