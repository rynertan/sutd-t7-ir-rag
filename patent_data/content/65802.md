# Device for packing a fowl.

## Abstract
A device for packing poultry with means 7 for support ing an opened bag 9 and means 12, 13 for pushing a fowl into said bag, a pushing member 12 having a vertical central baffle 26 oriented in the pushing direction to guide the legs of the fowl in order to prevent a twisting movement of the pushed fowl, that otherwise by times could happen and provoke irregularity or even disturbancy of the pushing operation.
