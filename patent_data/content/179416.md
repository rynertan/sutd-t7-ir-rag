# LONG WEAR COSMETICS

## Abstract
A composition adaptable for use as a long wear cosmetic or cosmetic base containing a pentaerythritol tetra C₂₀ C₂₄ aliphatic hydrocarbon carboxylate, particularly pentaerythri tol tetrabehenate is disclosed. Various uses of the compo sitions of the invention, e.g., as long wear eye cream, long wear eye stick, a long wear foundation, a long wear lipstick or lip cream, or as a long wear eyeliner or mascara are also disclosed.
