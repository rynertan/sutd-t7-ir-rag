# Vehicle bumper assembly with foamed core.

## Abstract
A vehicle bumper assembly comprises a channel section outer shell 12 of injection moulded plastics material extend ing transversely across the front or rear 20 of a motor vehicle and having spaced apart brackets 32 for mounting the bum per on the motor vehicle. The outer shell 12 is substantially filled with an energy absorbing foam 24 adhered to the inside of the outer shell 12 and the mounting brackets 32 have arm portions 36 moulded in the foam filling 24 .
