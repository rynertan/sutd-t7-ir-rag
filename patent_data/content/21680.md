# Glass fiber mat with improved binder and method of making it.

## Abstract
A glass fiber mat having excellent tensile strength and flexibility is provided herein. The mat includes a plurality of glass fibers and a binder therefor which is characterized by comprising about 25 to 90 by weight of a ureaformaldehyde resin and about 10 to 75 by weight of a styrene butadiene latex copolymer containing about 0.1 to 5 by weight of an acrylamide type monomer.
