# Apparatus for cutting connecting portions of sausage chain.

## Abstract
A sausage chain whose pose is corrected by a pose correcting roller 21 arrayed in the form of an arc, is fed to a driving drum 3 by a transferring means 1 . Pressing rollers 41 arranged to face the sausage receiving surface of the driving drum 3 are adapted to convey the sausage chain in sliding contact therewith. The connecting portions of the sausage chain are cut by a cutter 51 which is disposed along the sausage conveyance path.
