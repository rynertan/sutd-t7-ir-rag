# Integrated circuit, particularly for use as a digital analog converter.

## Abstract
A semiconductor circuit having an improved current switching function is disclosed. The circuit 20 comprises at least one current switch including a current source, a current output node 47 , a field effect transistor 21 connected between the current output node 47 and the current source 2 , an inverting amplifier 30 having an output 31 supplied to a gate 23 of the field effect transistor 21 and an input 32 connected to the junction point 44 of the current source 2 and the field effect transistor 21 and means 46 for controlling operation of the amplifier 30 .
