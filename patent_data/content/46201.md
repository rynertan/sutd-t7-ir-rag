# Antibiotic A 16686 factor A2, the process for the preparation thereof, and the co produced antibiotic A 16686 factors A1 and A3.

## Abstract
The present invention refers to a chlorine containing antibiotic substance, named antibiotic A 16686 factor A₂, in an essentially pure form. The invention also relates to the process for the pro duction of antibiotic A 16686 factors A₂ by cultivation of an Actinoplanes strain and to the co produced antibiotic A 16686 factors A₁ and A₃. Antibiotic A 16686 factors A₁, A₂ and A₃ as well as the corresponding non toxic physiologically acceptable acid addition salts are antimicrobial agents particularly active against gram positive bacteria.
