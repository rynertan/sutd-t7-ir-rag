# Screen printing device.

## Abstract
Screen printing device including a work table 1 having a printing position 2 , means 4 on this work table for holding a substrate 3 in this printing position, a holder for a screen provided with a mask, a microscope, a template 70 means 4 for adjusting the relative position of the screen holder, the microscope and the work table having holes 14 10 with associated illumination means 23 24 for simul taneously illuminating corresponding reference portions 75 78 47 50 of the mask and the template.
