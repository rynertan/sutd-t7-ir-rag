# Rainproof jacket.

## Abstract
Rainproof jacket 1 , particularly for riders, including a back panel having a ventilation window 8 which runs over its major portion. A rain protection flap 9 fully covers this ventila tion opening 8 . The upper edge 10 of the flap 9 is secured to the upper end of the back panel while the lower edge 11 of the flap 9 is releasably secured to the lower end of the back panel. The lateral edges 12 of the flap 9 are unattached, that is they remain free of the back panel and of the ventilation win dow 8 so that air is allowed to freely circulate between the ventilation window 8 and the rain protection flap 9 .
