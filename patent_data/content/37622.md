# Drug delivery insert for controlled ocular therapy.

## Abstract
An ocular insert is disclosed comprising a beta adrenergic blocking osmotic drug solute and a parasympthomimetic osmotic drug solute dispersed in a polymer with the solutes surrounded by the polymer. The system, when placed in the eye, delivers both drugs at a controlled rate over time. A method also is disclosed for the management of intraocular pressure using the ocular system.
