# Preparation of 2,2,4 trimethyl 1,2 dihydroquinoline.

## Abstract
The invention provides a process for producing 2,2,4 trimethyl 1,2 dihydroquinoline by the reaction of aniline with at least one member selected from acetone, diacetone alcohol and mesityl oxide, said reaction being carried out in the presence of a catalyst comprising hydrogen fluoride and boron trifluoride.
