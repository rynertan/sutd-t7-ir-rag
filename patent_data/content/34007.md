# Boot upper quarter moulding machine.

## Abstract
A machine for moulding the quarter of a boot upper by causing a female mould 36 to press the quarter against a male mould 74 . The male mould 74 is mounted bottom up by a stem 76 connected by the front portion 72 of a beam 70 to the machine frame 14 . The beam front portion 72 has a cavity 84 located above the rearmost end 82 of the male mould 74 .
