# LOCKING DEVICE, PARTICULARLY FOR CABLE ASSEMBLIES

## Abstract
A locking device 30 for attachment to an elongate member 28 to secure the latter to a fixed support 18 in an adjustable axial position The locking device according to the invention comprises a first part 32 slidably carried on the elongate member but prevented from rotating relative thereto, and a second part 34 which, in a first angular position, is axially movable together with the first part along the elongate member, and in a second angular position meshes with teeth formed on said member to secure said second part in an axial, selected position For use particularly in transmission and clutch cable assemblies.
