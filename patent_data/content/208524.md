# Catalyst component for olefin polymerization.

## Abstract
A titanium containing catalyst component for olefin poly merizations which comprises contacting a a metal oxide, b a dihydrocarbyl magnesium, and c a hydrocarbyloxy group containing compound with one another, contacting the thus obtained contact product with d a halogen containing alcohol, and finally contacting the thus obtained contact pro duct with e an electron donor compound and f a titanium compound.
