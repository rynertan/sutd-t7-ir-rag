# Processes for making can end stock from roll cast aluminium and product.

## Abstract
An aluminum container scrap alloy is processed by a modified chill roll cast process into a highly formable sheet material suitable for use as container end stock, by em ploying at least a 60 cold reduction followed by an anneal for about two hours at a temperature of from about 825 F. to about 900 F., followed by cold reduction to final gauge.
