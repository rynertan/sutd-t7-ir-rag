# Ultrafine particle spraying apparatus.

## Abstract
An ultrafine particle spraying apparatus of the type that innumerable ultrafine particles are sent under pressure by a carrier gas through a conveying pipe 1 and are sprayed onto a predetermined objective surface C through a forward end nozzle means 3 so that there may be formed on the surface C a predetermined adhesion deposit B of the ultrafine particles stick uses a bundle shaped nozzle means 3 comprising at least two nozzle pipes 2 bundled together.
