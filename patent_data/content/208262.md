# COMPACT KNIFE UNIT FOR SLITTING A WEB

## Abstract
A slim knife unit includes a guide member 7 having an L shaped body which supports an elongated fluid actuator 12 comprising a piston 11 connected by piston rods 10 to a base member 9 having a sliding track connection to a re movable knife holder 3 . The guide member 7 has a linear track 35 which coop erates with the rods 10 to guide the knife holder 3 , and a reasable latch 23, 24 secures the knife holder 3 to the base member 9 .
