# Mineral oil substitute for toiletries and cosmetics.

## Abstract
An ester blend, useful in cosmetic products, comprises tridecyl trimellitate TDTM blended with at least one additional ester. Additional esters include tridecyl stearate TDS , ne opentylglycol dicaprylate dicaprate NPGC and dipen taerythritol hexacaprylate hexacaprate DPHC .
