# An emi shielded electrical connector and method of making same.

## Abstract
The invention is an EMI shielded electrical connector characterized by a molded plastic housing 20 that has embedded therein an electrically conductive wire mesh 10 . The wire mesh 10 includes an electrically exposed internal shoulder 11 and ends 12 and 13 that may be connected to other electrically conductive members to ground out un wanted electromagnetic interference.
