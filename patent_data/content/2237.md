# Solid liquid separation apparatus.

## Abstract
A solid liquid separation element 2, 12 consisting of a stationary, inclined screen 12 , having on its back surface guide members 2 for accelerating the through flow of the liquid and, optionally, guide members 11 or transporting solid materials arranged on the surface of the screen 12 in substantially parallel and downward inclining direction from both edges of the screen 12 . The solid liquid separation apparatus having said separation element and a cleaning brush 20 which is reciprocated in rubbing contact with the back surface of the screen 19 is also provided. The range of the reciprocating motion of the brush is more than the width of the screen.
