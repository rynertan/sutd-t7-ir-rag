# Electronic musical instrument with performance mode selection.

## Abstract
This electronic musical instrument comprises means 13 for selecting one of a plurality of performance modes and musical tone production means 24, 51 having tone production channels less than the number of keys. Tones are produced through said channels based on the time division multiplex key data which are set according to the depressed status of all keys in the different manner ac cording to the selected performance mode of the mode selecting means 13 .
