# Device for converting a rotary motion into a linear motion.

## Abstract
The improvement consists therein that the housing 1 of the arrangement comprises several detacheable units 3 each provided with a ball bearing 4 which co operates with the shaft 13 . Eech unit 3 being set under a spring adjusting means 14 which influences the cooperation between the ball bearing 4 and shaft 13 . Depending upon its application housing 1 of the arrangement can be provided with the most effective number of units 3 . Also mal functioning units 3 can easily be replased by dismounting one of the covers 12 .
