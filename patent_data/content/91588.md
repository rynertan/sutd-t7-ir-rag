# SWIVEL ATTACHMENT FOR AUTOVEHICLE ADJUSTABLE SUN VISORS

## Abstract
The attachment comprises a body of polymeric material wherein a through socket 13 is formed rotatively holding one end of the visor suspension rod 11 , which end is formed with a cranked portion 11a engaged by two opposite cheeks 10a, 10b of the supporting body, extending through an arc at least as wide as the adjustment angle of the suspension rod 11 , and effective to limit the angular travel range of the visor suspension rod 11 cranked portion 11a .
