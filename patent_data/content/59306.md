# WALKING BEAM FURNACE

## Abstract
The walking beam furnace for heating metallurgical articles such as billets, at its discharge end, downstream of the zone occupied by the walking beams, comprises a storage zone 17 formed in part by an uninterrupted sole for the heated metallurgical articles B , and in this heated storage zone 17 said articles are laid down and are advanced by the pushing action of the walking beams 2 , so called pushing beams, which are provided for this purpose with at least one pushing head 23 , suitable means being provided to discharge one or more metallurgical articles at a time from said storage zone 17 .
