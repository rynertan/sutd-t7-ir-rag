# Starter dynamo.

## Abstract
A starter dynamo comprises a d.c. motor 19 having a hollow armature rotary shaft 24 , a rotary output shaft 18 passing through the hollow armature rotary shaft and connected to the armature rotary shaft through a one way clutch 26 , a transmission mechanism 17 provided at one end of the rotary output shaft to keep connection of the rotary output shaft to a crank shaft of an engine for revolution, and a generator provided with a flywheel magnet 101 disposed at the other end of the rotary output shaft 18 .
