# Cacao butter substitute composition.

## Abstract
A cacao butter substitute composition comprises a 80 wt. or lager of 1,3 disaturated 2 oleoyl glycerol comprising a 1 10 wt. or smaller of 1,3 dipalmitoyl 2 oleoyl glycerol, a 2 25 to 45 wt. of 1 palmitoyl 2 oleoyl 3 stearoyl glycerol and a 3 45 to 70 wt. of 1,3 distearoyl 2 oleoyl glycerol, containing less than 20 wt. of other triglycerides.
