# Ultrasonic sector scanner.

## Abstract
The ultrasonic sector scanner has a cover assembly in which a flexible membrane 64, 66 is provided with ribs 68 which engage grooves 62 in the wall of the housing 58 . A heat shrink tube 70 or other retaining ring is arranged around the flexible membrane 66 in order to press the ribs 68 into the grooves 62 . The ultrasonic sector scanner includes a rigid nose piece 60 inside and spaced from the flexible membrane 64 in order to increase the skin contact area when the flex ible membrane of the sector scanner is pressed against the skin of a patient being examined.
