# Heat shrinkable laminate tubular film and process for producing it.

## Abstract
Heat shrinkable laminate tubular film comprising a gas barrier layer of a copolymer of vinylidene chloride of thickness not less than 6 Âµm and not more than 30 of the total thickness of the film, outer layers of polyolefin, at least one intermediate layer of a polyamide or a thermoplastic polyester both having a crystal melting point of not more than 240 C and have a thickness of 5 to 40 of the total thickness of the film, and adhesive layers disposed between any of the above layers.
