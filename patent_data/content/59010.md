# Electrostatic copying machine provided with a photoconductive belt and with means for maintaining said belt flat in a processing zone.

## Abstract
Electrostatic cooying machine provided with a moving photoconductive belt 1 which is tensioned over two guide members 2, 3 and with a flat plate 4 which has a closed sur face and is disposed parallel to and at a short distance from the tensioned belt segment and which serves for suppressing vibrations in said belt segment.
