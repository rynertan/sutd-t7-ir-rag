# Medical solution containers and method of making them.

## Abstract
A medical container with attached tubing set, for use in the administration of fluids to a patient, comprises a reservoir in fluid communication with the tubing set and an enclosure integrally associated with the reservoir and adapted to coilably receive the tubing set when the container is not in use. The container further includes a seal over an opening to the enclosure which is removable to extend the tubing set, and provide a fluid path therethrough, from the reservoir to the patient during administration of the fluid.
