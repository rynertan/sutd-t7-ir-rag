# A SPACER FOR A CONCRETE SHUTTERING

## Abstract
A cone as part of a spacer for concrete shutterings, which cone comprises a metal bush provided with an essen tially continuous axial hole wherein a nut thread adapted to an associated screwed rod is formed. There is provided a ring shaped or cap shaped supporting element which rests against a cam shaped portion formed on the bush end di rected towards the shuttering and which extends to beyond said bush end. There may be provided a resilient member positioned between the cap shaped portion and the sup porting element for biasing the supporting element towards the shuttering, while around the metal bush there may be provided a sleeve of concrete or the like mortar, hard syn thetic plastics material or such like material.
