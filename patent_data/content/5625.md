# Apparatus for locating therein a pipe union.

## Abstract
This invention relates to apparatus for locating therein a pipe union. The apparatus comprises a housing adapted to be secured to a building structure such as a wall, the housing having a chamber adapted to receive the union, and means removably secured thereto so that the union is secured in the housing by the said means co operating with the housing. The invention is particularly adapted to be secured to a building structure for securing a pipe union adjacent the structure, the union being adapted to be connected to a conduit, such as a pipe, for supplying services in the building.
