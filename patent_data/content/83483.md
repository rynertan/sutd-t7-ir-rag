# Ultrapurificated factor VIII C preparation.

## Abstract
A method of preparing high purity procoagulant pro tein comprising the steps of a adsorbing a VIII C VIII RP complex from a plasma or commercial concentrate source of factor VIII onto agarose beads bound to a monoclonal antibody spoecific to VIII RP, b eluting VIII C with a salt solution, c adsorbing the eluted VIII C on a aminohexyl agarose column and eluting the VIII C with a salt solution.
