# Joystick with built in fire button.

## Abstract
A joystick apparatus includes a tiltably mounted tube 25 and a rod 40 reciprocally movably axially there through. The tube has a frustoconical first actuator 31 at one end thereof for operating engagement with a selected one or more of a plurality of first switches 32 as the tube is tilted. The adjacent end of the rod carries a part spherical second actuator 50 for operating engagement with a se cond switch 37 when the rod is extended from the tube, the shape of the second actuator keeping it in proper operating relationship with respect to the second switch regardless of the position of the tube.
