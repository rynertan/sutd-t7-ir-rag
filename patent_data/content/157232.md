# Hoist having worm safety device.

## Abstract
A hoist having a worm safety device in which the worm 30 is continuously meshed with a worm wheel 26 coupled to the drum 10 . The worm 30 is accelerated to a speed greater than that of the worm wheel 26 and at a torque sufficient to overcome the frictional contact between the worm 30 and worm wheel 26 when the hoist direction is reversed.
