# Laser discharge tube.

## Abstract
A laser discharge tube comprises a discharge envelope 8 and an anode 20 and a cathode 26 both disposed within the envelope 8 . The envelope 8 includes a capillary tube 10 having a hole 36 . In this hole 36 the tip portion of the anode 20 is inserted. Outside the envelope 8 there is pro vided a magnet 56 generating a magnetic field which lies in the vicinity of the anode 20 .
