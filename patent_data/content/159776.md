# Bladder assist device.

## Abstract
There is provided a bladder assist device for being capable of maintaining natural drainage by a repetition of repletion and contraction of the bladder 1 even when a urethral catheter 2 is embedded into the bladder 1 , in which a pressure control hole 14 selaed by a filter 15 having air permeation restrained property is equipped with a pressure control chamber 3 con taining a concentric type siphon tube 10 connected to a urethral catheter 2 at a particular part.
