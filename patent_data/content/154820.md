# Magnetically attachable sheet.

## Abstract
A flexible magnetic sign 10 , such as may be detachably provided on a body panel of a motor vehicle, in which bulging due to the presence of air or moisture is eliminated. The rear surface of the sign is formed with indentations 13 1 . This may take the form of parallel stripes, a checkered pattern, indenta tions having a random orientation, or a textured surface.
