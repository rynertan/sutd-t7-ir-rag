# Optical recording element.

## Abstract
An element comprising an assembly of two parallel sub strate plates 9, 10 made from a transparent synthetic resin and each bearing on their facing sides a recording layer 11, 12 which comprises a guide track in the form of a spiral like groove, and a foil 19 of synthetic resin which with both major surfaces engages or substantially engages the ridges 15, 16 of the recording layers present between the groove turns.
