# Modular orifice meter with isolation chamber valve closure.

## Abstract
A modular orifice meter construction comprising a main housing 12 wherein an orifice disc carrier 24 is positioned to seal around axially aligned flow tubes 14, 16 . A service housing 38 with an access opening for replacement of orifice discs 26 may be bolted into the main housing 12 . A closure 52 for said access opening is pivoted to the service housing 38 and is normally held closed by a wedging clamp lock 64 . A pinion 72 rotatably mounted on the service housing 38 raises and lowers the orifice disc carrier 24 by engagement with a rack 70 on the carrier 24 . An isolating gate valve 80 may be mounted between the main 12 and servie 38 housings.
