# METHOD AND MACHINE FOR PACKING ELONGATE ARTICLES INTO BOXES OR TRAYS

## Abstract
Elongate articles such as plastics bottles 2 are packed into a box or tray 1 by arranging the articles 2 side by side in a stack of superimposed horizontal rows and then displacing horizontally from the stack, and in the direction of their horizontal parallel axes, an array of the superimposed articles into the box or tray arranged on its side, the box or tray being then tipped onto its base.
