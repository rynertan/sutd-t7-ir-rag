# Improved polyester block copolymer composition.

## Abstract
An improved polyester block copolymer composition having a rubber like elasticity, particularly having excellent moldability, impact resistance, heat resistance and water resistance, which comprises a polyester block copolymer produced from a crystalline aromatic polyester and a lactone in melt admixture with a polytetramethylene terephthalate and optionally further with at least one epoxy compound.
