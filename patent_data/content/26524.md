# Apparatus for recording and or reproducing signals in a plurality of parallel longitudinal tracks on a magnetic tape.

## Abstract
The magnetic head 4 can be positioned relative to the magnetic tape by a positioning device 3 which comprises two positioning systems 9 15 which are provided with piezo electric elements. By means of a first positioning system 15 the magnetic head is movable in the width direction of the magnetic tape, whilst the magnetic head is pivotable by means of a second positioning system 9 .
