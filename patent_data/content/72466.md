# Web tracking apparatus.

## Abstract
Disclosed herein is apparatus for maintaining a moving endless web in alignment with a desired transport path. Such apparatus preferably includes a pair of web supporting rollers 12, 14 , drive means 22 for advancing the web along an endless path over such rollers, tension adjusting means 42, 50, 62, 70 for periodically reducing the tension in said web, and web positioning means 32, 34 for laterally adjusting the position of the web relative to at least one of such rollers during those intervals in which the web tension is reduced.
