# Apparatus and process for the aeration of a high solids concentration pseudoplastic liquid solid mixture.

## Abstract
A method for aeration of pseudoplastic mixtures by providing an enclosure with H D of 0.5 5.0, a vertical rotatable shaft 24 having bubble shearing means 35 with extending arms 37 , upwardly injecting gas bubbles at radial locations, and repeatedly shearing the rising bubbles by the rotating arms 37 . The method has par ticular application to the aeration of wastewater sludge.
