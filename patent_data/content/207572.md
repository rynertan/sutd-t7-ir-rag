# An implement for milking animals.

## Abstract
An implement for milking animals 1 , such as cows, comprising at least a milking parlour and a milking machine 14 and being provided with at least one teat cup 15 con nectable to a teat 17 of the animal. Thw teat cup 15 is provided with a cleaning device 16 which is positioned sub stantially symmetrically around the centreline of the teat cup 15 . Cleaning liquid or air may be supplied to the cleaning device through the milk tube. The teat cup 15 and the clean ing device 16 may be movable with respect to each other, so that the cleaning operation and the milking operation can be performed subsequently. The cleaning device 16 may com prise a rotary brush 23 .
