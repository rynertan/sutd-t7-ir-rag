# Liquid filled elastomeric bushings.

## Abstract
A liquid filled elastomeric bushing is disclosed, which comprises an inner sleeve 1 , an outer sleeve 2 , an elastomeric member 3 disposed between the inner and outer sleeves 1, 2 and provided at its axially middle portion with liquid chambers 5 , intermediate rings 4 interposed between the elastomeric member 3 and the outer sleeve 2 and bonded to both end portions of the elastomeric member 3 , and a restricted passage 9 establishing communication between the liquid chambers 5 .
