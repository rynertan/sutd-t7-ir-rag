# Stretchable cling film composition based on polyethylene.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a composition suitable for the manufacture of cling film, comprising 80 to 99 by weight of at least one polymer or copolymer of ethylene having a density less than or equal to 0.935, and from 1 to 20 by weight of the total composition comprising a A cling agent chosen from amongst liquid poly butenes and atactic polypropylenes and b At least one crystalline wax in a quantity of 0.01 and 50 by weight based on the weight of a and b together.
