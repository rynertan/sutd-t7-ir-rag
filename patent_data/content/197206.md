# ARTICLE FOR THE PRESENTATION OF A PASTY COSMETIC PRODUCTARTICLE FOR THE PRESENTATION OF A PASTY COSMETIC PRODUCT AND THE RELATED PRODUCTION PROCESS AND THE RELATED PRODUCTION PROCESS

## Abstract
On a supporting stratum 1 are impressed depictions 2 formed with a pasty cosmetic product. For the production of said product a process is used which calls for preparation of a dry part and a fatty part appropriately mixed together and with a solvent in such a manner as to create a pasty cosmetic product of suitable density for application to and subsequent retention on the supporting stratum.
