# Image reading apparatus.

## Abstract
An image reading apparatus such as a copier or facsimile machine for reading an image on a document, comprises a document placing plate 5 for placing thereon the document 1 and movable reciprocatively a document placing plate driving mechanism 6 for driving the docu ment placing plate 5 and an image reader 20 for reading the image information on the document. Preferably, a document holding member 8 is provided for ensuring the document to be brought into press contact with the docu ment placing plate 5 . The document placing plate 5 is usually kept in a first position where the document placing plate is housed in the image read apparatus and drawn out therefrom to reach a second position where the document can be set. The image reading apparatus sequentially reads the image information on the document while moving the document placing plate from the second position to the first position.
