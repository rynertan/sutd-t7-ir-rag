# DUAL CHANNEL AMBULATORY MONITORING UNIT AND SYSTEM

## Abstract
A heart analyzing system for an ambulatory patient including a fixed base procedure control system and a dockable programmable dual channel ambulatory satellite monitoring unit 10 connectable to an ambulatory patient and connectable to the procedure control unit 30 . The ambulatory monitoring unit includes a microprocessor with QRS classification criteria capabilities for analysis and classi fication of QRS events and including ST level analysis capabilities to provide ST level. The dual channel ambulatory monitoring unit also includes heart pacer spike detecting and control circuits for use of the ambulatory monitoring unit on heart pacer patients.
