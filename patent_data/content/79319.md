# A pneumatically powered rotation tool.

## Abstract
A pneumatic rotating grinding tool in which the motive air supply to the motor 12 is controlled by a speed governor as well as a releasable overspeed safety valve 51 . The speed governor comprises a fly weight operated valve sleeve 41 arranged to control the air flow through an inlet opening 30 in an air flow barrier means 23, 28 provided by parts of the rear end wall 22 of the motor 12 . These parts include an annular steel washer 28 which is located transverse to and coaxial with the rotation axis of the motor rotor 20 , and which forms said governor controlled air inlet opening 30 .
