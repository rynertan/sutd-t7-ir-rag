# Dental anchoring means.

## Abstract
Dental anchoring means includes a cylindrical shank 13 having a turned over portion at one end and an integral threaded pin 10 at the other end which is detachable from the shank. The anchoring means locates in holding means in the form of a sleeve 20 with a longitudinal opening. The shank extends through the opening and is held for rotation with the sleeve by a stop member 23 at one end of the sleeve which engages the turned over portion. The sleeve is located as a gear wheel in a dental hand piece which may be a self contained unit.
