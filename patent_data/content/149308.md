# Effervescent denture cleansing composition.

## Abstract
There is disclosed a denture cleansing composition wherein the bleaching agent is 1 a monoperphthalate, preferably the magnesium salt of monoperphthalic acid MPPA or 2 a monopersulphate, preferably a potassium monopersulphate, and a monoperphthalate. The denture cleansing composition possesses good stability.
