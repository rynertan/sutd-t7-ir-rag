# Light source device.

## Abstract
A light source device comprises a light source lamp 1 detachably fitted to a light source lamp rest 9 mount ed on a housing 4 , and a reflection mirror retracting me chanism 12 comprising a swing member 42 on which a reflection mirror 28 is carried, and which swings between a first position in which the swing member 42 causes the reflection mirror 28 to face one end of the light source lamp 1 and a second position in which the swing member 42 retracts the reflection mirror 28 from one end of the light source lamp 2 , and fixing means 43 for locking the swing member 42 in the first position. The arrangement allows for the fitting, removal or replacement of the light source lamp 1 without releasing the reflection mirror 28 from its place each time.
