# Apparatus for a dairy barn.

## Abstract
This invention is dairy barn apparatus including a plat form 11 with a number of milking stalls 21 and a floor 12 which can be raised and lowered adjacent the edge of the plaform 11 , so that in the raised position a cow can enter the barn and move over the floor and into a stall on the platform 11 . In the lowered position, the operator can enter a nook 14 in the edge of the platform where he can conveniently connect the milking apparatus which is in a flue 23 below the platform, but above ground level forward of the rear feet of the cow.
