# System for adjusting the position of a steering wheel on a vehicle.

## Abstract
The system comprises a movable support holding the steering wheel and comprised of a four link articulated quadrilateral mechanism having a base link 1 , two crank links 4, 5 , one link 4 being adjustable longitudinally and the other link 5 being slope adjustable and a rod link 6 hinged to said crank links and supporting the steering wheel 13 .
