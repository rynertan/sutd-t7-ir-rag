# PLANT GROWTH REGULATOR COMPOSITIONS AND METHOD

## Abstract
A turfgrass growth regulator composition is described, which comprises about 0.05 to about 1.0 part by weight of mefluidide and about 0.05 to about 5.0 parts by weight of α 1 methylethyl α 4 trifluoromethoxy phenyl 5 pyrimidinemethanol, or suitable salts thereof. A method for regulating the growth of such vegetation is also described.
