# Hydrated magnesium nitrate magnesium chloride reversible phase change compositions and their preparation.

## Abstract
A reversible liquid solid phase change composition comprising hydrated Mg NO₃ ₂ MgCl₂, and one or more of MgO, Mg OH ₂, CoSO₄, CuSO₄, MgSO₄, NiSO₄, CaK₂ SO₄ ₂, NaBO₂, added to the composition in an amount sufficient to suppress supercooling of the Mg NO₃ ₂ MgCl₂ liquid phase.
