# PROCESS FOR RECOVERING WASTE PRODUCTS OF GLASS FIBRES, ROCK WOOL, AND POLYURETHANE HARD FOAM END PRODUCTS OBTAINED THROUGH THIS PROCESS

## Abstract
Process for recovering waste products of glass fibres, rock wool and polyurethane hard foam, characterized in that it mainly consists in grinding waste products of glass fibres and or rock wool and or polyurethane hard foam spraying and mixing the so ground waste products with an isocyanate or phenolic resin and then compressing and heating the impregnated waste in order to obtain a plate with a specific weight comprised between approximately 300 and approximately 800 kg m.
