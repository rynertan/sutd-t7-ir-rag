# Magnetic head.

## Abstract
A magnetic head 30 comprising a head body 31 pro vided at its outer peripheral surface with a plurality of annular grooves 32 , a plurality of magnetic elements 14 connected to the corresponding peripheral surface portions 33 positioned between the adjacent grooves, one or more magnetic exciting means, and means for selectively connect ing any pair of the two adjacent magnetic elements to the magnetic exciting means. The head body 31 of the magnetic head may comprise nonmagnetic body on which a plurality of magnetic areas disposed so that on the outer surface of the head body the magnetic areas are positioned alternately.
