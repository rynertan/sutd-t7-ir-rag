# Water treatment apparatus.

## Abstract
A waste water treatment apparatus 10 having a tank 11 through which waste water flows, a rotary biological contactor 17 in the tank having an annular plastic media 19 fixed on a buoyant drum or tubular shaft providing buoyancy which maintains immersion to the topmost peripheral portion of the drum, mechanical bearings 25, 32 to stabilize the contactor in the water and a drive 35 connected to rotate the contactor.
