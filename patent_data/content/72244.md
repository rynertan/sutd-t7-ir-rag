# Drilling mud.

## Abstract
This invention concerns an oil based drilling mud com prising an organic liquid such as oil water an emulsifier a wetting agent a weighting material of sufficient quantity to achieve a density of 7 to 20 lb gallon and a neutralized sulphonated elastomer, said neutralized sulphonated poly mer having 5 to 30 meq. of sulphonated groups per 100 grams of the neutralized sulphonated polymer.
