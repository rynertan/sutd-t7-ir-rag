# A method of manufacturing braided cord, twine or twisted ropes and of manufacturing a netting of said materials, in particular a net destined for fishing purposes.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a method of manufacturing a braided cord, twine or twisted rope 1 , in particular a netting 5,6,7 of said materials such as a fishing net, said cord, twine or rope having a core 3,4 with or of thermoplastic material which after being softened is allowed to penetrate from the interior into the openings between the threads or filaments 2 of the cord twine or rope 1 , said penetration being per formed in that the cord etc. is subjected to a shrinkage treat ment whilst held under tension in the case of a knotted netting has to be from all sides.
