# Process for preparing carbonated beverages using coated charcoal and process for preparing the coated charcoal.

## Abstract
Carbonated aqueous liquid can be prepared at ambient pressure with crystalline zeolite or activated charcoals containing adsorbed carbon dioxide and a coating of a small organic compound such as a di or tri carboxylic acid, a C₁₀ C₁₈ fatty acid or a monocyclic terpene or related compound.
