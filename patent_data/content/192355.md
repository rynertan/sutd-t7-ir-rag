# Kitchen utensil.

## Abstract
A kitchen utensil for producing juice includes a rotary shaft 6 rotated by a motor 2 , a pre treatment rotary blade 7 secured to the end of the rotary shaft 6 which projects into a vessel 4 , a first grinder member 8 secured to the rotary shaft below the rotary blade in the vessel and a second grinder member 9 secured to a bottom plate 5 of the vessel so as to oppose the first grinder member. Fruit and or vegetables put into the vessel 4 together with a predeter mined amount of water is finely cut into pieces by the pre treatment rotary blade 7 and thereafter introduced into between the first and second grinding members 8 and 9 to be mashed and ground more finely, whereby an emulsified juice is obtained.
