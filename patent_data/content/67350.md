# All position tractor.

## Abstract
A tractor assembly for mounting on a track fixed to a workpiece the tractor used to support and guide a metal working tool along a fixed path, the tractor having three wheels 50, 52, 54 to engage opposite edges of the track 34 , one of said wheels being laterally displaceable to facilitate mounting of the tractor to the track 34 . Moreover the disclosure includes rest buttons 68, 70, 71 or supports to facilitate alignment of the tractor and additional roller supports engageable to said track if needed.
