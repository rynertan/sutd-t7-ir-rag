# Gas detecting element.

## Abstract
A gas detecting element 10 comprises a substrate 1 , a gas sensitive body 3 provided on the substrate, a pair of elec trodes 2 connected to the gas sensitive body 3 and a cata lyst layer 4 provided on the surface of the gas sensitive body 3 . The catalyst layer 4 includes a carrier material and a cata lyst material of copper and, optionally, tungsten. The gas detecting element 10 in accordance with the present inven tion has substantially equal sensitivity to a number of different gases.
