# Apparatus for supplying food to a plurality of cages.

## Abstract
A food supplying system for large cage batteries 22 , in which horizontal gutters 23,36 with food transport means 27,37 are supplied at intervals by means of vertical supply pipes 12 , which at their upper sides are connected to a supply conduit 11 preferably connected to a basic supply and return circuit 4,1,2,3,5,6,9,10 .
