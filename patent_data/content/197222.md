# Improvements in lollipops and their manufacture.

## Abstract
A process for the production of lollipops including forming two separate lollipop parts 52 and 53 from sugar paste 50 and 51 , inserting at least one sweet 47 to be enclosed in the lollipop between these parts 52 and 53 and joining together the above mentioned parts 52 and 53 so that they completely encapsulate the sweet or sweets 47 to be enclosed.
