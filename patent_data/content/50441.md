# Anhydrous battery or cell.

## Abstract
An anhydrous battery or cell comprises an anode, a cath ode, an electrolyte and an anhydrous solvent, the cathode comprising a mixture of p doped polyacetylene in admixture with 5 75 by weight of an electrically conductive material e.g. graphite or carbon black. Electrolytes include hexafluorophos phate, perchlorate etc. and suitable solvents are propylene carbonate, sulfolane etc.
