# Gas distribution plate for fluidisation.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a fluidised bed apparatus 1 comprising a fluidisation grid 2 arranged in the lower part of this apparatus, this grid being provided at its centre with a circular aperture 5 communicating with a discharge cham ber 9 and occurring in the form of a surface of revolution consisting of the joined lateral surfaces of at least two coaxial truncated cones of revolution TC₁, TC₂ , virtual vertices of which are oriented downwards.
