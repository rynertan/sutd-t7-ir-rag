# Cooling articles of newly moulded glassware.

## Abstract
A newly moulded article of glassware 12 is positioned on a dead plate 10 with a contral opening of the dead plate beneath a central region of the bottom of the article and a plurality of grooves 20 or openings extending from beyond the article to beneath the bottom thereof. Air is sucked through the central opening so that it flows through the grooves or openings past the bottom of the article. Air is also blown on the sidewalls of the article through a plurality of nozzles 26 uniformly spaced around the article.
