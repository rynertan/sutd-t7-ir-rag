# Tape drive device.

## Abstract
A tape drive device 44 is disclosed for driving a reel hub rotatably mounted within a tape housing of a cartridge type tape. The tape drive device is provided with a base member 10 , a spindle shaft assembly 30 and a resilient joint member 24 interposed between the base member and the spindle shaft assembly. The base member is adapted for rotation about an axis thereof by a driving source. The spindle shaft assembly is concentric with the base member and is adapted for being engaged with the reel hub and the resilient joint member couples the spindle shaft assembly to the base member.
