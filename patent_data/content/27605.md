# Light source device for an endoscope.

## Abstract
A photoreceptor 31 is provided for receiving a light emitted from an electronic flash unit 20 and an output of the photoreceptor 31 is supplied to a light emission amount calculating circuit. The light emission amount calculating cir cuit includes a light emission amount calculating constant setting device 34, 35a to 35f adapted to manually change a light emission amount calculating constant according to a photographing mode.
