# Parasol assembly.

## Abstract
A parasol suitable for sheltering a car or for providing shade during an outing. When used to provide shade for a car, the inventive parasol is unfolded and installed on a mounting bracket which comprises a socket tube, a spring and a hook, and is mounted on the top of the car. When the parasol is used apart from the car, a connecting tube is provided to support the parasol. As the parasol is unfolded and the lower end of the connecting tube is planted in the ground, the parasol acts as a sunshade.
