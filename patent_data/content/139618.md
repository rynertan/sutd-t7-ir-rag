# Method of producing common wood veneers whose surface imitates that of prized woods, and veneer obtained by said method.

## Abstract
The method according to the invention makes it possible to produce veneers made of common wood whose surface imitates that of prized woods. The method comprises the step of applying by means of a hot press on the surface of a veneer of common wood 3 a hot stamping foil 4 reproducing the appearance of the prized wood it is desire to imitate. The veneer obtained by means of said method has on at least one surface thereof the appearance of the prized wood it is desired to imitate.
