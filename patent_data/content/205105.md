# Vane core assembly for making centrifugal elastomer elastomeric impellers.

## Abstract
An elastomeric covered shrouded impeller, having a pair of disk like shrouds 16, 17 enclosing vanes and vane pas sages, which has an improved throat opening 10 having sweeping internal sidewalls is disclosed. A three piece mold core A,B,C is utilized to form the vane passage and the sweeping curved throat inlet of the impeller.
