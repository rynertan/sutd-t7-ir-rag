# Method of making polyamide imide foams.

## Abstract
A method of making a flexible, flame resistant polyamide imide foam comprises reacting an aromatic N substituted imidocarboxylic acid monoanhydride or an aromatic N,N min disubstituted bisimidodicarboxylic acid component A with an organic diisocyanate component B in the presence of a tertiary amine catalyst C and water to form a polyamide imide foam.
