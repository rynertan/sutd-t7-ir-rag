# Pharmaceutical Formulations comprising 1 substituted imidazoles, 1 substituted imidazoles and 1 substituted imidazoles for use in the treatment or prophylaxis of thrombo embolic disorders.

## Abstract
The invention relates to the use in medicine of 1 alkylimidazoles of formula The 1 alkylimidazoles in which n is 0 and R ist cycloalkyl or cycloalkenyl are novel, as are those when A and R together form an alkenyl radical. Methods of preparing the 1 alkylimidazoles are disclosed.
