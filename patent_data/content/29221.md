# Magnetic recording tape cassette.

## Abstract
In a magnetic recording tape cassette comprising a bottom case half 1a , a top case half 1b , the case halves are combined together and fastened by tapping screws 11 and a lid 10 member hingedly supported on the top case half so as to close the openings in the front side of the cassette case to protect a magnetic recording tape, each of the tapping screws are adapted to be entered through the top case half. Openings 18a, 18b are defined in the horizontal plate 10a of the lid corresponding to the through holes of the top case half.
