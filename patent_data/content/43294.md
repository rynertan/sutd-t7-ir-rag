# A method of and apparatus for embossing.

## Abstract
A method and apparatus for embossing characters on a plate such as a motor vehicle registration plate are provided according to which the blank plate 28 and dies 27 are located between the movable and stationary members of a press, the movable member 3 being contained in a cylinder 1 wherein pressurised fluid can cause it in the nature of a piston to move towards the stationary member 10 in order to effect the embossing operation on the plate 28 .
