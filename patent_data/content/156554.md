# Frangible fittings.

## Abstract
A frangible fluid fitting of the crash worthy type utilizing a detent holding a self closing valve in the open position. The detent engages a plurality of balls confined in a recess circumscribing the fitting attachment surface whereby only partial fracture of the fitting in any direction causes the self sealing valve to close. One embodiment of the fitting permits both gravity and pressurized tank filling with auto matic valve closing when the tank is full during pressurized filling.
