# SYSTEM FOR AUTOMATING BEAUTY TREATMENT.

## Abstract
A system for automating devices and tools for beauty treatments such as permanent depilation, facial treatment, skin treatment, weight reduction, and so forth, with measur ing devices 1,2,3,4,5,6,7 for measuring various conditions of the part of the body being subjected to the beauty treatments, e.g., skin moisture, temperature, pH, etc. Outputs obtained from these measuring devices are arithmetically processed by operational control sections 11,12,13,14 so that the oper ational conditions of beauty treatment devices 22,23,24,25 , e.g., the output intensities and the on off operations of the devices, can be controlled by the results of such arithmetic operations.
