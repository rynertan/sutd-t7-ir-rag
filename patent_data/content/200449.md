# ELECTRICAL CONNECTORS

## Abstract
An edge mounted multi contact printed circuit board connector has a body rebated to the edge of a custaway 2 of a printed circuit board 1 , the body carrying spaced strip like resilient contact elements 4 having outward portions 6 which engage respective surface contacts 7 of the circuit board and inward portions 12 which are curved to extend into a cavity 2 of the body for resilient engagement with contacts 11 of a cable connector 10 when inserted therein.
