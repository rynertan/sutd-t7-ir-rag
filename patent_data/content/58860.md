# Balancing machine for bodies of rotation.

## Abstract
The balancing machine comprises a rotation shaft 2 whereon the body 1 to be balanced can be mounted, a supporting structure 3 for the shaft 2 , and a carrying member for the supporting structure 3 , in the form of a resiliently flexible plate 4 . Detector means are provided for detecting the vibration generated by the unbalance of the body 1 to be balanced and tranferred thereto through the resilient plate 4 , and means 6, 7 for imparting rotation to the shaft.
