# Elevated airport runway, taxiway or threshold edge light with sealed glass dome.

## Abstract
A ball joint 27 centrally positioned in the bottom wall of a substantially hollow base 26 is adapted to receive an up standing conduit 16 . A cover comprises a lower annular por tion 12 and a hollow glass dome 11 including a prism por tion 25 sealed in the lower annular portion, said lower annular portion having an inner base portion 31 resting on the upper portion 32 of the base, whereby the apex of said hollow glass dome 11 is positioned a predetermined distance from said inner base portion 31 and the bottom wall of the base 26 , so that the prism portion 25 is in proper registration with respect to a light source 22 centrally supported on the base.
