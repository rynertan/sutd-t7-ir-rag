# Rolling mill stand.

## Abstract
A rod, bar or section mill has a train of rolling mill stands 12 , each of which is attached to a bed plate 13 with which the stand is introduced into, and withdrawn from, the mill. The bed plate 13 carries pedestals on which are secured guides those guides can be properly located on the bed plate prior to entry of the stand into the mill on the stand being positioned in the mill, the guides are then automatical ly located relative to the pass line and the down time of the mill on stand replacement is reduced.
