# ORTHODONTIC ATTACHMENT PLACEMENT DEVICE

## Abstract
Placement devices 10 for positioning orthodontic attach ments, such as, brackets on the lingual and facial side of teeth 44 comprise a tooth engagement plate 78 , which engages the facial side of a tooth for lingual placement or the lingual side for facial placement, and is mounted for pivotal movement 80 at least about a horizontal transverse axis. An orthodontic bracket holder 46 is mounted to a handle 16 for both hori zontal and vertical movement to permit placement of an ortho dontic bracket on the opposite side of a tooth to that engaged by the plate. A vertically adjustable tooth engaging member 84 is provided to engage the crown of the tooth to position the orthodontic attachment.
