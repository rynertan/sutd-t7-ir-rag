# Hose end fitting.

## Abstract
A one piece hose end fitting in which a ferrule is secured by swaging to an insert 1 prior to the fitting being inserted in the end of a hose has a ferrule comprising a ferrule body 9 and a ferrule collar 12 intermediate the body 9 and the insert 1 , the ferrule body 9 before swaging having an internal bore 10 throughout its length corresponding to the bore required to be swaged onto the hose and the ferrule collar 12 fitting within the body 9 before swaging and being adapted to be swaged onto the insert 1 when the body 9 is swaged onto the collar 12 .
