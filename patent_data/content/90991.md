# MACHINE FOR THE ENCIPHERMENT OF KEYS, IN PARTICULAR FOR TRANSFERRING A REFERENCE KEY PROFILE TO A KEY BLANK

## Abstract
The machine comprises a milling cutter 9 of the so called cup type whereto a manually movable bracket 29 is articulated. The bracket 29 has journalled thereon a stand 35 having the reference key 52 clamps 42,43 44,45 mounted thereon and the clamps 42,43 44,45 for the key blank 53 to be milled. The keys 52,53 lay in two respective parallel planes extending perpendicularly to the bracket 29 articulation axis and containing the cutter 9 rotation axis and the contact point of a feeler 11 arranged to sense the reference key 52 profile, respectively.
