# Method of continuously measuring a successively conveyed lengthy body.

## Abstract
A method of continuously measuring a successively conveyed lengthy body 2 . Light sensors 3, 4 are located above and laterally of the conveying path of a conveyor 1 and measure the distances from the sensors to various points on the surfaces of the lengthy body while being conveyed. The sectional area of the body is then calculated by a computer, and further the weight of the body is calculated. Various kinds of light sensors are applicable to the method of this invention.
