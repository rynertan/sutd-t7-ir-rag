# Guide track for slaughtered poultry.

## Abstract
A guide track for slaughtered poultry, which poultry is moved forward along this track 5, 6 , under the action of a driver 2 , hanging down and with at least one leg 18 supported by the guide track, in which the leg supports are formed by on the one hand a guide 5, 6 and on the other hand a support edge 12, 13 located opposite the guide 5, 6 and formed on the drive 2 .
