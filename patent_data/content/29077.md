# Liner for centre plate structure of a railway vehicle.

## Abstract
A wear resistant liner for a railway vehicle having a matrix 47E of an ultra high molecular weight polymeric material and reinforcing means 53E substantially surroun ded by said polymeric material, said reinforcing means comprising a structure having openings 54E therein for receiving said polymeric material completely therethrough, said means 53E serving to reinforce the liner and to pre vent substantial cold flow of said polymeric material.
