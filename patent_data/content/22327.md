# End face thrust seals.

## Abstract
An end face thrust seal, particularly for an automotive water pump, and comprising an annular elastomeric ring 11 having inner and outer rims 13 and 14 respectively, interconnected by a frustoconical portion 15 . A diaphragm spring 16 is embedded in the elastomeric ring 11 so that the outer annular portion 17 of the spring is at the outer rim 14 , and the spring fingers 18 extend inwards forming a frustoconical spring 19 in the frustoconical portion of the elastomeric ring 11 . The fingers 18 extend into the inner rim 13 and the inner portions 22 thereof are bent to extend axially away from the base of the cone and re inforce the inner rim 13 .
