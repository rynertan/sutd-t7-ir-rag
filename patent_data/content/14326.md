# Dye composition and its use for dyeing and printing cellulose fibers.

## Abstract
A dye composition containing C.I. Reactive Blue 19 and an alkylnaphthalene sulfonic acid formaidehyde conden sate, which is very readily soluble in water and aqueous alkaline liquors and useful for dyeing natural and regenerated cellulose fibers with high color yield, particularly as used in a one bath pad dyeing method.
