# Mould arrangement for a cyclicly operating glassware forming machine.

## Abstract
The mould arrangement comprises two supports 22 which support movable side portions 24 of a mould and two plenum chambers 50 each associated with one of the supports and movable therewith. Each plenum chamber extends above the mould side portion without obstructing an upwardly opening entrance 28 to the mould and has one or more exits 60 which communicate with entrances 40 of cooling passages 38 formed in the side portion. Air supplied to the plenum chambers passes through the exits into the cooling passages and serves to cool the side portions.
