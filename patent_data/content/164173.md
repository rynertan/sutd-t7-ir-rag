# Process for the production of a collapsible bottle.

## Abstract
A collapsible or foldable plastic bottle circular bellows like configuration capable of manufacture on current plastic bottle manufacturing equipment with current plastic materials ap proved for contact with foods and beverages. The bellows over center and fold to retain the folded condition without external assistance thus providing a self latching feature. In particular, the bellows of the bottle, when fully folded, cause the bottle to substantially fit within a cup fastened to the base of the bottle. The cup is generally formed by further extending the cup up ward and increasing the diameter slightly in comparison with the reinforcing cup on current soda pop bottles.
