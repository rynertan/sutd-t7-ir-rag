# LED LAMP AND MOUNTING BASE THEREFOR

## Abstract
An LED lamp comprises a light emitting member 36 having leads 12, 14 connected thereto which extend outwardly from a lens member 4 in which the light emitting member 36 is disposed. A base member 2 has the lens member 4 mounted therein. The base member 2 includes holes 18 extending therethrough and the leads 12, 14 are disposed within the base member 2 with post engaging sections 20 being coincident with the axes of the holes 18 whereby the post engaging sections 20 frictionally and electrically engage post member 46 of a board 48 .
