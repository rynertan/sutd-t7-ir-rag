# Method and apparatus for automatically packaging stockings.

## Abstract
A fully automatic system for folding stockings or a like long, substantially flat, non rigid article around a cardboard insert, including foldable insert, and enclosing the stockings with the insert in an envelope of transparent, thermoplastic material. Sealing of the envelope containing the stockings with gummed tapes may be performed fully automatically, also. The apparatus comprises a setting station, a transfer assembly 101 , an insert feeder assembly 201 , an insert gripper assembly 301 , a stocking folder assembly 401 , an envelope feeder assembly 701 , a conveyor assembly 501 , and a stocking encloser assembly 601 .
