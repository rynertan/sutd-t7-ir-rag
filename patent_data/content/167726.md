# Compositions, devices and method for controlling insects.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to novel insecticidal coat ing compositions comprising insecticides, such as pyrethroids or mixtures of insecticides, such as pyrethroids and organophosphates, and a vinyl dispersion which is a fluid suspension of a vinyl dispersion resin in a liquid plasticizer system. Additionally, devices coated with said compositions and method for the control of insects are presented.
