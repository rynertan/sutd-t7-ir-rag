# Safety device, in particular for an overhead door.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a safety device comprising fas tening means 17, 18, 19 for blocking the door 1 which means are operated by an auxiliary cable 12 under tension. The auxiliary cable 12 having a breaking element 15 is on the one hand connected to a reel provided with speed lim iting means for blocking the reel in case the rotation speed becomes beyond certain limits and on the other hand to the door via a lever shaped locking member 23 which coop erates with the fastening means. The fastening means are held in an inoperative position by said locking member, while upon breaking of the breaking element the fastening means are released for blocking the door.
