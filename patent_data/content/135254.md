# Rotary compressor.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a rotary compressor for refrigerating apparatuses and is intended to provide a discharge muffler formes by coverlingly placing a diaphragm 22 and discharge cober 21 over a surface opposite to the cylinder side of a bearing 14 opposite to the motor unit side, thereby to attain improvements in discharge silencing effect as well as miniaturization of rotary compressors.
