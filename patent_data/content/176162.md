# Process for regenerating cereals.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to the regeneration of cereals including barley, corn, wheat, rice and sorghum. The process comprises the steps of a culturing tissue obtained from a cereal plant on an induction medium comprising mineral salts, vitamins, suc rose and a hormone in an amount sufficient to ensure callus formation. b culturing the callus on a series of media, said series comprising at least one medium and each medium compris es mineral salts, vitamins, sucrose and a hormone in an amount sufficient to insure differentiation to plantlets having shoots and roots at the completion of the series, and c culturing the plantlets on an establishment medium comprising mineral salts, vitamins and sucrose, whereby the plantlets are established so that they can be transplanted to soil.
