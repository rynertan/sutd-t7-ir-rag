# A fastening device for releasable interconnection of panels.

## Abstract
A fastening device for releasable interconnection of two panels or elements, in one of which said device is housed and in the other of which an engagement hole is drilled, com prises a housing element 12 , a body 14 rotatable for at least an arc in said element, and a resilient element 26 bet ween said housing element and said body. The resilient ele ment has arms 72, 74 movable between a disengagement position, at which the arms are moved near one another and extended, and an engagement position, at which the arms are moved away from one another and retracted for traction engagement with the walls of the engagement hole. The body has cam means for forcing said arms from said disengage ment position to said engagement position, when said body rotates through said arc.
