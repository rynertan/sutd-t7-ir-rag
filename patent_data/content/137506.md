# Extraction of teichmonycin a2 from whole culture fermentation broth.

## Abstract
Teichomycin A₂ is recovered from whole culture fermen tation broth of Actinoplanes teichomyceticus sp. or any similar microoganism producing Teichomycin A₂ by extract ing the broth with a water miscible solvent, such as acetone, acetonitrile, dimethylsulfoxide, n propanol, methyl ethyl ketone or mixtures thereof.
