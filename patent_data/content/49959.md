# Fuel blending installation.

## Abstract
A blending installation is described in which the grade of fuel is adjusted to enable an engine to use the most economical grade of fuel at any time. A circuit 20 is connected to two reservoirs 12, 14 containing different grades of fuel. The fuel is constantly circulated by a pump 22 in the circuit 20 and fed to the engine 10 . A pump 18 meters the appropriate quantity of the higher grade fuel into the circuit 20 and the balance of lower grade fuel is supplied by gravity feed into the circuit 20 .
