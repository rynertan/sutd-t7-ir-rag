# Magnetic recording and or reproducing apparatus.

## Abstract
Magnetic recording and or reproducing apparatus has a magnetic head assembly 3 , a tape loading mechanism 6 for loading and or unloading a magnetic tape housed within a tape cassette onto or from the magnetic head assembly, a brake mechanism 63, 64, 65 which can engage with or release a supply reel table 4 and a take up reel table 5 , a common brake slider 62 for operating the brake mechanism 63, 64, 65 , and a cum gear formed of first and second cum portions which is driven by a single motor 18 , wherein when the cum gear is rotated, the tape loading mechanism is controlled in operation by the first cum portion and the common brake slider 62 is controlled in operation by the second cum portion.
