# N 2 fluoro 4 halo 5 substituted phenyl hydantoins, and their production and use.

## Abstract
N 2 fluoro 4 halo 5 substituted phenyhl hydantoins of the formula CHEM wherein X is a chlorine atom or a bromine atom and R 1 and R 2 are, same or different, each a C C alkyl group, a cyclopropyl group, a C3 C4 alkenyl group of a C3 C4 alkynyl group are disclosed, which are useful as herbicides.
