# Tire chain assembly and block therefor.

## Abstract
A tire chain assembly comprising a tire chain wherein the spaces 3 between the links 4,5 of the chain are, at least partly, occupied by a wear resistant elastomeric material 11 either moulded onto the chain or in the form of prefabricated blocks such that the combination of the material and tire chain form a belt 1 adapted to be removably located over a tire 2 of suitable size.
