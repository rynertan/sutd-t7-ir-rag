# Button actuating device.

## Abstract
This invention comprehends a button 80 mounted in the rear portion of the spinning style fishing reel to operate a self centering bail mechanism. The button 80 includes a canti lever spring arm 90 that cooperates with recesses 82, 83 formed in the button mounting passage. 81 Biasing means 92 are incorporated within the button 80 to prevent it from inadvertently sliding back and forth.
