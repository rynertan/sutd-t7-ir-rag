# A blast pipe.

## Abstract
A blast pipe 2 primarily intended for introducing gas, such as oxygen gas, into molten metal in metallurgical processes, such as steel manufacturing processes, includ ing a pipe element 1 made preferably of steel and coated at least externally with at least one protective layer 3 . The blast pipe 2 is particularly characterized by the fact that the pipe element 1 is provided with an external, preferably high compact layer 3, 4 which mainly comprises organic fibres, preferably wood fibres in the form of paper, and by the fact that the fibre layer 3, 4 is preferably com pletely or partially impregnated with a refractory substance.
