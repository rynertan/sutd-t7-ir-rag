# Overspeed protective apparatus for a portable tool.

## Abstract
Disclosed is an overspeed protective apparatus for an electric motor 13 having pairs of stator windings normally connected in parallel, the apparatus having an actuating mechanism 17 responsive to an overspeed condition of the motor 13 and an electrical switching assembly 18 normally connecting the stator windings in parallel but adapted upon actuation by the actuating mechanism to interrupt the parallel connection and connect pairs of windings in series.
