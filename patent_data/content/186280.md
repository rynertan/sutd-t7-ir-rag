# Breath activated medication spray.

## Abstract
A device for providing a metered dose of medication for inhalation by the patient. The device includes a case 10 having a conduit passage 68 connected to a mouthpiece 16 and having an angled door 58 activated to open the conduit passage when the patient inhales through the mouthpiece. The angled door is connected to an electrical circuit which is closed when the patient inhales through the conduit. The closed electrical circuit releases a spring tensioned medicinal cartridge 24 to force the cartridge release pin 46 into a conduit aperture to realease a dose of medicine into the conduit for inhalation by the patient.
