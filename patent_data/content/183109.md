# Azeotrope like compositions of trichlorotrifluoroethane, ethanol, nitromethane and 2 methylpentane or a mixture of hexanes.

## Abstract
Azeotrope like compositions comprising trichlorotri fluoroethane, ethanol, nitromethane and 2 methylpentane or a mixture of hexanes which are stable and have utility as degreasing agents and as solvents in a variety of industrial cleaning applications.
