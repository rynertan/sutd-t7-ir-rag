# Simulator for practising tracheal intubation.

## Abstract
Simulator for practising tracheal intubation comprising a manikin head attached to a support and having a skull 1 com prising a movable lower taw 2 , and a neck connected to the skull 1 and support so as to allow the manikin head to be tilt ed forwards and backwards, said simulator having an oral cavi ty and a throat composed of a thin walled funnel shaped ele ment 4 having at its upper edge a convex tongue element 9 attached to said lower jaw 2 and having at its lower end two ducts one simulating trachea 15 and the other simulating desophagus 14 .
