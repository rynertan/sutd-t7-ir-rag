# Clamping ring.

## Abstract
An attachment means for an article disposed in a panel opening, which attachment means is provided with a wedge which fits with clearance about the portion of the article pro jecting through the panel and which is fitted with circumferen tally spaced clamping elements axially adjustable relative to the ring and operative adjacent the inner edge of the ring and with adjusting elements for defining the axial position of the clamping ring relative to the rear wall of the panel.
