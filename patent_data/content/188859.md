# All plastic shotgun cartridge cases and method for the manufacture thereof.

## Abstract
An all plastic shotgun cartridge case is disclosed com prising a tubular casing 14 of biaxially oriented extruded poly ethylene and a two part, moulded plastics head, comprising a first part or basewad 12 of polyethylene moulded onto the end of the tube and a second part 13 of chemically dissimilar hard plastics material, e.g. nylon, which provides the rim of the cartridge case moulded into mechanical interlocking engage ment with interlocking channels moulded into the basewad.
