# STAB CONNECTOR ASSEMBLY

## Abstract
A stab connector assembly having a stab member and a socket member each having locking formations which are en gageable to prevent withdrawal of the stab member for the socket member. The locking formations include collet fingers secured at one end and deflectable about that end to allow pas sage of the fingers past the relevant locking formation on inser tion of the stab member into the socket member and thereafter toengage the locking formation to prevent withdrawal. The stab member may have a central mandrel on which the collet fingers are siidable, the free end of the collet fingers being trapped in use between the mandrel and the socket member.
