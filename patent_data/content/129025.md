# Print head for printing one or more lines of indicia.

## Abstract
The print head includes a plurality of print bands 310 mounted on upper and lower drive gears 312, 314 where the lower drive gears 314 are hexagonal in shape. A selector shaft which may be shifted to a selected print band 310 and then rotated to rotate the print band 310 to a selected posi tion includes a selector shaft detenting means 350, 352, 354 which detents the shaft close to the axis thereof.
