# Two package type pressure sensitive adhesive composition.

## Abstract
Disclosed in a two package type pressure sensitive adhesive composition which comprises 1 a polyol com ponent and 2 a polyurethane having terminal free isocya nate group obtained by the reaction of an isocyanate com ponent having oxadiazine 2,4,6 trione ring and if necessary, other organic diisocyanate at a specific proportion. The composition has an excellent heat resistance and stable pressure sensitive adhesion.
