# Expandable and curable polyester composition and method for making same.

## Abstract
An expandable and curable polyester composition comprising a mixture of a liquid, ethylenically unsaturated polyester resin composition a peroxide an organometallic promotor and a dialkyl or trialkyl hydrazine is disclosed. In addition, a method for forming said expanded and cured polyester resin composition, and the product formed in accordance with said method, an expanded and cured polyester resin composition, is taught.
