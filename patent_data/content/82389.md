# Fuel cell assembly with electrolyte transport.

## Abstract
A fuel cell assembly wherein electrolyte for filling the fuel cell matrix 10 is carried via a transport system comprising a first passage means 22 for conveying electrolyte through a first plate 17 and communicating with a groove 19 in a second plate 18 at a first point 19b , the first and second plates together sandwiching the matrix, and second passage means 22 acting to carry electrolyte exclusively through the second plate and communicating with the groove at a second point 19a exclusive of the first point 19b .
