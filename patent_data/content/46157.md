# Cellulosic fibers having occluded therein an alkali metal or ammonium salt of a copolymer of polyacrylic and methacrylic acid, process for the manufacture thereof and articles prepared therefrom.

## Abstract
Cellulosic fibers having improved water and fluid absorbency are made by incorporating therein an alkali metal or ammonium salt of a copolymer of acrylic acid and methacrylic acid. These fibres are produced by regen eration from a viscose solution containing from 2 to 40 by weight of the copolymer
