# Apparatus for transfer printing.

## Abstract
A transfer printing machine in which the articles 17 to be transfer printed are held in a holder 18 made of a material which does not substantially expand when heated and the articles are heated by an infrared radiation heater 22 to a predetermined temperature. A transfer sheet 5 having symbols and or designs formed thereon with thermo diffusible dye is pressed by pressing pad 27 against the surface of the articles so that the symbols and or designs are transfer printed on the surfaces of the heated articles.
