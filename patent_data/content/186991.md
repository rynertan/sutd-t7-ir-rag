# ANCHOR BOLT

## Abstract
An anchor bolt 10 which is adapted to be located in a drilled hole 12 , e.g. in a rock face, comprises an expandable shell 20 which is designed to be displaced radially outwardly into engagement with the surrounds of the drilled hole by ex pansion means, such as expansion member 18 or grout. The expandable shell is comprised of a plastic material which has been stretched in one or preferably two directions during manufacture to align the plastic molecular chains. The expand able shell is very strong and is corrosion resistant.
