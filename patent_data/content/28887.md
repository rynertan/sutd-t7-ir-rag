# Hairpiece retention system and method of applying same.

## Abstract
The present invention provides a means for anchoring an artificial hairpiece to the scalp by use of implanted filament strands to which the hairpiece is anchored. The retention strand 17 enters and exits the surface of the scalp at the same point 24 and perpendicularly to the surface, forming a retention loop 16 at the entry exit point. The retention loop is tied off at the scalp surface 19 , prevent ing creepage of the strand, and the perpendicular approach to the surface prevents ripping of the scalp under load.
