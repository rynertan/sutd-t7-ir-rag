# MOTOR MOUNTING

## Abstract
The motor mounting includes a motor support plate 8 attached to the motor in a plane generally at right angles to the motor drive shaft 4 , a motor support frame 27 and mounting devices 10, 12, 14, 16 mounting the support plate on the support frame. Each device is provided by a unitary member having two legs 18, 19 each terminating at its free end in a respective one of two ears 12, 25 extending generally at right angles to the main portion of its respective leg, one 24 of the ears of each device being secured to the motor support plate and the other 25 of the ears being resiliently mounted on the motor support frame.
