# Petroleum resins and their production.

## Abstract
Resins having an improved combination of softening point and tackifying properties together with good colour and heat stability are obtained by Freidel Crafts copoly merisation of 4 to 80 wt. a C₅ C₆ unsaturated feed 5 to 55 wt. of a terpene and 3 to 55 wt. of an unsaturated aromatic compound.
