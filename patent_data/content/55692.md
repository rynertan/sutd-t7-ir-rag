# Novel pyrimidinyl ethers, their use as herbicides, herbicidal compositions comprising said pyrimidinyl ethers and processes for the preparation thereof.

## Abstract
Pyrimidines having an amino group in the 2 position and a cyclopropylmethoxy group in the 4 position of the pyrimi dine nucleus. Compounds according to the invention have valuable herbicidal activity, particularly after pre emergence application in cotton and sunflower.
