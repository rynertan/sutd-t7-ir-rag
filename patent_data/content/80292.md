# Helicopter rotors.

## Abstract
A helicopter rotor having a rotor hub and a plurality of rotor blades includes for each blade a blade fold mechanism 10 comprising a reversible rotary power source 17, 18 and a rack and pinion mechanism 22, 41 adapted to operate one or more lock pins 24 and to fold the blades between an operational spread position and a folded position. Two embodiments of the invention are described and illustrated.
