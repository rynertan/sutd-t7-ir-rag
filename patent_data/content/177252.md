# Medical Forceps and like medical instruments.

## Abstract
A forceps or like medical instrument having a core 1, 22, 22 min of metal, its gripping members 3, 4, 21, 22, 21 min , 22 min being at least partially covered by a molded on layer of plastics in a sandwich like manner. In particular, a forceps or like medical instrument wherein the part of the core which is destined to become the gripping part 5, 24a, 24a min is covered by a plastic gripping means with or without teeth 12, 24, 24 min . Desirably, the gripping members 3, 4, 21, 22, 21 min , 22 min are of substantially identical construction.
