# COMPOSITION FOR FOAM FOODS

## Abstract
Herein disclosed are compositions for multi cellular foods such as ice cream, bavarian cream or mousse which comprise gelatin, alginic acid and or sodium alginate, edi ble fats and oils such as vegetable oil, animal oil, coconut oil, palm oil etc., and balance of water and which may op tionally be overrun, the composition makes it possible to improve the multi cellular food products in their shape re taining ability even at a relatively high temperature and in the resistance to water separation from the thawed food products, and these properties being maintained even when the acidic foods such as fruit juice or jam or sour milk beverages are added thereto, furthermore, the composition being solidified at a relatively high temperature, thus the process for manufacturing multi cellular foods can greatly be simplified.
