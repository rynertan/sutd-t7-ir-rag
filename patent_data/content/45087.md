# Cross flow fan for transverse engine vehicle.

## Abstract
Disclosed is a vehicle having a transversely mount ed engine 20 , an engine cooling radiator 22 mounted forward of the engine and substantially parallel to the rotational axis of the engine crankshaft, a cross flow fan 24 mounted for rotation about an axis substantially parallel to the crankshaft axis and between the crank shaft axis and plane of the radiator, and a mechanical drive 32, 34, 36 which rotates the fan in response to rotation of the crankshaft.
