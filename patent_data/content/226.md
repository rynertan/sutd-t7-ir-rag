# Laundry additive substrate for stain removal.

## Abstract
Additive products are provided for use in the washing of textiles comprising a mixture of an organic peroxy bleach precursor, a cationic surfactant and an alkoxylated nonionic surfactant of HLB 8 17 in water releasable combination with a non particulate substrate, the additive products being adapted for addition to conventional inorganic persalt containing detergent liquors to enhance the removel of bleachable and greasy stains.
