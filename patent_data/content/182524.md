# Hinge arrangement.

## Abstract
A hinge arrangement for an electrical cabinet allows a door to be rapidly removed and refitted. A hinge member 45 , to be coupled to another hinge member by a hinge pin 46 , is provid ed as a housing 47 mounting a projecting pin 49, 49a and a tubular portion 48 to be secured to a corner post 41 of the cabinet by a wedge arrangement 54 . A spring loaded catch 58, 59, 60 engages a peripheral lip 51 at the free end of the tubular portion 48 when the pin 49, 49a is engaged in the tubular portion 48 and moved towards the cabinet.
