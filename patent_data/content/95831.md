# AMORPHOUS METALS AND ARTICLES MADE THEREOF

## Abstract
An amorphous Fe B Si alloy and article made there from is provided having improved castability while main taining good magnetic properties, ductility and improved thermal stability. The alloy consists of 73 80 Fe, 4 10 B and 14 17 Si, in atomic percentages, and no more than in cidental impurities. A method of casting an amorphous strip material from the alloy is also provided.
