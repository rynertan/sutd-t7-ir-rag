# Balanced variable displacement drive.

## Abstract
A wobble plate drive mechanism 11, 13, 20, 30 for machines having axial pistons 4 or the like clustered about a shaft 11 . The moving parts are dynamically balanced over a range of wobble plate tilt angles Î³ by a balance ring 25 arranged outboard of the wobble plate 30 . The wobble plate restraint has balanced gimbal rings 60, 70 .
