# Adhesive and method of jointing articles of polyolefin using the same.

## Abstract
An adhesive, suitable for jointing cured polyolefin articles, comprising a water curable polyolefin having a melt index of from 0.1 to 20, and a method of jointing cured polyolefin articles, especially cured polyethylene pipes, comprising a step to apply the above adhesive to the surface of the articles and a step to joint both of the surfaces to each other with the melt of the adhesive.
