# Intumescent material.

## Abstract
An intumescent material comprises one or more organ ic polyhydroxy compounds as carbonific one or more organic polyamido compounds in free and or combined form as spumific an ammonium phosphate as activator for the carbonific and an amino resin as binder, the material further comprising sodium silicate in an amount insufficient to prevent the material intumescing on being subjected to elevated temperature. A method of manufacturing the intumescent material, comprises preparing an aqueous paste or slurry comprising the ingredients of the material and allowing or causing the paste or slurry to harden.
