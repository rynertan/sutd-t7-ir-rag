# Herbicidal sulfonamides.

## Abstract
4 choro 2 4 methoxy 6 methyl 1,3,5 triazin 2 yl ami nocarbonyl aminosulfonyl benzoic acid 1 methylethyl ester and its agriculturally suitable salts are highly active preemer gent and postemergent herbicides of particular interest for controlling blackgrass in cereal crops. The compound maybe made by a variety of routes, e.g. by reacting 1 methylethyl 4 chloro 2 isocyanatosulfonyl benzoate with 4 methoxy 6 methyl 1,3,5 triazin 2 amine.
