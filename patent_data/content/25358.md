# Liquid retaining synthetic fibre, process for producing the same, and products.

## Abstract
This invention relates to a liquid retaining synthetic fibre having a substantially pointed free end and includ ing a tapered portion having an acute ridgeline on its surface. It is prepared from a synthetic fibre having in sec tion at least one concave portion and at least one convex portion which gradually taper off toward the outside from the centre. These fibres are suitable for use in brushes, or for the manufacture of knitted or woven fabrics or flocked fabrics having an animal hair like or fur like touch or appearance.
