# Sterile air trolley.

## Abstract
Impeller 17 is operative to draw air into the mobile casing via inlets 14 and prefilters 18 and drive said air upwards through filters in chamber 20 to leave by way of apertures 22a , 22b in upper region 12 . A sterile bacteria free zone in which, for example, surgery may be performed or sterile instruments unwrapped, is thereby created adja cent the outlets 22a , 22b .
