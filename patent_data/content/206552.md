# Knife.

## Abstract
An orthopedic knife for a one handed person, the knife comprising a blade 14 , a hold down member 12 closely associated with the blade 14 , and an extending handle 10 pivoted to both blade 14 hold down member 12 at spaced points 16, 18 such that the blade 14 reciprocates as the handle 10 is oscillated and the hold down member 12 is lodged with respect to an object to be cut.
