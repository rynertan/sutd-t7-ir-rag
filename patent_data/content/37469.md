# Electronic lock, switching and hold devices for telephones.

## Abstract
A device containing accessory controls for an associated telephone 10 to control the operation of the telephone cir cuits. The device includes a housing 18, 52 which conforms to the contour of the telephone, so as to be conveniently mounted below the cradle 14 thereof. A card 19,70 contain ing logic to control the operation of the telephone circuits is positioned within the housing. An actuator assembly 58 may be included within the housing to cause the control logic to be selectively energized. In operation, the presently disclosed device is adapted to prevent the placing of unauthorized calls from the associated telephone, switch an incoming call to an accessory or extension telephone, or place the associated telephone on hold, so that the hand set can be returned to the cradle without disconnecting an existing caller.
