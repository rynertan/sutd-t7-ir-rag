# Vacuum sizing device.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a vacuum sizing 20 for cooling and sizing plastic extrusions immediately downstream of the extru sion die 22 . The device 20 includes a plurality of pairs 32 of upper and lower sizing elements 34 and 36 which are clamped between upper and lower backing members 20 and 30 . The elements 34 and 36 are shaped to define a passageway 26 through the sizing device having a cross sectional shape and size corresponding to the required profile shape and size of the extrusion. Vacuum chambers 44 , 44 and cooling chambers 52 , 52 are provided in the sizing elements 34 and 36 .
