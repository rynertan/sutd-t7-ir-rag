# Plug valve.

## Abstract
A rotary plug valve has a tapered plug 473 received within a frusto conical chamber in a valve housing 414 . A liner between the plug 473 and chamber wall comprises a rigid metal body 472 having sealing sleeves 481, 486 of plastics material to provide a seal with the plug and the chamber wall. The sleeves are retained by lips 482 provided in the metal body. The liner and plug are removable as a unit 471 from the chamber in the valve housing 414 .
