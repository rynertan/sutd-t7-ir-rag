# Electric induction crucible furnace.

## Abstract
The furnace comprises a crucible 10 surrounded by an induction heating coil 16 and having a filling mouth 12 and a pouring spout 11 . The crucible is cylindrical in shape having an oval external cross sectional profile 10a with a major diameter and a minor diameter, and an internal profile 10b of substantially constant diameter. In the portions of the crucible wall of greater thickness are formed a filling duct 14 and a pouring duct 13 which connect the filling mouth and the pouring spout respectively to the bottom of the cru cible.
