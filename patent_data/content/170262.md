# PROSTHETIC VALVE

## Abstract
A prosthetic valve comprising an annular member 4 to be fitted to the inner wall of a blood passage, a plurality of columns 6 each having one end connected to the inner wall 2 of the annular member 4 and their other ends fixed together in a point, and a flexible membrane 10 mounted between the columns 6 and having a skirt portion 10a which can, alternately, apply itself to or leave the inner wall surface of the annular member 4 depending upon the balance of blood pressure on both sides of the membrane 10 , thereby allowing for a unidirectional blood flow.
