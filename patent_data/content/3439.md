# Internal combustion engine.

## Abstract
An internal combustion engine 1 having at least one oil port 10 in the wall member of the cylinder 3 for injecting lubricating oil to the skirt portion 4a of the piston 4 of the engine in synchronism with the crank shaft, whereby oil film may be formed on the skirt portion 4a to prevent the swing of the piston 4 at the stroke ends to abate the piston slapping noise.
