# A purified polypeptide, a process for its preparation, its use and pharmaceutical compositions containing it.

## Abstract
A novel, purified peptide having the following amino acid sequence Arg Ser Leu Gln Asn Thr Glu Glu Lys Ser Arg Ser Phe Pro Ala Pro Gln Thr Asp Pro Leu Asp Asp Pro Asp Gln Met Thr Glu Asp may be used as medicament, e.g. in the treatment of gastroduodenal ulcers.
