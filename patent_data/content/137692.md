# NEW ANTIBIOTIC, NEOCOPIAMYCIN A AND THE PRODUCTION THEREOF

## Abstract
A new antibiotic, designated as neocopiamycin A, is useful as antifungal agent. Neocopiamycin A is fermentative ly produced as a minor component, in addition to copiamy cin, by a microorganism Streptomyces hygroscopicus var. crystallogenes FERM BP 576 which is known to produce the known antibiotic copiamycin.
