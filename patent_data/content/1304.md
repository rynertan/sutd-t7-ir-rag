# Coating composition having a prolonged pot life comprising a physical blend in an organic solvent of a polyhydroxy compound and polyisocyanate.

## Abstract
A coating composition having a prolonged pot life is disclosed which is based on a polyhydroxy compound and a polyisocyanate in an organic solvent, the composition containing 2 25 by weight of 2 methyl 2 propanol and or 2 methyl 2 butanol with the proviso that at least 0.8 equivalents of the alcohol are present per equivalent of the polyisocyanate.
