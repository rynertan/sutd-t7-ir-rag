# LOW LEAKAGE CAPACITOR HEADER AND MANUFACTURING METHOD THEREFOR

## Abstract
A capacitor header having the mechanical characteristics of a modulus of elasticity in the range from 50,000 to 200,000 psi and a tensile strength in the range from 3,500 to 80,000 psi has pressure formed therein on the aluminum terminal of which portions thereof have been increased in the range of .002 to .020 inch larger than the holes provided in the header.
