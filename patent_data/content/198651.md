# AN APPARATUS FOR APPLYING A VACUUM TO A PART OF A HUMAN OR ANIMAL BODY

## Abstract
An apparatus, particularly a breast pump, for the applica tion of a vacuum to a part of a human or other animal body com prising a cup member and a flexible diaphragm made preferably of a silicone rubber and fitted over the mouth of the cup mem ber. The diaphragm has an aperture around which there is preferably a tubular extension directed inwardly of the cup member. The diaphragm is provided also preferably with a return flange to enable the diaphragm to be connected detach ably to the cup member. A vacuum is provided either by de forming a wall of the apparatus or preferably by a vacuum pump which communicates with an aperture in the apparatus.
