# Drop dispenser.

## Abstract
An eyedrop dispenser A for dispensing droplets of liquid from a container B comprises a closure cap member 20 for liquid tight attachment to the container. At least one projection 60, 62 extends laterally outward from the cap member and has a laterally outward facing surface 64, 66 for bracing against a portion of the head of a person to locate and steady the dispenser during use.
