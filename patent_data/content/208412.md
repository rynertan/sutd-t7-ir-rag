# Valve assembly for pipeline.

## Abstract
A valve assembly 10 for a pipeline has a rotatable sleeve 24 within a chamber 16 which houses obturator means 52 such as a check valve assembly. The chamber 16 and sleeve 24 have through passages which can be brought into and out of register by rotating the sleeve 24. Thus the chamber can be isolated from the pipeline, and the obturator 52 can be servi ced and or removed and replaced.
