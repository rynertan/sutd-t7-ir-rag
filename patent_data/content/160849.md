# Needle disposal apparatus.

## Abstract
Apparatus for facilitating the safe disposal of used hypodermic needles. According to a preferred exbodiment, a funnel like member 124 adapted to be hand held, functions to support a needle sheath 54 during a needle sheathing operation. The converging walls 125 of such member shield the hand of the user from accidental needle sticks . Also disclosed are needle containers 41, 120 which incorporate means for facilitating the sheathing of used needles prior to entering the container.
