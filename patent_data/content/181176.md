# COMPUTED TOMOGRAPHY SCANNER

## Abstract
A computed tomography scanner comprising a frame 28 rotatably mounted on a gantry 34 and carrying an X ray source 14 arranged to direct radiation through a subject onto a detector array 12 from different directions as the frame 28 rotates wherein an electric motor 50 for rotating the frame 28 relative to the gantry 34 has one 60 of its field generating and field responsive parts 51, 53 and 60 directly mounted on one 37 of the races 37, 39 of a bearing 36 via which the frame 28 is mounted on the gantry 34 .
