# DRAUGHT INTENSIFYING APPARATUS

## Abstract
A draught intensifying apparatus for chimneys compris ing an inner gas channel 6 connected to the chimney having a chimney capital 2 . According to the improvement, the apparatus has a confusor 3 provided with an inner mantle 5 and an outer mantle 8 covered by a cap 4 , and the gas channel 6 is formed within the inner mantle 5 , and between the inner mantle 5 and the outer mantle 8 a confusor space 9 narrowing from the chimney capital 2 towards the cap 4 is provided, and gas outlets 12 are provided between the confusor 3 and the cap 4 .
