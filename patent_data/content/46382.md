# Staking or anchoring objects to the ground.

## Abstract
The object is connected to one end 13 of a flexible line 10 , engaging either the other end 15 of the line 10 or an anchoring member 13 or 50 attached to the other end 15 of the line with a mechanical driving tool 16, 17, 18 , forcibly driv ing the tool 16, 17, 18 into the ground until a predetermined length of the line 10 is embedded below ground level, disen gaging the tool 16, 17, 18 from the line 10 or from the an choring member 13 or 50 , and extracting the tool 16, 17, 18 from the ground.
