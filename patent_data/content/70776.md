# A compensating container, notably for pharmaceutical products.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a container which is notably intended to hols pills, and it comprises on the one hand an external, rigid envelope 10 and on the other hand an internal envelope 11 enclosing the pills, this internal envelope being elastically deformable and capable of an increase in volume which is sufficient for absorbing the surplus pressure resulting from the packing of the pills when the lid is applied, without crushing the pills. In a first embodiment, the internal envelope 11 is cylin drical and it has a convex, deformable base 12. In a second embodiment, the internal envelope is a sleeve provided with at least one slit allowing its radial expansion.
