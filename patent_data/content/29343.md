# LINING CUTTER AND CUTTING METHOD

## Abstract
In order to provide for the remote control and monitoring of a cutter support 48a for pipe linings a body 11 is mount ed, e.g. on skids not shown , for transport through a lined pipe. A unit 32 is mounted on the body 11 so as to be adjus table longitudinally by a motor 44 , angularly by a motor 37 , and radially by a motor 56 . The unit carries a motor 48 for the cutter 48a and also a camera 49 trainable on the opera tive end of the cutter mounted on the support 48a and also movable to a position 49a where it is trained along the lined pipe. A second camera 41 is mounted in fixed position on the unit 32 substantially in the plane of the cutter.
