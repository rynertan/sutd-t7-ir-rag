# Automatic parenteral infusion apparatus.

## Abstract
Automatic parenteral infusion equipment for safely delivering, under substantially constant pressure and at a substantially constant rate of flow, infusion liquid F to a patient s body. The apparatus includes a chamber 10b for containing the infusion liquid F, a pressure force P remote from the infusion liquid chamber 10b, a force transmission means 18 interposed between the pressure source P and the infusion liquid chamber 10b, vent means 10e to discharge compressible or incompressible fluid P used as a force transmission vehicle in order to prevent the possibility of embolism or toxicity to the patient, and flow regulating member 15 including a restricted orifice 15a to deliver infusion liquid F to the patient at a substantially constant rate. Alternative embodiments of the invention are disclosed for adaptation to both ambulatory and immobile patients.
