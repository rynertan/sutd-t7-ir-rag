# LESS IRRITANT HAIR RINSE COMPOSITION

## Abstract
Less irritant hair rinse compositions available to even aller gic persons susceptible to dermatitis or patients with atopic dermatitis comprise quaternary ammonium salt 1 5 w w , glycerin C₈ C₂₀ fatty acid ester 1 7 w w , C₈ C₂₀ alcohol 1 7 w w , and polyoxyethylene lanolin 0.1 3 w w .
