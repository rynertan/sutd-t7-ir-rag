# VAN BODIES

## Abstract
A curtainsided van body has at least one open vertical side closable by a curtain incorporating straps or similar for constraining the curtain in a vertical plane. The straps or similar have at or adjacent their lower ends rave engaging hooks. The van body has a floor for supporting cargo and a rave along said at least one open vertical side and engage able by said hooks to constrain the curtain vertically. The rave is height adjustable relative to the floor to provide a cill level with the floor to accommodate cargo extending to the floor edge, or above the floor to provide a bottom restraint for cargo. The rave may be unitary or comprise relatively adjustable inner and outer rave members.
