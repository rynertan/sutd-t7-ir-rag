# Self locking ribbon assemblies.

## Abstract
A ribbon assembly 10 including face to face bow ribbons 12 attached together at spaced locations 22, 24, 26 around a pull member 18 attached to their ends 15 that can be pulled to gather the bow ribbons 12 into a bow. Upon such pulling, an end portion 14 of the assembly 10 is pulled between the attachment locations 22, 24, 26 to hold the bow ribbons 12 to gether in the shape of a bow.
