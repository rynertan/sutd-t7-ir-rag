# Paper incorporating a partially embedded strip, methods for making paper and security documents.

## Abstract
A method of forming a paper 7 comprises depositing paper fibres onto a support surface 2 from a furnish 1 by drainage, laying a strip 4 having fibre deposition blocking regions 9 and fibre deposition permitting regions 8 onto the deposited fibres and continuing deposition to form a paper having the strip 4 partially embedded in the paper and partially exposed.
