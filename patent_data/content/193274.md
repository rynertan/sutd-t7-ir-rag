# Image forming apparatus.

## Abstract
An image forming apparatus having a cleaning member 14 bringing into and out of abutment against an image retainer 1 , wherein the cleaning member 14 is operated at the time except at least the time of an image write in step. The cleaning member 14 is brought into abutment against the image retainer 1 after the end of a final image writing operation for forming a transfer image whereas the release of the cleaning member 14 from abutment against the im age retainer 1 is started at the time when the image writing operation for forming a next transfer image is not conducted.
