# Containers and method and machine for making them.

## Abstract
The invention provides a container preferably made from sheet plastics comprising a bag 1 containing a tubular valve member 2 , the bag being sealed with a seam 3 at each end, the top seal having an opening 4 therein for entry for an access tube into the valve member, and the valve member having a sealing seam 5 which facilitates piercing of the access tube through the valve member into the bag. A second bag may be provided within the first bag. The invention includes a machine for making the contain ers continuously from sheets of material.
