# A device for dividing slaughtered poultry.

## Abstract
A device for dividing slaughtered poultry, comprising a stationary elongate conveyor member 5a 5d situated above a positioning member 31 and transporting the bird 30 suspended by its legs, and with a guide member 45 having a guide surface situated beneath the positioning member 31 and symmetrical with respect thereto, and comprising an upwardly sloping part 45 in front of the positioning member with a central wedge shaped position ing groove 46 for the bird s breast, and a substantially flat part 53 beneath the positioning member 31 through which the cutting means 20 extends.
