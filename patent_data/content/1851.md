# Highly concentrated pharmaceutical formulations of steroids and processes for their preparation.

## Abstract
The invention relates to highly concentrated liquid pharmacautical formulations of staroids of the oestrane, androstane and 19 nor prognane series comprising tocol or a derivative thereof that is fluid at normal temperature, or mixtures thereof, in an amount of at least 10 by weight of the formulation, and optionally one or more of the usual fluid cariers, such as vegetable oil, benzyl benzoate and or benzyl alcohol.
