# An article conveying and accumulating device.

## Abstract
In an article conveying and accumulating device, in which substantially identical articles A are being transferred by a pusher 2 from a conveyor 1 onto a receiving surface C adjacent the conveyor 1 , and accumulated on said receiving surface C by repeated strokes of said pusher 2 , the receiv ing surface C is formed by a conveyor 5 driven for constant movement at right angles to the conveyor 1 with substan tially the same speed as the pushing speed of the pusher 2 . The receiving conveyor is composed of two chains 5 and freely rotatable support rods 6 extending there between.
