# Communication apparatus.

## Abstract
In an electronic funds transfer system, in which a HOST transfers cash amounts between accounts which it main tains, as a result of transmission messages sent to it, a retailer R has a point of sale terminal POST , and each potential customer P has a portable terminal XATR , and a data storage and transfer card DSTC , forming a XATR DSTC sub assembly which can be selectively coupled to the POST. The POST and XATR DSTC have respective block cipher encryption units 28, 11 with respective keys K
