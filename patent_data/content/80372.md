# PROCESS FOR REMOVAL OF RESIDUAL ORGANIC MATTER AND COLOR FROM PURIFIED PHOSPHORIC ACID

## Abstract
This invention provides a process for removal of residual organic matter and color from purified wet process phosphoric acid that comprises contacting the purified phosphoric acid with hydrogen peroxide at a temperature between 100 C and 150 C, for between ¹ ₄ hour and 4 hours, and using a weight ratio of hydrogen peroxide to phosphoric acid between 0.001 and 0.05.
