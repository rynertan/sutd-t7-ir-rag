# Skin treatment composition.

## Abstract
A cosmetically acceptable composition for topical appli cation to human skin comprises from 0.1 to 20 by weight of a substituted 2 hyroxyoctanoic acid or 2 ketooctanoic acid or a mixture thereof, together with an amount of a special neutralising agent sufficient to adjust the pH of the composi tion to a value of from 3 to 7. The special neutralising agent is one having a cation whose ionic radius is at least 100 pm.
