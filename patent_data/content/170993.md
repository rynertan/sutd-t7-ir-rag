# NEEDLE SELECTING DEVICE FOR A CIRCULAR KNITTING MACHINE, IN PARTICULAR A HOSE KNITTING MACHINE

## Abstract
The device comprises a plurality of selection levers 2 journalled to a common structure 1 and being selectively movable from a first position whereat one end 2a thereof is adapted to interfere with the butts 7 and push the respective jacks 8 into an inoperative position, and a second position whereat that same end 2a is located between the jack butts 7 so as not to interfere therewith. The selection levers 2 are driven by intermediate drive elements 10 , each actuated by two actuator members 14 , alternately actuable to meet con tingent knitting requirements. Each of the intermediate drive elements 10 is engaged at all times with a corresponding se lection lever 2 .
