# GRATES

## Abstract
A grate 12, 112 characterised in that a fuel supporting and conveying surface is provided comprising at least one elongate channel 20, 120 having an open mouth and a bot tom,the or each of said channels 20, 120 having a transverse cross section which decreases in width in a direction extend ing away from the open mouth towards the bottom of the channel 20, 120 , air feed opening means 21, 121 to feed air to the interior of the channel 20, 120 at least in the region of the bottom thereof, and means 55 mounting the channel 20, 120 to permit the channel 20, 120 to be vibrated to cause fuel to be conveyed along the channel 20, 120 and longitudinally of the grate 12, 112 .
