# Insecticidal compositions having high concentration of active ingredients.

## Abstract
A liquid insecticidal composition having a high concen tration of active ingredients and good stability at low temperatures comprises 1 a specified carbamate insecticide, e.g. methomyl, oxamyl or aldicarb 2 a reaction product of 1 with formaldehyde or paraformaldehyde and 3 1 35 by weight, based on the weight of the composition, of water. The composition may be made by reacting the carba mate insecticide with formaldehyde or paraformaldehyde in the presence of small amounts of water and a base.
