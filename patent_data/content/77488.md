# A process for the preparation of thermoplastic polyphosphonatophenyl ester carbonates.

## Abstract
A two step catalyzed process for the preparation of thermoplastic polyphosphonatophenyl ester carbonates comprising first, the preparation of an aryloxy carbonyloxy benzoic acid aryl ester and second, its transesterification with a phosphonic acid diphenyl ester and a diphenol is disclosed.
