# Apparatus and method for fluid adhesive application.

## Abstract
This disclosure involves periodically bumping webs 5 such as a cigarette filter paper or the like against a shear surface 11 disposed beyond a multi nozzle adhesive extruder 7 to provide longitudinally intermittent bands C of adhesive coatings for the filters or the like, with optional provision for continuous marginal lines L of adhesive along and between the bands for adhesion on the head and tail ends of such filters or other articles as well.
