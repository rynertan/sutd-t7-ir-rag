# Acoustic liner for attenuating noise.

## Abstract
An acoustic liner for use with the silencers of vehicles efficiently attenuates noise over a wide frequency range and is suited for use in dirty conditions as frequently asso ciated with vehicle silencers. The acoustic liner is of simple and robust construction and comprises a plurality of cells each having a backing face 30 and a perforated facing sheet 32 arranged over and spaced from the backing face, the perforated facing sheet being on non uniform thickness in the direction of noise propagation.
