# Method of cultivating Ganoderma Lucidum FR Karst.

## Abstract
Fruit bodies of Ganoderma lucidum Fr. Karst, having a long stipe which are luxury and health foods, are prepared by a cultivating a seed culture of a strain of Ganoderma lucidum Fr. Karst, in an artificial solid medium to obtain a fungal tissue bed, b maintaining the fungal tissue bed at a relative humidity of at least 90 and an illumination intensity of at most 500 Ix to form selectively a fruit body primordium from the fungal tissue bed, and c maintaining the resulting culture at a relative humidity of from 40 to lower than 90 and an illumination intensity of at least 50 Ix to form a fungal pileus of the fruit body from the primordium.
