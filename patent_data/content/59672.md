# Parking brake arrangement.

## Abstract
A parking brake arrangement for a front wheel drive vehicle in which the engine and transmission are connected with drive shafts through universal joints. According to this invention, the parking brake arrange ment is independent from the usual front and rear brake assemblies 30, 34 , and comprises brake bands 58, 60 which surround outer surfaces 80, 82 on the universal joints 62, 64 and are frictionally engageable therewith during a parking brake application. For use in brake systems for automotive vehicles.
