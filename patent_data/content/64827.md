# ADVERTISING, DISPLAY OR DECORATIVE PANEL

## Abstract
A panel 18 comprises a laminate of metal 20 on a backing 22 , a suitable laminate being a copper circuit board photographically etched with a pattern or advertisement. The panels 18 may be located in a frame comprising channel shaped frame units 10 to form a lampshade, the light of the lamp being adapted to shine through the translucent backing 20 .
