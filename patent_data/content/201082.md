# Mounting structure for a vehicle suspension.

## Abstract
A structure 10 for mounting a suspension on a car body 18 in which a shock absorber 14 and an upper arm 12 connected to a steering knuckle 22 and extending in ward are supported by a bracket 20 . The bracket 21 is pi votally connected to the upper arm 12 and is connected to the shock absorber 14 . The upper arm 12 and the shock absorber 14 are mounted on the car body 18 via the bracket 20 .
