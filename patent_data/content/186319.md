# Body panel joint finishing moulded to vehicle body.

## Abstract
A body panel joint finishing method to produce a finished body panel joint 32 by moulding a bead 26 directly to a vehicle body 12 in a single operation without any need for secondary processing to remove excess materials. The method utilizes a foamable plastics material as the bead forming material to provide greatly improved sealing of the joint 32 against moisture penetration.
