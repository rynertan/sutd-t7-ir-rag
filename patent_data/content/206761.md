# Package and dispenser for adhesive coated notepaper.

## Abstract
Notepaper dispensers formed from inexpensive card stock material tend to weaken and tear when subjected to con centrated forces, whereas a dispenser provided with lengths of polymeric material 34 adjacent the edges 12, 14 of the dispensing opening which will bend and flex more reliably to reduce tearing and reduce curl of the dispensed sheets when a biasing member 40 is disposed in the container for biasing the notepaper 36 toward the dispensing opening.
