# Method and apparatus for ejecting tobacco from filter cigarettes.

## Abstract
Tobacco is reclaimed from filter cigarettes by applying sufficient pressurized air to the filter end of each cigarette to eject the tobacco from the cigarettes. A method and apparatus for reclaiming the tobacco involves a transporting a succession of filter cigarettes on a rotating drum 42 , in a discontinuous, indexing manner, b temporarily engaging the filter end of each cigarette with reciprocating mouthpiece means 47 during the pause between indexed movements and c applying pressurized air to each engaged cigarette via the mouthpiece means 47 to force the tobacco from the cigarette and into a suitable collection device.
