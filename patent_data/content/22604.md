# Dual latching mechanism for a flexible deck lid.

## Abstract
A dual latching mechanism for latching two spaced locations of a flexible lid 3 to a pair of similarly spaced anchors 6, 7 of a compartment to which said lid is movably mounted, said latching mechanism comprising first and second latching means 10, 110 for latching to said anchors, mounting means for mounting said latching means to said flexible lid at spaced locations thereon and latch control means 9 connected between said first and second latching means for limiting latching action of said latching means to concurrent only latching to said anchors, whereby independent latching of either one of said latching means to one only of said anchors is prevented.
