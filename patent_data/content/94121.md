# Thermoplastic compositions based on non polar organic polymers and zeolites in acid form, which have strong adhesion to metals, and composite objects obtainable therefrom.

## Abstract
A thermoplastic composition based on non polar organ ic polymers and crystalline zeolites in acid form of which the exchangeable ions are totally or partly substituted by H ions, the composition having strong adhesion to metals, the invention also relating to composite objects obtainable therefrom.
