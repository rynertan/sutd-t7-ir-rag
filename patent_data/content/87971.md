# Apparatus for removing covering material from underwater pipelines.

## Abstract
Apparatus 10 for removing covering material 16 from an underwater pipeline 12 includes a frame and means for clamping the frame to the pipeline. A plurality of pipeline covering material cutters 84 are cantileverly supported beyond the clamping means to provide a plurality of cutting passes simultaneously and away from the points of attach ment of the apparatus 10 to the pipeline 12 .
