# FILTRATE DISCHARGE SYSTEM FOR PRESSURE FILTER

## Abstract
A low pressure filtration and thickening device compris ing a tank 11 having a plurality of suspended filter elements 12 therein and a plurality of filtrate receiving compartments 21 in the upper portion of the tank for receiving filtrate from the filter elements. The filtrate discharge systemprovides separate and uninterrupted flow paths for the filtrate from each compartment 21 to a storage tank. Each such flow path has a discharge pipe 29, riser 30, and individual box 31 in a head box 32.
