# Container and method of handling, filling and sealing said container.

## Abstract
A container, of which the body 10 is downwardly tapered to interfit with others for storage or transport, has an outwardly extending circumferential top bead 13 . A separate top ring 14, 29, 39 is formed with an outer inverted annular channel 16, 32, 42 for engagement on the top part and bead 13 of the body 10 , and is also formed with an inner annular channel 15, 30, 40 to receive the downturned peripheral part of a conventional friction fit lid 22, 32, 41 .
