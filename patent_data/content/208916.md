# INNER LEAD BONDING APPARATUS

## Abstract
A inner lead bonding apparatus for bonding electrodes 54 of an IC chip 51 to inner leads 50 disposed on a plastic film 22 has a holding means 45 for holding the inner leads 50 and or the plastic film 22 at a predeter mined position to the IC chip 51 , thereby troublesome contact between the innerleads 50 and the IC chip 51 is prevented.
