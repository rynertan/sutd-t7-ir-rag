# PROCESS OF TREATING BIO SLUDGE

## Abstract
There is disclosed a method of dewatering biosludge which comprises adding to the sludge an effective amount for the purpose of a copolymer having a cationic charge density of from 80 to 90 , preferably 90 . A preferred copolymer is an acrylamide dimethylaminoethylmethacry late methylchloride copolymer having a cationic charge density of 90 .
