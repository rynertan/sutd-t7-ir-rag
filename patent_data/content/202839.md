# IMPROVEMENTS IN AND RELATING TO WATER DISPENSERS

## Abstract
Water dispensing apparatus which is intended to dis pense sterilised chilled water comprising a sterilisation unit having an inlet for connection to mains water supply and having its outlet connected to a refrigeration unit, the refrigeration unit having a tap or dispensing valve. Prefer ably, the sterilising unit comprises an ultra violet ray lamp surrounded by an outer tube through which water flows between inlet and outlet. If desired a water reservoir may be connected to the steriliser, the arrangement being such that water is sterilised both on entering the reservoir and on leaving the reservoir and so that make up air is sterilised on entering the reservoir.
