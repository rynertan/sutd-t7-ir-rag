# LAUNDRY DETERGENT COMPOSITION WITH ENHANCED STAIN REMOVAL

## Abstract
Laundry detergent compositions esentially free of about 3 to about 60 by weight of water insoluble sodium aluminosilicate or organic detergency builder components and from about 0.5 to about 10 by weight hydroxyethylethylenediaminetriacetic acid or salts thereof have enhanced organic stain removal characteristics.
