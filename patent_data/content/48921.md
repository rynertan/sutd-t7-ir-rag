# Easy opening container.

## Abstract
A closure for a container for pressurized contents, having an end member 10 , means 15, 20 for securing said end member to a container, a score in said end member 10 defining a tear out panel portion 32 , a tap 28 secured to said end member 10 having a nose overlying said panel portion, means 25 securing said tab 28 to said end member 10 outside said tear out panel portion, said tab 28 and means 25 securing said tab being formed and arranged to rupture said means 15, 20 securing said end member 10 to said container pursuant to lifting of the tab for venting the container and said tab operative thereafter to rupture the score and push said tear out panel portion 32 into the container.
