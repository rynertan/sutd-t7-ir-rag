# A scaffolding system.

## Abstract
In a scaffolding system an upright tube 2 is provided with an annular dish shaped protrusion 4 . A housing 7 on the end of a horizontal tube 3 carries a lever 16 with a cam face 18 that engages the protrusion 4 to lock the tubes together. The horizontal member 3 can be at any radial orientation relative to the upright tube 2 .
