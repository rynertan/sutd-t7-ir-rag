# Device for transporting a flexible strip.

## Abstract
A device for transporting a flexible strip 11 along a guide 15 by means of conical pressure rollers 35 and 45 , whose apices face each other. The pressure rollers 35 and 45 are loaded by a spring 65, 135 common to both rollers, as a result of which an accurately defined directional force is obtained towards the guide 15 .
