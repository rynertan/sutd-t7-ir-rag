# Shredded tobacco feeding apparatus for cigarette making machine.

## Abstract
A shredded tobacco feeding apparatus for a cigarette making machine id disclosed. It includes a storage reservoir 14 for temporarily storing shredded tobacco, a device 17, 18 for feeding a constant quantitiy of shredded tobacco from the reservoir continuously, a device 20 for classifying the shredded tobacco based on a required grain length, and a device 33 for cutting such classified shredded tobacco with a long grain length, such cut shredded tobacco being joined again with the other classified short shredded tobacco for feeding to a shredded tobacco dispersing device 25 38 .
