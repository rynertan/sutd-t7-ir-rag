# Lathe control system.

## Abstract
Lathe control system for numerically controlling a lathe 10 having a cutting tool 14 attached to at least one movable slide 22 30 , said control system including a velocity or acceleration command signal generator 52 causing the slide 22 30 to accelerate to a predetermined velocity prior to the initiation of thread cutting, and a digital or analog compensation network 84 86 causing the slide 22 30 to follow its associated command signal with zero following error.
