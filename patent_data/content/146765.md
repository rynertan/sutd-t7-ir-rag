# CONTINUOUS KNEADING MACHINE FOR THE PRODUCTION OF PLASTIC CURD CHEESES

## Abstract
A continuous kneading machine for the production of plastic curd cheeses, comprising at least one heating section provided with at least one screw 3, 4 and a plasticising kneading section disposed downstream of the preceding, in which the plasticising kneading section comprises a pair or rotary implements 7 , provided with at least one arm 17 projecting from their axis of rotation.
