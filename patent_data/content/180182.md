# Stuffing apparatus and stuffing method.

## Abstract
Apparatus and method for cleaning residual foodstuff from a disposable stuffing tube 14 while the stuffing tube is in a stuffing position. Just prior to the time when the casing sup ply is about to run out, a pusher 66 is activated to express re sidual foodstuff from the tube and into the casing remaining on the tube, thereby using the residual foodstuff to form encased product.
