# POURING SPOUT

## Abstract
A pouring spout for decanting liquids from cardboard and like containers including a tubular body 10 which is circular in cross section with at least a portion 22 of the length of the body tapering towards one end, means 16 on the smaller of the two body ends for cutting a hole into a container as it is rotated against the container and an aper ture 14 in the body 10 through which liquid in the con tainer may enter the body 10 when a predetermined length 22 of the body 10 is pressed into the hole in the container.
