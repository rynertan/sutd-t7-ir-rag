# PROGRAMMED CONTROLLER

## Abstract
An electronic controller for providing one or more sequ ences of control outputs for a plurality of controlled devices including a pulser which produces pulses at the rate of the frequency of an ac source, a divider for producing spaced pulses preferably at one second intervals, a counter for count ing a large number of these spaced pulses and one or more PROM s for selecting and actuating one or more sequences stored therein and producing controlling outputs on a plural ity of lines to a plurality of controlled devices.
