# Magnetic tape cassette apparatus.

## Abstract
In a magnetic tape cassette apparatus with an elec trical power supply 43, 62 , a power switch 5, 40, 61 for switching the power supply on and off, a capstan 24 for tape transport, and a speed switching element 20, 54, 67 which can be switched between a first and a second position, for switching the capstan drive to a first and a second speed respectively, switching means 12, 14 42, 45 64 are arranged between the power switch and the switching element. The switching means are such that at least during normal operation, each time that the power switch is switched off and is sub sequently switched on, the switching element is set to the first position in response to the actuation of the power switch.
