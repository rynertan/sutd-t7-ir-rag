# MANPOWER DRIVEN VEHICLE

## Abstract
A manpower driven vehicle with the characteristic that it consists essentially of a number of wheels 1, 2 a frame 3 supported by the wheels 1, 2 drive mechanisms 4 to transmit the power to the wheels 1, 2 , consisting mainly of a rod 7 that moves back and forward and a transmission 8 to convert the movement of the rod 7 into a rotation of the wheels 1, 2 and a seat 5 .
