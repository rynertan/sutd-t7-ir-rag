# SHIFT VALVE FOR AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION

## Abstract
A shift valve for an automatic transmission wherein governor pressure acts on a spool in such a direction as to compress a first spring, and throttle representative pressure acts on a plug in such a direction as to compress the first spring. The shift control valve further comprises a second spring arranged to act on one of the plug and the spool in the same direction as the direction in which the throttle repre sentative pressure acts thereon.
