# Plastic bituminous compositions and articles containing them.

## Abstract
Plastic bituminous compositions comprising a 100 30 w of a mixture of 1 45 99.8 w of a bituminous component having a penetration of less than 800 0.1 mm at 25 C, 2 0.1 30 w of a lithium salt of a C₁₀ C₄₀ hydroxy fatty acid, and 3 0.1 25 w of an elastomer and b 0 7 w of a filler have excellent self adhesive properties even when applied on wet or wetted surfaces. They are cold applicable and shear stable.
