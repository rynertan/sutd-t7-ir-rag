# WAVEGUIDE HANGER

## Abstract
A hanger 10 for attaching a length of waveguide 15 of elliptical cross section to a crossbar 30 on an antenna tower 12 comprises a resiliently yieldable and generally U shaped clip adapted to be slipped into straddling relation with the waveguide 15 and simultaneously inserted into a hole in the crossbar 30 with a snap fit.
