# Hair setting method using aminoalkyl substituted polydiorganosiloxane.

## Abstract
The specification describes a hair setting method that employs a hair fixative composition containing an aminoal kyl substituted polydiorganosiloxane as the effective compo nent. The hair fixative composition may be formulated into aerosol, pump spray, lotion, cream or mousse type composi tions for easy application to hair. The aminoalkyl substituted polydiorganosiloxane improves the curl retention of the hair after setting and provides conditioning to the hair.
