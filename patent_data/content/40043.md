# Voltage dependent resistor.

## Abstract
A voltage dependent resistor of the layered structure type comprises a zinc oxide layer adjacent to a metal oxide layer consisting of at least one of cobalt oxide Co₂O₃ , man ganese oxide MnO₂ , barium oxide BaO , strontium oxide SrO , lead oxide PbO or a rare earth metal oxide, and elec trodes applied to opposite surfaces of the zinc oxide layer and the metal oxide layer.
