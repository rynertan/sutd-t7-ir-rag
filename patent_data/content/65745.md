# Syn isomers of 7 substituted 3 cephem 4 carboxylic acid esters, and a process for production thereof.

## Abstract
Syn Isomers of 7 substituted 3 cephem 4 carboxylic acid esters of the formula CHEM wherein R is lower alkanoyloxy C2 C6 alkyl, isobutyryloxymethyl, 2 ethylbutyryloxymethyl or 3,3 dimethylbutyryloxymethyl, or salts thereof, and processes for their preparation and pharmaceutical composition containing them.
