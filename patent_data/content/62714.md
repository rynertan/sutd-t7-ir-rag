# Ashless dispersants for lubricating oils, lubricating oil compositions, additive packages for lubricating oils and methods for the manufacture of such dispersants, compositions and packages.

## Abstract
Lubricating oil dispersants are made by reacting an a alkenyl succinic anhydride, b an alcohol e.g. pentaerythri tol , c a hydroxy substituted amine e.g. tris nydroxy methylaminomethane , d a polyoxyalkylene amine, and d an alkenyl succinimide.
