# Composition for accelerating recovery of function of hematopoietic organs and the use thereof.

## Abstract
A composition for accelerating recovery of a hemato poietic function which is useful for recovering physical strength of patients by orally administering the composition is disclosed. The composition comprises a milt extract and vitamin C and or its salt and does not cause an increase of blood uric acid levels. The use of milt extract and vitamin C for preparing the composition is also disclosed.
