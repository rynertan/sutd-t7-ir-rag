# Magnetic bubble memory device.

## Abstract
A magnetic bubble memory device comprising a holder 16 provided in a main apparatus and a magnetic bubble memory cassette 1 which is inserted into the holder so as to be electrically connected with a bubble actuating circuit provided in the main apparatus 15 . The device further comprises a detector 22, 22a for detecting the operation of ejecting the Cassette from the holder for example, it may detect the movement of a push rod 21 actuating an ejector lever 20 . The detector transmits a signal for stopping the bubble motion in advance of the disconnection of the cassette from the bubble actuating circuit.
