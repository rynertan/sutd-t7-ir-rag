# Article suitable for wiping surfaces.

## Abstract
A wiping cloth or imilar article especially suitable for wip ing glossy hard surfaces to give a streak free result comprises a flexible absorbent substrate of fibrous material, advante geously consisting at least partially of natural cellulosic fibers, treated with a cationic polyacrylamide to enhance its soil cap ture capability. Preferably the article carries a fully formulated streak free cleaning composition.
