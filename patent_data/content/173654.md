# Method of carrying out a treatment on metal pieces with the addition of an added material and with the use of a power laser.

## Abstract
The method allows a treatment cutting, welding, alloy ing or surface hardening, etc. to be carried out on a metal piece 1 with the addition of an added material 4 and with the use of a power laser 7 . The metal piece 1 is heated locally and the added material 4 is brought into a plastic or liquid state by means of laser radiation 2 so as to fuse it with the piece 1 . The added material 4 used is a powder.
