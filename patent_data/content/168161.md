# A folder for surgical sutures.

## Abstract
A pack of needled sutures comprising first, second and third panels 1,3,4 of which second panel 3 is hinged along opposite sides of the other panels 1,4 , the first 1 and second 3 panels having recesses 9,12 which register in the folded pack and the second panel 3 having an incised tab 11 on one side of recess 12 and an aperture 13 on the other side of the recess 12 to receive and grip a curved needle 17 which extends across the recess 12 , the third panel 4 being shaped to engage the first panel 1 and hold the folder closed without obstructing access to the needle 17 .
