# TOOL HOLDER

## Abstract
A tool holder of the two jaw type is provided which has the centering accuracy and gripping power of a three jaw geared chuck, the tool holder including a body 10 having ways formed by a polygonal passageway 16 which is perpendicular to the central axis of the tool holder body. A differential screw 42, 44 mates, at one of its ends, with a nut 32 fixed to the body of the tool holder and, at its other end 44 , with a pair of anvil parts 68, 70 a face of the differential screw and the anvil parts moving toward and away from the central axis of the tool holder along the ways formed by the polygonal passageway and providing the desired gripping of the tool.
