# Hypodermic syringe.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a disposable syringe comprising a barrel 11 in which a piston 12 is provided and to which a needle 13 is or can be connected to the front, a stopper 17 being present in the barrel between piston and needle or needle connection means. The inner wall of the barrel is locally deformed 20,21 between stopper and needle or needle connection so that at the area of the deformation injection liquid can reach the cannula past the stopper and during nurse aspiration the stopper is held, in which body fluid can reach the part of the barrel present behind the stopper.
