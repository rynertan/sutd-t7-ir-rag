# SUBSTITUTED 2 1H QUINAZOLINONE 1 ALKANOIC ACIDS AND ESTERS

## Abstract
The synthesis of substituted 2 1H quinazolinone 1 al kanoic acids and their esters is described. The novel quin azolinones are renal vasodilators and as such reduce vascu lar resistance to renal blood flow. The quinazolinones are useful as cardiovascular agents.
