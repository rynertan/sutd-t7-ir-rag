# Article carrier.

## Abstract
A wrap around type article carrier having parallel top and bottom panels 1 14, 15 , parallel side panels 6, 7 , the side panels 6, 7 being joined to the bottom panels 14, 15 by means of sloping panels 10, 11 , multiple heel receiving apertures 43 48 formed in the sloping panels 10, 11 , a retention tab 49, 50 joined to one of the sloping panels 10, 11 around the periphery of a heel receiving aperture 43 48 , the retention 49, 50 tab being joined to the sloping panel 10, 11 by means of spaced connecting elements c, d .
