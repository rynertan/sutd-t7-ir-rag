# Lambda type suspension system for an overhead contact wire.

## Abstract
A lambda type direct suspending overhead contact system for electric railway comprises two or more pairs of anchor ears E1, E2 connected to a contact wire T and outer and inner suspension wires M2, M1 held by a suspension clamp on a fixed beam at a ratio of a tensile of said outer suspension wire to a tensile of said inner suspension wire of 2.0 to 3.5 1.
