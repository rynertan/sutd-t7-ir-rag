# Low silhouette adjustable latch with secondary lock.

## Abstract
A tension latch, embodying an over center latching prin ciple, includes means for adjusting the latch tension in the fully closed and latched position. The adjustment means screw 52 is readily accessible through a channel like opening formed by the latch handle 30 . The same portion of the latch handle con tains a secondary locking mechanism 64 which is laterally off set from the adjustment means and is activated from the side of the handle, thereby maintaining complete access for tension adjustment.
