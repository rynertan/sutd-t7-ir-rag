# Transportable computer.

## Abstract
A computer which is portable and transportable by a person and which can be used on a table, can be coupled with a support affording the possibility to use the computer in further operating conditions, particularly for use in ve hicles. The support comprises fixing means for fixing it in the cab of a vehicle as well as means for adjusting the com puter, when attached to the support, in height and about an upward and horizontal axis. The support can comprise at least two hinges which can be locked by a single clamping means.
