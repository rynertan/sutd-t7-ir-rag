# Novel aspergillus strain and use thereof.

## Abstract
A novel strain Aspergillus K27 belonging to genus Aspergillus which has the same taxonomical characteristics as those of Aspergillus fumigatus except that 1 its conidiophore is colorless and 2 its growing temperature range is between 10 and 55 C, which produces amylolytic enzymes which can hyd rolyze alpha amylase resistant starch as well as usual starch.
