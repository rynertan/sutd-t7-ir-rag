# Quick heat self regulating electric glow plug heater.

## Abstract
A sheathed electric heater especially of the type used as a diesel engine glow plug has a dual coil heating element including separate tip heater 44 and positive temperature coefficient body control 46 coils thermally isolated from one another by a low resistance connector section made from interengaging small diameter coiled connector portions 52, 60 integral with the tip and body coils and preferably welded together. Preferred material and dimensional specifications and pertinent design considerations are also included.
