# A 32724 antibiotics and process for production thereof.

## Abstract
The A 32724 antibiotics which comprise antibiotic A 32724 mixture of factors A, B and C, antibiotic A 32724 factor A, antibiotic A 32724 factor B and antibiotic A 32724 factor C are prepared by the submerged, aerobic fermentation of Chaetomella raphigera Swift NRRL 12331, optional separa tion and optional isolation. They have antihypertensive activity in mammals.
