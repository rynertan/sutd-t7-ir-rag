# Closure assembly.

## Abstract
An assembly for enclosing a telecommunication cable splice comprising a wraparound sleeve 14 having longitu dinal edges 16 which can be brought together and secured by for example a metal channel 18 . A seal is made between these edges by an internal sealing member 20 which bridges the abutting edges of the sleeve and is urged into contact with the sleeve by expansive urging means 24 between the sealing member 20 and underlying cable splice. The urging means 24 is preferably a foam strip.
