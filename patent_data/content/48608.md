# Panel air filter and packaging.

## Abstract
A replaceable filter cartridge has a pleated paper filter medium 4 mounted within a frame or casing 1 having a closed side and an open side, the open side being covered by a removable protective tray 6 or lid the internal shape of which has been used in manufacture for moulding a sealing surface 14 onto the periphery of the frame or casing, the closed side of the frame or casing being provided with a perforated portion 3 which is also removable for use of the filter cartridge.
