# Direction indicator switch.

## Abstract
A direction indicator switch including a body 11, a rotor 16 carried by the body 11 and movable manually relative thereto from a rest position to either of first and second operative positions on opposite sides respectively of the rest position, a striker collar 13 rotatable in use relative to the body 11 with the steering shaft of a vehicle utilizing the switch, to effect return movement of the rotor 16 from either of said operative positions to said rest position, and, a releasable latch 28 whereby the striker collar 13 is retained in a predetermined angular position relative to the body 11 dur ing assembly of the switch in the vehicle, the latch 28 being released after assembly of the switch to the vehicle to permit the collar 13 to rotate with the steering shaft of the vehicle relative to the body 11.
