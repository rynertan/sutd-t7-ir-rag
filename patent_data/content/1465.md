# Method for the continuous coating of webs having spliced joints.

## Abstract
Method for the continuous coating of webs having spliced joints. Elimination of web splice induced defects in the coating of webs with an aqueous solution of hydrophilic colloids. The webs 17, 10 are butt joined by a splicing tape 16 at their back side and are provided with a hydrophobic coating 18 at their front side at the splicing zone. The invention is particularly advantageous in the manufacturing of photographic material.
