# Vacuum drainage bottle.

## Abstract
A vacuum drainage bottle includes a chamber for receiv ing fluids from a patient and a pressure indicator 9, 21 for measuring the pressure in the chamber. Prior to use, the pres sure indicator is isolated from the chamber by means of a breakable seal. The seal may be in the form of a recessed membrane 43 , which may be ruptured by depressing the pressure indicator, causing a ball 45 to bear against the membrane 43 .
