# Magnesium titanium ferrosilicon alloys for producing compacted graphite iron in the mold and process using same.

## Abstract
Compacted graphite CG cast iron is obtained in the inmold casting process employing as an additive an alloy comprising 1.5 3 percent magnesium, 10 20 percent tita nium, 40 80 percent silicon, 0 2 percent rare earth, 0 0.5 percent calcium, 0 2 percent aluminum and balance iron.
