# Fastening housing for a rail clamp and method of embedding such a housing in a concrete sleeper.

## Abstract
A fastening housing 1 for a rail clamp 8 comprising a carrier plate 2 and a superstructure 3 having cavities 4 for accomodating an end part and a rail clamp 8 , said carrier plate 2 having substantially vertically directed anchoring members 5, 6, 7 extending in a concrete sleeper wherein the housing is made from synthetic resin.
