# Ester lubricants.

## Abstract
The invention relates to novel ester lubricants which can be used in combination with a hydrocarbon base fluid. The ester lubricants are mixtures of esters based on a 2 alkyl substituted propane diol 1,3, a trimethylol alkane C₁ C₄ , optionally a C₅ C₁₅ ether diol and mixture of C₆ C₁₀ and C₁₂ C₁₈ monocarboxylic acids.
