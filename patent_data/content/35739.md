# Fuel tank fitting structure.

## Abstract
A structure for fitting a fuel tank 7 in an automotive vehicle comprises a rear floor panel 1 fixed at a rear portion of the automotive vehicle, a rear seat cross member 10 fixed to the under side of the rear floor panel 1 across the automo tive vehicle, a seat belt anchor fixing cross member 11 fixed to the under side of the rear floor panel 1 across the automo tive vehicle, and a pair of bands 6 suspended between the rear seat cross member 10 and the seat belt anchor fixing cross member 11 for supporting the fuel tank 7 in position between the rear seat cross member 10 and the seat belt anchor fixing cross member 11 .
