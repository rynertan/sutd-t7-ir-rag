# Electrical connector locator plate.

## Abstract
A locator plate for positioning a plurality of different length multi contact insulation displacement connectors for connection to multi conductor flat cables has movable end guides 12, 14 for the connectors and a plurality of shims 16 individually slidable along a shim support rod 15 and pivotable on the shim support rod to a position between the end guides to define the proper spacing between the end guides for each of the connectors.
