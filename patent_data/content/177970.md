# Ethylene oxide adducts, method of producing same and cosmetics and ointments containing same.

## Abstract
Novel ethylene oxide adducts are provided. They have properties suited for their use as base components of cos metics and ointments. Thus, cosmetics and ointments con taining those ethyleneoxide adducts are also provided. There is further provided a method of producing such ethy lene oxide adducts in high yields.
