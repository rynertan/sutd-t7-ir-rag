# OPTICAL CONNECTOR

## Abstract
In an optical connector having a male half 2 with at least one projecting plug 34 connected to an optical fiber, and a female half 1 with at least one sleeve 7 for receiving said plug, said sleeve 7 is mounted in said female half 1 as a separate and exchangeable part so that a sleeve 7 for receiving a second plug 6 connected to a second optical fiber may be replaced by a sleeve for receiving an optoelec tronic device.
