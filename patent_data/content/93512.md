# Catheter guide.

## Abstract
A catheter guide 10 is provided which has a base 12 having a first channel 14 extending thereacross for receiv ing a catheter. An arcuately extending member 22 extends outwardly from the base 12 . A second channel 24 extends along the arcuately extending member 22 for also receiving a catheter. The first and second channels 14, 24 extending respectively across the base 12 and anlong the arcuately extending member 22 can be enclosed or partially enclosed for retaining a catheter within the channels 14,24 and within the catheter guide 10 .
