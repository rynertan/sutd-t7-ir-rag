# 4,7 Dimethyl 2 4 pyridinyl 1,2,4 triazolo 1,5 a pyrimidine 5 4H one.

## Abstract
The compound 4,7 dimethyl 2 4 pyridinyl 1,2,4 triazolo 1,5 a pyrimidin 5 4H one and the pharmaceutically acceptable salts and solvates thereof are analgesic and anti inflammatory agents. Methods for preparing and using the compound are described.
