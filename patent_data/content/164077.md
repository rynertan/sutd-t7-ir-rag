# Pin tractor.

## Abstract
Pin tractors which are used in various printers, typewriters, etc. for use as output devices for electronic computers, especially for personal computers. This pin tractor 1 has a synthetic resin frame 9, 10 around which a belt 6 carrying pins 4 is wound revolvably and a presser member 12 made of synthetic resin supported revolvably by said frame. Blank paper S having feed perforations S1 at its both edges is held between said frame and said presser member. Pins of said belt carrying pins are engaged with feed perforations of blank paper for feeding blank paper. Said presser member has pawl parts 36, 37 said frame has concaves 13 to correspond to said pawl parts. Said pawl parts are fitted in said concaves to keep said presser member opened in the specific degree.
