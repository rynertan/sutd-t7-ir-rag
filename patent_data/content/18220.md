# Butterfly valve.

## Abstract
A butterfly valve comprising a valve body 2 a disk shaped valve member 7 pivotally provided on the valve body 2 , and a seat ring 1 fixed to the valve body 2 , the seat ring 1 having a circumferential protrusion 3 on an inner surface thereof, said protrusion 3 having a seal ing surface 10 to engage the valve member 7 . In order to allow satisfactory closing of the valve the protrusion 3 has a greater width in a position 13 clrcumferentially re mote from the rotation axis of the valve member 7 than adjacent that axis.
