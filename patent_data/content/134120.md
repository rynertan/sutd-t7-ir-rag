# A non adhesive chewing gum base composition, and a non adhesive chewing gum composition.

## Abstract
A chewing gum base composition which does not adhere to dentures, removable and fixed oral prostethic devices, fill ings or natural teeth is disclosed. The chewing gum base composition comprises, in weight percent a an elastomer present in an amount of from 10 to 30 b an elastomer solvent present in an amount of from 2 to 18 c a polyvinyl acetate present in an amount of from 15 to 45 , and preferably having a medium average molecular weight in the range from 15,000 to 30,000 d an emulsifier present in an amount of from 2.0 to 10 e a low molecular weight polyethylene present in an amount of from 0.5 to 15 , and having an average molecular weight of at least 2,000 f a wax present in an amount of from 0.5 to 10 , and having a melting point above about 76 C 170 F and g a plasticizer in an amount of from 10 to 40 the chewing gum base composition either containing no filler or containing no more than 5 filler.
