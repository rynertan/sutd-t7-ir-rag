# EAR DEFENDER AND DIAPHRAGM THEREFOR

## Abstract
An Ear Defender has a cup shaped body 12 in which a cup shaped diaphragm 13 of poor sound conducting material, such as rubber, is located to provide a sound insulating chamber. The lip 16 of the diaphragm is fixed to the rim of the body 12 by annular clamping plate 14 which also provides a retaining edge for a loosly located foamed pad 21 .
