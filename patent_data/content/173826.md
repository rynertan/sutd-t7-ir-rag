# Fibre reinforced plastic core for steering wheels.

## Abstract
A core for steering wheels, comprising a boss 6 equipped with sleeves 7, 8, 9 in a unitary structure, each of the sleeves having a slit 7a in the axial direction thereof, said sleeves further being packed with superposed fiber strands a ring portion 1 and spoke portions 3, 4, 5 connecting said ring portion to said boss, being formed by winding and stretching continuous long staple fiber strands impregnated with a resin to form the superposed strands within said sleeves.
