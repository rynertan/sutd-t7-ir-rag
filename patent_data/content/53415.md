# Method of isolating sterols in commercial quantities from sterol containing material.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a method of isolating sterols in commercial quantities from sterol containing material by subjecting a solution of this material to a column chromatographic separation for isolating cholesterol from cholesterol containing material, silica gel is prefer ably used as column packing and a mixture of, for ex ample, heptane and acetone is preferably used as an eluent.
