# Pressure fixing toner for electrophotography and process for preparation thereof.

## Abstract
A pressure fixing toner suitable for use in electropho tography comprises electroscopic particles of irregular shape and comprising, as a pressure fixing binder compo nent, a composite binder component comprising A a wax component and B a resin component composed mainly of a styrene acrylic copolymer at an A B weight ratio of from 54 46 to 20 80, the wax component A being present in the form of spherical particles having an average particle size of smaller than 10 Âµm in a continuous phase of the resin component B .
