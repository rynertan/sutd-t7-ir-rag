# IMPROVEMENTS IN OR RELATING TO SHELF UNITS FOR REFRIGERATORS

## Abstract
A shelf unit 3 for a refrigerator cabinet includes an air flow passageway 6 connectible to a duct 1 located at the rear of the refrigerator. The flow of air through the passageway 6 is regulated by a flap whose movement is controlled by a thermostat 9 also mounted upon the shelf. The shelf unit, when in position in a refrigerator, enables a user to preset the tempera ture of the zone in the refrigerator cabinet beneath the unit.
