# METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR THE UNIVERSAL RECORDING AND PLAYBACK OF ENCODED DIGITAL VIDEO DATA

## Abstract
Method and apparatus for the recording onto a record ing medium and playback of digital data, such as digitally encoded audio data, such that the recording medium is playable on either of two different commercial television format video type players. For example, according to the present invention an optical disc having digitally encoded audio data recorded thereon may be playable on either a PAL compatible or an NTSC compatible optical video disc player with minimal modification of the player.
