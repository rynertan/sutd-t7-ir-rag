# Method and apparatus for the batchwise coating of articles.

## Abstract
A coating pan 1 having a perforated bottom 2 is rotated over a drying air plenum 6 from a zone 8 of which air is mainly excluded by baffles 7. The sprays 12 spray a bed 4 of the pharmaceutical tablets or like articles to be coated in the zone 8 and the articles are continuously transferred between the spraying zone 8 and the drying zone 6 by rotating the pan.
