# Device for actuating a foot instep presser particularly in ski boots.

## Abstract
The device for actuating a foot instep presser particularly in ski boots comprises a grip element 1 , within easy reach from a ski boot exterior and operating a linkage 2 5, 10 11a, 20,21 placed on the interior of the ski boot. A peculiar aspect of the invention is that the linkage 2 5, 10 11a, 20,21 is made to exert pressure at at least two discrete locations spaced apart along the shell 30 longitudinal direction of the ski boot, with a foot presser 6 located inside the boot shell 30 .
