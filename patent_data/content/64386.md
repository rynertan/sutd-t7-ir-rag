# Silicon carbide electrical insulator material of low dielectric constant and manufacturing method therefor.

## Abstract
A silicon carbide electrical insulator material of low dielectric constant comprising an electrically insulating sin tered body containing silicon carbide as a main constituent and an element providing electrical insulating properties in sintered state, wherein the sintered body comprises an element selected from V family in the periodic table and the carrier concentration within the crystal grain of silicon carbide is 5 10¹⁷ cm ³ or less.
