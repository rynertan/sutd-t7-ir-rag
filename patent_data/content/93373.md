# Method and composition for treating and preventing irritation of the mucous membranes of the nose.

## Abstract
A composition for preventing and treating irritation of the mucous membranes of the nose wherein a tricyclic anti depressant topically applied to the nose is effective prophylactically to prevent irritation and a combination of the tricyclic anti depressant with a vasoconstrictor is effec tive to prevent and to alleviate irritation of the mucous membranes of the nose.
