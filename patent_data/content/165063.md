# Switch incorporating liquid crystal material.

## Abstract
A switch comprises a membrane switch 6, 8, 9, 18 operation of which effects operation of an associated liquid crystal display 12, 13, 14, 18, 20 to display appropriate information, the display comprising a nematic curvilinearly aligned phases liquid crystal material 20 contained in containment means in the form of capsules 22 whereby the display is substantially unaffected by the application of the switch operation pressure thereto.
