# Training means for a weapon.

## Abstract
Training means for shooting with a weapon 1 intended for use with live ammunition. The training means comprises a sub calibre barrel 5 , which is attached inside a holder 6 , which is capable of being locked in the barrel of said weapon 1 . The sub calibre barrel can be zeroed by means of four adjusting elements 18 21 , of which two are spring loaded and are in contact with the sub calibre barrel 5 whereas the other two are capable of radial displacement in relation to the sub calibre barrel by means of individual operating rod 26 and 27 running axially to the sub calibre barrel.
