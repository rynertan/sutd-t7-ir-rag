# Vehicle body floor construction of motor vehicle.

## Abstract
A vehicle body floor construction of a motor vehicle, wherein a tunnel 2 upwardly projecting from the surface of a floor panel 1 and disposed in the longitudinal direction of the vehicle is formed at the central portion in the widthwise direction of the floor panel 1 , a fuel tank 3 is disposed in said tunnel 2 , the position of the upper surface 2A of the top portion of the tunnel 2 on the inboard side of a compartment is upwardly displaced at least on one side of an occupant s seat 4 by bulging out the tunnel 2 or covering the tunnel 2 by a reinforcing member, whereby the upper surface 2A of the tunnel 2 is used as an arm rest, the capacity of the fuel tank 3 is increased.
