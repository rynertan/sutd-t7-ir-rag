# 7 D Mandelamido 3 1 sulfomethyltetrazol 5 YL Thiomethyl 3 cephem 4 carboxylic acid monosodium salt.

## Abstract
7 D Mandelamido 3 1 sulfomethyltetrazol 5 yl thiomethyl 3 cephem 4 carboxylic acid monosodium salt, solvates and hydrates thereof are described for use as antibacterial agents. Processes for the preparation thereof are described, as are pharmaceutical compositions compris ing such compounds.
