# Article clamp.

## Abstract
The present invention provides for a cable clamp for releas ably securement of either round or flat articles, wherein the clamp is made having an integral plastic body with a base 12 a hinge 14 upstanding from one end of the base 12 , a rigid beam 16 cantilever supported by the hinge 14 and a latching member 18 upstanding from an opposite end of the base and formed so as to facilitate selective engagement with the beam 16 .
