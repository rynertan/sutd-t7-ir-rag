# Serial printer.

## Abstract
The present invention discloses a serial printer for reciprocating a carrier 8 along a platen 3 by means of a wire 10 , wherein the wire is connected to a wire retainer 15 , which is a member separated from the carrier, to form a wire route, the carrier being detachably mounted whereby the carrier may be removed with the wire remained stretch ed, maintenance, checking and repair are easily accom plished and initial assembling is accomplished in a very simple manner.
