# A flavoured tablet having a high flavour intensity.

## Abstract
A flavoured tablet having a high flavour intensity is disclosed. The tablet comprises a flavouring composition which contains a flavouring agent and a hydrophilic polym er, the hydrophilic polymer being characterized by a being water soluble, b being soluble in an organic solvent, c having a viscosity below 100 cps, at 25 C in a 10 by weight solution in water, and d being compatible with the flavouring agent.
