# Bag filling closure apparatus.

## Abstract
The disclosure relates to methods and apparatus for clos ing a bag mouth of a bag 18 formed from a heat weldable material after filling the bag through the open mouth thereof. In order to support and present the bag mouth for the closure operation the mouth is supported from within the mouth by flat elements 43 held apart by rams 44 across the mouth to draw the mouth into an elongate form and then the sides of the mouth are pressed together by rams 32 between an elongate heating element 31 and an elongate anvil 37 and the heating element is activated to heat weld the sides of the mouth together to close the mouth.
