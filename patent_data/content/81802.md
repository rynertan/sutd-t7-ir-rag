# Magnetic recording system.

## Abstract
In a video tape recorder of the rotary twohead helically scanning type, a magnetic recording reproducing system provided with a mode judging device Figs.21,23 in which a video signal is recorded onto a recording track A₁, A₂ ... B₁, B₂ ... in a multiplex manner together with a pilot signal f₁ f₄ for use in judgement of the recording time mode and when the record is reproduced the pilot signal is detected to thereby judge the recording time mode.
