# Disposable garments with multiple strand elasticized openings.

## Abstract
A disposal garment 10 having at least one opening 13 elasticized with two or three spaced elastic elements 30 having a cross sectional shape with an aspect ratio of 0.25 to 1, its shortest axis about 0.8 to 3.2 mm long and a cross sectional area of about 0.5 to 8 mm 2 . In particular, a disposable garment 10 having leg openings 13 elasticized with two or three such spaced elastic elements 30 and a waist opening 12 elasticized with a flat elastic ribbon.
