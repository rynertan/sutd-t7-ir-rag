# Cleaning wiper.

## Abstract
A cleaning wiper 10 adapted to automatically maintain a fresh cleaning surface 22 upon each utilization. Supply 28 and take up 34 holders are mounted in a body 12 for hold ing rolls of a cleaning web 16 . The cleaning web 16 , interme diate the supply and take up holders, passes over a support member 18 movably mounted within and accessible through a slot 20 in one side of the body 12 . Utilization of the clean ing wiper 10 causes the support member 18 to be moved from its resiliently biased position within the slot 20 and acti vates an advance linkage. The advance linkage advances the rotation of the take up holder 34 a predetermined amount.
