# Novel derivatives of gamma cyclodextrin.

## Abstract
Novel γ cyclodextrin derivatives which are γ cyclodextrin substituted with C₁ C₆ alkyl, hydroxy C₁ C₆ alkyl, carboxy C₁ C₆ alkyl or C₁ C₆ alkyloxycarbonylC₁ C₆ alkyl or mixed ethers thereof and their preparation. Compositions comprising such cyclodextrin derivatives and an active ingredient which is preferably a drug and the preparation of such compositions.
