# Anchoring system for a buoy, specially a measuring buoy.

## Abstract
An anchoring system for buoys in which an anchoring line 9 is connected to an anchoring line connection member 6 , said member being by at least two connection links 5 connected to the buoy, said connection links having equal length and the connection points q of the links with the anchoring line connection member having a configuration that is congruent to the configuration of the points p, where the links are connected to the buoy.
