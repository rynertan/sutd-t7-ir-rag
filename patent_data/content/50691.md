# Lead cutting machine.

## Abstract
Lead cutting machine for cutting the leads of compo nents mounted on printed circuit boards 83 , this machine including a conveyor mechanism 86, 87 for the boards and a rotating cutting knife 51 fixed on a carriage 38 which is slidably mounted in a horizontal plane and under the path of the boards 83 and which is coupled with the piston 34 of a pneumatically controlled pressurized air cylinder 28 .
