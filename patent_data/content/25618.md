# Method for increasing the stability of an artificial island by means of pre loading.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a method or pre loading the legs 2, 3, 4 of an artifical island 1 to increase the sta bility of the island said preloading being performed by means of ballast 7 17 placed in or on the legs said ballast being preferably located within flexible bags 17, 18, 19, 22, 23, 24, 25, 26, 27, 33, 34, 48, 49, 50, 51 placed upon the trussed members 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16 of the legs or upon a plateau 46 supported by beams 45 . Instead of bags a rigid receptacle formed by plates 37,40 can be used for receiving ballast. Further the hollow tu bular members 52, 53, 54, 55 can be used for receiving liquid ballast.
