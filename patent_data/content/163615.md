# DEVICE FOR CONNECTING A BICYCLE PEDAL TO A CYCLING SHOE

## Abstract
The device is for connecting a bicycle pedal with a cage structure 1 to a cycling shoe 2 with a stud 19 which is attached beneath the sole 18 and has a transverse groove 21 for engaging the rear cross member 3 of the pedal. The device includes a stop member 24, 33 carried by the stud 18 of the shoe 2 and a bolt locking member 9, 31, 34 which is carried by the pedal 1 and is arranged to cooperate with the stop member so as to keep the stud 19 in engagement with the rear cross member 3 of the pedal.
