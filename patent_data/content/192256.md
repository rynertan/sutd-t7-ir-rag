# Magnetooptical storage element.

## Abstract
A magnetooptical storage element K having a record ing layer 1, 3, 6, 7, 8 of a multi layer construction including a reflective layer 8 . The reflective layer 8 is made of an aluminium nickel alloy obtained by adding nickel to alumi nium such that the aluminium nickel alloy contains approxi mately 2 to 10 atomic percent of nickel.
