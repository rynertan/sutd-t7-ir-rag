# Automatic test system.

## Abstract
A plurality of test probes 12 , which may be articulated, are stored in a magazine 10 and can be selectively trans ferred to a test head 14 when it is positioned adjacent the magazine 10 step 1 . A base member 16 supports the head 14 and contacts the probes 12 step 2 . Then by supporting a circuit board 22 in contact with the probes 12 various tests can be effected via the probes 12 step 3 . Finally, the probes 12 can be restored to the magazine 10 step 4 .
