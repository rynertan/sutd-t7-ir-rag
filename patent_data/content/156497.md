# Slide illumination device.

## Abstract
A dias light 36 for a repro camera, adapted to be dis persed on the original holder 32 of said repro camera, the repro camera being provided with a light source for illumin ating the original holder 32 . The dias light 36 comprises a light source 42 and a light sensitive sensor device 43,44 which actuates the light source 42 in response to receiving light from the light source of the repro camera. A dias light of this kind is universally applicable to each existing repro camera without any need to alter the camera.
