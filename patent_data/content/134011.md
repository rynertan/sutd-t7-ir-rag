# FLOORCOVERINGS HAVING BUILT IN HEATER

## Abstract
A floorcovering having a built in heater which comprises a planar sandwich structure including an underlining layer of heat insulating material, at least one heating element provided on one surface of the underlining layer, an electric conductor provided on the one surface of the underlining layer so as to cover the heating element, a surfacing layer overlaying the conductor on one side of the heating element opposite to the underlining layer, and a lead wire for connecting the conductor to the ground.
