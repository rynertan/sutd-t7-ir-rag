# Equipment for loading bars to be machined on a device for presenting and feeding said bars to a machine tool.

## Abstract
A bar lifting equipment in automatic bar loading devices for a machine tool comprises a plurality of arms 14 driven to rotate in a vertical plane parallel to said bars 15 for displacing their end, which end is provided with means for grasping said bars 15 , from a first lower position in which one bar 15 is drawn out a supply source to a second upper position where a rotating comb device 29 is provided. Said comb device 29 being suitable for drawing out said bar and dropping it by gravity onto a channel shaped jaw 16 , said jaw 16 being suitable for introducing said bar 15 into the machine tool.
