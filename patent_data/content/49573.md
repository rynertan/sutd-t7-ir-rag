# Ball valve.

## Abstract
A ball valve includes a valve seat assembly having a lower valve seat 3 , an upper valve seat 2 , packings 4 located in openings formed by apertures in abutting sides of the valve seats 3 and 2 respectively , and annular retainers 5 located in central holes through the packings 4 . Each of the components has a profiled inner surface for slidable contact with the ball 9 controlling communication between the fluid passages 17a, 17b, 17c in the valve box 8 .
