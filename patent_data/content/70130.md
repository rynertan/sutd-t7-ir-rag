# Improvement in or relating to benzoylphenyl urea derivatives.

## Abstract
1 2,6 Dialkoxybenzoyl 3 substituted phenyl ureas are disclosed herein. These compounds are useful both alone and in combination with other herbicides for controlling the growth of undesired plants. These compounds are prefer ably prepared by reacting a 2,6 dialkoxybenzoylisocyanate with a substituted aniline.
