# Video tape recorders.

## Abstract
A video tape recorder includes a rotary magnetic head drum having a rotating member and a magnetic head 9 mounted on the rotating member, a laser element LD mounted on the rotating member, a current source 10 for supplying a constant current to the laser element LD , a circuit for electrically connecting a coil 8 of the magnetic head 9 in parallel with the laser element LD , a photoelec tric conversion member PD fixed in the video tape recorder, and members 12, 15 for optically coupling the laser element LD with the photoelectric conversion member PD .
