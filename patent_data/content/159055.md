# Resilient hinge.

## Abstract
A resilient hinge comprising a first support 10 that can be mounted on a member to be supported, such as the back of a chair, and a second support 10 that can be mounted on a main body, such as the remainder of a chair, wherein said two supports 10 are each connected hingedly to a hood 1 and wherein between each support and the hood two bodies 4 of elastic, resilient material are arranged.
