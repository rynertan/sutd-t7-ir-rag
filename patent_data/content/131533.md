# A polyurethane laminate of contrasting layers and process therefor.

## Abstract
A plurality of polyurethane layers 40, some of which have a contrasting color with respect to an adjacent layer is formed into a laminate. During preparation, each layer 42, 44, 46, is separately formed as by spraying and the contrasting color ensures that the preceding layer can be fully covered. The laminate 40 can be utilized to cover areas or objects which are otherwise difficult to ensure that it has a continuous or com plete coating. The invention is suitable for use as a coating and for the preparation of various in situ articles or enclosures 10, for example a fuel tank, such as commercial and military aircraft.
