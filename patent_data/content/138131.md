# CLOTHES DRYER

## Abstract
A clothes dryer has, within a cabinet, a vacuum cham ber in which a perforated drum is rotatable, a loading door way in the cabinet, through which clothes to be dried can be fed into the drum, being provided with a door which, when closed, seals the vacuum chamber. A motor rotates the drum and a vacuum pump withdraws air and water vapour from the vacuum chamber and the contained drum, electric heating elements in the chamber heating the drum and its contents.
