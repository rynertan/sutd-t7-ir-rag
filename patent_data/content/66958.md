# Endometrial sampling device.

## Abstract
An endomentrial sampling device comprises a handle 12 and a specimen collection probe 14 which has at least one groove 22 adjacent the distal end thereof, the groove 22 having a scraping edge formed by the peripheral surface of the probe 14 and a side wall of the groove 22 , the sidewall intersecting the peripheral surface at an acute angle. A plurality of grooves 22 may be provided, each having scraping edges. The handle 12 has means 46, 52 for determin ing by feel the orientation of the probe when inserted in the uterus of a patient.
