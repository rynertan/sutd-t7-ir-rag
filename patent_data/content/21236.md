# Method for optically assaying liquid samples.

## Abstract
There is disclosed a method for optically assaying a liquid sample which comprises placing the liquid sample in a cell for optical measurement equipped with a stirrer having a thick polygonal prism portion 3 made of a transparent material, said polygonal prism portion 3 of the stirrer being positioned within the parth of light used for the measurement, and measuring an optical property of the sample with stirring by rotation of the stirrer.
