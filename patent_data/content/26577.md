# Dehumidifying device.

## Abstract
For the dehumidification of a cavity preferably the intermediary space 3 between the glass sheets 1, 2 of a window, the dehumidifying device comprises a cell 5, 6,7 shaped as a cartridge to be inserted in an opening in one of the glass sheets and so arranged as to absorb and by electrolysis to dissociate the water molecules from said cavity 3 by application of external energy.
