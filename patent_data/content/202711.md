# Membrane for membrane switches and composing elements thereof.

## Abstract
Membrane for membrane switches and composing ele ments thereof, characterized thereby that it mainly consists of a profiled top layer 2 made of a resilient material and of an underlying supporting layer 1 that is provided with an open ing 3 , said top layer 2 having a recess fitting in said opening 3 and consisting of side walls 5 and a part suspended therebetween having the shape of a spherical segment consti tuting the membrane 6 proper.
