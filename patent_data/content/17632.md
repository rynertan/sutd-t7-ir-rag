# Method and apparatus for adjusting roller rings on a shaft.

## Abstract
Method and apparatus for adjusting roller rings 5 on a roller 4 in exact positions, and in which the apparatus comprises a parallelogram means 11 , an adjustment head 14 having a guide pin 18 adapted to co operate with the corresponding guide bore 17 in each roller ring, and which comprises both a drive mechanism 14 for positively moving the parallelogram means 11 and a locking means 19 for locking the parallelogram means in an adjusted position and a gauge apparatus 16 for exactly gauging the movements of the parallelogram means 11 .
