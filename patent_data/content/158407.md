# WELDING GUN CONNECTOR ASSEMBLY

## Abstract
A welding gun has a connector body 12 formed with passages for supply of gas and coolant and electrical power. Conduits 22, 24, 26 for supplying these services plug into the passages and are sealed by O rings 30, 31 . In order to secure the conduits simply they have overlapping flanges 32, 36 , the uppermost of which is retained by the head of a screw 38 screwed into the connector body 12 . Accord ingly this one screw secures all of the conduits in the con nector body.
