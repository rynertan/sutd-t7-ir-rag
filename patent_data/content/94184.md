# Indoline derivatives.

## Abstract
The invention provides novel spiro oxazolidine 5,3 in doline 2,2 ,4 triones of formula I bearing substituted benzyl or cinnamyl on the indoline nitrogen, together with salts and non toxic, biodegradable precursors thereof. The compounds are potent inhibitors of the enzyme aldose reductase and are useful in treating or preventing certain diabetic complications. The inven tion also provides pharmaceutical compositions containing, and processes for the manufacture of, the compounds of formula I and their salts and derivatives.
