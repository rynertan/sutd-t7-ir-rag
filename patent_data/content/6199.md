# Hydrophilic separating carrier and preparation thereof.

## Abstract
A hydrophilic separating carrier is a porous spherical gel having primary hydroxyl groups and is produced by an aqueous suspension homopolymerization of pentaerythritol dimethacry late or an aqueous suspension copolymerization of pentaery thritol dimethacrylate and a methacrylate monomer having hy drophilic group and or hydrophilic convertible group.
