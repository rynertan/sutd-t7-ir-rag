# Electrical edge connector.

## Abstract
An electrical edge connector suitable for Liquid Crystal Displays has contacts 8 arranged at a housing slot 3 with contact tails 9, 11 extending in a row, transversely of the slot 3 , the tails 9, 11 being connected to the contacts 8 by resil ient bight portions 9 of U form arranged in a row to allow for resilient flexure between the tails 11 and the contacts 8 .
