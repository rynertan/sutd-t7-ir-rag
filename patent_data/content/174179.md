# CORSET

## Abstract
A corset 10 comprises an inner waist encircling struc ture 11 in the form of a band like strip 13 of elastic material releasably securable about a wearer s waist, and an outer waist encircling structure 12 of flexible, inextensible material 21, 17 of variable length and an inflatable bag 25 supported by at least one of said structures 11, 12 between the two structures 11, 12 , means 26, 27, 28 being provided for inflating the bag 25 to apply inward pressure to the wearer s abdomen wall.
