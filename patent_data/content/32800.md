# Reticular web having reinforced selvages and method and apparatus for manufacturing the same.

## Abstract
A reticular web 9 of thermoplastic resin composed of split or slit fibres comprises a pair of laterally spaces selvages 24, 25 and a middle portion 23 therebetween. In order to dimensionally stabilise the selvages 24, 25 each selvage 24, 25 has at least one dimensionally stable thread 26, 27 attached thereto along the length thereof. The invention also includes method and apparatus for producing the web 9 .
