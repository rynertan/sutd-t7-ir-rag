# Fastener applying apparatus.

## Abstract
An apparatus for applying a fastener to a fabric includes a pair of levers 49, 50 disposed pivotably one on each side of a stationary die 52 for interlocking a punch driver 17, 21, 23 and an actuator 34, 39, 41, 47 therefor in such a manner that the former can be activated by the latter only after the levers 49, 50 have been lowered while supporting the fabric horizontally thereon.
