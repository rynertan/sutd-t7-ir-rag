# Bearing support structure.

## Abstract
The intermediate case 18 for turbo fan engines has a bearing support cone 38 extending forwardly from a support flange 28 in alignment with struts 24 in the case to provide a secure support for the bearing 8 for the high pressure shaft 6 . To accommodate the power shaft assem bly which extends through the cone 38 the latter has a bulge 50 in which is mounted a support cylinder 52 for the bearings 54, 56 for the power shaft 14 , this bulge 50 providing a support structure for the cylinder 52 and clearance for a bevel gear 12 on the power shaft 14 journaled in the cylinder 52 .
