# Vial suspender.

## Abstract
A vial suspender comprising a planar base plate and a suspending portion including a suspending strip and a suspending ring 4 and joined to the center of a base plate by a hinge portion 5 . The suspending portion is foldable at another hinge portion 6 and is accommodated in a folded position in recesses 2a, 2b formed in the base plate. The base plate is fastened to the bottom of a vial V with a heat shrunken resin film. When pulled out from the base plate to an unfolded position, the suspending portion has a sufficient length to support the vial V in a substantially vertical, inverted position.
