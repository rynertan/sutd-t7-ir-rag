# Ski holding device.

## Abstract
A ski holding device to be utilized for carrying skis, especially skis for downhill running. According to the invention, the ski holding device comprises an upwardly open box 2 to be placed en the outside of a boot, which box has a horizontal cross section, which as to width and, respectively, length exceeds the thickness and, respectively, width of the rear ends of a pair of skis, in which box the rear ends of a pair of skis are intended to be slipped down and supported by the bottom portion 8 of the box 2 .
