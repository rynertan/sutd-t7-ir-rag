# FLUID FILTER CARTRIDGE AND METHOD OF ITS CONSTRUCTION

## Abstract
An insert 10 for placement between concentric cylindrical filter elements 22, 24 comprises in a one piece construction a tubular cylindrical body 34 and pairs of parallel slits in the body each forming there between a strip 36 of material connected to the re mainder of the body at the ends of the strip. The strips are bowed inwardly away from the remainder of the body between their ends to form protrusions extending transversely from the remainder of the body.
