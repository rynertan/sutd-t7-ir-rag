# Machine tool.

## Abstract
A machine tool has a machine tool body 3 , a spindle 1 having a supporting portion 2 and being rotatably sup ported by the machine tool body 3 , and a tool holder 5 having an inserting portion 6 which is attached to the supporting portion 2 . A ceramic layer 10, 11 is provided on the supporting portion 2 of the spindle 1 and or the inserting portion 6 of the tool holder 5 .
