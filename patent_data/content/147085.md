# Substituted trans 1.2 diaminocyclohexyl amide compounds.

## Abstract
Substituted trans 1,2 diaminocyclohexyl amide com pounds demonstrating selective opioid receptor binding possess utility as analgesic, diuretic, and psychotherapeutic agents. A method of preparing the compounds, pharmaceu tical compositions employing the compounds, and a method of alleviating pain employing the compounds are also disclosed.
