# USE OF N 2 AMINOCYCLOHEXYL BENZENEACETAMIDES IN THE TREATMENT OF CEREBRAL ISCHEMIA

## Abstract
N 2 Aminocyclohexyl benzeneacetamides, e.g. 5α, 7α, 8β or 3,4 dichloro N methyl N 7 1 pyrrolidinyl 1 oxaspiro 4.5 dec 8 yl benzeneacetamide and or trans 2 3,4 dichlorophenyl N methyl N 2 1 pyrrolidinyl cyclohexyl benzeneacetamide, are of utility in the treatment of cerebral ischemia conditions.
