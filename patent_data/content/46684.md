# Apparatus for removing particles from a flexible member.

## Abstract
An apparatus in which a cleaning material is transported into contact with particles adhering to a flexible member 10 in a cleaning zone. The flexible member 10 is maintained at a pre selected tension by means 16 . During cleaning, the flexible member 10 is deflected by means 58 for transport ing the cleaning material into contact with the particles adhering to the flexible member 10 to form an extended cleaning zone.
