# Frame Structures.

## Abstract
A three way joint for a frame structure comprises a location piece 12 secured to one end of a first transverse member 10 and having two spaced protrusions 14,14 extending axially of the first transverse member 10 and a screw threaded nut not shown a second transverse member 20 to engage over the location piece 12 and having apertures to receive the protrusions 14,14 and a further aperture 24 and an upright frame member 26 having apertures 30 therein to receive the protrusions 14,14 and a further aperture 32 to receive a bolt, the bolt passing through the apertures 32,24 and engaging in the nut to clamp the three members 10,20,26 together. A reinforcing member 34 may be included.
