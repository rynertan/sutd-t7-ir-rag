# Thin elastomeric seat assembly.

## Abstract
The disclosure relates to a vehicle seat assembly A comprising a tubular frame means 10 for a seat 12 and backrest 14 , a seat 12 and backrest 14 comprised of side by side elastomeric filaments 50 prestretched across side frames 20,22 and a backrest frame 30 and secured to the frame portions, and a cover means 16,18 to cover the seat 12 and backrest 14 whereby a thin seat is provided.
