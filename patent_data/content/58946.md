# Coextruded chewing gum containing a soft gum core portion.

## Abstract
A coextruded chewing gum is provided which in cludes an extruded center portion surrounded by and bonded to an extruded outer shell portion. The center portion of the coextruded chewing gum will be of a ge nerally soft consistency, substantially softer than that ordinarily employed in chewing gums of conventional structure, while the outer shell portion will provide a protective layer or cating for the core portion to inhibit sweating and stickiness and thereby allow for use of conventional wrapping machines.
