# Apparatus for measuring brain activity.

## Abstract
Apparatus for measuring mental activity as an indication of blood flow rate comprises a meter having a finger receiving socket 463 between rods or optical fibres 461, 462 , one of which is adjacent a light source 468 and the other of which is adjacent a photo receptor 470 . A scale 445 having a needle 446 is present and is calibrated by knobs 447,448 to a minimum value.
