# High voltage thyristor valve.

## Abstract
A high voltage thyristor valve 30 of the air insulation type erected upright on a valve hall 32 is disclosed, which comprises a thyristor valve body 38 erected in the valve hall and a plurality of reinforcing means 34 stretched between the thyristor valve body 38 and inner walls of the valve hall, each of the reinforcing means including a wire rope 46 and an insulator 51 .
