# MOBILE PHASE SELECTOR DEVICE FOR A CHROMATOGRAPHIC APPARATUS

## Abstract
A device for the automatic selection of the mobile phase to be sent to a chromatographic apparatus, particularly HPLC. The reservoirs S1 S8 containing the different fluids are connected to the lateral channels 33 of a single mani fold 30 through solenoid valves EV1 EV8 controlled by the central processing unit C.P.U. of the chromatographic ap paratus.
