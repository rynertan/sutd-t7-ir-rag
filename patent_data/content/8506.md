# HERBICIDAL HETEROCYCLIC COMPOUNDS AND COMPOSITIONS AND METHODS FOR THEIR USE

## Abstract
The invention provides a method of combating weeds which comprises applying to the locus which is infested or liable to be infested with them an effective amount of A bromoxynil, B ioxynil, C CMPP and D linuron and or bentazone. The invention also provides a herbicidal composi tion comprising from 0.05 to 99 by weight of A , B , C and D in association with a suitable carrier and or surface active agent. Also provided are novel salts of bentazone with an alkylamine of 8 to 18 carbon atoms.
