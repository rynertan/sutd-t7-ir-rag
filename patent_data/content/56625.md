# BOLT WITH AN ANCHOR

## Abstract
A bolt with an anchor usable in the case where the threaded portion of the bolt is covered by a casing. The bolt with an anchor according to the present invention comprises a stem fixed to the end surface of the threaded portion of the bolt and a flexible anchor like locking member fixed to the stem so as to be expandable outwards when the bolt is loosened or unscrewed, in addition to the bolt. The anchor like locking member is made of a synthetic resin by molding with the stem implanted within the minimum radial dimen sion portion of the locking member.
