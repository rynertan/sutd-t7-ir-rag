# APPARATUS FOR AUTOMATICALLY INSERTING ELECTRONIC PART

## Abstract
An apparatus for inserting a plurality of kinds of electronic parts into a predetermined position of a printed board. An elec tronic part which is successively supplied from an electronic part supplying means V is fed to a predetermined supply po sitioning end 3 by a feeding means. An insertion unit INS carries the electronic part from this position to a predeter mined specific position thereby inserting the electronic part into the printed board. The printed board is held and posi tioned by an X Y table T . At a position corresponding to said specific position, the apparatus has a clinching means C for clinching the leads of the electronic part which is inserted into the printed board and a control means CON which controls a predetermined operation of each means.
