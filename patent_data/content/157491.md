# DRY MIX FOR PREPARATION OF PIE AND PASTRY FILLINGS

## Abstract
A dry particulate mix comprised of a sugar component consisting solely or essentially of granular sucrose, a starch component, acidulants and additional minor amounts of appro priate flavorants, colorants and the like, which can be hydrated in a room temperature or cold aqueous medium to directly and rapidly form, without need for heating or reliance upon an inde pendent or auxiliary gelation system, a firm, gelled mass suit able for use as a filling for pies, tarts or other pastry products. The starch component comprises a quick setting, cold water swellable starch, preferably derived from corn starch, pos sessing specified properties of solubility, pH, gel formation and gel strength. Also disclosed is a dry mix particularly adapted for preparation of a lemon pie filling.
