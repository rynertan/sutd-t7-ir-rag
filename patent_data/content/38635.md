# Resin composition for powder coating.

## Abstract
A resin composition for powder coating consists essen tially of A 60 to 97 by weight of a polyester resin having an acid value of 15 to 200 mgKOH g and a softening point of 80 to 150 C, and B 40 to 3 by weight of a glycidyl containing acrylic polymer unmodified or modified with a monobasic acid, and provides cured film coatings having good surface characteris tics and weatherability.
