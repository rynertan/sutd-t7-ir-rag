# Esters of 1,4 dihydro 2,6 dimethyl 3 alkoxycarbonyl or alkoxyalkoxycarbonyl 4 substituted phenyl pyridine 5 carboxylic acid.

## Abstract
Esters I Ar 3 nitrophenyl or 2,3 dichlorophenyl, A C₂ C₆ alkylene, R C₁ C₆ alkyl optionally C₁ C₆ alkoxy monosub stituted, R₁ C₁ C₄ alkyl have antihypertensive activity and are effective against coronary heart diseases, They are pre pared starting from the aldehyde ArCHO and esters of aceto acetic acid and 3 aminocrotonic acid, Pharmaceutical prepara tions containing them are also described.
