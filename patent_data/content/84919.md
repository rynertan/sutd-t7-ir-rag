# Solder bonding process.

## Abstract
Process for locating solder on a precalculated position on an electrical terminal. Metal strip stock 10 coated with a thin layer 15 of solder is fed through a strip guide for precisely positioning the metal strip stock 10 between a pair of rollers 16, 18 . A solder wire 32 is cold bonded to the metal strip stock 10 and the strip stock 10 , is subsequently stamped into a terminal.
