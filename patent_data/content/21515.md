# A starch product suitable for use in drilling muds, a method for producing the same, a drilling mud containing said product and a method for drilling a shaft.

## Abstract
The starch product is obtained by drying the debris recovered from peeling potatoes. The production is preferably performed by washing the potatoes and peeling them 51 , yielding rasped potatoes and peel debris. A great part of the water contained in said peel debris is removed in a decanter and the remainder is subsequently dried yielding the product.
