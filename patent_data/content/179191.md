# ROTARY TYPE INJECTION MOLDING MACHINE

## Abstract
This invention relates to a rotary type injection molding machine in which plural sets of molds 12, 17 are set on a turntable 5 provided on a machine bed 3 , and molding of articles by injection of synthetic resins and releasing of molded articles are carried out at different positions. The molding machine is designed so that even if the mold 12, 17 is provided with a runner passage 11 , the injection apparatus can be opposed to the clamping direction to cast the resins without being affected by the runner passage 11 , and even in molding articles having threads internally thereof, a releasing means need not be provided for each mold.
