# Two piece retainer for epicyclic transmission.

## Abstract
An epicyclic transmission having first and second con fronting surface regions 72, 53 and cyclindrical rollers 80 for transmitting torque therebetween includes a two piece re tainer 100, 102 for axially and radially positioning the rollers relative to the confronting surface regions. A first retainer piece 100 includes a top 106 and bottom 104 surface in terconnected by a plurality of bars 108 shaped and arranged into pairs 114 for rotatably supporting the rollers so that one end face of the rollers contacts and is supported by the bottom surface. A second retainer piece 102 confronts the top sur face 106 so as to support an opposite end face of the rollers.
