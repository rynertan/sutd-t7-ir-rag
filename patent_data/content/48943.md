# Oxygenator.

## Abstract
A hollow fiber type oxygenator comprising a housing 1 having an inlet port 5 and an outlet port 6 for oxygen and an inlet port 7 and an outlet port 8 for the blood, and a plurality of hollow fibers 2 for gas exchange disposed inside the said housing along the longitudinal direction thereof. In the said housing is formed an inward projection 9 midway in the longitudinal length thereof for fastening the bundle of hollow fibers, an there is formed a liquid passage 10 for discharging the water at the inward projection.
