# Horticultural enclosure and method for making it.

## Abstract
A horticultural enclosure that comprises a translucent flexible cover 10 of plastics to which narrow strips 12 of the same material are welded at intervals to form rows of pockets 14 . The cover is fitted on a frame, the interconnect ing members 20 of which are received in the pockets, the tension in the cover being such as to hold the frame together without any additional fastening means. The cover can readily be made by a continuous method.
