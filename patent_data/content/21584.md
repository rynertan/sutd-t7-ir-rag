# Motorised vehicle for effecting a spraying operation.

## Abstract
A motorised vehicle 100 for effecting a spraying operation onto an elongate structure e.g. a roadway crash barrier as the vehicle travels alongside said structure has an arm 105 extendible transversely of the vehicle and provided with spray means 106 for effecting said spraying operation associated with means 118 or 119 for following the elongate structure so that the spray is always directed towards the structure. The vehicle also has a tank 107 for holding a medium to be sprayed by the spray means 106, and means 108 or 109 for supplying said medium under pressure to the spray means.
