# Mixtures containing nitrilotriacetic acid and iminodiacetic acid or salts thereof as a sequestering agent.

## Abstract
A sequestering agent useful in chelation of calcium and or magnesium ions in aqueous solution comprises by weight from 2 percent to 20 percent iminodiacetic acid or a water soluble salt thereof and from 98 percent to 80 percent of nitrilotriacetic acid or a water soluble salt thereof. The sequestering agents of the invention are particularly useful as detergent builders.
