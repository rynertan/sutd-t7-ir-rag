# Closure.

## Abstract
A threaded thermoplastic closure 10 having a free float ing liner 15 is disclosed. To prevent backoff of the closure 10 from a container C , studs 20 are provided on the top wall 12 of the closure 10 which anchor the closure 10 to the liner 15 , thus utilizing the resistance to rotation present between the liner 15 and the container lip 80 .
