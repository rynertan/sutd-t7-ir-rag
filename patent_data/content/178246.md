# Capping method and apparatus for a beverage vendor.

## Abstract
The beverage vendor has a cup capping mechanism 20 in which a cap 70 is removed from a remote location to a stationary cup station 16 , placed on the cup 18 and then pressed down onto the rim 30 of the cup 18 by a sliding motion over the upper surface of the cup 18 beginning at one side and progressing to the other. A locking mechanism is provided which prevents opening of the cup station delivery door 56 during the capping operation.
