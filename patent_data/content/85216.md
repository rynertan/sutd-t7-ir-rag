# REFUSE COLLECTION AND COMPACTION VEHICLE

## Abstract
A refuse collection and compaction vehicle 10 has a chassis of a standard type of less than 2.25 metres in width, and carries a refuse compaction and collection body 13 having a pivotally mounted tailgate assembly 14 . The tailgate incorporates a sliding member 20 driven by power means 22 and carrying a compactor 23 driven by second power means 24 to pivot relative to the sliding member. The weight and capacity of the body are selected so that the maximum laden weight of the vehicle does not exceed 7,500 kilograms.
