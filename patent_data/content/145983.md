# Digital radio relay equipment and method.

## Abstract
Radio relay equipment for fully digitalized transmission network is disclosed. In the equipment, a received auxiliary signal can be by passed from receiver RX to transmitter TX via a by passing means 7, 8 provided in an auxiliary unit 4 when no additional auxiliary signal is input to the equipment. When an auxiliary signal B , for example, order wire signal from the telephone equipment, is input, a switch ing circuit 8 operatively disconnects the by passing means 7, 8 and switches the line to the additional auxiliary signal. The by passing means 7, 8 also operates according to a signal regarding failure of the built in circuit or power sup ply of the equipment.
