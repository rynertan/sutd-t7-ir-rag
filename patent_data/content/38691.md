# Impact printer including a replaceable print wheel.

## Abstract
An impact printer, of the type wherein a print wheel 1 is rotated to a print position by a motor 11 , and printing is caused by impacting a particular character 47 on the print wheel by a print hammer 41 , has the print wheel drive means 25 and the print hammer mechanism located on opposite faces of the print wheel, which greatly facilitates insertion of print wheels in the printer, and removal therefrom.
