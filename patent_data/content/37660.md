# An electric kettle.

## Abstract
An electric kettle comprises a bowl 1 closable by a lid 2 . An electric element 3 is connected to the lid 2 and is thereby suspended within the bowl 1 . A spout 6 is provided for pouring and through which the bowl 1 can be filled with water. Means 4 are also provided for the connection of the element 3 to an electricity supply.
