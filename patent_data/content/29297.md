# Electrode with disposable interface member.

## Abstract
An electrode for epidermical use for stimulation, ionto ploresis, monitoring and grounding electrcal contact applica tions to the skin may be provided having a self adhesive, dis posable, skin interface portion 11 and a non disposable backer portion 13 which is easily strippable from the skin in terface portion 11 whereof the interface portion 11 may be a cohesive and electrically conductive material having adhe sive properties and the backer portion 13 may be a durable, flexible and resilient material to which an electrical connection may be made to the interface portion 11 , the backer portion 13 and interface portion 11 being cleanly strippable from one another after use.
