# DEVICE FOR SUPPORTING A ROLL OF MATERIAL TO A VEHICLE

## Abstract
A device 130 for supporting a roll 134 of material to a vehicle 34 utilizing a first and second arm 132 140 having a tensioning mechanism 40 for tightening the roll 134 of material. The device 130 also includes a support bracket 144 which adjustably holds the second arm 140 in relation to the first arm 132 .
