# Multi purpose fan.

## Abstract
A multi purpose fan including an electrically driven fan blade assembly 25 , a heating coil 31 positioned in the front of the blade assembly and rotatable air directing means 19 which in use causes a gyratory exeunt air current. The fan also includes a micro processor operatively associated with temperature sensors 71,72 and control means to effect automated use of the fan in either a cooling or a heating mode.
