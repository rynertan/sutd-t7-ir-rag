# Internal shoe drum brake.

## Abstract
An internal shoe drum brake has a pair of shoes 1, 2 mounted on a backplate 3 , and a mechanical shoe expansion mechanism which includes a lever 9 movable angularly in a plane parallel to the backplate. An abutment device 13 is mounted on the shoe 2 and is retained on the shoe by a separate shoe hold down device 14 , the abut ment device extending to a position adjacent said lever to provide a backstop for the latter in order to set a predeter mined retracted position for the lever.
