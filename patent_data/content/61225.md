# Stabilization ballast for operating a gas and or vapour discharge lamp.

## Abstract
The invention relates to an inductive stabilization ballast provided with a connecting plate 1 . In accordance with the invention, the connecting plate 1 is provided at the side facing an electric coil 2 of the stabilization ballast with an insulating plastics material layer 5 which by means of conical feet 5a, which cooperate with likewise conical holes 8 to 23, inclusive in the connecting plate 1 , is bonded to the connecting plate 1 .
