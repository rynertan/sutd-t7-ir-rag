# Guide device for guiding a movable part of an apparatus relative to a fixed part of the apparatus.

## Abstract
A guide device for guiding a movable part 14 of an ap paratus relative to a fixed part 12 of the apparatus, includes a first guide rail 16 adapted to be provided on the movable part and a second guide rail 16 adapted to be provided on the fixed part. Bearing means comprising a plurality of roller el ements 22 disposed in a roller support 20 is disposed in a longitudinal groove between the first and second guides 16 and movable relative thereto. Biasing means 28 is provided which biases the bearing means towards a predetermined posi tion.
