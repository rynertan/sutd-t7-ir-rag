# Exercise apparatus.

## Abstract
This invention relates to physical exercise apparatus providing variable resistance to the user comprising a weight stack 65 movable along a vertical guide 63 and a carriage 71 movable along another guide 85 , the guides being disposed at a predetermined angle to each other and the carriage and the weight stack being connected to each other through a rigid link 70 . The carriage is movable by the user against a variable force dependent on the position of the weight stack in its guide, the angle between the guides and the weight of the stack.
