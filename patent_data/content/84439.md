# Novel 6 bromocholesterol derivatives.

## Abstract
Novel 6 bromo derivatives of cholesterol have the formula Such compounds are prepared from the known 6 iodocholesterol by treatment with cuprous bromide. These compounds, labelled with radioisotopes of Br 82 or Br 77, are localized in the adrenal, mammary and ovary tissue of female mammals and in the adrenal or prostate tissue of males when administered to such individuals. This provides a method for imaging adrenal, ovary or prostrate tissue which is superior to use of the prior art 6 iodo cholesterol.
