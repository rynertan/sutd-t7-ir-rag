# Guar gum food bar.

## Abstract
Disclosed is a ready to eat high carbohydrate, low fat, high guar gum snack bar composition for a Type II diabetic comprising 50 75 carbohydrates, 10 15 protein, 8 15 fat and 8 12 guar gum. Snack bars are to be consumed before or during meals to reduce the insulin in the blood stream, permitting smooth blood sugar changes, and gradu ally reduce lipid fat storage.
