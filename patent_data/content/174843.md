# Sewing machine.

## Abstract
A sewing machine for forming stitches in a substrate 16, 116 , for example in forming stitches in tissue during surgery, comprises a needle 6 106 20, 30 for passing thread 9,109,24 into the substrate from one side thereof at a first location and for withdrawing the thread from the substrate at a second location spaced from the first location. The needle is removably operable solely from the said one side of the substrate. A stapling machine is also disclosed operating on similar principles for similar purposes.
