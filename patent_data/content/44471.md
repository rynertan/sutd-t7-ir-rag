# A separator electrolytic cell.

## Abstract
A separator electrolytic cell is disclosed which comprises a first electrode compartment 24 surrounded with a releasable wall plate 15 and a glove shaped finger 19 and a second electrode compartment 25 surrounded with remaining cell walls, a cell top cover 16 and the glove shaped finger 19 . A sheet like separator 7 is immediately installed to the cell without any peculiar processing and the cell facilitates assembly and disassembly and provides tight sealing.
