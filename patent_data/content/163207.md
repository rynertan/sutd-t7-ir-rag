# Focusing head for a laser beam cutting machine.

## Abstract
Focusing head 1 for a laser beam cutting machine on which the said head is designed to turn, in relation to the machine structure 4 , round two axes combining to form an angle, the said head consisting of two portions 15, 24 one of which 24 is provided with an output nozzle 43 and a focusing lens 42 and moves axially in relation to the other by means of a servomechanism 49 controlled by a position sensor 46 in sliding contact with the sheet material 3 being cut, the said sensor 46 being tubular, being assem bled in sliding manner on to the end of the said nozzle 43 and constituting a guide for a cover gas.
