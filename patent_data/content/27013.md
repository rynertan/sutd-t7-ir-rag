# INSECTICIDAL SYNERGISTIC MIXTURES OF 0,0 DIETHYL 0 3,5,6 TRICHLORO 2 PYRIDINYL PHOSPHOROTHIOATE AND 3 PHENOXYBENZYL CIS TRANS 2,2 DIMETHYL 3 2,2 DICHLOROVINYL CYCLOPROPANE CARBOXYLATE AND METHOD OF KILLING AND CONTROLLING INSECTS

## Abstract
Insecticidal compositions containing a mixture of 0,0 di ethyl 0 3,5,6 trichloro 2 pyridinyl phosphorothioate and 3 phenoxybenzyl cis trans 2,2 dimethyl 3 2,2 dichloro vinyl cyclopropane carboxylate are disclosed. Such composi tions are useful in the kill and control of insects, particularly in sects of the Lepidoptera order and especially of the genus He liothis.
