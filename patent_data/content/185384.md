# Fluid jet printing device.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a fluid jet printing device A to U having an inlet 14, an outlet nozzle 13 and a valve 3, 5 located between the inlet and the outlet. The valve comprises a movable actuation member 3 cooperating with a valve seat. For enhancing the drop generation frequency, a dia phragm like partition wall PW is arranged between the inlet and the outlet. Said partition wall includes the valve seat VS.
