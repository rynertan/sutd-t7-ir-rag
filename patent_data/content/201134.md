# DEODORANT FORMULATION WITH MODULATING EFFECT ON PERSPIRATION

## Abstract
A deodorant formulation with a modulating effect on perspiration is disclosed, using microcapsules of tannic acid and or derivatives thereof as active astringent and de odorant agents, with the preferable addition of perfume and anti bacterial agent, in such a way so as to obtain an effec tive sustained action in time, and modulated in relation to the extent of the perspiration of the person.
