# Catheter.

## Abstract
A nutritive catheter is assembled by attaching a head 1 to the distal end of a main tubing having a duct 10 therethrough, the head 1 being formed from a composition comprising a flexible synthetic resin and a finely divided metal material in a ratio by volume of resin to metal mate rial of at least 2 and having a specific gravity of at least 2. An opening 2 is provided in the head in communication with the tubing duct 10 .
