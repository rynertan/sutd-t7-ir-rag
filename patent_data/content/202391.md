# Flexible packaging apparatus and method.

## Abstract
Methods and apparatus are provided for the continuous and rapid production of a plurality of flexible packages 54 containing a non solid substance such as an ointment, by continuously forming and vacuum sealing the packages from two webs 16,18 of material in the nip 42 of cooperating roller surfaces. The packages each contain a precise quantity of medication 32 which may be, for example, the kind of medication which is self administered by the user by transdermal application. The method and apparatus herein joins together two specially prepared webs 16,18 of material continuously drawn from supply rolls 20,22 while at the same time incorporating and vacuum sealing a desired quantity of the medication 32 therebe tween.
