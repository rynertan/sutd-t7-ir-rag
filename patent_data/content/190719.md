# PUNCTURING APPARATUS

## Abstract
A transmission transducer 9 34 produces an ultrasonic wave and applies it to the blood vessel 12 , and a reception transducer 9 52 provided at a puncturing needle 1 receives the ultrasonic wave reflected by the blood vessel 12 through the puncturing needle 1 . When the reception transducer 9, 52 receives the reflected ultrasonic wave, the puncturing needle 1 faces the blood vessel 12 .
