# Method and kit for serum pretreatment for digoxin assay, and digoxin concentrate obtained thereby.

## Abstract
Chromatographic separation is used to obtain a digoxin concentrate from a serum sample for use in an assay for digoxin. Serum is passed through a column containing silica gel alkylated with lower alkyl groups. The column is then washed and the digoxin eluted with aqueous methanol to pro vide the digoxin concentrate.
