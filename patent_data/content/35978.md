# PROCESS FOR OBTAINING BAKING PRODUCTS, AND PRODUCTS THEREBY OBTAINED

## Abstract
A baking process, wherein through use of amylaceous or starchy products, absolutely free from wheat, barley, oat and rye gluten, that is through use of amylaceous or starchy sub stances deriving in particular from rice, maize, soy bean and potatoes, products are obtained of the type of bread, bread sticks and the like, absolutely free from gluten which is harm ful to the health of people suffering from coelical disease or nephrosis. Products obtained through such process.
