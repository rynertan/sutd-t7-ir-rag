# Blends of poly p methylstyrene with polyolefins.

## Abstract
There is provided a blend of poly p methyl styrene with small amounts of poly C₂ C₄ olefin, olefin copolymer or styrene butadiene rubber. The blend can be formed into shaped articles, such as food containers, which can be cross linked by irradiation at low doses. Such articles are resistant to microwave oven conditions.
