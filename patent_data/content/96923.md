# Stone trap for combine harvesters.

## Abstract
A combine harvester comprising a crop elevator 5 , a threshing mechanism 11 to which in use the crop elevator feeds crop material, and a removable stone trap 24 located between the crop elevator and the threshing mechanism and having an inlet 40 disposed generally at the transition be tween the crop elevator and the threshing mechanism, the stone trap being supported in the operative position by first releasable pivot means 53 and being retained in the operative position by latch means 49 , the arrangement being such that on release of the latch means the stone trap pivots forwardly of the machine about the first pivot means whilst still being sup ported by the first pivot means, the latter being disengaged on final removal of the stone trap from the machine.
