# New derivatives of alkanediols having hypolipidaemic activity, methods for their preparation and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.

## Abstract
Alkanediols derivatives having hypolipidaemic activity, of general formula CHEM and salts therewith of pharmaceutically acceptable acids, wherein R and R1 independently represent a hydrogen atom or a straight or branched alkyl group having from 1 to 5 carbon atoms, and n is an integer from 4 to 12 and of general formula CHEM wherein R min 1 represents a straight or branched alkyl group having from 2 to 4 carbon atoms, W represents hydroxymethyl or the COOM group in which M stands for a hydrogen atom, an alkali metal or an equivalent of an alkali earth metal and n is an integer from 6 to 8 and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.
