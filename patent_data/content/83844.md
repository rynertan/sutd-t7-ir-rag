# Display element and display panel using the same.

## Abstract
A display element D is formed by a four sided right prismatic block member 1 which is rotatable about the axis of its own, has embedded therein first and second magnetic pieces m₁, m₂ to extend along edges t₁, t₃ lying on the same diagonal line and has two display surfaces d₁, d₂ formed by two adjacent ones of four outer peripheral surfaces of the block member 1 . A display panel comprises a display surface structure 6 , a display switching unit 7 , and a drive unit 8 . The display surfaces structure 6 has such display elements D sequen tially disposed on each line of arrangement at predetermined intervals. The display switching unit 7 has an erasing magnetic head E
