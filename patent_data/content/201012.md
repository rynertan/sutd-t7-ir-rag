# Inflatable shelter.

## Abstract
An inflatable shelter having a plurality of sections wherein each section has a series of inflatable arched tubes 14 that are arranged parallel to each other and in successive tangen tial abutting contact. Adjacent tubes 14 in each section are interconnected along their abutting surfaces. Each section has a cover that overlies such section and provides means for se curing the sections together and to the adjoining ground against movement. End flaps 95 are connected to the shelter to fully enclose such shelter.
