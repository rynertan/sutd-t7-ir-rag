# Spin welding apparatus.

## Abstract
Apparatus for spin welding comprises a spin welding ma chine having a spin welding head 16 , a ram assembly 15 , and a feed mechanism 12 the ram assembly is driven from a shaft 8 and the feed mechanism comprises a rotating turret mounted on a shaft 13 which is driven from the shaft 8 via a Geneva mechanism 14 . The spin welding head 16 is driven by a low inertia DC servo motor 17 operated by a programma ble logic controller and timed from a switch unit 18 driven off the shaft 8 .
