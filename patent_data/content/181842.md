# Gas collection device.

## Abstract
A collection device 50 for collecting and withdrawing anesthetic or analgesic gas exhaled by a patient 56 . The device 50 includes a member 52, 54 which is applied to the patient s chin and is provided with at least one suitably shaped opening 62 positioned adjacent the patient s mouth, which communicates through tubing 66 with a source of suction. The opening 62 is positioned at the patient s chin to receive gases as they are exhaled by the patient 56 .
