# Disposable hypodermic syringe.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a disposable syringe comprising a barrel 11 which has on its front end a needle connection 14 with injection needle 12 and needle guard 13 . The injection is provided with a sealing member for the needle guard which consists of a sleeve of shrinkable plastic sheet 18 , which sleeve is shrunk on the one side around the needle connection and or around the barrel or a front part of the barrel and on the other side around a part of the needle guard adjoining the needle connection.
