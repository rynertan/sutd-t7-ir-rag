# Fiber optic connector having lens.

## Abstract
A fiber optic connector comprises a resilient ferrule member 10, 10A, 10B, 10C, 10D having a lens 24, 24A, 24B, 80 at a forward end 12, 14 which communicates with an internal bore 20, 20C, 76 in which a fiber optic transmission member 36, 36A, 36C, 36D is disposed with the end of the fiber optic transmission member being located substantially at the focal point of the lens. An index matching material 48 is located in the bore 20, 20C of the ferrule member 10, 10A, 10B, 10C, 10D to protect the fiber optic transmission member 36, 36A, 36C, 36D .
