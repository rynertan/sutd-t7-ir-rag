# FORMWORK PANEL

## Abstract
A formwork panel is formed from metal longitudinal members 1,2 and metal transverse members 3 and means such as screws 4 are provided to interconnect the members to form a dismountable formwork panel. The panels may be interconnected by connector means 10A, 20, 23 to form larger composite panels X,Y,Z of various sizes and shapes. A kit of parts is provided from which the panels may be formed.
