# A breadmaking method for the production of crisp long term preservation small loaves, and a system therefor.

## Abstract
A low water content lower than than 40 bulk based on the flour bulk breadmaking dough prepared from a high gluten content proteins in excess of 12 flour, is rolled until a visco elastic characteristic breadmaking dough is obtained metered amounts of the breadmaking dough are then pressed into respective molds to yield a compressed, sheet like loaf blank. The sheet like loaf blank is rolled up, given a desired shape, allowed to leaven, and following a baking step, dried down to a lower moisture content than 10 .
