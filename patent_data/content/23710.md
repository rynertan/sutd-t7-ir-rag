# Folding mechanism for use on a cartoning machine.

## Abstract
A folding apparatus for use on a cartoning machine which folds and secures the end flaps of a blank to form a carton including a pivoting rocker arm 18 positioned adjacent to the path of said carton movement and a fold blade 30 pivotedly attached to one end of said rocker arm which engages the flap 15 and moves it into con tact with the side of the carton 17 . A device 26 for manipulating said rocker arm 18 is provided so that the fold blade 30 will engage and disengage the flap 15 and a yieldable means associated with said fold blade 30 to apply a selected pressure to said flap 15 with a roller assembly for engaging the fold blade 30 and control the pressure applied by said yieldable means.
