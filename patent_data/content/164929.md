# Process for producing gluten having high solubility.

## Abstract
A process for producing gluten having high solubility which comprises heating an aqueous acidic solution of gluten and one or more proteins selected from the group consisting of albumins and globulins in an amount of one half or less of that of gluten under acidic conditions and then, after cooling, neutralizing the solution, if necessary. The product is suitable for use in foodstuffs without further treatment.
