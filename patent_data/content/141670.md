# Collection chute for automatic weighing apparatus.

## Abstract
A collection chute in an automatic weighing apparatus comprises a pair of first and second upper chutes 21₁, 21₂ each composed of semicircular inner and outer collection members, and a pair of first and second lower chutes having upper ends connected to lower ends of the upper chutes and outlet ports in lower ends thereof. The first lower chute is connected to a path defined by the outer and inner collection members of the first upper chute and a path defined by the inner collection member of the second upper chute. The second lower chute is connected to a path defined by the outer and inner collection members of the second upper chute and a path defined by the inner collection member of the first upper chute.
