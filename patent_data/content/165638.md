# Apparatus for farming vegetation lying on the ground.

## Abstract
In an apparatus for farming vegetation lying on the ground, comprising a central frame 2 , two first carrying arms 3 that are pivotably mounted at the opposite sides of the frame 2 and two second carrying arms 7 that are pivotably mounted at the ends of said first carrying arms 3 each second carrying arm 7 is mechanically connected with the frame 2 in such a way that pivoting the first carrying arm 3 automatically leads to pivoting the second carrying arms 7 . For this purpose a coupling bar 11 can be provided between the central frame 2 and each second carrying arm 7 .
