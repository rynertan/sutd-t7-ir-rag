# BREAST CANCER DIAGNOSTIC BLOOD TEST

## Abstract
A method for detecting an antigen in a plasma or serum sample, which comprises conditioning the sample, wherein conditioning comprises either diluting the sample by a factor of at least 100 or dissociating the antigen in the sample from binding proteins with acid, base, or a chaotropic agent drying the conditioned sample and detecting the antigen in the dried sample using an immunological assay is disclosed. The method is particularly useful for the detection of antigens associated with breast cancer.
