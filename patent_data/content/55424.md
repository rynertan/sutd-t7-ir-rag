# Flash back arrestor for high pressure gas cylinders.

## Abstract
A flash back arrestor for high pressure gas cylinders wherein a cylinder 22 gas permeable at both ends thereof is joined to an inner end of a valve body 9 in a high pressure gas cylinder 7 in such a manner that said cylinder 22 is communicated with a gas passage 11 in said valve body 9 . Filter plates 25 are provided at upper and lower portions of said cylinder 22 , and a packing 30 consisting of a non combustible granular material is inserted in the portion of the interior of said cylinder 22 which is between said filter plates 25 .
