# Decorative laminate.

## Abstract
A decorative laminate of thermoplastics material which is capable of obscuring surface imperfections in the surface to which it is applied comprises a base layer 1 containing not more than 2 by weight of plasticiser and a decorative layer 2 , the base layer being two to four times the thickness of the decorative layer and the laminate having a total thickness of between 250 and 450 microns and a shrinkage of less than 0.5 at ambient temperatures.
