# Improvements in or relating to a wire cut, electric discharge machine.

## Abstract
In a wire cut, electric discharge machine equipped with an automatic wire electrode threading mechanism, a straight ening guide 20 having a curved surface which guides there across a wire electrode 2 while straightening it out is provided right in front of feed up rollers 7a, 7b which grip the wire elec trode 2 paid out from a supply reel 3 and guide it into a machin ing starting hole 1a of the work 1.
