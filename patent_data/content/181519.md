# Radiation image recording and read out apparatus.

## Abstract
A built in type radiation image and read out apparatus comprises a circulating and conveying means 3 for repe atedly conveying stimulable phosphor sheets 2 through an image recording section 5 , an image read out section 6 and an erasing section 7 . The image recording section has a transfer means 91 for adjusting the distance between an object support member 51 and a stimulable phosphor sheet retainer 52 , whereby recording of enlarged radiation im ages is possible.
