# MALEIC ANHYDRIDE GRAFTS OF OLEFIN POLYMERS

## Abstract
Succinic acid or succinic anhydride grafts of HDPE are blended with LLDPE and or with LDPE to form blends having useful adhesive properties. The succinic acid or succinic anhydride groups are provided by grafting, respectively, maleic acid or maleic anhydride onto HDPE.
