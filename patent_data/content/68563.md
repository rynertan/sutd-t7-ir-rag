# HETEROCYCLIC ACETIC ACID COMPOUNDS AND COMPOSITIONS FOR TREATING BONE DISEASES

## Abstract
Certain acetic acid derivatives of benzothiophene, benzimidazole, phenothiazine, benzofuran, dibenzothio phene, and similar heterocyciic compounds have been found to inhibit bone resorption and to enhance bone formation. The compounds of this invention are there fore useful in the treatment of diseases characterized by anomalous bone metabolism.
