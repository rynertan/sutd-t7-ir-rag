# Food preparation.

## Abstract
A food product for a desired ultimate dish includes a divided main ingredient such as meat coated with dry particulate additional ingredients such as flavouring, thicken ing etc. agents. Preparation includes admixing 14 the main and additional ingredients supplied at prescribed rates 10, 12 with the main ingredient offering surface adherence for the additional ingredients, and the final product remains divided. The desired ultimate dish is prepared in any required quantity simply by heating up the divided final product in a suitable cooking medium.
