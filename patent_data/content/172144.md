# Sealing strip for motor vehicle doors having rounded edges.

## Abstract
The invention refers to a sealing strip 6 for doors 13 with rounded edges consisting of a first portion 6 in plastics or semi stiff rubber which can be put on the metal edge 4 of the car body and of a second portion 10 in solid spongy rubber glued on the first portion 5 and suitable to act as a sealing strip for the door 13 and to cover the gap between the door 13 and the car body 2.
