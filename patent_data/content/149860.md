# Contact fixing device.

## Abstract
A contact fixing device in which a toner image is fixed on a sheet material 4 by heating the toner image and pressing the heated toner and the sheet material 4 together between a contact fixing medium 1 and a pressure element 3 , said contact fixing medium being cleaned by contacting it with a cleaning roller 7 which is provided with perfora tions 12 which communicate with a cavity in the roller in order to discharge surplus toner to the interior of the cleaning roller.
