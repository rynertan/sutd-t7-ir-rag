# Heating device for intermediate ladles.

## Abstract
Heating device for intermediate ladies tundishes pre ferably for continuous casting which according to the invention comprises one, two or more coil coils 3 with an iron core which is are fixedly arranged in relation to a movable ladle 1 , or vice versa. The ladle 1 is adapted to be placed in a heating position adjacent the coil coils 3 , which then connect closely with the bottom and or the lower parts 2 or corners of the ladle in order primarily to heat the lowermost parts 2 of the melt stored in the ladle 1 .
