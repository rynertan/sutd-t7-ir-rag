# Benzylpiperazine compound, preparation thereof, pharmaceutical composition, and use.

## Abstract
A novel benzylpiperazine compound of the formula CHEM or a pharmaceutically acceptable acid addition salt thereof, which has excellent hypolipidemic activity without undesirable side effect, a pharmaceutical composition containing the compound as an active ingredient, preparation of the compound, and its use in the preparation of a pharmaceutical composition for the prophylaxis or treatment of hyperlipidemia.
