# Bubbles emitting toy vehicle.

## Abstract
A toy vehicle emitting bubbles of the type of soap bubbles comprises a wheeled frame 1, 2 whereon a puppet 30 is effective to slide in a vertical direction being urged to a raised position by a spring 52 on the frame 1, 2 is further supported a blowring 58 adapted to be dipped, with the puppet in the raised position thereof, into a reservoir 57 containing a bubble forming liquid, a hose 42 being provided for blowing air through the blowring 58 with the puppet depressed against the bias of the spring 52 .
