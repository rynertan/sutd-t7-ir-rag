# A packing container with suction tube and a method for the manufacture of the same.

## Abstract
A packing container of the non returnable type of beverages ready for consumption is provided sometimes with an externally applied suction tube. To make possible the application of the suction tube 9 of the greatest possible length without the tube with any part extending outside the contour of the packing container, the suction tube is placed so that it extends over two wall panels 1, 3 adjoining each other, which are situated at an angle to one another. The tube is bent at an appropriate angle and is placed along the joint diagonal line of the wall panels. The invention also relates to a method for the manufacture of packing container provided with suction tube.
