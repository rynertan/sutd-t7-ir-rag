# Fiber optic connector housing.

## Abstract
A housing member has a profiled bore 16 extending therethrough in one section of which a ferrule member 64 containing an electro optic member is secured by a remov able retaining clip member 14 that also acts as a heat sink member. Another ferrule member 92 of a fiber optic con nector 90 terminated onto a fiber optic transmission member 106 is disposed in another section of the profiled bore 16 and is latchably secured therein by spring latch ing members 12 secured in the housing.
