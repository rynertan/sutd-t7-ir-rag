# Radiation image storage panel.

## Abstract
A radiation image storage panel 10 comprising a sub strate 11 , a fluorescent layer 13 provided on the substrate 11 and comprising a binder and a stimulable phosphor 131 dispersed therein, and a protective layer 14 provided on the fluorescent layer 13 . The panel 10 is edge reinforced by coating the edge faces thereof with a polymer material 20 comprising polyurethane or acrylic resin.
