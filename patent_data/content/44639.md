# A buckle for belts or the like.

## Abstract
A buckle device 10 comprising a plug member 13 and a receptacle member 21 , each of said members 13,21 having a means 29,28 of retaining a loose end of a belt or the like. The receptacle member 21 has one 27 of its ends open for receiving the plug member 13 . Projecting lugs 23c,23d integral with the receptacle member 21 are provided on opposite sides thereof, leaving a gap 26 therebetween for unobstructed movement of the fingers of the user.
