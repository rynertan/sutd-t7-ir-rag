# Pipe joint and seal.

## Abstract
A ring seal for seating in the seal groove 6 of a pipe joint between a first pipe 2 and a second pipe 16 consists of a ring body, an annular cavity 44 defined by the ring body, a pair of outer annular sealing lips 32 and inner annular sealing lips 28 axially spaced on the ring body and joined by a mid portion 30 of the body and a passage 38 through the ring body defines a fluid path from the zone between the sealing lips 28,32 to a test orifice 40 in one of the pipes, flanges or sleeve. Various other configurations of seals having the two pairs of sealing lips are disclosed, and also a method of pressure testing the joint, and a method of charging the joint with root growth inhibiting chemicals.
