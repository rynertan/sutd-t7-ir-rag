# INDEPENDENT WHEEL SUSPENSION FOR REAR DRIVEN WHEELS OF MOTOR VEHICLES

## Abstract
In a rear suspension assembly with independent and driving wheels of the kind with telescopic strut 1 secured transversally by the axle shaft 9 and be steering system type tie rods 12 , the elastic springing elements and the longitudinal anchorage arm consist of transversal 19, 21 and longitudinal 18, 20 sections of one or more torsion blades.
