# Disk drive apparatus.

## Abstract
A disk drive apparatus having a housing 10 containing a disk and one or more heads for reading and or writing data on the disk, the disk, being rotated in relation to the heads and the arrangement being such that the heads land on the disk when rotation of the disk is halted, comprises a vent tube 24 providing fluid communication between the interior of the housing 10 and atmosphere 20 surrounding the housing. The tube 24 has a length which is great compared to its diameter. A desiccant tube 26,27 is located within the housing 10 and is connected to an end of the vent tube 24 in fluid communication with the internal bore of the vent tube. The desiccant tube contains a desiccant material 27 which absorbs water.
