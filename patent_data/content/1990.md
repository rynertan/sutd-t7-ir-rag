# Hair simulating fibre and method of forming the same.

## Abstract
A composite hair simulating fiber with characteristics similar to human hair which includes a metallic core member 20 coated with a plastic material 30 to form a fine strand, the core member and or the coating member being flattened to provide a strand which will have a curling characteristic with a self retaining form which can be readily styled by an operator to provide various curves and convolutions as desired similar to that capable in human hair but with greater extension and retention properties.
