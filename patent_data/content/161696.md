# METHOD FOR TREATING COPPER AND FOR USING THE THUS TREATED COPPER

## Abstract
There is described a method for treating copper which will be used to manufacture a bimetal plate to be deep drawn or formed, from a copper layer and a chromium nickel steel layer, which comprises adding to a liquid cop per bath, a grain growthinhibiting component, which is pre ferably comprised of boron, zirconium and or titanium.
