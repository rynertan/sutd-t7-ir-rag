# Device for picking up crop from the field.

## Abstract
A device for picking up swaths Z of crop mainly comprising a frame 1 movable across the field and crop harvesting means 2 carried by said frame 1 , wherein in front of the crop picking up means 2 , with respect to the direction of movement, there is arranged a driven member 4 for spreading the crop in a transverse direction to the swath, said spreading member is preferably constucted in the form of one or more downwardly directed tines 5 , which are adapted to reciprocate in a transverse direction P 2.
