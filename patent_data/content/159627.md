# REVOLVING TRANSFER ROLL

## Abstract
A revolving roll apparatus is disclosed in which a rotatable platen means is carried on the roll, discrete strips of material are severed on the rotatable platen means from a continuously moving endless length of material at a cutting station, the strips are rotated by the rotatable platen means, while revolv ing with the roll, from their orientation at the cutting station to a second orientation at a transferring station, and the strips are transferred from the platen on the roll to a continuously moving receiving surface at the transferring station. The strips are severed at the cutting station by a cutting means which en gages the strips and the rotatable platen means to sever the strips on the platen means. The platen means is rotated by a rotating drive means mounted entirely on the roll.
