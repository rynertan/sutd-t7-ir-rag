# Protective cap for a magnetic recording tape cassette.

## Abstract
A protective cap 10 for protecting the magnetic tape of a tape cassette 14 from damage and exposure to dust while the tape cassette is not in operation. A plastic strip 12 covers the apertures of the engaging wall 15 of the tape cassette and is held in position by prongs 11 integral therewith. The prongs project into the apertures. The plastic strip can be removed by gripping it at one end 13 at which it extends beyond the end of the wall and by pulling it away from the tape cassette.
