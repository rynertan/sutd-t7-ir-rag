# Measuring apparatus.

## Abstract
Measuring apparatus, characterized in that it comprises substantially the combination of a measuring device 1 a measuring element 2 secured at one of its ends to the said measuring device 1 a measuring body 3 secured on the free end of the said measuring element 2 a pendulum 4 capable of oscillating the said measuring element 2 means for actuating the said measuring device 1 means for actuating the said pendulum 4 and a logic control unit.
