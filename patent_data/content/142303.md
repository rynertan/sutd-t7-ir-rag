# PHARMACOLOGICALLY ACTIVE BENZO AND DIBENZO PYRONES AND USE

## Abstract
Use of compounds of formulae Ia and Ib In vivo applications include the relief or amelioration of conditions wherein there is tissue hypoxia in vitro the com pounds are of use in the storage of mammalian, including human, red blood cells to maintain their oxygen delivery capacity and prolong their useful storage life. Also provided is a sterile, sealed vessel containing an anticoagulant, a non toxic amount of a compound of formula Ia or Ib and optionally mammalian, in particular human, red blood cells.
