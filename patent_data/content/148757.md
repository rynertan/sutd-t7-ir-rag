# A control device for an inertia reel seat belt.

## Abstract
A member 10 across which a seat belt 40 passes carries a movable member 11 which moves between first and second positions in which the seat belt is respectively gripped to inhibit rewinding and released to permit rewinding. The member 10 is attachable to a seat belt guide 13 in a vehicle by means of a screwed rod 22 which is pivotally connected to a guide engageable hook 32. The rod 22 permits the member 10 to follow twisting movement of the seat belt.
