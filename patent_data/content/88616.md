# A REGULATOR UNLOADER FOR FLUID SYSTEMS

## Abstract
A regulator unloader for fluid systems comprises in a hous ing 10 , an inlet passageway 18 , an outlet passageway 16 connected to said inlet passageway, a piston 11 movable within said outlet passageway, an outlet valve 28 connected to said inlet passageway 18 and to said outlet passageway 18 outwardly of said piston 11 , a bypass passageway 38 connected to said inlet passageway, and a rod 17 spaced within said inlet passageway 18 and connected at one end to said piston 11 and at the other end to said bypass valve 22 , said rod being pivotally mounted in the housing at a point 19 intermediate its ends.
