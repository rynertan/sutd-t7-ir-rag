# Colored jacket for floppy disk.

## Abstract
A colored jacket for a floppy disk is fabricated by folding or bonding a plastic sheet or sheets containing at least one col or pigment, and 5 to 50 parts by weight of an inorganic pigment per 100 parts by weight of a resin. Or, the plastic sheet or sheets contain at least one color pigment, 5 to 40 parts by weight of an inorganic pigment and 0.005 to 0.5 parts by weight of carbon black per 100 parts by weight of a resin.
