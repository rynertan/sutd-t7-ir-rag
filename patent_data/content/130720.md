# Directionally variable lighting assembly.

## Abstract
An apparatus containing a headlight 13 gimballed for rotation within the enclosure of the apparatus. The headlight 13 is capable of exposure through an opening in the enclosure, and is capable of directional adjustment in accordance with signals produced at a remote location. A cover 15 is provided for selectively enclosing the opening which exposes the headlight 13 and is capable of selectively enclosing or exposing the opening in accordance with signals produced at a remote loca tion.
