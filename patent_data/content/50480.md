# Long lasting multi layered film preparation and process for its production.

## Abstract
The present invention provides multi layered film prepara tions which comprise one or more water soluble polymer bases, one or more water insoluble polymer bases, one or more plasticizers and one or more prostaglandins, and option ally contain one or more organic acids and they are composed of at least two drug release controlling layers 1 and one or more drug storing layers 2, 3 . The multi layered film prepara tions of the present invention are characterized by that the prostaglandin s containing therein exhibit the desired long lasting release patterns at the concentration required for therapeutic purpose.
