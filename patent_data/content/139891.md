# PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS WITH ANTIBIOTIC IMMUNOSTIMULATING ACTIVITY

## Abstract
Pharmaceutical compositions containing erythromycin, or an ester thereof, and inosine, or an acidic derivative thereof, in molar ratios varying from 5 1 to 1 5, particularly in the form of a salt of erythromycin dipropionate or of eryt hromycin ethylsuccinate with inosinic acid in the molar ratio 2 1, display antibiotic and immunostimulating properties, which make them particularly useful from the therapeutic point of view.
