# A tool for producing bends in curved surfaces.

## Abstract
The tool according to the present invention is intended for producing bends or steps in curved surfaces and includes an upper tool part 9 and a lower tool part 9 min which are arranged to work towards one another, when they are active. Surfaces 17, 18 on the tool parts have a shape which identifies with or conforms to the shape of the curved surface in which a bend or bends is or are to be formed. The surfaces 17, 18 have the form of form tools 21, 21 min resiliently mounted 26, 26 min and guided in the upper and lower tool parts 9, 9 min , the form tools also having shape identity with the original curved surface. The bending tool according to the invention is suitably arranged in a press not shown .
