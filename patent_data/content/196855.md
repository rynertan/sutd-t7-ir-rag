# TIOCONAZOLE AND RELATED COMPOUNDS FOR PREVENTION OF SEXUALLY TRANSMITTED DISEASES AND CONTROL OF HERPETIC INFECTIONS

## Abstract
The use of tioconazole, econazole, clotrimazole, ketoco nazole, miconazole, butaconazole or a pharmaceutically acceptable acid addition salt of any of these for the manufacture of a medicament for the prevention of sexually transmitted diseases or control of herpetic infections.
