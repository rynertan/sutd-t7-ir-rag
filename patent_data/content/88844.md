# Games table.

## Abstract
A games table comprising a first games playing surface 18 and a second table top surface normally disposed above the games playing surface 18 and formed by two panel elements 30, 31 or two groups of panel elements connected to the games playing surface whereby the panel elements can be moved bodily and pivoted to a position adjacent to and substantially below the level of the playing surface 18 , and an elevating mechanism 40, 41 to enable the playing surface 18 to be moved to a level suitable for use as a games table.
