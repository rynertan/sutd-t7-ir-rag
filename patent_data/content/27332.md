# Comminution apparatus.

## Abstract
An apparatus for comminuting cut crop comprises an advancement duct 10 and comminuting means 30 provid ing one or more cutting edges 20 spanning, or in combina tion spanning a cross sectional dimension of the duct. The advancement means 12 moves a compressed column 14 of the cut crop along the duct towards the comminuting means where the or each cutting edge is arranged to cut the column along a cutting plane containing that edge and lying substan tially parallel to the direction of crop advancement through the duct. At least some of the cutting edges may be movable in said direction.
