# Poultry feeder having sequential control.

## Abstract
There is disclosed a system for conveying feed to a plural ity of groups of animals such as poultry. The system includes feed conveying means 50, 52 associated with each group of animals for conveying feed to each group of animals, feed supply means 30 connected in common to all of the feed conveying means for supplying feed thereto, and control means 70 for sequentially operating the conveying means one at a time. As a result, any number of animal groups may be fed even though the feed supply means may have a limited feed supply rate capacity.
