# Aquarium filter assembly.

## Abstract
An aquarium filter having a housing unit which is adapted to be mounted onto a wall of an aquarium tank. The housing includes an inlet chamber 36 for receiving con taminated water from the aquarium tank and a filter chamber 38 for filtering the contaminated water from the inlet chamber. A motor unit 18 is supported by the housing and includes stator laminations which can be energized by means of a coil. A removable unitary intake assembly 22 is positioned in the housing The intake assembly includes an intake tube 166 for supplying the contaminated water from the aquarium tank to the inlet chamber. It also includes a rotor assembly having an impeller 274 mounted on it. The rotor assembly extends into the intake storage chamber 36 and is magnetically coupled to the stator laminations to thereby drive the impeller pump.
