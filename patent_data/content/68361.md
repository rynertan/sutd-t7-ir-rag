# Front brake tube retaining structure for automotive vehicle.

## Abstract
A front brake tube 18 is arranged to pass under an arch portion 12 of a hood ledge 10 for thereby extending between the inside and outside of an engine compartment 28 . A support 42 is provided which has an upper end attached to the hood ledge 10 at a location adjacent the arch portion 12 and a lower end projecting downwardly from the arch portion 12 for retaining thereat the brake tube 18 . The support 42 is located close to a vertical reference plane extending across the vehicle and contain ing the axis of a drive shaft 14 extending across the hood ledge 10 .
