# Grating devices as a floor element in a stable and grating assembly.

## Abstract
Grating 1 used as a floor element in a stable and com prising bars 2 arranged side by side and interconnected by transverse joints whereby a plurality of bars 2 are equipped with a profile rising or lowering the bars 2 at one or more places at substantially equal distances from the bar ends or at such a distance that the profile has a continuous pattern.
