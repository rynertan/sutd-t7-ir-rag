# Pneumatic run flat tire for motorcycles.

## Abstract
A pneumatic run flat tire 1 for motorcycles having a high handling stability during straight and zigzag runnings, corner ing stability and durability, even when the tire is punctured while running and the pneumatic pressure inside of the tire becomes equal to atmospheric pressure. The run flat tire has a pair of sidewall reinforcing layers 12 , each comprising an elastic filler 13 extending from an end location adjacent a bead core 7 in a bead portion 3 to the other end location in a tread portion 2 through a sidewall portion 4 of the tire, the elastic filler 13 being reinforced with at least two reinforcing plies 14, 14a , one of which extends along one side surface of the elastic filler toward the tread portion 2 , and then, addi tionally extends in to the tread portion 2 , and the other one of which extends along the other side surface of the elastic filter toward the tread portion 2 , and then, additionally extends into the tread portion 2 .
