# Pipe repair clamp.

## Abstract
A pipe repair clamp comprises a two piece type split collar, the collar being formed by joining the two pieces 1 together about the surface of the pipe to be repaired. The pieces 1 are shaped to form scarf type joints 5 and are provided with, affixed to their internal surfaces, portions 12 of a gasket for sealing the surface of the pipe to be repaired from the external surface of the collar.
