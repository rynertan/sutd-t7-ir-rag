# Dental apparatus for tooth reaming and process.

## Abstract
The new apparatus for tooth reaming by means of a flexible reamer, for devitalization of the tooth, comprises a front cutter 10 to establish a reference surface in the tooth, means 22 to detect a measurement of the tooth by using the reference surface, and substantially rigid adaptors 24, 124 to be applied on the reamer 30 to make it fit to the detected measurement.
