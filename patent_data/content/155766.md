# Method and apparatus for glueing corrugated board.

## Abstract
A method for glueing corrugated board and an appar atus for carrying out same, wherein starch glue 34 is heated to a predetermined temperature range by heat exchange the starch glue is supplied to a glue pan 32 placed in a hot atmosphere of a heat insulated space 120 to maintain the glue in heated state a corrugated core sheet 10 and a liner 12 are fed into the heat insulated space the heated starch glue is applied on ridge portions of the corrugated core sheet 10 and the corrugated core sheet and liner are bonded together.
