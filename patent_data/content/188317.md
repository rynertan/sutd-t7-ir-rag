# HINGE LEAF

## Abstract
A vehicle including a door hingedly connected to the vehicle body by at least one hinge, said one hinge including a pair of hinge leaves hingedly connected to one another, at least one of said hinge leaves including at least one hinge pin boss secured to a hinge leaf body, the hinge leaf body including a main body portion extending through an aper ture formed in a wall forming part of the door or the vehicle body to which said one hinge leaf is attached, the main body portion being secured internally of the wall, and a cover wall portion secured to the or each boss and the main body portion, the cover wall portion overlying said aperture and being secured to the portion of the wall surrounding the aperture.
