# Flame resistant acrylic resin composition and process for its production.

## Abstract
A flame resistant acrylin resin composition comprising a polymer composed of from 40 to 88 by weight of methyl methacrylate, from 1 to 15 by weight of Î± methyl styrene, from 5 to 15 by weight of styrene, from 5 to 15 by weight of maleic anhydride and from 1 to 15 by weight of methacrylic acid, and a flame retardant.
