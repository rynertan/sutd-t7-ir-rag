# Cryogenic storage container.

## Abstract
A storage container 10 for shipping transportable materials at cryogenic temperatures including a vessel which opens to the atmosphere and contains a micro fibrous structure 27 for holding a liquified gas such as liquid nitrogen in adsorption and capillary suspension. The micro fibrous structure comprises a core 28 permeable to liquid and gaseous nitrogen and an adsorption matrix 30 com posed of a web of inorganic fibers surrounding the core in a multi layered arrangement.
