# Radiation curable silicon release compositions, their use in making coated articles and laminates and the articles and laminates produced thereby.

## Abstract
There is disclosed a silicone release coating composi tion comprising a an organopolysiloxane containing an average of at least one acryloxy and or methacryloxy group per molecule, b a low molecular weight polyacrylyl crosslinking agent and, optionally, c a photosensitizer. There is also disclosed a method of producing an article from such a composition and also the articles made there by.
