# Process for producing a coal water slurry.

## Abstract
A process for producing a coal water slurry of low viscos ity and high coal concentration with a low specific energy and a good efficiency is provided, which process comprises wet grinding coal by means of a wet vertical ring roll mill 3 in the presence or absence of a surfactant and recycling a part of the coal ground by the mill 3 , as it is, without classifying it, to the mill 3 through a splitter 13 .
