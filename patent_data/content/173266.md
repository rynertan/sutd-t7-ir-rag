# COMPOSITE OPTICAL WAVEGUIDE AND PRODUCTION OF THE SAME

## Abstract
A composite optical waveguide comprising a flexible tube member 10 having at least one continuous bore along at least a part of its length at least one of which is filled with a transpar ent elastomeric material 11 serving as an optical waveguide part, which optionally further comprises at least one elongate element 31, 32 embedded in the transparent material or a wall of the tube member 10 .
