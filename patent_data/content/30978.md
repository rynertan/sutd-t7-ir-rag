# DATA TRANSFER CONTROLLING SYSTEM.

## Abstract
An information transfer control system including a bus control unit 3 coupled to a common bus 1 , which unit controls the information transfer through the bus, and a plurality of transmitter receiver units 2 i which transfer information mutually through the common bus. The bus includes a transfer request signal line 5 coupled to each of the transmitter receiver units, one or two permit signal lines 6 or 6 1 , 6 2 which permits a transmitter receiver unit, adapted to deliver a transfer request signal, to transfer information, an acknowledge signal line 7 and a transfer complete signal line 9 . The bus does not include a sup press signal line which is included in the conventional system. The transmitter receiver unit receiving a permit signal begins to transfer information when both the permit signal and a transfer complete signal are over.
