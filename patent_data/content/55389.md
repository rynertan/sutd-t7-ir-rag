# Locking device reacting to inertia forces for retractors.

## Abstract
A locking mechanism for seat belt retractors responding to movement or accelerations of a vehicle, including a freely movable inertia body 2, 24, 25 and a pivotable pawl 3 , in which a mechanism 31 35 on the pawl 3 can engage the inertia body and movement of the inertia body is limited by a means 1, 12, 13 arranged next to the inertia body.
