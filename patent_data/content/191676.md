# A MULTIPLE GRAIN FLAKE FOR HOT CEREAL AND METHOD

## Abstract
This invention is a process for producing a mixed grain ins tant hot cereal product. The process comprises of the steps of cutting different grains preferably with steel blades, mixing the dry grains together, steaming the mixture, rolling the cooked grains on a flaking roller to form flakes, and baking the flakes. The process produces thicker than normal toasted flakes of high flake integrity that withstand packaging, shipping, and re hydration cooking. The invention includes the product of the process.
