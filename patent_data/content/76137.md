# Process for the manufacture of soap powder.

## Abstract
A process for making washing powder having a high content of sodium soap in which the tendency to form clots and gels is reduced. The reduction is effected by impregnat ing spray dried or spray coolod powder with a specified amount of a water soluble sodium salt. The amount speci lied is sufficient to produce a solution of sodium ions having 0.5 to 4 molar initial concentration in the immediate locality of the individual spray dried particles.
