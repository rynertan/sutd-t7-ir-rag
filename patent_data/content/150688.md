# Salts of L carnitine and alkanoyl L carnitines and process for preparing same.

## Abstract
Novel L carnitine and alkanoyl L carnitine salts and a process for their preparation are disclosed. The salts have general formula Since they are not hygroscopic, these salts can be easi ly compounded and are favourably suitable for manufactur ing solid administration forms. Their aqueous solutions are less acid than those of the corresponding chlorides conse quently, these salts are also suitable for manufacturing in jectable administration forms.
