# Vehicle control apparatus.

## Abstract
Vehicle control apparatus for controlling movement of a vehicle having a movable component comprising an electric al circuit 17 controlled by operation of a gear lever 13 of the vehicle and a switch 43 for actuating the circuit 17 when the component 11 has moved from a rest position. The arrangement is such that retarding means of the vehicle is actuated if the vehicle is driven forwardly for a pre determined distance with the component 11 raised from the rest position.
