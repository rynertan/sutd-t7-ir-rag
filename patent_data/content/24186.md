# Catheter assembly.

## Abstract
The present invention provides a catheter assembly which comprises catheter tubing 1 a hollow needle 2 and a guide member 4 with a longitudinal bore 5 there through for aiding insertion of the hollow needle into the catheter, which catheter assembly is characterised in that the longitud inal bore in the guide member has a narrower portion and a communicating broader portion, the narrower portion being adapted to receive in close fit the hollow needle and the broader portion being adapted to reveive in close fit the cathe ter whereby in use the catheter tubing may be positioned for insertion of the hollow needle.
