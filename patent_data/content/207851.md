# Combination hand weight and water dispenser.

## Abstract
A combination hand weight and water dispenser has a hollow D shaped watertight body 1 with a straight portion 3 , suitable to be hand gripped by the user a water filling and discharging aperture 15 , and a teat drinking assembly screwed around the aperture. The combination allows easy filling of the hollow body 1 and withdrawing of water when the teat assembly is operated by the mouth of the user.
