# Sliding door cupboard structure.

## Abstract
The sliding doorcupboard structure comprises a body 1 closeable frontally by at least two mutually sliding doors 2 , and a guide plate 10 supported on the body 1 and spanning substantially the full width of the body 1 . With the guide plate 10 there operatively interact support elements 25 for each of the sliding doors 2 , to which there are connected link elements 31 operative with the guide plate 10 which are adapted to permit free displacement of the support elements 25 with respect to the guide plate 10 at all places on the plane defined by the guide plate 10 itself.
