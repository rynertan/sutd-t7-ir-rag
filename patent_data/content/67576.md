# Motor vehicle with fuel tank.

## Abstract
A fuel tank construction of a motor vehicle, wherein a fuel tank 13 disposed in a tunnel 2 projecting upwardly from the surface of a floor panel 1 , opening downwardly, disposed in the longitudinal direction of the vehicle and formed at the central portion of the floor panel 1 is of such an arrangement that a pair of upper and lower tanks 13A, 13B being of substantially triangular shape are welded into a substantially square shape in cross section to form the fuel tank 13 and seam portions 14 formed by the welding are located at opposite corner portions 2A, 2B and on one of the diagonal lines of a space formed by the substantially square shape.
