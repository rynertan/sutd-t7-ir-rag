# Ink supply system for a printer.

## Abstract
An ink supply system for a printer comprising an ink container 18 containing liquid ink a printing head 11 an ink passage 15,17,20 connecting a portion of the ink con tainer 18 to said printing head 11 , and air trapping and or detecting means 14,15,20 22 for trapping and or detecting air bubbles in said ink characterised in that the air trapping and or detecting means 14,15,20 22 is disposed in said ink passage 15,17,20 .
