# Axial gap type core less electric motor.

## Abstract
In an axial gap type core less electric motor, a frame bracket 16 , which is disposed facing a field magnet 12 with a rotary armature 14 inserted inbetween, has radial ribs 19 formed outside thereof and disposed radially from the central part to the peripheral part of the frame bracket, the ribs being formed tapered with their heights and with their widths gradually narrowed from the center part to the peripheral part of the frame bracket.
