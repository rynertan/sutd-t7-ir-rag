# LIQUID SPRAY APPARATUS

## Abstract
The liquid reservoir tank of plant spraying equipment of the haversack type is pressurised by a pair of foot pumps 10 worn by the operator under his shoes 14 . Co operating for mations 28 and 29 are provided within the chamber formed by the resiliently deformable air pump body 13 of each foot pump 10 which has concertina sidewalls 23 so that the enclosure surrounded by the sidewalls 23 is substantially filled by the formations 28 and 29 when it is collapsed.
