# Lighting fixture.

## Abstract
A ceiling lighting fixture adapted to be mounted on a ceiling C from below comprises a frame 10 with an opening 23 for receiving a reflector and a slot 25 , or a portion easily removed to provide the slot, whereby the frame can be passed through a ceiling aperture 27 of the same size and shape as the reflector opening, and seated on the ceiling with the open ing and aperture aligned. Fixing clips 28 of U shape cross section have upper legs 29 slidable in radial guideways 34 on the frame and lower limbs 30 sharpened to become embedded in the ceiling at the edge of the aperture to fix the frame in position, the clip being driven into the ceiling from within the aperture.
