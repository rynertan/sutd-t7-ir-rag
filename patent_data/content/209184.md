# CENTRALISED DRIVE DEVICE IN A PACKAGING MACHINE

## Abstract
A centralised drive device in a machine for packaging products in continuous plastic film which unwinds from a re al comprising essentially a feed unit for said products, an individual product feed unit, a unit for wrapping said pro ducts in said film, and a unit for welding und cutting the indi vidual packaged products, in which a rigid transmission shaft extends along the entire length of the interior of the packaging machine from a central variable speed drive. A series of individual motion transmissions, each operating one of the units of the machine, branches from the shaft, there being also connected to the variable speed drive a further variable speed drive which, by positive transmis sion, drives a belt for conveying the products to be pack aged, in accordance with the predetermined succession.
