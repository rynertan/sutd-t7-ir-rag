# Integral fluted tube for sound suppression.

## Abstract
An integral fluted tube for sound suppression or ex haust ejection shown in several embodiments comprising a tube 30 , having a plurality of troughs 52 , which extend through a constricted region characterized by apexes. In operation, the gaseous flow passes through the constricted portion where a venturi effect is created while another por tion bypasses the constriction through the troughs.
