# End component and side wall for a container.

## Abstract
An end component for a spin welded container compris es a plug 3 which is received within the container side wall 1 for spin welding thereto. An annular bead 10 co operates with a step 6 on the wall 1 to locate the plug. During welding, flash generated at the main weld area below the bead is prevented from escaping upwardly by the bead. A cavity 12 is provided above the bead to retain any material which passes the bead or is formed by melting of the bead.
