# Arrangement for detachably receiving cassette containing photosensitive film sheets.

## Abstract
An arrangement K for detachably receiving a cassette 1 containing photosensitive film sheets S , which includes a drive device 50 for driving a light shielding cover 4 of the cassette 1 , an electrical lock device 40 for locking the cassette 1 on the basis of an electrical signal detecting that an opening of the cassette 1 is uncovered by the light shielding cover 4 , and a mechanical lock device 30 for locking the cassette 1 such that locking of the cassette 1 by the mechanical lock device 30 is cancelled upon manipula tion of the mechanical lock device 30 .
