# Method of manufacturing shadow mask.

## Abstract
A method of manufacturing a shadow mask having ap ertures of precise shape and size, comprises the steps of forming an etching protective film having a pattern of a num ber of apertures on a surface of a thin metal plate containing iron and nickel as major components, and etching the thin metal plate using an etching solution with a viscosity of 1 to 5 centipoise cP so as to form a number of apertures therein.
