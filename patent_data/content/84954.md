# System of and method for venting cooling air from filaments.

## Abstract
A system of and method for venting cooling air from a plurality of filaments with which said air has been associated, employs a Coanda flow attachment means 30 , including a Coanda flow attachment surface 38 , to separate the fila ments and air into respective streams 33, 33 , and then to divert said air stream 33 in a direction away from said fila ment stream 33 .
