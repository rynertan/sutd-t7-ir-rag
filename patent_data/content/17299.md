# Method and apparatus for installing a sealing ring.

## Abstract
A sealing ring 4 is installed in a groove chamber 3 of a socket 2 by impressing the sealing ring 4 and pushing the impressed sealing ring into the socket, whereafter the impression is terminated and the sealing ring is received in chamber 3 . An apparatus for carrying out the method comprises a sealing ring impression member 10 and sealing ring retaining means 5, 6, 7 for maintaining the sealing ring in the impressed state.
