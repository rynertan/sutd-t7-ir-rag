# Ski boot inner shoe.

## Abstract
An inner shoe for ski boots comprising a lower portion 2 enclosing the skier s foot, and an upper portion 3 connected to said lower portion 2 and spanning the front portion of the skier s leg. A peculiar feature of the invention is that it includes a flexible section 10 located between said lower portion 2 and said upper portion 3 to allow mutual flexing of such por tions 2, 3 .
