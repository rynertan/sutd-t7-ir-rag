# Aerial power transmission system.

## Abstract
Aerial power transmission system comprising power transmission cables supported between steel towers and insulators for supporting the power transmission cables on said steel towers as well as lightning arrestors 5 54 between said steel towers 1 and said power transmission cables 31, 32, 33 , characterized in that an insulator 51 containing a lightning arrestor 54 is connected between said power transmission cables 31, 32, 33 in different phases and said cables are supported on said towers 1 .
