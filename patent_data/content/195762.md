# Exhaust silencer device for an internal combustion engine.

## Abstract
The invention relates to an exhaust silencer device for an internal combustion engine, for placement in the exhaust pipe of the engine. The device according to the invention comprises a damper plate 6 disposed in the exhaust pipe and pivotabie about a shaft 7 substantially perpendicular to the longitudinal axis of the exhaust pipe between a closed position, in which the damper plate 6 covers the major portion of the exhaust pipe flow area, and an open position, in which it permits substantially free flow through the exhaust pipe. An operating means 8, 10, 11 is arranged to actuate the damper plate 6 to adjust its position dependent on the exhaust flow through the exhaust pipe.
