# Punch press with laser cutting head attachment.

## Abstract
A turret punch machine tool 10 equipped with control led automatic x y axis workpiece movement mechanisms 21 for moving the workpiece W.P. over a stationary work table section 11a adjacent a punching station 23 is provided with a separate laser beam generator 15 and with a tool frame carried vertically retractable and extendable laser cutting head 17 in optical communication with the beam generator 15 . The beam generator 15 is capable of producing both laser pulses of varying duration and spacing or continuous beam. The cutting head 17 is provided with two gas inputs 67, 68 one for use with the continuous beam, a second for use with the pulse beam, and controls 18 are provided to automatically provide varying combinations of use of the punch 10 and laser 15 .
