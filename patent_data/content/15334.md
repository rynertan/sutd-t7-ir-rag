# Carminative preparations containing peppermint oil or its active components.

## Abstract
Essential Oils including Aromatic Carminatives and their active components are selectively administered to the intestine to treat irritable colon syndrom and other intesti nal disorders responsive to treatment with a carminative. The oils are presented in a rectal or, preferably, enteric preparation, especially an enterically coated hard gelatine capsule. Peppermint oil is presently preferred.
