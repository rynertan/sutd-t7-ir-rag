# Talc containing moulding compositions on the basis of vinyl chloride polymers.

## Abstract
Polyvinyl chloride moulding compositions comprising polyvinyl chloride and talc, wherein at least 85 percent by weight of said talc has a particle size of not more than 10 microns and said composition contains from about 0.1 to 30 parts by weight of an amide compound and or a polyamide having a softening point or a melting point of not higher than 200 C per 100 parts by weight of said talc, and optionally other additives.
