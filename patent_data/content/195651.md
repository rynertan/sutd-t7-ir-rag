# A DEVICE FOR TOWING LUGGAGE

## Abstract
A device for towing luggage incorporated into a wall of the luggage 9 comprises a pair of fixed tubes 4, 5 a pair of push pull tubes 2, 3 and a U shaped tube 6 wherein each end of the U shaped tubes 6 is connected to the push pull tubes 2, 3 . A pair of levers 12, 13 one end of each being overlapped and hinged on the middle of the U shaped tube 6 and a pair of reversed L shaped links 19 which are fulcrumed at their angled parts and are hinged on the other ends of the levers 12, 13 . By pushing down and pulling up the handle 1 , the wheel units 20 can be extended outward or retracted inward.
