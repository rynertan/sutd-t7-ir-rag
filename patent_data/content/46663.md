# Fluid flow control valve with fire safe seal.

## Abstract
A fluid flow control valve has a metallic seat ring 50 with a resilient seal ring 54 in a leading face thereof to seal against a valve closure member 18 . A thin, metallic flexible diaphragm 62 is clamped around its outer edges 70 to the body and around its inner edge 64 to tne seat ring. An annular sealing ridge 72 , which is concentric to and smaller than the resilient seal, is provided around the face of the seat ring to provide a metal to metal seal in the event of destruction of the resilient seal.
