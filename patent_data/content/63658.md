# Writing instrument with retractable tip.

## Abstract
Writing instrument 10 having a flexible shield 16 comprising a piece of flexible material shaped to fit into and be attached to the interior of the barrel 11 of a retractable tip pen in promixity to the tip and having a central aperture 17 which may comprise cross slits 18 whose edges are adapted to move away from each other to expand the aperture when the tip is emitting from the barrel and to return to normal substantially closed position over the tip when the tip is retracted into the barrel to perform the dual function of inhibiting the passage of air to dry out the tip and to wipe the tip free of accumulated liquid build up or dust.
