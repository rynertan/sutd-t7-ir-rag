# Cutting torch height control apparatus.

## Abstract
An apparatus 10 for controlling the height of one or more cutting torches 12 above a workpiece 14 . The apparatus 10 senses the actual height of the torch 12 above the workpiece 14 and compares the actual height with a desired reference height. The apparatus 10 moves the torch 12 up or down until the actual height and reference height are substantially equal. The apparatus 10 is operable in a manual mode, a semi automatic mode or a fully automatic mode allowing an operator to select a desired degree of con trol during a cutting operation.
