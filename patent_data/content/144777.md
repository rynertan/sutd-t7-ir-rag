# Button for garment.

## Abstract
A button for attachment to a garment fabric C compris es a capped button body B and a tack member A adapted to be joined with the button body B , wherein a shank 11 of said tack member A includes a free end portion 14 being deformable into a radially uniformely and axially compress ed configuration, a cold pressed recessed portion 20 disposed adjacent to said end portion 14 , and a deforma tion free base portion 13 .
