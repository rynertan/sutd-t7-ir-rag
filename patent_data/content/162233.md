# Two axis touch probe.

## Abstract
A touch probe is disclosed having a stylus holder 1 mounting arrangement utilizing the combined effects of a planar reed spring 8 and at least one linear wire spring 9A, 9B to restrict permissible movement of the stylus holder 1 relative to the probe housing 16 to two axes. Therefore, the disclosed touch probe finds particular utility in gaging ap plications where an indication of contact between the probe and a workpiece is desired.
