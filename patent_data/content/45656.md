# Vision enhancing system.

## Abstract
A sight facilitating apparatus, capable of providing mag nifications of about 4X to 8X, comprises an elongate casing having a light entry at one end and a light exit at the other. The casing contains an adjustable prismatic telescopic lens system arranged such that the majority of the focal length extends parallel to the longitudinal axis of the casing which is preferably mounted on top of or incorporated into an eyeglass frame, substantially horizontally between the wear er s eyes, with the light exit adjacent an eye of the wearer. The wearer may look through the apparatus by tilting his eye through an angle of about 10 .
