# SILYL COMPOUNDS, THEIR USE FOR PRODUCING SILICA REINFORCED RUBBER, AND SILICA REINFORCED RUBBER

## Abstract
At least one of an alkoxy alkyl substituted silylazide, an alkoxy alkyl substituted silyl thiosulfenamide, a silacycloalkyl mercaptan and or a bis silacycloalkyl disulfide is employed as a coupling agent or promoter in a silica filled rubber.
