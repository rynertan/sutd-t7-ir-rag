# Money receiving and dispensing system.

## Abstract
A money receiving and dispensing system comprising a plurality of money cases 44A 44C for storing monies of sev eral denominations, respectively, a stock money cartridge 44D removably mounted in the system for collectively stor ing monies available in the system, a plurality of entering means 38A 38D each for entering monies into the corres ponding one of the money cases and stock money cartridge, a plurality of feeding means 45 for respectively feeding the monies stored in the money cases and stock money cartridge, a money receiving means 14 for receiving the money depo sited into the system to store the received money in one of the money cases and stock money cartridge, and a money dis pensing means 37 for dispensing the money fed by one of the feeding means.
