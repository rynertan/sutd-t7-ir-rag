# Magnetic tape cassettes.

## Abstract
A magnetic tape cassette comprises a cassette housing 1 having an upper and a lower half 2, 3 , and a pair of tape reels 4, 5 therein. A rib 36 is formed on the lower peri phery of a window 14 in the upper wall 2a of the upper half 2 so as to prevent the pair of tape reels 4, 5 held in the cassette housing 1 from excess movement towards the upper half 2 .
