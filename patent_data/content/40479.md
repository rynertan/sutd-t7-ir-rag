# Shuttle retaining apparatus.

## Abstract
A loom has a shuttlebox 12 rotatable through 180 from a first, shuttle receiving position to a second, picking position. Means are provided for checking and positioning the shuttle 26 to a predetermined picking position with the shuttlebox, and means are provided for picking the shuttle 26 . Control apparatus 32,34 is provided for guiding and maintaining the shuttle 26 in the predetermined picking position during rota tion of the shuttle box 12 . A braking element 44 is provided for braking the shuttle only when it is in the second position.
