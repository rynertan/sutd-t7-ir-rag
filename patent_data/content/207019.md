# SHIELDING GAS

## Abstract
A shielding gas for gas metal arc welding, including short arc welding, spray arc welding and pulsed arc welding, in the first instance in stainless steel, both in steels which are sensitive and in steels which are not sensitive to carburiza tion. The shielding gas comprises Ar, He and O₂, the con centration of O₂ being in the range of 0.5 1.5 per cent, the concentration of He being in the range of 25 40 per cent and the remainder consisting of Ar.
