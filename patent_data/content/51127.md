# Direct firing downhole steam generator.

## Abstract
Direct firing downhole stream generator basically com prises an injector assembly axially connected with a combus tion chamber. Downstream of the combustion chamber and oriented so as to receive its output is a heat exchanger wherein preheated water is injected into the heat exchanger through a plurality of one way valves, vaporized and injected through a nozzle, packer and check valve into the well formation.
