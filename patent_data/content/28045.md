# Mowing device.

## Abstract
A mowing device 1 comprises behind its cutting members 5 a swath former 57 consisting of a worm rotor 63 conveying the cup crop transversely 58 of the travelling direction 2 of the mowing device. In order to surely convey the cup crop irrespective whether the volume of the cut crop is small or great the worm rotor 63 rotates in such a sense that its front 61 moves upwardly 62 .
