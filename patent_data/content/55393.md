# An endoscope with an air liquid suction device.

## Abstract
An endoscope comprises an air liquid selector valve 8 and a suction control valve 9 attached to a control section 2 of the endoscope, a common tube 26 extending through an insertion section, having one end connected with the suction control valve and the other end opened at the distal end portion of the insertion section 3 of the endoscope, a connecting tube 18 for connecting the suction control valve 9 and the air liquid selector valve 8 . The air liquid selector valve has a feed air port, a feed liquid port, and a piston cylinder for selectively connecting the ports with the con necting tube, and the suction control valve 9 has a suction port and a piston cylinder for selectively connecting the suction port and the connecting tube with the common tube.
