# Tractor.

## Abstract
The tractor shown in Fig. 2 has a main frame 11 , supported by a pair of steering driving wheels 20 , to which is fitted the engine 10 and transmission. A pair of booms 31 are pivotally mounted on the frame or on a sub frame 27 pivotally connected to the frame and are provided with castor wheels 39 . The track between the castor wheels is selectively adjustable by hydraulic rams 33 connected to the booms 31 and the frame 11 or sub frame 27 . Agricultural implements may be connected to the frame, to the booms inside the wheelbase of the tractor or to a draw bar interconnecting the booms.
