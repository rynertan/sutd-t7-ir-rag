# Copolymer for fluorine containing elastomer having excellent low temperature resistance and alcohol resistance.

## Abstract
A copolymer for an fluorine containing elastomer hav ing excellent low temperature resistance and alcohol resist ance comprises vinylidene fluoride units and fluorovinyl ether units having the formula CF₂ CFOX X represents a C₃ C₉ perfluoroalkyl group which has ether bond having 1 3 oxygen atom at a molar ratio of vinylidene fluoride units to fluorovinyl ether units of 95 5 to 50 50 and at a total content of the units of at least 80 mole .
