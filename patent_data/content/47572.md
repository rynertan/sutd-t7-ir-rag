# Branched chain ketones, process of organoleptically modifying, augmenting or enhancing therewith and a process for producing a staple single phase aqueous alkaline metal hypochlorite solution containing the same.

## Abstract
C₁₁ C₁₂ branched aliphatic unsaturated ketones are described, as well as a process of organoleptically modifying augmenting or enhancing therewith and a process for producing a staple single phase aqueous alkaline metal hypochlorite solution containing the same.
