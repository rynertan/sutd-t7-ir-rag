# Hot melt butylene ethylene copolymer adhesives.

## Abstract
Hot melt adhesive with long open time good bonding to cold metals are made of blends of a maleic anhydride modified copolymer of butene 1 and ethylene, an aliphatic substantially non polar tackifier resin, an antioxidant and, optionally, one or more of a microcrystalline wax, a block copolymer and atactic polypropylene.
