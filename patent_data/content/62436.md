# Wire reinforced hose and method.

## Abstract
A hose includes an inner polymeric tube 12 which has a relatively hard outer surface, a tightly packed wire reinforce ment 16, 20 e.g. braid or spiral, telescoped over the tube 12 , and an expanded polymeric cushion matrix 14, 18 encapsu lating at least a portion of the strands of the reinforcement 16, 20 and also promoting wire to wire and tube to wire adhe sion.
