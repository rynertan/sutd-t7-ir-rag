# LIQUID DETERGENT COMPOSITION

## Abstract
An aqueous liquid detergent composition comprises, in addition to water, detergent active compound, at least one water soluble dicarboxylic acid having from 5 to 7 carbon atoms in the molecule and having a solubility in water of from 1 to 70g 100g water at 20 C, and at least one organic solvent having a boiling point of from 120 to 280 C and chosen from alkylene and polyalkylene glycols having from 2 to 9 carbon atoms in the molecule, and the C₁ to C₄ alkyl ethers thereof containing a total of from 3 to 14 carbon atoms in the molecule.
