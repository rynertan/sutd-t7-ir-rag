# A FORMABLE VINYL CHLORIDE BASED RESIN COMPOSITION

## Abstract
The invention proposes a foamable vinyl chloride based resin composition capable of giving a shaped article having a cellular foamed structure and a beautiful surface with metallic luster. The composition comprises a 100 parts by weight of a vinyl chloride based resin compound of a transparency formulation having a specified trans parency, b 0.1 5 parts by wight of a powder of a metal or alloy having a specified average particle diameter, c 0.01 2 parts by weight of an organic dye or pigment and d 0.1 5 parts by weight of a blowing agent.
