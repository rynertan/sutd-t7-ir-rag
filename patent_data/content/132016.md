# Wheeled garment bag.

## Abstract
A multipurpose piece of luggage with wheels 18a, 18b and a handle assembly 20, 50 having a garment enclosure 10 in which large articles of clothing may be hung and lesser enclosures 36a, 36b for storage of small articles. The article also serves as a cart for other luggage. Its handle assembly 20, 50 may be collapsed, support feet 16a, 16b retracted and the garment enclosure 10 folded to give it the appear ance and utility of a normal suitcase.
