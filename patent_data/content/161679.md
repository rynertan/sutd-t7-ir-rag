# Mixing screw for ice cream production apparatuses.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a screw 1 for apparatuses for icecream production with a vertical whipping vessel 20 . The screw comprises at least one supporting metal blade 5 fixed to a rotating hub and carrying clutches for remov ably and substitutably fixing thereon a scraping vane 6 . The screw may further comprise at least a mixture spread ing vane acting in cooperation with said scraping vane.
