# Pressure roller device for laterally retaining boxes in packaging machines with automatic box feed.

## Abstract
A support device for a pressure roller, forming a lateral retention means and associated with the upper working head of a packaging machine, comprises a plate 8 , essentially in the form of a circular sector, from which a support arm 7 for the roller 5 projects, said plate being mounted rotatable on a vertical pivot 11 fixed to the machine working head assembly and cooperating with position locking means 10 , the roller being carried at the end of said support arm by a vertical rotation pin 6 .
