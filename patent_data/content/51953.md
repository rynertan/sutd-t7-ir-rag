# A cooling device for a bearing.

## Abstract
A bearing support 3, 4 adapted to mount the bearing means 2 in position is formed with a hollow chamber 8, 9 part filled with a working fluid 14 such as ammonia, fluorocar bon, etc. to cool the bearing by its evaporation, the evaporated working fluid 14 being fed to a heat exchanger 10 to be con densed and recycled.
