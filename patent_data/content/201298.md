# AUTOMATIC ALIGNING INTERCHANGEABLE WORK SUPPORT

## Abstract
Means for mounting a workpiece holder such as a collet on a support element such as the arbor of a machine tool in prepa ration for machining comprises a support member 10 100 for attachment to the arbor and a plate member 60 150 carrying the collet, the support member and the plate member having respectively a frustoconical boss 55 152 and a complemen ary recess 88 102 . The support member has latch elements 38 104 which move in and out under the control of a locking ring 44 130 and engage groove means on the plate member. The workpiece mounting can thus be changed rapidly and reli ably with most of the set up being carried out away from the machine tool.
