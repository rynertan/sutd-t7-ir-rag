# A cured rubber skin stock and its use.

## Abstract
Disclosed is a composition for the improvement of the adhesion between conventional rubber skim stock composi tions, utilized in the manufacture of tires, conveyor belts, hoses and the like, and metallic reinforcement cords or mem bers such as bright steel. The invention composition is pre pared by adding an organosilane compound to the rubber skim stock prior to contacting the metallic reinforcement. Tests conducted with bright steel showed improved metal adhesion with use of the rubber skim stock of this invention when compared to existing formulations without the additive.
