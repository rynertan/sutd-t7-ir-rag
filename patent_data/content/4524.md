# Control unit for starting a climatization procedure in a building.

## Abstract
In a control unit 18 for starting a climatization procedure in a building to create agreeable climatic conditions at a pre determined time, and equivalent electric circuit is used to imitate the thermal properties of the building. A pulse generator 37 is provided to generate pulses of a very high frequency, said pulses being fed to a pulse frequency divider 39 which is, in turn, connected to a decadic counter 41 . The end figure 5, 7, 9 of the decadic suit of figures is used to start different steps in the procedure.
