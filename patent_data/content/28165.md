# An electrostatographic reproduction system.

## Abstract
An electrostatographic reproduction system including a finishing station having a compiler 82 for compiling sheets arriving from a xerographic processor 11 into sets and a stitcher, stapler or other binding device 84 for binding the compiled sets. The stapler 84 with its cooperating anvil is pivotally mounted for movement be tween an inoperative position which it assumes during com piling and whereat it is outside the path of sheets being compiled and an operative position in which it is arranged to apply a staple to the set.
