# ADJUSTABLE ARTICLE DISPLAY APPARATUS

## Abstract
An adjustable article display apparatus includes a plurality of mounting brackets 44 which are detachably affixed to a carrier board 10 . A plurality of support components 32 are each carried on a mounting bracket in any one of a plurality of lateral positions. The components cooperate to form the ad justable article display apparatus and each component may be releasably locked on its associated mounting bracket in any one of the lateral positions, thereby permitting precise adjust ment. The support components are provided with stops 51 which prevent the accidental removal of the components from the carrier board.
