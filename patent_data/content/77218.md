# RIBBON CARTRIDGE FOR IMPACT PRINTERS

## Abstract
A ribbon cartridge 10 including an end of ribbon sensing system which is equally compatible with various arrangements of external sensors 50, 58 as utilized in different models of serial impact printers. A clear transpa rent tape segment 68 located near the end of the ribbon is provided to allow light to pass from a light source 64 on a sensor 58 to a reflector 48 positioned on the side of the ribbon 14 remote from the sensors and back to a photo dectector 66 , on the sensor. For plural such sensors 58 the sensing system includes a compound reflector 48 , having a first reflective portion 74 for use in cooperation with one sensing device 58a and having a second reflec tive position 72 for use in cooperation with another sensing device 58b .
