# Fuel control system for a diesel internal combustion engine and method of energizing a fuel injector within a cylinder of such an engine.

## Abstract
Fuel control system for energizing a fuel injector within a cylinder of a diesel engine, which fuel injector includes a metering chamber and a two coil solenoid having a movable plunger and operatively situated relative to the metering chamber for permitting a controlled quantity of fuel to flow into said metering chamber.
