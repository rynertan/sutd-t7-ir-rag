# Method of preparing sprayable polyurethane compositions.

## Abstract
A method of forming a sprayable polyisocyanurate com position having short cream and cure times which comprises mixing and allowing to react substantially equal volumes of an A side component comprising a low functionality methyl ene diisocyanate or a prepolymer thereof with a low hydroxyl polyether triol and a B side component comprising a diol, a low hydroxyl polyether triol and a catalyst system comprising a trimerization or cream time catalyst and a heat activatable catalyst.
