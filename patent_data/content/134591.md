# Curing apparatus.

## Abstract
An apparatus for use in curing articles with light from an ultraviolet lamp 28 has a conveyor 30 on which articles are passed under the lamp 28 which is contained in a housing 12 , the conveyor exit and entry sides of the housing 12 being provided with baffles 64 which prevent escape of light from the housing 12 while allowing air flow there through to enhance cooling within the housing 12 .
