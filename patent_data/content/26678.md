# Electrostatographic printing machine.

## Abstract
An electrostatographic printing machine having a development system 18 in which a plurality of spaced apart magnetic members 81 transport developer material 56 from a supply 52 thereof. The spaces 82 between adjacent magnetic members 81 enables extraneous developer material 56 to pass therethrough and return to the storage supply 52 thereof. The magnetic members 81 are supported on bars 80 which connect together two spaced apart discs in a generally cylindrical configuration.
