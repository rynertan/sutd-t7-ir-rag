# 4 4 Phenyl 1 piperidinyl methyl 5 acyl 1,3 dihydro 2H imidazol 2 ones.

## Abstract
1,3 Dihydro 2H imidazol 2 ones having 5 acyl and 4 4 phenyl 1 piperidinyl methyl substituents, are useful as car diotonics, antihypertensives and antithrombotic agents. The compounds are obtained by the reaction of an appropriate substituted piperidine with a substituted 4 bromomethyl 1,3 dihydro 2H imidazol 2 one.
