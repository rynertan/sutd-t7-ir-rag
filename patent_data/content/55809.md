# Production of enduracidin and microorganisms therefor.

## Abstract
Enduracidin is produced by cultivating an enduracidin producing microorganism, belonging to the genus Strep tomyces strains of Streptomyces fungicidicus GAB 453 IFO 14036 , Emt 36 3 IFO 14037 and Emt 2 140 IFO 14088 and having enduracidin excreting ability or m fluoro DL tyrosine resistance or both these properties, until enduraci din is substantially accumulated in the culture broth, and then recovering the same.
