# TETRAZOLE DERIVATIVES

## Abstract
The invention describes various novel tetrazone deriva tives of the formula I in which R¹ and R² are independently hydrogen, trifluoromethyl or 1 6C alkyl but R¹ and R² is not greater than 6 carbon atoms , or R¹ is optionally substituted phenyl and R² is hydrogen, Y is vinylene, n is 1 or 2 and m is 1, 2 or 3, or a pharmaceutically acceptable salt thereof, for use in conjunction with their pharmaceutical compositions in the treatment of various pulmonary and or vascular disorders. Also described are various processes and intermediates for the manufacture of the novel compounds.
