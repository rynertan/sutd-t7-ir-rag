# 3 HYDROXY 3 METHYLGLUTARIC ACID MONOAMIDE, DERIVATIVES THEREOF, INTERMEDIATES, METHODS OF PREPARATION AND COMPOSITIONS

## Abstract
Disclosed as novel compounds are 3 hydroxy 3 methylglutaric acid monoamide and the lower alkyl esters and salts thereof, 3 hydroxy 3 methylglutaramide mononitrile and 3 hydroxy 3 methylglutaramide. 3 Hydroxy 3 methylglutaric acid monoamide and derivatives thereof exhibit anti germination and anti growth utility against a wide variety of perennial and annual weed grasses and have furthermore pharmaceutical utility as serum lipid lowering agents. 3 Hydroxy 3 methylglutaramide mononitrile and 3 hydroxy 3 methylglutaramide have utility as intermediates in the preparation of 3 hydroxy 3 methylglutaric acid monoamide.
