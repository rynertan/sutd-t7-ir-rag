# A manure transfer pump.

## Abstract
A manure transfer pump 19 with a cylinder 28 , a piston 30 reciprocable in the cylinder 28 , a connecting rod 22 connected to a mid section of the piston 30 and a gate assembly 84 pivotally mounted inside the piston 30 bet ween the axis of the connecting rod connection 110, 112 and the rod end of the piston. In operation, the gate assembly 84 swings open during a return stroke and allows manure to pass through the piston 30 . On a pumping stroke, the gate assembly 84 closes and manure is forced out the discharge end of the cylinder 28 and sucked in the inlet end at the same time.
