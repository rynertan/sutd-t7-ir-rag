# A box like pallet, especially for maturing cheese.

## Abstract
A box like pallet provided with a bottom 1 and two or more interconnected, upright side walls 2, 3, 4, 5 . The two side edges of at least one side wall are con nected to the adjacent side walls, in an upwardly and down wardly movable manner relative to the bottom, by means of at least two strips 6,7 which are pivotably mounted on the side walls.
