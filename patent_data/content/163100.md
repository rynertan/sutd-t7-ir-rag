# Weight distributing prosthesis attached to the bone for below and above the knee amputations.

## Abstract
A prosthesis made of metal, plastic, or combination, to be installed at the cut edge of the bone during amputation of the limb in order to enlarge the weight bearing area of the bone at the stump, the said prosthesis including a wide base 7 , provided with a socket 6 or pin, by which it will be attached to the bone.
