# NUTRIENTS FOR MUSHROOM CULTURE

## Abstract
A growth medium for mushrooms contains one or more nutrients absorbed into particles of an inert material having a porous structure. The inert material may be vermiculite which may contain at least 50 of its own weight of nutrient. Preferred nutrients, present in an amount of from 0.01 to 10 wt of dry growth medium, may be sugar alcohols and lipids. The growth medium may also contain 0.1 to 10 of one or more ohter nutrients, eg aldehyde treated protein, added in conven tional manner.
