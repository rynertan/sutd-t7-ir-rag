# Bifocal intraocular lens structure.

## Abstract
The invention contemplates structure for surgical im plantation within an eye and providing the user with more than one focal length, for viewing at correspondingly differ ent object ranges. In the embodiments disclosed, one intra ocular device 10 is relatively fixed in the eye to provide a conventionally available viewing range, for example, dis tance viewing, and another lens element 12 is movably mounted with respect to the fixed distance viewing lens 10 in such manner as to provide its selective use in combi nation with the fixed lens 10 , for close object viewing.
