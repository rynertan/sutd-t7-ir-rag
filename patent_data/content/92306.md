# Electronic mobile.

## Abstract
A device 44 for storing and delivering rotational en ergy to a mobile 12 is used in combination with an elec trical motor 40 and a control device 28 adapted for inter mittent actuation of the motor 40 for a predetermined pe riod at predetermined intervals. The energy storage device 44 has at least one multi filament non braided cord 54 connected between first and second end caps 50 . The first end cap is detachably connected to the motor 40 and the second end cap is detachably connected to the mobile 12 . The cords 54 are wound by and in response to actuation of the motor 40 and urge the mobile 12 to rotate following deactivation of the motor 40 .
