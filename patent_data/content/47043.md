# VENTILATION SYSTEM FOR AN ANIMAL HOUSE

## Abstract
A ventilation device of an air circulation system for an animal house and, more specifically, for poultry such as lay hens brooding and rearing poultry which are confined in cages, with a floor 7 which is foraminous so that it will let through the droppings and underneath it, comprises a conveyor 8 to collect and transport the droppings of the animals, of which the space 20 bounded by the conveyor belt, has been closed at both sides such, that it is part of the circulation system.
