# Apparatus for injecting plants.

## Abstract
A two component, telescopically compressible, hollow container for plant injectible fluid compositions is provided with a sealing mechanism for negating capillary or other type of fluid leakage between closely fitting telescopic portions of the container walls. In a preferred form, the sealing mechanism comprises one or more circumferential ridges 60,62,64 extending between the telescopic portions of the container walls.
