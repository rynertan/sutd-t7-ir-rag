# Automatic sample apparatus and sampling method.

## Abstract
The invention relates to an automatic sampling apparatus for sample injection in a gas chromatographic column. In order to allow automatic injection with non vaporizing direct on column injector, the apparatus comprises a switch valve, an on column injector and a pre column having passages for the injection syringe with diameter greater than 0,5 mm. The invention also relates to a device provided with a small arms driving the needle during injection, a switch valve featured and operating in a way to prevent any contamination of the injector by the sample during the injection step, as well as an injection method which can be used with said apparatus and valve.
