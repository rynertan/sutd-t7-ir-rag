# AUTOCORRECTIVE SIDE SHIFT EXERCISE INCENTIVATED BY EXTERNAL APPARATUS, FOR THE TREATMENT OF ADOLESCENT IDIOPATHIC SCOLIOSIS

## Abstract
To correct the spine deformities, in particular to correct the scoliosis by lateral translation, it is foreseen the genera tion of stimuli apt to incentivate in the subject the correction of the spine by lateral shift obtained with ones muscles, and to induce such stimuli on the patient only if and when the same is not in a suitable position of the back to have a correction of the deformity, and also in such a way to interrupt the stimuli if and when the patient assumes the required position of the back for a correct treatment. An apparatus for this purposes utilized the trunk deformity.
