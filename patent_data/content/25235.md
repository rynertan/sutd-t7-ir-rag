# Arrangement for the advance of packaging material.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a regulating arrangement in par ticular on packing machines for the feed of weblike packing material in register with decorations or folding indications, so called crease lines, present on the web 2 . In accordance with the invention the desired control is obtained in that the tension of the web 2 is regulated with the help of a so called guide roller 6 over which the web 2 is passed, the speed of the guide roller 6 being regulated so that a web tension required for a feed according to register is obtained.
