# FLAME RETARDANT NON DRIPPING POLYCARBONATE COMPOSITIONS EXHIBITING IMPROVED THICK SECTION IMPACT

## Abstract
Flame retardant, non dripping polycarbonate com positions exhibiting good thick section impact comprised of, in admixture i a minor amount of a fluorinated polyolefin and ii a carbonate copolymer based on the reaction products of a a carbonate precursor, b at least one halogen free and sulfur free dihydric phenol, and c at least one halogen free thiodiphenol.
