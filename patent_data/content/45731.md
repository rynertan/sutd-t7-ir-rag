# A prefabricated balcony unit.

## Abstract
A prefabricated balcony unit including a platform 8 , safety rails 9 , and attachment means 10 , for renovation of balconies in old buildings. The platform 8 is in the shape of a coffer which is open towards the wall to which the balcony unit is to be anchored, said coffer arranged to enclose the free surfaces of the existing but damaged concrete platform of the original balcony. The balcony unit 7 is also provided with attachment means designed to anchor the balcony unit to the building and dimensioned jointly to take normal loads on the balcony with the prescribed safety margin and preferably also the dead load of the concrete platform of the original balcony.
