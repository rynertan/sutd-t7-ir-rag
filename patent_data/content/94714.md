# GEL LIKE CALCIUM SALT OF 1 HYDROXY 3 AMINOPROPANE 1,1 DIPHOSPHONIC ACID

## Abstract
Calcium 1 hydroxy 3 aminopropane 1,1 diphosphonate forms a gel when mixed with water. As compared to soluble salts of APD, the gel provides slow systemic release and reduced tissue damage when used in the treatment of certain disorders in warm blooded animals.
