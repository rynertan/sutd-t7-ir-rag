# Chemical product, process for the preparation and coating compositions thereof and articles coated therewith.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a chemical product produced by reacting a methylolamino compound and at least one polyhy dric alcohol of functional derivative thereof the said alcohol being selected from diols of formula I The invention further relates to a process for preparing the chemical product of the invention and compositions there of. The chemical product of the invention is of particular use in the preparation of coating compositions.
