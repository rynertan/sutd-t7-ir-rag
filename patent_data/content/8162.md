# Text processing and editing device.

## Abstract
A text editing device has an input keyboard 10 with data keys 13 and control keys 14 . A digital controller 11 couples to the keyboard for receiving input signals representative of the keys as they are manually depressed, for storing digital data words representative of the depressed data keys, and for performing predetermined editing opera tions on the data words in response to the depressed control keys. The editing operations produces edited data words. An electrochemical keypusher 12 is coupled to receive the output signals. The keypusher mounts on any office typewritter and depresses keys thereon corresponding to the edited data words represented by the output signals.
