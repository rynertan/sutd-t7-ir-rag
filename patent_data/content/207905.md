# COOKING IMPLEMENT ASSEMBLY WITH BUILT IN OVEN AND HOB

## Abstract
An oven and hob assembly, suitable to a built in construc tion inside a top plane of a kitchen furniture, includes an edge projecting all around the hob for bearing upon said top plane. The knobs and the other control and alarm devices, are located on the hob itself, the overall height of the assembly being smaller than the difference in level between said top surface and the floor supporting the kitchen.
