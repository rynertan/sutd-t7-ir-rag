# An X ray computerized tomographic apparatus.

## Abstract
In an X ray computerized tomographic apparatus for utilizing a Scanographic scanning operation taken prior to a CT scanning operation, it is desirable to designate a plurality of CT scanning portions of the patient by using a horizontal line on a display screen where intervals between the successive lines are freely adjustable in accordance with the portions of the patient s body which will receive the CT scanning. To this end, there is provided a converting means 10 for converting the X ray data from the detector array into actual position data for the patient who which will be scanned by CT mode.
