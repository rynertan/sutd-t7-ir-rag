# IMPROVED PACKAGING

## Abstract
A pallet container comprises a carton body 8 formed from stiff folded material, the base 6, 7 of which supports pallet feet 1,2 . The pallet feet are secured in place by slidably engaging the feet over keyway strips 1a, 1b, 2a, 2b which prevent movement of the feet in directions parallel to the base but normal to the engaging direction and in the direction away from the base normal thereto.
