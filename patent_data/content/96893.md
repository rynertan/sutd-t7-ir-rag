# Thermoplastic resin composition.

## Abstract
A thermoplastic resin composition having improved weld strength, said composition consisting essentially of A 45 to 94.9 by weight of a polycarbonate resin, B 5 to 40 by weight of a rubber modified styrene ma leic anhydride copolymer resin, and C 0.1 to 15 by weight of an epoxy resin and or a phen oxy resin.
