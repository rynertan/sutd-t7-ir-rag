# Drive belt.

## Abstract
Drive belt comprising an endless carrier band 4 and plate shaped transverse members 1 with a central recess 5 for the band 4 the top edge 9 of the recess, starting from one of the upstanding side edges 8, extending over at least half the width of the carrier band 4 and being connected to a narrow slot 13 which opens into the top edge 10 of the member 1 and via which the transverse members 1 can be fitted around the band 4 with the slot 13 alternately facing one or the other edge of the band.
