# CYLINDER HEAD FOR A DIRECT INJECTION DIESEL ENGINE FOR MOTOR VEHICLES

## Abstract
A cylinder head 10 for a direct injection diesel engine for motor vehicles has, for each cylinder, an air inlet 14 con trolled by an inlet valve, and first and second inlet ducts 28, 30 disposed transversely on opposite sides of the inlet 14 and opening into the latter substantially tangentially in posi tions such as to cause the air admitted to the inlet 14 to ex hibit rotary motions with same directions of rotation.
