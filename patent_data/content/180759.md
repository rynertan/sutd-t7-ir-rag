# Magnetic tape cassette with reel brake mechanism.

## Abstract
A magnetic tape cassette is provided with a reel brake 20 and a tape protecting lid 17 . The tape protecting lid 17 has side plates 31 extending parallel to the side walls 24b of the cassette casing 10 and pivoted on the side walls 24b . The reel brake mechanism comprises a brake plate 20 free to move between a braking position and a brake release position and normally biased toward the braking position. One of the reel brake plate 20 and the side wall 31 of the tape protecting lid 17 is provided with means for maintain ing interengagemant between the reel brake plate 20 and the side wall 31 to as to ensure cooperation therebetween for automatic brake release in reesponse to opening of the tape protecting lid 17 .
