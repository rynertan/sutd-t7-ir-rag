# A device for securing the ends of a strap detachably to eyeglasses legs.

## Abstract
The device comprises a sleeve element 7 located on a portion of each eyeglasses leg 1, 2 coaxially therewith, and a half tubular element 14 provided with a plate like end 15 for connection to the end 5 of the strap 4 , said half tubular element 14 having an inside profile that conforms with the outside profile of said sleeve 7 whereover it is engageable by snap action.
