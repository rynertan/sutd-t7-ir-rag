# AZAHOMOERYTHROMYCIN D DERIVATIVE AND INTERMEDIATES THEREFOR

## Abstract
Antibacterial 9 deoxo 9a methyl 9a aza 9a homo erythromycin D, pharmaceutically acceptable salts thereof, pharmaceutical compositions comprising antibacterially effective amounts thereof, a method of treatment of bacterial infections with antibacterially effective amounts thereof, and intermediates for the synthesis thereof from erythromycin D.
