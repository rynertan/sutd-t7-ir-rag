# Welding head for resistance welding.

## Abstract
A welding head for resistance welding, in particular for spot welding, comprising a frame to which a first welding electrode is fastened in a fixed position and a carrier for a second electrode lying substantially opposite the first elec trode, said carrier being movable with respect to the frame in a manner such that the second electrode can be displac ed in the direction to the first electrode, the second elec trode being fastened to an element which is displaceably connected with the carrier in a manner such that it can per form a movement in a direction substantially parallel to the direction of movement of the carrier.
