# A method of hormonal treatment of peri menopausal, menopausal and post menopausal disorders and multi preparation pack therefor.

## Abstract
Pharmaceutical compositions comprise an estrogen and a progestogen, generally in the form of a unit dosage containing both or a pack containing unit dosages of each. The size of each unit dosage is normally between 0.005 mg and 2.5 mg estrogen and 0.25 mg and 30 mg progestogen. Slow release compositions may provide similar dosages. The administration of these low dosages continuously, or of progestogen continuously and estrogen for periods of 20 to 120 days followed by an interval of 3 to 7 days, is useful for treating menopausal including peri menopausal and post menopausal disorders in women.
