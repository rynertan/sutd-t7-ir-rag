# Method of mounting a grinding wheel on a spindle.

## Abstract
A grinding wheel 18 is mounted on a tapered spindle 10 by die moulding in which bond material is injection moulded into a central hole 20 in the wheel about the end of the spindle but is prevented from entering the space between the die liner 26 and tapered spindle 14 by a seal 36 at the end of the die liner.
