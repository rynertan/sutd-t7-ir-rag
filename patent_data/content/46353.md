# ANTISTATIC FIBRE LOCK WEAVE PRIMARY CARPET BACKING

## Abstract
Primary carpet backing comprising a woven tape backing with staple fiber fleece needlebonded thereto wherein conduc tive yarns are woven into the backing in superposed or piggy back relationship to warped yarns, 2 to 8 ends per inch 0.79 to 31 ends cm of the conductive yarn being used. The product serves as a primary backing for carpets, the pile yarn being tufted therein. Good anti static properties are obtained.
