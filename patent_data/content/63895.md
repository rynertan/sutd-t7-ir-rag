# Improvements in and relating to drive mechanisms.

## Abstract
A bicycle 120 having a frame 121 , a driven wheel 123 rotatably supported on the frame 121 , a drive sprocket 126 rotatably mounted on the frame 121 , a driven sprocket 125 fixed to said wheel 123 , a chain 127 extending between the sprockets 125, 126 , a pair of levers 130, 131 pivotally attached to the frame 121 , and a pair of links 128, 129 extending between the drive sprocket 126 and levers 130, 131 , a pedal 132 fixed to the extremity of each lever, and wherein said drive sprocket 126 is rotated by the reciprocation of said pedals 132 .
