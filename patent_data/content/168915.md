# Producing anaerobic condtions.

## Abstract
Anaerobic conditions are produced in a main chamber 10 having selective communication 18 with a lock chamber 12 from separate supplies of anaerobe nuture gas 20 , inert gas 22 and oxygen consuming gas 32 . Flushing is from the lock chamber 12 and oxygen consuming gas is supplied 34 only after flushing and first, if not only the lock chamber 12 .
