# Wound drainage device.

## Abstract
The invention provides a wound drainage device compris ing a manually operable wound suction pump 16 for removing and collecting drainage fluid from a body wound 14 , the pump having an inlet port 40 for receiving drainage fluid from a body wound, and an outlet 60 for discharging drainage fluid therefrom, a fluid drainage storage container 18 having an in let port 78 adapted for connection with the pump outlet for receiving and storing discharged drainage fluid from the pump, and connection means 66 for releasably connecting the con tainer and pump together in side by side relation.
