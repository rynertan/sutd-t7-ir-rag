# A vitrious solid lithium cation conductive electrolyte.

## Abstract
A solid state electrolyte for solid state cell systems com prising a vitreous lithium cation conductor of the composi tion SiS₂, xLi₂S, yLil where x is from 0.8 to 1.5, where y is from 0 to about 2 and wherein said composition has a con ductivity of at least 0,75 10 ⁴ ohm ¹ cm ¹ at 25 C.
