# An apparatus to cut out the vent of a bird.

## Abstract
An apparatus to cut out the vent of a bird to be made ready for consumption substantially comprises a number of rotatingly driven hollow cylindrical knives 27 which each are secured to a slide 2 provided with a roller 4 which engages a curve path 6 of the apparatus of the kind of a roundabout, and a corresponding number of positioning means 3 also provided with a roller 5 which engages an other curve path 7 . The problems relating to different dimensions of the birds and its legs are overcome by a structure causing that the rollers do not necessarily follow the pertaining curve paths.
