# Submarine cable joint housing.

## Abstract
An armoured submarine cable joint comprising a tubular housing 6 with internally tapered end portions 6a, 6b closely fitting the outer sheath 5 of the submarine cable wherein the armouring wires 4 or tapes adjacent each cable end are splayed and deformed within each tapered end portion. The housing is fabricated from glass filament wound epoxy resin potted with a filled epoxy resin system 11.
