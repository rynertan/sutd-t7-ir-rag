# Antihypertensive 3 aryl 1,2 benzisoxazolsulfonyl and sulfinyl alkanoic acids, a process for their preparation and their use for the preparation of medicaments.

## Abstract
Novel 1,2 benzisoxazol sulfinylalkanoic and sulfonyl alkanoic acids and derivative compounds, methods for preparing them and methods of treatment administering compositions containing the compounds which are useful as antihypertensives.
