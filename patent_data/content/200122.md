# Slide fastener suitable for use on articles made of plastics material.

## Abstract
A slide fastener suitable for use on an article made of a plastics material comprises a pair of stringer tapes 11, 11 each including an element supporting portion 12 formed of a woven or knit fabric, supporting on and along its one longitudinal edge a row of coupling elements 14 and disposed in confronting relation to a guideflange 18 of a slider 15 , and a web portion 13 made of a plastics material and joined with the element supporting portion 12 for the attachment to the plastic article. Such combination of the fabric element supporting portion 12 with the plastic web portion 13 provides prolonged service life of the slide fastener without being effected by changing ambient tem perature.
