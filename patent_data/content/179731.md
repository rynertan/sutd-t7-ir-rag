# NEOALKOXY TITANATE IN HIGH DENSITY MICA LAMINATES

## Abstract
The present invention comprises a high density, mois ture resistant, high temperature stable mica laminate. Said laminate comprises plurality of mica papers, impregnated with a polysiloxane binder containing an organic neoalkoxy titanate and a metal napthenate. A process of preparing said mica laminates is also disclosed. The laminates of this invention have exceptional structural qualities, moisture resistance, stain resistance, and densities of 1.5 g cc or greater.
