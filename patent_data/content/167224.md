# Tyre building apparatus.

## Abstract
Tyre building apparatus in which a former 20, 35 is mounted on a first rotatable turret 10, 12 , and a component carrier 30, 51 of tubular form is mounted on a second rotatable turret adjacent the first turret. The former and the component carrier are arranged to be relatively movable at a turret station to enable the former to be surrounded by the component carrier so as to permit transfer of a tyre building component between the former and the component carrier.
