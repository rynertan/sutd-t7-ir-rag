# Water heaters.

## Abstract
A water heater has an inlet 12 and an outlet 13 and electrical heating means 17 21 to theat water which flows through the heater, the electric heating means 17 21 comprising a plurality of heating elements each adapted to be switched on and off in response to a control means 30 whereby the heat dissipated to the flowing water from the electric heating means 17 21 can be varied by arranging for the elements to be switched on and off in different combinations, and at least one 17 of the elements being burst fired.
