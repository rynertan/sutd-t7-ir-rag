# Beta lactam antibiotics.

## Abstract
7 beta 2 2 Aminothiazol oxazol 4 yl 2 oxomino acetamido 3 substituted thiazolium 3 yl methyl 3 cephem 4 carboxylates and the correspondingly substituted 1 oxadethia and 1 carbadethia 3 cephem compounds are potent antibacterials useful in a therapeutic method for treating bacterial diseases. Also provided are pharmaceutical formulations of these thiazolium substituted compounds and intermediates useful in the preparation thereof.
