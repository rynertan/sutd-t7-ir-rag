# A connecting device between two wheel rims.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a connecting device 3 between two wheel rims 1, 2 comprising an annular intermediate piece in the embodiment of a split ring 10 being radially expandable by means of tensioning means 12 at the position of the dividing slit 11 , the end edges of the intermediate piece 10 being outwardly offset whereby they are permitted to engage behind cams 6, 7 provided at the inner circumferential side of the facing edge portions 4, 5 of both rims and may be clamped by radially expanding the intermediate piece 10 against the relative rim edge portions 4, 5 .
