# Bearing and universal joint assembly.

## Abstract
A wheel support assembly for a motor vehicle compris ing a bearing unit with inner 10 and outer 11 members and rolling elements 12, 13 between them and a universal joint member 17 with a retaining element 20,23 which has been deformed to hold the members together, and the removal of which to permit dismantling of the assembly leaves at least one of the members undamaged and fit for further service. Torque transmission between the members may be by way of tapered splines 16 .
