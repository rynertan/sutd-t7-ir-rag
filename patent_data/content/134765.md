# Motor vehicle having a conveyor for air for cooling the radiator for the engine coolant liquid.

## Abstract
A motor vehicle has a radiator 12 for the liquid of the engine cooling circuit supported by the front part 10 of the motor vehicle bodywork, and a conveyor 25 for air cooling the radiator having a part 26 which is movable resiliently towards the front part of the bodywork in the case of a frontal collision of the vehicle.
