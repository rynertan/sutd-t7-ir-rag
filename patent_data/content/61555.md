# Spectacle frame with flexible parts.

## Abstract
This invention concerns a frame for spectacles compris ing one or more flexible elements fitted between parts of the front and of the sides, in order to make the frame itself highly adaptable to the anatomy of the face said flexible elements comprise a helical spring 10 and two pins 11 and 11 , one end of which engages with said spring 10 whereas the opposite end is secured to said parts of the front and of the sides 14 and 14 the threaded ends 12 and 12 of said pins are equal in diameter d to the internal diameter of said spring 10 and have a pitch equal to the pitch of the coils of said spring, whilst their combined length I₁ I₂ is less than the length I₃ of said spring.
