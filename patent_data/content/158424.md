# Method for producing hydroquinone.

## Abstract
Hydroquinone is produced by contacting the cells de fined below and or after treatment products thereof contain ing an oxidase system with benzene and or phenol in the presence of molecular oxygen or air and an aqueous medium and, furthermore, in the presence or absence of an electron source, said cells being obtained by culturing in a culturing medium a microorganism belonging to Rhodococcus or My cobacterium and capable of oxidizing benzene and or phenol.
