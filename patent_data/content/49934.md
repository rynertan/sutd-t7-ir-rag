# Optically readable information disk.

## Abstract
Optcally readable information disc having a radiation cured lacquer layer comprising an optically readable infor mation track whereby the lacquer layer has a minimum identation hardness which corresponds to a maximum depth of penetration of 5 µm of a needle which is placed on the lacquer layer and which has a ball shaped tip manufactured from a hard material and having a diameter of 18 1 µm and presses on the layer with a weight of 2.5 0.1 g.
