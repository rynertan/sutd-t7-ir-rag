# Polypeptide relating to alpha hANP.

## Abstract
The polypeptides represented by the general formula R Asn Ser Phe Arg Tyr OH wherein R is H, Cys, Gly Cys, Leu Gly Cys, Gly Leu Gly Cys, Ser Gly Leu Gly Cys, Gln Ser Gly Leu Gly Cys, Ala Gln Ser Gly Leu Gly Cys, Gly Ala Gln Ser Gly Leu Gly Cys, or Ile Gly Ala Gln Ser Gly Leu Gly Cys, and the salt thereof, useful for development of reagents for clinical diagnosis of essential hypertension or in research of pathophy siology.
