# Slide fastener.

## Abstract
A slide fastener 10 includes a row of coupling elements 13, 14 secured by sewing stitches 21 to one longitudinal edge of a support tape 11, 12 made of a fabric material free of threads woven or knitted together. The sewing stitches 21 are formed of at least one thread 23 having loops passing through the support tape 11, 12 . The thread 23 is made of a monofilamentary material which is free from thermal defor mation. The slide fastener 10 can retain, after heat set treat ment, sufficient mechanical strength at the connection bet ween the sewing stitches 21 and the row of coupling ele ments 13, 14 .
