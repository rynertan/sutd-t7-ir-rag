# Protective cover for electrical connector receptacle open end.

## Abstract
The invention is directed to a protective cover for the open face of the receptacle section of a multi con tact electrical connector when the receptacle section and the plug section are not mated. The protective cover comprises a barrel 22 and a cover plate 28 closing one end of the barrel. Inside the barrel there is positioned a spring means 60 so located that a disc 54 placed over the spring means can be controllably moved longitudinally in said barrel to contact the end of said receptacle open face. At the outer end of said barrel are indexing means 42 for polarised alignment with indexing means on said receptacle and locking means 50 , for locked retainment of said cover to said receptacle socket.
