# Polymeric compositions endowed with improved flame resistance properties.

## Abstract
Polymeric compositions endowed with improved self extinguishing properties, comprising by weight A from 60 to 98.9 of a thermoplastic polymer B from 0.1 to 10 of an antimony and or bismuth compound C from 0 to 1 of a promoter of free radicals D from 1 to 30 of melamine bromohydrate.
