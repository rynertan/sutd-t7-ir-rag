# Dunnage bags, their production and their use in shoring freight.

## Abstract
An inflatable dunnage bag 10 is formed by a flat, flexible triangular envelope constructed of heavy kraft paper 16, 18 and a sealed inner plastic liner or bladder 11 which is inflatable through a valve 14 . The bag 10 is used for shoring a load by standing it upright with its base topmost, horizontally oriented, and positioned adjacent the top of the load, and with its apex 25 positioned contiguous to the bottom perimeter of the load.
