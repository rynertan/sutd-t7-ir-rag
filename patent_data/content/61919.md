# IMPROVEMENTS IN COMMERCIAL VEHICLE BODIES

## Abstract
A vehicle body floor comprises a one piece moulded or extruded structure of fibre reinforced plastics material 12 incorporating a structural core 16 , with an integrally associated sub frame in the form of longitudinal and or transverse reinforcing ribs 18, 19 into which the structural core is extended. Analogously moulded frames 36 or pillars 34 are bonded by plastics adhesive to the floor 10 to form a vehicle body structure.
