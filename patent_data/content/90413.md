# CARTRIDGE AND DISPENSER THEREFOR

## Abstract
A cartridge holds liquid, fluid or semi fluid substances in tubes, and the cartridge is set in an extractor. The cartridge can all extract the charged substance, prevent it from flowing out, and extract the substance quantitatively. Another embodiment of the cartridge is integrally pro vided with a needle so that it is applied as an injector, or to a container of holding a desired liquid or other extractor.
