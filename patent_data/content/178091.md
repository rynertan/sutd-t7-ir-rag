# Enclosure.

## Abstract
A controlled access enclosure comprises an external frame 1 within which is suspended a cover 15 . Access is gained to and from the unit by means of a zipped opening 20 in one part of the enclosure and a flap door arrangement in another part of the enclosure, the flap door being surrounded by a flange 21 which may be sealed to a hazardous working area to prevent contamination escaping from the working area into the sur rounding clean environment.
