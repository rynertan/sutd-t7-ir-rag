# Level indicator.

## Abstract
For displaying the ratio in decibels between the instantane ous value and the maximum value of a digital signal a rectified digital signal is serially applied to an input 4 of a level indicator. This indicator comprises a shift register 10 which, in the case of an indicator with a resolution of 2 dB, comprises three series connected flip flops 11, 12, 13 . The input M of the first flip flop 11 is connected to the input 4 of the level indicator. The flip flops 11, 12, 13 each comprise an output Q
