# A thermoplastic polyurethane product.

## Abstract
An antithrombogenic thermoplastic polyurethane pro duct, and process for preparing the same, the product comprising a substrate and at least one layer of a polyurethane alloy complex comprising a thermoplastic polyurethane and completely dispersed therein a preformed complex of an antithrombogenic material and or an antibio tic agent ionically bonded with a quaternary ammonium compound.
