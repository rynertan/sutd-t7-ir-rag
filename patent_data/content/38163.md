# Apparatus and method for recognizing spoken words.

## Abstract
A speech recognition technique is disclosed for recogniz ing words that are spoken at speeds that approach the speed of continuous speech. The characteristic form of each vocabulary word spoken by a user is stored in a reference array 110 . The process of in put speech recognition then includes the steps of detecting pauses in the speech so as to define strings of words or syl lables, and segments within each string extracting 60 fea tures from the input speech and using them to compare each segment and each combination of adjacent segments with the stored words 130 to obtain the best match and finally deter mining 140 the optimum sequence of vocabulary words for each word string.
