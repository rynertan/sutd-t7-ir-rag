# Process for preparing pyridine derivatives and intermediates useful in the process.

## Abstract
The invention provides a process for preparing 2 4 3 or 5 fluoromethylpyridyl 2 oxy phenoxy propionic acids and their derivatives by reducing 2 4 3 or 5 fluoromethylpyridyl 2 oxy phenoxy acrylic acids and their derivatives. It also provides the acrylic acids and their deriva tives themselves.
