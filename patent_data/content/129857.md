# Angle adjustable saddle.

## Abstract
An angle adjustable saddle comprising a bearing portion 2 secured directly to a saddle base 1 and having a lower surface curved longitudinally of the base 1 , a support portion 5 provided at the upper end of a seat pillar 4 and formed on its upper side a curved surface 51 conforming to the curved surface 23 of the bearing portion 2 , a threaded rod for detachably fastening together the bearing portion 2 and the support portion 5 with the curved surfaces 23, 51 fitting to each other and an internally threaded member having the threaded rod screwed therein, one of the bearing portion 2 and the support portion 5 being formed with a slot 7 extending longitudinally the base 1 .
