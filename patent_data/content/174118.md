# METHOD FOR PREPARING 6 CHLORO N METHYL 2,3,4,5 TETRAHYDRO 1H 3 BENZAZEPINE

## Abstract
A novel process for preparing 6 chloro N methyl 2,3,4,5 tetrahydro 1H 3 benzazepine comprising cyclizing N methyl N 2 2 chlorophenyl ethyl 2 chloroethylamine hydrochlo ride in a solution of trichlorobenzene and aluminum chloride.
