# An aqueous antimony sol composition.

## Abstract
An aqueous antimony sol composition useful in restoring the activity of metal contaminated molecular sieve cracking cat alysts which comprises a major portion of an aqueous anti mony sol containing between 1 50 by weight of antimony ox ide as Sb₂O₅ and between .1 10 by weight of a compatible water soluble surfactant which is capable of producing an oil in water emulsion and having an HLB of at least 8.0.
