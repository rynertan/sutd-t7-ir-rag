# Traction control device for a motor vehicle.

## Abstract
A traction control device in an engine which has an intake passage. A throttle valve 10 and a flow control valve 14 are arranged in the intake passage 11 . Where ac celeration of the motor vehicle is carried out, when the speed of rotation of the driven wheel or acceleration of the speed of rotation of the driven wheel 20, 21 exceeds a predetermined level, the flow control valve 4 is closed. When the speed or rotation of the driven wheel becomes lower than a predetermined level or deceleration of the speed of rotation of the driven wheel becomes larger than a predetermined level, the flow control valve is opened.
