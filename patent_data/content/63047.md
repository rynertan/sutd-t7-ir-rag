# APPARATUS FOR AND METHODS OF MONITORING READING STATEGIES

## Abstract
A reading protocol recorder which enables a record to be obtained of the strategies adopted by the reader of pages of text monitors the sequence in which text elements are read. An irradiating source 13 is directed towards a target zone into which coded areas 12 of pages 10, 11 are moved as the pages are turned. Sensors 15 view the target zone and a digital and or analog display and or record 18, 19 is obtained. The time intervals between turning of the pages are preferably also monitored.
