# IMPROVED PHOTOVOLTAIC CELL

## Abstract
A photovoltaic cell having improved stability and life time comprising a cadmium sulfide film, a copper sulfide film and deposited on the copper sulfide film, a film select ed from the group consisting of a film of chromium, a film of chromium having deposited thereon a film of copper, a film of copper having deposited thereon a film of chro mium and a film of an admixture of chromium and copper. A method for making such a photovoltaic cell is also dis closed.
