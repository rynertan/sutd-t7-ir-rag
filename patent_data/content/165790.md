# An accessory for a toilet receptacle.

## Abstract
The invention relates to an accessory 10 for a toilet receptacle which includes an auxiliary seat 18 adapted to be positioned on the existing ring seat 16 of a toilet receptacle 12 and act as a toilet seat for small children. The auxiliary seat is mechanically connected to a platform 20 which can serve as a step for small children to enable them to seat themselves on the auxiliary seat 18 , the seat 18 including the platform 20 being displaceable between inoperative and operative positions, so that use of the toilet receptacle by adults is not unduly inhibited.
