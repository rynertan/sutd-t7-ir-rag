# Sealing cup for hydraulic piston cylinder device.

## Abstract
In a sealing or piston cup 3 for maintaining lubricating oil on a sealing surface 4 of a hydraulic piston cylinder device, the sealing surface 4 thereof is formed with a circumferentially extending angular recess or groove 4a which functions to retain lubricating oil. The sealing cup with the recess can ensure a smooth and stable operation of the device.
