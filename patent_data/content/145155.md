# Dispenser for a flowable product.

## Abstract
A dispenser of the pressure accumulated type includes a unitary pump plunger 31 actuated for reciprocation within a variable volume pump chamber 33 defined by an accumu lator 23 which is displaced upon an accumulation of pressure for opening the discharge as a discharge valve 28 thereon becomes unseated, the compression stroke of the plunger acting in a direction opposite displacement of the accumulator in a direction opening the discharge.
