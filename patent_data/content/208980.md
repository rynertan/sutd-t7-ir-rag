# Apparatus for supporting vehicles and the like.

## Abstract
An apparatus for supporting a vehicle having a front wheel and a rear wheel on the same side of the vehicle. The ap paratus includes a wheel bar which extends adjacent to the wheels on one side of the vehicle and includes pairs of wheel supports positioned below and engaging two points on each wheel. The bar and supports are secured to the vehicle to firmly engage the wheels against the pairs of supports. The ve hicle is thereby supported by such wheel bars extending along both sides of the vehicle.
