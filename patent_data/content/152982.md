# Process for the preparation of air freshener gels.

## Abstract
Process for the preparation of aqueous air freshener gels having an increased perfume content of at least 5 and prefer ably more than 10 of the total weight of the gel by taking up the perfume in finely divided, porous, water insoluble, synthetic polymer as the carrier and subsequently combining the thus obtained perfume laden carrier with an aqueous gel or with the gelforming components.
