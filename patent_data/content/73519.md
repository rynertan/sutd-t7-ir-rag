# Optical disc player.

## Abstract
An optical disc player comprises a rotating member for rotating the optical disc 1 , an optical pickup member 4 for reproducing a digital audio signal including an error correcting code like a Reed Solomon Code from the disc 1 , an error correcting decoder 10 for decoding the error correcting code so as to produce a corrected or interpo lated digital audio signal and condition flag data indicating the correction of the digital audio signal, and a display 16 for indicating the condition flag data as stain condition of the disc 1 .
