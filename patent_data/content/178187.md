# Apparatus and method for continuously producing filled dough pieces.

## Abstract
An apparatus and method for continuously producing filled dough pieces 19 is provided. A pair of toothed rotary disc cutters 2,2 are arranged on a conveyor belt 4 feeding a filled cylindrical dough body 18 . A plurality of dividers 5 mounted on the periphery of a wheel 11 are disposed be tween and above the teeth. The dividers 5 and the teeth 1 rotate about their own axes to flatten the cylindrical dough body and to split it by their cooperatively synchronized movement whereby completely incrusted spherical or var iously shaped filled dough pieces 19 are produced.
