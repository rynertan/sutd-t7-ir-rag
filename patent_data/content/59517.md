# Device for separating and processing the gizzards of slaughtered poultry.

## Abstract
A device for separating the gizzard from the connected entrails of slaughtered poultry comprises an inlet 8 , two oblong cylindrical parallel rotating transport elements 2, 3 and a gizzard separating device 2b, 3b the first element 2 with a helical transport ribbon 9 along its circumference and the second element 3 with at least one row of longitudinal short ribs 11 projecting from the surface and fitting between the screw ribbon 9 of the first element 2 .
