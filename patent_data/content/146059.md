# INSECTICIDAL COMPOSITION

## Abstract
Water insoluble, photodynamic insecticidal composi tions, particularly comprising the free acids of erythrosin B, phloxin B, rose bengal, octabromofluorescein or fluorescein or the aluminum lakes thereof, in combination with a dispersant for use in aqueous dispersions of the same a method for com batting adult insects and insect larvae uses the above free ac ids or lakes.
