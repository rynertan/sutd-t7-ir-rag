# Bar feed.

## Abstract
A bar stock feed arrangement for an automatic machine tool, in which a pusher rod 3 , movable along the axis of a feed tube 6 to advance the bar stock B , is supported by an annular collar 8 . While the pusher rod 3 remains within the feed tube 6 the collar 8 is locked to the rod 3 by means of a ball and socket arrangement 9 11 . To permit the pusher rod 3 to emerge from the feed tube 6 the locking action is removed, the collar 8 still assuming a supportive role at the end of the feed tube 6 . Additional supportive means, in the form of split bushings 12, 13 , and a sound damping arrangement are also described.
