# Carbamates and oxalyl amides of amino N 1H tetrazol 5 yl benzamides.

## Abstract
Alkoxycarbonyl and oxalyl derivatives of amino N 1H tetrazol 5 yl benzamides are described herein. The com pounds involved are useful as antiallergic agents. These compounds can be prepared by the reaction of an amino N 1H tetrazol 5 yl benzamide with an appropriate acid chloride.
