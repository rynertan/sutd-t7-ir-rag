# Improvements in self energising disc brakes.

## Abstract
A self energising disc brake of the spreading type is provided with an actuator unit 21 incorporating hydraulical ly operable means 24 and mechanically operable means 40 . The unit comprises a member 35 which is detachably secured to the housing 4 of the brake and provides a mounting for both said means which extend into the housing through a common opening 20 . The hydraulically operable means comprises a cylinder 24 in which work opposed pistons 26, 27 acting to separate lugs 28, 29 on the pressure plates 5, 6 and the mechanically operable means may take any convenient form. The cylinder 24 is tangen tially arranged with respect to the plates 5, 6 .
