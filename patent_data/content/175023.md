# Sheet feeding apparatus.

## Abstract
A modular sheet feeding apparatus 10 for connection to a copying machine 1, 2 in which a plurality of cassettes 12a 12e are placed on a series of tables. The tables are mounted as a group and individually positioned for feeding sheets into the copying machine 1, 2 . The modular sheet feeding apparatus 10 can either include its own feed rolls 13a 13e for each table or use one feed roll that is mounted within the main frame 1 to be moved into and out of sheet feeding position.
