# Automatic milking apparatus.

## Abstract
An automatic milking apparatus and method in which milk from the teats is drawn past one or more non return valves without the introduction of air into the milk flow upstream of the valve or valves. In one embodiment, a number of clawpieces 14 are included in a milking installa tion to allow milk flow in one direction only, there being no air bleed during milking to the milk carrying core regions of the teat liners upstream of the clawpieces.
