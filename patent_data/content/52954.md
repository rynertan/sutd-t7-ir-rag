# Flame retardant polymers and monomers useful in producing them.

## Abstract
The novel compounds 2,2 bis bromomethyl propane 1,3 bis allylcarbonate and 2,2 bis bromomethyl propane 1,3 bis methallylcarbonate The compounds are flame retardant reactive monomers which can be homo or co polymerised e.g. with daily diglycol, acrylates or styrenes to produce flame retardant polymers.
