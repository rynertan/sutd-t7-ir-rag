# Device for applying a liquid or semi liquid medium to a web of flexible material.

## Abstract
A wallpaper pasting machine comprises a tray 12 for the paste, a roller 14 for transferring paste to the paper and guide means 16, 18 extending across the tray for training the paper over the rollers. The guide means are engaged by resi lient side wall portions 35 of the tray and are removable by deforming the side wall portions. A cutting template 22 for guiding the cutting of the paper is also described.
