# Penile erectile system.

## Abstract
A penile erectile system 10 which is adapted to be surgically implanted in man for the treatment of erectile impotence includes a penile implant 11 comprising an elongated, flexible cylindrical member adapted to be im planted in a corpus cavernosum of a penis. The cylindrical member including a pair of concentric chambers 17,18 with both the inner and outer chambers substantially filled with fluid 20 and connected by a passage 21 . The inner chamber 17 is non distensible so that when it is filled and pressurized by transferring fluid 20 thereto from the outer chamber 18 it becomes rigid.
