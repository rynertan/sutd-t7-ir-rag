# BOTTLE MOUNTING FOR CYCLE

## Abstract
This invention relates to a bottle mounting for use in mounting a beverage bottle 2 on a cycle frame 11 which mounting comprises a support means 4 for detachably engaging said bottle 2 to support said bottle 2 and a fixing means 3,7 for securing said support means 4 to a cycle frame member 11 . Known mounting systems give rise to turbulence and increased drag during riding of the cycle. According to this invention the mounting includes a pair of skirt means 5 disposed on said fixing means 3,7 so as to substantially cover recesses or gaps extending on either side of said mounting between the frame member 11 and the bottle 2 .
