# IMPROVED SNUFF AND PREPARATION THEREOF

## Abstract
An improved snuff containing substantially pure salts of nicotine diluted with organic sugars which is substantially absorbed by the nasal membranes when applied thereto. Flavoring and or coloring agents are optionally present. The improved snuff is prepared by adding an acid to a nicotine solution, separating the nicotine salt from the solution, dilut ing the nicotine salt to a usable concentration, and optionally adding flavoring and or coloring agents to enhance the fra grance and appearance of the improved snuff.
