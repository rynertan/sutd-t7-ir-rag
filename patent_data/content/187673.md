# Water resistant composition and heat sensitive recording sheet containing the same.

## Abstract
This invention relates to a water resistant composition which comprises A polyvinyl alcohol, B a chitosan, the aqueous 1 solution of which has a viscosity not greater than 70 centipoise at 20 C, and C an aldehyde compound, as well as a heat sensitive recording sheet which comprises said composition. The composition has excellent water resistant properties and the heat sensitive recording sheet has improved color forming properties and excellent resist ance to water and solvents.
