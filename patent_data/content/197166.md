# Catalyst prepared from organomagnesium compound, organic hydroxyl containing compound, reducing halide source and transition metal compound and a process for alpha olefin polymerization using such catalyst.

## Abstract
A catalyst for polymerizing olefins is the product result ing from mixing in an inert hydrocarbon diluent mixutre of A an organomagnesium material, B an organic hydroxyl containing material, C a reducing halide source and D a transition metal compound.
