# Polymer blends and articles prepared therefrom.

## Abstract
A polyester based intimate fusion blended composition comprising an admixture of a poly ethylene terephthalate and a copolyester of isophthalic acid terephthalic acid, ethylene glycon and 1,3 bis 2 hydroxyethoxy benzene. The composi tion is suitable for fabrication into container parison, hollow containers, sheet stock and film.
