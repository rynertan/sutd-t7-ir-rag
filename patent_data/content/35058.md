# Composition for the treatment of hemorrhoids by oral administration.

## Abstract
A composition and a method for treatment of hemor rhoids are disclosed. The composition comprises a combi nation of thiamin, riboflavin, pyridoxine, cobalamin, niacin amide, and pantothenic acid, preferably in liquid form. In preferred embodiments, the composition may also contain whole liver and or yeast or their extracts. The method com prises oral administration of the composition to a person suffering from hemorrhoids.
