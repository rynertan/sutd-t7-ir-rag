# Process and apparatus for applying labels.

## Abstract
To apply automatically a label to an object C , particularly a food product, of substantially cylindrical irregular shape, said object is introduced into a loop 2 purposely formed in a conveyor belt 1 , with one end of the label 3 interposed be tween said object and said belt, it is enveloped almost entirely with said belt inside said loop together with said label,where upon the conveyor belt is moved causing the rotation of said object and of said label, the ends of which are overlapped and then glued together.
