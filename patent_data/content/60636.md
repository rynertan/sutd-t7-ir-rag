# Splicing apparatus and cassette therefor.

## Abstract
A tape splicer comprises a base 10 with cutting plates 13 and 14 having cooperating cutting edges. A cassette 22 con tains adhesive items mounted on a roll of carrier strip and has an arcuate and grooved recess 59 formed in its base for receiv ing the sprocket wheel 30 of the drive mechanism whereby the carrier strip passes between the base and the wheel when driven by the actuating lever 34 via ratchet wheel 33. The cas sette housing includes an elongate delivery passage 52 which terminates in a curved portion 58 carrying an edge 58A pro vided to separate the adhesive items from the carrier.
