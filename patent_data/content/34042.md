# Recoverable closure assembly.

## Abstract
An assembly for sealing and protection of cable splices comprising a recoverable, preferably heat recoverable, outer sleeve 5 surrounding a cable or other body 1 a thermoplas tic support layer 7 surrounding the body 1 and within the sleeve 5 and a heat barrier layer, preferably foamed, be tween the support 7 and the sieve 5 , to prevent thermal de formation of the support 1 during neat recovery of the sleeve 5 or during other heat treatment.
