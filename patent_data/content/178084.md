# Ratchet mechanism.

## Abstract
A ratchet mechanism comprising a disc 21 rotatable on a pivot pin 11 , said disc 21 being provided with radially disposed ratchet teeth on a face of the disc, an engagement plate 12 provided with channels corresponding to and engage able with the teeth of the disc, the ratchet face of the rotatable disc being biassed into engagement with the ratchet face of the plate, and means for disengaging the ratchet disc 21 from the plate 12 . The mechanism is particularly suitable for the con struction of a hand brake for an automotive vehicle using ther moplastics as the material of construction.
