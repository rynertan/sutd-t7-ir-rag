# Optical connector.

## Abstract
An optical connector in which a pair of ceramic optical plugs 7 having optical fibers 1 extending coaxially therethrough are fitted within a ceramic sleeve 9, 11 . In one embodiment, an inner surface of the sleeve is provided with a longitudinal groove extending from one end of the sleeve to the other so that air and dust will be expelled from the sleeve when plugs are inserted therein. A housing 12 may be provided around the sleeve with a flange being formed on the housing. In another embodi ment, a protective cover 30 is provided surrounding each plug which is movable axially along the plug.
