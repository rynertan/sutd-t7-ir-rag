# An improved method of character recognitionand apparatus therefor.

## Abstract
This invention relates to an improved method of recog nizing a character on a sheet of paper 3 . By utilizing the method according to the present invention, characters or figures which have been unrecognizable by ordinary ways of distinction becomes much more automatically readable, due to the incorporation of an additional method of character frame discrimination followed by synthesizing both of the results of distinctions in terms of features and frame construction of the character.
