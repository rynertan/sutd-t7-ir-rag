# TUBE ARRANGEMENT FOR FURNACE WALL

## Abstract
A spiral wound furnace has the spiral wound tubes 18 terminated at the upper end by a vertically sinuous tube 25 with an upper return bend 36 and a lower return bend 38 . The lower return bend 38 has a restricted area connection 42 connected to spiral wound tube 18 , while the upper return bend 36 has a restricted area connector 44 fluidly connected to header 16.
