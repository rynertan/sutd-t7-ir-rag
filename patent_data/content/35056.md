# Replaceable cartridge valve assembly and sanitary fitting.

## Abstract
A replaceable cartridge valve assembly 20 adapted for use in a sanitary fitting 10 which includes a pair of discs 40, 42 , each disc having at least one opening or orifice therein to provide the valving function and an auxil iary valve means is disclosed. The valve assembly 20 is made in the form of a removable cartridge and includes an arrangement of the valve stem 32 which cooperates with an opening 23 in the cartridge housing to provide the auxiliary valve means for throttling downstream from the at least one disc orifice of the disc valve means to provide a stepdown in pressure drop and thereby inhibit and or reduce cavitation and other objectionable noises.
