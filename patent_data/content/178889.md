# Ink reservoir system for ink jet apparatus.

## Abstract
Ink jet apparatus for use with hot melt ink has an integrally connected ink jet head and reservoir system, the reservoir system having a sloping flow path 58 between an inlet position and a sump 60 from which ink is drawn to the head, the reservoir being housed in a housing 40 of good heat conductivity material with a heater 51 connected thereto, and further having one or more heat conducting elements 55, 57 positioned between the inlet position and the sump, which elements are constructed to act both as baffles and as heat conducting fins.
