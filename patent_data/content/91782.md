# Inclusion compound of Lankacidin group antibiotic and use thereof.

## Abstract
An inclusion compound of a lankacidin group antibiotic with cyclodextrin, having enhanced water solubility and stability and a veterinary composition containing the same, is useful in the treatment of swine dysentery. The inclusion compound can be prepared by adding a lankacidin group antibiotic to an aqueous cyclodextrin solution and stirring.
