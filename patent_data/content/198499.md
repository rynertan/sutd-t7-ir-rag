# CLEANING DEVICE FOR USE IN IMAGE FORMING APPARATUS

## Abstract
A cleaning blade 66 for use in an image forming appa ratus removes residual developer from a photosensitive member 20 . The cleaning blade 66 can be contact with the photosensitive member 20 . The blade extends sub stantially at right angels to the direction in which the photo sensitive member moves. It has a lubricant layer WX form ed on both end portions in the longitudinal direction of the blade, which forms part of a surface contacting the photo sensitive member.
