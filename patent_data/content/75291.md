# Beam indexing color picture tube arrangement.

## Abstract
A frontal light extraction type beam indexing color picture tube arrangement comprises a phosphor screen which is formed on the inner surface of the tube panel 2 and includes an array of color phosphor stripes each of which has an appropriate afterglow time and an array of index phosphor stripes 12a 18 I₁, I₂ each of which has a short afterglow time and emits a light index signal representative of the scanning position of an electron beam in the forward direction of the tube panel. A photo electric converter 7 for receiving the light index signal is provided in front of the tube panel.
