# Process for the preparation of hydrocarbons.

## Abstract
C₅ hydrocarbons are prepared from C₄ hydrocarbons by a two stage process comprising steam reforming fol lowed by Fischer Tropsch synthesis over a special cobalt containing catalyst a gaseous fraction comprising uncon verted H₂ and CO as well as C₄ hydrocarbons and CO₂ formed as by products is separated from the synthesized product and recycled to the steam reformer.
