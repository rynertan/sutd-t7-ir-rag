# Notepaper dispensing tray.

## Abstract
A notepaper dispenser for holding a pad of notepaper and permitting the dispensing of sheets singly from a pad 30 of notepaper and having a support surface for the pad, a clamping plate 25 for holding the bottom sheet of the pad to the support surface against movement in the plane of the support, and an abutment 31 for engaging an edge of the pad opposite the edges of the sheets joined together.
