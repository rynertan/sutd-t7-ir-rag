# PROCESS FOR PREPARING PIRBUTEROL

## Abstract
Pirbuterol is prepared by heating 5 hydroxy 2 1 hydroxy 2 t butylaminoethyl pyridine with aqueous formal dehyde in the presence of a weak base, e.g. triethylamine. The pirbuterol is conveniently isolated as the hydrochloride. Pirbuterol is a well known bronchodilator.
