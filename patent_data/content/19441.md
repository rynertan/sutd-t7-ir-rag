# Process and apparatus for producing moulded confections.

## Abstract
A process for forming a moulded confection, such as an ice confection, which has a protruding or re entrant shape i.e. with a surface portion having a negative draft angle with respect to a desired direction of mould release , characterised by a filling a mouldable confection mix into a thin walled flexible elastic mould having a cavity corresponding to said protruding or re entrant shape, and also having in its wall at least one thin walled flexible elastic cleft or pleat and b solidifying the mouldable mix within the mould, and withdrawing the solidified confection mix from the mould so as to cause the mould to distort and the cleft or pleat to open or unfold and release a confection of said protruding or re entrant shape from the mould.
