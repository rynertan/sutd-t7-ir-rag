# Dialysis needle.

## Abstract
A dialysis needle 22 or the like comprises a tube 28 with a razor edge cutting blade 32 extending diametrically across its forward end. The blade 32 has a generally V shape with a pair of razor sharp cutting edges 34 converging forwardly from opposite sides of the tube 28 and termi nating in a needle sharp point 36 . A pair of diametrically opposed, smooth, tissue spreading ribs 40 are located in a plane normal to the cutting edges 34 end converge for wardly from opposite sides of the tube 28 to positions im mediately rearwardly adjacent of the point 36 . Two specific embodiments are described an all stainless steel version and a plastic version with a cutting blade insert.
