# Endotracheal tube with movable endobronchial blocker.

## Abstract
An endotracheal tube with movable endobronchial blocker for one lung anesthesia comprising an endotracheal tube 35 with a cuff 41 having a small channel 37 formed within the wall of the endotracheal tube 35 along almost the entire length thereof and a catheter with a cuff 41 for endobronchial blockade named endobronchial blocker 51 which is housed in the small channel 37 and can be let out when in use.
