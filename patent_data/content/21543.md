# Gear case for a rotary cutter head of a suction dredger.

## Abstract
A gear case 2 for a rotary cutter head 7 of a suction cutter dredger, comprising at least two connecting members 3,4 each suitable for pivotally coupling the gear case to the ladder frame 1 , said connecting members being located at the acute angles of an imaginary right angled triangle, and the drive shaft 12 extending approximately along, or approximately parallel to, one of the legs of said triangle.
