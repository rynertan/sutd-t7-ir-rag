# Dialkyl fumarate vinyl acetate copolymers useful as dewaxing aids.

## Abstract
Copolymers of dialkyl fumarate and vinyl acetate having Specific Viscosities of at least 2.5 at 2 polymer concen tration in xylene at 40 C, and wherein at least 50 wt. of said alkyl groups are C₂₀ to C₂₄, are useful as dewaxing aids, particularly in propane dewaxing.
