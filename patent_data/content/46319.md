# Dock borne hull treating device.

## Abstract
A dock device having a bottom for supporting a ship or the like and longitudinal walls standing up from the bottom, a carriage adapted to be moved by driving means along a longitudinal wall, an arm pivotably and deflectably connected with the carriage, swinging and deflecting means for moving the arm with respect to the carriage, a head 7 carrying a processing member and being rotatably and tiltably connected with the free end of said arm and tilting and rotating means for moving said head 7 with respect to the arm, com prises horizontal 89 and vertical 81 guide wheels to be alternately actuated by setting means 70 are con nected with the head 7 .
