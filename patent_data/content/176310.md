# COILING MACHINE

## Abstract
A wire coiling machine 10 includes direct one to one synchronized drive from a rear drive arbor 30 through a pair of friction drive rolls 38a, 38b astraddle it, to a pair of flexible drive shafts 50a, 5Ob connecting the drive rolls to a pair of front forming rolls 60a, 60b of essentially the same diameter as the friction drive rolls, the forming rolls being astraddle a front forming arbor 34 , and the forming arbor having a diameter essentially the same as the internal diameter of the coil being formed thereon.
