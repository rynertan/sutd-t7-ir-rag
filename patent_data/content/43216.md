# Fastener setting tool.

## Abstract
Power operated rivet nut setting tool has a screw threaded pull bar 56 and a drive nut 58 by which the bar 56 is pulled rearwardly when restrained from rotation. A friction drive from the drive nut 58 to the bar 56 to rotate the bar 56 and a screw threaded mandrel 100 releasably coupled to it is provided by a split nut 68 keyed to the drive nut 58 and resiliently enhancing the thread of the pull bar 56 .
