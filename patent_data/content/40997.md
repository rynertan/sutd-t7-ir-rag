# Method of making a pressure tight seal for a glow plug.

## Abstract
Method of making a pressure tight seal for a glow plug characterized by applying a high current to heat a groove 31 in the outer shell 3 of a glow plug while simultaneously press ing together the sealing elements adlacent the groove 31 , i.e. an insulator 2 , a gasket 1 , the flared end 42 of a protective tube 4 and a shoulder 32 within the shell 3 . The groove 31 concentrates the heat and allows the shell 3 to be com pressed around the elements of the seal.
