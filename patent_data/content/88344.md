# Electrically powered device for opening and closing the visor of a crash helmet.

## Abstract
The device comprises a small electric motor 6 driving, through reduction gearing 7 , a gear segment 8 asso ciated with the visor pivot point 3 . The gear segment 8 has a configuration which includes a quarter of a circle 10 and a straight protion 9 which imparts the visor 4 with an initial forward movement followed by a pivotal movement during the opening phase, and viceversa during the closing phase.
