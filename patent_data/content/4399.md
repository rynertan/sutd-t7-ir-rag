# Photographic material suited for use in diffusion transfer photography and method of diffusion transfer photography using such material.

## Abstract
Said material contains a quinone type compound which corresponds to one of the formulae A or B and which is capable in reduced state and under alkaline conditions of releasing a photographically useful group e.g. a dye At least one of the substituents R¹, R², R³, R⁴, or R⁵ is a ballasting group X.
