# A closure device.

## Abstract
A closure device for fluid tight closure of an opening of a fluid container. The device comprises a casing part 1 and a cap part 2 . These parts are injection molded from different plastic materials, preferably from PVC and polypropylene, respectively, one partially against the other. A tubular por tion 12 of the cap part 2 intrudes sealingly into a substan tially cylindrical hollow space between an internal and an external sleeve portion 3, 4 of the casing part 1 .
