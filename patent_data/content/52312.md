# Low fire hazard rigid urethane insulation foam, polyol mixtures used in the manufacture thereof and method for manufacture thereof.

## Abstract
A low fire hazard rigid urethane insulation foam having high compressive strength and low friability is disclosed. Rigid foam products of this invention have a Class I flame hazard rating according to the ASTM E 84 Steiner tunnel test. The urethane foam contains a halogenated base polyol and a modifying amount of a 2,5 bis hydroxymethyl furan com ponent.
