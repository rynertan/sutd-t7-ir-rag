# Partially pressure sensitive recording paper.

## Abstract
A recording paper such as a business form which is partially pressure sensitive is prepared by applying a not yet hard binder to the or each area which it is desired to make pressure sensitive of a surface of a sheet material 1 and causing microcapsules 2 containing a colour former in solution 5 to adhere to the said not yet hard binder so that, when the binder 3 has hardened, for the majority of the microcapsules 2 at least a part of each microcapsule is exposed through the binder 3 .
