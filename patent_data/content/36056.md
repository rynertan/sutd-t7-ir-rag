# Transfer apparatus for natural tobacco leaves.

## Abstract
An apparatus for transferring a natural tobacco leaf from a continuous, flexible conveyor belt C to a generally flat leaf receiving member D at a preselected position in the normal path of travel of the belt. The apparatus includes a plenum box 200 having a pressure opening defined by a peripheral edge 202 of the box, an arrangement 230, 232, 250 for forcing the belt and box from the normal path position toward the receiv ing member D at the preselected position and a mechanism for creating a positive pressure in the box to force a leaf from the belt onto the receiving member.
