# Surgical instruments.

## Abstract
A set of surgical instruments for use in the implantation of a knee prosthesis include alignment rods 6, 35 inserted into the femur and tibia, two femoral saw guides 28 and 21 for resecting the femur, and a tibial saw guide 36 for resecting the tibia, a broach 45 and a reamer 42 for cutting recesses in the tibia, a femoral pusher for locating a femoral component of a knee prosthesis over the resected end of the femur, and a tibial impactor for tapping a tibial component home within the tibia.
