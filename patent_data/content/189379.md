# Kneader for paper products and or similar waste products.

## Abstract
A kneader for paper products and or similar waste products, comprising a tank 2 provided with an impeller 3 on the bottom, and also provided with a helix or propeller element 6 which is not coupled with said impeller and is apt to feed the worked product to the same impeller, said helix or propeller element 6 being substantially disposed in an axial relation to said impeller 3 .
