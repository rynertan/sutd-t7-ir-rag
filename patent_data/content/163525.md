# Catheter introducing device.

## Abstract
Catheter introducing device 11 for inserting a dilatation catheter into a guiding catheter having a flexible tubular member 12 formed of a flexible material. The tubular member has a spiral cut 13 extending longitudinally throughout the entire length of the tubular member 12 . A pull member 14 is secured to the tubular member 12 to facilitate removal of the device 11 after it has been utilized for introducing the dilatation catheter into the guiding catheter.
