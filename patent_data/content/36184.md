# PIPE BUILDING METHOD AND APPARATUS

## Abstract
Method and apparatus for producing a tube containing sand particles bonded by a thermosetting resin. Examples of such resin include unsaturated polyesters and epoxy resins. Further, the sand core can be bonded by reinforcing material on the inner and outer surfaces of the sand core. The products are useful in situations where liquid or gaseous corrosive or abrasive materials are conveyed. Additional utility will be found in the sewer and water distribution markets.
