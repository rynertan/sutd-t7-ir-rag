# Magnetic reproducing apparatus capable of receiving radio broadcast programmes.

## Abstract
Magnetic reproducing apparatus is disclosed which is capable of receiving radio broadcast programmes when a cassette type tuner unit 33 is inserted in a cassette holding portion of the apparatus. The apparatus includes a protection mechanism 88 which prevents the pinch roller 51 of the apparatus from contacting the capstan 50 when the appar atus is operating in the radio broadcast receiving mode.
