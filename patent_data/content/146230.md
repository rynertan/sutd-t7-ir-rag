# Method and apparatus for recording medical information on a strip chart.

## Abstract
For recording medical information on a strip chart 10 by a strip chart recorder 20 associated with a foetal mon itoring device 21 , a communication is initially entered into a data entry system 30 which includes a switching mem brane 32 having a plurality of switches 36 forming a switching network underlying the membrane. A micropro cessor scans the network to identify the communication and thereafter supplies the same to a liquid crystal display 34 for view by a user of the system prior to transmission of the communication to the recorder 20 for recording.
