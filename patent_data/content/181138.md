# Combinations of materials and method for forming a splint.

## Abstract
A combination of materials that can be used to form a splint or protective covering 29 including a support mat 12 including a water hardenable resin impregnated fabric 14 with water restricting film 16 along at least one of its major surfaces, and a pressure sensitive adhesive coated padding 20 that can be adhered to the support mat 12 after the fabric 14 is exposed to water so that the resultant laminate can be applied to a person 30 with a dry surface of the padding against his skin.
