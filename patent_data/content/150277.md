# An attachment for an irrigation sleeve.

## Abstract
An attachment 20 for an irrigation sleeve 18 used by colostomy patients 14 , comprising attaching means 68, 70, 72 formed integrally with said attachment 20 for attaching said attachment 20 to the sleeve 16 in a snap on fashion, thereby forming a fluid tight seal between said attachment 20 and the sleeve 18 , said attaching means 68, 70, 72 including an annular lip 68 having an annular groove 70 formed therein, said groove 70 being sized and shaped so as to releasably and frictionally receive a mating element 50 formed on the sleeve 18 .
