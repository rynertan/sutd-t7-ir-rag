# Electrolytic cell of the filter press type.

## Abstract
An electrode structure comprising an electrically con ductive sheet 2 material, a plurality of projections 4, 5 on at least one surface of the sheet material and preferably on both surfaces, which are spaced apart from each other in a first direction and in a direction transverse thereto, and a flexible electrically conductive foraminous sheet or sheets 8, 9 electrically conductively bonded to the projections 4, 5 .
