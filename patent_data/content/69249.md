# Tube package.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a tube package of plastic material which consists of a tubular shell 1 part together with end closures front and back 3, 6, 7 . The shell part is constituted of a cylinder of molecular oriented plastic mate rial, and its front end closure of a curved shrunk portion of the extension of the shell part. The rear closure of the shell part is constituted of a transversely sealed portion of the shell part.
