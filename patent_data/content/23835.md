# Container system for dangerous material and method of opening a container.

## Abstract
A container system for dangerous or harmful substances which comprises a container having a base 1 and lid 2 , and a tray 6 for carrying the container. The tray is equipped with two upstand portions 5 and 12 reference 5 is hidden in Fi gure 4 which respectively engage the bottom of the base 1 and the threaded bottom portion of the lid 2 when the latter is removed. The lid may additionally comprise means 14, 15 for engaging the screw top 8 of a vial 7 housed within the con tainer.
