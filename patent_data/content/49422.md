# Stable nonaqueous solution of tetracycline salt.

## Abstract
Nonaqueous solution of a tetracycline antibiotic salt which is stable upon extended storage comprises a mixture of a tetracycline antibiotic salt, nonaqueous diluent, non aqueous solvent, and nonaqueous nonionic solubilizer. It preferably also contains an anti oxidant and a nonaqueous anionic solubilizer.
