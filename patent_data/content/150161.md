# SLIDE MOUNTING WITH PROVISION FOR A SOUND TRACK, AND PROJECTION, RECORDING AND SOUND REPRODUCTION APPARATUS FOR SLIDES USING SUCH A MOUNTING

## Abstract
A slide mounting with provision for a sound track has a quadrangular frame 10 which is intended to hold a strip of photographic film and is provided with a sound track carrier consisting of a track 16 inserted within the frame and movable therein for the recording and reproduction of a sound signal through recording and reproduction access apertures 22, 24 formed in the periphery of the frame 10 . A projector incorporating a stationary recording and playback head 40 and friction drive means 76, 48 for the sound track 16 is used for the projection of slides utilising such a mounting.
