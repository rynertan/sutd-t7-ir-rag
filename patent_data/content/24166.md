# Operating lever assembly for an electrical switch.

## Abstract
An operating lever assembly for a multifunction switch wherein the lever 13 carries a rotary switch 14 along its length and a push action switch 26, 27, 28, 33 at its end. The operating member 28 of the push action switch is movable longitudi nally relative to the lever 13 to operate the push action switch and rotatable about the axis of the lever 13 to operate the rotary switch 14.
