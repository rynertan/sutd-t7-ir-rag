# Automobile antenna system.

## Abstract
The present invention provides an automobile antenna system including a small sized loop antenna disposed in close proximity to the vehicle body and adapted to detect surface currents induced on the vehicle body by radio or other waves, the loop antenna being in the form of a single winding coil 42 which is housed within a casing 32, 34 rigidly mounted on the vehicle body near the marginal edge thereof, the casing being provided with an opening 36 for receiving the marginal edge of the vehicle body such that the loop antenna can properly be positioned relative to the marginal edge of the vehicle body.
