# Disperse monoazo dyestuffs.

## Abstract
Disperse monoazo dyestuffs from 2,3 , 3,4 or 2,5 dicyanoanilines as diazo components and N,N dialk en yl anilines having an acylamino or alkoxycarbonyl amino group in the 3 position as coupling components. The dyestuffs give red shades on synthetic textile materials.
