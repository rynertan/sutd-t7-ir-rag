# ISOPROPYLMYRISTATE 4 ALKYL 2,6 BIS TERT BUTYL PHENOL MEDICAMENT

## Abstract
Herpes infections are treated with a mixture comprising a major amount of isopropylmyristate and a minor amount of a 4 lower alkyl 2,6 bis tert butyl phenol, with 2,6 bis tert butyl 4 hydroxy toluene being a preferred embodiment of the 4 lower alkyl 2,6 bis tert butyl phenol.
