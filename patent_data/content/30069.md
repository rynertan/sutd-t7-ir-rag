# Sheet inverter and method of reversing the direction of sheets.

## Abstract
A sheet inverter 10 which accommodates the reversal of motion of sheets of different sizes within a curved fixed length inversion chute 14 having a variable buckle control provided by highly flexible and low force spring members 40 chordally intersecting the inverter chute which provide assistance in positively feeding the sheet back out of the chute, after it has been positively buckled therein against the chute end 22 , but allows the undistributed formation of buckles of various dimensions within the chute depending on the size of the sheet.
