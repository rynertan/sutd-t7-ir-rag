# Device for conveying chickens to a slaughtering plant.

## Abstract
Device for conveying living chickens to a slaughtering plant, comprising means 6 for separating the chickens one by one, conveying them with the separated legs into leg clamps 39,40 on a turn tables 38 which cooperate with means 42 which synchronize all chickens into an identical position in which they hang with their heads downwardly and with the legs into a position which allows taking over of each chicken by clamping frames with neck clamp 55 and leg clamps 56,57 of an other conveyor 4 which feeds the chickens towards the slaughter hooks 59 of the slaughter house conveyor 5 .
