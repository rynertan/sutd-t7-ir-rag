# Float recovery system.

## Abstract
A method and apparatus are provided for recovering a towed body 11 to onboard a towing ship 10 while the towing ship is underway. A saddle 20 is employed in coop eration with a rudder 28 to engage one end of the towed body 11 and, subsequent to engagement, the saddle means 20 is slid forward to engage the opposite end of the towed body 11 .
