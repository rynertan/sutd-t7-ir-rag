# Method of and device for cutting off knotted ends of tube bags.

## Abstract
An annular stopper 12, 13 is placed against one end of the bag 10 to prevent it from advancing, the knotted portion 10b of the bag 10 protruding from the stopper 12, 13 is pinched by a plier 23 both or either of the stopper 12, 13 and the plier 23 is pulled to extend the portion 10c between the peripheral portion of the bag restricted by the plier, and then the extended portion is cut by the blade of a cutter 47 .
