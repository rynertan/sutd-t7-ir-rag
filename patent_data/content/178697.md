# Tape applicator with cutting device for cardboard box sealing machines.

## Abstract
A cutting blade 19 is fixed to a supporting frame 20 rotatable about an inlet applicator roller 1 . Locator projec tions 22, 23 work together to stop said frame 20 in a predetermined angular position, in which the blade 19 carries out the cutting of the adhesive tape 8 on the applicator roller 1 .
