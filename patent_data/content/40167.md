# Adjustable retention latch assembly.

## Abstract
The latch assembly for aligning the holes in a panel 12 and a frame 38 , such as those used in aircraft doors, includes a sliding hook mechanism 10 having a hook 23 for engagement with a striker 33 to retain the panel and a slide 21 operable by a camming means to bring the holes into alignment thereby freeing both hands ofa workman to secure or release fasteners 42 .
