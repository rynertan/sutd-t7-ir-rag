# Reinforcing member and its use for reinforcing a panel of an automobile.

## Abstract
A material for reinforcing a panel 20 includes a resin material 24 and a expandable material 26 . The expandable material 26 is centered onto the resin material 24 in such a manner that the edges 25a, 25b of the resin material are exposed and can adhere to the panel 20 . The expandable material 26 includes an easy bending portion 29 in a position corresponding to a bent portion 22 of the panel. The easy bending portion 29 is formed by a groove 30 or by perforations 41 in the expandable material 26 .
