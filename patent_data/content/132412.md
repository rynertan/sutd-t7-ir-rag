# MACHINE FOR PRODUCING AND DISPENSING ICE LOLLIES AND THE LIKE

## Abstract
An automatic coin fed dispensing machine comprises an ice lolly mould in two parts 9A, 9B kept at a freezing tempera ture by a refrigeration plant a controlled supply of pre cooled syrups from 17 and apparatus for separating the product from the mould by inducing one part 9A to move f9 relative to the other 9B . The product then either drops out of the tilted mould, or is lifted by its stick 15 .
