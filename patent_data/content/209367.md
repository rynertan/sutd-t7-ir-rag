# Ink composition for writing board.

## Abstract
A wipe erasable ink composition for a writing board has good erasability and long period stability. This ink composition comprises a coloured polymer formed by dyeing an organic solvent soluble resin having a sulphonic acid group or a salt thereof with a basic dye, a volatile organic solvent and, as ad ditives, A a nonionic surface active agent having an HLB value of 8 to 14 and or a cationic organic compound, and B a slightly volatile or nonvolatile organic compound.
