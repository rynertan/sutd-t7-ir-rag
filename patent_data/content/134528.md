# Dental prostheses alloy.

## Abstract
Disclosed is an alloy that is a combination by weight percent of 78 to 84 percent nickel, 11 to 15 percent chromium, 3 to 5 percent vanadium, and 1 to 2 percent beryllium. Any balance of other elements make up less than 7 percent with aluminum, manganese, silicon, tin, lanth anum, cerium, praseodymium, neodymium, samarium, gadolinium, molybdenum, niobium, tantalum, tungsten, titanium, iron, boron, and carbon specifically disclosed. The alloy is, in particular, a dental casting alloy or dental veneering alloy, and dental prostheses containing the alloy a feature of the disclosure,
