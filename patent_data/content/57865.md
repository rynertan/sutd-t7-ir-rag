# Platform structure of the pallet type.

## Abstract
A cardboard pallet 1 comprises a folded foot 3 defining cardboard sheet rigidly associated to a cardboard flat member 2 . In the outer hollows 9 defined by the cardboard sheet folds there being engaged reinforcement members 10 effective to enhance the strength and rigidity characteristics of the pallet 1 . Thus a pallet is provided which is formed from cardboard throughout, without resort ing to any reinforcement parts made of metal or the like materials.
