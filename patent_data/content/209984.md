# Screw pump.

## Abstract
A screw pump including a screw assembly in a sleeve, the assembly comprising a drive screw having protruding thread ed sides, and multiple driven screws having concave threaded side which are closely meshed with the drive screw, thus forming a series of sealed chambers. The screw pump further includes one or more channels which are formed in the cir cumferential direction around the periphery of the driven screws, so that the final sealed chamber adjacent a discharge port of the pump partially communicates with the port through the channels shortly before the chamber completely opens to the port.
