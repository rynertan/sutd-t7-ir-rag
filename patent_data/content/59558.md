# Improvements in or relating to relief valves for sanitation systems or the like.

## Abstract
A relief valve for sanitation systems, said relief valve incorporating a valve seat 11, 25, within a valve chamber 5, 22, and a freely flexible valve element 12, provided for engagement with the valve seat 11, 25 to prevent inflow of air to the valve chamber 5, 22 through an air inlet 10, 26. Guide means 16, 17 are provided for assisting the valve element 12 into sealing engagement with the valve seat 11.
