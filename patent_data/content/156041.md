# Roll baling machine.

## Abstract
A machine 10 for continuously forming roll bales of crop material has a lower apron 20 and forward and rearward upper aprons 26, 52 cooperating to define front and rear bale forming chambers 50, 64 . A rotatable arm assembly 90 supports the rearward upper apron 52 , and a control linkage 104 is provided for holding the arm assembly 90 , when the rear frame 22 is pivoted upwardly, in a position so that it does not interfere with the discharge of a bale from the rear chamber 64 .
