# APPORTIONING AND DISPENSING APPARATUS FOR DOUGH AND BATTER

## Abstract
An apparatus to apportion and dispense dough and batter, which comprises a hopper 20 for the material to be dispensed wherein the material is discharged through an outlet 24 of the hopper having a lower, circumferential cutting edge 70 and is cut off by a piston 180 having an upper, circumferential cutting edge 190 , the piston 180 has an upper, fixed, coaxial boss 200 , which has a convex, frusto conical surface 210 extending upwardly and being spaced from the cutting edge 190 of the piston 180 . Also, an annulus 50 providing the outlet 24 has a concave, frusto conical surface 220 , which flares upwardly.
