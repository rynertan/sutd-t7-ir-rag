# Triazol and imidazol derivatives processes for their preparation, pesticidal compositions containing them and their use preparation of intermediates.

## Abstract
Compounds of the formula CHEM and isomers thereof wherein R 1 and R 2 are unsubstituted or substituted alkyl, cycloalkyl or phenyl, Y is N or CH and esters, ethers, salts and metal complexes thereof, have fungicidal and plant growth regulating activity.
