# Compact plenum type engine air intake manifold.

## Abstract
A compact ram tube plenum type intake manifold 43 for a V type internal combustion engine comprises a generally barrel shaped housing 52, 54, 55 mountable between the engine cylinder banks and defining a compact space 62 in which ram intake passages 63 57 are interdigitally coiled around the periphery of the walls with remaining portions of the interior space being utilized as an intake plenum 74. Manufacture in separable sections 44, 46 and other features are also provided for.
