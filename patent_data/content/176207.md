# Electrostatographic copier.

## Abstract
A modular developer and cleaner housing 46 for use in an electrostatographic reproducing apparatus of the type wherein a recyclable imaging member completes two cycles for each copy produced, includes a development portion 47 and a cleaning portion 7 , with the development portion including means 54, 55 for continuous development en gagement with an imaging surface, and the cleaning portion including a cleaning blade 60 able to be positioned in and out of cleaning engagement with the imaging surface. The cleaning portion further includes a used toner collection enclosure 7 to collect surplus toner displaced from the imaging surface by the cleaning blade, which enclosure is separated from the development portion of the housing whereby toner cleaned from the imaging surface is collected separately from the developer.
