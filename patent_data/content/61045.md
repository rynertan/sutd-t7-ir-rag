# Method and apparatus for non destructive inspection of tyres.

## Abstract
Tire testing apparatus has an ultrasonic acoustic receiver 210 arranged to scan the moving outside wall of a tire 112 . The apparatus includes seals 108 and 110 which mount the tire in its inflated state and signal processing means 302, 402, 416, 414, 408, 412, 420 which is connected to the receiver for detecting ultrasonic signals generated when air escapes from a leak in the tire wall.
