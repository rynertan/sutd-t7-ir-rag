# Spoken instruction controlled system for an automotive vehicle.

## Abstract
A single actuator can be activated repeatedly or a plurality of actuators can be activated sequentially by repeatedly depressing a recognition switch 3 within a predetermined time period after a spoken instruction corres ponding to the single actuator or actuators has been recognized by a speech recognizen 100 . Therefore, it is possible to activate a actuator repeatedly or actuators sequentially without uttering the same spoken instruction repeatedly. The spoken instruction controlled system com prises a two circuit analog switch 16 , at least one analog switch 17 1 , at least one retriggerable timer unit 20 1 , an AND gate 24 , an OR gate 23 , an shift register 27 , in addition to a speech recognizer 100.
