# A COSMETIC BREATH FRESHENER AND PALATE COOLANT COMPOSITION AND METHOD OF USE

## Abstract
A cosmetic breath freshener composition having slow release comprising a multiplicity of microencapsulated liquid droplets of flavoring material contained in a carrier. The microencapsulated droplets are soluble in the saliva in the mouth to release the flavoring material. The composition can be used in conjunction with a dental adhesive particularly for use with dentures to release the flavoring material at a sustained rate for long periods of time in the mouth to mask bad breath.
