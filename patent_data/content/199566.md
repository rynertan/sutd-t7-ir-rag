# SHEET HEATER

## Abstract
A wall, floor of ceiling heater comprises a plurality of self regulating, preferably PTC conductive polymer, heater ele ments 50 each of which is connected to two or more connection elements 53, 54 . The elements are held by a support 55 . In a preferred embodiment the heater elements 50 are positioned parallel to each other, and perpendicular to the connection elements 53, 54 . The support 55 is preferably made from a rigid material which is hinged to allow the heater to be folded or rolled for easy transport.
