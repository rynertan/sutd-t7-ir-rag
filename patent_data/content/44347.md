# Toy pistol.

## Abstract
A toy pistol comprises a loading and releasing trigger 7 and a housing adapted for accommodating a small boomer ang 2 therein and carrying hinged thereto a lever 11 biased by a coil spring 16 and effective to grip the boomerang 2 under the control of the trigger 7 to perform an angular movement toward the housing to load the coil spring 16 and cause the lever 11 to launch the boomerang 2 .
