# Lawn mower.

## Abstract
This invention relates to lawn mowers. In particular, the invention relates to a hover type lawn mower including a cylindrical cutting unit 12 . In particular, a mower according to the invention comprises a cylindrical cutting unit 12 mounted for rotation about a substantially horizontal axis 14 and means for establishing a cushion of pressurised air for supporting the mower above a ground datum when the mower is operative, characterised in that the mower includes a first hood 15 which serves to separate the said cushion of air from the cylindrical cutting unit 12 and from means for collecting mown grass 16 .
