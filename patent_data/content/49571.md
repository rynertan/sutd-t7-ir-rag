# An overcentre tensioning device.

## Abstract
A load binder of the overcentre tensioning type having an operating lever, comprising a handle portion 11 acting through a crank mechanism 10 to which a tensile load is connected, the lever 11 being connected to the crank mechanism 10 by a pivotal connection 21,30 which provides a degree of lost motion, thereby enabling the crank mechanism 10 to overrun the operating lever 11 on passing the dead centre position.
