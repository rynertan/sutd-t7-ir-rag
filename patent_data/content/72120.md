# Applicator.

## Abstract
An applicator for spraying material such as fertiliser on a vegetative surface for use in domestic horticultural purposes. A pump 17 for supplying liquid to spray nozzles 26 is driven on rotation of the applicator wheels 15 . The applicator is nor mally pushed over the ground using a handle 16 . The pump drive axis and wheel axis are co axial and the wheel is support ed on a single one piece shaft 18 which also forms the drive shaft for the pump without an intermediary coupling. This pro vides a compact and efficient machine. The pump 17 is mounted on a bearing housing 30 for the shaft 18 into which housing and bearing housing 40 on the other side fit the ends of the handle 16 .
