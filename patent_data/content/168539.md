# Blends comprising copolymers of polyphenylene oxides and poly aryl ether s, styrene polymers and or copolymers, poly aryl ether s and or polyphenylene oxides.

## Abstract
Described are blends of block copolymers of poly phenylene oxides and poly aryl ether s, a styrene based polymer or copolymer, and a poly aryl ether and or a poly phenylene oxide.
