# BOTTOM SUPPORTED BASKET

## Abstract
A bottom supported plastic basket for use with a wheeled cart frame having an upwardly facing frame mounting portion. The two sides, the front and the bottom of the basket are integrally molded. The bottom portion of the basket includes a plurality of quick release locking features which permit the basket to be readily coupled and decoupled from a cart chassis having corresponding mounting features. The basket may also be coupled to a conventional cart chassis.
