# PHOTOSENSITIVE IMAGING MEMBER

## Abstract
A photosensitive imaging member comprising a con ductive substrate 11, 12 having thereon a photoresponsive layer 14, 15 where said photoresponsive layer can be a single or a composite photoresponsive layer, the improve ment consisting of positioning between said conductive substrate and the photoresponsive layer, a layer 13 of an acetal of poly vinyl alcohol .
