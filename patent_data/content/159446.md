# COSMETIC SCALP TREATMENT

## Abstract
A method of treating the scalp to promote hair growth which comprises bathing the scalp with the output from a cosmetic medical laser for periods in the range of ten mi nutes and one hour, and preferably between twenty minutes and forty minutes, the periods being spaced from a few days to several weeks and, preferably, also specifcally, applying the laser to acupuncture points which are directly related to hair growth.
