# An earth connector for a rubber cushioned rail vehicle wheel.

## Abstract
A device for providing electrical connection through a rubber cushioned rail vehicle wheel 1 5 having a central part 1, 2 , a flanged wheel tyre 3 and rubber elements 4 inbetween. A conductor 6 comprises a wire 7 having a central tag 8 attached to the central part 1, 2 and at its ends U shaped spring clips 9 astraddling a circumferential internal ridge 10 in the tyre 3 between the rubber elements 4 .
