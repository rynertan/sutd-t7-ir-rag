# Apparatus for pressing trousers.

## Abstract
In a trouser treating apparatus the legs and the upper portion are pressed in a single operation. The apparatus comprises a vertically oriented buck 14 with pants retaining means 32, 34 and pressing means 50, 52 movable into pressing relationship with the buck 14 to press the legs of the pants the upper portion of the pants being pressed by moving the buck relative to a pressing member 60 such that said pressing member 60 is inserted into the upper portion of the pants, ex panding the pressing member 60 inside the pants and moving a pair of pressing heads 72, 74 into pressing relationship with the upper portion of the pants. The pants can also be pressed by blowing a gas in cluding steam into the pants, thereby inflating the same, and moving pressing heads into pressing relationship with the inflated pants.
