# TREATMENT OF GAS MIXTURES TREATMENT OF GAS MIXTURES

## Abstract
CO₂ is separated from a gas mixture by passing the mixture through a first gas liquid contact zone co currently with a liquid absorbent for CO₂, passing the gas mixture after treatment in the first contact zone through a second gas liquid contact zone co currently with a liquid absorbent for CO₂, and passing the liquid absorbent from the second absorption zone to the first absorption zone.
