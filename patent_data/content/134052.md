# Apparatus for measuring and delivering predetermined quantities of liquid, for example quantities of water.

## Abstract
The invention relates to an apparatus for measuring and delivering predetermined quantities of liquid, for exam ple quantities of water, comprising a measuring vessel, to which a liquid supply pipe and a liquid discharge pipe are connected. According to the invention a lower and an upper stationary measuring means are mounted in the measuring vessel, which measuring means serve for measuring a fix ed quantity of liquid in the measuring vessel and for control ling the liquid supply to and the liquid discharge from the measuring vessel, while the measuring vessel further com prises a displaceable measuring means, which causes the liquid supply pipe to close and the liquid discharge pipe to open when a set residual quantity of liquid is supplied in the measuring vessel.
