# A device for manufacturing multicoloured marble soaps from a base paste and one or more liquid pigments.

## Abstract
Pressurized coloured liquids are inserted in the base mass, immediately upstream or in correspondance with the last homogenizing means 7 then a rotor 5 ar ranged between the last perforated refining means 7 and the extrusion hole 8 provides for a partial mixing of the paste. The speed of rotation of the rotor 5 is adjustable from outside of the extrusion head, and in dependent of the speed of the extrusion screw.
