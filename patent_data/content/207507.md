# LAMP HOLDER SUPPORTING STRUCTURE FOR FLOODLIGHTS

## Abstract
Lamp holder supporting structure for flood floodlights, in dust proof and humidity proof execution, comprising a sup porting element equipped with a sealing gasket, a support equipped with perimetrical teeth, which are suited to engage in some restraints shaped around the hole of the optic cham ber case, and some guides in which the lamp holder lamp group is positioned and fixed.
