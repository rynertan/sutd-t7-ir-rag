# Apparatus and method for forming a multi compartmented tray from sheet material.

## Abstract
An apparatus and method are disclosed for forming a multi compartmented tray 10 from a sheet material 118 without tearing or bowing. At least a portion of one compart ment 12 , and preferably the entire compartment, is formed in advance of other compartments 14, 16 . The apparatus for do ing so comprises a preformer 88 on the male die 76 for forming the first compartment before the rest of the male die engages the workpiece.
