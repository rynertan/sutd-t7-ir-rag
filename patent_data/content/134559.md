# CATHODIC SPUTTERING APPARATUS

## Abstract
A rotatable magnetron cathode comprising an elongated cylindrical tubular member 21 , magnetic means 22 dis posed in said tubular member, a plurality of individual target strips 31 provided with the same or different coating materials 32 to be sputtered removably secured to the tubular member, said target strips 31 extending the axial direction of the tubular member 21 and disposed in spaced parallel relation around the periphery thereof, and means 53 for rotating or indexing the tubular member 21 to bring selected target strips 31 in position for sputtering opposite magnetic means 22 .
