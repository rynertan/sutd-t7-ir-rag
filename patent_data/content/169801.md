# Turbine side plate assembly.

## Abstract
The axid restrain of the rear disk rim side plate 80 of the 1st turbine stage 10 is the seal 22 extending between the 1st and 2nd stage turbines 10,16 . The fir tree broach 82 on the side plate 80 fits into the broached rim of the turbine disk 12 which is extended axially to radially restrain each side plate 80 . This configuration eliminates the heretofore used bolts and nuts or rivets.
