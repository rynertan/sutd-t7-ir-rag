# INNER CUTTER FOR A FOIL TYPE DRY SHAVER

## Abstract
An inner cutter for a dry shaver is formed from a tube of hardenable steel which is slotted transversely 1 to from a series of bridge members 2 on which individual teeth 3 are formed by shallow annular grooves 4 which locally reduce the thickness of the bridge members. Such a cutter may be conveniently finish sized by centreless grinding.
