# Sign verification.

## Abstract
A self contained writing pad unit 1 comprises a pressure pad 7 for sensing movement of a writing instrument over a contact surface 2 of the unit when a user writes a sign, for example a signature, either directly on the contact surface or on a writing medium 4 supported by the contact surface. Electronics 8 including a RAM and timing systems deter mine data relating to the original signature. A power supply 9 is provided to enable operation of the electronics 8 . The unit 1 is portable and the data represents dynamic char acteristic features of the writing signature.
