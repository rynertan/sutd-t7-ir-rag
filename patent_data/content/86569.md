# Preparation of bis carboxy ethyl germanium sesquioxide and its propionic acid derivatives.

## Abstract
Reaction mixture of germanium dioxide and hypo phosphorous acid or its salts in hydrochloric acid is ex tracted with organic solvent. This resultant is then reacted with acrylic acid or its derivatives, and further hydrolyzed to provide bis carboxy ethyl germanium sesquioxide or its propionic acid derivatives.
