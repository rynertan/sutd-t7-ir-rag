# SLEEP INDUCING AGENTS

## Abstract
A compound capable of inducing sleep in mammals comprising a peptidoglycan monomer obtained by lysozyme digestion of linear non crosslinked peptidoglycan polymer chains present in culture fluids of penicillin treated Brevibac terium divaricatum mutant NRRL 2311 together with hy drolysis of at least one amide group of said monomer.
