# Dual magnification rear projection lectern.

## Abstract
A dual magnification rear screen projector 10 having a fixed projection lens 22 and two auxiliary lenses 3, 7 which may be positioned before and after the projection lens 22 in the light path 32 to change the magnification of the system. The system is parfocal in that none of the lenses 3, 7,22 need be repositioned axially along the light path 32 to focus the system at either magnification.
