# Rotor blade pitch control for helicopter with coaxial rotors.

## Abstract
A directional and yaw control mechanism for coaxial helicopters, comprising a three coaxial ring system 109,30,72 interconnected through a cam and link assembly for transmitting control signals from a steering station 94 to change the rotor blade pitch either collectively to provide lift or independently in opposing directions to control yaw. Cyclic pitch control is also similarly provided.
