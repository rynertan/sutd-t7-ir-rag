# Process for depressurizing fly ash.

## Abstract
Process for depressurizing fly ash being contained in a first vessel 5 comprising a pressurized gas by expanding the gas through at least one long and narrow pipe 8, 9 . The inlet 7 of the pipe s dips under the upper level of the fly ash in the first vessel 5 . The outlet of the pipe s is situ ated in a second vessel 11 being kept at a relatively low pressure. The gas flow through the pipe s entrains the fly ash.
