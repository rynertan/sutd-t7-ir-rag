# Charging device.

## Abstract
A charging device comprises electric components ar ranged as a charging circuit for charging a rechargeable electric power source. The charging device comprises at least two sections which can be assembled in two different positions relative to each other, namely a rest position and a charging position, a first section being adapted to receive at least one rechargeable electric power source and a second section being provided with connector pins for co operation with an electric mains socket. The connector pins are connected to the components of the charging circuit in the second section. In the rest position the contact pins are covered by the first section and in the charging position the connector pins are clear of the first section and the re chargeable power source in the first section is electrically connected to the charging circuit in the second section.
