# AUTOMATIC ELECTRONIC PART INSERTING APPARATUS.

## Abstract
An automatic electronic part inserting apparatus for mounting an electronic part on a printed wiring board, capable of mounting an electronic part of any shape, such as an axial taped electronic part, a radial taped electronic part and a stick loaded electronic part on a printed wiring board by a single apparatus and more particularly an automatic electronic part inserting apparatus having a turret like insert spindle and adapted to carry out a part inserting operation in a single predetermined position.
