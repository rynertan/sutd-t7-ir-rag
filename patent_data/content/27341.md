# Bottom sheet separator feeder.

## Abstract
A bottom sheet separator feeder for separating and for warding sheets seriatim, particularly in an automatic docu ment handler, comprises a vacuum sheet feeder 37,41 in conjunction with an air knife 12 , a single blower 55 with a cyclically operated air valve 57 being utilised to provide sub atmospheric air for the separator 37,41 and pressurised air for the air knife 12 to provide enhanced feeder perfor mance and automatically compensate for variable paper weights.
