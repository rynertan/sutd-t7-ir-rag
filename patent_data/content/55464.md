# Injection molding machine for the manufacture of information carrier discs.

## Abstract
An upright injection compression pressing machine for the manufacture of information carrier discs, such as photo graph records and video discs, comprises a mold clamper assembly including a pair of stamper bearing molds mov able relative to each other in a vertical direction close towards and away from each other, and an injector assembly mounted on the clamper assembly in alignment with the direction of the relative movement of the molds.
