# Cutting guides and a method of cutting coving.

## Abstract
A guide is disclosed for guiding a saw for a mitre cut, particularly at 45 , through a piece of coving. The guide provides guide faces 13,14 for sliding engagement by a saw 26 or other cutter. The coving 4 is typically placed on a bench with the intermediate rear surface 3 of the coving flat on the bench. The guide is placed on top of the coving with notches 19,20,22,23 or other engaging means locating the guide on the coving and a cut is made with the saw in contact with either face.
