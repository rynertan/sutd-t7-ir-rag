# Camera.

## Abstract
A camera has a movable lens unit 2 having a taking lens 5 , a driving means 9 for moving said movable lens unit in the direction of the optical axis thereof, straight guiding shafts 3 and rollers 15 for guiding said movable lens unit 2 so as to move straight in the direction of the optical axis, and a driving force transmitting member 6 for transmitting the driving force of said driving means 9 to a couple of positions facing each other on the circumference of said movable lens unit 2 with the optical axis between said positions.
