# Flow through chamber forming part of a blood duct.

## Abstract
A flow through chamber, e.g. a drip chamber or an expansion chamber 1 , intended to form part of a duct for blood or similarly sensitive fluids and comprising at least one inlet 2 and at least one outlet 4 and provided with special means 8 for the deflection of the fluid flow after the inlet 2 . The said means is formed by the actual wall of the chamber 1 in that a portion 8 of the same is intented. The chamber 1 may be manufactured by blow mould ing with simultaneous formation of the said indented part 8 .
