# Electrical element unit for water heaters.

## Abstract
For use in domestic electric water heaters, a replacement or originally installed inlet outlet assembly comprising a flanged sleeve adapted to be mounted at the bottom of the tank. The flange portion is provided with a threadably received plug carrying an electric element within the sleeve. Inlet and outlet tubes extend along outer generatixes of the sleeve. A second, closed top cylindrical unit is received on top of the sleeve, extending the sleeve and the outlet tube. A series of double tube sections, fitting one on top of the other, form extention of the outlet tube and a riser tube for water heated within the sleeve. A valved opening is provided for introducing and selectively shutting off the water flow into the sleeve, for replacing the electric element without need to empty the whole tank. The units of the assembly are integrally formed by plastic injection molding.
