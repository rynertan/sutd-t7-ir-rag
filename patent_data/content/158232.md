# Process and apparatus for direct heat transfer temperature swing regeneration.

## Abstract
Process and apparatus for cleaning an impurity containing adsorbent by pressure swing regeneration, com prising a warming the impurity containing adsorbent by direct heat transfer with warm purge gas 2 , b heating the cooled down purge gas to produce hot purge gas 11, 13 , c cleaning the warm inpurity containing adsorbent by contact with hot purge gas 4 , d cooling the clean adsorbent by direct heat transfer with clear purge gas to produce warm purge gas of steps a , b .
