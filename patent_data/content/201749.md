# CONTACT WITH EXCHANGEABLE OPTO ELECTRONIC ELEMENT

## Abstract
Active special contact 20 , adapted for insertion into a hybrid contact insert 1 of a connector, the active contact including a housing 25 , first contact elements 41, 42, 27 disposed in the housing, a supporting element 32 for receiving an electro optic transducer 34 , second contact elements 38 disposed in the supporting element, means 43, 44 for the detachable connection of the housing and the supporting element, and means for releasably connecting the first and second contact elements.
