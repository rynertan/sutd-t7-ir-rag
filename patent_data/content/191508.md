# Syringe.

## Abstract
The invention relates to an injection syringe having an ampoule 11 in which a plunger 12 and a sealing stopper 17 are provided, and a needle holder 18 comprising a collar 21 to be provided on the ampoule in a sealing manner and a sleeve 20 closed at its front end by means of a wall. Said end wall comprises on its outside a neck 19 for connecting an injection needle, an aperture 28 being recessed in the said front end of the sleeve. The side wall of the sleeve comprises at least one longitudinal duct 22 which, over a small part of its length, is in open communication with the room bounded by the inner walls of the sleeve.
