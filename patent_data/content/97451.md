# 2 Alkyl 5 pyrimidines.

## Abstract
Novel 2 alkyl 5 halopyrimidines, e.g., 5 chloro 2 1,1 dimethylethyl pyrimidine, which are useful as intermediates in the preparation of, e.g., 0,0 diethyl 0 2 1,1 dimethylethyl 5 pyrimidinyl phosphorothioate which is useful as an insecticide against corn rootworm and cutworm.
