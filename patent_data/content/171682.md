# Self tensioning hair waving rod.

## Abstract
A self tensioning hair waving rod is disclosed compris ing an elongated generally cylindrical water absorbing sponge body 10 for receiving a strand of hair wrapped therearound, support means 11 extending axially through said sponge body 10 , and retainer means 12 associated with the outer end portions 13 of said support means 11 for holding the wrapped hair strand around the sponge body 10 . Said sponge body 10 is capable of expanding from a substantially dry condition upon application of water and increasing its volume by from 50 to 250 , whereby the wrapped strand is tightened and drawn into the close proximity of the outer surface of said sponge body 10 .
