# Ball bearing for linear motion.

## Abstract
The bearing comprises a housing 1 consisting of two detachable parts. an outerrace part 4 made of steel plate, a cage 7 of plastic or the like material and a hollow shaft 10 provided with race tracks. The housing parts 1 , 1 are con nected to each other by bolts 3 . Due to these specific shaped bearing parts composing of the bearing is simplified and particularly the plate like outerrace part 4 with the cage 7 will garantee smooth running of the housing 1 over a fixed shaft 10,15 or visa versa.
