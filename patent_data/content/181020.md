# Apparatus for reproducing information from a record carrier.

## Abstract
An apparatus for reproducing information in the form of one or more information blocks in the track of a record carrier, for example a compact disc digital audio player, is equipped with a memory 6 having n storage locations 6.1, 6.2, . . . 6.n for storing in each of these storage locations one sequence number i corresponding to an information block N By means of such an apparatus searching for a following track number than has been selected can be effected more rapidly.
