# Turbine blade repair.

## Abstract
A method of repairing a turbine blade 10 of the type having one or more lacing wire holes 20, 21 comprising the steps of welding additional metal 17 to the blade 10 , subjecting the blade 10 to a stress relieving heat treatment process before which the or each lacing wire hole 20, 21 is effectively eliminated by filling the hole or holes 20, 21 with material the same as or at least compatible with the material from which the blade is made. The turbine blade 10 may be subjected to the application of mechanical forces to restore its form and machining to restore its shape can be gauged to ensure both its form and shape is correct.
