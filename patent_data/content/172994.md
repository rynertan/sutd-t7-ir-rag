# Heating apparatus.

## Abstract
A fireplace or stove apparatus for efficiently providing heat to a structure without loss of interior air to the flue draft. A blower 20 forces outside air through a conduit 22 located in the exhaust flue 21 into a grate 24 located in a fireplace or stove. The fuel loading opening 26 of the fireplace or stove is made substantially air tightly sealed. An air opening in the grate provides air to be consumed by the fire. A second air opening in the grate is fluidly connect ed by means of a conduit 46 to the structure to be heated.
