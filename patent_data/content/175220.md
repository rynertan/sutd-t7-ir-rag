# THERMOPLASTIC RESINOUS BLEND AND A METHOD FOR ITS PREPARATION

## Abstract
A thermoplastic blend comprising a C₁ C₄ alkylene ter ephthalate , a polycarbonate resin and a butadiene or an acry late based graft copoiymer was found to be amenable without pre extrusion drying, to thermoplastic processing upon the admixing therewith an additive amount of a polymeric modifier.
