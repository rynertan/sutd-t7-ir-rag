# Floppy disc envelope container.

## Abstract
The invention provides a fabric suitable for use as a liner for a floppy disc envelope container, said fabric comprising a thermal bonded and emboss bonded nonwoven fabric composed of textile grade polyester staple fibers and polyester staple fibers having a melting point below about 150 C. The invention further provides a floppy disc envelope container comprising sheet material, e.g. pvc, fabricated into an envelope, said sheet material having an inner surface on which is laminated the aforementioned fabric.
