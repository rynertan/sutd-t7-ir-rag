# Stabilised granular nematocidal and insecticidal composition having O ethyl S,S dipropyl dithiophosphate as an active substance, and method of preparing it.

## Abstract
Stabilized granular nematicidal and insecticidal composition having O ethyl S,S dipropyl dithiophosphate as an active substance. The invention relates to a stabilized granular nematicidal and insecticidal composition having O ethyl S,S dipropyl dithiophosphate as an active substance which contains pumice granules as a stabilizing carrier.
