# Improvements relating to a method of forming beaded edges on plastics material articles and articles formed by such a method.

## Abstract
There is disclosed a method of forming plastic sheet material into articles. The sheet 28 is heated and is formed into the article in a mould 10,12 . The mould parts 34,44 severe the portion of the sheet which is to be formed into the article and at the peripheral edge of the said portion the mould parts 32,40 squeeze the plastics material to cause it to flow outwardly into a bead cavity 30 42 in which the material forms into a thickened bead.
