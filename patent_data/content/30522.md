# A SELF SUPPORTING PANEL OF POLYURETHANE RESINS OR THE LIKE, A MANUFACTURING METHOD THEREOF AND AN APPARATUS USED THEREWITH

## Abstract
A self supporting panel particularly suitable for use in the building industry, consists of an enbloc structure having a central core and a peripheral frame and is fabricated from polyurethane resins or the like with dif ferent densities in the core and frame, the panel struc ture perferably comprises protective and or decorative coating sheets covering both the core and frame, said frame being manufactured in such a manner that it completely and permanently adheres to the core and to the coating sheets, due to an intramolecular fusion which takes place amongst the panel constituents contacting surfaces.
