# Composite dual tubing.

## Abstract
A metallic dual tubing comprises an inner tube 10 and an outer tube 20 and is provided with an inter mediate layer 18 comprising combustible material filled between the inner tube 10 and the outer tube 20 . This layer may be a single layer consisting of the combustible material or of the mixture of such material with a heat resistant material. The intermediate layer may be also a dual layer of the combustible material and the heat resistant material.
