# Pharmaceutical antifungal composition.

## Abstract
A valuable alternative antifungal antibiotic, for treatment especially of candidiasis, particularly vaginal candidiasis, is proposed The antibiotic is a bile acid or simple derivative salt or conjugate thereof. Cholic, deoxycholic, chenodeox ycholic and lithocholic acid are preferred bile acids. The antibiotic is formulated for topical application. It can be used in association with an anti inflammatory steroid for the treatment of fungal infections of the skin. It is also proposed to inhibit fungal growth in a variety of pharmaceutical compositions containing some other active ingredient by including in the composition, or coating a tablet, pill or capsule with, the bile acid component.
