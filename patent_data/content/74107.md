# SOFA BED

## Abstract
An armless sofa or arm chair bed of the kind compris ing a three fold foldable upholstered padded structure 2 which, standing folded, provides an armless long seat A or an arm chair and flattened out provides a couch, characte rized by a space 11 saving pedestal 1 to receive a drawer 3 for bedding d . The drawer bottom 31 is provided with swingable wheels 33 to run it on the floor in and out of pedestal 13 . The bottom wall of mattress like padding 2 is centrally fastened with ropes 5 or the like to the structure 1 so that its margins 20 may be raised for dressing with bed clothing 4 .
