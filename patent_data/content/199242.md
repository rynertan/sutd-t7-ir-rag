# Device for controlling doors, drawers, and the like movable members.

## Abstract
The device comprises a rotor 18 having a central pin 183 , a plurality of concentrically arranged cylinders 180 182 and a sprocket wheel 14 , adapted for mesh engage ment with a rack fixed to the sidewall of a drawer compart ment. The device also comprises a stator 17 adapted for being attached to a drawer and rotatably mounted on the pin 183 , and having a plurality of concentrically arranged cylinders 170 172 interposed between those of the rotor 18 . Relative movement of the rotor 18 and the stator 17 is controlled by an arrangement of co operating grooves, a cam, and a ball, and a high density fluid LS interposed between the cylinders 170 172, 180 182 .
