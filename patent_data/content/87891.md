# Overhead electric transmission systems.

## Abstract
A simple and inexpensive dead end fitting for securing an overhead conductor loosely housing an optical guide to a tower comprises a sleeve 1 of malleable metal or metal alloy, open at each of its ends, for compression jointing to the conductor a steel termination 2 in screw threaded engage ment with the sleeve and having a clevis 4 for securing the fitting to a tower and a lug 7 welded to the sleeve for effecting electrical connection to a jumper cable. The lug 7 engages in a notch 8 in the adjacent end 3 of the termination 2 to lock positively the screw threaded engagement between the sleeve 1 and termination.
