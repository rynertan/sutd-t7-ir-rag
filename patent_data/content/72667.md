# Plastic vial rupturing apparatus.

## Abstract
Vial rupturing apparatus is disclosed for destroying vials 14,52 , typically small medicinal vials. Each vial is split longitudinally by a thin vertical blade 13,50 while the vial is maintained horizontal. This is done either by moving the blade 13 while holding the vial stationary 14 or by urging the vial 52 onto a stationary blade.
