# FISH BREEDING PLANT COMPRISING AT LEAST ONE TANK SUBMERGIBLE TO THE DESIRED DEPTH AND HAVING AN AIR CHAMBER

## Abstract
A plant for breeding salmon or salmon like fish in open sea environment comprises at least one closed tank 1 sub mergible to the desired depth and provided with feeding and submersion control equipment 2 . Mounted within the tank 1 is an air chamber 5 together with means 2, 6 for supply ing air thereto in order to form an artificial water surface for the fish enclosed within the tank. A lighting means 7 is mounted above the air chamber 5 to illuminate said artifi cial water surface.
