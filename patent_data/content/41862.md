# IMPROVEMENTS RELATING TO AIR HEATERS

## Abstract
An air heater comprising an outer shell 10 , an air heating space 11 provided within said shell 10 , a furnace 12 within said shell 10 , an exhaust flue 22 , means 18, 14, 20, 21 placing said furnace 12 in communication with said exhaust flue 22 , means 14a to fire said furnace 12 and means 25 to feed air to be heated through said air heating space 11 .
