# Double isolated jounce bumper system.

## Abstract
A double isolated jounce bumper system comprises first and second shaped members 30, 32 mounted upon the chas sis 34 and a control arm 14, 16 of a motor vehicle, respec tively. Each shaped member 30, 32 comprises a rigid arma ture 46 and an elastomeric covering 42 and bears a shape which is complementary to the shape of the other.
