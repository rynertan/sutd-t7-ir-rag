# Ultrasonic scanning apparatus and positioning system.

## Abstract
Positioning apparatus for vertically moving a scan ning assembly includes a vertical support 34 , a first arm pivotally attached to the support aid to the scanning assembly, and a second arm pivotally attached to the support and to the scanning assembly with the first and second arms being parallel. A counter balance is provided and a counterweight 46 is attached to the supporty by third and fourth arms 48, 50 which are pivotally at tached to the support 34 and to the conunterweight 46 .
