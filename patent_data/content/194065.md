# Optical accessory for use with eye glass frame.

## Abstract
There is disclosed an improved optical accessory 10 of the sun shade type for use with a pair of prescription spectacles 12 . The accessory 10 includes a bridging assembly 38 having a resiliently flexible wire like member 46 which cooperates releasably with a brow bar 30 on the spectacles, and allows a finger 42 to releasably cooperate with the bot tom of a nose bridge 32 of the spectacles.
