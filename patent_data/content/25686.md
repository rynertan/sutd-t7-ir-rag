# Pharmaceutical composition containing paracetamol, treatment pack comprising said composition and method of reducing liver toxicity effect of paracetamol compositions.

## Abstract
The acute liver toxicity effects of paracetamol are re duced by co formulating with sustained release ascorbic acid which produces high liver concentrations of ascorbate anion after oral administration of the co formulation. A suitable form of sustained release ascorbic acid is prepared by microencap sulation of particles of ascorbic acid in membranes which act as microdialysis cells.
