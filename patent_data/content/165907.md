# Machine for making espresso coffee, particularly for domestic use.

## Abstract
An espresso coffee making machine 10 , particularly for domestic use, comprises a boiler 18 with electrical heating resistors 28 and a filter cup 64 releasably attached beneath the boiler and connected thereto. The boiler 18 is connected to the filter cup 64 by an upwardly extending pipe 48 which communicates at its lower end with a lower zone of the boiler 18 for discharging water therefrom, and at its upper end with an upper zone of the boiler 18 located above the maximum level L reachable by the water in the boiler, for the subsequent discharge of steam.
