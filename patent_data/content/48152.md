# A PCM signal processor.

## Abstract
A decoder for PCM signals modulated in a TV format signal with a control data word for use in the control of a decoding operation. The processor is provided with a PCM signal decoder 12, 13, 14 , means for extracting the control data word from the TV format signal, means 24, 25 for latching the extracted control data word, means 29 for checking agreement between a newly extracted control data word and the latched control data word, and means for applying the agreement checked control data word to the PCM signal decoder.
