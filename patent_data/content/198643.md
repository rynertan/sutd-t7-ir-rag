# MINERAL CUTTING PICKS

## Abstract
The invention provides a method of, apparatus for, flush ing with water a mineral cutter pick of the kind having a head 5 terminated with a hard cutting tip 6 , in which one or more jets of water 13 are directed from nozzles 12 in the pick holder 8 at the pick at an acute angle to the head of the pick so that the water strikes 14 the pick head and is carried for ward over the surface of the pick head by its own momentum into the vicinity of the cutting tip.
