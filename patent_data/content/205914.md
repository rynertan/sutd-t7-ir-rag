# Sealing compound.

## Abstract
A PVC free sealing compound, particularly a sealing compound for use on containers, comprising a mixture of low density polyethylene or linear low density polyethylene a styrene isoprene styrene block copolymer or a thermo plastic elastomeric blend of an ethylene propylene copoly mer such as EPM or EPDM, EVA and process oil, one or more fillers such as talc, and optionally cork powder.
