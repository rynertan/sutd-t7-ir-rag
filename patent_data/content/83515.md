# Improved control of fuel injection apparatus for internal combustion engines.

## Abstract
A fuel injection apparatus 5 for an intemal combustion engine comprising a metering device to adjust the quantity of fuel delivered each cycle of the device in response to conditions in the engine induction passage, a control 104 activating said device to effect a base of the engine, and a sensor 106 responsive to transient engine conditions to increase the number of cycles of the metering device above the base number per revolution.
