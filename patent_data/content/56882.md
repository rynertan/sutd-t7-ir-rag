# Device for transferring eggs.

## Abstract
An egg handling machine having one and preferably two or more egg feeding chains first retaining members 4 to which eggs are transferred from the chains and which are each provided with a gripper 5, 6 to positively hold an egg and rotate it to orient its axis vertical and second retaining members 10 moving in a line or preferably two or more lines vertically aligned with the first ones for each receiving an egg from one first retaining member.
