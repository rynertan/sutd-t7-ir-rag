# Screw compressor.

## Abstract
A screw compressor comprises a casing 1 defining there in a pair of cylindricyl chambers 14, 15 intersecting with one another parallel to their axes and a pair of male 4 and female 5 screws in an intermeshing relationship. The casing 1 in cludes an inlet port 20 composed of an open area fully open to the chambers 14, 15 except for seals 22 around the shafts 40, 50 of the screws 4, 5 , the profile of the inlet port 20 being equal to the profile of the chambers 14, 15 , such that the fluid can flow freely into said chambers 14, 15 without turbulent fluid flow.
