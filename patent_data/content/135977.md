# ARTICLE DISTRIBUTING MECHANISM

## Abstract
Apparatus is provided by which articles being conveyed in a first direction can be transferred to a selected one of a number of conveyors travelling in other directions. A trans fer conveyor receives the articles from a conveyor travel ling in the first direction, and carries the articles past the other conveyors. A control is provided to activate the inter ception device associated with the selected output con veyor, and this device removes the article from the transfer conveyor so that it can move on to the selected output conveyor.
