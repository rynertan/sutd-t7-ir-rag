# Process for producing natural fruit candy.

## Abstract
A process for producing a natural fruit candy is de scribed herein and comprises the steps of mixing enzyme deactivated dehydrated fruit and enzyme deactivated fruit juice concentrate to provide a mixture in the form of a plastic mass having a moisture content in the range of 8 to 50 by weight the relative proportion of dehydrated fruit and fruit juice concentrate being limited only by the need to achieve a moisture content in the plastic mass which falls within the required range. The plastic mass is then formed to the required product configuration.
