# Process for moulding reinforced tyres.

## Abstract
The method of molding a pneumatic tire 10 having a radially outer tread portion, a tire casing 20 underlying the tread portion 22 and a reinforcing cord belt 24 interposed between the tread portion 22 and casing 20 in which the tread portion 22 is spin cast and then partially cured in the mold 30 , the reinforcing cord belt 24 is then fastened to the tread portion 22 , a core 32 inserted in the mold and the tire casing spin cast and cured. The reinforcing cord belt may be adhered to the tread portion by staples 60 or a skim coat adhesive 62 to hold the belt in place during the spin casting of the tire casing.
