# Adjusting device, particularly for ski boots.

## Abstract
The device comprises a bar 1 carried rotatably on the ski boot and provided with first and second oppositely threaded portions 4,5 rotatively engaged by first and second blocks 6,7 effective to translate along the bar 1 . To the blocks 6,7 there are articulated respective ends of first 10 and second 11 connecting rods, both thereof are articulated with their other ends to an adjuster 15 .
