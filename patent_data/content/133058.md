# Alignment of tubular piles.

## Abstract
To enable alignment of two tubular piles 10, 12 for join der, an aligner 11 extends from one end 26 of one pile 10 for insertion in the other pile 12 . One or more shim members 17, 18, 19 are disposed between and in engagement with the outer suface 25 of the extending portion 20 of the aligner 11 and the inner surface 28 of the other pile 12 when the extending portion 20 is inserted therein for maintaining the two piles 10, 12 in alignment for joinder.
