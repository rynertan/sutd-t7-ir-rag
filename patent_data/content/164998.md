# COMPOSITIONS AND METHODS OF ENHANCING TRANSDERMAL AND TRANSMEMBRANE PENETRATION OF TOPICAL AND SYSTEMIC AGENTS

## Abstract
Composition and methods useful for enhancing the transdermal and transmembrane drug delivery of topical and systemic agents. The compositions and methods comprise the active agent together with an effective amount of certain amides of heterocyclic amines as defined herein as penetra tion enhancers and topically administering a human or animal skin or other membranes the resulting compositions.
