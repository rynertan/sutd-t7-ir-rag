# PARALLEL WHEELED DEVICE.

## Abstract
A parallel wheeled device consisting of a pair of parallel wheels 1 , 1 , a crankshaft 3 to the opposite ends of which the wheels are attached, upwardly extending steering rods 5 fastened to an axle 4 so that the steering rods can be turned therearound, and lower lever portions 7 each of which contacts with the point on the ground surface other than the intersection point of the vertical line extending from the axle and the ground surface. In this parallel wheeled device, the wheels float above the ground surface when the steering rods are pulled, so that the direction of movement the device can be changed simply.
