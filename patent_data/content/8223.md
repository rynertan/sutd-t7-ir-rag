# Dispenser support.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a support for holding dis pensers such as rolls of metal foil, cellulose sheet mate rial used in the kitchen. Previously, no convenient manner of supporting such articles has been proposed. The sup port includes a frame 10 have two side edges 22 which are provided with a series of horizontally aligned slots 24 . The slots 24 receive a bracket 16 in such a manner that the bracket 16 is held by the frame 10 so that dispenser 26 such as cuboidal boxes of the abovementioned rolls may be conveniently held by the support.
