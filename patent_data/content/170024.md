# USE OF 2 ACYLAMINOMETHYL 1,4 BENZODIAZEPINE DERIVATIVES FOR THE PRODUCTION OF PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS AND PROCESS FOR THE PREPARATION FOR PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITIONS

## Abstract
Certain 2 acylaminomethyl 1,4 benzodiazepine deriva tives, optical isomer thereof, pharmaceutically acceptable salts and quaternary ammonium salts thereof are used for the production of pharmaceutical compositions which anta gonize the function of cholecystokinins in humans.
