# Belt filter device.

## Abstract
Belt filter device comprising a frame 7 , an endless conveyor belt 1 guided along drums 2, 3 and at least one casing 4 arranged below the upper run of the belt, in which subatmospheric pressure can be created for sucking fluid from material lying on the conveyor belt during operation through holes provided for this purpose in the conveyor belt 1 , said casing 4 being pivotable with respect to the frame between a first position suitable for operation in which a cover of the casing having at least one passage for the fluid is in contact with the bottom side of the upper run of the conveyor belt and a cleaning position in which the casing is turned downwards in a direction away from the upper run of the conveyor belt, said casing being pivotable with respect to the cover.
