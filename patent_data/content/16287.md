# A wire drawing arrangement formed by a plurality of roller die assemblies.

## Abstract
A wire drawing arrangement formed by a plurality of roller die assemblies and method of setting up same in which each roller die assembly includes two grooved rollers 3 mounted in a pair of beartng frames 5 which are themselves mounted in a pair of housing frames 9 , said plurality of roller die assemblies being detachably interconnected with the axes of the grooved rollers 3 of two adjacent roller die assemblies arranged perpendicular to each other with a distance between the centres of two adjacent roller die assemblies being smal ler than the diameter of the grooved rollers 3 .
