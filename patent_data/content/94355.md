# PET FEEDING STICK, RIGIDLY FIXABLE TO PET CAGES, PARTICULARLY FOR BIRDS AND OTHER PET ANIMALS

## Abstract
The pet feeding stick comprises a molded plastic joining head A , connected with a stick support B which supports glued feed C around it, wherein said joining head A has a plurality of grooves for rigidly inserting the feeding stick between two wires of a cage, fixedly oriented to the respective cage wall, as for example in horizontal position, therefore making feeding easier for the pet animal.
