# Sheet forming apparatus.

## Abstract
An apparatus for forming a double side polished sheet of thermoplastic synthetic resin is improved in that a second cooling roll having a smaller diameter than a first cooling roll is disposed so as to be movable along the outer circum ference of the first cooling roll, there is provided a guide roller for guiding a sheet having left the second cooling roll directly into a water tank, and the water level in the water tank is made variable.
