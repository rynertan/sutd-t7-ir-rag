# Device for mounting and removing a cutter at the end of the cutter shaft from a cutter dredge.

## Abstract
The cutter boss 5 is provided with an inner screw thread and the cutter shaft 4 with an outer screw thread. The device is comprised of two concentric rings 6,7 those surfaces of which facing one another are provided with teeth 8,9 fitting into one another, whereby the slanting of said teeth is oppo site to the angle of said screw threads, and means 16 are provided to lock both rings during the screwing of the cutter on the shaft thereof, in such a position relative to one another where said rings have so rotated relative to one another that the joint thickness thereof is increased as compared with the joint thickness of the toothed rings when the teeth have com pletely rotated into one another.
