# ELECTRICAL CONNECTORS

## Abstract
An electrical socket connector in which individual contact members are located and retained on pegs projecting from a body member of electrically insulating material, the contact members being arranged for insulation displacement electrical connection at one end to insulated wires of two different thick nesses, and having means to set the gap between contact making cantilever springs for co operation with an associated plug connector. Outer covers may be latched to the body mem ber, and the body member or one of said covers may allow ac cess to said contact members for test probes.
