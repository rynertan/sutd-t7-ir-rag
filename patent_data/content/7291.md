# An apparatus for sealing a connection between two mutually shiftable components.

## Abstract
Two components 11,28 , for example, conveyors, a interconnected by the intermediary of a connecting portion. The component 28 connected to the connecting portion 11 is shiftable along an elongate opening in the other com ponent. The elongate opening is closable by means of a flex ible web 37 length which extends in the longitudinal direc tion of the elongate opening and in a loop 38 about the connecting portion of the movable component. The connect ing portion has guide means 42 for successive lifting out and laying of the web length respectively away from and towards the elongate opening on shifting of the connecting portion therealong.
