# Keel tipped stylus, and method and device for making keel tipped stylus.

## Abstract
A keel tipped stylus 30 has a constricted terminal portion 48 which is mounted on narrow shoulders 44, 46 which is in turn mounted on wide shoulders 40, 42 . The keel tipped stylus 30 is formed from a V shaped stylus which engages into a spiral groove formed in an abrasive lapping disc 60 . As the lapping disc rotates, the V shaped stylus runs along the groove to lap the constricted terminal portion together with the narrow and wide shoul ders.
