# WITHDRAWING MECHANISM FOR THE CASTING IN A HORIZONTAL CONTINUOUS CASTING MACHINE

## Abstract
A withdrawal mechanism for a horizontal continuous casting machine has a disc 6 on a rotatable shaft 3 , a pivoted lever 11 displaceable with respect to the disc, means for 12,13 displacing the lever, a clutch for releasably 14,15 coupling the lever to the disc, a brake mechanism 7 for preventing rotation of the shaft, and a pair of pinch rolls, 1,2 with one of the rolls being in driving relation with the rotatable shaft.
