# Hydraulic shock absorber.

## Abstract
Disclosed herein is a hydraulic shock absorber exhibiting a constant vibration damping force. The shock absorber is provided with a deep groove 14 formed on an upper surface 5a of a piston 5 in a circle where piston ports formed through the piston lie and communicated with the piston ports 12 . The groove has an outer peripheral wall 15 in such a manner that an upper opening is upwardly enlarged.
