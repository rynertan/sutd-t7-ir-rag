# Device for quickly locking the paint containing vessels in apparatus for mixing paint and the like.

## Abstract
There is disclosed a device for locking the paint contain ing vessels in apparatus for mixing or blending paints and the like, comprising two movable plates 1, 1 firmly coupled to corresponding parallely extending supporting rods 3, 3 each whereof supports a fixed gear wheel 5 , at the central portion of which there is provided a lever 6 forming one of the two ends of an open frame structure and thereon there are pivoted two arms 9, 10 in turn pivoted to the movable plates 1, 1 , thereto there are coupled the vessel clamping rotatable disks 2, 2 .
