# Preparation and use of new solvates of beclomethasone 17,21 diproprionate.

## Abstract
Novel di isopropyl ether solvates of beclomethasone 17,21 dipropionate are prepared by dissolving beclometha sone 17,21 dipropionate in an organic solvent and precipitat ing the solvate by addition of di isopropyl ether. The solvates are substantially bulk stable and find utilisation, inter alia, in aerosol formulations.
