# Co refining of aramid fibrids and floc.

## Abstract
An aqueous slurry of 0.2 to 2.0 weight percent aramid fibrids and aramid floc in a weight ratio of 0.25 to 20, respec tively, is refined in a disc refiner having grooved facing plates with surface dams, the distance between the facing plates being 0.20 to 1.0 mm.
