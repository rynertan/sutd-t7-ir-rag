# Metal component for a colour cathode ray tube.

## Abstract
A component suitable for use in a colour cathode ray tube is formed from a metal including iron Fe as its chief element and has an oxidised layer on its surface. The metal further includes silicon Si , aluminium Al and chromium Cr in weight amounts satisfying the following equation Cr 1 3 Al Si . The component may be a mask frame 14 , a shadow mask 16 or an inner shield attachment 24 to the mask frame.
