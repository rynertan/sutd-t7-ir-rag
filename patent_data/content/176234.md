# Conveyor having curved track section.

## Abstract
A conveyor system for a driverless vehicle includes a channel shaped track 12,14,16 adapted to be mounted in a floor 18 . The track has at least one curved section 14,16 . Along the inner leg of the curved section there is provided a polymeric plastics liner 32 which is characterised by a low friction and high abrasion resistance. The liner 32 is adapted to contact a side face of a chain 42 disposed within the track.
