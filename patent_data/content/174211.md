# LOCKING SYSTEM ESPECIALLY FOR PALLETS

## Abstract
A locking system for holding a bank of pallets to a track T comprises an abutment A to be urged against the outermost pallet P and having a cam surface C1 , and an element E engagable with the track T and having a cam surface C2 to be moved by a handle H at the end of a disc 9 to urge the cam surfaces C1,C2 together, and locking means 12,13 to hold the disc 9 in the locking position.
