# Inorganic binder composition and use of said composition for preparing refractory binder coated fabrics.

## Abstract
The invention relates to inorganic binder compositions comprising a colloidal silica, b monoaluminum phosphate c aluminum chlorohydrate and d an amount of an alkyl tin halide catalyst effective to increase the bonding of said composition when applied to a substrate and the use of said composition for preparing refractory binder coated fabrics in the preparation of fabrics with high temperature resistance and flame resistance.
