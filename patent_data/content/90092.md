# Exercise device for boardsailing.

## Abstract
This exercise device simulates the conditions of board sailing and develops the particular body muscles used in boardsailing. The device 10 comprises a curvilinear boom 12 , a first tension line 16 secured at each end thereof to the boom adjacent the extended ends of the boom, a tension transmission member 20 adjustably secured to the first tension line intermediary of the ends thereof, a second line 22 , one end of the second line being secured to the tension transmission member and the other end being adapted to be secured to an anchor member 24 , and an adjustment plate carried by the second line for varying the distance between the tension transmission member and the anchor member.
