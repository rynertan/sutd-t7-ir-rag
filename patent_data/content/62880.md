# Axial flow valve.

## Abstract
A coupling member 10 adapted for joining in tandem a pair of axial flow valves 12, 14 includes a central hub region having back to back bosses 60, 62 extending longitudinally therefrom and a plurality of rib members 76a, 76b extend ing radially therefrom. The spaces between the rib members provide a fluid flow path between the valve bodies. The interior of each of the bosses is hollow to provide means for communicating with the interior of the valve bodies.
