# Improvements in or relating to automatic clothes washing machines.

## Abstract
A clothes washing machine has a cabinet with a back panel 1 or front panel integral with two side panels 2 and a removable front back panel 4 . A platform 26 integral with or mounted on stiffening members 10 is fixed to the cabinet intermediate of the height thereof by shear resistant fastenings 12 and a lightweight outer container 25 containing an inner perforated bowl 24 is mounted on the platform 26 . The washing and spinning mechanism 20,21,22, and 23, is also supported by the platform.
