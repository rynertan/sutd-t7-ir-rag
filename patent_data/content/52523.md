# Gate valve.

## Abstract
A gate valve 11 has within a valve housing 12 a ceramic valve gate 44 , which is surrounded by a peripheral ly disposed compression band 47 for providing strength thereto, and which is engaged by a pair of ceramic valve seats 45, 46 , which are in turn urged inwardly for such engagement by ceramic springs 30, 32 . The ceramic springs comprise a plurality of plates of ceramic material separated by spacers which are disposed in a staggered array to form the ceramic spring.
