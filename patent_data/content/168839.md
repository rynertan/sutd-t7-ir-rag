# Modeling material composition.

## Abstract
A composition for modeling material comprising a syn thetic resin, a wooden powder and an oil formulated and kneaded. The synthetic resin is a rubber reinforced styrene resin. The oil is a hydrocarbon oil having a specified viscos ity, and restriction of the content of an aromatic ring forming carbon. The composition is formed into flat board, round rod, etc. and used as modeling material for shaping pat terns or models.
