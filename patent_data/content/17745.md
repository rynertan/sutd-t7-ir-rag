# Pinball game machine and drop target assembly for it.

## Abstract
Pinball game machine having a playfield 11 for supporting a rolling ball and comprising the combination of a drop target assembly supported by the playfield 11 and having first means for advancing a target 33 of the assembly from a play position above the playfield 11 to a drop position below the playfield 11 when a ball engages such a target 33 , and advancing means, including a ball actuable switch assembly 70a disposed on the playfield 11 , for advancing such a target 33 to the dropped po sition independently of a ball engaging the target 33 . A corresponding drop target assembly for such a pinball game machine is also disclosed.
