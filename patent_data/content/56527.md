# Multi directionally oriented films.

## Abstract
Imparting of a random multi directional orientation to a malleable film by pulling the film 12 through at least one pair of multi directional orientation rollers 20 which are angularly threaded and grooved along the entire length, the grooves 24 of each roller 20 being positioned to receive the threads 22 of the oppositely disposed roller 20 . The oriented film may be laminated to another malleable film which has also been multi directionally oriented.
