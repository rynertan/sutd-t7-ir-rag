# Electrical switch.

## Abstract
A switch for mounting on a substrate comprises a housing 1 for substantially U shaped cross section the side walls 2 being formed with cantilever arms 4 which extend obliquely inwardly of the housing 1 . A slider 9 mounted in the housing 1 and carrying contact members 13 , is formed with shoulders 11 which engage over the arms 4 to retain the slider 9 in the housing 1 . The free ends 6 of the arms 4 engage scalloped surfaces 12 on the slider 9 to provide a detent action.
