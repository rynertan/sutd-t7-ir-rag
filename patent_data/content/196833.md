# VEHICLE BODY

## Abstract
A road goods vehicle body 10 is divided by a horizontal partition 22 into upper and lower compartments. Either or both compartments may be sub divided into sub compartments by part or full height vertical dividers 28, 30, 32, 34. Preferably at least some of the dividers are thermally insulating and at least some of the compartments or sub compartments can be environmentally controlled inde pendently others. The rigifying effect of the dividers on the vehicle body can increase its strength. Preferably the compartments have guide means and drive means for movable load carrying units used to carry load items within the vehicle body.
