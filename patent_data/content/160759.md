# Racquets.

## Abstract
A racquet comprises a frame made from a suitable bent length of extruded aluminium tubing 14 . The frame has a groove 32 around the greater part of its internal periphery, the side walls of this groove converging towards its mouth. The groove 32 serves to hold a one piece crenellated wire string securing member 38 , consisting of loops 40 con nected by connecting portions 42 . The connecting portions 42 are trapped in the groove beneath respective wedge shaped nylon securing members 40 , such that the loops 40 project radially into the space within the frame.
