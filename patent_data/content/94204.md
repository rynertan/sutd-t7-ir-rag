# Sunbeam concentrating and collecting apparatus.

## Abstract
A sunbeam concentrating and collecting apparatus has a lens support frame comprising a honeycomb or regular n sided polygonal frame 1 the frame 1 being made up of at least one regular n sided polygonal holding frame 1A the or each holding frame 1A being formed by coupling together a plurality of frame pieces 2, 3, 4 each of which is provided with at least one angled portion 2A, 3A, 4A , the lens 7 or lenses 6 being disposed along one end plane of said holding frame 1A .
