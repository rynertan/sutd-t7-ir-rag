# A video tape recorder and a method of assembling a video tape recorder.

## Abstract
An assembly of components of a video tape recorder includes for simple assembling, setting and design a sub mounting 18 having mounted thereon a rotary head 1 located in a cylindrical part 19, 19a of the sub mounting 18 . Electrical control elements such as a full width erase head 4 , audio erase head 9 , audio control head 10 together with tape drive and guide elements such as pinch roller 3 and guide pins 5 to 8 are also mounted on the sub mounting 18 . The whole forms an assembly which is adapted to be mounted on a chassis 21 of the video tape recorder.
