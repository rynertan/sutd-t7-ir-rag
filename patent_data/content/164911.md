# Improvements in or relating to beer production.

## Abstract
The invention relates to beer production and intermedi ate products of use therein. In particular it relates to a process for production of a beer wherein prior to fermenta tion the wort is subjected to a separating technique to produce one product lower in fermentable low molecular weight components of the wort in substantially the same concentration as the feedstock and then fermenting that product to produce a beer. The invention also relates to the wort products obtained by the separation technique.
