# Packaging apparatus.

## Abstract
A sealing and stripping assembly 10 for a packaging apparatus, the assembly includes two shafts 16 which are held in a spaced generally parallel co extensive relationship with an arm 15 extending from each shaft, and co operating pairs of closing and stripping bars 21, 22 mounted on the radial extremity of each arm 15 so as to engage a bag material passing through the assembly so as to seal and strip same during a packaging operation, with said arms 15 being driven in synchronism and in opposite rotational directions.
