# Method and apparatus for striping inside seams of cans.

## Abstract
A method and apparatus for coating the inside seam of a can according to the invention, comprises airlessly spraying a thick coat of protective coating material onto a seam 18 of the can body 14 . In order to spray a thick coating onto the seam 18 of the can body 14 without significant rebound of the coating material, the coating material is ejected from a spray nozzle 46 close to the portion of the same 18 being coated, such that the coating material impacts the seam 18 prior to atomisation 69 .
