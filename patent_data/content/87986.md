# Multiprogrammable pinch valve module.

## Abstract
The valve module 60 including a housing, 62 a valve body and means for a plurality of individual flexible conduits 120, 122, 124, 126 threaded through the valve body, the flexible conduits being acted upon in a programmed manner to change the condition thereof between permitting and preventing passage of fluids therethrough cam 112 and removable floating followers 118 individual to each flexible conduit being prepositioned in a variable program of operation by simple manipulations, placement and number of the followers. Operable by a simple stepping motor plural valves are capable of being ganged in arrays for operation by a common drive. Additionally, the valve is capable of being enabled to control the sequential operations of the testing apparatus associated therewith.
