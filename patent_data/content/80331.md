# WINDING MACHINE

## Abstract
A machine 10 for winding a metal band helically on to a cylinder includes a winding machine 12 and a corrugating ma chine 14 . The winding machine 12 has spindles 16, 18 and a cylinder 22 . Guide prongs 36, 38 guide a metal band on a tube 22 for forming a helix. A welding gun 39 welds the sides of the helix as it is formed.
