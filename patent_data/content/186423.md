# LIFT TRUCK

## Abstract
A lift truck is provided with an electromagnetic retarder 1 coupled in its drive train so that an electromagnetic braking effect may be applied thereto. The retarder may be mounted on the gearbox 2 , in the propellor shaft 4 , or on an axle 3 of the vehicle. The retarder may be operated by a hand lever 8 or may be connected to the brake pedal or accelerator pedal of the lift truck.
