# CELL STRUCTURE SUN VISOR AUTOMOBILE VEHICLES INCLUDING RETENTION AND SNAP ACTION POSITIONING MEANS

## Abstract
The sun visor comprises a load bearing structure 11 including a shaped element die cut from a sheet of a cell polymeric material having straight parallel channel like cells a , and a suspension and securing rod 20 engaged in one of the cells a , the cell a engaging with the rod 20 , or alternatively an insert 30 intervening between the rod 20 and cell a , having at least one raised rib 34 formed thereon which is adapted to engage with the rod surface for frictional retention, and at least one longitudinal groove 24 in the rod 20 adapted for snap action locking the sun visor.
