# Tetrazolothienopyridines.

## Abstract
1H Tetrazol 5 yl tetrazolo 1,5 a thienopyridines and related compounds which are useful as antiallergic agents are described herein. The compounds are prepared by the reaction of an appropriate halocyano quinoline with ammo nium chloride and an azide such as sodium azide in an inert solvent such as dimethylformamide.
