# DISPOSABLE INCONTINENCE PRODUCT

## Abstract
A disposable diaper 10 110 having improved liquid receiving and retaining capabilities, includes a waterproof barrier formed as a flattened bag 12 112 and having a single opening 14 114 located to be adjacent the perineal area of the body of the wearer. Two layers of filler material are positioned within the barrier bag. The upper layer adjacent the opening is a wicking material 16 116 , while the lower layer is a superabsorbent material 18 118 . In a male version of the diaper, a pouch 136 is formed over the opening into which the wearer s penis is inserted to direct voided urine through the bag opening.
