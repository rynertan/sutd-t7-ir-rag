# Fuel cut off system for a motor vehicle.

## Abstract
A system which has an accelerator pedal switch 1 for sensing the deceleration of a motor vehicle, and an electro nic circuit operative to cut off fuel during deceleration of the motor vehicle in order to reduce the fuel consumption. If the switch 1 fails and remains closed during start up of the vehicle, the electronic circuit operates to maintain the supply of fuel in order to prevent stalling of the engine.
