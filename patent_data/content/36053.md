# Method and device for backtracking thread chains with a two needle overlock sewing machine.

## Abstract
Thread chain sewing method and device for use in the two needle overlock sewing machine wherein an inner chaining off finger 12 is arranged retractable from its advanced position in which it is parallel to an outer chaining off finger 13 to a direction opposite to the fabric material feeding direction. Î± The inner chaining off finger 12 is held in its advanced position during a sewing process and a thread chain forming period right after the finish of sewing process in a sewing machine operation. Thread chains A, A formed before the start of sewing process are entangled only around the outer chaining off finger, 12 while thread chains formed right after the finish of sewing process are bridged inner and outer chaining off fingers 12 13 .
