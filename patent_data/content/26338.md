# Antibiotic C 15003 PHM, production thereof and microorganisms for its production.

## Abstract
Novel antibiotic C 15003 PHM, which is produced by cul tivating a microorganism belonging to the genus Nocardia and being capable of producing antibiotic C 15003 PHM, and novel C 15003 PHM acylate, which is produced by subjecting C 15003 PHM thus obtained to acylation reaction with an acylating agent, have antiprotozoan and antitumor activities.
