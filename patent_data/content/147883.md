# AQUEOUS NON AGGRESSIVE ANTHELMINTIC POUR ON FORMULATIONS

## Abstract
A spot on formulation for combatting helmintic infesta tions in non human animals containing per 100 ml from 0.04 to 0.15 moles of a pharmaceutically acceptable acid addition salt of tetramisole or levamisole and a vehicle consisting of from 1 to 60 v v of a penetration enhancing and or moistening agent in water.
