# Engine starter and accessory drive apparatus.

## Abstract
An engine starter and accessory drive apparatus of the kind comprising a starting means air turbine 28 , first 2 and second 3 gear systems, and an overrunning clutch 16 permitting an associated engine 35 to be driven from said starting means as long as it does not reach its operating speed and then disconnecting it from said starting means. According to the invention, the overrunning clutch 16 is interposed between the two gear systems 2, 3 and one member 20 of said clutch and the input member 9 of the second gear system 3 are coupled to an accessory 33 for driving same from either the starting means or the engine once this latter is started. For use in aircraft engine starting systems.
