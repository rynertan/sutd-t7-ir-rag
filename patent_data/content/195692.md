# DISPENSING OF BEVERAGES

## Abstract
The invention proposes, in the dispensing of carbonated beverage from closed container 12 through a tap 16 , the progressive introduction of CO₂ into the free space above the surface of the beverage from a capsule 14 thereof, as dispensing proceeds, the added CO₂ maintaining carbona tion of the beverage at a requisite level and facilitating dispensing of the beverage.
