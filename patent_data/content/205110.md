# Footwear structure incorporating a heating device particularly for ski boots.

## Abstract
The footwear structure comprises a shell 2 , including a sole portion 2b and having a quarter 3 connected thereto. The sole portion 2b incorporates an electrical resistance heating circuit 51 , electrically connected to terminals 52 and at least one rechargeable storage battery unit 40 . The structure further comprises a housing 60 , positioned at a rear portion of the quarter 3 , protruding rearwardly, and being adapted for removably receiving the rechargeable stor age battery unit 40 , and a circuitry unit 70 for selectively electrically connecting the battery unit 40 to the electrical resistance heating circuit 51 .
