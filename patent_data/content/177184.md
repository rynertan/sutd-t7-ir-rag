# Universal joint dismantler.

## Abstract
A device for dismantling a universal joint comprises a bridge member 2 having a central hole 10 formed in it and parallel spaced arms 8 extended at one side. There is a U shaped grasping member 15 having a threaded hole 24 formed in its top and opposing fingers 18 formed in its lower edge. A tightening bolt 12 is slidably received in the bridge member and threaded in the grasping member. The grasping member is received between the arms of the bridge member and tightening of the bolt draws the grasping member up with respect to the bridge member.
