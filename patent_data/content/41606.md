# Composite label arranging device.

## Abstract
A composite label arranging device is disclosed wherein a composite label L having a label material superposed on a backing material 71 with feed flaps formed in both is engaged with feed pins 22 of a feed roller 21 , by which the feed flaps are formed upright. The feed roller 21 intermit tently relates in synchronism with the operation of a hand lever 3 for advancement of the label on a platen 30 . A label arranging members 54 is disposed in opposition to the platen 30 so as to descend during the squeezing operation of the hand lever 3 , thereby flattening the upright flaps 74a, 75a .
