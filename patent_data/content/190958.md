# METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR EDGE PREPARATION OF SPINNING BLANKS

## Abstract
A work roller 44 , having an external circumferential groove 46 , edge conditions the peripheral portion of a blank 18 held in a rotatable chuck 10 . Thereafter the peripheral blank portion is draw spun with a spinning roller 47 . Both rollers 44, 47 are fixedly mounted on a common tool slide 40 .
