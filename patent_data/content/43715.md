# Lubrication system.

## Abstract
The system comprises a viscosity pump 7 mounted on the shaft within the thrust bearing housing 33 , a main lubri cant reservoir 34 and a closed auxiliary reservoir 37 . The pump inlet 40 or 40a is connected to the auxiliary reservoir 37 below the level at which the main reservoir 34 is in com munication with the auxiliary reservoir 37 , the latter level be ing the desired level 36 of lubricant in the housing 33 . The pump outlet 41 is arranged to supply lubricant to the bear ings.
