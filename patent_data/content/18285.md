# Fixture for holding a dual diaphragm pression capsule.

## Abstract
Pressure capsule having a first and a second quartz plate 102, 104 and a fixture comprising a first holding member having first means 184 for receiving a surface of said first quartz plate 102 and for aligning said first quartz plate relative to a reference feature on said first holding member, a second holding member spaced by said pressure capsule from said first holding member and having second means 186 for receiving a surface of said second plate 104 and for aligning said second plate relative to a reference feature on said second holding member, means 190, 192 for aligning said first holding member to said second holding member and for registering said first plate in contacting engagement with said second plate, and means for uniformly radiating, during heating, said first and second plates.
