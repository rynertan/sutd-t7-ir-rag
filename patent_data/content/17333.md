# Packaging machine.

## Abstract
A packaging machine for loading a group of primary packages such as bottles through the open ends of an open ended sleeve container C includes a fixed horizontally disposed base plate 50 over which a plurality of transverse parallel spaced apart metering bars 51 are movable in spaced relation to the base plate so that an open ended sleeve carton disposed in the spaces between the metering bars is slidable over the base plate together with fixed guide means 86 89 93 96 disposed alongside the base plate for receiving primary packages from an infeed conveyor, the fixed guide means being disposed at an acute angle to the path of movement of the metering bars so that primary packages are supplied by the fixed guide means into the spaces between the metering bars and thence into the open ends of the sleeve type containers disposed therebetween.
