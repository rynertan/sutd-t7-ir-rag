# Process for the preparation of alkanoylaminophenyl alkyl ketones.

## Abstract
A chemical process for preparing compounds of structure 1 CHEM in which R is C1 4alkyl and R 1 is C1 4alkyl or C1 4haloalkyl which comprises reacting a compound of structure II with a compound of structure III CHEM in the presence of aluminium trichloride, characterised in that the solvent comprises trichlorobenzene.
