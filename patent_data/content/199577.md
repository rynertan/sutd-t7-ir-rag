# An applicator tool.

## Abstract
An applicator tool comprising a body 10 with a lever 12 pivotally mounted with the body. An applicator needle 26 is coupled via a coupling arrangement 15, 20, 22 to the lever 12 . The applicator needle 26 is adapted at the free end 27 thereof to engage with the leading portion 50 of a tag T . Overall the applicator needle 26 is of curved shape and it is located by guide means 31 such that as the lever 12 is pivotally moved this movement is reflected in movement of the applicator needle 26 on an arcuate path.
