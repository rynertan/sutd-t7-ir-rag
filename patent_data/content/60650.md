# Credit control systems.

## Abstract
This invention relates to portable devices enabling a cus tomer to acquire or to gain rapid access to goods and ser vices, more particularly it relates to such devices in which in formation may be stored and in which the said information may be amended or updated. In more detail a portable credit device according to the in vention having formed thereon a pattern comprising individual segments each of which can be activated by an externally ap plied signal to give a visible and persistent display of informa tion which remains visible in the absence of the said signal and each of which can be deactivated to erase the said visible dis play also by means of an externally applied signal.
