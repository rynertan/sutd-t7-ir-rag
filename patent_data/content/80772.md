# Jack and plug electrical assembly.

## Abstract
A multiconnection electrical assembly employing a jack 10 and complementary plug 112 . The jack has cavities 34, 36, 38 , for receiving two inserts 14, 16 containing multiple spring wire conductors 90 and a plug containing multiple insulated wires 86 , pierced by insulation displacement contacts 84, 88 . The assembly provides for electrically connecting the wires of the plug with the spring wires of the two inserts.
