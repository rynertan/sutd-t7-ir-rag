# A rack and pinion type steering device.

## Abstract
A rack and pinion type steering device 44, 48, 54 is ar ranged so that one of axially opposed ends of the device is located in the vicinity of one of the steered road wheels. A rack cover 46, 50, 56 mounted on the end of the device is a bel lows boot and is constructed to axially expand and contract in response to the axial movement of the rack shaft of the device.
