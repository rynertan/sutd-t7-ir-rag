# Automatic roulette apparatus.

## Abstract
Automatic roulette apparatus having a wheel 3 with ball receiving cups at its periphery and means for identifying the cup into which a ball 22 projected on to the wheel falls. The idenitfying means includes a counter 30 for counting the passage of cups past a reference position a detector 29 for the passage of a reference cup, and a sensor 23 for sensing the passage of the ball in a cup as the wheel spins.
