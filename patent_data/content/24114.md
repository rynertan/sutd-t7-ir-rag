# 5 Keto L ascorbic acid derivatives, their preparation, their use for inhibiting nitrosamine formation and for retarding oxidation, and the preparation of L ascorbic acid therefrom.

## Abstract
5 keto L ascorbic acid 5 hydrate and 5,5 dimethyl ketal and their alkali metal and alkaline earth metal salts are claimed, together with processes for their preparation, and their use as antioxidants and as intermediates for the produc tion of L ascorbic acid. 5 keto L ascorbic acid and its 5,5 dimethyl ketal exhibit antiscorbutic activity.
