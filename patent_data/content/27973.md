# A binder for a polydiene composite propellant.

## Abstract
A binder obtained by reaction of a combination of a a polydiene glycol having a hydroxyl group number of 1.8 2.1 and a number average molecular weight of 1,000 5,000, b a polydiene polyol having a hydroxyl group number from more than 2.1 to less than 4.0 and a number average molecular weight compound of 1,000 5,000, and c a diisocyanate.
