# TERMINAL CONSTRUCTION

## Abstract
A rolled up terminal end 22 adapted for insertion into an opening 37 in a printed circuit board PCB 3 or the like comprising a first end section 30 with an effective width d₁ less than the diameter of the PCB opening 37 , a second section having a terminal retain ing ramp means 32 with an effective width d₂ greater than the diameter of the PCB opening 37 and an elon gated slot 35 extending throughout at least a portion of the second section.
