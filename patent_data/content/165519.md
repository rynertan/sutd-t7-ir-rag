# A blood filtering system.

## Abstract
A blood filtering system acomprising means 2, 3 for the withdrawal of the blood from a patient 1 or some other source, a filter 11 , by means of which a filtrate can be withdrawn from the blood as well as means 4, 5 for the return of the remainder of the blood to the source together with any replacement fluid, a measuring device 26 being arranged for the measurement of the amount of the filtrate withdrawn. In connection with the said measuring device 26 a pressure equalizing device 20 is arranged for the equalizing of the pressure in the flow of filtrate conducted to the measuring device 26 . The system in accordance with the invention is intended in particular to be used in conjunction with haemofiltration or plasmafers where a replacement fluid is supplied in place of the filtrate withdrawn.
