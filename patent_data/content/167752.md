# Apparatus for the separation of solid particulates from a gaseous effluent.

## Abstract
Apparatus for separating solids and vapors from a suspension, this apparatus comprising a disengaging cham ber 104 , a riser conduit 101 open at the upper end, surrounded at the upper end by annular shroud 107 comprising a hollow truncated conical element 108 . This element enhances directional flow and prevents return of solids to the separation apparatus. Cyclone separator means are in open lateral communi cation 109 with said shroud
