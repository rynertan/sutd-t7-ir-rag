# HINGE DEVICE PARTICULARLY FOR EYEGLASSES

## Abstract
The hinge device comprises a structure having a base 2 associable with a leg 3 and rigid with a box like body 5 in cluding a seat for an elastic element 22 and two shoulders 18 . An L like element 23 is hinged to the body 5 and is associable with the leg of a pair of eueyeglasses. A connection element 30 being hingedly connected to the L like element 23 and adapted to be rigidly associated with the front 31 of a pair of eyeglasses.
