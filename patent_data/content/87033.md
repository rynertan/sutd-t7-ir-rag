# Connecting device for endoscope.

## Abstract
A connecting device includes a cylindrical body 11 to both ends of which are connected an endoscope and a television camera and from which a light guide provided with an eyepiece section 28 at its one end for connecting a steal camera is extended. In the cylindrical body 11 is provided a support in which first and second prisms 21 , 22 having different light splitting ratios are movably provided to selectively face the other end of the light guide 25 .
