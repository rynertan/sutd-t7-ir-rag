# Crank device for an opening roof for a vehicle.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a crank device comprising a crank 1 and adapted to rotate an at least approximately vertical driving shaft 2 , which may actuate a displaceable panel of an opening roof for a vehicle. According to the invention a coupling member 7 is mounted between the crank 1 and the driving shaft 2 and aher uncoupling, the crank 1 is rotatable about the central axis of the driving shaft 2 without rotation of this driving shaft 2 .
