# Combination bumper and radiator structure.

## Abstract
Herein disclosed is a combination bumper and radiator structure for attachment to the front end of an automotive vehicle having a fore and aft direction, comprising a front bumper 11 having a horizontally elongated base wall portion 12 , and upper and lower wall portions 13, 13 elongated along and rearwardly of the base wall portion 12 , the base wall portion 12 being formed with at least one air inlet opening 14 , and a radiator grille 15 positioned between the upper and lower wall portions 13, 13 of the front bumper 11 and partially exposed to the open air through the air inlet opening 14 .
