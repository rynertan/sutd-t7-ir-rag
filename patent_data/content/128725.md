# Preparation of penem derivatives.

## Abstract
Certain 2 substituted 2 penem 3 carboxylic acid com pounds and pharmaceutically acceptable salts thereof can be prepared from the appropriate xanthate or trithiocarbonate by desulfurization and addition of an electrophilic sulfur com pound followed by halogenation and ring closure. The corre sponding desulfurized and halogenated olefinic intermediates are disclosed.
