# A fungicidal composition.

## Abstract
A fungicidal composition which comprises an inert carrier and as an active ingredient E 1 2,4 dichlorophenyl 4,4 di methyl 2 1,2,4 triazol 1 yl 1 penten 3 ol and an N haloalkyl thiomide fungicide in the ratio of 1 to 0.1 20 by weight in a total amount of 0.1 to 99.9 by weight.
