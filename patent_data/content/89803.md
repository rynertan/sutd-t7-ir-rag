# POSITIVE DRIVE ROTATOR

## Abstract
In a booth 10 in which articles 11 are carried along a pathway for treatment with sprayed material directed through nozzles 50 or the like at the pathway, apparatus is provided for positively rotating the conveyed articles to assure uniform treatment on all sides thereof. Article sup ports 20 are connected to the conveyor 22,24,26 , each having a first portion 52 carried by the conveyor and a second portion 54 rotatable relative to the first portion for holding an article 11 . A toothed member 62 on the second portion is positively engaged by and driven by links of a link belt 58 to rotate the second hanger portion 52 and the article 11 held thereon.
