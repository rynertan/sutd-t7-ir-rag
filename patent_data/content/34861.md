# Cheese mould and or follower and method for manufacturing them.

## Abstract
A cheese mould having a wall of plastic material, which wall is provided with fine holes and the inner surface of which may be provided with grooves, said inner surface being sub jected to a roughening treatment, for instance rubbing with a steel brush, course sand paper, a rasp or a scouring sub stance, so that small scratches are made, in order to improve the rind forming properties of the mould.
