# Nose mask gas delivery device.

## Abstract
A nose mask 11 for administering one or more gases such as oxygen to a human being comprises, in combination, I a shell 10 having a front portion 12 , a rear portion 13 and a first opening comprising sub stantially the entire horizontal area beneath the wearer s nose and II a hollow gas delivery tube 15 containing at least one gas delivering and dispersing means 16 . The mask 11 enables a wearer to breathe, eat and con verse in a substantially normal fashion while efficiently providing the wearer with a cloud of gas enriched in the gas or gases being delivered directly beneath the nose and in front of the mouth of the wearer. The mask 11 is particularly useful in long term oxygen therapy applications.
