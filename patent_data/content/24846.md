# IMPROVEMENTS IN THE DELIVERY OF LIQUID

## Abstract
A pumping unit for pumping liquid, especially ale or lager beer, from a vehicle to tanks in a cellar as demountable. The unit comprises as components a pump 10 , a motor driven reeled hose 11 and a delivery meter 16 . The components are mounted and arranged on a supporting framework to form an integral movable unit suitable for installation on a flat platform vehicle. The framework has a load supporting base 2 , intermediate connecting means 1 and a load supporting roof 7 8 comprising pockets to receive the forks of a fork lift truck to enable the unit to be transported from place to place. The unit is also provided with a rack 39 and clamps 40 for clamping several carbon dioxide containing cylinders 17 within the unit, the carbon dioxide gas for aiding in pumping the liquid.
