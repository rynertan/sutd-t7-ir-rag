# An industrial robot.

## Abstract
An industrial robot having a stationary base 10 , a robot trunk 40 rotatably and vertically movably mounted on the stationary base, a robot arm means 46 horizontaly movably supported by the robot trunk a robot wrist 50 attached to the free end of the robot arm means, and first, second and third actuating means, the first actuating means having two electric motors 12, 32 for the robot trunk, the second actuating means having an electric motor 52 for the robot arm, and the third actuating means having two electric motors 66, 68 for the robot wrist.
