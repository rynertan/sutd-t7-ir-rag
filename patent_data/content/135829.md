# Enteric coating liquid.

## Abstract
An aqueous enteric coating liquid having a good disper sion stability and a good resistance to gastric juice comprising an alkali metal salt of an acid having an acid dissociation con stant of at least 3 at 25 C and a water insoluble oxycarboxylic acid type cellulose derivative dispersed in water or a mixture of water and at most 20 by weight of a lower alcohol.
