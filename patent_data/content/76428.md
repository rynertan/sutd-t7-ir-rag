# Microcapsules and method of preparing same.

## Abstract
Ethycellulose microcapsules, wherein a polymer mate rial which shows at least 1.2 times increase in weight by immersing it in water at 37 C is incorporated into the ethylcellulose coating walls and or the core material thereof, are disclosed. Said ethylcellulose microcapsules show rapid release of the core material in digestive organs such as stomach.
