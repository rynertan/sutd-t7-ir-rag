# Liquid delivery system.

## Abstract
A fluid delivery system including a flexible plastic con tainer 10 having a filling inlet 36 in the upper portion thereof and a feeding outlet 40 in the lower portion thereof with a control valve 47 to control flow from the outlet. The container is provided with a first hanger member 32 for hanging the container on a first hanging axis A A . The container is further provided with a second hanger member 34 for hanging the container on a second hanging axis B B which extends at right angles to the first hanging axis. The container is provided with an internal barrier means 22 which extends from one edge of the container to a point spaced from the opposite edge of the container along a line parallel to the second hanging axis.
