# Automated apparatus and method for assay plate reading.

## Abstract
A method for determing the end point in a viral assay comprising electronically scanning a microtiter plate to obtain a plurality of data points from each well, transmitting the data points to a digital computer pro gramed to analyze the data points from each well to deter mine whether each well is positive or negative for CPE in accordance with predetermined parameters, and calculating the viral titer from the positive or negative results for each well.
