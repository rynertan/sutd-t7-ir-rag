# ELASTOMER COATED BIAS REINFORCEMENT FABRIC AND METHOD AND APPARATUS FOR PRODUCING SAME

## Abstract
Method of making a flexible reinforcing strip or the like having a layer of fabric means 12 and polymeric layer means 22 secured on at least one side of said layer of fabric means. The method comprises the steps of providing via 55 said layer of fabric means on a tenter frame means 34 , applying said polymeric layer means in liquid form to said layer of fabric means while said layer of fabric means is on said tenter frame means, drying at 38 said polymeric layer means, and transversely stretching at 76 78 said layer of fabric means with said tenter frame after said polymeric layer means has been applied thereto and before said polymeric layer means has been dried thereon.
