# Swivel cable coupling for an air carbon arc cutting and gouging torch.

## Abstract
A swivel coupling of the type for mounting a concentric cable consisting of an electrical conductor inside of an insulated conduit to an apparatus such as an air carbon arc cutting and gorging torch wherein a connector used to mount the cable to the torch holds a retainer which can swivel 360 relative to the connector, the retainer in turn holds a cable coupling which is adjusted to hold the conductor and the conduit, the conduit positioned to cooper ate with a spring and washer to provide intimate contact between internal electrically conductive parts of the cou pling.
