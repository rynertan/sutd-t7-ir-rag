# A method of sealing bridge deck joints.

## Abstract
A method of sealing bridge deck joints wherein the deck members 11, 12 have the road surfacing 13, 14 adjoining the joint removed therefrom and a flexible composition of chips of aggregate 17 in a rubberized binder 18 , such as bitumen, pitch, pitch tar or tar is inserted in the channel thus formed as a resilient sealing material.
