# Device for chipping wood like material.

## Abstract
A device for chipping wood with the aid of a combina tion of a cylinder 3 rotatable about its longitudinal axis and provided with one or more protruding, longitudinal knives 16 and a counter knife 7 disposed alongside the cylinder 3 wherein along at least three quarters of its length the cylinder 3 has a surface with transverse grooves 11 and, viewed in the direction of rotation, in front of each knife 16 a slot 12 for temporarily collecting and conducting away the chipped wood.
