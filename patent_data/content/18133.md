# Electrical switch with several selectors.

## Abstract
An electrical switch for use as a coding device com prises a body 1 of electrically insulating material carry ing first and second bus contacts 2, 3 and a plurality of individual contacts 4 , and a plurality of selector members 7 respectively associated with the individual contacts 4 and each operable selectively to connect the associated individual contact 4 with either the first 2 or the second 3 or neither bus contact. The selector members 7 are held in position by a cover 12 having slots 13 through which operating members 14 of the selector members 7 project.
