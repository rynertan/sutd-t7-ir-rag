# Thermally developable, light sensitive material.

## Abstract
A thermally developable, light sensitive material is dis closed having at least one thermally developable, light sensi tive layer formed on a support which comprises a a light sensi tive silver halide, b an organic silver salt, c a reducing agent and d a binder, said binder containing gelating and or a gelatin derivative and a poly vinyl alcohol having a viscosity average polymerization degree of not more than 700.
