# Test container and method of using it.

## Abstract
The present invention describes an apparatus and method for conducting immunochemical reactions in a self contained sealed unit that requires only the addition of an unknown sample and water. The apparatus comprises a test tube 10 with at least three chambers 14, 16, 18 each containing different chemicals, including a solid sphere 24, 26, 28 , and separated from each other by a water soluble barrier 20, 22 .
