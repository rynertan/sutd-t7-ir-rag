# Construction of door glass guide in motor vehicle.

## Abstract
A construction of a door glass guide in a motor vehicle, wherein a door glass 10 of a three dimensional curved surface is vertically movably guided, characterized in that slide members 14 each having a spherical head portion 14C being shifted into a compartment 12 are mounted at least at upper end portions of front and rear end edges 10A and 10B of the door glass 10 a pair of front and rear guide channel portions 18A and 18B are provided on a door frame 16 , the guide channel portions 18A and 18B having curvatures substantially equal to respective curvatures of the front and rear end edges 10A and 10B , of the door glass 10 in the longitudinal section thereof, for vertically, slidably guiding the slide members 14 .
