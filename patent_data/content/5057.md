# A material and composition for reducing blood pressure.

## Abstract
A material and composition for reducing blood pressure are provided. The blood pressure in animals is reduced by administering tyrosine, a tyrosine precursor, tryptophan or mixtures thereof, either alone or in combination with a substance which is known to reduce blood pressure. A composition is provided comprising a unit dosage form of tyrosine, a precursor for tyrosine, tryptophan or mixtures thereof and a substance which is known to reduce blood pressure.
