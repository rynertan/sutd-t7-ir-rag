# 1H Imidazo 4,5 c quinolines and 1H imidazo 4,5 c quinolin 4 amines.

## Abstract
Classes of substituted 1H imidazo 4,5 c quinolines and 1H imidazo 4,5 c quinolin 4 amines are disclosed as bron chodilator agents and antiviral agents, respectively. Pharmacological methods of using the compounds as bron chodilators and antiviral agents, pharmaceutical com positions containing the compounds and synthetic inter mediates for preparing the compounds are also described.
