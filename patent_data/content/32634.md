# Fishing line control device and method of making same.

## Abstract
A spin casting style fishing reel that can sit on top a fishing rod or be suspended thereunder. The reel has a line spool 130 with a flange 133, 136 that is positioned inside the spinner head. In order to prevent untensioned fishing line from getting caught underneath the spinner head, a rim 144, 147 on the edge of the flange has a plurality of flocked fibers 146 thereon. The invention also comprehends a method of coating the flange rim with fibers.
