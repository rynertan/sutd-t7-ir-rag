# Method and apparatus for automatically applying a milking cluster.

## Abstract
An animal to be milked and the milking cluster are first coarsely positioned relatively to each other, followed by detecting the position of at least the animal s teats relative to at least the teat cups 1 4 of the milking cluster by means of ultrasonorous waves 12 , then accurately positioning the teat cups 1 4 of the milking cluster relatively to the teats, starting from the detected position of the teats, and finally applying the teat cups 1 4 to the teats.
