# Armor piercing projectile.

## Abstract
An armor piercing projectile is provided having har dened multi layers and being relatively simple in design. The projectile includes an axial core 1 , a continuous strip 4 of metallic glass wound about the core to form for the projectile a laminated body 3 , a generally conical frontal surface 6 , and a transverse rear surface 5 , and a bonding agent for joining the adjacent laminated surfaces.
