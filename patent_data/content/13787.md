# Octahydro pyrazolo 3,4 g quinolines, process for their preparation and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.

## Abstract
Octahydropyrazolo 3,4 g quinolines of the general formula These compounds, dopamine agonists, useful in treat ment of Parkinsonism and in inhibiting secretion of prolac tin, are prepared by reacting a corresponding 7 dimethyl aminomethylene 6 oxo derivative with hydrazine hydrate.
