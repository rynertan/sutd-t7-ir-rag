# APPARATUS FOR MOUNTING ELECTRICAL PART.

## Abstract
Apparatus for mounting an electrical part 2 on a printed board. The board is conveyed to a predetermined position and fixed there. A part mounting head 8 for inserting a part 2 into the board and an anvil head 10 for bending the legs of a part inserted into the board are moved integrally in the horizontal plane, and are movable at every time between an independent isolated supply unit 1 and the predetermined position of each board.
