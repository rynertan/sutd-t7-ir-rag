# Accessory drive device in engine.

## Abstract
A device which drives accessories such as an alternator or a cooling water pump utilizing output of an automobile en gine, includes an input shaft installed to an output shaft of the engine, a nonstage transmission installed between the shaft and a pulley connected to the accessory, and a one way clutch to allow the whole device to rotate at the same direction and the same speed as that of the input shaft if the input shaft and the pulley are directly coupled due to fault of the nonstage transmission.
