# Micronutrient fertilizers and method of growing plants.

## Abstract
Micronutrient fertilizer consisting of iron, manganese, copper, boron, and zinc in which the manganese to iron ratio is 8 to 20 , the copper to iron ratio is 5 to 10 , the boron to iron ratio is .4 to 1.0 and the zinc to iron ratio is 6 to 12 and preferably molybdenum in a molybdenum to iron ratio of about 0.02 to 0.07 . A method of growing plants in containers in which said micronutrient fertilizer of the preceding paragraph is mixed with a soil medium and basic fertilizer supplying nitrogen, phosphorons, potassium, calcium, magnesium and sulfur and is used in the container as the growing medium for the plant.
