# Improvements in or relating to chain components.

## Abstract
A chain sling assembly includes a main link 2 provided with two separable connector links 14 each carrying two shackles 50 and shortening clutches 66. Each connector link 14 has a first portion 16 profiled to co act with the main link 2 and a second portion 18 profiles to co act with the shackles 50, hubs 42 of the first potion 16 extending externally of hubs 44 of the second portion 18 and being connected with a pin 20. The shortening clutches permit adjustment of the free length of a fall portion of chain 83 by engaging a link 81 in a jaw portion 76.
