# HUMAN ENDOGENOUS CANCER REGULATORY FACTOR, PROCESS FOR PREPARING THE SAME, PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITION CONTAINING THE SAME AND HUMAN MONOCYTIC LEUKEMIA CELL LINES

## Abstract
A human endogenous cancer regulatory factor KBS having a molecular weight of 82,000 10,000 and an isoelectric point of pH 6.5 0.5, a process for preparing KBS and a pharmaceutical composition containing KBS are disclosed. The KBS of the present invention is useful as a potent tumoricidal or anti tumor agent.
