# Spin finish for textile fibre or yarn including lubricant and, as antistatic agent, a quaternary ammonium salt derived from an N,N dialkylamino alcohol alkoxylate, preparation and uses thereof.

## Abstract
A spin finish composition for textile fibre or yarn includ ing a lubricant and antistatic agent, optionally an additional emulsifier and an antioxidant, in which the antistatic agent is an N, N dialkylamino alcohol alkoxylate. The finish is less prone to coking of heater plates in yarn texturing machines.
