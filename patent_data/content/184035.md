Compounds of this type, which have bromine atom at 8 position can not find in the prior arts. We have found that the introduction of a bromine atom to the 8 position in the antibacterial quinolonecarboxylic acid derivatives give rise to enhance their activity. The compounds of the present invention are used as the intermediate for the synthesis of the agent. The compounds of the formula I are synthesized as below. The compound of the formula 11 ,EMI2.1 wherein R1 is lower alkyl group, X and Y are each independently halogen atom, is reacted with an equimolar or excess amount of orthoformic ester in acetic anhydride 1 to 20 fold volume per the total volume of the other,reagents at room temperature to 200 C, preferably 100 to 1500C for several hours to give the compound of the formula III ,EMI2.2 2 1 wherein R is lower alkyl group and R , X and Y have the abovestated meanings. The compound of the formula III is converted by treating with cyclopropylamine an equimolar or excess amount in a solvent such as ethanol to the compound of the formula IV , EMI3.1 wherein R1, X and Y have the above stated meanings. The compound of the formula IV is treated with an appropriate base such as sodium fluoride, potassium fluoride or sodium hydride in a solvent such as dioxane, alcohol, dimethylformamide, dimethyl sulfoxide and sulfolane at 0 to 200 C, preferably 50 to 1500C for one or several hours to give the compound of the formula I ,EMI3.2 wherein R1 and X have the above stated meanings. The compound of the formula I can be hydrolyzed by the usual acid or alkali hydrolysis to the compound of the formula I , EMI4.1 wherein X has the above stated meaning.
