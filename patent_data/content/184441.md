# CULTURE MONITORING SYSTEM

## Abstract
A system for continuously monitoring the chemistry of a liquid medium within a container comprising an assaying means located exteriorly of the container, a conduit con nected to the assaying means and leading to the container, a probe connected to the conduit and penetrating a wall of the container, the probe including a microporous filtering ele ment for separating a filtrate from the culture medium, the filtering element having a surface roughness less than about 50 microinches, and means for delivering the filtrate from the probe through the conduit to the assaying means.
