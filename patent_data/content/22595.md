# Machine for wrapping elongated articles in plastic film.

## Abstract
A machine for the production of a web with individually wrapped drinking straws from two separate plastic films C₁, C₂ . The machine includes a rotatable drum 8 around which one of the plastic films C₁ is led and simultaneously given a corrugated form. The drinking straws are thereafter placed one by one in the recesses, and the second plastic film C₂ is applied and heat sealed to said corrugated first film C₁ so that the drinking straws are completely enclosed in air tight packages.
