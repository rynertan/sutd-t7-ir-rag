# Method of forming a crimpable hose fitting.

## Abstract
A crimped fitting for flexible hose wherein the fitting consists of three separate components, namely a nipple 10 , socket 22 and cap 32 , the annular socket 22 being compressed upon the annular cap 32 in a mechanically integral relationship, and the cap 32 being radially com pressed upon the tubular nipple 10 . The fitting components are characterized by their economy of manufacture and assembly.
