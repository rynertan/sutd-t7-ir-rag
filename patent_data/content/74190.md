# WRITING DEVICE FOR PLOTTING AND RECORDING APPARATUS

## Abstract
A recording or plotting apparatus has a pen 2 which is movable on a record surface and which is supported on a penholder or carriage 1 . Two portions of cable 3 are connected to the penholder or carriage 1 and the lengths of these cable portions can be varied by a driving means 7 in order to move the penholder or carriage on the record surface. The cable portions are maintained under tension.
