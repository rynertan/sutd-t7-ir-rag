# Electromagnetic lock.

## Abstract
An electromagnetic lock for, for example, a door compris ing an electromagnet 13 attached to the frame 12 and a mag netisable member 18 attached to the door the magnetisable member being attracted to the electromagnet when the door is in its closed or otherwise locked position, shoulder means 22, 23 being provided on or adjacent the electromagnet and the magnetisable member whereby if the door is forced to over come the electromagnetic attraction the shoulder means en gage to prevent unauthorised unlocking of the door.
