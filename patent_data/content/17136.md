# Plastic post apparatus and methods.

## Abstract
A post made of one piece of molded polymeric material having a vertically upwardly extending elongate post portion 26 for supporting apparatus mounted thereon a driving head 23 connected to and mounted below the lower end of the post portion for application of force thereto without application of force to the upper end of the post means portion for forcibly driving the post apparatus into the ground a stabilizer 24 mounted on the driving head below the lower end of the post portion for stabilizing and supporting the post apparatus in a vertical attitude after being forcibly driven into the ground and a ground penetrat ing shaft 20 connected to and mounted below the stabilizer for initial penetration of the ground during forcible driving of the post apparatus into the ground.
