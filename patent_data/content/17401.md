# Articles of manufacture having adhesive properties.

## Abstract
Various articles of manufacture are described, each such article having adhesive properties and comprising a plastics matrix in which is dispersed a gelatinous adhesive composi tion comprising the product resulting from heating together components a and b to promote chemical reaction between the two said components, component a being at least one polyhydric alcohol, and component b being a material selected from gelatin, naturally occurring high molecular weight polysaccharide gums, and resins which are a copolymer of a vinyl ether and an organic acid anhydride or its free acid.
