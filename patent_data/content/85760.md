# NOVEL 1 4 BIS SUBSTITUTED AMINO 5,8 DIHYDROXYANTHRAQUINONES AND LEUCO BASES THEREOF

## Abstract
This disclosure describes novel 1,4 bis substituted amino 5,8 dihydroxyanthraquinones and leuco bases there of, useful as chelating agents and for inhibiting the growth of transplanted mouse tumors. This disclosure also describes compositions of matter useful as inhibitors of transplanted mouse tumor growth and the method of inducing the regression and or palliation of leukemia and related cancers in mammals therewith.
