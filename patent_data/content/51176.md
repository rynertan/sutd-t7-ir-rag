# Tape joint for cylindrical members.

## Abstract
A tape joint 10 for cylindrical members is provided wherein a flexible tape member 16 is inserted into a cavity 24 between telescoping first 12 and second 14 cylindrical members in a twisted manner so as to lock the members together as a unit. The tape member is manually slip fitted into position and is provided with a handle 30 for aiding in the insertion and removal of the tape member.
