# Device for manoeuvring fast connection tangential button operated of splined profile clutches, especially on end forks of agriculture cardanic shafts.

## Abstract
Actuating device for a quick release splined coupling with a tangential latch pin, comprising an actuating ring 4 having an internal ramp s 6 to engage the latch pin s 2 . Rotation of the ring 4 in the direction fA shown, de pressing the pin s 2 to clear the splined profile 1B against the action of springs 3 which bias the ring 4 to a coupling engaged position.
