# Valve core assembly for a shut off equalizing valve.

## Abstract
A valve core assembly 18 for a shut off equalizing valve has a cylindrical metallic core member with an enlarged barrel portion having a cross bore formed therethrough. A seal element 34 is moulded onto the barrel portion and includes first and second circumferential ribs 36 adjacent the ends of the barrel portion, and pairs of longitudinal ribs 38 in straddling relation to the openings of the cross bore.
