# A visible double heterostructure semiconductor laser.

## Abstract
A visible double heterostructure semiconductor laser comprising an lnGaAs, lnAlp, lnAlAs, lnAlSb or lnGaSb layer, a cladding layer on the lnGaAs, lnAlp, lnAlAs, lnAlsb or lnGasb layer, an active layer on the cladding layer and a second clad ding layer on the active layer, wherein a mixed crystal of lnGaAs, lnAlp, lnAlAs, lnAlSb or lnGaSb is used, as a substrate crystal for growing of said lnGaAs, lnAlP, lnAlAs, lnAlSb or lnGaSb layer thereon, resulting in the approximate coincidence between the lattice constant of the substrate crystal and the lattice constant of each of these layers, an energy difference of 0.2 eV or more between the direct transition and the indirect transition within said active layer, and an energy difference of 0.2 eV or more between the active layer and either of the first or the second cladding layers.
