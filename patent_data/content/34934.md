# A process for the production of layered glass batch pellets, pellets produced by such process and apparatus for producing such pellets.

## Abstract
The invention provides a process for the production of layered glass batch pellets, pellets produced by such process and apparatus for producing such pellets. To improve homogeneity layered glass batch pellets formed in a pelletiz ing zone from sand, a calcium oxide source and aqueous caustic soda, use recycled pellets as nuclei.
