# Continuous yarn dyeing.

## Abstract
A process for continuously dyeing yarn comprises depositing on the surface of yarn 1 to be dyed a uniform coating of dye liquid in a quantity such that the amount of dye liquid deposited per unit area of yarn surface is less than the same unit area of the yarn could absorb naturally then heating the coated yarn so as first to remove substantially all the liquid from the dye and then to fix the dye in the yarn. The yarn is ready for use immediately after the heat treatment is completed.
