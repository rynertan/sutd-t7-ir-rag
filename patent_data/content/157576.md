# Automobile suspension system.

## Abstract
A suspension system for an automobile wherein after hav ing been changed over to the HARD mode by judging the commencement of the turning, the characteristic of the sus pension is not restored to the standard condition until the com pletion of the turning is detected, so that a stable running per formance is obtained irrespective of the status of the curve of a road while a good riding comfort at a normal running is also maintained.
