# Embroidery machine.

## Abstract
An embroidery machine comprises stitch forming means I for forming stitches on a fabric, fabric holding means II for holding the fabric, and driving means III arranged to regulate the relative position between the fabric holding means II and the stitch forming means I . Pattern determining means IV include a display unit IV 1 for two dimensionally displaying a selected pattern and an input unit IV 2 for feeding data of a pattern to be displayed on the display unit. Control means V are arranged to control the operation of the driving means III in accordance with a pattern produced by the pattern determining means IV and to control the operation of the stitch forming means I in synchronism with the action of the driving means.
