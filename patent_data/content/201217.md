# Heat shield for refractory tube in molten glass feeder.

## Abstract
A heat shield for the refractory tube 26 in a molten glass feeder has tubular metal sheath means 200 with semi circular insulating segments 210, 212 stacked therein. The sheath means may be made in half sections 200a, 200b to permit easy removal and the segments are provided in sets with relieved areas to accommodate the reciprocating refractory plungers 12 of the feeder.
