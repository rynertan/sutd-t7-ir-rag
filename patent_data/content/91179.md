# Synergistic antiphlogistic compositions and process for the preparation thereof.

## Abstract
The invention relates to synergistic antiphlogistic phar maceutical compositions comprising a pyrido 1,2 a pyrimidine derivative of the general Formula I The advantage of the compositions is that as a result of the synergistic activity of the compound of the general Formula I the antiphlogistic Indomethacin of the Formula II can be administered in significantly lower doses and conse quently the unfavourable side effects of the latter compound are mitigated.
