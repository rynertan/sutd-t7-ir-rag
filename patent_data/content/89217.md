# An article of furniture.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a reclining chair or lounge having a pivotally connected back portion 6 . The preferred form is a moulded plastic sun lounge which can recline to function as a couch or bed. The sun lounge is able to be stacked when in the fully reclined position, and has arm rests 11 which are adjusted in attitute according to the position of the back rest 6 so as to maintain comfortable arm support throughout all back rest positions.
