# A stopper for containers for use in analyses.

## Abstract
A stopper for containers for use in analyses comprising a resilient body 1 having an axial cavity 5 closed by a perforatable transverse wall 2 provided with slits 3 defining segments 4 , said slits 3 allowing for the opening of the stopper when a tubular member 9 is inserted in the cavity 5 and closing thereof when the segments 4 return to the initial position thereof. The cavity is provided with a plurality of longitudinal ribs 6 adapted for defining pas sages 10 for air between the body 1 and the tubular member 9 . The stopper may slide within the container.
