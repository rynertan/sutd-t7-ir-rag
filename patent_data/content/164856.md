# An aluminium alloy support for a lithographic printing plate.

## Abstract
An aluminium alloy support for a lithographic printing plate, suitable for an electrochemical roughening treatment and excellent in fatigue resistace, heat softening resistance and printability, is provided which comprises aluminium and contains 0.05 to 1 by weight of Mn, not more than 0.2 by weight of Si, not more than 0.5 by weight of Fe and unavoidable traces of impurities.
