# Catalysts containing boron and halogen for the polymerization of halophosphazene trimers.

## Abstract
A compound described by the molecular formula BNP₂O₂X₆, wherein X is selected from the group consisting of P, Cl, Br, I and mixtures thereof and having the structure A process for its manufacture by reacting in a halogen ated solvent a compound of the formula X₂P O NHP O X₂ with a compound of the formula BX₃, wherein X is selected from the class consisting of P, Cl, Br, I and mixtures thereof. Use thereof 2 as catalyst in the polymerisation of halophos phazene trimers and other oligomers.
