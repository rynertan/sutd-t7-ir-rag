# Crystalline forms of N 2 6 methoxy benzothiazolyl N phenyl urea and process for their preparation.

## Abstract
Crystalline monohydrate Form III and anhydrate Form IV of N 2 6 methoxy benzothiazolyl N phenylurea frentizole are described herein, which are stable, have a greater bulk density, are less electrostatic and are more freely flowing than previous crystalline forms.
