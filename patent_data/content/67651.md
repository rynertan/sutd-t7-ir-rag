# Vehicle body floor construction of motor vehicle.

## Abstract
A vehicle body floor construction of a motor vehicle, wherein a tunnel 2 upwardly projecting from the surface of a floor panel 1 , downwardly opening and disposed in the longitudinal direction of the vehicle is formed at the central portion in the widthwise direction of the floor panel 1 , a fuel tank 3 is disposed in the tunnel 2 , and a protector 4 is disposed in a manner to block the bottom opening of the tunnel 2 at least within a range of the fuel tank 3 and connected to the floor panel 1 at opposite sides of the opening.
