# Method of manufacturing thin metal wire.

## Abstract
A thin metal wire of circular cross section is formed by ejecting molten metal from a spinning nozzle 9 into contact with a layer of liquid coolant which is supplied through a nozzle 10 and is maintained by centrifugal force against the inner surface of a curved run of a conveyor belt 1 , the wire being led from the belt 1 and wound on to a reel 11 .
