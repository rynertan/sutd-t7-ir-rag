# Skid rail.

## Abstract
A skid rail for a reheat furnace which supports the slabs or work pieces moving therethrough without excessive cooling or tiger striping of such work pieces includes a hollow structur al member 11 having an upper surface 12 , and a wear bar 14 having a plurality of support blocks 16 secured thereto for supporting the wear bar in a position above the upper surface of the structural member. Cleats 20 engage the blocks 16 to secure the wear bar on the surface and insulation 26 under the wear bar and around the blocks and cleats, the material of the wear bar and the blocks having low thermal conductivity and high strength when subjected to the elevated tempera tures of the reheat furnace.
