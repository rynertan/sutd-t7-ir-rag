# Burnable poison rod for a nuclear reactor fuel assembly.

## Abstract
A burnable poison rod 10 for use in a nuclear reactor fuel assembly which includes concentrically disposed tubes 12, 14 having an annular space 22 therebetween which receives annular burnable poison pellets 24 and is closed at both ends by plugs 16, 18 . A C shaped spring clip 26 located in the plenum space above the pellet 24 has alternating flat and round areas 36, 38 , the round areas 38 conforming to the outer rods 14 inner surface to provide a retentive force to the pellets 24 .
