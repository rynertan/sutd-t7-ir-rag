# Polyoxyalkylenepolyamine curing agents for epoxy resins, method of making the curing agents, and curing method.

## Abstract
Disclosed are curing agents for epoxy resins consisting of modified polyoxyalkylenepolyamines obtained by the addition reaction of an alkyleneimine to a polyoxyalkylene polyamine, preferably a polyoxypropylenepolyamine or polyoxyethylenepolyalkylamine, and ethyleneimine or prop yleneimine in acid medium.
