# Apparatus for trimming a hedge.

## Abstract
Concave recesses 40 are provided in the side walls 29 of slots 28 in the guide member 12 of a rechargeable, battery powered reciprocating hedge and shrub trimmer. The recesses 40 have inwardly facing portions 42 which extend a distance w greater than 50 of the effective height h of the blade 34 of the cutter bar 5 associated with the guide member 12 .
