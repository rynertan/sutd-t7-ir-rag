# Soluble quinazolino 1,4 benzodiazepin 5,13 dione derivatives, process for their preparation and pharmaceutical composition containing them.

## Abstract
Novel quinazolino 1,4 benzodiazepin 5,13 dione deriva tives, which are soluble in aqueous media and which have been found to be antagonists of the function of cholecystoki nins CCK , to the preparation of these compounds from compounds prepared by aerobic fermentation of certain Aspergillus alliaceus strains, and to the use of these compounds to antagonize the function of CCK, which antagonism is useful, e.g., for the treatment and prevention of disorders of the gastrointestinal, central nervous and appetite regulatory systems of mammals, especially of hu mans.
