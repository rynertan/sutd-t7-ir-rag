# A liquid reservoir and a liquid supply arrangement.

## Abstract
A tube 22 contains a liquid absorbent material 28, an end portion of which can be immersed in the liquid contained within a bag 20 positioned about a tree 30 and transfer the liquid at a rate dependant on the attitude of the tube 22 to the end 27 of the tube 22 inserted in the ground adjacent the root structure 31 of the tree 30.
