# Derivatives of di 2 chloroethyl aminophenyl alanine with antitumour activity.

## Abstract
Compound with high anti tumour activity and of moder ate toxicity are described which are constituted by tripeptides formed from dichlorodiethylaminophenylalanine, para fluorophenylalanine and methionine bonded together by peptide links. The tripeptides of the invention are particularly useful in the treatment of malignant tumours.
