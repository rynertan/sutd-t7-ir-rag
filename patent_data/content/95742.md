# Improved form and method of fabricating a glass lampshade.

## Abstract
A sectional form 10 for fabricating Tiffany type lamp shades and a method for fabricating such lampshades include a form having an alignment grid 16 affixed to the outer sur face thereof. Cartoon strips corresponding in shape to the re gions of the alignment grid are releasably secured to the outer surface of the form and cut glass is thereafter assembled into sections of a glass lampshade on the form. Single or multiple sets of cartoon strips may be used to assemble a lampshade having either a repeating or nonrepeating design.
