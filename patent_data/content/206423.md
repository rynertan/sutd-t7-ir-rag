# Process for an optical control to be exerted in a cloth mill on the fabrics and control device used hereby.

## Abstract
Process for executing an optical control on the fabricat ed fabrics in a weaving mill, characterized in that it mainly consists in moving the fabric 8 alongside a control plan 9 between the weaving loom 2 and the fabric roll 4 , letting pass an optical feeler 10 alongside the control plan 9 comparing the measured signal with one or more compari son values and the transmission of defects and or stopping the weaving loom 2 when the detected signal differs much from the set comparison value.
