# Display device.

## Abstract
A large sized display device includes a display means 2 having e.g. a number of heat generating display elements such as lights, etc. and is provided internally with a cooling means 4 which prevents external moisture, etc. from creating problems in the internal equipment. Air is blown to cool the display means 2 . An air inlet 7 and an air outlet 8 are provided for cooling means 4 , which outlets are preferably provided with adjustable covers 9 .
