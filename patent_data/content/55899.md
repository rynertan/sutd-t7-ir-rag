# Antiallergic imidodisulfamides, process for their production and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.

## Abstract
Imidodisulfamide derivatives useful in the treatment of allergic conditions are prepared by reaction of an approp riately substituted tetrahydroisoquinoline and bis chlorosulfonyl imide in the presence of a non nucleophilic organic base. Pharmaceutical compositions and methods of inhibiting the symptons of an allergic response are also disclosed.
