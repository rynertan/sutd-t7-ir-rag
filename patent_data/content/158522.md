# CONTINUOUS MONITORING OF WATER QUALITY

## Abstract
A supply of water is continuously monitored to ensure purity by dividing the supply into a number of parallel streams and flowing each through a fish tank 14 containing a fish or other aquatic animal , the health of each fish being monitored by electrodes in each respective tank. The fish tanks are located together in a main tank 12 and the water supply is drawn from a header tank 16 via pipework 24 , the tank 16 being fed from supply 18 . The apparatus includes an electronic system to monitor the activity of the fish.
