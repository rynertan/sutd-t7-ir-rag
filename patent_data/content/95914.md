# ELECTRONIC TYPEWRITER

## Abstract
The typewriter has a keyboard 11 , a printing device 15 and a control unit 16 which includes a real time integrated circuit 23 adapted to provide the functions of clock and calen dar. The date can be inserted as a variable component in con stant sentences, which are stored in a non volatile memory dar. The date can be inserted as a variable component in con 22 for being printed afterwards at the operator s command. The typewriter includes a display 12 where, at the operator s requent and or automatically, the time and the date generated by the real time circuit 23 are displayed.
