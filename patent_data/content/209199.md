# High pressure discharge lamp.

## Abstract
The high pressure discharge lamp has electrodes which comprise a tungsten rod 24 which projects inside the lamp vessel and around whose end 25 projecting in side the lamp vessel a tungsten wire portion 26 is helically wound. The ends 30, 31 of the helically wound wire portion 26 are located within the sheath 29 of this wire portion 26 and the end faces 32, 33 are rupture surfaces devoid of burrs.
