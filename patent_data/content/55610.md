# Coffee maker.

## Abstract
A coffee maker is provided with a mill mechanism, a drip mechanism and a control device 46 . The control device contains a mill time setting counter 51 for setting a mill time of coffee beans in the form of digital data, a drive circuit 53 for driving the mill mechanism and a drive circuit 55 for driving the drip mechanism. When a start signal S39 is supplied to the control device 46 , the mill mechanism is driven only for the mill time set. After the lapse of the mill time, the drip mechanism is automatically actuated to brew coffee.
