# Machine for automatically inserting parallel lead electronic components into a printed circuit board.

## Abstract
Machine for processing and inserting parallel lead electronic components into lead receiving openings in a printed circuit board which comprises a frame 37 feed means for feeding an electronic component web carrier 2 carrying a plurality of electronic components 1 cutting means for separating each electronic component from said electronic component web carrier fed by said feed means, insert means for receiving said each electronic component at a predetermined position and inserting same into said printed circuit board, and transfer means 35 adapted to make movement between said cutting means and said insert means, said transfer means 35 including a lead chuck mechanism adapted to grip lead of said electronic component and to transfer same to said insert means after said electronic component has been separated from said electronic component web carrier by said cutting means. Said cutting means includes an obliquely holding member 100
