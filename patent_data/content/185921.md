# Fragrance and flavor compositions.

## Abstract
There is described a fragrance and flavor compositior containing 1,5,8,8 tetramethylbicyclo 8.1.0 undec 5 en 2 one. This sesquiterpene ketone has an odor reminiscent of a variety of odors based on the strong powdery, woody note and furthermore has high diffusivity and retentivity and accords well with a number of perfume, fragrance and flavor materials.
