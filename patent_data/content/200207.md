# Porcelain composition having a high dielectric constant.

## Abstract
A high dielectric porcelain composition of the Pb Mg 1 3, Nb 2 3 O₃ PbTiO₃ Pb Mn 1 3, Nb 2 3 O₃ system comprising a main component including 95 mol of Pb Mg 1 3, Nb 2 3 O₃, 5 mol of PbTiO₃ and 0.5 weight of Pb Mn 1 3, Nb 2 3 0₃ and a sub component including PbO and ZnO, and capable of being sintered at a temperature lower than 1000 C.
