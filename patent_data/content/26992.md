# Oral evacuation device.

## Abstract
A dental appliance for evacuating debris and liquid from the mouth during dental operations comprising a collector member 10 having a semi rigid bite block portion 47 and a combination tongue guard and a collector membrane holder 49 connected to the bite block portion 47 and carrying a flexible collector membrane 24 detachably secured to the bite block portion 47 , the collector membrane 24 in use engaging the tissues of the mouth so as to separate the mouth into two compartments, a forward compartment for working in and a rear compartment.
