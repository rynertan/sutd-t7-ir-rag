# WEAPON CARRYING VEHICLE

## Abstract
The invention relates to a vehicle 1 for transporting a heavy mortar 7 carried by a stand 6 which is connected to hydraulic cylinders 2, 4 with the aid of which the stand is movable from a position on the vehicle to a position on the ground. To prevent recoil force from being transferred to the hydraulic cylinders, the stand is provided with a foot plate 12 , taking up recoil force and being movable in the direction of the recoil force relative the rest of the stand.
