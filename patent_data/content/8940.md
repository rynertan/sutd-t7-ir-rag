# Roll handling apparatus.

## Abstract
Apparatus for handling large rolls R of paper or spools of wire including a pair of outwardly extending arms 2 , supported by air bearings 12 , which straddle the roll R along the axis in a lower hemisphere. The roll R is lifted upon inflation of the bearing 12 and transported to its desired location. The parallel arms 2 are adjustable toward and away from each other to accommodate rolls R of vary ing diameters.
