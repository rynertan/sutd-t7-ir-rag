# Grading device.

## Abstract
Device for grading or sorting objects such as eggs, provided with weighing devices which are located in at least two mutually parallel endless tracks and are adapted to be moved one after another by conveying members 1,2,3,4,5 along said tracks, each of said weighing devices being provided with a dischargeable gripper 47 . Along said tracks devices 8, 9 being mounted for feeding objects to said grippers 47 , each of said tracks having a part S in which said weighing devices are free to reach an equilibrium position and further separate discharge station 11 26 being provided near each of said tracks, each of said stations 11 26 being allotted to a predetermined weight class of the objects.
