# A joint for connecting wooden beams to each other and the use of the joint in roof truss structures.

## Abstract
A joint in which wooden beams 1 , 4 are made up of beam members side by side in the longitudinal direction of the beams, and in which beam joint element 7 , provided with nails 6 , are fitted on the inner surfaces of the beam members in such a way that they do not extend all the way to the outer surface of the beams. The use of the joint in truss structures is advantageous.
