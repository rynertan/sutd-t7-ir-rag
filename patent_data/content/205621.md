# LINING MATERIAL FOR PIPES.

## Abstract
Lining material formed by providing a specially shaped unwoven cloth on the inner side of a conventional air tight tubular woven cloth which has a film of a synthetic resin on its outer surface, and which is inserted into a pipe as it is reversed by a fluid pressure. This lining material has both the various properties required by a conventional lining material of this kind and some other properties, such as the earthquake proof characteristics and the shape retaining characteristics with respect to an external hydraulic pres sure, and is suitably used to reinforce various types of underground pipes.
