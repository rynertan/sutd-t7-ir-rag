# GEAR PUMP LIQUID GAS MIXER WITH IMPROVED GAS INTRODUCTION

## Abstract
A two stage mixing gear pump for dispersing a gas into a liquid wherein a metered quantity of liquid from the first stage of the pump is supplied to gas filled intertooth spaces of the second stage pump. The gas is introduced into the intertooth spaces of the second stage of the pump through a gas inlet which is adjacent the low pressure side of the gear teeth meshing zone of the second stage of the pump and the liquid enters the gas containing intertooth spaces downstre am of the gas inlet.
