# Push button type sprayer.

## Abstract
A push button type sprayer 10 comprises a movable push button 12 which is attached to a container 14 containing a liquid to be sprayed. A stopper 20 is formed integrally with the push button 12 to extend along the moving direction of the push button 12 . Formed in the outer peripheral surface of the container 14 is a recess 24 which contains the stopper 20 and has a shoulder portion 26 on which the stopper 20 abuts.
