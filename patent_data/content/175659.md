# An assembly with a lamp to be embedded having a member that can be engaged in the embedding seat and forming the housing for the lamp.

## Abstract
An assembly with a lamp to be embedded comprises a member 3 with annular development with a cylindrical wall and two flanges, one inside 35 and the other 33 outside suitable to place itself against the mouth of the embedding seat in said member 3 a housing suitable to receive with elastic yielding the lamp to be embedded 5 appendixes 10 rise from said annular member 3 towards the inside of the embedding seat, and engage spring type means 12 for retaining said member 3 within the embedding seat and in the lamp 5 a spherical surface 51 that is received in said housing and allows the lamp orientation.
