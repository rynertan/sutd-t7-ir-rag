# MOTORCYCLE SAFETY HELMET AND BRAKE LAMP SYSTEM

## Abstract
A safety helmet and brake lamp system is provided for a motorcycle rider, wherein an auxiliary brake lamp is incorpo rated into a motorcycle rider s safety helmet for illumination concurrently with illumination of at least one conventional brake lamp on the motorcycle. In one form, the auxiliary brake lamp is mounted on the helmet and connected via a quick detachable electrical connector in parallel with the brake lamp on the motorcycle. In another form, the auxiliary brake lamp is mounted on the helmet, together with a radio signal receiver apparatus for causing illumination of the auxiliary brake lamp upon receipt of an actuation signal from a radio transmitter on the motorcycle. The radio transmitter provides the actuation signal whenever the motorcycle brake lamp is illuminated.
