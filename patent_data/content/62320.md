# Catalyst for the oxychlorination of ethylene and use thereof.

## Abstract
A catalyst suitable for use in the preparation of 1,2 dichloroethane by oxychlorination of ethylene con taining cupric chloride and, as a promotor, a combina tion of potassium chloride and cesium chloride, which can inhibit production of ethyl chloride by product and has excellent selectivity to 1,2 dichloroethane and cata lyst life.
