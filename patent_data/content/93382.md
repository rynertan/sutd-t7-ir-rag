# Oil seal and sleeve installation tool.

## Abstract
A tool assembly is disclosed for assembling a polytetra fluoroethylene shaft seal to a wear or service sleeve 23 while simultaneously forming the polytetrafluoroethyylene wafers 20,21 into a desired configuration. The seal is formed and introduced onto the sleeve with a single reciprocation of the tool. The tool 24,28,34 includes a series of specially arranged surfaces having different diameters for forming the wafers of the seal into a frustoconical shape, aligning the seal and sleeve, and inserting the sleeve into the seal.
