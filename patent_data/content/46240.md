# Sled body and adjustable attachment means for snow skis.

## Abstract
A sled body apparatus 20 having an adjustable at tachment device 26 for connection to the conventional ski bindings 30, 32 mounted on a pair of snow skis 28 and which can be adapted to connect to the bindings of both down hill and cross country skis. The attachment device 26 of the preferred embodiment includes adjust able front 60 and rear segments 62 which can be indi vidually pre positioned relative to the sled body 20 to allow connection to substantially any desired size and style of ski binding. Alternatively, the adjustable attach ment device can be utilized to mount a pair of snow skis to a backpack frame 108 for carrying the skis, or to con vert a pair of skis into an equipment carrier by using an appropriate sled body frame.
