# Camera status and diagnostics display system.

## Abstract
An operator is provided with a continuous display, on a single convenient monitor 108 of the extent of progress, the status, etc., of each camera 102 in a multi camera system, during a microprocessor controlled automatic setup proce dure. The display is superimposed on a video picture generated by the selected camera, which corresponds to one of various video signals selectable by the operator. Insert means 14,18,20,28 are coupled to the monitor for generat ing an insert window in the video signal and a display generator 46,56 generates within the window a display of the status data.
