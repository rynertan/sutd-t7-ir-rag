# Photoconductive element.

## Abstract
The present invention relates to a photoconductive ele ment 10 comprising a photoconductive layer 16 and one or more blocking layers 14 , 18 adjacent thereto and hav ing space charge layers 22 , 24 interposed between the photoconductive layer 16 and the blocking layers 14 , 18 to increase the voltage acceptance potential of the photo conductive element 10 .
