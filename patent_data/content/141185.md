# Coater.

## Abstract
An appliance for a double sided coating of a moving web by means of two coating substance applicators 2,4 and a smoothing nip 11 comprising a back up roll 9 on one side of the web and a adjustable levelling blade 10 or the like on the other side of the web. The coating substance is supplied on the web 1 directly on the side facing the levelling blade 10 and on the surface of the back up roll 9 or the web 1 on the other side of the web 1 . The smoothing nip 11 and the both applicators 2, 4 are located within a 30 degrees sector of the back up roll 9 , preferably within a 20 degrees sector.
