# TOOTHBRUSH WITH REFILLABLE TOOTHPASTE RESERVOIR

## Abstract
The rifillable tube structure 1 comprises a tubular body 1 , having a dispensing outlet 2 at one end thereof. The other end 4a of said tubular body 1 also being apertured for refilling purposes and being closable by means of a stopper element 4 , provided with threads 6 for engaging a threat formation 5 formed on the tubular body 1 .
