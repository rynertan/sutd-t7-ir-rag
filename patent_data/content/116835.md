# FCC sulfur oxide acceptor.

## Abstract
An FCC sulfur oxide acceptor, its method of manufacture and use in the FCC process. The acceptor, a particulate solid containing magnesium, sodium and aluminum, the precur sor of which comprises a mixture of precipitates. One precipitate is a compound of aluminum and another is a compound of magnesium. The precipitates are simul taneously precipitated from a common solution in which they have a highly limited solubility.
