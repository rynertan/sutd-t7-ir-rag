# WEIGHING DEVICE

## Abstract
A weighing device having an endless track 1 to which balances 13, 18 are connected, said track having a region 4 for loading said balances with objects, a region 5 for letting the balances damp out to reach an end position, and means 7,9,35,36,37,38 for detecting said end position and indicate it in digital form for deriving the weight of the objects.
