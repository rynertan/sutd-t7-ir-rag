# Fluid Containers.

## Abstract
A rupturable pod for storing an aqueous alkaline photographic processing composition and dispensing the composition when ruptured, comprising an alkali resistant lining 2 in contact with the processing composition and adhered to itself in regions forming a container enclosing the composition, a gas and moisture barrier 4 of alu minum to prevent the passage of gas or moisture into or out of the container, and a resilient mechanical barrier layer 3 positioned between and adhered to the aluminum 4 and the lining 2 .
