# Device for lowering and lifting containers.

## Abstract
The device M has a rotary drum 4 provided with a plurality of guide recesses 4a , an outer cylindrical plate 1 surrounding the rotary drum 4 and a helical bar 6 disposed between the outer peripheral surface of the drum 4 and the plate 1 . Containers B are lowered and lifted along the bar 6 while being moved up and down in the respective guide recesses 4a .
