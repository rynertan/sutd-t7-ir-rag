# A 38533 antibiotics, their preparation, formulations containing them, and micro organism employed in their production.

## Abstract
Novel peptide antibiotics, A 38533 factors A₁, A₂, B and C, effective against Pseudomonas species are described. Also described are a process for preparation of the antibiotic factors by submerged aerobic fementation of a culture containing Streptomyces species NRRL 11298, a synthetic culture medium containing that organism and the organism per se.
