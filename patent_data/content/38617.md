# Diastereomers of amino acid esters, pesticidal compositions including them and method of controlling pests using them.

## Abstract
The two specific diastereomers S α cyano 3 phenoxybenzyl R 2 2 chloro 4 trifluoromethyl phenylamin 3 methylbutanoate and S α cyano 3 phenoxybenzyl R 2 4 trifluoromethylphenylamino 3 methylbutanoate are effective for the control of insect or acarid pests. The former compound is preferred.
