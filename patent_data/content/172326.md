# Compositions for the treatment of wounds.

## Abstract
The present invention provides a composition suitable for application to skin wounds, which comprises a a starch hydrolysate having a Dextrose Equivalent less than 35, and b i one or more compounds selected from alphaketoglutaric acid and salts thereof, and or ii one or more ferrous salts, and, if desired, c i one or more compounds selected from ascorbic acid and salts thereof, and or ii one or more amino acids.
