# Antibiotic crisamicin, its preparation and use and a microorganism capable of producing the same.

## Abstract
A novel Micromonospora strain is provided which pro duces Crisamicin, a complex having gram positive antibac terial, antiviral and antitumor activities. Crisamicin contains as major components compounds Crisamicin A and Crisamicin B, and minor amounts of compounds Crisamicin C, D and E.
