# CABLE TAPPING CLAMB

## Abstract
A branch clip for connecting a conductor to an electric main conductor having a body and two clamping jaws being rotatable about parallel axes and at least one clip member for connecting the branch conductor and a tapped hole holding a branch bolt directed to the main conductor, and a connecting block being coupled with tail pieces of said jaws during sightening the branch bolt such that the clamping jaws are clamped against the main conductor.
