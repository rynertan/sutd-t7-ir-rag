# METHOD FOR DRYING, CRUSHING AND DISTRIBUTION OF SOLID FUEL FOR A SOLID FUEL BOILER

## Abstract
Method for drying, crushing and distribution of solid fuel for a solid fuel boiler, for example a fluidized bed boiler. Ac cording to the invention moist fuel 9 is crushed and dried by intermixing gas and or ash 13, 14 in a unit 12 . The fuel is set in motion, for example in a cyclone 15 , together with gas 13 , and is divided into several subflows 27 29 to be fed into the boiler at different points 4 .
