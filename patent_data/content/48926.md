# High efficiency, fast extruders for extruding and refining soap and detergents.

## Abstract
Extruder for the extrusion and or refining and finishing pasty materials, such as soap, characterized by the high speed of the helix 7a, 7b, 7c in the range of 30 to 60 r.p.m. and high ratio length diameter La D, Lb D, Lc D of the helix ranging from 5 to 15.
