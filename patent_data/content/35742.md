# Homocitric acid oligoriboside derivatives, process for their manufacture and their use as preventives of dental caries.

## Abstract
The invention relates to a homocitric acid oligoriboside derivative of the formula It may be obtained by cultivating a streptomyces, such as streptomyces SP MF 980 CF1 FERM P5430 , and isolating it from the culture broth. It is useful as a preventative for dental caries.
