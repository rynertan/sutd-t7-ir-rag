# High molecular antitumor antibiotics AN 7D.

## Abstract
There is disclosed the newly found high molecular antitumor antibiotics AN 7D, which have a broad antitumor activity against cells of mouse leukemia and which therefore are expected to be useful for the treatment of human cancer. The antibiotics are obtained by culturing microor ganisms of the genus Streptomyces, capable of producing the antibiotics, for example Streptomyces griseoincarnatus FERM BP 129.
