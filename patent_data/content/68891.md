# CRT display terminal with modular design.

## Abstract
A cathode ray tube display terminal includes a printed circuit logic board permanently mounted in a moulded plastic enclosure assembly 9 during on the shelf storage and shipment of the enclosure as a spare part and during normal operation of the CRT display terminal. The enclo sure assembly includes a top portion 12 and a lower portion 20 which snap together. The printed circuit logic board 14 is assembled between the upper and lower portions of the enclosure which are secured by a single screw 24. A snap out section 7 allows replacement of a read only memory on the logic board.
