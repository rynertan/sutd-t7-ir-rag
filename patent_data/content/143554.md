# Disc reproducing apparatus.

## Abstract
In a compact disc player 1A in order to protect a disc D upon unloading and loading, at least one pair of rollers 40a, 40b is provided for holding therebetween and transporting the disc D inserted thereinto. The rollers 40a, 40b are of tapered shape, so that the diameter of each roller 40a, 40b is relatively small over a central portion thereof and gradually increases therefrom to each end, so each roller 40a, 40b con tacts only with the peripheral edge portion of the inserted disc D .
