# WIPER BLADE

## Abstract
A wiper blade for a windshield glass of a vehicle, including a pair of opposing blades which extend approximately parallel from the side edges of the face of a blade body, said body being provided with a component at its base for engagement to a supporting wiper arm. A center rib extends from the center of the face of the blade body between the two opposing blades, and on at least the inside of each of the opposing blades, a wiping piece projects in the longitudinal direction in such a manner that it is in contact with the surface of the glass when the wiper blade is operated.
