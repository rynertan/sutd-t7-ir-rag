# Magnetic recording tape cartridge.

## Abstract
A magnetic recording tape cartridge having means for placing the cartridge in a standard position of a tape player by means of at least one standard pin pro vided in the tape player, said cartridge comprising a pair of through holes defined in the bottom part of the cartridge case with a diameter fairly larger than the diameter of the standard pin and a positioning means comprising a movable member biassed to oppose the through hole by means of a resilient member so that when the standard pin is inserted into the through hole the standard pin is clamped by the movable member and the inner wall of the hole to secure the cartridge in the standard position.
