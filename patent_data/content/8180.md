# Fabrics for papermaking machines.

## Abstract
A fabric 10 , adapted for use in a belt of a papermak ing machine, is formed of interwoven machine direction warp 12 and cross machine direction shute 14 type strands, at least the shute strands 14 being woven in a repetitive pattern of passing under at least three adjacent warp strands 12 and then passing over at least two ad jacent warp strands 12 so as to form a ² ₃ type weave pattern.
