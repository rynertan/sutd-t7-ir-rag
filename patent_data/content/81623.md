# Feeding apparatus for paperboard sheets.

## Abstract
A sheet feeder for corrugated paperboard sheets utiliz ing timed intermittently rotatable belts 42 against which either the top or bottom sheet, depending on the configura tion chosen, of a stack 16 is brought into contact to feed such sheet in synchronism with adjacent processing ma chinery and utilizing continuously applied negative atmo spheric pressure to hold such sheet against the belts with out the need for valving or otherwise breaking the suction pressure.
