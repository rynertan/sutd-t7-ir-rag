# BAKED PRODUCTS EXHIBITING STABLE PROPERTIES CHARACTERISTIC OF FRESHLY BAKED PRODUCTS

## Abstract
Disclosed herein are flour based baked products, particu larly cookies, which exhibit storage stable textural properties characteristic of corresponding freshly baked products. The cookies are prepared by impregnating at least portions of the surface of a cookie dough bakeable to a chewy cookie product with a crispness contributing component to provide a finished, baked cookie exhibiting a combined chewy and crisp texture characteristic of freshly baked cookies.
