# Polyphenylene ether resin composition.

## Abstract
A polyphenylene ether resin composition comprising A a polyphenylene ether resin, B a polystyrene resin, C an acrylonitrile conjugated diene elastomeric poly mer, D a graft copolymer obtained by reacting butyl rubber and an unsaturated polyolefin in a bifunctional phenol as a reac tion medium, and E an aromatic phosphate.
