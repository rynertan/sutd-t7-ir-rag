# Method of servicing a vehicle air spring suspension strut.

## Abstract
In a method of servicing a vehicle air spring suspension strut, the upper mount 36 of the suspension strut 10 is detached from the sprung portion 14 of the vehicle and the strut is swung on the ball joint 20 of the lower controi arm 18 to a service position in which on board repairs can be effected, including the exchange of an elastomeric air sleeve 52 by using a special inner clamp ring 74 which is manually installed using a tool 76, to provide optimized clearance with respect to the dust tube, and allowing unrestricted reciproca tory strut movements during vehicle operation subsequent to re attachment of the strut to the sprung portion of the vehicle.
