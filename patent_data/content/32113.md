# ANTIINFLAMMATORY 4,5 DIARYL ALPHA POLYHALOALKYL 1H IMIDAZOLE 2 METHANOLS

## Abstract
Abstract of the Disclosure Antiinflammatory 4,5 diaryl α polyhaloalkyl 1H imidazole methanols, such as 4,5 bis 4 fluorophenyl α,α di trifluoromethyl 1H imidazole methanol are useful in treatment of arthritis. Cross Reference to Related Applications This application is a continuation in part of U.S. Patent Application Serial No. 181,991, filed August 28, 1980, which in turn is a continuation in part of U.S. Patent Application Serial No, 109,923, filed January 7, 1980.
