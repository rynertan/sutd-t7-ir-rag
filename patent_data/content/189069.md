# Pyrazolesulfonamide derivative, process for its production and herbicide containing it.

## Abstract
A pyrazolesulfonamide derivative represented by Formula I CHEM wherein Q, m, R, B, D, E, W and G are as defined in the specification. The compound is useful as herbicide having not only an excellent killing effect on weeds but also the selectivity on certain crops. Useful processes for synthesizing the compound are also disclosed.
