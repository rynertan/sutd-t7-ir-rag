# IMPROVEMENTS IN OR RELATING TO AN ADAPTOR FOR A PRINTED CIRCUIT BOARD CONNECTOR

## Abstract
An adaptor is provided for a printed circuit board connec tor 2 of the kind having a grid array of contacts 4 . The adap tor 10 has a plurality of electrical conductors 20, 22, 24 a terminal end 20 , 22 , 24 of each of which is adapted for at tachment to a respective contact 4 of the grid array of con tacts of the connector 2 . The other ends of the electrical con ductors 20, 22, 24 of the adaptor 10 are arranged to define an in line array of contacts, so that the adaptor 10 is able to serve to convert a grid array contact arrangement into an in line contact arrangement.
