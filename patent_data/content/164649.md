# Envelope delivery device.

## Abstract
A delivery device includes a lower belt 9 arranged along the delivery direction, an upper belt 8 arranged in opposi tion to the lower belt 9 and adapted to be guided for up and down movement, and a mechanism for causing up and down movement of the upper belt 8 and allowing the upper belt 8 to be supported only on an article E to be carried on the lower belt 9 , when the upper belt 8 is in a descend ing position.
