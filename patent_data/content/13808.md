# A process for the production of basic magnesium sulphonates and the products obtained by this process.

## Abstract
A process for the production of basic magnesium sul phonates comprising carbonating a mixture containing a an oil soluble sulphonate or sulphonic acid b a stoichiometric excess of a magnesium oxide above that required to react with a . c a hydrocarbon solvent d water e a C₃ to C₆ ketone f one or more reaction promoters selected from the group consisting of carboxylic acids or salts thereof, amines, C₄ to C₆ diketones.
