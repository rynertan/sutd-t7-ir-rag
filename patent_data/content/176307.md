# Television cameras.

## Abstract
A television camera incorporates two solid state image sensors CCD₁, CCD₂ , one of which is intensified II and one of which is not, in the same camera head, and switching one or other into operation depending upon the ambient lighting conditions by switching HM,A B the light received by the camera to the appropriate sensor. Thus a good image of a scene under all normal night and day ambient light conditions is obtained.
