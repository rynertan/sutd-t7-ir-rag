# Structure of a device for varying the inclination in ski boots.

## Abstract
The device for varying the inclination in ski boots comprises, on either 3 of the two mutually movable parts 3, 50 of a ski boot, an engagement block 2 releasably lockable at a presettable position of said part and engageable by contact with an abutment detent 40 defined on the other 50 of said parts.
