# Hinge and method for manufacturing said hinge.

## Abstract
A hinge comprises leaves 1 and a knuckle which includes tabs 5 extending from the leaves and support ing hingeing pivot 3 means. At least one of the tabs 5 of one leaf 1 supports at least one of the tabs 5 of another leaf 1 and the construction also includes strength ening webs 6 extending from each tab 5 to the respec tive leaf 1 and located on one side only, with respect to the hingeing pivot axis 3 , of each respective tab 5 .
