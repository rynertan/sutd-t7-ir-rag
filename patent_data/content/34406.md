# Rotary die cutters.

## Abstract
A rotary die cutter comprises a rotary soft anvil 10 coact ing with a rotary hard anvil 11 mounting one or more cutting rules, or formes 13 . Each cutting rule 14 has within the area defined by the rule a metal or metal containing pusher adapted to be moved radially outwardly of the rule at a pre determined location by a magnetic source 19 to eject from the rule any cut waste board 20 contained therein.
