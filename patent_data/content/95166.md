# Avionic clamp having separate strap and locking member.

## Abstract
A wire bundle or cable clamp 10 primarily adapted for use in the aviation industry is disclosed. The clamp 10 includes a flexible strap 14 having a serrated portion 28 and a strap receiving member 12 to which the strap is removably mounted. A pawl 16 including a serrated plate 52 is pivotally mounted to the strap receiving member 12 , and is biased to permanently but reversibly engage the serrations 30 of the strap which is looped around an object such as a cable, wire bundle 17 or the like.
