# A gear box.

## Abstract
A planetary gear arrangement including a driving shaft 12 , a planetary gear change connecting the driving shaft 12 with a hub 17 and wherein the planetary gear train 19,22,23,24 has two ratios selected by means of longitudinal movement of the planetary gear train 19,22,23,24 longitu dinally of the shaft 12 in order to hold either a sun gear 23 or a ring gear 24 stationary with respect to the planetary gears.
