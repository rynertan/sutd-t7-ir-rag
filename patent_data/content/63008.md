# LOAD CARRYING ROAD VEHICLE

## Abstract
The invention relates to a load carrying road vehicle of the kind devoid of an underframe and having wheels 12 of conventional size so that there is considerable ground clearance beneath the body 10 . So that an increased load carrying capacity can be obtained by utilising the ground clearance beneath the body 10 , the vehicle is provided with at least one load carrying body or pod 16 releasably secured beneath the main box like body 10 .
