# Shock absorber.

## Abstract
A shock absorber has a piston 32 which comprises upper and lower members 48, 50 . The upper and lower members are fixedly assembled to define therein the vortex chamber 68, 70 . The piston has vertically extending chan nels on the periphery 56, 58 thereof to define the vertical section of the vortex passages together with the inner periphery of the cylinder tube.
