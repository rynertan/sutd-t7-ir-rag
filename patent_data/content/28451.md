# Oscillator system for fishing reel.

## Abstract
This invention comprehends an improved means for providing a reciprocating motion for the spool of the spinning style fishing reel. In the normal rewind mode of operation, the spool oscillates back and forth relative to the line winding means. In this reel, the oscillating motion is produced by a cam yoke arrangement 130,140 mounted within the housing and unique in the fishing reel art.
