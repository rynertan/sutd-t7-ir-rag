# Casting device.

## Abstract
Casting device for simultaneous casting of at least two casting moulds or casting balls in a casting line. According to the invention the device comprises a carriage 11 movable in the direction of the casting line, the carriage is provided with two gutters 14, 15 from respective furnace spouts to the respective sprues 20 of the moulds 18, 19 , and at least one gutter is adjustable in the longitudinal direction of the line.
