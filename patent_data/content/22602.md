# A dough tray for a proofer.

## Abstract
The tray is provided with two head walls 1, 2 with means 10 for coupling the dough tray to a chain 11 or the like, an element transporting a plurality of dough trays through the proofer and with a carrier frame 3, 4, 5, 6 extending between the head walls for at least one endless strip of cloth 12 or the like material with a part receiving dough lumps and sagging between two frame portions, provided with positive drive means 14, 15, 16, 17, 18 for displacing the cloth over the frame.
