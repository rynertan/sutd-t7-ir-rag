# HAND CARRIER FOR FLANGED BOTTLES

## Abstract
A hand carrier 20 for flanged bottles FB comprises a pair of tubular members 22a,22b hingedly connected to one another at FL . Each of said members accommodates a half section of a neck portion of each flanged bottle, so that a lower side of a flange BF in each bottle is supported by a bottom plate 33a,33b 35a,35b of the cylindrical members.
