# Electrical resistance furnaces.

## Abstract
An electrical resistance furnace which includes a number of electrical resistance heating elements 11 arranged around the sides and surrounding a space 12 containing a crucible 13 or similar container to be heated, the elements 11 aupported at least at their ends by fire brick material 19, 21 and being surrounded by heat insula tion 22, 23 wherein both ends of each resistance element 11 extend through apertures 18 inthe fire brick material 19 so that by removing part of the heat insulation 22, 23 access may be gained to both ends of the element 11 from the exterior of the furnace to permit removal and repair or replacement of the element 11 .
