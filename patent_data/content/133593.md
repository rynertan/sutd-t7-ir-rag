# Scraping peeling device.

## Abstract
Scraping peeling device, e.g. for potatoes, comprising a rotatable cylinder or drum 2 consisting of a number of adjoining rings internally provided with means to effect a scraping or peeling action, and with means 10, 11, 10 , 11 to cause independent rotation of the rings with different speeds of rotation. The cylinder 2 is further provided with means 3 for introducing the products to be scraped or peeled, and with means 4 for removing the finished products.
