# A rotor diverter valve.

## Abstract
A rotor diverter valve operable for diverting materials in a pneumatic conveying line to secondary locations with the rotor diverter valve including a housing 45 and pivotally mounted rotor 35 having a pair of converging passages 36,37 therein that can direct material from an inlet in the housing to one of a plurality of outlets on the housing through proper position of the rotor 35 within the housing 45 . The housing 45 includes interchangeable inlet and outlet atachments that can be tightly sealed around the converging passages in the rotor.
