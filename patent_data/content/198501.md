# Gripping Device.

## Abstract
A grip device wherein the outer peripheral edge of an object W to be grasped is gripped as a place to be grasp ed, comprising a base 1 of the device, grip members 4, 5 which grip the object to be grasped, elastic members 2, 3 which are disposed between the base and the grip mem bers 4, 5 so as to urge the respective grip members 4, 5 in an opening direction or a closing direction, and a driver 6 for the grip members, which is made of a linear or flat member of a shape memory alloy and which is connected across the grip members 4, 5 .
