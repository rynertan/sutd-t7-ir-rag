# Method of producing a wire drawing die.

## Abstract
The invention relates to the manufacture of wire drawing dies and provides a method for securing a core in a metal housing. It comprises the clamping of an annulus 43 , con sisting of a hardenable metal alloy, around a cylindrical core 42 consisting of a material such as polycrystalline diamond or boron nitride, with the object of obtaining a permanent clamping. The core annulus combination 42 43 is secured in a metal housing 45 of a conventional shape.
