# Tape feeding device for stapling machines.

## Abstract
A stapling machine for hook and eye fasteners includes an improved tape feeding device 10 which comprises a roller means 29,29 having means 30,30 on its circumfer ence for continuously engaging a reinforcing tape T , and means 22,24,25,28 for rotating the roller means in one direction. The roller means, upon rotation, feeds a predeter mined length of the tape reliable into a work station 33 where a hook or eye fastener 19 , a backing plate 20 and the tape are attached to the fabric of a garment.
