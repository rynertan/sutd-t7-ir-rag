# WATER SWELLABLE SEALANTAND METHOD OF PREPARATION.

## Abstract
A water swellable, humidity curing type of one pack polyurethane sealant containing as necessary ingredients 1 a water swellable urethane prepolymer having 1.5 to 10 terminal NCO groups and being prepared by reacting a polyoxyalkylene ether polyol in which oxyethylene group accounts for 50 to 90 of the total oxyalkylene chain molecular weight with a polyisocyanate, 2 a water nonswellable urethane prepolymer containing terminal NCO groups and being obtained by reacting a polyoxyalkylene ether polyol having C₃ to C₄ alkylene groups with a polyisocyanate, and 3 a filler, has an improved storage stability.
