# Electronic control system for a transfer device.

## Abstract
This disclosure illustrates and describes an electronic program control for controlling the filling operation for cartons supplied by one turret mechanism 24 having a plurality of equally spaced mandrels 22 to a compart mented conveyor which carries the cartons in pairs beneath a pair of filler nozzles 50 . The electronic program control includes the switches, AND gates, shift registers and flip flops required to assure that, in the event a carton is missing for any reason from a conveyor compartment, the appropri ate filler nozzle will not be actuated.
