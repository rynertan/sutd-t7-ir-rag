# Monoclonal antibody to human T cells, method for preparing it, hybrid cell line producing it, therapeutic composition comprising it and diagnostic method using it.

## Abstract
Hybrid cell line for production of monoclonal antibody to an antigen found on all normal human T cells. The hybrid is formed by fusing splenocytes from immunized Balb cJ mice with P3X63Ag8UI myeloma cells. Diagnostic and therapeutic uses of the monoclonal antibody are also disclosed.
