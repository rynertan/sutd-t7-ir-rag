# Apparatus for mapping and identifying an element within a field of elements.

## Abstract
Apparatus for identifying an element within an array of elements, is disclosed as including a video camera 12 , with a fixed reticle 16 for sighting to facilitate the operator to position the camera in an orientation so that the camera is aligned with or sight ed upon the element 32 within the array, an operator controllable device 18 coupled to the video camera to permit the operator to variably position the camera, orientation sensing revolvers 20a, 20b coupled to the sighting camera to provide an output signal indicative of the particular orientation of the camera, and a micro processor 22 responsive to the position indicating signals to provide a manifestation indicating the el ement s position within the field, as sighted by the operator.
