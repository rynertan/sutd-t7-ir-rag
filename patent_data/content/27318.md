# Fishing reel.

## Abstract
This invention comprehends a means for coupling an oscillator mechanism of a fishing reel to the spool of the fishing reel so that the spool will reciprocate back and forth as the fishing line is wound thereon. The spool shaft 300 is received in a mounting mechanism 147,200 associated with the yoke strap cam follower portion of the oscillating mechan ism 130,140 . An undercut groove 304 on the shaft 300 facilitates its fastening to the yoke strap 147 .
