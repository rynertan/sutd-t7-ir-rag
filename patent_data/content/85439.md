# Overvarnish apparatus for decorator machine.

## Abstract
An overvarnish unit 20 for a can 28 decorator machine mounted on an adjustable frame 36, 38 which supports an adjustable applicator roll 34 for applying a coating material associated with a pre spin wheel 33 for rotatable mandrels 26 and also supports an adjustable coating material meter roll 48 associated with an adjustable fountain means 50 .
