# Valve operator.

## Abstract
A valve operator whereby movement of linkage arms 20, 21, 24, 25 , connected to a spring loaded movable pressure plate 16 and to a valve stem support 32 opens and closes the valve diaphragm. A manual override clutch assembly 61, 59, 57a, 54, 50 disengages the drive means 53, 52 and permits manual operation of the valve.
