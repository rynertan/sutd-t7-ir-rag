# Bi concave small incision intraocular lens.

## Abstract
An intraocular lens 11 for implantation within the eye comprising an optic 13 having an optical axis 17 , concave anterior 19 and posterior 21 faces and an interior gas filled chamber 23 . The chamber has concave forward 25 and rear 27 surfaces which extend transverse to the optical axis 17 . A fixation member 15 fixes the optic 11 within the eye. The optic 13 and fixation member 15 are both constructed of a soft, deformable material.
