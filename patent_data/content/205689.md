# A surgical operating room structure.

## Abstract
A surgical operating room structure 10 having prep room 31 , operating room 32 and post operative room 33 around a central core 12 and separated by flexible curtains 16, 17, 18 . The core 12 comprises independently movable sections 13, 14, 15 each having an arm supporting an operating table 22, 23, 24 which is rotatable about the core 12 from one room to the next adjacent room through the curtains 16, 17, 18 .
