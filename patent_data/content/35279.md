# Compositions of alkylene alkyl acrylate copolymers having improved flame retardant properties.

## Abstract
The disclosure of this application is directed to com positions having improved flame retardant properties con taining an alkylene alkyl acrylate copolymer, a halogenated flame retardant additive and an oxide, carbonate, hydro xide or sulfate of magnesium or calcium in an amount more than 20 and up to about 30 percent by weight. The compositions disclosed are useful as jacketing material and as insulation about wire and cable.
