# APPARATUS FOR PHOTOELECTRIC DETECTION OF OBJECTS

## Abstract
The invention comprises a unit 1 implemented with two elongate chambers 3, 4 provided with openings or gaps 7, 8 on one wall 5, 6 . At one end of one chamber one or more LED 9 are adapted, and opposite hereto at the end of the other chamber there is arranged a receiving means 10 . After one or more reflections by the reflecting walls of the first chamber 3 the radiation departs through the opening or gap 7 and after reflection by an object 13 on the outside returns through the gap 8 of the other chamber 4 , to be reflected by the mirror walls of the chamber such as to strike the receiving means 10 after reflection.
