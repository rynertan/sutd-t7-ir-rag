# TAPE CASSETTE CARRIER

## Abstract
A removable tape cassette carrier for carrying a plurality of individual tape cassettes 80 has a pair of parallel plates 72 spaced apart by pillars 77 . Flexible guides 74 between the plates 72 serve to hold the cassettes in position. The plates are provided with a central aperture 78 locating over the shaft of an automatic cassette changing apparatus. Next to each cassette position in the carrier is provided a locator opening 81 which co operates with a locator key on the tape deck of the apparatus to locate the carrier accurately relative to the playing head after rotation of the carrier, for example.
