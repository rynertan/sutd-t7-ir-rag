# Fuel control apparatus for a gas turbine engine.

## Abstract
A fuel control apparatus 34 for a gas turbine engine, wherein an electronic governor generates a signal indicative of the desired fuel air ratio of the engine, and a fuel metering valve 180 is positioned in response to a hydraulic control pressure Pf to meter the fuel flow Wf to the engine According to this invention, there are provided means 100 for generating said control pressure Pf as a copy of the pneumatic compressor discharge pressure Pc of the engine, and means 190 for modulating said control press ure Pf in response to said electrical signal E For use in fuel control systems for gas turbine engines.
