# Mesh tissue fasteners.

## Abstract
Various fasteners are provided to hold together portions of mammalian tissue. The fasteners include fastening mem bers with spaced apart legs 66A adapted to be passed through the tissue portions 54, 52 . One embodiment of the fastener has a mesh like receiver 76E around each leg for engaging the legs 66A of another identical fastening member inserted from an opposite side of the tissue portions 54, 52 . In order embodiments, the receiver is a separate element 62A and is applied to a side of the tissue portions 52, 54 opposite the fastening member to engage the legs 66A of the fastening member. The receiver 62A may have a variety of forms. A number of structures are disclosed for effecting engagement of the fastening member legs 66A with the receiver.
