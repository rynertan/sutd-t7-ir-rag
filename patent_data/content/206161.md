# AUTOMATIC TELEPHONE LINE FAULT LOCATOR

## Abstract
This automatic telephone line fault locator comprises a portable box type structure which comprises an interface sec tion 14 16 including switches 16a 16d and having input terminals for connection to a line 1 3 being controlled, a volt age and current measuring section 10 13 electrically con nected to the interface section for selectively measuring cur rents and voltages of suitable portions of the line being con trolled, a control unit 19 29 including a logic arithmetic and control section 22 coupled to the interface section and the measuring section for controlling the position of the switches aond receiving the measured current and voltage values and calculating therefrom the position of any fault on the line.
